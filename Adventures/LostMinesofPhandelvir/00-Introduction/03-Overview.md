# Overview

> **Source**: Lost Mines of Phandelver p.3–4

## Content

Lost Mine of Phandelver is divided into **four parts**.

### Part 1: Goblin Arrows

The adventurers are on the road to **Phandalin** when they stumble into a **goblin ambush**. They discover that the goblins (who belong to the **Cragmaw tribe**) have captured their dwarf friend ****Gundren Rockseeker**** and his escort, a human warrior named ****Sildar Hallwinter****. The characters must deal with the ambushers and then follow their trail back to the goblins' hideout. They rescue Sildar and learn that Gundren and his brothers discovered a famous lost mine. Sildar knows only that Gundren and his map have been taken to somewhere called **"**Cragmaw Castle**."**

### Part 2: Phandalin

The characters arrive in **Phandalin** to find it terrorized by the **Redbrands**, a gang of miscreants led by a mysterious figure called **Glasstaff**. A number of interesting NPCs can be found in Phandalin, laying the hooks for short adventures in Part 3. The Redbrands try to run the characters out of town, so the characters storm the Redbrand lair. In a hidden stronghold beneath an old manor, they find that ****Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek****, the leader of the Redbrands, is taking his orders from someone called **the Black Spider** — and that the Black Spider wants the adventurers out of the picture.

### Part 3: The Spider's Web

The characters undertake several short adventures in the region around Phandalin as they search for more information about the Black Spider and the lost mine:

- ****Conyberry** and **Agatha**'s Lair** — seek the advice of a dangerous banshee
- ****Old Owl Well**** — spy on a mysterious wizard
- ****Wyvern Tor**** — oust a band of orcs
- ****Ruins of Thundertree**** — investigate the ruined town

Several of these leads point to ****Cragmaw Castle****, the stronghold of ****King Grol****, leader of the Cragmaw goblins. Here the characters discover that the Black Spider is a **drow adventurer named **Nezznar****, and that the Cragmaw goblins work for him. More importantly, they recover **Gundren Rockseeker's map** to the lost mine, or learn the mine's location from one of the other leads.

### Part 4: Wave Echo Cave

Following the map brings the characters to ****Wave Echo Cave****. The lost underground complex is now overrun by undead and strange monsters. **Nezznar** is there with his loyal followers, exploring the mines and searching for the legendary ****Forge of Spells****. The adventurers have the opportunity to:

- Avenge **Gundren Rockseeker**
- Ensure the prosperity and security of **Phandalin** by clearing the mine
- Put an end to the Black Spider's schemes
