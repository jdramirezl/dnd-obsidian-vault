# Adventure Hook

> **Source**: Lost Mines of Phandelver p.4

## Content

You can let players invent their own reasons for visiting **Phandalin**, or you can use the following adventure hook. The backgrounds and secondary goals on the pre-generated character sheets also provide characters with motivations for visiting Phandalin.

### Meet Me in Phandalin

The characters are in the city of [[Neverwinter]] when their dwarf patron and friend, ****Gundren Rockseeker****, hires them to escort a wagon to **Phandalin**. Gundren has gone ahead with a warrior, ****Sildar Hallwinter****, to attend to business in the town while the characters follow with the supplies.

**Payment**: 10 gp each, paid by the owner of ****Barthen's Provisions**** in Phandalin when they deliver the wagon safely.

### The Forgotten Realms

The adventure is set in the **Sword Coast** region of the Forgotten Realms, part of the North — a vast realm of free settlements surrounded by wilderness and adventure. The Sword Coast holds the greatest bastions of civilization, including the coastal city of [[Neverwinter]].

Key characteristics of the setting:
- Medieval high fantasy with knights, castles, elven cities, dwarven mines, and fearsome monsters
- Known roads may be well traveled but aren't necessarily safe
- Even farms within a day's walk of a city can fall prey to monsters
- No place is safe from the sudden wrath of a dragon

## DM Quick Reference

### The Hook
- **Who**: **Gundren Rockseeker** (dwarf, patron/friend)
- **What**: Escort a supply wagon from [[Neverwinter]] to **Phandalin**
- **Why**: Gundren has gone ahead with **Sildar Hallwinter** to attend to business
- **Payment**: 10 gp each, paid at **Barthen's Provisions** on delivery
- **What Gundren doesn't tell them**: He's found **Wave Echo Cave** and is heading there with the map

### Alternative Hooks
- Players can invent their own reasons for visiting Phandalin
- Pre-generated character sheets include backgrounds and secondary goals tied to Phandalin
