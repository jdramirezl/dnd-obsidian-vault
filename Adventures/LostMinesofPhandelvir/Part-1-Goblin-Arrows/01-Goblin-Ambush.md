# Goblin Ambush

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Triboar Trail | **Page**: 6–7

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Llevan medio día recorriendo el Sendero de Triboar. Al rodear un recodo del camino, divisan dos caballos muertos tirados en el sendero, a unos quince metros de ustedes, bloqueando el paso. Cada uno tiene varias flechas de plumas negras clavadas en el cuerpo. El bosque se cierra a ambos lados del camino — un terraplén empinado y matorrales densos flanquean la senda.

**Si se acercan a investigar:**

> Las alforjas han sido saqueadas. Cerca yace un estuche de cuero para mapas... completamente vacío.

## DM Notes

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Captured goblins can reveal the location of Cragmaw Hideout and that Gundren was taken to Cragmaw Castle. A goblin guide lets the party skip both trail traps. See [[Cragmaw-Hideout/00-Overview#What the Goblins Know]].

- The dead horses belong to **Gundren Rockseeker** and **Sildar Hallwinter**. Characters using the "Meet Me in Phandalin" hook can identify them on closer inspection.
- The horses have been dead about a day. Arrows killed them.
- **4 [[Goblins#Goblin|goblins]]** hide in the woods (2 on each side of the road). They wait until someone approaches the bodies, then attack.
- The party **cannot** surprise the goblins. The goblins **might** surprise the party.

### Surprise Check
- Roll **one** Dexterity (Stealth) check for all goblins: `d20 + 6`
- Compare result to each character's **passive Perception**
- Any character with a passive Perception lower than the goblins' roll is **surprised** (loses their first turn)

### Goblin Tactics
- **Round 1**: 2 goblins rush forward for melee attacks; 2 goblins stay 30 ft away and fire bows
- **When 3 goblins are defeated**: The last goblin **flees** toward the goblin trail

### Developments
- **If the goblins win**: They leave the party [[Unconscious|unconscious]], loot them and the wagon, and return to Cragmaw Hideout. The party can recover, buy gear in Phandalin, and return to find the trail.
- **Captured goblins**: Any melee weapon can knock a goblin unconscious (declare non-lethal). After a few minutes, a captured goblin can share info (see [[Cragmaw-Hideout/00-Overview#What the Goblins Know]]). A goblin can also be persuaded to lead the party to the hideout, avoiding traps.
- **If the party skips the trail**: Elmar Barthen in Phandalin will tell them Gundren never arrived and suggest they investigate the ambush site.
