# Goblin Trail

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Trail to Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 7–8

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Detrás de los matorrales al norte del camino, descubren un sendero oculto que serpentea hacia el noroeste, adentrándose en el bosque. Las huellas en el barro son inconfundibles — docenas de pisadas pequeñas, y los surcos de dos cuerpos del tamaño de un humano arrastrados por la tierra.

## DM Notes

- **Finding the trail**: DC 10 Wisdom (Survival) check. On success, the character identifies ~12 [[Goblins#Goblin|goblin]] tracks and signs of two human-sized bodies dragged away.
- The trail leads **5 miles northwest** to the Cragmaw Hideout.
- The party can steer the wagon off the road and tie off the oxen before following the trail.
- **Establish marching order** — the lead character triggers traps.
- If a captured goblin guides the party, they **avoid both traps** automatically.

## Monsters

None on the trail itself.

## Treasure

None.

## Traps & Hazards

> [!danger] Trap: Snare
> DC 12 Perception to spot. Lead character makes DC 10 DEX save or is suspended 10 ft up, restrained. 1d6 bludgeoning if cut down without being lowered.

> [!danger] Trap: Pit
> DC 15 Perception to spot. DC 10 DEX save or fall 10 ft for 1d6 bludgeoning. No check to climb out.

### Snare (10 minutes down the trail)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Detection** | Passive Perception 12+ auto-detects; otherwise DC 12 Wisdom (Perception) |
| **Trigger** | Lead character walks into it |
| **Effect** | DC 10 DEX save or suspended upside down 10 ft in the air, **[[Restrained|restrained]]** |
| **Escape** | 1+ slashing damage to the cord frees the character |
| **Fall damage** | 1d6 bludgeoning if not carefully lowered |

### Pit (20 minutes down the trail)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Detection** | Passive Perception 15+ auto-detects; otherwise DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) |
| **Dimensions** | 6 ft wide, 10 ft deep, camouflaged |
| **Trigger** | Creature moves across it |
| **Effect** | DC 10 DEX save or fall in for 1d6 bludgeoning |
| **Escape** | Walls aren't steep — no check needed to climb out |
