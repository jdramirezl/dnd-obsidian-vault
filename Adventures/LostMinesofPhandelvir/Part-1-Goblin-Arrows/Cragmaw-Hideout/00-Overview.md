# Cragmaw Hideout — Overview

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 8

## Overview

The Cragmaw tribe has established a hideout in a hillside cave to harass traffic along the Triboar Trail. The tribe is named for their sharpened, jagged teeth. The hideout's boss is a [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbear]] named **Klarg**, who has orders from **King Grol** (chief of the Cragmaws) to plunder caravans. Recently, a messenger from Cragmaw Castle brought new orders: capture the dwarf **Gundren Rockseeker** and anyone traveling with him.

## What the Goblins Know

> [!tip] DM Tip
> This is the single most important info dump in Part 1. Every captured goblin can reveal Cragmaw Castle's existence, the Black Spider, and where Sildar is held. Let players earn this intel early.

If captured or [[Charmed|charmed]], [[Goblins#Goblin|goblins]] can reveal:

- Fewer than **20 goblins** currently in the lair
- Their leader is a **bugbear named Klarg**
- Klarg answers to **King Grol**, chief of the Cragmaw tribe, who dwells in **Cragmaw Castle** (~20 miles NE, in Neverwinter Wood)
- Someone called **the Black Spider** is paying the Cragmaws to watch for the dwarf **Gundren Rockseeker**
- Gundren was captured along with **a map** and sent to King Grol at Cragmaw Castle
- The dwarf's human companion (**Sildar Hallwinter**) is held in the "eating cave" ([[06-Goblin-Den]])
