# 5. Overpass

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 10

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El pasaje del arroyo continúa ascendiendo más allá de otro tramo de escalones irregulares, curvándose hacia el este. El rugido de una cascada resuena desde alguna caverna más adelante.

**Si no vieron el puente desde el área 4:**

> Un puente desvencijado cruza el pasaje, conectando dos túneles a unos seis metros por encima del arroyo.

## DM Notes

- **1 [[Goblins#Goblin|goblin]]** stands watch on the bridge, **hiding**.
- Characters can spot it: **Wisdom (Perception) check** contested by the goblin's **Dexterity (Stealth) check**.
- The guard is **lazy and inattentive**. If no characters use light, each can attempt Dexterity (Stealth) vs. the goblin's passive Perception to creep by unnoticed.
- **If the goblin spots the party**: It signals [[07-Twin-Pools-Cave|area 7]] to release a flood, then throws javelins down at the characters.

### Bridge
- Spans the passage **20 ft above** the stream
- Climbing the cavern walls to the bridge: **DC 15 Strength (Athletics)** — walls are rough but slick with spray
- **AC 5, 10 hp** — if destroyed, creatures on it must make a **DC 10 DEX save** or fall 20 ft for **2d6 bludgeoning** and land [[Prone|prone]]. Those who succeed hold on and must climb to safety.

### Flood!

> [!danger] Trap: Flood
> If the bridge goblin spots the party, goblins in area 7 release a water surge on Round 2. DC 15 STR save or knocked prone, washed to area 1, 1d6 bludgeoning. Can happen twice.

The large pools in [[07-Twin-Pools-Cave|area 7]] have collapsible walls. When signaled by the bridge lookout:
- **Round 1**: Goblins in area 7 start knocking away supports
- **Round 2** (on goblins' initiative): Water surge pours from area 7 down to [[01-Cave-Mouth|area 1]]

> *"¡El pasaje se llena de repente con un rugido ensordecedor, mientras una enorme oleada de agua se precipita desde arriba!"*

**Flood mechanics:**
- Creatures on the bridge or climbing walls are **safe**
- Within 10 ft of the side passage (area 4) or steps to area 3: **DC 10 DEX save** to dodge into cover
- If caught in the flood: **DC 15 STR save** to hold on
- **On failure**: Knocked **prone**, washed down to area 1, take **1d6 bludgeoning**
- The goblins can release a **second flood** (second pool), but only if the bridge goblin signals again after seeing the first flood didn't clear the intruders
