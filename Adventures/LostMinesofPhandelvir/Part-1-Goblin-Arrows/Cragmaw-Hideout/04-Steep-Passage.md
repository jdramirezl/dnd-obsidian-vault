# 4. Steep Passage

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 9–10

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El pasaje principal desde la boca de la cueva asciende abruptamente, con el arroyo cayendo y salpicando por su lado oeste. Entre las sombras, un pasaje lateral cruza hacia el oeste, al otro lado del arroyo.

**Si tienen visión en la oscuridad o una fuente de luz y miran hacia arriba:**

> En las sombras del techo hacia el norte, alcanzan a distinguir la silueta tenue de un puente desvencijado de madera y cuerda que cruza el pasaje por encima de ustedes. Otro pasaje se cruza con este, a unos seis metros del suelo.

## DM Notes

- From this point on, characters **without darkvision need light** to see.
- Characters who can see the bridge at [[05-Overpass|area 5]] might also notice the [[Goblins#Goblin|goblin]] guard: **Wisdom (Perception) check** contested by the goblin's **Dexterity (Stealth) check**.
- **The goblin on the bridge** notices the party if they carry light or don't use stealth. It does **not attack** — instead it sneaks east to signal [[07-Twin-Pools-Cave|area 7]] to release a flood.
- The goblin moves undetected if its Stealth check exceeds the party's passive Perception.

### Western Passage
- Choked with rubble and steep escarpments — **difficult terrain**
- The ledge between escarpments is **fragile**: any weight over **100 lbs** causes collapse

## Monsters

None stationed here. The goblin on the bridge above belongs to [[05-Overpass|area 5]].

## Treasure

None.

## Traps & Hazards

> [!danger] Trap: Fragile Ledge
> Collapses under 100+ lbs. DC 10 DEX save or 2d6 bludgeoning and knocked prone. Half damage on success.

### Fragile Ledge (Western Passage)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Trigger** | Weight exceeding 100 lbs on the ledge |
| **Effect** | DC 10 DEX save — **2d6 bludgeoning** on failure, half on success |
| **Additional** | Creature falls **[[Prone|prone]]** on a failed save |

### Flood Zone
This passage is in the path of the flood from [[07-Twin-Pools-Cave|area 7]]. Characters within 10 ft of the side passage or steps to area 3 can attempt a **DC 10 DEX save** to dodge out of the way. See [[05-Overpass#Flood!]] for full mechanics.
