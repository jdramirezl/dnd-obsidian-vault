# 6. Goblin Den

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 10–11

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta gran caverna está dividida en dos por un escarpado de tres metros de altura. Una escalera natural de piedra sube desde la parte baja hasta la cornisa superior. El aire está brumoso por el humo de una fogata, y apesta a pieles mal curadas y goblins que no conocen el agua.

## DM Notes

- **6 [[Goblins#Goblin|goblins]]** inhabit this den. **5 ordinary goblins** tend the cooking fire in the **lower (northern)** part. **Yeemik** (the leader, 12 hp) rests in the **upper (southern)** part.
- ****Sildar Hallwinter**** is held prisoner, securely bound on the southern ledge. He is weak and at **1 hp**.

### Yeemik's Negotiation (Hostage Situation)

> [!warning] Hard Fight
> Yeemik holds Sildar (1 hp) hostage on the ledge. If the party refuses to parley, he shoves Sildar off for 1d6 bludgeoning — instant death saves. Have a plan to stabilize Sildar fast.

If Yeemik sees the party gaining the upper hand, he grabs Sildar and drags him to the edge of the upper level:

> *"¡Tregua, o el humano muere!"*

- **Yeemik's offer**: Kill Klarg in [[08-Klargs-Cave|area 8]] and bring back his head. He'll release Sildar in exchange.
- **Sildar warns** (groggily) that the goblin can't be trusted — and he's right.
- **If the party accepts**: After they kill Klarg, Yeemik **betrays them** and demands a rich ransom for Sildar anyway.
- **If the party refuses to parley**: Yeemik **shoves Sildar off the ledge** — 1d6 bludgeoning damage, which drops Sildar to **0 hp**. Quick-acting characters can try to stabilize him.

### Roleplaying Sildar Hallwinter

> [!info] Quest Hook
> Sildar reveals 4 key plot threads: Wave Echo Cave exists, the Black Spider ordered Gundren's capture, Gundren's map went to Cragmaw Castle, and Iarno Albrek is missing in Phandalin.

- Kindhearted human male, nearly 50 years old
- Member of the famous **griffon cavalry of Waterdeep**
- Agent of the **Lords' Alliance**
- Came to Phandalin to investigate the disappearance of **Iarno Albrek** (fellow Lords' Alliance wizard)

**Sildar's key info (4 pieces):**
1. The Rockseeker brothers (Gundren, Tharden, Nundro) found the entrance to **Wave Echo Cave** — the lost mines of the Phandelver's Pact
2. **Klarg** had orders to waylay Gundren. The **Black Spider** sent word to capture the dwarf.
3. Gundren's **map** was taken. Both dwarf and map were sent to **King Grol** at **Cragmaw Castle**.
4. Sildar's contact in Phandalin is a wizard named **Iarno Albrek** — who has gone missing.

**Sildar's offer**: 50 gp to escort him to Phandalin (he'll secure a loan upon arrival).

### If Rescued
- Sildar joins the party but is **anxious to reach Phandalin** quickly
- He has no weapons or armor — can take a shortsword from a defeated goblin or borrow one
- See NPC Party Members guidelines: let PCs make decisions, keep Sildar's combat actions simple, he can stabilize dying characters or guard prisoners
