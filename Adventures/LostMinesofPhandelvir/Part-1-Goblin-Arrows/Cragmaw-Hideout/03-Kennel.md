# 3. Kennel

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 8–9

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Justo dentro de la boca de la cueva, unos escalones de piedra irregulares suben hacia una pequeña cámara húmeda y oscura en el lado este del pasaje. La cueva se estrecha hasta convertirse en una grieta empinada al fondo, y está impregnada de un hedor a bestia salvaje. Gruñidos feroces y el repiqueteo de cadenas les dan la bienvenida — dos lobos están encadenados justo dentro de la entrada, cada cadena sujeta a una barra de hierro clavada en la base de una estalagmita.

## DM Notes

- **3 [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Wolf|wolves]]** are actually confined here (read-aloud says two, but the mechanics specify three).
- Wolves **can't reach targets standing on the steps**, but attack any non-goblin creature that enters the room.
- Goblins in nearby caves **ignore** the sounds of fighting wolves — they constantly snap and snarl at each other.

### Calming the Wolves
- DC 15 Wisdom (Animal Handling) — on success, wolves allow free movement in the room
- **DC drops to 10** if the wolves are given food

### Fissure / Natural Chimney

> [!tip] DM Tip
> The chimney (DC 10 Athletics) is the easiest way to surprise Klarg. Remind players about it if they're struggling — it bypasses the entire upper level.

- Narrow opening in the east wall leads to a **natural chimney** that climbs **30 ft** to [[08-Klargs-Cave|area 8]]
- Rubbish discarded from above at the base
- **DC 10 Strength (Athletics)** to climb — move at half speed on success
- Result 6–9: No progress gained or lost
- Result 5 or less: Fall — **1d6 bludgeoning per 10 ft fallen**, land [[Prone|prone]] at base
- ⭐ **This is the easiest way to surprise Klarg** — he doesn't expect an attack from this direction

### Wolves Breaking Free
If goaded by enemies beyond their reach, wolves attempt to yank the iron rod free each round:
1. **DC 15 Strength check** — on success, rod loosens, DC drops to 10
2. **DC 10 Strength check** — on success, rod yanked loose, chains freed
- A [[Goblins#Goblin|goblin]] or [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbear]] can use its action to release one wolf from its chain
