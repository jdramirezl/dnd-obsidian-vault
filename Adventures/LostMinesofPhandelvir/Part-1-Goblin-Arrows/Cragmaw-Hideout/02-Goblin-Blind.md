# 2. Goblin Blind

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 8–9

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Al cruzar al lado este del arroyo que brota de la cueva, descubren un pequeño claro excavado entre los matorrales de zarzas — una especie de puesto de vigilancia improvisado. Tablones de madera aplanan las zarzas y crean un espacio donde los guardias pueden ocultarse y observar el área... incluyendo un par de goblins que acechan ahí mismo, en este instante.

## DM Notes

- **2 [[Goblins#Goblin|goblins]]** are stationed here as lookouts, but they are **bored and inattentive**.
- **If they notice intruders in area 1**: They open fire through the thickets, likely catching the party by surprise.
- **If they don't notice intruders in area 1**: They spot the party when they splash across the stream — neither side is surprised.
- **Stealth approach**: Characters scouting ahead can attempt Dexterity (Stealth) checks contested by the goblins' **passive Perception (9)** to surprise them.

### Cover
- The thickets provide **three-quarters cover** for goblins firing from area 2 into area 1.
- Thickets around the clearing are **difficult terrain** and provide **half cover** to creatures attacking through them.
