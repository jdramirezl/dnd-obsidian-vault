# 8. Klarg's Cave

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 12

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Sacos y cajas de provisiones saqueadas se apilan en el extremo sur de esta gran caverna. Hacia el oeste, el suelo desciende en pendiente hacia una abertura estrecha que se pierde en la oscuridad. Una abertura más grande al norte baja por unos escalones de piedra natural, con el rugido de agua cayendo resonando desde más allá. En el centro de la caverna, las brasas de una gran fogata todavía arden con un resplandor rojizo.

## DM Notes

> [!warning] Hard Fight
> Klarg (27 hp, 2d8+2 morningstar) + Ripper the wolf + 2 goblins is deadly for level 1. If Ripper dies, Klarg flees down the chimney. Consider having him flee at half HP if the party is struggling.

### Klarg
- **[[Bugbears#Bugbear|Bugbear]]** boss of the Cragmaw Hideout
- Filled with **delusions of grandeur** — views himself as a mighty warlord beginning his career of conquest
- **Not entirely sane** — refers to himself in the third person: *"Who dares defy Klarg?"* or *"Klarg will build a throne from your bones, puny ones!"*
- The goblins under his command **resent his bullying**

### Tactics
- **If warned** by goblins from [[07-Twin-Pools-Cave|area 7]]: Klarg and his wolf **hide behind stalagmites**; goblins take cover behind supply piles — they attempt to **ambush** the party
- **If NOT warned**: The party has a good chance to **surprise** them, especially via the chimney from [[03-Kennel|area 3]]
- **If the [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Wolf|wolf]] (Ripper) is killed**: Klarg attempts to **flee** down the chimney to area 3 and escape the cave complex

### Supplies
- Piles of sacks and crates provide **half cover** for fighting or hiding behind them
- Most are marked with a **blue lion** — the symbol of the **Lionshield Coster** (merchant company with a warehouse in Phandalin)
- Hidden among the supplies is Klarg's **unlocked treasure chest** — any character who searches the supplies finds it

## XP Award

Clearing the Cragmaw Hideout and defeating Klarg: award each character **275 XP**.
Combined with the 75 XP from the ambush, this totals **350 XP per character** — enough for **level 2**.
