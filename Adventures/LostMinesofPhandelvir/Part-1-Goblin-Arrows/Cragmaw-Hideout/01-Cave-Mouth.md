# 1. Cave Mouth

> **Part**: 1 — Goblin Arrows | **Location**: Cragmaw Hideout | **Page**: 8

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Siguiendo el rastro de los goblins, llegan a una gran cueva abierta en la ladera de una colina, a ocho kilómetros de la emboscada. Un arroyo poco profundo fluye desde la boca de la cueva, que está oculta tras una espesa maraña de zarzas. Un sendero seco y estrecho se adentra en la cueva por el lado derecho del arroyo.

## DM Notes

- The thicket screening area 2 is **impenetrable from the west side** of the stream. Characters must cross to the east side to see the goblin blind.
- The [[Goblins#Goblin|goblins]] in [[02-Goblin-Blind|area 2]] are supposed to be watching this area but are **not paying attention**.
- **If the party makes a lot of noise** (arguing loudly, setting up camp, cutting brush), the goblins in area 2 notice and attack through the thicket with **three-quarters cover**.

## Monsters

None stationed here. See [[02-Goblin-Blind]] for the guards.

## Treasure

None.

## Traps & Hazards

None at the entrance itself. The flood from [[07-Twin-Pools-Cave|area 7]] washes creatures down to this area (see [[05-Overpass#Flood!]]).
