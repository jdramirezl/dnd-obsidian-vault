# Part 1: Goblin Arrows

> **Adventure**: Lost Mines of Phandelver | **Pages**: 6–13 | **Levels**: 1 → 2

## Overview

The adventure begins with the party escorting a wagon of provisions from Neverwinter to Phandalin along the Triboar Trail. Half a day from Phandalin, they're ambushed by goblins from the Cragmaw tribe. Following the goblins' trail leads to the Cragmaw Hideout — an 8-room cave dungeon where the bugbear Klarg commands a band of goblins.

**Key objectives:**
- Survive the [[01-Goblin-Ambush|Goblin Ambush]]
- Follow the [[02-Goblin-Trail|Goblin Trail]] (2 traps)
- Clear the [[Cragmaw-Hideout/00-Overview|Cragmaw Hideout]] (8 rooms)
- Rescue **Sildar Hallwinter** from the goblins
- (Optional) Recover Lionshield Coster supplies for a reward in Phandalin

## Adventure Hook

Gundren Rockseeker hired the party in Neverwinter to deliver a wagonload of supplies to Barthen's Provisions in Phandalin — 10 gp each on delivery. He rode ahead with his escort, Sildar Hallwinter, saying he and his brothers had found "something big."

## The Wagon

- **Pulled by**: 2 oxen (stop if no one holds the reins)
- **Cargo**: Mining supplies and food — dozen sacks of flour, casks of salted pork, 2 kegs of ale, shovels/picks/crowbars (~12 each), 5 lanterns, barrel of oil (~50 flasks)
- **Total value**: 100 gp
- Any character can drive; no skill check required

## Marching Order

Before starting, establish:
1. Have players introduce characters to each other
2. Ask how they know Gundren Rockseeker
3. Determine marching order — who drives the wagon, who walks alongside, who scouts ahead

## XP Awards

| Milestone | XP per Character |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Defeating goblin ambush + finding Cragmaw Hideout | 75 XP |
| Clearing Cragmaw Hideout + defeating Klarg | 275 XP |
| **Total Part 1** | **350 XP** (enough for level 2) |

## What's Next

After clearing the hideout, the party should head to **Phandalin** (Part 2) to:
- Deliver the wagon to Barthen's Provisions (payment)
- Escort Sildar Hallwinter (50 gp reward)
- Return Lionshield Coster supplies (50 gp reward)
- Follow up on Gundren's kidnapping and the Black Spider
