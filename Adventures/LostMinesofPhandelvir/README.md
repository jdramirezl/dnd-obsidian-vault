# Lost Mines of Phandelver

> **Levels**: 1–5 | **Pages**: 64 | **Setting**: Sword Coast, Forgotten Realms
> **Source**: `dnd-5ed-adv-lost-mine-of-phandelver_text.pdf`

A D&D starter adventure where heroes escort a wagon to Phandalin, uncover a goblin conspiracy, confront the Redbrands, explore the Sword Coast wilderness, and delve into the legendary Wave Echo Cave to stop the Black Spider.

**🎭 Read-aloud text is in Spanish for players. DM notes are in English.**

## Structure

### [[01-Running-the-Adventure|00-Introduction]] — 4 files
Background, overview, adventure hook, the Forgotten Realms.

### [[Part-1-Goblin-Arrows/README|Part 1: Goblin Arrows]] — 12 files (Levels 1→2)
The opening ambush on the Triboar Trail + Cragmaw Hideout dungeon (8 rooms). Rescue Sildar Hallwinter.

### [[Part-2-Phandalin/README|Part 2: Phandalin]] — 26 files (Levels 2→3)
The town of Phandalin (10 locations, 11 NPCs) + Redbrand Hideout dungeon (12 rooms). Confront Glasstaff.

### [[Part-3-Spiders-Web/README|Part 3: The Spider's Web]] — 34 files (Levels 3→4)
Five wilderness locations: Agatha's Lair, Old Owl Well, Ruins of Thundertree (13 areas + Venomfang!), Wyvern Tor, Cragmaw Castle (14 rooms). Rescue Gundren, get the map.

### [[Part-4-Wave-Echo-Cave/README|Part 4: Wave Echo Cave]] — 22 files (Levels 4→5)
The final dungeon (20 areas). The Forge of Spells, Mormesk the wraith, and the boss fight against Nezznar the Black Spider.

### Appendices — 2 files
[[Magic-Items|13 magic items]] + **27 monster stat blocks**

## Quick Reference

### Key NPCs

| NPC | Role | Location |
|-----|------|----------|
| Gundren Rockseeker | Quest giver, captured | Cragmaw Castle → Wave Echo Cave |
| Sildar Hallwinter | Ally, Lords' Alliance | Cragmaw Hideout → Phandalin |
| Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek | Redbrand leader | Redbrand Hideout |
| Nezznar "Black Spider" | Main villain (drow) | Wave Echo Cave |
| Reidoth | Druid guide | Thundertree |
| Venomfang | Young green dragon | Thundertree (optional!) |

### Adventure Flow

```
Goblin Ambush → Cragmaw Hideout → Phandalin (hub)
                                      ↓
                              Redbrand Hideout
                                      ↓
                    ┌─── Agatha's Lair (quest)
                    ├─── Old Owl Well (quest)
         Phandalin ─┼─── Thundertree (Venomfang!)
                    ├─── Wyvern Tor (quest)
                    └─── Cragmaw Castle (rescue Gundren)
                                      ↓
                            Wave Echo Cave (finale)
```
