# Appendix A: Magic Items

> **Source**: Lost Mines of Phandelver p.52–53

## Using a Magic Item

- **Identifying**: Cast *identify*, or concentrate on the item during a short rest to learn its properties. Potions can be identified by taste.
- **Attunement**: Some items require attunement (a short rest concentrating on the item, separate from identification). A creature can attune to a maximum of **3 items** at a time. Attunement ends if the item is more than 100 ft away for 24 hours, or on death, or voluntarily during a short rest.

## Item Descriptions

### +1 Armor
*Armor (any), uncommon*

You have a +1 bonus to your Armor Class while wearing this armor. A suit of +1 armor never rusts or deteriorates, and it magically resizes to fit its wearer.

### +1 Weapon
*Weapon (any), uncommon*

You have a +1 bonus to the attack rolls and damage rolls you make with this weapon. Some +1 weapons (swords in particular) shed light.

### Boots of Striding and Springing
*Wondrous item, uncommon*

Your walking speed becomes 30 feet (unless already higher), and your speed is not reduced by encumbrance or heavy armor. Whenever you jump, you can jump **three times** the normal distance.

### Gauntlets of Ogre Power
*Wondrous item, uncommon*

Your Strength becomes **19** while wearing these gauntlets. No effect if your Strength is already 19 or higher.

### Potion of Flying
*Potion, very rare*

Grants a flying speed equal to your walking speed for **1 hour**. If the potion wears off while airborne, you must use your movement to descend. If you fail to land within 1 minute, you fall.

### Potion of Healing
*Potion, common*

Regain **2d4 + 2** hit points when you drink this potion.

### Potion of Invisibility
*Potion, very rare*

You (and your equipment) become invisible for **1 hour**. The invisibility ends if you attack or cast a spell.

### Potion of Vitality
*Potion, very rare*

Removes any exhaustion, cures any disease or poison, and maximizes the effect of any Hit Die you spend to regain hit points within the next **24 hours**.

### Ring of Protection
*Ring, rare (requires attunement)*

+1 bonus to **AC and saving throws** while wearing and attuned.

### Spell Scroll
*Scroll, varies*

Bears the words of a single spell in a mystical cipher. If the spell is on your class's spell list, use an action to read the scroll and cast the spell without components. If the spell is higher level than you can normally cast, make a spellcasting ability check (DC = 10 + spell level). On failure, the spell disappears with no effect. The scroll is consumed after casting.

### Spider Staff
*Staff, rare (requires attunement)*

A black adamantine staff shaped like a spider (6 lbs). Must be attuned to gain benefits.

- Can be wielded as a quarterstaff; deals **1d6 extra poison damage** on a hit
- **10 charges**, regains 1d6 + 4 at dusk
- Spells (must be on your class's spell list): *spider climb* (1 charge), *web* (2 charges, DC 15)
- If last charge expended, roll d20; on a 1, the staff crumbles to dust

> Used by [[Elves-Drow#Drow|Nezznar the Black Spider]].

### Staff of Defense
*Staff, rare (requires attunement)*

A slender, hollow staff made of glass, as strong as oak (3 lbs). Must be attuned to gain benefits.

- **+1 bonus to AC** while holding
- **10 charges**, regains 1d6 + 4 at dawn
- Spells (must be on your class's spell list): *mage armor* (1 charge), *shield* (2 charges)
- If last charge expended, roll d20; on a 1, the staff shatters

> Found in the **Redbrand Hideout**, carried by **Glasstaff**.

### Wand of Magic Missiles
*Wand, uncommon*

**7 charges**. Use an action to fire *magic missile* (no components required), expending 1–3 charges. Each charge beyond the first increases the spell level by 1. Regains 1d6 + 1 charges at dawn. If last charge expended, roll d20; on a 1, the wand crumbles to ash. **No spellcasting ability required.**
