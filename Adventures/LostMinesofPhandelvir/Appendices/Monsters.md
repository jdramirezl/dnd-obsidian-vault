## By Challenge Rating

### CR 0
- **Commoner** — Humanoid (any race), 4 HP

### CR 1/8 (25 XP)
- **Cultist** — Humanoid (any race), 9 HP, Devotion (advantage vs charmed/frightened)
- [[Stirge]] — Beast, 2 HP, Blood Drain (attach and drain)
- **Twig Blight** — Plant, 4 HP, False Appearance (hides among vegetation)

### CR 1/4 (50 XP)
- **Goblin** — Humanoid (goblinoid), 7 HP, Nimble Escape (bonus Disengage/Hide)
- [[Skeletons#Skeleton|Skeleton]] — Undead, 13 HP, vulnerable to bludgeoning
- **Wolf** — Beast, 11 HP, Pack Tactics, can knock prone
- [[Zombies#Zombie|Zombie]] — Undead, 22 HP, Undead Fortitude (CON save to stay at 1 HP)

### CR 1/2 (100 XP)
- **Hobgoblin** — Humanoid (goblinoid), 11 HP, Martial Advantage (+2d6 with ally nearby)
- **Orc** — Humanoid (orc), 15 HP, Aggressive (bonus action move toward hostile)
- **Redbrand Ruffian** — Humanoid (human), 16 HP, Multiattack (2 shortsword)

### CR 1 (200 XP)
- [[Bugbears#Bugbear|Bugbear]] — Humanoid (goblinoid), 27 HP, Brute (+1 damage die), Surprise Attack (+2d6)
- **Evil Mage** — Humanoid (human), 22 HP, 4th-level spellcaster (charm person, hold person, misty step)
- [[Ghouls#Ghoul|Ghoul]] — Undead, 22 HP, paralyzing Claws (DC 10 CON, elves immune)
- [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Giant Spider|Giant Spider]] — Beast, 26 HP, poisonous Bite (DC 11 CON), Web (restrain)
- **Sildar Hallwinter** — Humanoid (human), 27 HP, Multiattack, Parry reaction

### CR 2 (450 XP)
- **Grick** — Monstrosity, 27 HP, resistant to nonmagical B/P/S, Stone Camouflage
- [[Elves-Drow#Drow|Nezznar the Black Spider]] — Humanoid (elf), 27 HP, 4th-level wizard, Spider Staff, drow innate spellcasting
- [[Nothic]] — Aberration, 45 HP, Rotting Gaze (3d6 necrotic), Weird Insight (learn secrets)
- [[Oozes#Ochre Jelly|Ochre Jelly]] — Ooze, 45 HP, immune to lightning/slashing, Split reaction
- **Ogre** — Giant, 59 HP, Greatclub (2d8+4)

### CR 3 (700 XP)
- [[Doppelganger]] — Monstrosity (shapechanger), 52 HP, Shapechanger, Read Thoughts, Surprise Attack (+3d6)
- [[Wraith|Mormesk the Wraith]] — Undead, 45 HP, incorporeal, Life Drain (reduces max HP), sunlight sensitivity
- [[Owlbear]] — Monstrosity, 59 HP, Multiattack (beak + claws), Keen Sight and Smell
- [[Beholders#Spectator|Spectator]] — Aberration, 39 HP, 4 Eye Rays (confusion/paralyze/fear/wound), Spell Reflection

### CR 4 (1,100 XP)
- [[Flameskull]] — Undead, 40 HP, 5th-level spellcaster (fireball!), Rejuvenation, Magic Resistance

### CR 8 (3,900 XP)
- **Young Green Dragon** — Dragon, 136 HP, Multiattack (bite + 2 claws), Poison Breath (12d6, DC 16 CON, 30-ft cone)
