> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Page**: 29

> [!tip] Parley Over Combat — Kost Is Worth More Alive
> Hamun Kost is a Red Wizard of Thay with 12 zombies, but he **prefers deals over fighting**. Parley yields 200 XP and two useful side quests (remove orcs at Wyvern Tor + ask Agatha his question). Combat yields 800 XP but is a Hard encounter. Even mid-fight, he'll negotiate if given the chance.

## DM Notes

### Background
Built thousands of years ago by a long-vanished empire, Old Owl Well is a ruined watchtower — little more than crumbling walls and a broken tower stump. An old well in the courtyard still delivers clean, fresh water. The site lies in the wild hills south of the Triboar Trail. Any NPC in Phandalin can provide directions.

Recently, prospectors noted someone has set up camp here with undead guardians.

**No map is provided** for this location.

### Approaching the Ruins

Characters can enter from any direction — following old footpaths or scrambling up the slope through gaps in the rubble walls.

- **12 [[Zombies#Zombie\|zombies]]** lurk inside the crumbled tower shell — **not visible from outside**
- Passive Wisdom (Perception) **10+**: smell a deathly odor from the tower's direction
- When characters approach the tower or tent, the zombies **shamble out**

### Hamun Kost's Reaction

If battle erupts, Kost emerges from his tent and asks: *"What is the meaning of this?"*

- If **any character** attempts to talk — even calling out a greeting or answering his questions during combat — he **temporarily calls off the zombies**
- He is **not particularly aggressive** and willing to strike a deal

### Identifying Kost

| Check | DC | Result |
|-------|-----|--------|
| Intelligence (Arcana) | 10 | Recognizes the forehead tattoo as a necromantic symbol |
| Intelligence (History) | 10 | Identifies the red robes as Thayan garb; tattoo = wizard's school (necromancy) |

### Kost's Requests

Kost stays tight-lipped about why he's here (exploring the site for arcane lore). He'll share information if the party does him a favor:

1. **Remove the orcs at [[01-Wyvern-Tor|Wyvern Tor]]** — they've scouted his camp and seem inclined to cause trouble
2. **Ask [[01-Agathas-Lair|Agatha]] a question**: *"What is the name of the wizard who built the tower at Old Owl Well?"* — Kost won't risk the [[Banshee|banshee]]'s anger himself. (Answer: **Arthindol**)

### Tactics (if combat)

- Kost is an **Evil Mage** — uses his spells at range
- The 12 zombies swarm the nearest targets
- Kost will try to parley even mid-combat if given the chance

## NPCs

- **Type**: **Evil Mage** (Red Wizard of Thay)
