# Triboar Trail

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Page**: 27

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una llovizna ligera humedece las llanuras ondulantes mientras viajan hacia el norte. Al mediodía, se detienen a almorzar bajo un árbol solitario. El camino serpentea entre colinas cubiertas de hierba y bosques dispersos, con las Montañas de la Espada alzándose al sur y la oscura línea del Bosque de Neverwinter al norte. El viejo sendero de Triboar se extiende ante ustedes — cien millas de territorio salvaje entre la civilización y lo desconocido.

## DM Notes

"Triboar Trail" is the local name for this stretch of foothills and rolling, sparsely wooded plains between the Sword Mountains (south) and Neverwinter Wood (north). An old trail runs from the town of Triboar in the east, winding westward through the abandoned village of Conyberry, then north of Phandalin to the High Road along the coast.

### Running Overland Travel

- **Don't just say** "you walk for several miles and encounter nothing of interest"
- **Do describe** weather, terrain, small details — a rock that looks like a grinning face, a hawk circling overhead, the distant silhouette of Wyvern Tor
- Keep the story moving; vivid but brief

> [!tip] Establish Marching and Watch Order Now
> Ask for marching order and watch rotation **before** the first travel day. These matter for random encounters and ambushes. Write them down — you'll reference them repeatedly throughout Part 3.

### Marching Order

Before travel, ask the players:
1. **Marching order** — who leads, who's in the rear?
2. **Watch order** — who takes which watch at camp?

This matters for encounters and ambushes.

> [!info] Hobgoblin Bounty — Black Spider Foreshadowing
> If hobgoblins appear in a random encounter, one carries a sketch of a party member with a 25 gp bounty and the Black Spider's symbol. This is great foreshadowing — the villain knows about the party and is actively hunting them.

### Encounter Notes

- **Goblins**: Know Cragmaw Castle location. Can provide directions if captured and threatened. Each carries a pouch with 1d10 cp.
- **Hobgoblins**: Actively seeking the adventurers to collect a bounty. One carries a crudely drawn sketch of a party member with "25 gold pieces for this one" and a Black Spider symbol. Know Cragmaw Castle location but won't share unless [[Charmed|charmed]].
- **Orcs**: Scouts from the band at [[01-Wyvern-Tor|Wyvern Tor]]. Looking for travelers to ambush or homesteads to burn.
- **Owlbear**: Picks up the characters' scent and pursues relentlessly.
