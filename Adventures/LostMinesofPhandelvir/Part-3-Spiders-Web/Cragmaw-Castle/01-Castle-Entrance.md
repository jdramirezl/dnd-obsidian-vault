# 1. Castle Entrance

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 36

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El castillo consiste en siete torres derruidas de diferentes tamaños y alturas, pero los pisos superiores están todos en diversos estados de colapso. Una corta escalinata conduce a una terraza frente a la entrada principal. Más allá de los restos de un par de puertas destrozadas se extiende un salón en sombras. Torres redondas se alzan sobre la entrada, con oscuras aspilleras que miran hacia la terraza.

## DM Notes

- The main gates (bronze-covered wood) are **corroded and collapsed** — open passage
- No monsters dwell here, but the [[Goblins#Goblin\|goblin]] sentries in [[03-Archer-Post|area 3]] are supposed to be keeping watch
- Sentries glance only **occasionally** out of the arrow slits — characters who move quietly might creep past

### Stealth Check
- Each character makes a **Dexterity (Stealth)** check
- The **lowest result** becomes the DC for the goblins' **Wisdom checks** to notice the party

### If Spotted (or open approach)
- Goblins shoot arrows from behind arrow slits (three-quarters cover)
- They **cannot fire directly** at enemies at or past the broken gate
- Goblins **shout loudly** to alert comrades in [[04-Ruined-Barracks|area 4]] and [[06-Hobgoblin-Barracks|area 6]]

## Monsters

None stationed here. See [[03-Archer-Post]] for the sentries.
