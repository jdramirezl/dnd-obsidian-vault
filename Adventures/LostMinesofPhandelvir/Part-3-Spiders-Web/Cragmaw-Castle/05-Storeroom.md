# 5. Storeroom

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 37

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Viejos barriles de carne salada y sacos de grano podrido llenan esta zona de almacenamiento. Entre las provisiones, ven una cota de malla ensangrentada, una ballesta pesada y una espada larga desenvainada con el emblema de Neverwinter grabado en la empuñadura.

## DM Notes

- Provisions from caravans raided by the Cragmaws along the High Road and Triboar Trail
- Stores are **not edible by human standards**, but goblins tolerate them when fresher food isn't available

### Dwarven Brandy
- One small cask contains **exceptional dwarven brandy** (the goblins overlooked it because of its size)
- Contains the equivalent of **20 glasses**
- **1 glass:** Regains **1 hit point**
- **2 glasses within 1 hour:** Character becomes **[[Poisoned|poisoned]]** for 1 hour

### Sildar's Gear
- The chain mail and longsword belong to **Sildar Hallwinter**
- Sildar is grateful if at least his **longsword** is returned to him
