# 14. King's Quarters

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 40

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta cámara ha sido acondicionada como un tosco espacio habitable, con gruesas pieles arrojadas sobre el suelo a modo de alfombras, viejos trofeos colgados en las paredes, una cama grande al norte y un brasero de brasas ardiendo intensamente. Una mesa redonda con varias sillas se encuentra al sur, cerca de la puerta. Junto a la mesa, en el suelo, hay un enano inconsciente que parece haber sido brutalmente golpeado.

*Nota: Cualquier personaje que escuche a la puerta oye dos voces en una acalorada discusión — una voz fuerte y gruñona exigiendo un pago por algo, y una respuesta suave como la seda.*

> [!warning] Hard Fight — King Grol + Snarl + Vyerith (Doppelganger)
> Three enemies with different tactics: Grol hits hard with Surprise Attack (+2d6), Snarl knocks prone, and Vyerith ambushes from behind the door. If warned, Grol takes Gundren hostage. If Grol dies, Vyerith tries to kill Gundren and flee with the map — act fast.

## DM Notes

### King Grol
- A fierce old [[Bugbears#Bugbear\|bugbear]] with **45 hit points**
- Rules the Cragmaws through **pure intimidation**
- Age has stooped his shoulders and hunched his back, but he remains **surprisingly agile and strong**
- **Demanding and vindictive** — no Cragmaw dares to cross him

### Snarl
- King Grol's [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Wolf\|wolf]] companion with **18 hit points**

### Vyerith (Doppelganger)
- A [[Doppelganger]] disguised as a **female drow**
- Messenger from the **Black Spider**, come to collect Gundren Rockseeker and the map of Wave Echo Cave
- Grol wants to **sell the map** instead of surrendering it — they are negotiating a price
- Vyerith first wants to **question Gundren** to find out if anyone else knows the mine's location
- Then the doppelganger intends to **kill the dwarf and destroy the map**

### Gundren Rockseeker
- Dwarf **Commoner**, **[[Unconscious|unconscious]] but stable at 0 hit points**
- Located near the southwest corner of the room
- If revived, he thanks the party but **won't leave without his map** — he doesn't know where Grol has hidden it

### Tactics — If Warned
- Vyerith **hides behind the door** to the northeast, leaving it open a crack — hoping to attack from the rear
- Grol **holds Gundren hostage**, ready to kill the dwarf if the characters don't back off

### Developments
- If **Grol is killed**: Vyerith tries to **kill Gundren and flee with the map**, heading toward [[11-Ruined-Tower|area 11]] and escaping through the concealed canvas door
- If **cornered**: The doppelganger **fights to the death** rather than allow itself to be captured

### Arrow Slits
- 15 feet above the ground outside — the creatures here **aren't keeping watch** and are unlikely to notice intruders outside

### Northwest Room
- Partially collapsed chamber, once a comfortable bathroom
- Still contains a large tile tub, unused by the castle's current occupants

### Details
- The hobgoblins have **no treasure**, but 1d4 of them carry **bloody sacks** each containing a **severed elf head** — trophies from a recent victory over an elf hunting party
- **Targor Bloodsword** has long aspired to lead the Cragmaw tribe

### Diplomacy
- Clever characters might urge Targor to think of himself as **Grol's successor** rather than Grol's avenger
- **DC 15 Charisma (Persuasion)** — one or more characters must succeed

### XP Award

500 XP (divided equally) for defeating the war band or coming to terms with Targor.

> [!info] Quest Pivot — Gundren's Map to Wave Echo Cave
> This room contains the **key transition to Part 4**. Gundren's map (under Grol's mattress) reveals Wave Echo Cave's location. If Gundren survives, he offers 25 gp each + 10% mine share and asks the party to find his brothers.

## What's Next?

If **Gundren Rockseeker** survives:
- He offers the characters his thanks and asks them to escort him back to **Phandalin**
- He then asks them to venture to **Wave Echo Cave** to learn the fate of his brothers, **Nundro** and **Tharden**
- He knows that someone called the **Black Spider** orchestrated his capture
- **Reward:** 25 gp each + a promise of **10% share** of the mine's wealth once his operation is up and running

The party now knows the location of **Wave Echo Cave** — proceed to **Part 4**.
