# 10. Postern Gate

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 39

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> En el lado sur del viejo castillo, un sendero cubierto de maleza conduce a un pasaje que asciende hacia el interior del muro. Una gran puerta de hierro se alza aquí, protegida del ataque directo desde el exterior. Aspilleras a tres metros del suelo vigilan el sendero.

## DM Notes

- This side entrance is **locked but unguarded**
- The iron door can be opened with **thieves' tools** and a successful **DC 15 Dexterity** check, or knocked down with a successful **DC 25 Strength** check

### Arrow Slits
- Any character who pauses and listens near the arrow slits hears, from [[07-Banquet-Hall|area 7]], an occasional **clatter of crockery** and angry [[Goblins#Goblin\|goblins]] arguing over whether the dishes need cleaning
- The goblins **aren't keeping watch** from these arrow slits
- However, if the characters make a lot of noise (e.g., knocking down the door), the goblins come and look — if they see intruders, they **shout an alarm**
