# 2. Trapped Hall

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 36

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Hay puertas cerradas al norte y al sur, con un montículo de escombros que oscurece parcialmente el pasillo sur. Hacia el este, un amplio corredor termina en dos puertas más que dan al sur y al este. El corredor está lleno de escombros polvorientos y yeso caído de un derrumbe parcial del techo.

> [!tip] Pincer Attack — Reinforcements from Both Sides
> If the sentries in area 3 raised the alarm, goblins from area 4 (north) and hobgoblins from area 6 (south) attack **simultaneously** through both doors. This is the castle's main defensive response — the party gets caught in a crossfire.

## Treasure

None.

> [!danger] Tripwire Trap — Cave-In (3d6 Bludgeoning)
> Copper tripwire hidden in rubble near the door to area 8. Passive Perception 20 to spot, DC 10 active search. Triggering it causes a cave-in (DC 10 DEX, 3d6 bludgeoning, half on save) and **alerts areas 3, 7, 8, and 9** — potentially bringing the whole castle down on the party.

## Traps & Hazards

### Tripwire Trap (in front of door to area 8)

Dusty plaster and rubble conceals a copper tripwire connected to linchpins hidden in the ruined ceiling.

| Check | DC | Effect |
|-------|-----|--------|
| **Spot trap (passive)** | Passive Perception **20** | Reveals the tripwire |
| **Spot trap (active search)** | DC 10 Wisdom (Perception) | Reveals the tripwire |
| **Disarm** | — | Once spotted, easily avoided and disarmed (no check required) |
| **Trigger** | Walk over/through rubble | Cave-in of wooden beams and heavy stones |
| **Damage** | DC 10 Dexterity saving throw | **3d6 bludgeoning** (half on success) |

**Alert:** The noise of the collapse puts monsters in [[03-Archer-Post|areas 3]], [[07-Banquet-Hall|7]], [[08-Dark-Hall|8]], and [[09-Goblin-Shrine|9]] on alert.

**XP:** 100 (divided equally) for detecting or surviving the trap.
