# 3. Archer Post

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 36

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta pequeña habitación está llena de escombros. La aspillera frente a la puerta ofrece un excelente campo de tiro sobre la terraza frente a las puertas del castillo.

## DM Notes

- Cragmaw Castle's main defenses are its **secret location** and the appearance of being abandoned
- There are **two of these rooms** (one on each side of the entrance), each with 2 [[Goblins#Goblin\|goblins]]
- By taking turns shooting and ducking back, **both archers can fire each round** at targets outside
- When characters enter the room, goblins **drop shortbows and draw melee weapons**
- If they spot intruders at [[01-Castle-Entrance|area 1]], they alert [[04-Ruined-Barracks|area 4]] and [[06-Hobgoblin-Barracks|area 6]]
