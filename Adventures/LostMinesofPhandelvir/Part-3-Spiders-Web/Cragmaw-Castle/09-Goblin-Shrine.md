# 9. Goblin Shrine

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 39

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta cámara ocupa la torre norte del castillo. Un altar de piedra se alza en el centro de la sala, cubierto con un paño negro manchado de sangre. Implementos rituales dorados — un cáliz, un cuchillo y un incensario — están cuidadosamente dispuestos sobre el altar. Dos arcos al sur están cubiertos con pesadas cortinas.

## DM Notes

- Goblins have no use for human gods — the Cragmaws have rededicated this place to **Maglubiyet**, the god of goblins and hobgoblins
- The bloodstained cloth completely covers the stone altar, whose sides are engraved with images of the same gods from [[08-Dark-Hall|area 8]]

### Lhupo (Goblin "Priest")
- [[Goblins#Goblin\|Goblin]] with **12 hit points**, wears filthy robes over armor
- Claims to hear **Maglubiyet speaking to him** — possesses **no actual divine powers**
- Owner of the **Grick** in [[08-Dark-Hall|area 8]]

### Tactics
- If the goblins heard fighting the grick in [[08-Dark-Hall|area 8]], they **hide behind the altar** and attempt to **surprise** the characters
- Otherwise, all three goblins are **kneeling before the altar**, praying to their evil god
