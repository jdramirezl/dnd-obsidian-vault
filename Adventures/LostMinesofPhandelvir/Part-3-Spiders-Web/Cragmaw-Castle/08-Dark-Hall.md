# 8. Dark Hall

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 38

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Este alto y estrecho salón parece haber sido parte de una capilla o santuario en algún momento. Figuras angélicas están esculpidas en las partes altas de la sala, mirando hacia el suelo. Al norte, pesadas cortinas bloquean un par de arcos simétricos. Entre los arcos hay un brasero de piedra ornamentado y agrietado.

*Nota: Esta zona no tiene luz exterior, ni siquiera de día. El texto asume que los personajes tienen visión en la oscuridad o una fuente de luz.*

## DM Notes

### The Grick
- This chamber contains a **Grick** — the special pet of the goblin **Lhupo** ([[09-Goblin-Shrine|area 9]])
- The grick likes to **climb up to a ledge** hidden in the shadows of the statuary in the upper reaches of the room
- It **quietly observes** intruders before **dropping down to strike**
- Compare the grick's **Dexterity (Stealth)** check vs the characters' **Wisdom (Perception)** checks (or passive scores) to determine surprise
- The grick knows that **goblins are not to be eaten** unless Lhupo says so
- The rest of the Cragmaws are **terrified** of Lhupo's pet and hurry through this room in twos or threes

### Chapel Decor
- Any cleric who examines the decor can attempt a **DC 10 Intelligence (Religion)** check
- **Success:** Identifies the deities once revered here — **Oghma** (knowledge), **Mystra** (magic), **Lathander** (dawn), and **Tymora** (luck)
- This is an obvious sign that the builders of the castle were **human**

### Developments
- If combat erupts here, the goblins in [[09-Goblin-Shrine|area 9]] **cannot be surprised**

## Treasure

### Hidden in the Stone Brazier

| Item | Value | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Gold statuette of a sun elf (wrapped in crimson cloth) | **100 gp** | Buried under a mound of coal. A goblin hid it here hoping others wouldn't steal it |

**Magic:** *[[Detect Magic|Detect magic]]* reveals **divination magic**. Any non-evil creature grasping the statue can ask it a question and receive a telepathic response, as though it had cast *[[Augury|augury]]*. Once used, that creature can never activate it again.
