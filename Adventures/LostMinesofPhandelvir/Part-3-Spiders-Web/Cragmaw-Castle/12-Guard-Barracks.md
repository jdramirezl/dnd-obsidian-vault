# 12. Guard Barracks

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 39

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Un brasero de piedra lleno de brasas arde en el centro de este pequeño barracón. Cuatro jergones de paja están alineados a lo largo de la pared este. La pared sur se ha derrumbado, pero una puerta de madera con barra en esa dirección sigue despejada. Una cortina cuelga de un arco al norte.

## DM Notes

- Two [[Hobgoblins#Hobgoblin\|hobgoblins]] stand guard here — they are **smart, tough, and loyal** to King Grol
- They keep watch from the arrow slit, so characters creeping around the **east side** of the castle are likely to be **spotted and attacked**
- This area was once a parlor for the castle's human occupants; its rotted furnishings were broken up by the Cragmaws and used for firewood

### Tactics
- At the start of combat, **one hobgoblin runs to warn King Grol** in [[14-Kings-Quarters|area 14]]
- That hobgoblin **returns 2 rounds later** to rejoin the fray
