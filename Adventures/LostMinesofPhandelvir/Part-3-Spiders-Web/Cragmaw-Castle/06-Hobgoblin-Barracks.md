# 6. Hobgoblin Barracks

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 37

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Cuatro simples jergones de paja y sacos de dormir están alineados en el suelo de este barracón. Soportes en las paredes sostienen varias armas: lanzas, espadas, luceros del alba y más. La pared norte muestra signos de daño, pero el suelo está barrido y limpio de escombros.

## DM Notes

- The Cragmaws are a mixed tribe of goblinoids — a handful of [[Bugbears#Bugbear\|bugbears]] lord over larger numbers of miserable [[Goblins#Goblin\|goblins]] and a few [[Hobgoblins#Hobgoblin\|hobgoblins]]
- The hobgoblins plan to dispose of the bugbears and take over someday, but for now the bugbears are too strong
- Because their goblin neighbors are always getting into fights, the hobgoblins **don't pay attention to noise** in [[02-Trapped-Hall|areas 2]] or [[03-Archer-Post|3]]
- However, they are **quick to defend their tower** if intruders appear, or to respond to an alarm raised by the goblin sentries
