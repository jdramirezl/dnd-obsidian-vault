# Cragmaw Castle — Overview

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Cragmaw Castle | **Page**: 35

## Background

The Cragmaw tribe consists of marauding bands and rival goblinholds scattered throughout the Triboar Trail area and Neverwinter Wood. One chieftain is grudgingly recognized by all others as supreme: **King Grol** of Cragmaw Castle.

Cragmaw Castle is not a [[Goblins#Goblin\|goblin]] construction. Raised by a talented wizard-noble of old **Phalorm** — an ancient realm that once controlled much of the North — the stronghold consists of seven overlapping towers. Its upper levels have long since collapsed to heaps of crumbling masonry; only the ground floor is still sound enough to be habitable.

**Quest:** **Sildar Hallwinter** asks the party to find Cragmaw Castle, rescue **Gundren Rockseeker**, and retrieve his **map to Wave Echo Cave**.

## Entrances

1. **Main Gate (Area 1)** — Bronze-covered wood doors, corroded and collapsed. Watched by goblin sentries in [[03-Archer-Post|Area 3]]
2. **Postern Gate (Area 10)** — Locked iron door on the south side. DC 15 Dexterity (thieves' tools) or DC 25 Strength
3. **Concealed Door (Area 11)** — Canvas-covered entrance on the north side. DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) to spot, DC 10 if actively searching

> [!tip] Infiltration Option — Disguise as Redbrand Emissaries
> The party can bluff their way in wearing Redbrand cloaks and claiming to work for the Black Spider. Both factions serve the same master, so goblinoids won't attack if the deception holds. Group Charisma (Deception) vs. monsters' Wisdom (Insight) — advantage if well-roleplayed. Award XP for monsters bypassed.

## Disguised Characters

Rather than storm the castle, clever characters might try to talk their way inside — for example, donning scarlet Redbrand cloaks and claiming to be emissaries from Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek.

- Both the Cragmaw tribe and the Redbrands work for the **Black Spider**, so goblinoids won't attack if the party claims to be working in the Black Spider's interest
- **Group deception:** Each character makes a **Charisma (Deception)** check contested by the monsters' **Wisdom (Insight)** checks. **Advantage** if the deception is well-planned or roleplayed
- If at least one character wins the contest, the deception succeeds
- Award XP for monsters fooled by deception
- Additional checks may be required deeper in the castle

> [!tip] Alert Chains — Noise Cascades Through the Castle
> Cragmaw Castle is tightly connected. The tripwire trap in area 2 alerts four rooms at once. Sentries in area 3 alert areas 4 and 6 for a pincer attack. Guard barracks (area 12) sends a runner to warn King Grol. Track which rooms are alerted — it changes the entire dungeon.

## Alert Chains

```
Area 3 (sentries) → alerts Areas 4 & 6
Area 2 (trap noise) → alerts Areas 3, 7, 8, 9
Area 4 (noise) → alerts Area 7
Area 8 (combat) → Area 9 cannot be surprised
Area 12 (combat) → 1 hobgoblin runs to warn King Grol (area 14), returns in 2 rounds
```
