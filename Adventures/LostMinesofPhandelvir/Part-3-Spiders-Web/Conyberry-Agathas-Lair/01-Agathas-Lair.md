> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Page**: 28

### Acercándose a la Guarida

> El bosque se oscurece y se queda inmóvil a medida que el sendero se adentra entre los árboles. Enredaderas gruesas y capas densas de musgo cubren las ramas, y el aire se siente notablemente más frío que en la aldea en ruinas. Al doblar un recodo del camino, ven una pantalla formada por las ramas retorcidas de árboles que crecen muy juntos, entrelazadas en un refugio con forma de cúpula entre las sombras. Una entrada baja conduce al interior.

### Interior del Refugio

> Una especie de hogar se resguarda dentro de la cúpula de ramas entretejidas. Está escasamente amueblado con cofres, estantes, una mesa y un diván reclinado, todo antiguo y de artesanía élfica.

### Agatha se Manifiesta

> El aire se vuelve gélido, y una poderosa sensación de pavor los invade. Una luz fría y pálida parpadea en el aire, tomando rápidamente la forma de una elfa, su cabello y sus ropas ondeando en un viento espectral. Pudo haber sido hermosa alguna vez, pero una expresión de odio ahora tuerce sus rasgos. "Mortales insensatos," gruñe. "¿Qué quieren aquí? ¿Acaso no saben que buscarme es la muerte?"

## DM Notes

### Background
The town of Conyberry was sacked by barbarians years ago and now lies in ruins. The Triboar Trail runs through the abandoned town. An old trail leads northwest from Conyberry into Neverwinter Wood to Agatha's lair, a few miles outside town.

**No map is provided** for this location. It contains only one point of interest.

### Dealing with Agatha

Agatha senses the characters' intrusion and manifests shortly after they enter her home.

- **If rude, disrespectful, or threatening**: Agatha scowls and disappears permanently. She does not attack, nor does she return.
- **If respectful and polite**: DC 15 Charisma (Persuasion) check
  - **Advantage** if the player roleplays the encounter well
  - **Automatic success** if they present **Sister Garaele**'s silver comb as a gift
- **On success**: Agatha agrees to answer **one question only**

### Agatha's Answer (Bowgentle's Spellbook)

> *"Muy bien," dice la figura fantasmal con fría diversión. "Sé que buscan muchas cosas. Háganme una pregunta, y les daré una respuesta."*

She traded the book to a necromancer named **Tsernoth** from the city of **Iriaebor** more than a hundred years ago. She does not know what became of it afterward. This answer is truthful and is all Sister Garaele needs.

> [!tip] One Question Only — Choose Wisely
> Agatha answers exactly **one question**, then vanishes forever. The silver comb from Sister Garaele guarantees success, but the party might ask about Cragmaw Castle, Wave Echo Cave, or the Black Spider's identity instead of Bowgentle's spellbook. Let them debate — this is a great roleplaying moment.

### Alternative Questions

The characters might ask about something else instead. Agatha is well-informed and a capable diviner — she can answer almost any single question about the adventure:

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Bowgentle's spellbook | Traded to Tsernoth of Iriaebor, 100+ years ago |
| Location of Cragmaw Castle | She knows and will reveal it |
| Location of Wave Echo Cave | She knows and will reveal it |
| Identity of the Black Spider | She knows and will reveal it |
| Who built Old Owl Well? | Arthindol (Hamun Kost's question) |

> ⚠️ **She answers only ONE question.** The characters should choose carefully.
