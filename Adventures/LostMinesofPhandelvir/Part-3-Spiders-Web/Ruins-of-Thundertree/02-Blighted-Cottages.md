# 2. Blighted Cottages

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 30

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Estas cabañas en ruinas, una al lado de la otra, parecen haber sido los hogares de prósperos comerciantes o granjeros acomodados en su época. Todo lo que queda son muros derrumbados y montones de escombros. Varios árboles jóvenes han crecido en medio de las ruinas.

## DM Notes

- **6 [[Blights#Twig Blight\|twig blights]]** lurk among the ordinary foliage, concealed by the overgrowth.
- Spotting them requires a **Wisdom (Perception)** check contested by the blights' **Dexterity (Stealth)**.
- These blights are hungry and **fight until destroyed**.
- **One round after combat starts**, the 2 twig blights from [[01-Westernmost-Cottage|area 1]] join the fray (total: 8 blights).

### Hidden Treasure
A merchant who once lived here hid a chest under the flagstone floor. A thorough search of the **eastern cottage** interior + **DC 10 Wisdom (Perception)** reveals the old chest among the roots of the tree growing through the house.
