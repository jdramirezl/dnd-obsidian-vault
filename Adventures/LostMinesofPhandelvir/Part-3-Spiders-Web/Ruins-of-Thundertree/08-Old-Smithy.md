# 8. Old Smithy

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 33

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una amplia chimenea y montones podridos de leña amontonados fuera de las paredes de este edificio hundido sugieren que fue una herrería en su día.

## DM Notes

- **2 [[Zombies#Zombie\|ash zombies]]** are slumped on the floor inside.
- When characters enter, the zombies **spend half their speed to stand up** (prone), then attack.
- Once they've caught sight of the characters, they **pursue no matter where the party goes**.
- ⚠️ **Ash Puff**: DC 10 CON save within 5 ft on first damage — disadvantage for 1 minute on failure. See [[00-Overview#Ash Zombies — Special Rule]].

### Interior
A variety of old tools — tongs, bellows, hammers, and a pair of iron anvils — are scattered around the interior. None are valuable.
