# 6. Ruined Store

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 32

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> En una intersección cerca del centro del pueblo, un estrecho camino sube serpenteando por la empinada ladera hacia el norte. Directamente al sur hay un edificio en ruinas que podría haber sido una tienda o taller. Telarañas se extienden a través del camino, desde el edificio hasta los árboles en el lado norte del camino.

## DM Notes

- **2 [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Giant Spider\|giant spiders]]** hide on the inner walls — **not visible from outside**.
- Trailing lines from the webs in the lane let the spiders sense when prey moves through the webs, at which point they scuttle over the wall and attack.
- The alert spiders **surprise** any character whose **passive Wisdom (Perception) is less than 17**.

### Webs
- Fill two squares north of the doorway (and the square marked "6")
- **Difficult terrain** — moving through requires **DC 10 Strength (Athletics)**
- On failure: creature is **[[Restrained|restrained]]**
- Restrained creature can attempt to break free each round: **DC 12 Strength check**, or cut free with a light slashing weapon
- Webs: **AC 10, 5 HP**, vulnerability to fire, immunity to bludgeoning/poison/psychic
- Going around the webs is difficult (thickets to the north); going around the building to the south leads to [[05-Blighted-Farmhouse|area 5]]'s twig blights

### Dead Adventurer
A male elf adventurer is cocooned in spider silk in the western half of the building — shriveled and sucked dry.
