# 13. Dragon Cultists

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 34–35

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta pequeña granja parece ser simplemente otro hogar vacío a primera vista. Sin embargo, todas las puertas están cerradas y las ventanas tienen los postigos echados.

> [!danger] Favric's Betrayal — He Will Offer the Party to the Dragon
> If the party accepts Favric's "alliance," he leads them to Venomfang and offers them as tribute. In the resulting fight, all 6 cultists side with the dragon. The *potion of flying* around Favric's neck and the 3 diamonds (300 gp) are worth taking before any alliance.

## DM Notes
A group calling itself the **Cult of the Dragon** seeks to forge alliances with powerful dragons of the Sword Coast. Four cultists recently tracked a green dragon to Thundertree and are waiting for the right moment to approach and broker an alliance.

### Defenses
- Doors are **barred from inside**: **DC 20 Strength** to force open
- Shutters are **barred from inside**: **DC 15 Strength** to force open
- **6 [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Cultist\|human cultists]]** inside — 4 stand guard (2 per room), 2 rest in the larger chamber

### Appearance
- Black cloaks cut to resemble **dragon wings**
- Black leather masks with stylized **dragon horns**
- Speak **Common** and **Draconic**

### Roleplaying the Cultists

**Leader: Favric** — evil, ambitious young man hoping to rise through the ranks by earning the green dragon's allegiance. His fellow cultists lack his ambition and **flee if Favric is captured or killed**.

The cultists are **not interested in fighting** and prefer to be left alone.

| Approach | Favric's Response |
|----------|-------------------|
| Party asks about the dragon | Explains they've come to treat with the green dragon (doesn't know its name) |
| Party expresses similar desire | Suggests an **alliance** — but really plans to **offer the characters to the dragon as tribute** |
| Combat breaks out | Cultists **side with the dragon** if Favric is alive |
| Favric captured/killed | Remaining cultists **flee** |

### ⚠️ Favric's Betrayal
If the party accepts Favric's "alliance," he leads them to [[07-Dragons-Tower|area 7]] and offers them to Venomfang as part of his tribute. In any resulting fight, the cultists fight alongside the dragon.

### Interior
Dusty, strung with cobwebs. Only furnishings: a small stove, a table, two chairs, and a bunk (shared by the cultists).

> [!tip] Potion of Flying — Game-Changer for the Venomfang Fight
> The *potion of flying* around Favric's neck is extremely valuable at this level. A flying character can match Venomfang's aerial advantage. If the party defeats or pickpockets the cultists first, this potion dramatically improves their odds against the dragon.
