# 1. Westernmost Cottage

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 30

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Acurrucada a la sombra de un viejo árbol hay una cabaña de piedra derrumbada sin techo. Las malas hierbas crecen descontroladas aquí.

## DM Notes

- **2 [[Blights#Twig Blight\|twig blights]]** hide among the weeds flanking the cottage's open doorway.
- Make a **Dexterity (Stealth)** check for the blights vs. the party's **passive Wisdom (Perception)** to determine if they're spotted.
- The blights **do not attack on their own** (except in self-defense) but quickly come to the aid of the twig blights in [[02-Blighted-Cottages|area 2]] if combat erupts there.

## Monsters

| Creature | Qty | HP | AC | Attack | Damage |
|----------|-----|----|----|--------|--------|
| [[Blights#Twig Blight\|Twig Blight]] | 2 | 4 | 13 | +3 to hit | Claws: 3 (1d4+1) |

- **False Appearance**: While motionless, indistinguishable from dead shrub

## Treasure

None.

## Connections

- **East**: [[02-Blighted-Cottages]] — blights reinforce area 2 after 1 round of combat
- **South**: Trail continues into the village
