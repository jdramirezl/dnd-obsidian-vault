# Ruins of Thundertree — Overview

> **Part**: 3 — The Spider's Web | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 30

## Overview

The abandoned village of Thundertree stands near where the Neverwinter River emerges from Neverwinter Wood. Once a prosperous community of woodcutters and trappers, it was devastated **30 years ago** by the eruption of **Mount Hotenow**. A plague of strange zombies followed, killing or driving off survivors. Strange magic has since mutated the local vegetation into dangerous forms.

Two notable visitors remain:
- **Reidoth the druid** (area 4) — keeps a wary eye on the dangers
- **Dragon cultists** (area 13) — seeking alliance with the green dragon **Venomfang** (area 7)

### Quest Hook

**Qelline Alderleaf** in Phandalin suggested the party consult the druid Reidoth about the location of Cragmaw Castle and/or Wave Echo Cave.

>
> Un letrero de madera está clavado en un poste cercano. Dice: **"¡PELIGRO! ¡Monstruos vegetales Y zombis! ¡Den la vuelta ahora!"**

> [!warning] Venomfang — Young Green Dragon in Area 7
> A CR 8 dragon against a level 3 party. Poison Breath (12d6, DC 16) can instantly kill characters. The party is **not expected to fight it head-on** — driving it off at half HP (68 damage) is the intended outcome. Reidoth (area 4) will guide the party to Wave Echo Cave only if they deal with the dragon first.

## DM Notes

Reidoth placed the warning sign to discourage treasure seekers from stirring up the monsters.

## Key NPC: Reidoth the Druid (Area 4)

- Gaunt, white-bearded human; terse speaker
- Member of the **Emerald Enclave**
- **Knows**: Dangerous spiders at base of hill, cultists ("folk in black masks and cloaks"), green dragon in tower
- **Warns** characters to leave Thundertree
- **Cragmaw Castle**: Gladly provides directions if asked
- **Wave Echo Cave**: Will guide party there ONLY if they chase off the dragon first
- If attacked: transforms into **gray squirrel** and flees through a crack in the wall
- **Emerald Enclave recruitment**: If party drives off Venomfang, Reidoth offers membership (title: **Springwarden**)

> [!danger] Ash Zombies — Special Debuff Rule
> Unlike normal zombies, ash zombies release a poison cloud when first damaged. Any creature within 5 ft must pass DC 10 CON or gain **disadvantage on attacks, saves, and ability checks for 1 minute**. This stacks badly if the party clusters up — warn them to spread out.

## Ash Zombies — Special Rule

These zombies use the [[Zombies#Zombie\|zombie]] stat block with an additional trait:

> **Ash Puff.** The first time the zombie takes damage, any living creature within 5 feet must succeed on a **DC 10 Constitution saving throw** or gain **disadvantage on attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks for 1 minute**. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect early on a success.
