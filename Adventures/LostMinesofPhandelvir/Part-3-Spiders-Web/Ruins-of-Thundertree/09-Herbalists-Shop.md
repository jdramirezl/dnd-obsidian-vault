# 9. Herbalist's Shop

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 33–34

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta tienda en ruinas está abarrotada de estantes de almacenamiento hundidos y muebles rotos. Fragmentos de vidrio y trozos de cerámica brillan entre las malas hierbas y los escombros junto a libros podridos y barriles.

## DM Notes

- This was an herb and alchemy shop belonging to the family of **Mirna Dendrar**, now a resident in Phandalin (encountered in the Redbrand Hideout).
- All reagents, concoctions, and books are ruined beyond use.
- A **small wooden case** is hidden in a compartment beneath the storage shelves.

### Finding the Heirloom
- **DC 15 Wisdom (Perception)** to find the case while searching through the wreckage
- The check **succeeds automatically** if Mirna sent the party to find the heirloom
- The case itself is worthless — the treasure is inside

### Quest Reward
If the party **returns the necklace to Mirna** instead of keeping it: **200 XP** (no XP if they keep it).
