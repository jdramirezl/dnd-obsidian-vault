# 12. Weaver's Cottage

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 34

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Montones de escombros llenan el interior de esta ruina. En una esquina se alza un telar roto.

## DM Notes

- **6 [[Blights#Twig Blight\|twig blights]]** lurk in the thicket **south** of this ruin.
- Allow each character to attempt a **Wisdom (Perception)** check contested by the blights' **Dexterity (Stealth)** to avoid being surprised.

### ⚠️ Alert the Cultists
Any **loud noises** here alert the cultists in [[13-Dragon-Cultists|area 13]], who **quietly and cautiously investigate**. This can lead to a two-front encounter if the party is still fighting blights when the cultists arrive.
