# 4. Druid's Watch

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 31–32

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta pequeña casa parece estar en mejores condiciones que las estructuras arruinadas y destartaladas cercanas. Las puertas están reforzadas con pesadas bandas de hierro, y gruesos postigos protegen las ventanas.

> [!info] Quest Hub — Reidoth Connects Multiple Storylines
> Reidoth freely gives Cragmaw Castle directions, but will only guide the party to **Wave Echo Cave** if they drive off Venomfang first. He also offers Emerald Enclave membership (title: Springwarden) to characters who help. If attacked, he turns into a gray squirrel and escapes.

## DM Notes

- **Appearance**: Gaunt, white-bearded human
- **Personality**: Doesn't use two words when one will do. Reasonably hospitable despite few visitors.
- **Faction**: **Emerald Enclave** — wilderness survivalists who preserve the natural order

### What Reidoth Knows
- Dangerous **spiders** lurk in the ruins at the base of the hill (areas 5–6)
- "Folk in **black masks and cloaks**" skulking around the eastern side of town (area 13 — cultists)
- A **[[Dragon-Green#Young Green Dragon\|green dragon]]** has moved into the tower (area 7) since his last visit
- He warns the characters of all these threats and suggests they **leave before they get killed**

### Quest Resolutions

| Topic | Reidoth's Response |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Cragmaw Castle** | Gladly provides directions — no conditions |
| **Wave Echo Cave** | Will NOT reveal location, but offers to **guide the party there** in exchange for chasing off the dragon in area 7 |
| **If attacked** | Transforms into a **gray squirrel**, scurries through a crack in the wall, vanishes into the woods, waits for hostiles to leave |

### Joining the Emerald Enclave
If the party drives off Venomfang, Reidoth privately approaches characters who exemplify protecting the natural order and urges them to join. Accepting members receive the title **Springwarden**.
