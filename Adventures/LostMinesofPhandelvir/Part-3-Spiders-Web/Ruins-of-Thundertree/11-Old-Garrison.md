# 11. Old Garrison

> **Part**: 3 | **Location**: Ruins of Thundertree | **Page**: 34

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El cuartel parece haber resistido los años mejor que la mayoría de los edificios del pueblo. Su techo presenta un simple almenado, y las ventanas con aspilleras confirman que fue construido para servir como un pequeño fuerte en tiempos de emergencia.

## DM Notes

- **5 [[Zombies#Zombie\|ash zombies]]** lurk within — former members of the garrison.
- They still wear remnants of **rusted mail and soldiers' surcoats** (these scraps do NOT improve their AC).
- The zombies animate and attack if any living creature disturbs their rest.
- ⚠️ **Ash Puff**: DC 10 CON save within 5 ft on first damage — disadvantage for 1 minute on failure. See [[00-Overview#Ash Zombies — Special Rule]].

### Interior Layout
- Main room has a **ladder** leading through a **trapdoor to the roof** (battlement)
- North chamber: 2 double bunks
- South chamber: 3 double bunks (quarters for 10 soldiers total)
- Northwest: Kitchen and pantry — piles of well-rotted sacks and barrels (all foodstuffs devoured by vermin long ago)

### Tactical Note
5 ash zombies in an enclosed space means multiple Ash Puff triggers. If the party fights in the doorway, they can limit exposure — but the zombies will pursue relentlessly.
