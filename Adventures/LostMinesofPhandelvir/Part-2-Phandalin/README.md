# Part 2: Phandalin

> **Adventure**: Lost Mines of Phandelver | **Pages**: 14–19 | **Levels**: 2–3

The frontier town of Phandalin is the adventure's central hub. Characters arrive, explore the town, meet NPCs, gather quest hooks, and eventually confront the Redbrand threat.

## Town Overview

Phandalin is a small frontier settlement of 40–50 log buildings built on the ruins of a much older town. Hardy settlers from Neverwinter and Waterdeep have reclaimed it over the last 3–4 years. The town has no local lord — and a gang called the **Redbrands** has controlled it for the past two months under a mysterious leader known as **Glasstaff**.

See [[01-Town-Overview]] for the full arrival description and DM guidance.

## Flow of Play

1. Characters arrive in Phandalin late in the day
2. Visit [[Stonehill-Inn]] first — the rumor hub that points everywhere else
3. Over 1–2 days, visit locations and gather quest hooks
4. Redbrand confrontation becomes inevitable (at [[Sleeping-Giant]] or in the street)
5. Characters investigate [[Tresendar-Manor]] → Redbrand Hideout (Part 2b)
