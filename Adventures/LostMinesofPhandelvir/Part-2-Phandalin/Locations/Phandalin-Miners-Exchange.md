# Phandalin Miner's Exchange

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 17–18

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Este puesto comercial funciona como oficina de registros y lugar de intercambio donde los mineros locales pesan, miden y cobran por sus hallazgos valiosos. El lugar bulle con la actividad de prospectores curtidos por el sol. Detrás del mostrador principal, una mujer de mirada calculadora los observa con interés apenas disimulado.

## DM Notes

- Miners have finds weighed, measured, and paid out here. Also serves as **unofficial records office** for claims.
- No real gold rush, but enough wealth in nearby streams to support independent prospectors.
- Great place to meet people who spend time in the countryside.
- **Halia Thornton** is the guildmaster — ambitious, calculating, secretly a **Zhentarim** agent working to bring Phandalin under her control.
- She doesn't know the location of Cragmaw Castle, but knows the Redbrands have a **goblin minion** who might.
- She leverages this info to persuade characters to help deal with the Redbrands.

## Quest Hooks

### Halia's Job Offer
- Approaches characters she believes she can control
- Offers **100 gp** to eliminate **Glasstaff** and bring her any **correspondence** from his quarters
- **DC 15 Wisdom (Insight):** She has ulterior motives — wants to take over the Redbrand operation herself

### Faction: Zhentarim
- After the Redbrand leader is dealt with, Halia approaches characters who share pursuits of wealth and power
- Even if the party wipes out the gang, she may still extend the offer to gain friends (and spies)
- Offers membership with the title **Fang**
