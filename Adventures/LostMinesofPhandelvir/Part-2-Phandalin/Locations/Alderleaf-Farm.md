# Alderleaf Farm

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 18

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una granja modesta pero bien cuidada se extiende al borde del pueblo. Una halfling de mediana edad con manos curtidas por el trabajo y ojos sabios los recibe con una sonrisa cálida. Un niño halfling de unos diez años asoma la cabeza por detrás de ella, con los ojos brillantes de emoción al ver aventureros.

## DM Notes

- **Qelline Alderleaf** is a wise, pragmatic farmer who seems to know everything going on in town.
- Kind host — willing to let characters **sleep in her hayloft** as alternative to [[Stonehill-Inn]].
- If she figures out the characters are looking for Cragmaw Castle or Wave Echo Cave, she suggests visiting **Reidoth the Druid**.

### Carp's Story
- Son **Carp** is a spirited, precocious halfling lad of ten.
- Was playing near [[Tresendar-Manor]] when he found a **secret tunnel** in a thicket.
- "Big ugly bandits" came out and met with Redbrands — they didn't see him.
- Can take characters to the tunnel or provide directions.
- Tunnel leads to **area 8** in the Redbrand Hideout.

## Quest Hooks

### Reidoth the Druid

> [!info] Quest Hook
> Qelline's friend Reidoth the Druid is in Thundertree (50 mi NW). He can help find both Cragmaw Castle and Wave Echo Cave — the most versatile lead in Part 2.

- Qelline's longtime friend — "there's not an inch of the land he doesn't know"
- Recently set out for the ruins of **Thundertree** (50 miles NW of Phandalin)
- Qelline provides directions
- **Destination:** Ruins of Thundertree (Part 3, p30)
- Reidoth can help find **Cragmaw Castle** and **Wave Echo Cave**

### Secret Entrance to Redbrand Hideout
- Carp's tunnel provides a **back entrance** (area 8 — the Crevasse)
- Bypasses the main entrance through [[Tresendar-Manor]]
