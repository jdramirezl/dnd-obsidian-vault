## DM Notes

- Rundown, dirty, dangerous watering hole at the end of Phandalin's main street.
- Operated by **Grista** (surly female dwarf). Frequented by Redbrand thugs.
- **Visiting triggers the Redbrand Ruffians encounter.**

### Redbrand Ruffians Encounter

> [!warning] Hard Fight
> 4 Redbrand ruffians (thugs, 16 hp each) is a tough encounter for level 2 characters, especially if the party split up in town. If 3 are defeated, the last flees to Tresendar Manor.

- **Enemies:** 4 [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Thug|Redbrand ruffians]] | **XP:** 400 (divided equally)
- Continue insults/baiting as long as you like — Redbrands attack in 1–2 rounds if characters don't.
- Neither side is surprised. If 3 are defeated, the last **flees toward [[Tresendar-Manor]]**.

### What the Redbrands Know (captured/[[Charmed|charmed]])
- Leader is **Glasstaff** — a human wizard with a glass staff
- **Black Spider** hired them to frighten adventurers and intimidate locals
- Black Spider sent **[[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]]** as reinforcements
- Lower hideout guarded by a hideous **"eye monster"** ([[Nothic|nothic]])
- **Captives** held "near the old crypts," guarded by [[Skeletons#Skeleton|skeletons]]

### Developments
- Most townsfolk are grateful if ruffians are killed
- Harbin Wester fears retaliation — warns but doesn't punish
- Harbin won't keep prisoners until whole gang is defeated (can be persuaded for a few days)
