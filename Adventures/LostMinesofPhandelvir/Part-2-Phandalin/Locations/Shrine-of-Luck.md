# Shrine of Luck

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 18

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El único templo de Phandalin es un pequeño santuario construido con piedras tomadas de las ruinas cercanas. Está dedicado a Tymora, diosa de la suerte y la buena fortuna. Una joven elfa de aspecto erudito atiende el lugar con una mezcla de devoción y preocupación evidente en su rostro.

## DM Notes

- Small shrine made of stones from nearby ruins, dedicated to **Tymora** (goddess of luck).
- **Sister Garaele** is a scholarly elf acolyte and **Harper** agent — reports to superiors on events around Phandalin.
- She despairs of ever ridding Phandalin of the Redbrands.
- Her superiors asked her to persuade a banshee named **Agatha** to answer a question about a spellbook — Agatha did not appear for her.
- She returned wounded and exhausted (the rumor Narth shares at [[Stonehill-Inn]]).

## Quest Hooks

### The Banshee's Bargain

> [!info] Quest Hook
> Sister Garaele needs someone to bring Agatha a jeweled silver comb and ask about Bowgentle's spellbook → leads to Conyberry (Part 3). Reward: 3 potions of healing.

- Garaele needs an intermediary to bring Agatha a **jeweled silver comb** (she provides it)
- Must **flatter Agatha's vanity** and persuade her to reveal the location of **Bowgentle's spellbook**
- **Reward:** 3 potions of healing
- **Destination:** Conyberry / Agatha's Lair (Part 3, p28)

### Faction: Harpers
- If the party helps learn the fate of Bowgentle's spellbook, Garaele approaches characters who exemplify positive change through information and secrecy
- Offers membership with the title **Watcher**
