# Townmaster's Hall

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 18–19

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El salón del alcalde tiene muros sólidos de piedra, un techo de madera a dos aguas y una torre con campana en la parte trasera. Junto a la puerta principal hay un tablón de anuncios con un aviso escrito en Común:
>
> *"RECOMPENSA — ¡Orcos cerca de Wyvern Tor! Aquellos dispuestos a enfrentar la amenaza orca deben preguntar adentro."*
>
> El aviso lleva el sello del pueblo y una firma indescifrable.

## DM Notes

- Townsfolk elect a townmaster each year — serves as judge in minor disputes and keeps records.
- Current townmaster: **Harbin Wester** — fat, pompous old fool, completely intimidated by Redbrands.
- Claims they're "just a mercenary guild, and not all that much trouble, really."
- Small but serviceable **jail** in the cellar (two cells). Harbin carries the keys.
- **Sildar Hallwinter** establishes himself here after resting at [[Stonehill-Inn]].

## Quest Hooks

### Orc Trouble (from Harbin)
- Orcs near **Wyvern Tor** on the Triboar Trail | **Reward:** 100 gp
- **Destination:** Wyvern Tor (Part 3, p35)

### Finding Cragmaw Castle (from Sildar)
- **500 gp** to locate Cragmaw Castle and defeat/drive off the chieftain
- Suggests searching Triboar Trail for raiding parties

### Finding Iarno (from Sildar)

> [!info] Quest Hook
> Sildar's missing contact Iarno Albrek IS Glasstaff, the Redbrand leader. 200 gp reward for eliminating the Redbrand threat. Capturing Iarno alive doubles XP and Sildar wants him sent to Neverwinter.

- Iarno Albrek disappeared near [[Tresendar-Manor]] ~2 months ago
- "A short, dark-bearded human wizard in his thirties"
- **Reward:** 200 gp for eliminating the Redbrand threat
- **Twist:** Iarno IS Glasstaff. Once Sildar learns this, he wants Iarno captured and sent to Neverwinter.

### Faction: Lords' Alliance
- After eliminating goblins from Cragmaw Castle OR uncovering Iarno's treachery, Sildar approaches characters who value security of civilization through action
- Offers membership with the title **Cloak**
