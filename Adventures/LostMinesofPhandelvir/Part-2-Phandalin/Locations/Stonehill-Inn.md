# Stonehill Inn

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 15–16

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> En el centro del pueblo se alza una posada grande y recién construida de piedra de campo y maderas toscamente talladas. La sala común está llena de lugareños bebiendo jarras de cerveza o sidra, todos observándolos con curiosidad.

## DM Notes

This is the **primary information hub** of Phandalin. If players aren't sure where to go, start them here. The NPCs are "pointers" who direct characters toward adventure opportunities throughout town.

- **Rooms:** 6 rooms for rent (Sildar Hallwinter takes one). See rulebook for pricing.
- **Alternative lodging:** Camp outside town, or persuade Daran Edermath or Qelline Alderleaf for a hayloft.
- Any NPC at the inn can tell the characters that the Redbrands frequent the [[Sleeping-Giant]] — and that they're trouble.
