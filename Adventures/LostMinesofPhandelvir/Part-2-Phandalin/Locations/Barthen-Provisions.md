# Barthen's Provisions

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 16

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> El puesto comercial más grande de Phandalin es un edificio amplio y bien surtido. Sus estantes están repletos de bienes y suministros ordinarios — mochilas, petates, cuerdas y raciones. Un hombre delgado y calvo de unos cincuenta años los recibe con una sonrisa amable desde detrás del mostrador.

## DM Notes

- **Stock:** Most ordinary goods and supplies. No weapons or armor. Nothing over 25 gp. Directs to [[Lionshield-Coster]] for weapons/armor.
- **Clerks:** Ander and Thistle help load/unload wagons and serve customers.
- **Delivering Supplies:** If the party has the wagon from "Meet Me in Phandalin," Barthen pays **10 gp per character**.
- **Gundren's Capture:** Barthen is saddened, encourages rescue. Considers Gundren a friend.
- **DC 15 Intelligence (History):** Learn about the Phandelver's Pact and the lost mine (if not already heard from Sildar).
- **Rockseeker Brothers:** Nundro and Tharden are camped outside town — hasn't seen them in a tenday. (Tharden is dead; Nundro is a prisoner — Part 4.)
- **Barthen's News:** Redbrands shaking down businesses. If party seems inclined to act, directs them to [[Sleeping-Giant]].
