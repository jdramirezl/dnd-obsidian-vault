# Lionshield Coster

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 17

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Colgando sobre la puerta principal de este modesto puesto comercial hay un letrero con forma de escudo de madera con un león azul pintado en él. El interior huele a cuero y aceite para armas, con estantes de suministros y un mostrador atendido por una mujer de mirada aguda.

## DM Notes

- Owned by the **Lionshields**, a merchant company based in **Yartar** (100+ miles east).
- Ships finished goods to Phandalin and other settlements, but hard hit by banditry.
- Most recent caravan never arrived — attacked by **Cragmaw goblins** (Part 1).
- Back room has **armor and weapons** for sale (see rulebook for prices).
- Linene **won't sell weapons** to anyone she thinks is a threat, including Redbrands.
- Warns characters about Redbrands; advises avoiding the [[Sleeping-Giant]].

### Recovered Goods
If characters return stolen goods from **area 8 of Cragmaw Hideout** (or reveal location):
- **Reward:** 50 gp
- Linene promises to help the adventurers any way she can
