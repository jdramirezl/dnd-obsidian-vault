# Edermath Orchard

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Page**: 16–17

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una cabaña ordenada se encuentra junto a un huerto de manzanos, sus ramas cargadas de fruta. Un semielfo de cabello plateado y porte atlético, que aparenta más de cien años pero se mueve con la gracia de un guerrero experimentado, los saluda desde el porche con una mirada evaluadora.

## DM Notes

- **Daran Edermath** is a retired fighter who served as a marshal and herald in the Dragon Coast. Returned to the Neverwinter region (his original home) upon retiring.
- Member of the **Order of the Gauntlet** — though no longer active, he keeps an eye on happenings around Phandalin.
- Happy to trade news with fellow adventurers, especially those who share the order's virtues.
- **On the Redbrands:** Wants adventurers to teach them a lesson. Knows:
  - The leader is called **Glasstaff**
  - They hang around the [[Sleeping-Giant]]
  - Their main safe house is under [[Tresendar-Manor]]

## Quest Hooks

### Old Owl Trouble
- Prospectors NE of Phandalin report someone digging at the ruins of **Old Owl Well**
- Several prospectors chased away by **undead**
- The ruins are an old watchtower of **Netheril** — dangerous magic may be dormant
- **Destination:** Old Owl Well, couple days' march NE (Part 3, p29)

### Faction: Order of the Gauntlet
- If the party deals with Redbrands AND investigates Old Owl Well, Daran approaches characters who exemplify honor and vigilance
- Offers membership with the title **Chevall**
