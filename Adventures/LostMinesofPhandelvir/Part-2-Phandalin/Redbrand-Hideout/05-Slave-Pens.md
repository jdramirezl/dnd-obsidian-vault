# 5. Slave Pens

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 22

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta larga sala está dividida en tres áreas, con barras de hierro separando el norte y el sur. Paja sucia cubre el suelo de esas celdas, cuyas puertas con bisagras están aseguradas con cadenas y candados. Un par de mujeres humanas desaliñadas están retenidas en la celda del sur, mientras que un muchacho humano está confinado en la del norte. Todos visten túnicas grises sencillas y llevan collares de hierro alrededor del cuello.
>
> Un montón de ropa descartada está apilado descuidadamente contra la pared del fondo.

## DM Notes

For the past two months, the Redbrands have been capturing travelers and holding them here until they can be sold into slavery.

- Two [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Thug|ruffians]] stand guard, spending most of their time taunting the prisoners
- If they hear fighting in area 5 (from the crypts direction), they take positions against the wall near the door to **surprise** intruders
- The captives are too intimidated to shout warnings
- The heap of clothing belongs to at least a dozen captives held here over the last two months

**Cell Doors:**
- DC 10 Dexterity (thieves' tools) to pick
- DC 22 Strength to wrench open by brute force

### Captives — The Dendrar Family

- ****Mirna Dendrar**** — human woman (commoner)
- ****Nars Dendrar**** — her son, age 13 (commoner)
- ****Nilsa Dendrar**** — her daughter, age 18 (commoner)

Their husband/father **Thel Dendrar** was murdered by the Redbrands a few days ago (his body is in area 8). The gang abducted the family that night.

**What they know:**
- The boss is a wizard (they haven't met him, don't know his name)
- He has "tall, furry monsters with big ears" ([[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]]) working for him

### Side Quest: Mirna's Heirloom

> [!info] Quest Hook
> Mirna reveals an Emerald Necklace hidden in her family's old herb shop in Thundertree → connects to Part 3. Rescuing the Dendrars also awards 100 bonus XP.

Mirna tells the characters that when she was young, her family fled from **Thundertree** after undead overran the place. Their herb and alchemy shop had a case containing an ****Emerald Necklace**** hidden beneath storage shelves in the southeast part of Thundertree. She never dared return. → See Part 3: Thundertree.
