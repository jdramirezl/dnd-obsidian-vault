# 11. Wizard's Workshop

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 25

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta sala parece ser el taller de un mago. Una rata cruza corriendo el suelo y se refugia bajo una gran mesa de trabajo equipada con alambiques, retortas, serpentines de destilación y otros dispositivos alquímicos, todo hirviendo y burbujeando.
>
> Las estanterías están abarrotadas de fajos de pergamino y tomos de aspecto extraño.

## DM Notes

**DC 15 Wisdom (Perception)** at either door to hear faint bubbling and dripping sounds.

### Iarno's Rat Familiar

> [!tip] DM Tip
> The rat is Iarno's alarm system. If it sees the party and isn't killed, Iarno flees from area 12 with his staff and scrolls. Kill the rat first or Glasstaff escapes.

- A **[[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Rat|rat]]** lurks here, watching for intruders
- Shares a **telepathic bond** with **Iarno** — sends a warning as soon as it detects intruders
- **AC 10, 1 HP**, no effective attacks. Speed 20 ft. If killed, it disappears
- If left unharmed, it follows the party as though curious or hungry. May feign affection for a character who feeds it — but remains **absolutely loyal** to Iarno

⚠️ **If the rat warns Iarno, he has time to prepare or flee from area 12.**

### Books and Notes

- Iarno is trying to master brewing *potions of invisibility* — hasn't succeeded yet. Any character proficient in **Arcana** can tell
- Among the books: a tome in **Dwarvish** — the journal of an adventurer named **Urmon**
  - Describes the history of the **Lost Mine of Phandelver** and the **Forge of Spells**
  - Records that a magic mace named ****Lightbringer**** was commissioned by priests of Lathander from the mages of the Phandelver's Pact
  - The mace was lost when Wave Echo Cave vanished from history (may be found in Part 4)

## Monsters

- **Iarno's Rat Familiar** ×1 (AC 10, 1 HP) — not a real combat threat, but a critical alarm system
