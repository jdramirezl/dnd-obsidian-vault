# 9. Guard Barracks

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 24

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Este cuartel contiene cuatro literas de madera toscamente construidas, con mantas amontonadas y platos sucios esparcidos por todas partes. Un fuerte olor a cuerpos sin lavar y carne podrida llena el aire.
>
> Tres humanoides altos y peludos están holgazaneando entre el desorden, ladrando órdenes a un triste y pequeño goblin que se humilla para su diversión. Tu repentina aparición hace que el goblin se desmaye.

## DM Notes

**DC 10 Wisdom (Perception)** at the door to hear gruff voices issuing demeaning commands in Goblin: "Lick the floor!" and "Roll like a dog!" — the bugbears are bullying their goblin slave.

### Bugbears

> [!warning] Hard Fight
> 3 bugbears (27 hp each, surprise attack for +2d6 damage) is the deadliest encounter in the hideout. Scarlet cloaks + DC 15 Deception can trick them into thinking you're Redbrands.

- Work for the **Black Spider**, sent to help Iarno keep the Redbrands and Phandalin in line
- Leader is ****Mosk**** — wears a jeweled eye patch even though he has both eyes (he thinks it's fancy)
- They **avoid** the human Redbrands
- If characters wear **scarlet cloaks**, [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]] assume they serve Iarno. Clever PCs can persuade them to help deal with "traitors" elsewhere — **DC 15 Charisma (Deception)** if the roleplaying isn't convincing

### Droop the Goblin

- ****Droop**** falls **[[Unconscious|unconscious]]** at the sight of the party. Another creature can use an action to wake him, otherwise he's out for **1d10 minutes**
- Cowed by the bugbears — follows orders from whoever is strongest
- If combat starts while conscious, he **hides** and avoids the fight. If ordered to fight, does so with **disadvantage**
- **Knows:** General layout of the hideout, location of secret doors and traps (but details may be confused or mixed up)
- If bugbears are dispatched, tries to ingratiate himself with the party
- **Knows** Cragmaw Castle is "up north, in the forest" but doesn't remember the route
- Suggests capturing a Cragmaw [[Goblins#Goblin|goblin]] patrol near Phandalin to learn more
- Can be kept as an **NPC party member** (see NPC Party Members sidebar, p. 11)

### Key Intelligence

The bugbears are the **only ones** in the hideout who know the location of **Wave Echo Cave** — but they won't share it willingly (they fear the Black Spider more than the PCs). They also know the location of **Cragmaw Castle**.

- **DC 15 Charisma (Intimidation)** to pry Cragmaw Castle's location from a captured bugbear

## Monsters

- [[Bugbears#Bugbear|Bugbear]] ×3 (HP: 27 each)
  - **Mosk** (leader): same stats, carries master key + jeweled eye patch
  - **Tactics:** Brute force. Surprise attack with Brute trait for extra damage on first hit
- [[Goblins#Goblin|Goblin]] ×1 — ****Droop**** (HP: 7, non-combatant)

**XP:** 600 (divided equally) — for defeating the bugbears

- **Mosk's belt pouch:** 33 sp
- **Mosk's eye patch:** Black leather set with semiprecious stones (50 gp)
- **Mosk's iron key:** Locks and unlocks **all doors** in the Redbrand hideout
