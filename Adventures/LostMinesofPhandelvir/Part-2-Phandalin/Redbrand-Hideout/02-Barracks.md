# 2. Barracks

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 21

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Este lugar parece ser un almacén convertido en alojamiento. Dos literas dobles se apoyan contra la pared cerca de la puerta, mientras que barriles y cajas llenan la mitad sur de la cámara.

## DM Notes

Most Redbrands have lodgings in town. This barracks is used to lie low after shaking down locals.

- Three [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Thug|Redbrand ruffians]] are resting here
- If they hear loud noise in area 1 (voices, barrels rolling), they prepare for a fight and try to **surprise** intruders
- The barrels contain similar provisions to area 1

## Monsters

- [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Thug|Redbrand Ruffian]] ×3 (HP: 16 each)
  - **Tactics:** If alerted by noise in area 1, they creep into that room to ambush. If two are defeated, the last may flee toward the secret door in area 1

**XP:** 300 (divided equally)

## Treasure

Each ruffian wears a belt pouch:
- Ruffian 1: 16 sp, 7 gp
- Ruffian 2: 12 sp, 5 gp
- Ruffian 3: 15 ep, 2 garnets (10 gp each)

Additionally: 3 dirty scarlet cloaks hang from the bunks (useful as disguises throughout the hideout).
