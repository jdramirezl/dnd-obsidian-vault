# 4. Tresendar Crypts

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 22

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Tres grandes sarcófagos de piedra se alzan dentro de esta polvorienta cripta, y apoyado contra cada sarcófago hay un esqueleto humano vestido con restos de cota de malla oxidada. Falsas columnas a lo largo de las paredes están talladas con la imagen de robles extendidos. Las puertas dobles en la esquina sureste están revestidas de cobre deslustrado.

## DM Notes

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Wearing a scarlet Redbrand cloak or speaking the password "Illefarn" prevents the skeletons from attacking. Players who looted cloaks from area 2 can walk right through.

The elders of the Tresendar family were laid to rest here.

- The three [[Skeletons#Skeleton|skeletons]] are **animated** and attack any creature that comes within **10 feet** of the door to area 5 or area 6
- **Bypass:** Wearing a scarlet Redbrand cloak OR speaking the password **"Illefarn"** (name of an ancient elven nation) prevents the skeletons from attacking
- Sarcophagus lids are carved to depict the entombed: two human males and one human female, all of noble bearing. Inside: mostly moldering bones and scraps of clothing, plus treasure

**Developments:** Fighting here alerts the Redbrands in area 5.

## Monsters

- [[Skeletons#Skeleton\|Skeleton]] ×3 (HP: 13 each)
  - **Trigger:** Activate when any creature without a scarlet cloak (or the password) comes within 10 ft of the doors to areas 5 or 6
  - **Tactics:** Attack the nearest intruder. They do not pursue beyond this room

**XP:** 150 (divided equally)

## Treasure

Each sarcophagus contains:
- **Platinum Signet Ring** (50 gp each) — 3 total = 150 gp value
