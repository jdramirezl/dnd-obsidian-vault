# 10. Common Room

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 25

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Varias mesas y sillas desgastadas están esparcidas por esta gran sala. Bancos de madera están arrimados contra paredes decoradas con cortinas marrones y rojas, y varios barriles de cerveza están apoyados y abiertos.
>
> Cuatro guerreros humanos de aspecto rudo, vestidos con capas escarlata, están reunidos alrededor de una de las mesas. Un montón de monedas y baratijas está apilado sobre la mesa entre ellos.

## DM Notes

This area serves as headquarters and meeting room for the Redbrands, doubling as an off-duty common room.

- **DC 10 Wisdom (Perception)** at the door to hear knucklebones game — mysterious rattling, shouts, groans, gabble of voices as wagers are paid
- If characters **burst in**, they **automatically surprise** the occupants
- The four [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Thug|ruffians]] are drinking heavily and are **[[Poisoned|poisoned]]** (disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks)
- The dice are loaded — the ruffian who owns them is naturally winning, and the game is close to turning violent

### Deception

- Redbrands **immediately recognize** scarlet cloaks as impostors
- Fast-talking characters can pass as **"new recruits"**, especially if they offer to join the game
- **DC 10 Charisma (Deception)** if the roleplaying isn't convincing enough
- Knocking over the table or mixing up the loot is a great way to **distract** them briefly

## Monsters

- [[Appendix-B-NPCs#Thug|Redbrand Ruffian]] ×4 (HP: 16 each)
  - **Condition:** All four are **[[Poisoned|poisoned]]** (disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks)
  - **Tactics:** Disorganized due to drunkenness. Easy to surprise. Fight sloppily

**XP:** 400 (divided equally)

## Treasure

All wealth is on the table (the gambling stakes):
- 75 cp, 55 sp, 22 ep, 15 gp
- Gold earring set with a tiny ruby (30 gp)
