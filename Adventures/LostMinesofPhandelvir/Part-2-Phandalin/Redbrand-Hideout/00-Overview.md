# Redbrand Hideout — Overview

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 20

## Background

The Redbrands' base is a dungeon complex under **Tresendar Manor**. Before the manor was ruined, its cellars served as safe storage for food and water, while an adjoining crypt provided a resting place for the Tresendar family. The Redbrands have expanded the cellars, adding slave pens, workshops, and barracks.

**Leader:** **Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek** — a former Lords' Alliance member turned outlaw, allied with the **Black Spider** (a drow). He wields a [[Magic-Items#Staff of Defense|staff of defense]].

**Enforcers:** Three [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]] sent by the Black Spider, led by **Mosk**. They keep the Redbrands and Phandalin citizens in line.

## Entrances

1. **Tresendar Manor** — Stone staircase off the ruined kitchen leads down to [[01-Cellar|Area 1: Cellar]]
2. **Secret Tunnel** — A rough-hewn passage exits the forest ~100 feet south of the manor, leading to [[08-Crevasse|Area 8: Crevasse]]. Carp Alderleaf knows about this entrance.

## General Features

- **Ceilings:** 10 feet high unless noted otherwise
- **Floors:** Flagstone throughout; walls of dressed stone blocks
- **Light:** Most areas brightly lit by oil lamps in wall sconces, refilled every few hours
- **Doors:** Wood with iron handles, hinges, and built-in locks. Unlocked unless stated otherwise. Locked doors: DC 10 Dexterity (thieves' tools) to pick, or DC 20 Strength to break down
- **Secret Doors:** Stone, blend with walls. Passive Perception 15 to spot within 10 ft, or DC 10 Wisdom (Perception) with active search. Swing on hidden iron hinges, not locked
- **Master Keys:** **Iarno** (area 12) and **Mosk** (area 9) each carry an iron key that locks/unlocks every door

## What the Redbrands Know

If [[Charmed|charmed]] or questioned, Redbrands reveal:

- Their leader is a human wizard called **Glasstaff** — his magic staff is made of glass. His chambers are in the western end (areas 11–12)
- A mysterious figure called the **Black Spider** hired them to frighten off adventurers and intimidate locals. The Black Spider sent [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]] as reinforcements (area 9)
- The lower part of the complex is guarded by a hideous "eye monster" (area 8 — the [[Nothic]])
- They have captives in a holding area "near the old crypts," guarded by [[Skeletons#Skeleton|skeletons]] (areas 4–5)
