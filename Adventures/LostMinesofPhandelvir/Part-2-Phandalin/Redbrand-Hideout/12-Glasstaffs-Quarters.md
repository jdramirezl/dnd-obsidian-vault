## DM Notes

### Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek

****Iarno**** is a former member of the **Lords' Alliance** who seized an opportunity to line his own pockets. Originally tasked with setting up a constabulary in Phandalin, he instead assembled a group of outlaws. He allied with the ****Black Spider**** (a drow) who promised to share the secrets and wealth of the Forge of Spells.

**Personality:** Puts on airs of gentility. Addresses ruffians as "my good gentlemen." Refers to kidnapping as "that unpleasant little business." Calls the characters "guests." Beneath the veneer — just as thuggish and arrogant as any Redbrand.

### Encounter Flow

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Capturing Iarno alive doubles XP (200 → 400) and Sildar wants him for trial. He surrenders at 8 hp. Approach via the secret door from area 7 to surprise him — the best outcome for the party.

**If approached via secret door from area 7:** Characters can **surprise** Iarno.

**If the rat in area 11 warned him:** Iarno grabs his [[Magic-Items#Staff of Defense|staff of defense]], the scrolls from his chest, and **flees** through the secret door in the northeast corner. In his haste, he:
- Leaves behind the **Black Spider's letter** on his desk
- Doesn't fully close the secret door — characters gain **advantage** on checks to find it

**Escape route:** Area 12 → areas 7 → 8 → area 1 (grabs cistern satchel). If the [[Nothic]] is alive in area 8, Iarno instructs it to waylay pursuers. If caught, he quaffs the *potion of invisibility* from the satchel and flees the hideout entirely. He could reappear later in the adventure at DM's discretion.

### Combat Tactics

- Casts **mage armor** on himself using the **Staff of Defense**
- Casts offensive spells at visible enemies
- Uses the **shield** power of his staff for added protection
- If reduced to **8 HP or fewer** with no escape, he **surrenders** — values his life above all else
- Remains a model prisoner, hoping the Black Spider will arrange his freedom

### Black Spider's Letter (found on desk)

> *Lord Albrek,*
>
> *My spies in Neverwinter tell me that strangers are due to arrive in Phandalin. They could be working for the dwarves. Capture them if you can, kill them if you must, but don't allow them to upset our plans. See that any dwarven maps in their possession are delivered to me with haste.*
>
> *I'm counting on you, Iarno. Don't disappoint me.*

### What Iarno Reveals (if captured)

All true:
- The **Black Spider** is a **drow** (dark elf)
- The Black Spider sent three bugbears to help Iarno, but the Redbrands managed without them. The bugbears know the way to **Wave Echo Cave**, but Iarno does not
- The Black Spider is searching Wave Echo Cave for the **Forge of Spells** — dwarves and gnomes of the Phandelver's Pact used it to fashion powerful magic items
- No other members of the Lords' Alliance know of Iarno's betrayal

### Developments

If captured, ****Sildar Hallwinter**** arranges to have Iarno incarcerated in the townmaster's hall until he can be transported to Neverwinter for trial.
