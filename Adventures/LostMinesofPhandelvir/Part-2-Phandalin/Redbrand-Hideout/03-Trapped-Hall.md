# 3. Trapped Hall

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 21

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una gruesa capa de polvo cubre las losas de este sombrío pasillo. Las paredes están decoradas con falsas columnas cada tres metros, y las puertas dobles en el extremo oeste del pasillo están revestidas de cobre, ahora verde por el paso del tiempo. Un relieve tallado de un ángel afligido adorna las puertas.

## DM Notes

This area was part of Tresendar Manor's original cellars. The Redbrands dug out dirt beneath the stone floor to create a hidden pit trap in the middle of the hallway.

The false floor consists of loose stone tiles laid atop breakaway timbers. Collapses under 100+ pounds of weight.

## Traps & Hazards

> [!danger] Trap: Pit
> DC 15 Perception to spot. Falls 20 ft for 2d6 bludgeoning, lands prone. DC 15 DEX save to catch the edge. Narrow ledges on north/south sides (DC 10 Acrobatics) bypass it.

### Pit Trap (center of hallway)

| Check | DC | Effect |
|-------|-----|--------|
| **Spot trap** | DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) | Reveals the covered pit and narrow ledges on north/south sides |
| **Skirt the ledge** | DC 10 Dexterity (Acrobatics) | Move around the pit using the narrow ledge |
| **Catch the edge** | DC 15 Dexterity [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throw]] | Triggered if you fall in or fail the Acrobatics check |
| **Fall damage** | — | 20 feet down → **2d6 bludgeoning**, land **[[Prone|prone]]** on dirt floor |

**XP:** 100 (divided equally) for avoiding or surviving the trap.
