# 6. Armory

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 22

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Estantes de armas recubren las paredes de esta cámara, incluyendo lanzas, espadas, ballestas y virotes. Una docena de capas rojas sucias cuelgan de ganchos junto a la puerta.

## DM Notes

The door to this room is **locked** from the outside. The Redbrands have ambitious plans to expand their numbers and have been stockpiling arms and armor.

- Across from the locked door is a **secret door** leading to area 7
- The 12 scarlet cloaks can be used as disguises

**Weapon inventory:**
- 12 spears
- 6 shortswords
- 4 longswords
- 6 light crossbows
- 8 quivers (20 crossbow bolts each)

## Monsters

None.

## Treasure

The weapons themselves are standard equipment (no special value beyond their normal prices). The 12 scarlet cloaks are useful for infiltration.
