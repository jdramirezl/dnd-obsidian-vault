# 1. Cellar

> **Part**: 2 — Phandalin | **Location**: Redbrand Hideout | **Page**: 20

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> La puerta se abre a un rellano de un metro y medio de ancho, a unos cinco metros por encima de un gran sótano, con escaleras de piedra que descienden al suelo en dos tramos cortos. Otra puerta se encuentra bajo las escaleras, al norte. Una gran cisterna de piedra ocupa la parte occidental de la sala, cuyas paredes están forradas de barriles y toneles.

## DM Notes

This room is the main entrance from Tresendar Manor. The Redbrands want to keep their base of operations hidden, so other than the barrels filled with fresh provisions, nothing gives away their presence.

- **Barrels:** Contain salted pork, beef, flour, sugar, apples, and ale. Moving barrels around to search them is noisy and attracts the Redbrands from area 2.
- **Cistern:** Rectangular reservoir, clean and filled with cold fresh water. 10 feet deep with a rim 2 feet higher than the floor (bottom is 8 feet below floor level). Drain pipes from the manor roof fill it.
- **Hidden Satchel:** A waterproof satchel hangs from a submerged rope on the south wall, ~2 feet below the surface. **DC 15 Wisdom (Perception)** to find from above, or automatic if probing with a pole or jumping in. This is Iarno's emergency getaway kit.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> The cistern satchel contains a Potion of Invisibility — Iarno's escape plan. If the rat in area 11 warns him, he grabs it and flees. Search the cistern early to cut off his escape.

- **Secret Door:** Southwest corner of the room. DC 10 Wisdom (Perception) with active search, or passive Perception 15 to spot within 10 ft.

**Developments:** No enemies here, but ruffians in area 2 notice loud noise. They creep in and gain surprise if unheard. If two ruffians are defeated, the last may flee through the secret door, revealing it.
