## DM Notes

### Background

Phandalin is built on the ruins of a much older settlement. The original town was allied with the dwarves and gnomes of the Phandelver's Pact, but was destroyed by the same orc horde that sacked Wave Echo Cave. In the last 3–4 years, settlers from Neverwinter and Waterdeep have reclaimed the ruins.

The **Redbrands** — a gang led by the mysterious **Glasstaff** — have controlled the town for the past two months, extorting and bullying everyone.

### Arrival Timing

Characters arrive **late in the day** and can only visit 1–2 locations before needing lodging. This naturally funnels them to [[Stonehill-Inn]] first.

### Sildar's Plan

If [[02-Important-NPCs#Sildar Hallwinter|Sildar]] is with the party:
- He wants to rest at [[Stonehill-Inn]]
- Then search for the missing wizard **Iarno Albrek**
- When his investigation hits a dead end, he sets up at [[Townmasters-Hall]]

### Suggested First Stops

- **[[Barthen-Provisions]]** — if the party has the supply wagon from the "Meet Me in Phandalin" hook
- **[[Lionshield-Coster]]** — if they recovered stolen goods from the Cragmaw Hideout
- **[[Stonehill-Inn]]** — default starting point, the rumor hub

### Running the Town

Think of it like directing an old-style western movie. Present scenes where adventurers walk into locations and meet NPCs. These are **roleplaying encounters** spread over a couple of in-game days.

- The town is small — minutes to walk end to end
- No need to track exact time at each location
- If players split up, take turns running each interaction
- Splitting up makes the Redbrand encounter more dangerous

### The Redbrand Confrontation

Sooner or later, the Redbrands become unavoidable:
- Characters go looking for them → run the encounter at [[Sleeping-Giant]]
- Characters ignore them → Redbrands find the party in the street
- Characters go straight to [[Tresendar-Manor]] → skip to Redbrand Hideout

### Finding Cragmaw Castle

No one in town knows the location directly. Three NPCs can suggest leads:
- [[02-Important-NPCs#Qelline Alderleaf|Qelline Alderleaf]] → suggests finding Reidoth the Druid in Thundertree
- [[02-Important-NPCs#Sildar Hallwinter|Sildar Hallwinter]] → offers 500 gp reward, suggests searching Triboar Trail
- [[02-Important-NPCs#Halia Thornton|Halia Thornton]] → knows Redbrands have a goblin minion who might know
