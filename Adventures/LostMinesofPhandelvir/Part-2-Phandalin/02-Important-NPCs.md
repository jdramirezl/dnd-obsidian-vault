|--------|------|

| **Role** | Innkeeper |

| **Description** | Short, friendly, young. Native of Triboar. Came to prospect but realized he was better at running an inn. |

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race/Gender** | Human male, ~50 |
| **Role** | Shopkeeper |
| **Location** | [[Barthen-Provisions]] |
| **Description** | Lean, balding, kindly manner. Employs two clerks: Ander and Thistle. |

**What He Knows:**

- Excited about the lost mine of the Phandelver's Pact

| **Role** | Retired adventurer |
| **Location** | [[Edermath-Orchard]] |
| **Faction** | Order of the Gauntlet |
| **Description** | Fit, silver-haired. Former marshal and herald in the Dragon Coast. |

**What He Knows:**
- Redbrand leader is called Glasstaff
- Redbrands hang around the [[Sleeping-Giant]]
- Main Redbrand safe house is under [[Tresendar-Manor]]

**Quest Hook:** **Old Owl Trouble** — investigate the ruins of Old Owl Well (Part 3, p29). An ancient Netheril watchtower where dangerous magic may be dormant.

**Faction Recruitment:** If the party deals with the Redbrands and investigates Old Owl Well, Daran offers membership in the **Order of the Gauntlet** (title: **Chevall**).

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|

| **Role** | Farmer |
| **Location** | [[Alderleaf-Farm]] |
| **Description** | Wise, pragmatic, kind host. Seems to know everything that goes on in town. |

**What She Knows:**

## Sildar Hallwinter

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Race/Gender** | Human male |
| **Role** | Lords' Alliance agent |
| **Location** | [[Stonehill-Inn]] → [[Townmasters-Hall]] |
| **Faction** | Lords' Alliance |
| **Description** | Knight and veteran. Goal: bring law and order to Phandalin. Believes prosperity from the mine will civilize the town. |

**What He Knows:**
- **Iarno Albrek**, a fellow Lords' Alliance member, disappeared near [[Tresendar-Manor]] ~2 months ago
- Describes Iarno as "a short, dark-bearded human wizard in his thirties"
- Does NOT know that Iarno = Glasstaff (learns this later)

**Quest Hooks:**
- **Finding Cragmaw Castle** — 500 gp reward to locate the castle and defeat/drive off the chieftain. Suggests searching Triboar Trail for raiding parties.
- **Finding Iarno** — investigate [[Tresendar-Manor]] to find Iarno. 200 gp reward for eliminating the Redbrand threat. Once he learns Iarno is Glasstaff, he wants the wizard captured and sent to Neverwinter.

**Faction Recruitment:** After eliminating the goblin threat or uncovering Iarno's treachery, Sildar offers membership in the **Lords' Alliance** (title: **Cloak**).
