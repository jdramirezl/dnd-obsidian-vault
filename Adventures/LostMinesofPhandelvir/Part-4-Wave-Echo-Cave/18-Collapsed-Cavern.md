# 18. Collapsed Cavern

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 49

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una amplia grieta ocupa la mitad oriental de esta caverna. Un arroyo brota de la pared oeste, luego cae en cascada hacia la grieta y fluye de nuevo hacia el norte. Varias cuerdas están aseguradas a estacas de hierro a lo largo del borde occidental de la grieta, descendiendo hasta el fondo del abismo.

> [!warning] Stop Vhalak — Doppelganger Escape Makes Boss Fight Harder
> If 2+ bugbears die, Vhalak (doppelganger disguised as drow) flees to area 19 and assumes Nundro's appearance. Nezznar then uses the fake "dwarf" as a hostage to force surrender. **Stopping Vhalak is critical** — block the north exit or kill it fast.

## DM Notes

Nezznar's servants occupy this cavern, guarding against undead incursions and carefully sifting through rubble. The Black Spider's divinations suggest valuable treasure is hidden at the bottom of the rift.

**Enemy Positions:**
- 1 [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbear]] stands guard in the **western half**
- 2 bugbears are clearing rock on the **rift floor** (climb up ropes if fight breaks out)
- **Vhalak** ([[Doppelganger|doppelganger]] disguised as a male [[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]]) supervises the operation

**Rift:** 20 ft deep.
- Climbing without rope: DC 10 Strength (Athletics)
- Fail by 5+: 1d6 bludgeoning per 10 ft, land prone
- Ropes are in place along the western edge

### Developments

- If **2+ bugbears are killed**, the doppelganger tries to **retreat to [[19-Temple-of-Dumathoin|area 19]]** to warn Nezznar
- ⚠️ If Vhalak reaches area 19, it assumes the guise of **Nundro Rockseeker** so Nezznar can use the "dwarf" as leverage to force the party's surrender (though the drow won't actually harm the doppelganger)
- **Stopping Vhalak is critical** — it makes the boss fight much harder

## Monsters

- [[Bugbears#Bugbear|Bugbear]] ×3 — Nezznar's workers
  - HP: 27 (5d8 + 5) each
  - 1 on guard (west), 2 in the rift (need 1 round to climb up)

- [[Doppelganger]] ×1 — Vhalak, disguised as male [[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]]
  - HP: 52 (8d8 + 16)
  - AC: 14
  - Shapechanger: Can polymorph into any Small/Medium humanoid it has seen
  - Read Thoughts: Magical insight into surface thoughts (DC 13 Wis save to resist)
  - Will flee to warn Nezznar if 2+ bugbears fall

> [!info] Hidden Treasure — Gauntlets of Ogre Power (DC 20 Perception)
> Buried under rubble at the rift bottom on a crushed dwarf skeleton. DC 20 Perception, one check per hour. Sets Strength to 19. This is easy to miss — if the party doesn't search the rift thoroughly, they'll walk right past one of the best items in the adventure.

## Treasure

  - DC 20 Wisdom (Perception) to find (remains hidden from view)
  - Each character can attempt **one check per hour**
  - On a crushed dwarf skeleton
  - Sets Strength to 19
