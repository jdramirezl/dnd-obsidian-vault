# 12. Smelter Cavern

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 46

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Un alto horno y un fuelle mecánico impulsado por una rueda hidráulica dominan esta gran cámara. El horno está frío y oscuro, pero montones de carbón se apilan cerca, junto con carros llenos de mineral sin refinar. La rueda hidráulica se asienta en un canal de tres metros de ancho cortado en el suelo de la sala, pero el canal está seco. Pasajes salen al oeste, sur y este. El canal vacío sale al norte y al este.
>
> Más de una docena de cadáveres marchitos están esparcidos por la sala. Estos enanos y orcos caídos aún llevan los restos de sus armaduras. Flotando sobre ellos hay un cráneo envuelto en llamas verdes.

> [!danger] Flameskull Rejuvenation — It Comes Back in 1 Hour
> If destroyed, the flameskull **reforms in 1 hour** unless *dispel magic* or *holy water* is applied to its remains. If the party doesn't know this, they'll face it again on the way out. Its *fireball* (8d6 fire, DC 13 DEX, 20-ft radius) can TPK a 4th-level party in the enclosed space.

## DM Notes

This chamber is a **serious obstacle** preventing Nezznar from reaching the Forge of Spells. The [[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]] is still formulating a plan to get past the undead here.

This was the heart of the mining operation — dwarves melted ore to refine silver, gold, and platinum ingots. The dry channel diverted the stream from [[18-Collapsed-Cavern|area 18]] to power the waterwheel and bellows.

**Zombie Behavior:** The 8 [[Zombies#Zombie|zombies]] rise and pursue living creatures but **do not pursue outside this area for more than 1 round**. The [[Flameskull|flameskull]] is far more dangerous.

**Flameskull Tactics:** This is an intelligent undead guardian — a former servant of the human wizards. It continues to act on ancient instructions to prevent intruders from passing through.
- Opens with *fireball* (8d6 fire, DC 13 Dex save, 20-ft radius) — devastating in the enclosed space
- Uses *magic missile* and *flaming sphere* for sustained damage
- **Rejuvenation:** If destroyed, the flameskull reforms in 1 hour unless *dispel magic* or *holy water* is applied to its remains

**Dry Channel:** 5 ft below floor level — no check to scramble in/out. Characters in the channel can follow it north or east, though the ceiling is only 5 ft high after exiting the room.

⚠️ **This is a Hard/Deadly encounter.** The flameskull's fireball can TPK a 4th-level party. Warn through description — the green flames should feel menacing.

> [!warning] Hard/Deadly Encounter — Flameskull + 8 Zombies
> The flameskull opens with *fireball* in an enclosed space — 8d6 fire damage to the whole party. The 8 zombies have Undead Fortitude and won't pursue beyond this room. Consider the bypass route through areas 10 → 17 → 18 to avoid this entirely. Nezznar himself hasn't figured out how to get past this room.

## Monsters

- [[Zombies#Zombie|Zombie]] ×8 — withered dwarf and orc corpses
  - HP: 22 (3d8 + 9) each
  - Undead Fortitude: DC 5 + damage taken Con save to drop to 1 HP instead of 0 (except radiant/crit)
  - Won't pursue beyond 1 round outside this room

- [[Flameskull]] ×1 — floating skull engulfed in green flame
  - HP: 40 (9d4 + 18)
  - Spells: *fireball* (1/day), *magic missile*, *flaming sphere*, *shield*, *blur*
  - Illumination: sheds dim light 15 ft
  - **Rejuvenation** in 1 hour unless dispelled or doused in holy water
