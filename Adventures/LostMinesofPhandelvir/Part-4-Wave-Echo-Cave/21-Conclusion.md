# Conclusion

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 51

## Adventure Complete 🎉

With hard work and a little luck, the adventurers have:
- **Defeated the Black Spider** and undone his destructive plots
- **Cleared Phandalin** of the ruffians who threatened its people
- **Reclaimed the lost mine** of Wave Echo Cave

Their deeds will be long remembered in this corner of the Sword Coast.

## The Mine's Future

**Gundren and Nundro Rockseeker** take over administration of the new mine. For the adventurers' service to their family, they gladly award the party a **10 percent share of the mine's profits**.

In years to come, the restored mines of the Phandelver's Pact will bring great riches to Phandalin and help establish peace and prosperity in the area.

## Settling Down or Moving On

If the characters want to **remain in Phandalin**:
- They could restore **Tresendar Manor** or establish homes of their own
- The people of the area are glad to have them stay

If they choose to **move on** in search of new adventures:
- They'll always have a warm welcome in Phandalin
- The mine shares provide ongoing passive income

## XP Awards Summary

| Award | XP |
|---|---|
| Monster encounters | Per stat block (see **Appendix B**) |
| Nezznar captured alive + delivered | **Double** his XP value |
| Nundro rescued and survives | 200 XP (divided among party) |

By the end of the adventure, characters should be **5th level**.

## The Forge of Spells

The [[15-Forge-of-Spells|Forge of Spells]] remains a powerful resource even in its diminished state. The Brazier of Green Flame can still temporarily enchant weapons and armor (+1 for 1d12 hours). Control of the Forge is one of the mine's greatest assets — and a reason others may come seeking it.

> [!info] Loose Threads for Future Adventures
> Halia Thornton may break Nezznar out of jail for the Zhentarim. Mormesk (if alive) still controls the undead. The hidden map in area 14 leads to a new dungeon. The Forge's rediscovery will attract powerful enemies. Pick 1-2 hooks that match your party's interests.

## Future Adventure Hooks

1. **The Hidden Map** — The map found in [[14-Wizards-Quarters|Mormesk's quarters]] (DC 12 Investigation) shows the location of a dungeon of the DM's creation — the most direct hook for the next adventure.

2. **Nezznar's Escape** — If captured, Halia Thornton may break Nezznar out of jail and deliver him to the **Zhentarim**. The Black Network wants to learn everything about Wave Echo Cave. What happens next is up to the DM.

3. **The Forge's Reputation** — Word of the Forge of Spells' rediscovery will spread. Wizards, warlords, and worse may come seeking its power.

4. **Mormesk's Undead** — If [[14-Wizards-Quarters|Mormesk the Wraith]] was not destroyed, he continues to lead the undead in Wave Echo Cave. The mine cannot be fully operational while he remains.

5. **Unfinished Business in Phandalin** — Any loose threads from Parts 2 and 3 (Zhentarim, Harpers, Lords' Alliance, Order of the Gauntlet) can drive future adventures.

6. **The Sword Mountains** — Wave Echo Cave is just one location in the Sword Mountains. What else lurks in these ancient peaks?
