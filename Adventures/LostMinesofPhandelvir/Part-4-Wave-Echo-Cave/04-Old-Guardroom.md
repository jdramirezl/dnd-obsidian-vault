# 4. Old Guardroom

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 44

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Bancos de piedra astillados y montones de escombros de un techo parcialmente derrumbado llenan esta sala. Entre literas de piedra arruinadas y estantes de armas volcados, se ven los huesos de varios enanos y orcos.

## DM Notes

This guardroom once protected the nearby entrance. It was overrun early when the orcs attacked.

**Skeleton Animation:** One round after any living creature enters the chamber, the bones begin to stir and knit together, forming 9 [[Skeletons#Skeleton|skeletons]]. They fight until destroyed.

The delay gives the party one round to react before combat — perceptive players may notice the bones shifting.

## Monsters

- [[Skeletons#Skeleton|Skeleton]] ×9 — animate 1 round after a living creature enters
  - HP: 13 (2d8 + 4) each
  - Vulnerable to bludgeoning damage
  - Fight until destroyed — no retreat
