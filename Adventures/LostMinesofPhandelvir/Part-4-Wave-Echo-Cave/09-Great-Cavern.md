# 9. Great Cavern

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 46

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Escarpados desniveles dividen esta gran caverna en tres secciones: repisas elevadas a cada extremo y una sección más baja en el centro. Escaleras talladas en la piedra ascienden a las repisas. Dos grandes mesas se encuentran en la sección central, junto con un par de viejos braseros. Una mesa más pequeña se encuentra en la repisa oriental. Los restos esqueléticos de docenas de guerreros muertos — enanos, gnomos, orcos y ogros — dan testimonio de la ferocidad del combate que tuvo lugar aquí hace mucho tiempo.

> [!warning] Hard Encounter — 7 Ghouls Ambush from Above
> The ghouls lurk on the western ledge (10 ft up) and leap down on the party. Paralyzing Claws (DC 10 CON) can chain-paralyze multiple characters. Elves are immune to the paralysis. This is the dungeon's central hub — the party will likely pass through multiple times.

## DM Notes

This cave once served as the banquet room, meeting area, and mead hall for the miners. It's a **central hub** connecting many areas of the dungeon.

**Ghoul Ambush:** 7 [[Ghouls#Ghoul|ghouls]] lurk in the shadows on the **western ledge**. They notice any light or noise and quickly bound down to attack. They are hungry and fight until destroyed.

**Escarpments:** 10 ft high.
- DC 12 Strength (Athletics) to climb
- Fall or knocked off = 1d6 bludgeoning + land prone
- The ghouls use the height advantage — they leap down on the party

**Tactics:** The ghouls swarm the party from above. Their paralyzing touch can quickly overwhelm a group. Focus fire on isolated characters. This is a **Hard** encounter.

## Monsters

- [[Ghouls#Ghoul|Ghoul]] ×7 — lurking on the western ledge
  - HP: 22 (5d8) each
  - Paralyzing Claws: DC 10 Con save or paralyzed 1 min (elves immune)
  - Bound down from ledge to attack any light/noise
  - Fight until destroyed
