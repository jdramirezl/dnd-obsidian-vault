# 10. Dark Pool

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 46

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Una piscina inmóvil llena gran parte de esta caverna. El agua es oscura, revelando poco de lo que pueda yacer en su interior. La orilla de la piscina consiste en una fina capa de conchas rotas de extraños mejillones pálidos, y un olor a pescado flota en el aire.
>
> Un pasaje conduce al sur desde esta zona, y un tramo de escalones asciende hacia el este. Un arroyo perezoso fluye fuera de la caverna hacia el noreste.

## DM Notes

The pool is **20 ft deep** in the middle. The stream to the northeast is 3 ft deep with a ceiling only 2–3 ft above the water — characters can easily wade through to [[18-Collapsed-Cavern|area 18]]. This is a **key bypass route**.

**Underwater Skeleton:** A character who explores the pool finds an old skeleton lying on the bottom, 10 ft from shore under 10 ft of water. These are the remains of a human wizard from old Phandalin who died defending the mines. Several orc arrows are still lodged in the ribcage.

The skeleton is not immediately visible — the water is dark. Characters must actively search or swim to find it.

> [!info] Underwater Treasure — Wand of Magic Missiles
> A dead wizard's skeleton lies 10 ft underwater, 10 ft from shore. The **Wand of Magic Missiles** (7 charges) is one of the best items in the dungeon. The party must actively search the dark water to find it — it's not visible from the surface.

## Treasure

On the underwater skeleton:
- 2 platinum rings (75 gp each)
- [[02-Magic-Item-Descriptions#Wand of Magic Missiles|Wand of Magic Missiles]] — 7 charges, fire magic missile (1–3 charges per cast), regains 1d6+1 at dawn

> [!tip] Bypass Route — Stream Northeast to Area 18
> The stream flowing northeast is only 3 ft deep with a low ceiling — characters can wade through to area 18, completely bypassing the flameskull in area 12. This is the key alternate route through the dungeon.
