# 2. Mine Tunnels

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 44

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Esta zona consiste en numerosos pasajes que se cruzan entre sí. Los techos aquí son de apenas metro ochenta, y varios de los pasajes terminan en frentes de roca parcialmente excavados. El aire es húmedo y opresivo, y el eco de las olas retumba débilmente a través de la piedra.

## DM Notes

These are dead-end passages where miners gave up and moved on. An **[[Oozes#Ochre Jelly|ochre jelly]]** patiently stalks the party through these tunnels, instinctively waiting for an opportunity to attack a lone target.

**Tactics:** The jelly follows the party silently. It waits until someone separates from the group — checking a dead end, falling behind, or scouting ahead alone. Then it strikes.

The 6-ft ceilings make this area cramped for taller characters.

## Monsters

- [[Oozes#Ochre Jelly|Ochre Jelly]] ×1 — stalking through the tunnels
  - HP: 45 (6d10 + 12)
  - Lurks in a dead-end passage, then follows the party
  - Splits into two when hit by lightning or slashing damage
