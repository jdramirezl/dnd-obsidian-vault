# 8. Fungi Cavern

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 45–46

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Densas alfombras de hongos extraños cubren grandes secciones del suelo de esta caverna. El crecimiento incluye bolas de esporas de treinta centímetros de diámetro, extraños hongos de repisa que crecen sobre las estalagmitas, y grandes tallos y sombreros de metro y medio de altura. Algunas de las bolas de esporas brillan con una inquietante fosforescencia verde, bañando la caverna en una luz fantasmal.

> [!danger] Poison Gas Trap — 3d6 Poison + Poisoned for 10 Minutes
> Crossing the cavern triggers poison gas (DC 11 CON). Failed save = 3d6 poison damage + poisoned 10 min. Gas persists 1 minute — anyone lingering must save again each turn. **Dash through** (1 save) rather than walking (multiple saves). This is what's blocking Nezznar from reaching the Forge.

## DM Notes

This cave has hindered Nezznar's explorations. The [[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]] suspects the magic workshops are close by but is reluctant to risk the fungi.

The green-glowing fungi provide **natural light** — creatures can see the entire cavern without darkvision or light sources.

**⚠️ Poison Gas Trap:** Whenever a creature attempts to cross the cavern, the fungi release poisonous gas:
- **DC 11 Constitution saving throw**
- **Fail:** 3d6 poison damage + poisoned for 10 minutes
- Gas disperses after 1 minute
- **While gas persists:** Any living creature that ends its turn in the cavern must repeat the save
- **Strategy:** Dash through quickly (1 save) vs. lingering (multiple saves)
