# 19. Temple of Dumathoin

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 49–50

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Seis columnas de mármol agrietadas se alinean a lo largo de las paredes de este salón. En el extremo norte se alza una estatua de casi tres metros de un enano sentado en un trono, con un poderoso martillo de guerra de piedra sobre su regazo. Grandes esmeraldas relucen en los ojos de la estatua.
>
> El polvo y los escombros que cubrían el suelo han sido barridos a un lado, y una especie de campamento se extiende ahora frente a la estatua. Media docena de petates y mochilas están ordenadamente dispuestos alrededor de una hoguera improvisada. Una mesa de madera se encuentra en el lado oeste de la sala, entre dos columnas.

*Si los ocupantes no son conscientes de la presencia del grupo, añade:*

> Dos bugbears están junto a la mesa, flanqueando a un elfo oscuro vestido con armadura de cuero negro y túnicas. Empuña un bastón negro con una araña tallada en la punta y frunce el ceño al veros.
>
> "Parece que debo encargarme de vosotros personalmente. Una lástima que tenga que terminar así."

> [!warning] 🕷️ BOSS FIGHT — Nezznar the Black Spider (DEADLY Encounter)
> Nezznar + 2 bugbears + 4 hidden giant spiders. If warned (Vhalak escaped area 18), Nezznar goes invisible and the spiders ambush with webs. Reinforcements from area 18 arrive after 3 rounds if not cleared. **Capture alive for double XP** — but watch for Halia Thornton breaking him out of jail afterward.

## DM Notes

### ⚔️ THIS IS THE BOSS FIGHT

Nezznar the Black Spider uses this room as his headquarters while exploring the mines. This is a **DEADLY** encounter — the climax of the entire adventure.

### Enemy Forces

**If NOT expecting trouble:**
- Nezznar stands by the table with 2 [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]]
- 4 giant spiders lurk behind the pillars (hidden)
- Standard initiative

**If EXPECTING trouble** (e.g., Vhalak warned him from [[18-Collapsed-Cavern|area 18]]):
- Nezznar casts **invisibility** on himself and stands near the table
- 4 [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Giant Spider|giant spiders]] hide behind pillars — make Dexterity (Stealth) check for them
- Spiders try to **entangle with webs** before closing to melee
- Nezznar joins the fray **on the round after** the spiders attack

**If Vhalak ([[Doppelganger|doppelganger]]) escaped here:**
- It assumes the guise of **Nundro Rockseeker**
- Nezznar uses the "dwarf" as leverage to force surrender
- The [[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]] won't actually harm the doppelganger — it's a bluff
- DC 15 Wisdom (Insight) to sense something is off about "Nundro"

### Nezznar's Tactics

1. **Round 1:** If invisible, observes. If visible, opens with *spider climb* to reach ceiling/pillar for cover, then casts offensive spells
2. **Ongoing:** Uses *web* to control the battlefield, *poison spray* for close targets, [[Magic-Items#Spider Staff|Spider Staff]] for melee
3. **Spiders first:** The giant spiders engage first to web and restrain targets while Nezznar picks them off
4. **Retreat:** Nezznar is arrogant but not suicidal — if clearly losing, he may try to bargain or flee toward [[20-Priests-Quarters|area 20]] to use Nundro as a hostage

### Roleplaying Nezznar

Even though he intends to kill the characters, Nezznar is **curious about them**. Given the chance, he quizzes them about their identities, allegiances, interests, and goals — filing it all away.

- He **admits to being the Black Spider**
- He admits to using the Cragmaw goblins and Redbrands to keep Wave Echo Cave secret
- He will **say or do anything** to put the characters off guard — promising surrender, proposing cooperation against the monsters
- **He always betrays** the characters as soon as they outlive their usefulness

### Reinforcements ⚠️

The creatures in [[18-Collapsed-Cavern|area 18]] can hear combat here. If not already dealt with, they **arrive after 3 rounds** and act immediately after Nezznar's giant spiders in initiative.

> [!danger] Statue Trap — Fake Emerald Eyes Trigger Ceiling Collapse
> The emerald eyes are worthless glass (DC 15 Investigation). Removing one triggers a ceiling collapse: DC 15 DEX save, **4d10 bludgeoning + prone** on failure, half on success. This hits **everyone in the room** — including Nezznar and his allies. Could be used tactically, but risky.

### Statue of Dumathoin

The statue depicts **Dumathoin**, the dwarven god of mining (DC 10 Religion to identify).

**⚠️ STATUE TRAP — The emerald eyes are FAKE:**
- DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) reveals they're worthless glass
- *Detect magic* shows strong abjuration aura around the statue
- DC 10 Strength to pry an eye loose
- **If either eye is removed:** Pillars crack, triggering **ceiling collapse**
  - DC 15 Dexterity saving throw
  - **Fail:** 4d10 bludgeoning damage + prone
  - **Success:** Half damage
  - This affects **every creature in the room** — including Nezznar and his allies

### Developments — After the Fight

- **Captured alive:** If delivered to the townmaster's hall in Phandalin, Nezznar is locked up until Sildar or a Lords' Alliance representative can escort him to Neverwinter
- **⚠️ Jailbreak:** Unless the characters post guards, **Halia Thornton** breaks him out, smuggles him from Phandalin, and delivers him to the **Zhentarim** — who want to learn everything about Wave Echo Cave
- **Double XP** if Nezznar is captured alive and delivered to Sildar/Townmaster

## Monsters

- **Nezznar the Black Spider** ([[Elves-Drow#Drow|Drow]]) ×1 — main villain
  - HP: 27 (6d8)
  - AC: 15 (studded leather) + *shield* spell
  - Spells: *spider climb*, *web*, *poison spray*, *invisibility*, *suggestion*, *shield*, *magic missile*
  - [[Magic-Items#Spider Staff|Spider Staff]]: +1d6 poison on hit, 10 charges (*spider climb* 1 charge, *web* 2 charges, DC 15)
  - Fey Ancestry: Advantage vs. charmed, immune to magical sleep
  - Sunlight Sensitivity: Disadvantage on attacks/Perception in sunlight
  - **Double XP if captured alive**

- [[Bugbears#Bugbear|Bugbear]] ×2 — flanking Nezznar
  - HP: 27 (5d8 + 5) each
  - Brute + Surprise Attack

- [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Giant Spider|Giant Spider]] ×4 — hidden behind pillars
  - HP: 26 (4d10 + 4) each
  - Web (recharge 5–6): +5 to hit, range 30/60, restrained on hit (DC 12 Str to escape)
  - Bite: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing + 2d8 poison (DC 11 Con, half on save)
  - Spider Climb, Web Sense, Web Walker
  - Defend Nezznar to the death
