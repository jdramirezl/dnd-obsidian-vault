# Part 4: Wave Echo Cave

> **Adventure**: Lost Mines of Phandelver | **Pages**: 42–56 | **Levels**: 4 → 5

## Overview

Wave Echo Cave is the climactic final dungeon — the lost mine of the Phandelver's Pact, hidden fifteen miles east of Phandalin in the Sword Mountains. Nezznar the Black Spider ([[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]]) has been exploring the cave searching for the Forge of Spells, but restless undead and dangerous monsters have slowed his progress. The adventurers must navigate 20 rooms of undead, oozes, and Nezznar's minions to rescue Nundro Rockseeker and stop the Black Spider.

**Key objectives:**
- Find and stop [[19-Temple-of-Dumathoin|Nezznar the Black Spider]]
- Rescue [[20-Priests-Quarters|Nundro Rockseeker]]
- Discover the [[15-Forge-of-Spells|Forge of Spells]]
- Claim the legendary magic items within the mine

> [!warning] Level Check — Party Should Be Level 4+
> This dungeon is designed for level 4 characters (2,700+ XP each). If the party skipped too many Part 3 encounters, Wave Echo Cave will be brutally difficult. The flameskull, wraith, and Nezznar encounters are all Hard/Deadly at the intended level.

## Character Level

This part is designed for characters of **at least 4th level** (2,700+ XP each). If the party skipped too many optional encounters in Part 3, many encounters here will be very difficult. Characters should reach **5th level** by the adventure's end.

## XP Awards

XP is awarded per monster defeated (see stat blocks in **Appendix B**). Additional awards:
- **Double XP** for Nezznar if captured alive and delivered to Sildar/Townmaster
- **200 XP** (divided) if Nundro is rescued and survives

## Booming Waves 🌊

The rhythmic thunder of pounding waves echoes throughout the mine — loud enough to make the stone underfoot shiver. Waves come about **every 2 minutes**, growing louder toward the northeast. The source is a water-filled cavern (area 16) connected to an underground hot spring. Use this sound as atmospheric flavor to lure the party deeper.

> [!tip] Key Bypass Route — Areas 10 → 17 → 18
> The old streambed (area 17) lets the party skip the flameskull + 8 zombies in area 12 entirely. Nezznar hasn't discovered this route. Smart players who explore the Dark Pool (area 10) and follow the stream northeast will find it — reward their curiosity.

## Dungeon Flow

```
ENTRANCE (1) → Pit down
  ├── NW → Mine Tunnels (2)
  └── E → Old Entrance (3)
              ├── Old Guardroom (4)
              ├── Assayers' Office (5)
              ├── South Barracks (6)
              └── Ruined Storeroom (7) [SAFE REST]

Fungi Cavern (8) — poison gas barrier
Great Cavern (9) — central hub
  ├── Dark Pool (10) → stream to (18)
  ├── North Barracks (11) — Nezznar's front line
  └── Smelter Cavern (12) — flameskull blocks path

Starry Cavern (13) — contains:
  ├── Wizards' Quarters (14) — Mormesk the Wraith
  └── Forge of Spells (15) — PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

Booming Cavern (16) — atmospheric dead end
Old Streambed (17) — bypass route around area 12

Collapsed Cavern (18) — Nezznar's workers
Temple of Dumathoin (19) — BOSS FIGHT (Nezznar)
  → Priests' Quarters (20) — Nundro (locked)
```

**Key routes:**
- **Main path**: 1 → 2/3 → 9 → 12 → 13 → 15 (through undead gauntlet)
- **Bypass**: 10 → 17 → 18 (avoids area 12 [[Flameskull|flameskull]])
- **Boss approach**: 11 or 18 → 19 → 20
