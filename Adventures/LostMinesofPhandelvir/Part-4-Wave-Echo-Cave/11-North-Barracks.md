# 11. North Barracks

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 46

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Viejas literas de piedra recubren las paredes de este cuartel, iluminado y calentado por un brasero de hierro incandescente en el centro de la sala.

*Si el grupo entra desde el oeste, añade:*

> Al otro lado de la sala hay otra puerta, esta bloqueada por una barricada hecha con los restos de una mesa de madera.

## DM Notes

Five [[Bugbears#Bugbear|bugbears]] reside here — loyal minions of Nezznar. This room marks the **front line** in the Black Spider's assault on Wave Echo Cave. The bugbears are here to prevent [[Ghouls#Ghoul|ghouls]], [[Zombies#Zombie|zombies]], or other undead from troubling their master in [[19-Temple-of-Dumathoin|area 19]].

The bugbears removed the corpses they found and built the barricade on the east door.

**Perception at doors:**
- DC 10 Wisdom (Perception) at either door to hear gruff voices speaking Goblin, talking about how hungry they are
- **East door:** Barricaded from inside — DC 20 Strength to force open

**Tactics:** The bugbears fight aggressively. They use their Brute and Surprise Attack features if they get the drop on intruders. They won't flee — they fear Nezznar more than the party.

## Monsters

- [[Bugbears#Bugbear|Bugbear]] ×5 — Nezznar's front-line guards
  - HP: 27 (5d8 + 5) each
  - Brute: Extra die of weapon damage on melee hits
  - Surprise Attack: +2d6 damage if they surprise a creature in round 1

## Treasure

- Largest bugbear's pouch: 15 cp, 13 ep
- **Potion of Vitality** — removes exhaustion, cures disease/poison, maximizes Hit Dice healing for 24 hours
