# 20. Priests' Quarters

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 50

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Cortinas polvorientas adornan las paredes de esta habitación, que también contiene una cama y un brasero. Un enano muy desaliñado yace atado e inconsciente en el frío suelo de piedra.

> [!tip] Deal With Area 19 First — Noise Here Alerts Nezznar
> Unless the party is stealthy, any activity at this locked door alerts Nezznar and his allies in area 19. He sends giant spiders to investigate. **Clear the boss fight first**, then come back for Nundro with the anvil-shaped key from Nezznar's body.

## DM Notes

This room formerly belonged to the priest in charge of Dumathoin's temple ([[19-Temple-of-Dumathoin|area 19]]). Nezznar has appropriated it as a cell.

**Door:** Locked — DC 15 Dexterity with thieves' tools to open. Nezznar carries the key (anvil-shaped iron key).

⚠️ **Unless the characters are being stealthy**, any activity at the door attracts the attention of Nezznar and his allies in area 19, prompting the [[Elves-Drow#Drow|drow]] to send his [[Appendix-A-Miscellaneous-Creatures#Giant Spider|giant spiders]] to investigate. **Deal with area 19 first** or be very quiet.

### Nundro Rockseeker

The bound dwarf is **Nundro**, a dwarf commoner and the youngest of the three Rockseeker brothers. Nezznar spared him because he thought the dwarf might know more about the mine. The drow has **interrogated Nundro harshly** once or twice a day since capturing him.

**Developments:**
- Nundro is **grateful** if rescued and offers to tag along
- He **doesn't know** the mine layout — he hasn't much useful information
- After Nezznar is defeated, this area serves as a **safe and comfortable rest area**

**XP Award:** If Nundro is rescued and survives the adventure, divide **200 XP** equally among the party.
