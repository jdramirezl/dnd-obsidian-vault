# 17. Old Streambed

> **Part**: 4 — Wave Echo Cave | **Page**: 49

## Texto para Leer en Voz Alta 🎭

> Este pasaje tiene apenas un metro veinte de altura y está obstruido por cantos rodados y guijarros. Podría haber sido el lecho de un arroyo, aunque ya no fluye agua por aquí.

## DM Notes

This former streambed is a **key bypass route**. The dwarves originally diverted the stream into the channel leading to [[12-Smelter-Cavern|area 12]] to drive the waterwheel. Then the earthquakes during the final spell battle collapsed the floor in [[18-Collapsed-Cavern|area 18]], diverting the stream again.

**Strategic importance:** This passage circumvents the undead in area 12 ([[Flameskull|flameskull]] + 8 [[Zombies#Zombie|zombies]]). **Nezznar has not yet discovered this route** — clever players who find it gain a significant advantage.

The passage is only **4 ft high** — Medium creatures must crouch or crawl. This slows movement but is otherwise safe.
