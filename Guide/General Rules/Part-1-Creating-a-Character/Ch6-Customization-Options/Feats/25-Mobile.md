## Original Text

**Mobile**

You are exceptionally speedy and agile. You gain the following benefits:

- Your speed increases by 10 feet.
- When you use the Dash action, difficult terrain doesn't cost you extra movement on that turn.
- When you make a melee attack against a creature, you don't provoke opportunity attacks from that creature for the rest of the turn, whether you hit or not.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Move faster, ignore difficult terrain while dashing, and freely disengage from anything you swing at — the ultimate hit-and-run feat.

- **+10 ft speed** (applies to all movement types you have)
- **Dash through difficult terrain** without extra movement cost
- **No opportunity attacks** from any creature you make a melee attack against that turn (hit or miss)

> [!tip] DM Note
> One of the best feats for [[01-Monk-Overview|monks]] and [[01-Rogue-Overview|rogues]] who want to dart in, attack, and retreat without wasting a bonus action on Disengage. The OA immunity is per-creature and triggers on any melee attack, including bonus action attacks. Stacks well with classes that already have high speed. The difficult terrain clause is situational but clutch in outdoor encounters.
