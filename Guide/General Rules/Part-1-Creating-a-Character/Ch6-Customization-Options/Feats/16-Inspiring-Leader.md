## Original Text

**Inspiring Leader**

*Prerequisite: Charisma 13 or higher*

You can spend 10 minutes inspiring your companions, shoring up their resolve to fight. When you do so, choose up to six friendly creatures (which can include yourself) within 30 feet of you who can see or hear you and who can understand you. Each creature can gain temporary hit points equal to your level + your Charisma modifier. A creature can't gain temporary hit points from this feat again until it has finished a short or long rest.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** A 10-minute speech grants up to 6 allies temporary HP equal to your level + CHA modifier, refreshing on short or long rest.

- **Prerequisite:** Charisma **13 or higher**
- **10-minute speech** to up to **6 friendly creatures** within 30 ft (can include yourself)
- Creatures must **see or hear you** and **understand you**
- Each gains **temp HP = your level + CHA modifier**
- Once per creature per **short or long rest**

> [!tip] DM Note
> One of the best "party buffer" feats in the game. At level 10 with +4 CHA, that's 14 temp HP × 6 creatures = 84 effective HP added to the party before every fight. Temp HP doesn't stack, so it competes with other temp HP sources (e.g., [[Armor of Agathys|*armor of Agathys*]], Fiend [[01-Warlock-Overview|warlock]]). Best on [[01-Bard-Overview|Bards]], [[01-Sorcerer-Overview|Sorcerers]], Warlocks, and [[01-Paladin-Overview|Paladins]] who already invest in CHA. Encourage players to actually roleplay the speech for fun.
