## Original Text

**Keen Mind**

You have a mind that can track time, direction, and detail with uncanny precision. You gain the following benefits.

- Increase your Intelligence score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You always know which way is north.
- You always know the number of hours left before the next sunrise or sunset.
- You can accurately recall anything you have seen or heard within the past month.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Perfect mental compass, clock, and photographic memory for the past month, plus +1 INT.

- **+1 Intelligence** (max 20)
- **Always know north**
- **Always know hours** until next sunrise or sunset
- **Perfect recall** of anything seen or heard within the **past month**

> [!tip] DM Note
> Primarily a ribbon/utility feat. The perfect recall is the standout feature — the player can ask the DM to repeat any detail from the past month of in-game time, which rewards attentive players and can solve mysteries. The navigation benefits eliminate the need for a compass or survival checks for direction. Best taken as a half-feat to round up an odd INT score on [[01-Wizard-Overview|Wizards]] or Investigators.
