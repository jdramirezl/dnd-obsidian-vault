## Original Text

**Heavily Armored**

*Prerequisite: Proficiency with medium armor*

You have trained to master the use of heavy armor, gaining the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You gain proficiency with heavy armor.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Upgrade from medium to heavy armor proficiency with a +1 STR bonus.

- **Prerequisite:** Proficiency with **medium armor**
- **+1 Strength** (max 20)
- **Heavy armor proficiency**

> [!tip] DM Note
> Niche but powerful for medium-armor classes that want to maximize AC. Most useful for [[01-Cleric-Overview|Clerics]] (non-Life/War domains) or multiclass builds. The STR bump helps offset heavy armor's movement penalty requirement. Competes with just taking a level in [[01-Fighter-Overview|Fighter]] for the same proficiency plus more features.
