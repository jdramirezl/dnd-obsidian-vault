## Original Text

**Observant**

Quick to notice details of your environment, you gain the following benefits:

- Increase your Intelligence or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- If you can see a creature's mouth while it is speaking a language you understand, you can interpret what it's saying by reading its lips.
- You have a +5 bonus to your passive Wisdom (Perception) and passive Intelligence (Investigation) scores.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Sharpen your senses — boost passive Perception and Investigation by +5, read lips, and round off a mental ability score.

- **+1 Intelligence or Wisdom** (max 20)
- **Lip reading:** interpret speech by sight if you know the language
- **+5 passive Perception** and **+5 passive Investigation**

> [!tip] DM Note
> The +5 to passive scores is enormous — a Wisdom-focused character can easily reach passive Perception 20+, detecting nearly all hidden creatures and traps without rolling. Lip reading enables creative espionage in social encounters. Pairs exceptionally well with the [[01-Alert|Alert]] feat for characters who want to never be caught off guard. The +1 WIS also helps round off odd Wisdom scores for [[01-Cleric-Overview|clerics]] and [[01-Druid-Overview|druids]].
