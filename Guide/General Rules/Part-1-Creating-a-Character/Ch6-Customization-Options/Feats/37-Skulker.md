## Original Text

**Skulker**

*Prerequisite: Dexterity 13 or higher*

You are expert at slinking through shadows. You gain the following benefits:

- You can try to hide when you are lightly obscured from the creature from which you are hiding.
- When you are hidden from a creature and miss it with a ranged weapon attack, making the attack doesn't reveal your position.
- Dim light doesn't impose disadvantage on your Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on sight.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Enhanced stealth — hide more easily, stay hidden after missed shots, and see better in dim light.

- **Prerequisite:** Dexterity 13 or higher
- **Hide when lightly obscured** (e.g., fog, foliage, dim light) — normally requires heavy obscurement
- **Missed ranged attacks don't reveal you** — stay hidden even when you miss
- **No Perception disadvantage** in dim light for sight-based checks

> [!tip] DM Note
> Niche but thematic for sniper-style characters. The second bullet is the standout — it lets [[01-Rogue-Overview|rogues]] and [[01-Ranger-Overview|rangers]] keep firing from hiding without risk of exposure on a miss. Note: a *hit* still reveals your position. Pairs well with the [[08-Elf-Subrace-Wood-Elf|Wood Elf]]'s Mask of the Wild or a ranger's Natural Explorer. Generally weaker than other DEX-based feat options like [[34-Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]].
