## Original Text

**Moderately Armored**

*Prerequisite: Proficiency with light armor*

You have trained to master the use of medium armor and shields, gaining the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You gain proficiency with medium armor and shields.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Step up from light to medium armor and shields, with a +1 ability bump — a significant AC boost for lightly armored classes.

- **Prerequisite:** Proficiency with light armor
- **+1 Strength or Dexterity** (max 20)
- **Gain proficiency** with medium armor and shields

> [!tip] DM Note
> Primarily useful for [[01-Rogue-Overview|rogues]], [[01-Warlock-Overview|warlocks]], and [[01-Bard-Overview|bards]] who start with only light armor. Going from studded leather (12 + DEX) to half plate + shield (15 + DEX mod, up to 2, + 2 shield = 19 AC) is a massive defensive jump. The half-feat +1 helps round off an odd ability score. Hexblade warlocks already get medium armor and shields, so this is redundant for them.
