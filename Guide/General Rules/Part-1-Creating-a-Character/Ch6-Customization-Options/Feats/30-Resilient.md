## Original Text

**Resilient**

Choose one ability score. You gain the following benefits:

- Increase the chosen ability score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You gain proficiency in saving throws using the chosen ability.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Shore up a weak saving throw — gain proficiency in one save of your choice plus a +1 to that ability score.

- **Choose one ability score**
- **+1 to the chosen ability** (max 20)
- **Gain saving throw proficiency** in that ability

> [!tip] DM Note
> Constitution is the most popular choice — CON saves maintain concentration on spells and resist many deadly effects (poison, death effects). Wisdom is the second most common pick to defend against charm, fear, and other mind-affecting spells. The value of this feat scales with level as proficiency bonus grows. Particularly strong for spellcasters who need concentration protection but don't want to multiclass.
