## Original Text

**Alert**

Always on the lookout for danger, you gain the following benefits:

- You gain a +5 bonus to initiative.
- You can't be surprised while you are conscious.
- Other creatures don't gain advantage on attack rolls against you as a result of being hidden from you.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Permanent combat awareness — you act first, can't be caught off guard, and hidden enemies gain no attack advantage against you.

- **+5 initiative** bonus
- **Can't be surprised** while conscious
- **Hidden attackers** don't gain advantage on attack rolls against you

> [!tip] DM Note
> One of the strongest feats in the game. Negating surprise is extremely powerful for parties that frequently get ambushed. The initiative bonus alone can shift encounter dynamics, especially for spellcasters who want to land control spells early.
