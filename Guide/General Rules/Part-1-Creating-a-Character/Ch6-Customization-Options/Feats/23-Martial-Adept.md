## Original Text

**Martial Adept**

You have martial training that allows you to perform special combat maneuvers. You gain the following benefits:

- You learn two maneuvers of your choice from among those available to the Battle Master archetype in the fighter class. If a maneuver you use requires your target to make a saving throw to resist the maneuver's effects, the saving throw DC equals 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (your choice).
- You gain one superiority die, which is a d6 (this die is added to any superiority dice you have from another source). This die is used to fuel your maneuvers. A superiority die is expended when you use it. You regain your expended superiority dice when you finish a short or long rest.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Gain a taste of the Battle Master's toolkit — two maneuvers and a single superiority die to fuel them, refreshing on a short rest.

- **Learn 2 maneuvers** from the Battle Master list
- **Gain 1 superiority die (d6)** — added to any existing dice
- **Maneuver save DC:** 8 + proficiency bonus + STR or DEX modifier
- **Recharges** on a short or long rest

> [!tip] DM Note
> A single d6 die limits the impact, but the maneuvers themselves (Trip Attack, Riposte, Precision Attack) can be game-changing at the right moment. Battle Master [[01-Fighter-Overview|fighters]] who take this gain an extra die and two more maneuvers, making it far stronger for them. For non-fighters, it's a fun dip into tactical combat without multiclassing.
