## Original Text

**Ritual Caster**

*Prerequisite: Intelligence or Wisdom 13 or higher*

You have learned a number of spells that you can cast as rituals. These spells are written in a ritual book, which you must have in hand while casting one of them.

When you choose this feat, you acquire a ritual book holding two 1st-level spells of your choice. Choose one of the following classes: bard, cleric, druid, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard. You must choose your spells from that class's spell list, and the spells you choose must have the ritual tag. The class you choose also determines your spellcasting ability for these spells: Charisma for bard, sorcerer, or warlock; Wisdom for cleric or druid; or Intelligence for wizard.

If you come across a spell in written form, such as a magical spell scroll or a wizard's spellbook, you might be able to add it to your ritual book. The spell must be on the spell list for the class you chose, the spell's level can be no higher than half your level (rounded up), and it must have the ritual tag. The process of copying the spell into your ritual book takes 2 hours per spell level and costs 50 gp per spell level for the rare inks needed to inscribe it.

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Gain a ritual book with two 1st-level ritual spells from any one class list, and copy more rituals as you find them — powerful utility without spending spell slots.

- **Prerequisite:** Intelligence or Wisdom 13+
- **Choose a class:** bard, cleric, druid, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard
- **Gain 2 first-level ritual spells** from that class's list (must have ritual tag)
- **Spellcasting ability:** CHA (bard/sorcerer/warlock), WIS (cleric/druid), INT (wizard)
- **Copy new rituals** found in written form:
  - Must be on your chosen class's spell list with the ritual tag
  - Spell level ≤ half your level (rounded up)
  - Cost: **2 hours + 50 gp per spell level**

> [!tip] DM Note
> [[01-Wizard-Overview|Wizard]] is the strongest choice — their ritual list includes [[Detect Magic|*detect magic*]], [[Find Familiar|*find familiar*]], [[Identify|*identify*]], [[Comprehend Languages|*comprehend languages*]], [[Leomunds Tiny Hut|*Leomund's tiny hut*]], and more. This feat gives non-casters access to powerful utility magic at no slot cost. The copying mechanic means the ritual book grows over time, rewarding exploration. Requires the book in hand — losing it means losing access until you can recreate it.
