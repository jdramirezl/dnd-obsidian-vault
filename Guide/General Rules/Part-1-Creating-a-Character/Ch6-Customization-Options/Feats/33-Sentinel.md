## Original Text

**Sentinel**

You have mastered techniques to take advantage of every drop in any enemy's guard, gaining the following benefits:

- When you hit a creature with an opportunity attack, the creature's speed becomes 0 for the rest of the turn.
- Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from you even if they take the Disengage action before leaving your reach.
- When a creature within 5 feet of you makes an attack against a target other than you (and that target doesn't have this feat), you can use your reaction to make a melee weapon attack against the attacking creature.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** The ultimate melee lockdown feat — enemies can't escape you, and you punish them for attacking your allies.

- **OA reduces speed to 0** for the rest of the turn (no escape after being hit)
- **Disengage doesn't work** — creatures still provoke opportunity attacks from you
- **Reaction attack** when a creature within 5 ft attacks someone other than you (target must not also have Sentinel)

> [!tip] DM Note
> One of the best "tank" feats in the game. Combines powerfully with [[29-Polearm-Master|Polearm Master]] (OA when enemies *enter* reach, then speed drops to 0 so they can't reach you). The third bullet is a reaction, so it competes with the OA — you only get one per round. Note the exception: if the other target also has Sentinel, the reaction attack doesn't trigger.
