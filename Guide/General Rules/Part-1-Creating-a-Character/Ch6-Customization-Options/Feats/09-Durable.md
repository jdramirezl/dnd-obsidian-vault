## Original Text

**Durable**

Hardy and resilient, you gain the following benefits:

- Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- When you roll a Hit Die to regain hit points, the minimum number of hit points you regain from the roll equals twice your Constitution modifier (minimum of 2).

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Half-ASI feat (+1 CON) that guarantees a minimum return on Hit Dice spent during short rests.

- **+1 CON** (max 20)
- **Minimum HD healing = 2 × CON modifier** (minimum of 2) per Hit Die rolled

> [!tip] DM Note
> With a +3 CON modifier, the minimum healing per HD becomes 6 (instead of the normal minimum of 4 = 1 + 3). This matters most at low levels with small Hit Dice (d6/d8). In campaigns with frequent short rests, this adds up. Generally considered a weak feat — the +1 CON is often the main draw for rounding an odd score.
