## Original Text

**Great Weapon Master**

You've learned to put the weight of a weapon to your advantage, letting its momentum empower your strikes. You gain the following benefits:

- On your turn, when you score a critical hit with a melee weapon or reduce a creature to 0 hit points with one, you can make one melee weapon attack as a bonus action.
- Before you make a melee attack with a heavy weapon that you are proficient with, you can choose to take a −5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, you add +10 to the attack's damage.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** The quintessential heavy-weapon damage feat — bonus action attack on crits/kills and a high-risk/high-reward power attack option.

- **Bonus action attack** when you score a **critical hit** or **reduce a creature to 0 HP** with a melee weapon
- **Power Attack (−5/+10):** Before a melee attack with a **heavy** weapon you're proficient with, take **−5 to hit** for **+10 damage**

> [!tip] DM Note
> One of the most impactful feats in the game. The −5/+10 trade is almost always worth it against low-AC targets and when the character has advantage (Reckless Attack, [[Faerie Fire|*faerie fire*]], Pack Tactics). At higher levels with larger attack bonuses, the penalty becomes trivial. The bonus action attack on kill/crit rewards cleave-style play against groups of weaker enemies. Pairs exceptionally with [[01-Barbarian-Overview|Barbarian]]'s Reckless Attack and [[01-Fighter-Overview|Fighter]]'s Action Surge.
