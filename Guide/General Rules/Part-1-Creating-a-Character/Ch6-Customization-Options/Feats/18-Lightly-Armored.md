## Original Text

**Lightly Armored**

You have trained to master the use of light armor, gaining the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You gain proficiency with light armor.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Gain light armor proficiency and +1 STR or DEX — for classes that start with no armor proficiency.

- **+1 Strength or Dexterity** (max 20)
- **Light armor proficiency**

> [!tip] DM Note
> Mainly relevant for [[01-Wizard-Overview|Wizards]], [[01-Sorcerer-Overview|Sorcerers]], and [[01-Monk-Overview|Monks]] (though Monks generally prefer Unarmored Defense). Studded leather (12 + DEX) is a meaningful AC upgrade over [[Mage Armor|*mage armor*]] (13 + DEX) only if the character doesn't want to spend a spell slot/known spell on it. Rarely taken since a 1-level multiclass dip often provides more value. The DEX bump makes it a reasonable half-feat choice.
