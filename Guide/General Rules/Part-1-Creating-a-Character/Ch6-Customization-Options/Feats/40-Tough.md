## Original Text

**Tough**

Your hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your level when you gain this feat. Each time you gain a level thereafter, your hit point maximum increases by an additional 2 hit points.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Flat +2 HP per level — equivalent to increasing your Constitution modifier by 2, retroactively and going forward.

- **+2 HP per level** (retroactive to all current levels, plus each future level)
- At level 10, that's +20 HP; at level 20, +40 HP

> [!tip] DM Note
> Simple and effective. Mathematically equivalent to a +4 CON increase purely for HP (but doesn't affect CON saves or concentration checks). Excellent for d6 hit die classes ([[01-Wizard-Overview|wizards]], [[01-Sorcerer-Overview|sorcerers]]) who need survivability. Competes with a CON ASI, but Tough gives more HP if your CON is already at an even number. One of the best "boring but strong" feats.
