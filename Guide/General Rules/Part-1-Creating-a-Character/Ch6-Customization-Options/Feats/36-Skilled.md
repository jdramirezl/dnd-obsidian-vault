## Original Text

**Skilled**

You gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools of your choice.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Gain three skill or tool proficiencies of your choice — pure versatility with no combat benefit.

- **3 proficiencies** in any combination of skills or tools
- No restrictions on which skills or tools you choose

> [!tip] DM Note
> A utility feat with no combat impact. Best for characters in roleplay-heavy or exploration-heavy campaigns where skill checks matter frequently. Competes poorly with ASIs or combat feats in optimised builds, but can fill critical gaps (e.g., a party with no Perception or Thieves' Tools proficiency). Can be taken multiple times.
