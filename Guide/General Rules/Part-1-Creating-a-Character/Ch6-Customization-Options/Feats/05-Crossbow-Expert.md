## Original Text

**Crossbow Expert**

Thanks to extensive practice with the crossbow, you gain the following benefits:

- You ignore the loading quality of crossbows with which you are proficient.
- Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls.
- When you use the Attack action and attack with a one-handed weapon, you can use a bonus action to attack with a loaded hand crossbow you are holding.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Removes all crossbow limitations and enables a bonus action hand crossbow attack — the premier ranged damage feat.

- **Ignore loading** property on proficient crossbows (enables Extra Attack with crossbows)
- **No disadvantage** on ranged attacks within 5 ft of a hostile creature
- **Bonus action attack** with a hand crossbow after attacking with any one-handed weapon

> [!tip] DM Note
> The second bullet applies to *all* ranged attacks, not just crossbows — this includes spell attack rolls. The bonus action attack effectively gives an extra attack each turn, making this one of the strongest damage feats. Note: the hand crossbow must already be loaded and held.
