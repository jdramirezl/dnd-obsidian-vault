## Original Text

**Savage Attacker**

Once per turn when you roll damage for a melee weapon attack, you can reroll the weapon's damage dice and use either total.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Once per turn, reroll melee weapon damage dice and keep the better result — a simple, consistent damage boost.

- **Once per turn**, reroll melee weapon damage dice
- **Use either total** — the original or the reroll
- Applies only to **melee weapon attacks**

> [!tip] DM Note
> Mathematically modest — the average damage increase is small, especially on single-die weapons like a longsword (d8). It scales better with weapons that roll multiple dice (e.g., greatsword 2d6) or when combined with features like Brutal Critical. Generally considered one of the weaker feats compared to alternatives like [[12-Great-Weapon-Master|Great Weapon Master]].
