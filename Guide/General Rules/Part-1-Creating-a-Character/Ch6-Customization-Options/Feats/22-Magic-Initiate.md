## Original Text

**Magic Initiate**

Choose a class: bard, cleric, druid, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard. You learn two cantrips of your choice from that class's spell list.

In addition, choose one 1st-level spell from that same list. You learn that spell and can cast it at its lowest level. Once you cast it, you must finish a long rest before you can cast it again.

Your spellcasting ability for these spells depends on the class you chose: Charisma for bard, sorcerer, or warlock; Wisdom for cleric or druid; or Intelligence for wizard.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Learn 2 cantrips and 1 first-level spell (1/long rest) from any spellcasting class's list.

- **Choose a class:** Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard
- **Learn 2 cantrips** from that class's spell list
- **Learn 1 first-level spell** from the same list — cast once per **long rest** at its lowest level
- **Spellcasting ability:** CHA (Bard/Sorcerer/Warlock), WIS (Cleric/Druid), INT (Wizard)

> [!tip] DM Note
> Extremely versatile feat with many strong options. Popular picks: [[01-Warlock-Overview|Warlock]] list for [[Eldritch Blast|*eldritch blast*]] + [[Hex|*hex*]], [[01-Cleric-Overview|Cleric]] list for [[Guidance|*guidance*]] + [[Healing Word|*healing word*]], [[01-Druid-Overview|Druid]] list for [[Shillelagh|*shillelagh*]] + [[Goodberry|*goodberry*]], [[01-Wizard-Overview|Wizard]] list for [[Find Familiar|*find familiar*]] + utility cantrips. The 1/long rest spell cannot be cast with existing spell slots (RAW) unless it's already on your class list. Great for non-casters wanting utility or casters wanting cross-class options.
