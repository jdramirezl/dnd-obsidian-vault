## Original Text

**Medium Armor Master**

*Prerequisite: Proficiency with medium armor*

You have practiced moving in medium armor to gain the following benefits:

- Wearing medium armor doesn't impose disadvantage on your Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
- When you wear medium armor, you can add 3, rather than 2, to your AC if you have a Dexterity of 16 or higher.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Master medium armor — remove its stealth penalty and squeeze an extra point of AC from high Dexterity.

- **Prerequisite:** Proficiency with medium armor
- **No stealth disadvantage** while wearing medium armor
- **+3 DEX modifier cap** (instead of +2) if your DEX is 16+
  - Half plate: **17 AC** instead of 16

> [!tip] DM Note
> With half plate and 16+ DEX, this yields 18 AC — matching plate armor without stealth disadvantage. Excellent for [[01-Ranger-Overview|rangers]], [[01-Druid-Overview|druids]], and hexblades who invest in Dexterity. The stealth benefit alone makes it worthwhile for any medium-armor wearer who needs to be sneaky.
