# 🏆 Top 10 Feats by Role

> [!info] When Do You Get Feats?
> Feats replace Ability Score Improvements (ASIs), typically at levels 4, 8, 12, 16, and 19. Variant Humans get a bonus feat at level 1. See the [[00-Feats-Index|Feats Index]] for the complete list.

---

## 🛡️ Tank (Frontline Defender)

*Goal: High AC, control the battlefield, protect allies, absorb damage.*

| # | Feat | Why It's Great |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[33-Sentinel\|Sentinel]] | **The** tank feat. OA reduces enemy speed to 0 (they can't escape). Disengage doesn't work against you. Reaction attack when enemies hit your allies. You become a wall. |
| 2 | [[29-Polearm-Master\|Polearm Master]] | OA when enemies *enter* your 10-ft reach. Combined with Sentinel, enemies stop 10 ft away and can never reach you or your backline. The best feat combo in the game. |
| 3 | [[35-Shield-Master\|Shield Master]] | Bonus action shove (knock prone for ally advantage or push off cliffs). Add +2 to DEX saves. Reaction for Evasion-like no-damage on DEX saves. |
| 4 | [[40-Tough\|Tough]] | +2 HP per level. At level 10, that's +20 HP. Simple, effective, always useful. Equivalent to +4 CON for HP only. |
| 5 | [[15-Heavy-Armor-Master\|Heavy Armor Master]] | Reduce all nonmagical bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage by 3. At low levels (1–6), this is incredible — most enemies deal nonmagical damage. Falls off at higher levels when enemies deal magical damage. |
| 6 | [[30-Resilient\|Resilient]] (CON) | Proficiency in CON saves. Protects concentration spells (Paladins, Clerics) and resists poison, death effects. Scales with proficiency bonus. |
| 7 | [[01-Alert\|Alert]] | +5 initiative means you act first to position, cast buffs, or lock down enemies before they scatter. Can't be surprised = no ambush wipes. |
| 8 | [[20-Lucky\|Lucky]] | 3 luck points/day. Turn a failed save into a success, negate a crit against you, or ensure a key attack lands. Universally powerful. |
| 9 | [[14-Heavily-Armored\|Heavily Armored]] | For Clerics/Rangers who want plate armor. +1 STR + heavy armor proficiency. Niche but transformative for the right build. |
| 10 | [[21-Mage-Slayer\|Mage Slayer]] | Reaction melee attack when adjacent caster casts a spell. Disadvantage on their concentration saves. Advantage on saves vs adjacent spells. Situational but devastating vs enemy casters. |

## ⚔️ Damage Dealer (Melee)

*Goal: Maximize damage per round, cleave through enemies, crit hard.*

| # | Feat | Why It's Great |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[12-Great-Weapon-Master\|Great Weapon Master]] | −5 to hit / +10 damage on heavy weapons. Bonus action attack on crit or kill. The single biggest melee damage increase in the game. Use with advantage (Reckless Attack, Faerie Fire) to offset the −5. |
| 2 | [[29-Polearm-Master\|Polearm Master]] | Bonus action d4 attack = extra chance to apply GWM +10 damage. OA on entering reach = more attacks per round. Three attacks at level 5 with a glaive. |
| 3 | [[33-Sentinel\|Sentinel]] | Locks enemies in melee with you. Prevents them from running to your squishy allies. The OA on ally-targeting attacks is a free extra attack. |
| 4 | [[20-Lucky\|Lucky]] | Turn a miss into a hit on your biggest attack. Negate a save that would take you out of the fight. 3/day super-advantage. |
| 5 | [[01-Alert\|Alert]] | Going first means attacking before enemies can spread out. Alpha-strike potential. Barbarians and Fighters with +5 initiative dominate round 1. |
| 6 | [[25-Mobile\|Mobile]] | +10 ft speed. No OA from creatures you attack. Hit-and-run without wasting a bonus action on Disengage. Perfect for Monks and melee Rogues. |
| 7 | [[30-Resilient\|Resilient]] (CON) | Stay conscious through big hits. Maintain concentration on Haste, Holy Weapon, or Hex. Dead damage dealers do zero damage. |
| 8 | [[04-Charger\|Charger]] | — just kidding. Take **[[07-Dual-Wielder\|Dual Wielder]]** instead if you dual-wield: +1 AC, use non-light weapons (1d8+1d8 instead of 1d6+1d6), draw both weapons at once. |
| 9 | [[23-Martial-Adept\|Martial Adept]] | 2 Battle Master maneuvers + 1 superiority die (d6). Trip Attack for prone + advantage, Riposte for reaction attacks. Mini-Battle Master for any martial. |
| 10 | [[40-Tough\|Tough]] | Melee characters take hits. +2 HP/level keeps you standing. Boring but you can't deal damage while unconscious. |

## 🏹 Damage Dealer (Ranged)

*Goal: Maximum ranged DPR, ignore cover, never miss.*

| # | Feat | Why It's Great |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[34-Sharpshooter\|Sharpshooter]] | −5/+10 damage (ranged GWM). Ignore half and ¾ cover. No disadvantage at long range. The #1 ranged damage feat. |
| 2 | [[05-Crossbow-Expert\|Crossbow Expert]] | Ignore loading (Extra Attack works with crossbows). No disadvantage in melee. Bonus action hand crossbow attack = 3 attacks/turn at level 5. Combined with SS = highest sustained DPR in the game. |
| 3 | [[01-Alert\|Alert]] | +5 initiative. Ranged characters want to act first — drop enemies before they close distance. |
| 4 | [[20-Lucky\|Lucky]] | Ensure your −5/+10 Sharpshooter shot hits when it matters most. |
| 5 | [[37-Skulker\|Skulker]] | Hide when lightly obscured. Missed ranged attacks don't reveal your position. No Perception penalty in dim light. The stealth-archer feat. |
| 6 | [[25-Mobile\|Mobile]] | +10 ft speed to maintain distance. No OA from creatures you attack in melee (escape if they close in). |
| 7 | [[30-Resilient\|Resilient]] (CON/WIS) | CON for concentration (Hunter's Mark, Hex). WIS for the most common save-or-suck effects. |
| 8 | [[10-Elemental-Adept\|Elemental Adept]] | For Arcane Archer or spell-using Rangers. Ignore resistance, treat 1s as 2s. Fire is the best choice. |
| 9 | [[07-Dual-Wielder\|Dual Wielder]] | Niche: draw two hand crossbows. Mostly outclassed by Crossbow Expert. |
| 10 | [[40-Tough\|Tough]] | Ranged characters still get hit by AoE and stray attacks. Extra HP is always welcome. |

## 🔮 Caster (Spellcaster)

*Goal: Maintain concentration, land spells, survive long enough to cast.*

| # | Feat | Why It's Great |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[41-War-Caster\|War Caster]] | Advantage on concentration saves. Cast with hands full (sword + shield). Cast a spell as an OA (Booming Blade!). The #1 caster feat for anyone in melee range. |
| 2 | [[30-Resilient\|Resilient]] (CON) | Proficiency in CON saves. At higher levels (prof +4/+5), this beats War Caster's advantage for concentration. Take War Caster early, Resilient later. |
| 3 | [[20-Lucky\|Lucky]] | Save a failed concentration check. Pass a crucial save. Ensure a key spell attack hits. 3/day insurance policy. |
| 4 | [[01-Alert\|Alert]] | +5 initiative. Casters want to go first to drop control spells (Hypnotic Pattern, Web, Fireball) before enemies spread out. Going first = winning the encounter. |
| 5 | [[38-Spell-Sniper\|Spell Sniper]] | Double spell attack range. Ignore half/¾ cover. Learn one attack cantrip. Great for Eldritch Blast warlocks (300 ft range!) and ranged casters. |
| 6 | [[10-Elemental-Adept\|Elemental Adept]] (Fire) | Ignore fire resistance (the most common resistance). Treat 1s as 2s on Fireball, Scorching Ray, etc. Best for Evocation Wizards and Draconic Sorcerers. |
| 7 | [[22-Magic-Initiate\|Magic Initiate]] | 2 cantrips + 1 first-level spell from another class. Cleric list for Guidance + Healing Word. Warlock list for Eldritch Blast. Wizard list for Find Familiar. |
| 8 | [[31-Ritual-Caster\|Ritual Caster]] | Cast ritual spells without spell slots. Find Familiar, Detect Magic, Comprehend Languages — all free. Best for non-Wizards who want utility. |
| 9 | [[40-Tough\|Tough]] | d6 hit die casters are fragile. +2 HP/level is the difference between consciousness and death saves. |
| 10 | [[16-Inspiring-Leader\|Inspiring Leader]] | 10-min speech = temp HP (level + CHA mod) for 6 allies. At level 10 with +4 CHA, that's 84 total party HP before every fight. Best on CHA casters (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock). |

## 🎭 Support (Healer, Buffer, Face)

*Goal: Keep allies alive, buff the party, dominate social encounters.*

| # | Feat | Why It's Great |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[16-Inspiring-Leader\|Inspiring Leader]] | Temp HP for the whole party, refreshing on short rest. Scales with level. The best non-spell party buff in the game. |
| 2 | [[41-War-Caster\|War Caster]] | Maintain concentration on Bless, Spirit Guardians, Haste — the spells that define support casters. |
| 3 | [[30-Resilient\|Resilient]] (CON) | Same reason — protect your concentration spells. |
| 4 | [[20-Lucky\|Lucky]] | Save a failed concentration check on Bless. Pass the save against the dragon's breath. Universal insurance. |
| 5 | [[13-Healer\|Healer]] | With a Healer's Kit: stabilize + restore 1 HP, OR heal 1d6+4+creature's max HD as an action. No spell slots needed. Incredible for non-caster supports or when spell slots run dry. |
| 6 | [[01-Alert\|Alert]] | Go first to cast Bless/Bane before the first round of attacks. Support spells are most valuable when cast early. |
| 7 | [[22-Magic-Initiate\|Magic Initiate]] | Grab Healing Word (Cleric) for emergency ranged healing. Or Guidance (Cleric) for +1d4 to every ability check. |
| 8 | [[03-Actor\|Actor]] | +1 CHA, advantage on Deception/Performance to impersonate, mimic speech. The ultimate social infiltration feat for Bards and face characters. |
| 9 | [[28-Observant\|Observant]] | +5 passive Perception and Investigation. Spot traps, ambushes, and hidden details before anyone else. Read lips across a room. |
| 10 | [[31-Ritual-Caster\|Ritual Caster]] | Free utility spells without slots. Detect Magic, Comprehend Languages, Speak with Animals — all at no cost. |

## 🔍 Skill Monkey (Exploration, Utility, Out-of-Combat)

*Goal: Succeed on every check, know everything, go everywhere.*

| # | Feat | Why It's Great |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[20-Lucky\|Lucky]] | Failed a crucial skill check? Reroll it. 3/day. Works on everything. The best "skill" feat because it applies to all checks. |
| 2 | [[36-Skilled\|Skilled]] | 3 skill or tool proficiencies. Proficiency bonus on 3 more things. Simple, effective, fills gaps in your skill list. |
| 3 | [[28-Observant\|Observant]] | +5 passive Perception and Investigation. You notice everything. Lip reading lets you eavesdrop from across a room. +1 INT or WIS. |
| 4 | [[03-Actor\|Actor]] | +1 CHA, advantage on Deception/Performance for impersonation, mimic voices. Turns social encounters into playgrounds. |
| 5 | [[01-Alert\|Alert]] | Can't be surprised. Hidden creatures don't get advantage. You're always aware. Pairs with high Perception for the ultimate scout. |
| 6 | [[02-Athlete\|Athlete]] | Stand from prone for 5 ft. Running jump from 5 ft. Climbing costs no extra movement. +1 STR/DEX. The exploration feat. |
| 7 | [[08-Dungeon-Delver\|Dungeon Delver]] | Advantage on Perception/Investigation for traps and secret doors. Resistance to trap damage. Search at normal travel pace. The dungeon-crawl specialist. |
| 8 | [[17-Keen-Mind\|Keen Mind]] | +1 INT. Always know north and time. Recall anything from the past month. Great for note-taking-averse players. |
| 9 | [[19-Linguist\|Linguist]] | +1 INT, 3 languages, create written ciphers. Niche but invaluable in intrigue campaigns. |
| 10 | [[31-Ritual-Caster\|Ritual Caster]] | Detect Magic, Comprehend Languages, Find Familiar (scout!), Water Breathing — all free. The best utility feat for non-casters. |
