## Original Text

**Weapon Master**

You have practiced extensively with a variety of weapons, gaining the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You gain proficiency with four weapons of your choice. Each one must be a simple or a martial weapon.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** A half-feat granting +1 STR or DEX and four weapon proficiencies — niche, for classes that lack martial weapon access.

- **+1 Strength or Dexterity** (max 20)
- **Proficiency with 4 weapons** of your choice (simple or martial)

> [!tip] DM Note
> One of the weakest feats in the game. Most martial classes already have all weapon proficiencies, and multiclassing typically grants them more efficiently. Only relevant for classes like [[01-Wizard-Overview|wizard]], [[01-Sorcerer-Overview|sorcerer]], or [[01-Druid-Overview|druid]] who want a specific martial weapon (e.g., a druid wanting a longsword). The +1 ASI is the main draw, with the proficiencies as a minor bonus. Almost always outclassed by a racial trait or a one-level multiclass dip.
