## Original Text

**Elemental Adept**

*Prerequisite: The ability to cast at least one spell*

When you gain this feat, choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.

Spells you cast ignore resistance to damage of the chosen type. In addition, when you roll damage for a spell you cast that deals damage of that type, you can treat any 1 on a damage die as a 2.

You can select this feat multiple times. Each time you do so, you must choose a different damage type.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Specialise in one elemental damage type — your spells bypass resistance and roll slightly higher damage. One of the few feats that can be taken multiple times.

- **Prerequisite:** Ability to cast at least one spell
- **Choose one type:** acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder
- **Ignore resistance** to the chosen damage type on your spells
- **Treat 1s as 2s** on damage dice for spells of that type
- **Repeatable** — take again for a different element

> [!tip] DM Note
> Fire is the most commonly resisted element, making it the strongest choice. Note: this does *not* overcome immunity, only resistance. The 1→2 reroll is minor but adds up on spells with many dice (e.g., [[Fireball|*fireball*]]). Evocation [[01-Wizard-Overview|wizards]] and draconic [[01-Sorcerer-Overview|sorcerers]] benefit most from specialising in a single element.
