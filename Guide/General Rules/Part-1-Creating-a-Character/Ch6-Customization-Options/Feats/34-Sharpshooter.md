## Original Text

**Sharpshooter**

You have mastered ranged weapons and can make shots that others find impossible. You gain the following benefits:

- Attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged weapon attack rolls.
- Your ranged weapon attacks ignore half cover and three-quarters cover.
- Before you make an attack with a ranged weapon that you are proficient with, you can choose to take a −5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, you add +10 to the attack's damage.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** The ranged equivalent of Great Weapon Master — removes range and cover penalties and offers a massive −5/+10 damage trade.

- **No disadvantage** at long range for ranged weapon attacks
- **Ignore half and three-quarters cover** on ranged weapon attacks
- **−5 to hit / +10 damage** option on proficient ranged weapon attacks (declared before the roll)

> [!tip] DM Note
> Arguably the strongest ranged feat. The −5/+10 trade is almost always worth it when you have advantage or the target's AC is low. Ignoring cover removes one of the few defences NPCs have against ranged attackers. Pairs extremely well with the Archery fighting style (+2 to hit offsets the −5 penalty) and [[05-Crossbow-Expert|Crossbow Expert]] for bonus action attacks.
