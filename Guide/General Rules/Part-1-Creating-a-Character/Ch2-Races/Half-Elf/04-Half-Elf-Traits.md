# Half-Elf Traits

## Original Text

Your half-elf character has some qualities in common with elves and some that are unique to half-elves.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 2, and two other ability scores of your choice increase by 1.

**Age.** Half-elves mature at the same rate humans do and reach adulthood around the age of 20. They live much longer than humans, however, often exceeding 180 years.

**Alignment.** Half-elves share the chaotic bent of their elven heritage. They value both personal freedom and creative expression, demonstrating neither love of leaders nor desire for followers. They chafe at rules, resent others' demands, and sometimes prove unreliable, or at least unpredictable.

**Size.** Half-elves are about the same size as humans, ranging from 5 to 6 feet tall. Your size is Medium.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

**Darkvision.** Thanks to your elf blood, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Fey Ancestry.** You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.

**Skill Versatility.** You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common, Elvish, and one extra language of your choice.


## Condensed Version

**Half-Elf Racial Traits**: Mechanical abilities all half-elves possess.

### Core Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|Ability Scores]]**
- Charisma +2
- Two other ability scores of your choice +1 each

**Age**
- **Maturity**: Same rate as humans
- **Adulthood**: Around age 20
- **Lifespan**: Often exceeding 180 years

**Alignment**
- **Typical**: Chaotic
- **Values**: Personal freedom and creative expression
- **Neither** love of leaders nor desire for followers
- Chafe at rules, resent demands
- Sometimes unreliable or unpredictable

**Size**
- **Height**: 5 to 6 feet tall (about the same as humans)
- **Size category**: Medium

**Speed**
- **Base walking speed**: 30 feet

### Special Abilities

**Darkvision** (60 feet)
- See in dim light as if bright light
- See in darkness as if dim light
- Can't discern color in darkness (only shades of gray)
- Source: Elf blood

**Fey Ancestry**
- **Advantage** on [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] against being [[Charmed|charmed]]
- **Magic can't put you to sleep**

**Skill Versatility**
- Gain proficiency in **two skills** of your choice
- No restrictions on which skills

**Languages**
- Common, Elvish, and one extra language of your choice

**Key Concept**: Half-elves are one of the most versatile races mechanically. The Charisma +2 plus two flexible +1s, two free skill proficiencies, and darkvision make them excellent at nearly any class — especially Charisma-based casters like [[01-Bard-Overview|bards]], [[01-Sorcerer-Overview|sorcerers]], [[01-Warlock-Overview|warlocks]], and [[01-Paladin-Overview|paladins]]. No subraces.
