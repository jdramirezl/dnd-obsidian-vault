# Dwarf Subrace - Hill Dwarf

## Original Text

As a hill dwarf, you have keen senses, deep intuition, and remarkable resilience. The gold dwarves of Faerûn in their mighty southern kingdom are hill dwarves, as are the exiled Neidar and the debased Klar of Krynn in the Dragonlance setting.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Dwarven Toughness.** Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.


## Condensed Version

**Hill Dwarf Identity**: Dwarves with keen senses, deep intuition, and exceptional resilience.

### Examples in Campaign Settings

**Forgotten Realms**: Gold dwarves of the southern kingdom

**Dragonlance**: 
- Exiled Neidar
- Debased Klar

### Hill Dwarf Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Wisdom +1
- *Combined with base dwarf*: Constitution +2, Wisdom +1

**Dwarven Toughness**
- [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] maximum increases by 1 at 1st level
- Increases by 1 every time you gain a level
- **Total bonus**: +1 HP per character level (including 1st)

**Example**:
- 1st level: +1 HP
- 5th level: +5 HP total
- 10th level: +10 HP total
- 20th level: +20 HP total

### Complete Hill Dwarf Traits Summary

**From Base Dwarf**:
- Constitution +2
- Darkvision 60 ft
- Dwarven Resilience (poison advantage/resistance)
- Dwarven Combat Training
- Tool Proficiency (choose one)
- Stonecunning
- Languages: Common, Dwarvish
- Speed: 25 ft (not reduced by heavy [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]])

**From Hill Dwarf Subrace**:
- Wisdom +1
- Dwarven Toughness (+1 HP per level)

**Key Concept**: Hill dwarves are the toughest dwarf subrace, gaining bonus [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] at every level. The Wisdom bonus makes them well-suited for [[01-Cleric-Overview|cleric]] and other Wisdom-based classes.
