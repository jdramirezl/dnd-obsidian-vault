# Dwarf Subrace - Mountain Dwarf

## Original Text

As a mountain dwarf, you're strong and hardy, accustomed to a difficult life in rugged terrain. You're probably on the tall side (for a dwarf), and tend toward lighter coloration. The shield dwarves of northern Faerûn, as well as the ruling Hylar clan and the noble Daewar clan of Dragonlance, are mountain dwarves.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 2.

**Dwarven Armor Training.** You have proficiency with light and medium armor.


## Condensed Version

**Mountain Dwarf Identity**: Strong, hardy dwarves accustomed to difficult life in rugged mountain terrain.

### Physical Characteristics

**Build**: Tall for a dwarf

**Coloration**: Tend toward lighter skin tones

### Examples in Campaign Settings

**Forgotten Realms**: shield dwarves of northern Faerûn

**Dragonlance**:
- Ruling Hylar clan
- Noble Daewar clan

### Mountain Dwarf Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Strength +2
- *Combined with base dwarf*: Constitution +2, Strength +2

**Dwarven [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] Training**
- Proficiency with light armor
- Proficiency with medium armor

### Complete Mountain Dwarf Traits Summary

**From Base Dwarf**:
- Constitution +2
- Darkvision 60 ft
- Dwarven Resilience (poison advantage/resistance)
- Dwarven Combat Training (battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, warhammer)
- Tool Proficiency (choose one)
- Stonecunning
- Languages: Common, Dwarvish
- Speed: 25 ft (not reduced by heavy [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]])

**From Mountain Dwarf Subrace**:
- Strength +2
- Dwarven Armor Training (light and medium armor)

### Why This Matters

**Exceptional [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**: Mountain dwarves get +2 to TWO abilities (Con and Str)
- Makes them incredibly tough and strong
- Rare among races

**[[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] Flexibility**: Light and medium armor proficiency allows:
- [[01-Wizard-Overview|wizard]] to wear armor without multiclassing
- More build variety
- Mountain dwarf wizards are viable and interesting

**Key Concept**: Mountain dwarves are the strongest dwarf subrace, with exceptional ability score bonuses (+2 Con, +2 Str) and armor proficiency that opens unique character build options, especially for typically unarmored classes like wizards.
