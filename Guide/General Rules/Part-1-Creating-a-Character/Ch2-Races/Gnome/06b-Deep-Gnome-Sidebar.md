# Gnome - Deep Gnomes (Svirfneblin)

## Original Text

Deep Gnomes

A third subrace of gnomes, the deep gnomes (or svirfneblin), live in small communities scattered in the Underdark. Unlike the duergar and the drow, svirfneblin are as good as their surface cousins. However, their humor and enthusiasm are dampened by their oppressive environment, and their inventive expertise is directed mostly toward stonework.


---

## Condensed Version

**Deep Gnomes (Svirfneblin)**: A subterranean gnome subrace living in the Underdark.

- **Also known as**: Svirfneblin
- **Habitat**: Small communities scattered in the Underdark
- **Alignment**: Good (unlike duergar and [[06b-Darkness-of-the-Drow|drow]])
- **Personality**: Humor and enthusiasm dampened by oppressive environment
- **Expertise**: Inventive skills directed mostly toward stonework

**Key Concept**: Deep gnomes are the Underdark counterpart to surface gnomes—retaining their good nature but subdued by their harsh environment. Unlike other Underdark races (duergar, drow), they remain good-aligned.

## 🔧 Homebrew: Svirfneblin Stonework Devices

> **DM Note**: The PHB mentions Deep Gnomes direct their inventive expertise toward stonework, but provides no mechanics. These homebrew devices mirror the [[06-Gnome-Subrace-Rock|Rock Gnome Tinker]] trait: **1 hour + 10 gp to craft, lasts 24 hours (1 hour repair to extend), AC 5 / 1 HP, max 3 active at a time.** No damage, no combat advantage — utility and flavor only.

| Device | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Echo Stone** | When placed, repeats the last phrase spoken into it when touched again |
| **Tremor Pebble** | Vibrates when a Large or larger creature walks within 30 ft (primitive alarm) |
| **Glow Crystal** | Emits dim light in a 5-ft radius (no flame, safe underground) |
| **Camouflage Dust** | Enough powdered stone to grant advantage on one Stealth check in rocky terrain |
| **Mapping Stone** | Flat stone that, when pressed against a wall, leaves a faint mark visible only to darkvision |
| **Resonance Chime** | Small stone tuning fork that hums near hollow walls or hidden passages (within 5 ft) |

### Creative Uses
- **Echo Stone** → leave messages for allies, distract guards with voices
- **Tremor Pebble** → early warning system while resting in the Underdark
- **Glow Crystal** → mark paths without torches (won't alert darkvision-only creatures at range)
- **Resonance Chime** → find secret doors without burning spell slots on *Detect Magic*
