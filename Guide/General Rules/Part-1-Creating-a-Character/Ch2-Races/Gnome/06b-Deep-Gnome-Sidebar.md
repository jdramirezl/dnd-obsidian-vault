# Gnome - Deep Gnomes (Svirfneblin)

## Original Text

Deep Gnomes

A third subrace of gnomes, the deep gnomes (or svirfneblin), live in small communities scattered in the Underdark. Unlike the duergar and the drow, svirfneblin are as good as their surface cousins. However, their humor and enthusiasm are dampened by their oppressive environment, and their inventive expertise is directed mostly toward stonework.


---

## Condensed Version

**Deep Gnomes (Svirfneblin)**: A subterranean gnome subrace living in the Underdark.

- **Also known as**: Svirfneblin
- **Habitat**: Small communities scattered in the Underdark
- **Alignment**: Good (unlike duergar and [[06b-Darkness-of-the-Drow|drow]])
- **Personality**: Humor and enthusiasm dampened by oppressive environment
- **Expertise**: Inventive skills directed mostly toward stonework

**Key Concept**: Deep gnomes are the Underdark counterpart to surface gnomes—retaining their good nature but subdued by their harsh environment. Unlike other Underdark races (duergar, drow), they remain good-aligned.
