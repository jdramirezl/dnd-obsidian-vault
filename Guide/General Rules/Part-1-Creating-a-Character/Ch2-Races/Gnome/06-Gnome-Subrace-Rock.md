# Gnome Subrace - Rock Gnome

## Original Text

As a rock gnome, you have a natural inventiveness and hardiness beyond that of other gnomes. Most gnomes in the worlds of D&D are rock gnomes, including the tinker gnomes of the Dragonlance setting.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Artificer's Lore.** Whenever you make an Intelligence (History) check related to magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices, you can add twice your proficiency bonus, instead of any proficiency bonus you normally apply.

**Tinker.** You have proficiency with artisan's tools (tinker's tools). Using those tools, you can spend 1 hour and 10 gp worth of materials to construct a Tiny clockwork device (AC 5, 1 hp). The device ceases to function after 24 hours (unless you spend 1 hour repairing it to keep the device functioning), or when you use your action to dismantle it; at that time, you can reclaim the materials used to create it. You can have up to three such devices active at a time.

When you create a device, choose one of the following options:

**Clockwork Toy.** This toy is a clockwork animal, monster, or person, such as a frog, mouse, bird, dragon, or soldier. When placed on the ground, the toy moves 5 feet across the ground on each of your turns in a random direction. It makes noises as appropriate to the creature it represents.

**Fire Starter.** The device produces a miniature flame, which you can use to light a candle, torch, or campfire. Using the device requires your action.

**Music Box.** When opened, this music box plays a single song at a moderate volume. The box stops playing when it reaches the song's end or when it is closed.

## Condensed Version

**Rock Gnome Identity**: The most common gnome subrace, known for natural inventiveness and hardiness.

### Culture

- **Prevalence**: Most gnomes in D&D worlds are rock gnomes
- **Dragonlance**: Includes the tinker gnomes of that setting
- **Defining trait**: Natural inventiveness and hardiness

### Rock Gnome Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Constitution +1
- *Combined with base gnome*: Intelligence +2, Constitution +1

**Artificer's Lore**
- For Intelligence (History) checks about magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices:
  - Add **double** [[03-Proficiency-Bonus|proficiency bonus]] (instead of normal)

**Tinker**
- Proficiency with artisan's tools (tinker's tools)
- **Construct Tiny Clockwork Devices**:
  - Cost: 1 hour + 10 gp materials
  - Stats: AC 5, 1 hp
  - Duration: 24 hours (or 1 hour repair to extend)
  - Dismantle: Use action, reclaim materials
  - Limit: Up to **3 active** at a time

**Device Options**:

| Device | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Clockwork Toy** | Moves 5 ft. random direction each turn, makes creature noises |
| **Fire Starter** | Produces miniature flame (light candle/torch/campfire), requires action |
| **Music Box** | Plays a single song at moderate volume until end or closed |

### Complete Rock Gnome Traits Summary

**From Base Gnome**:
- Intelligence +2
- Darkvision 60 ft
- Gnome Cunning (advantage on INT/WIS/CHA saves vs. magic)
- Languages: Common, Gnomish
- Speed: 25 ft
- Size: Small

**From Rock Gnome Subrace**:
- Constitution +1
- Artificer's Lore (double proficiency on relevant History checks)
- Tinker (construct clockwork devices)

**Key Concept**: Rock gnomes are the inventive, hardy subrace—combining Constitution with expertise in magical/technological lore and the ability to build clockwork devices. The Tinker feature provides creative utility and roleplaying opportunities, while Artificer's Lore makes them excellent at identifying magical and technological objects.
