# Gnome Traits

## Original Text

Your gnome character has certain characteristics in common with all other gnomes.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 2.

**Age.** Gnomes mature at the same rate humans do, and most are expected to settle down into an adult life by around age 40. They can live 350 to almost 500 years.

**Alignment.** Gnomes are most often good. Those who tend toward law are sages, engineers, researchers, scholars, investigators, or inventors. Those who tend toward chaos are minstrels, tricksters, wanderers, or fanciful jewelers. Gnomes are good-hearted, and even the tricksters among them are more playful than vicious.

**Size.** Gnomes are between 3 and 4 feet tall and average about 40 pounds. Your size is Small.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 25 feet.

**Darkvision.** Accustomed to life underground, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Gnome Cunning.** You have advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Gnomish. The Gnomish language, which uses the Dwarvish script, is renowned for its technical treatises and its catalogs of knowledge about the natural world.

**Subrace.** Two subraces of gnomes are found among the worlds of D&D: forest gnomes and rock gnomes. Choose one of these subraces.

## Condensed Version

**Gnome Racial Traits**: Mechanical abilities all gnomes possess.

### Core Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Intelligence +2

**Age**
- **Maturity**: Same rate as humans
- **Adulthood**: Expected to settle down around age 40
- **Lifespan**: 350–500 years

**Alignment**
- **Typical**: Good
- **Lawful**: Sages, engineers, researchers, scholars, investigators, inventors
- **Chaotic**: Minstrels, tricksters, wanderers, fanciful jewelers
- Good-hearted; even tricksters are more playful than vicious

**Size**
- **Height**: 3–4 feet tall
- **Weight**: Average 40 pounds
- **Size category**: Small

**Speed**
- **Base walking speed**: 25 feet

### Special Abilities

**Darkvision** (60 feet)
- See in dim light as if bright light
- See in darkness as if dim light
- Can't discern color in darkness (only shades of gray)
- Reason: Accustomed to underground life

**Gnome Cunning**
- **Advantage** on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] against magic

**Languages**
- Common and Gnomish
- **Gnomish**: Uses Dwarvish script
- Renowned for technical treatises and natural world catalogs

**Subrace**
- Must choose: [[05-Gnome-Subrace-Forest|Forest gnome]] or [[06-Gnome-Subrace-Rock|Rock gnome]]
- Subrace grants additional traits

**Key Concept**: Gnomes are naturally clever (Intelligence bonus), adapted to underground life (darkvision), and exceptionally resistant to magic (Gnome Cunning). Their Small size and 25-foot speed reflect their diminutive stature, while Gnome Cunning is one of the strongest racial defensive features in the game.
