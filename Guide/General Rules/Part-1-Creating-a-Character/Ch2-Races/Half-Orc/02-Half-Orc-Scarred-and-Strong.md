# Half-Orc - The Mark of Gruumsh

## Original Text

The one-eyed god Gruumsh created the orcs, and even those orcs who turn away from his worship can't fully escape his influence. The same is true of half-orcs, though their human blood moderates the impact of their orcish heritage. Some half-orcs hear the whispers of Gruumsh in their dreams, calling them to unleash the rage that simmers within them. Others feel Gruumsh's exultation when they join in melee combat—and either exult along with him or shiver with fear and loathing. Half-orcs are not evil by nature, but evil does lurk within them, whether they embrace it or rebel against it.

Beyond the rage of Gruumsh, half-orcs feel emotion powerfully. Rage doesn't just quicken their pulse, it makes their bodies burn. An insult stings like acid, and sadness saps their strength. But they laugh loudly and heartily, and simple bodily pleasures—feasting, drinking, wrestling, drumming, and wild dancing—fill their hearts with joy. They tend to be short-tempered and sometimes sullen, more inclined to action than contemplation and to fighting than arguing. The most accomplished half-orcs are those with enough self-control to get by in a civilized land.

## Grudging Acceptance

Each half-orc finds a way to gain acceptance from those who hate orcs. Some are reserved, trying not to draw attention to themselves. A few demonstrate piety and good-heartedness as publicly as they can (whether or not such demonstrations are genuine). And some simply try to be so tough that others just avoid bothering them.


## Condensed Version

**The Mark of Gruumsh**: The orcish god's influence on half-orcs and their intense emotional nature.

### Gruumsh's Influence

**The One-Eyed God**:
- Gruumsh created the orcs
- Even orcs who reject his worship can't fully escape his influence
- Half-orcs feel this too, though human blood moderates the impact

**How It Manifests**:
- Some hear Gruumsh's whispers in dreams — calling them to unleash inner rage
- Others feel his exultation during melee combat
  - Some exult along with him
  - Others shiver with fear and loathing

**Moral Nature**:
- Half-orcs are **not evil by nature**
- But evil does lurk within them
- Each must choose: embrace it or rebel against it

### Emotional Intensity

**Half-Orcs Feel Everything Powerfully**:
- **Rage**: Doesn't just quicken the pulse — makes their bodies burn
- **Insults**: Sting like acid
- **Sadness**: Saps their strength
- **Joy**: Laugh loudly and heartily

**Simple Pleasures** (fill their hearts with joy):
- Feasting, drinking, wrestling
- Drumming and wild dancing

**Temperament**:
- Short-tempered, sometimes sullen
- More inclined to action than contemplation
- More inclined to fighting than arguing
- Most accomplished half-orcs have enough self-control for civilized lands

### Grudging Acceptance

**Strategies for Gaining Acceptance** (in societies that hate orcs):
- **The Reserved**: Try not to draw attention to themselves
- **The Pious**: Demonstrate piety and good-heartedness publicly (genuine or not)
- **The Tough**: Be so intimidating that others avoid bothering them

**Key Concept**: Half-orcs carry Gruumsh's influence as an inner tension — a simmering rage they must manage. Their emotional intensity is both their greatest strength and greatest challenge. In civilized society, each half-orc must find their own strategy for acceptance.
