# Elf Subrace - Dark Elf (Drow)

## Original Text

Descended from an earlier subrace of dark-skinned elves, the drow were banished from the surface world for following the goddess Lolth down the path to evil and corruption. Now they have built their own civilization in the depths of the Underdark, patterned after the Way of Lolth. Also called dark elves, the drow have black skin that resembles polished obsidian and stark white or pale yellow hair. They commonly have very pale eyes (so pale as to be mistaken for white) in shades of lilac, silver, pink, red, and blue. They tend to be smaller and thinner than most elves.

Drow adventurers are rare, and the race does not exist in all worlds. Check with your Dungeon Master to see if you can play a drow character.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Superior Darkvision.** Your darkvision has a radius of 120 feet.

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** You have disadvantage on attack rolls and on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.

**Drow Magic.** You know the *dancing lights* cantrip. When you reach 3rd level, you can cast the *faerie fire* spell once per day. When you reach 5th level, you can also cast the *darkness* spell once per day. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

**Drow Weapon Training.** You have proficiency with rapiers, shortswords, and hand crossbows.

## Condensed Version

**Dark Elf (Drow) Identity**: Elves banished to the Underdark for following the goddess Lolth into evil and corruption.

### Physical Characteristics

**Skin**: Black, resembling polished obsidian

**Hair**: Stark white or pale yellow

**Eyes**: Very pale (often mistaken for white) — lilac, silver, pink, red, or blue

**Build**: Smaller and thinner than most elves

### Dark Elf Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Charisma +1
- *Combined with base elf*: Dexterity +2, Charisma +1

**Superior Darkvision** (120 feet)
- Double the range of standard elf darkvision (60 ft)
- Adapted to the lightless depths of the Underdark

**Sunlight Sensitivity** ⚠️
- **Disadvantage** on attack rolls in direct sunlight
- **Disadvantage** on Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on sight in direct sunlight
- Applies when you, your target, or what you're perceiving is in direct sunlight
- *Major drawback for surface adventuring*

**Drow Magic** (Charisma-based)
- **Level 1**: *dancing lights* cantrip (at will)
- **Level 3**: *faerie fire* (1/day)
- **Level 5**: *darkness* (1/day)
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma

**Drow [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Training**
- Proficiency with: rapier, shortsword, hand crossbow
- *Note: Different weapons than high elf/wood elf training*

### Complete Dark Elf Traits Summary

**From Base Elf**:
- Dexterity +2
- Darkvision 60 ft (upgraded to 120 ft by subrace)
- Keen Senses (Perception proficiency)
- Fey Ancestry (advantage vs charm, can't be magically slept)
- Trance (4-hour rest)
- Languages: Common, Elvish
- Speed: 30 ft

**From Dark Elf Subrace**:
- Charisma +1
- Superior Darkvision (120 ft)
- Sunlight Sensitivity (disadvantage in sunlight)
- Drow Magic (dancing lights, faerie fire, darkness)
- Drow Weapon Training (rapier, shortsword, hand crossbow)

**Key Concept**: Drow trade the surface-friendly traits of other elf subraces for powerful Underdark adaptations. Superior Darkvision and innate magic make them formidable in darkness, but Sunlight Sensitivity is a significant penalty for surface campaigns. The Charisma bonus and spell progression make them natural warlocks, sorcerers, and bards. Check with your DM before playing a drow — they don't exist in all settings.
