# Elf Subrace - High Elf

## Original Text

As a high elf, you have a keen mind and a mastery of at least the basics of magic. In many of the worlds of D&D, there are two kinds of high elves. One type (which includes the gray elves and valley elves of Greyhawk, the Silvanesti of Dragonlance, and the sun elves of the Forgotten Realms) is haughty and reclusive, believing themselves to be superior to non-elves and even other elves. The other type (including the high elves of Greyhawk, the Qualinesti of Dragonlance, and the moon elves of the Forgotten Realms) are more common and more friendly, and often encountered among humans and other races.

The sun elves of Faerûn (also called gold elves or sunrise elves) have bronze skin and hair of copper, black, or golden blond. Their eyes are golden, silver, or black. Moon elves (also called silver elves or gray elves) are much paler, with alabaster skin sometimes tinged with blue. They often have hair of silver-white, black, or blue, but various shades of blond, brown, and red are not uncommon. Their eyes are blue or green and flecked with gold.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Elf Weapon Training.** You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.

**Cantrip.** You know one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.

**Extra Language.** You can speak, read, and write one extra language of your choice.


## Condensed Version

**High Elf Identity**: Elves with keen minds and mastery of basic magic.

### Two Types of High Elves

**Type 1 - Haughty and Reclusive**:
- Believe themselves superior to non-elves and even other elves
- Examples: Gray elves (Greyhawk), Valley elves (Greyhawk), Silvanesti (Dragonlance), Sun elves (Forgotten Realms)

**Type 2 - Friendly and Common**:
- More sociable, often found among humans and other races
- Examples: High elves (Greyhawk), Qualinesti (Dragonlance), Moon elves (Forgotten Realms)

### Physical Variations (Forgotten Realms Examples)

**Sun Elves** (Gold Elves, Sunrise Elves):
- **Skin**: Bronze
- **Hair**: Copper, black, or golden blond
- **Eyes**: Golden, silver, or black

**Moon Elves** (Silver Elves, Gray Elves):
- **Skin**: Alabaster (sometimes tinged with blue)
- **Hair**: Silver-white, black, or blue (also blond, brown, red)
- **Eyes**: Blue or green, flecked with gold

### High Elf Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Intelligence +1
- *Combined with base elf*: Dexterity +2, Intelligence +1

**Elf [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Training**
- Proficiency with: longsword, shortsword, shortbow, longbow

**Cantrip**
- Know one cantrip from [[01-Wizard-Overview|wizard]] spell list
- Intelligence is spellcasting ability
- Always available (cantrips don't use spell slots)

**Extra Language**
- Speak, read, and write one additional language of your choice

### Complete High Elf Traits Summary

**From Base Elf**:
- Dexterity +2
- Darkvision 60 ft
- Keen Senses (Perception proficiency)
- Fey Ancestry (advantage vs charm, can't be magically slept)
- Trance (4-hour rest)
- Languages: Common, Elvish
- Speed: 30 ft

**From High Elf Subrace**:
- Intelligence +1
- Elf [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Training (4 weapons)
- Cantrip ([[01-Wizard-Overview|wizard]] list)
- Extra Language

**Key Concept**: High elves combine elven agility with intelligence and innate magic. They're excellent wizards and make versatile characters with weapon proficiency, a free cantrip, and an extra language.
