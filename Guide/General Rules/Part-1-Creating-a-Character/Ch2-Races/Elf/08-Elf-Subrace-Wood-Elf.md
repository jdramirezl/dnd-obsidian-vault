# Elf Subrace - Wood Elf

## Original Text

As a wood elf, you have keen senses and intuition, and your fleet feet carry you quickly and stealthily through your native forests. This category includes the wild elves (grugach) of Greyhawk and the Kagonesti of Dragonlance, as well as the races called wood elves in Greyhawk and the Forgotten Realms. In Faerûn, wood elves (also called wild elves, green elves, or forest elves) are reclusive and distrusting of non-elves.

Wood elves' skin tends to be copperish in hue, sometimes with traces of green. Their hair tends toward browns and blacks, but it is occasionally blond or copper-colored. Their eyes are green, brown, or hazel.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Elf Weapon Training.** You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.

**Fleet of Foot.** Your base walking speed increases to 35 feet.

**Mask of the Wild.** You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.


## Condensed Version

**Wood Elf Identity**: Elves with keen senses, intuition, and exceptional speed through forests.

### Examples in Campaign Settings

**Greyhawk**: 
- Wild elves (grugach)
- Wood elves

**Dragonlance**: Kagonesti

**Forgotten Realms**: Wood elves (also called wild elves, green elves, or forest elves)
- Reclusive and distrusting of non-elves

### Physical Characteristics

**Skin**: Copperish hue, sometimes with traces of green

**Hair**: Browns and blacks (occasionally blond or copper-colored)

**Eyes**: Green, brown, or hazel

### Wood Elf Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Wisdom +1
- *Combined with base elf*: Dexterity +2, Wisdom +1

**Elf [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Training**
- Proficiency with: longsword, shortsword, shortbow, longbow

**Fleet of Foot**
- Base walking speed increases to **35 feet** (from 30)
- Fastest of the common player races

**Mask of the Wild**
- Can attempt to hide when only **lightly obscured** by:
  - Foliage
  - Heavy rain
  - Falling snow
  - Mist
  - Other natural phenomena
- Normally need to be heavily obscured to hide
- This is a significant stealth advantage in natural environments

### Complete Wood Elf Traits Summary

**From Base Elf**:
- Dexterity +2
- Darkvision 60 ft
- Keen Senses (Perception proficiency)
- Fey Ancestry (advantage vs charm, can't be magically slept)
- Trance (4-hour rest)
- Languages: Common, Elvish
- Speed: 30 ft (increased to 35 by subrace)

**From Wood Elf Subrace**:
- Wisdom +1
- Elf [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Training (4 weapons)
- Fleet of Foot (35 ft speed)
- Mask of the Wild (hide in light natural cover)

**Key Concept**: Wood elves are the stealthiest and fastest elf subrace, excelling in natural environments. The Wisdom bonus makes them excellent rangers, druids, and [[01-Cleric-Overview|cleric]], while Mask of the Wild provides unmatched stealth in wilderness settings.
