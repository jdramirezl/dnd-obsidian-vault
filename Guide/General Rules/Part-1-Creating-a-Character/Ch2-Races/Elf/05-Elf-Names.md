# Elf Names

## Original Text

Elves are considered children until they declare themselves adults, some time after the hundredth birthday, and before this period they are called by child names.

On declaring adulthood, an elf selects an adult name, although those who knew him or her as a youngster might continue to use the child name. Each elf's adult name is a unique creation, though it might reflect the names of respected individuals or other family members. Little distinction exists between male names and female names; the groupings here reflect only general tendencies. In addition, every elf bears a family name, typically a combination of other Elvish words. Some elves traveling among humans translate their family names into Common, but others retain the Elvish version.

**Male Adult Names:** Adran, Aelar, Aramil, Arannis, Aust, Beiro, Berrian, Carric, Enialis, Erdan, Erevan, Galinndan, Hadarai, Heian, Himo, Immeral, Ivellios, Laucian, Mindartis, Paelias, Peren, Quarion, Riardon, Rolen, Soveliss, Thamior, Tharivol, Theren, Varis

**Female Adult Names:** Adrie, Althaea, Anastrianna, Andraste, Antinua, Bethrynna, Birel, Caelynn, Drusilia, Enna, Felosial, Ielenia, Jelenneth, Keyleth, Leshanna, Lia, Meriele, Mialee, Naivara, Quelenna, Quillathe, Sariel, Shanairra, Shava, Silaqui, Theirastra, Thia, Vadania, Valanthe, Xanaphia

## Condensed Version

**Elven Naming Tradition**: Unique system involving child names and self-chosen adult names.

### Coming of Age

**Childhood**: 
- Considered children until after 100th birthday
- Called by child names during this period

**Adulthood Declaration**:
- Elf declares themselves an adult (sometime after age 100)
- Selects their own adult name
- Old friends may continue using child name

### Name Types

**Child Names** (17 examples):
Ara, Bryn, Del, Eryn, Faen, Innil, Lael, Mella, Naill, Naeris, Phann, Rael, Rinn, Sai, Syllin, Thia, Vall

**Adult Names**:
- **Unique creation** by the individual
- May reflect respected individuals or family members
- Little distinction between male/female (groupings show general tendencies only)

**Male Adult Names** (29 examples):

### Naming Format

**Full Name Structure**: [Adult Name] [Family Name]
- Example: Quarion Amakiir (Quarion Gemflower)
- Example: Keyleth Naïlo (Keyleth Nightbreeze)

**Translation**: Some elves translate family names to Common when traveling among humans; others keep the Elvish version.

**Key Concept**: Unlike other races, elves choose their own adult names as a rite of passage, reflecting their individualism and long coming-of-age period. Family names are poetic, often nature-themed combinations of Elvish words.
