# Elf Traits

## Original Text

Your elf character has a variety of natural abilities, the result of thousands of years of elven refinement.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Age.** Although elves reach physical maturity at about the same age as humans, the elven understanding of adulthood goes beyond physical growth to encompass worldly experience. An elf typically claims adulthood and an adult name around the age of 100 and can live to be 750 years old.

**Alignment.** Elves love freedom, variety, and self-expression, so they lean strongly toward the gentler aspects of chaos. They value and protect others' freedom as well as their own, and they are more often good than not.

**Size.** Elves range from under 5 to over 6 feet tall and have slender builds. Your size is Medium.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

**Darkvision.** Accustomed to twilit forests and the night sky, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Keen Senses.** You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

**Fey Ancestry.** You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.

**Trance.** Elves don't need to sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply, remaining semiconscious, for 4 hours a day. (The Common word for such meditation is "trance.") While meditating, you can dream after a fashion; such dreams are actually mental exercises that have become reflexive through years of practice. After resting in this way, you gain the same benefit that a human does from 8 hours of sleep.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Elvish. Elvish is fluid, with subtle intonations and intricate grammar. Elven literature is rich and varied, and their songs and poems are famous among other races. Many bards learn their language so they can add Elvish ballads to their repertoires.

**Subrace.** Ancient divides among the elven people resulted in three main subraces: high elves, wood elves, and dark elves, who are commonly called drow. This document presents two of these subraces to choose from. In some worlds, these subraces are divided still further (such as the sun elves and moon elves of the Forgotten Realms), so if you wish, you can choose a narrower subrace.


## Condensed Version

**Elf Racial Traits**: Mechanical abilities all elves possess, refined over thousands of years.

### Core Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Dexterity +2

**Age**
- **Physical maturity**: Same as humans
- **Cultural adulthood**: Around age 100 (when worldly experience is gained)
- **Lifespan**: Up to 750 years

**Alignment**
- **Typical**: Chaotic good
- **Chaotic**: Love freedom, variety, self-expression
- **Good**: Value and protect freedom (their own and others')

**Size**
- **Height**: Under 5 to over 6 feet tall
- **Build**: Slender
- **Size category**: Medium

**Speed**
- **Base walking speed**: 30 feet

### Special Abilities

**Darkvision** (60 feet)
- See in dim light as if bright light
- See in darkness as if dim light
- Can't discern color in darkness (only shades of gray)
- Reason: Accustomed to twilit forests and night sky

**Keen Senses**
- Proficiency in Perception skill
- Represents heightened awareness

**Fey Ancestry**
- **Advantage** on [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] against being [[Charmed|charmed]]
- **Immunity**: Magic can't put you to sleep
- Reflects elven connection to fey magic

**Trance** (Elven "Sleep")
- Don't need to sleep
- Meditate deeply for 4 hours (semiconscious)
- Can dream (mental exercises)
- Gain same benefit as 8 hours of human sleep
- **Practical benefit**: Only need 4-hour rest instead of 8

**Languages**
- Common and Elvish
- **Elvish characteristics**: Fluid, subtle intonations, intricate grammar
- **Elven literature**: Rich, varied, famous songs and poems
- Many bards learn Elvish for their repertoires

**Subrace**
- Must choose: High elf or Wood elf
- *Note: Drow (dark elves) exist but not in this document*
- Some worlds have further subdivisions (sun elves, moon elves, etc.)

**Key Concept**: Elves are naturally agile (Dexterity bonus), perceptive, resistant to charm magic, and don't need traditional sleep. Their fey ancestry gives them unique magical resistances.
