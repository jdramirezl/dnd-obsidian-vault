# Halfling Subrace - Stout

## Original Text

As a stout halfling, you're hardier than average and have some resistance to poison. Some say that stouts have dwarven blood. In the Forgotten Realms, these halflings are called stronghearts, and they're most common in the south.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Stout Resilience.** You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage.


## Condensed Version

**Stout Halfling Identity**: Hardier halflings with poison resistance, possibly with dwarven ancestry.

### Characteristics

**Physical**: Hardier and tougher than average halflings

**Ancestry Theory**: Some say stouts have dwarven blood (explaining their toughness and poison resistance)

### Examples in Campaign Settings

**Forgotten Realms**: 
- Called "stronghearts"
- Most common in the south

**Greyhawk**: Stout halflings (standard name)

### Stout Halfling Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Constitution +1
- *Combined with base halfling*: Dexterity +2, Constitution +1

**Stout Resilience**
- **Advantage** on [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] against poison
- **Resistance** to poison damage
- Identical to Dwarven Resilience (supports dwarven ancestry theory)

### Complete Stout Halfling Traits Summary

**From Base Halfling**:
- Dexterity +2
- Lucky (reroll 1s on d20)
- Brave (advantage vs [[Frightened|frightened]])
- Halfling Nimbleness (move through larger creatures' spaces)
- Languages: Common, Halfling
- Speed: 25 ft
- Size: Small

**From Stout Subrace**:
- Constitution +1
- Stout Resilience (advantage vs poison, resistance to poison damage)

### Why This Matters

**Durability**:
- Constitution bonus increases [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]]
- Poison resistance adds survivability
- Tougher than typical halflings

**Versatility**:
- Still has high Dexterity (from base halfling)
- Added Constitution makes them less fragile
- Good for front-line halfling builds

**Comparison to Lightfoot**:
- **Lightfoot**: Charisma + stealth focus (social/sneaky)
- **Stout**: Constitution + durability focus (tough/resilient)

**Key Concept**: Stout halflings trade social grace for toughness, gaining Constitution and poison resistance similar to dwarves. They're hardier halflings suited for more dangerous adventuring roles.
