# Halfling Traits

## Original Text

Your halfling character has a number of traits in common with all other halflings.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Age.** A halfling reaches adulthood at the age of 20 and generally lives into the middle of his or her second century.

**Alignment.** Most halflings are lawful good. As a rule, they are good-hearted and kind, hate to see others in pain, and have no tolerance for oppression. They are also very orderly and traditional, leaning heavily on the support of their community and the comfort of their old ways.

**Size.** Halflings average about 3 feet tall and weigh about 40 pounds. Your size is Small.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 25 feet.

**Lucky.** When you roll a 1 on the d20 for an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.

**Brave.** You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.

**Halfling Nimbleness.** You can move through the space of any creature that is of a size larger than yours.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Halfling. The Halfling language isn't secret, but halflings are loath to share it with others. They write very little, so they don't have a rich body of literature. Their oral tradition, however, is very strong. Almost all halflings speak Common to converse with the people in whose lands they dwell or through which they are traveling.

**Subrace.** The two main kinds of halfling, lightfoot and stout, are more like closely related families than true subraces. Choose one of these subraces.


## Condensed Version

**Halfling Racial Traits**: Mechanical abilities all halflings possess.

### Core Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Dexterity +2

**Age**
- **Adulthood**: Age 20
- **Lifespan**: Middle of second century (around 150 years)

**Alignment**
- **Typical**: Lawful good
- **Good**: Good-hearted, kind, hate seeing others in pain, no tolerance for oppression
- **Lawful**: Orderly, traditional, rely on community and old ways

**Size**
- **Height**: Average 3 feet tall
- **Weight**: About 40 pounds
- **Size category**: Small
- **Mechanical impact**: Trouble with heavy [[03-Weapons|weapons]]

**Speed**
- **Base walking speed**: 25 feet

### Special Abilities

**Lucky**
- When you roll a **1** on d20 (attack, ability check, or [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]):
  - Can reroll the die
  - Must use the new roll (even if it's another 1)
- Represents halfling luck and fortune

**Brave**
- **Advantage** on saving throws against being [[Frightened|frightened]]
- Reflects courage despite small size

**Halfling Nimbleness**
- Can move through the space of any creature **larger than you**
- Essentially all Medium and larger creatures
- Represents ability to dodge between legs and slip through crowds
- **Doesn't provoke opportunity attacks** (just moving through)

**Languages**
- Common and Halfling
- **Halfling language**: 
  - Not secret, but halflings reluctant to share
  - Very little written literature
  - Strong oral tradition
- Almost all halflings speak Common for communication with others

**Subrace**
- Choose: Lightfoot or Stout
- More like closely related families than true subraces
- Both common

**Key Concept**: Halflings are naturally lucky, brave, and nimble despite their small size. Their Dexterity bonus and special abilities make them excellent [[01-Rogue-Overview|rogue]] and other agile characters.
