# Halfling Subrace - Lightfoot

## Original Text

As a lightfoot halfling, you can easily hide from notice, even using other people as cover. You're inclined to be affable and get along well with others. In the Forgotten Realms, lightfoot halflings have spread the farthest and thus are the most common variety.

Lightfoots are more prone to wanderlust than other halflings, and often dwell alongside other races or take up a nomadic life. In the world of Greyhawk, these halflings are called hairfeet or tallfellows.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Naturally Stealthy.** You can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than you.


## Condensed Version

**Lightfoot Halfling Identity**: The most common and sociable halfling subrace, exceptional at hiding and getting along with others.

### Characteristics

**Personality**:
- Affable and sociable
- Get along well with others
- More [[Prone|prone]] to wanderlust than other halflings

**Lifestyle**:
- Often dwell alongside other races
- May take up nomadic life
- Most widespread halfling variety

### Examples in Campaign Settings

**Forgotten Realms**: Most common halfling variety (spread the farthest)

**Greyhawk**: Called "hairfeet" or "tallfellows"

### Lightfoot Halfling Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- Charisma +1
- *Combined with base halfling*: Dexterity +2, Charisma +1

**Naturally Stealthy**
- Can attempt to hide when obscured only by a creature **at least one size larger**
- Can use Medium or larger creatures as cover to hide
- Exceptional stealth ability
- Example: Hide behind a human, dwarf, or any Medium+ creature

### Complete Lightfoot Halfling Traits Summary

**From Base Halfling**:
- Dexterity +2
- Lucky (reroll 1s on d20)
- Brave (advantage vs [[Frightened|frightened]])
- Halfling Nimbleness (move through larger creatures' spaces)
- Languages: Common, Halfling
- Speed: 25 ft
- Size: Small

**From Lightfoot Subrace**:
- Charisma +1
- Naturally Stealthy (hide behind larger creatures)

### Why This Matters

**Perfect for [[01-Rogue-Overview|rogue]]**:
- High Dexterity (stealth, attacks, AC)
- Naturally Stealthy (hide more easily)
- Lucky (reroll critical failures)
- Nimbleness (move through enemies)

**Social Characters**:
- Charisma bonus helps with social skills
- Good for bards, face characters

**Key Concept**: Lightfoot halflings are the stealthiest halfling subrace and most common variety. The Charisma bonus and Naturally Stealthy trait make them exceptional rogues and social characters who can hide behind allies or enemies.
