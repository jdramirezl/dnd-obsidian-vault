# Dragonborn - Overview

## Original Text

Born of dragons, as their name proclaims, the dragonborn walk proudly through a world that greets them with fearful incomprehension. Shaped by draconic gods or the dragons themselves, dragonborn originally hatched from dragon eggs as a unique race, combining the best attributes of dragons and humanoids. Some dragonborn are faithful servants to true dragons, others form the ranks of soldiers in great wars, and still others find themselves adrift, with no clear calling in life.

---

## Condensed Version

**Dragonborn Identity**: A proud, dragon-descended race that combines draconic and humanoid traits, greeted by the world with fearful incomprehension.

### Origins

- **Created by**: Draconic gods or the dragons themselves
- **Origin**: Originally hatched from dragon eggs as a unique race
- **Nature**: Combines best attributes of dragons and humanoids

### Roles in the World

- Some are **faithful servants** to true dragons
- Some form **ranks of soldiers** in great wars
- Some find themselves **adrift**, with no clear calling

**Key Concept**: Dragonborn are a unique race born from dragon eggs, not half-dragons or shapeshifted dragons. They walk proudly despite being met with fear and misunderstanding by other races.
