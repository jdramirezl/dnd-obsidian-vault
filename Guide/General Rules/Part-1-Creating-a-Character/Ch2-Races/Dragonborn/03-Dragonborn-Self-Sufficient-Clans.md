# Dragonborn - Self-Sufficient Clans

## Original Text

To any dragonborn, the clan is more important than life itself. Dragonborn owe their devotion and respect to their clan above all else, even the gods. Each dragonborn's conduct reflects on the honor of his or her clan, and bringing dishonor to the clan can result in expulsion and exile. Each dragonborn knows his or her station and duties within the clan, and honor demands maintaining the bounds of that position.

A continual drive for self-improvement reflects the self-sufficiency of the race as a whole. Dragonborn value skill and excellence in all endeavors. They hate to fail, and they push themselves to extreme efforts before they give up on something. A dragonborn holds mastery of a particular skill as a lifetime goal. Members of other races who share the same commitment find it easy to earn the respect of a dragonborn.

Though all dragonborn strive to be self-sufficient, they recognize that help is sometimes needed in difficult situations. But the best source for such help is the clan, and when a clan needs help, it turns to another dragonborn clan before seeking aid from other races—or even from the gods.

---

## Condensed Version

**Clan Culture**: The clan is everything—above gods, above self. Honor and self-sufficiency define dragonborn society.

### Clan Devotion

- **Clan > everything**: More important than life itself, even the gods
- **Honor system**: Individual conduct reflects on the entire clan
- **Dishonor = exile**: Bringing shame can result in expulsion
- **Station and duty**: Every dragonborn knows their place and role

### Self-Improvement

- **Core drive**: Continual self-improvement
- **Values**: Skill and excellence in all endeavors
- **Hate failure**: Push to extreme efforts before giving up
- **Lifetime goal**: Mastery of a particular skill
- **Earning respect**: Others who share this commitment earn dragonborn respect easily

### Hierarchy of Aid

1. **Self-sufficiency first**
2. **Own clan** if help is needed
3. **Another dragonborn clan** if the clan needs help
4. **Other races or gods** — absolute last resort

**Key Concept**: Dragonborn society revolves around clan honor and self-sufficiency. They value mastery and excellence above all, and their hierarchy of loyalty runs: self → clan → other dragonborn clans → other races → gods.
