# Dragonborn - Proud Dragon Kin

## Original Text

Dragonborn look very much like dragons standing erect in humanoid form, though they lack wings or a tail. The first dragonborn had scales of vibrant hues matching the colors of their dragon kin, but generations of interbreeding have created a more uniform appearance. Their small, fine scales are usually brass or bronze in color, sometimes ranging to scarlet, rust, gold, or copper-green. They are tall and strongly built, often standing close to 6½ feet tall and weighing 300 pounds or more. Their hands and feet are strong, talonlike claws with three fingers and a thumb on each hand.

The blood of a particular type of dragon runs very strong through some dragonborn clans. These dragonborn often boast scales that more closely match those of their dragon ancestor—bright red, green, blue, or white, lustrous black, or gleaming metallic gold, silver, brass, copper, or bronze.

---

## Condensed Version

**Physical Appearance**: Dragon-like humanoids—tall, powerfully built, and covered in fine scales.

### Build

- **Height**: Close to 6½ feet tall
- **Weight**: 300 pounds or more
- **Build**: Tall and strongly built
- **Hands/Feet**: Strong, talonlike claws with three fingers and a thumb
- **Notable absences**: No wings or tail

### Scale Coloration

**Typical Scales**:
- Usually brass or bronze
- Can range to scarlet, rust, gold, or copper-green
- Generations of interbreeding created a more uniform appearance

**Strong Bloodline Scales** (clan-specific):
- Some clans have strong ties to a specific dragon type
- Scales more closely match their dragon ancestor:
  - **Chromatic**: Bright red, green, blue, white, lustrous black
  - **Metallic**: Gleaming gold, silver, brass, copper, bronze

**Key Concept**: Dragonborn resemble upright dragons without wings or tails. Most have brass/bronze scales, but those with strong draconic bloodlines display vivid chromatic or metallic coloration matching their ancestor dragon type.
