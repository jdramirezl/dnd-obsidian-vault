# Tiefling Names

## Original Text

Tiefling names fall into three broad categories. Tieflings born into another culture typically have names reflective of that culture. Some have names derived from the Infernal language, passed down through generations, that reflect their fiendish heritage. And some younger tieflings, striving to find a place in the world, adopt a name that signifies a virtue or other concept and then try to embody that concept. For some, the chosen name is a noble quest. For others, it's a grim necessity.

**Male Infernal Names:** Akmenos, Amnon, Barakas, Damakos, Ekemon, Iados, Kairon, Leucis, Melech, Mordai, Morthos, Pelaios, Skamos, Therai

**Female Infernal Names:** Akta, Anakis, Bryseis, Criella, Damaia, Ea, Kallista, Lerissa, Makaria, Nemeia, Orianna, Phelaia, Rieta

**"Virtue" Names:** Art, Carrion, Chant, Creed, Despair, Excellence, Fear, Glory, Hope, Ideal, Music, Nowhere, Open, Poetry, Quest, Random, Reverence, Sorrow, Temerity, Torment, Weary


## Condensed Version

**Tiefling Naming Traditions**: Three distinct naming categories reflecting different approaches to identity.

### Three Naming Categories

**1. Cultural Names**
- Tieflings born into another culture (usually human)
- Take names reflective of that culture
- Most common for tieflings raised in human communities

**2. Infernal Names**
- Derived from the Infernal language
- Passed down through generations
- Reflect fiendish heritage

**3. "Virtue" Names**
- Adopted by younger tieflings striving to find their place
- Signify a virtue or concept the tiefling tries to embody
- For some: a noble quest
- For others: a grim necessity

### Example Names

**Male Infernal Names** (14 examples):
Akmenos, Amnon, Barakas, Damakos, Ekemon, Iados, Kairon, Leucis, Melech, Mordai, Morthos, Pelaios, Skamos, Therai

**"Virtue" Names** (21 examples, used by any gender):
Art, Carrion, Chant, Creed, Despair, Excellence, Fear, Glory, Hope, Ideal, Music, Nowhere, Open, Poetry, Quest, Random, Reverence, Sorrow, Temerity, Torment, Weary

### Virtue Name Themes

**Positive Aspirations**: Art, Chant, Excellence, Glory, Hope, Ideal, Music, Poetry, Quest, Reverence
**Dark or Defiant**: Carrion, Creed, Despair, Fear, Nowhere, Sorrow, Temerity, Torment, Weary
**Neutral/Abstract**: Open, Random

**Key Concept**: Tiefling names reflect their struggle for identity. Cultural names show assimilation, Infernal names embrace heritage, and "Virtue" names represent a personal declaration — the tiefling choosing who they want to become rather than accepting what the world says they are.
