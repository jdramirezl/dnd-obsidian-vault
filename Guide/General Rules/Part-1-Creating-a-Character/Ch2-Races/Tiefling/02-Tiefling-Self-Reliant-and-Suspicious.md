# Tiefling - Self-Reliant and Suspicious

## Original Text

Tieflings subsist in small minorities found mostly in human cities or towns, often in the roughest quarters of those places, where they grow up to be swindlers, thieves, or crime lords. Sometimes they live among other minority populations in enclaves where they are treated with more respect.

Lacking a homeland, tieflings know that they have to make their own way in the world and that they have to be strong to survive. They are not quick to trust anyone who claims to be a friend, but when a tiefling's companions demonstrate that they trust him or her, the tiefling learns to extend the same trust in return. And once a tiefling gives someone loyalty, the tiefling is a firm friend or ally for life.


## Condensed Version

**Tiefling Society**: A people without a homeland, surviving through self-reliance and hard-won trust.

### Where Tieflings Live

**Urban Minorities**:
- Small minorities in human cities or towns
- Often in the roughest quarters
- Grow up to be swindlers, thieves, or crime lords

**Enclaves**:
- Sometimes live among other minority populations
- Treated with more respect in these communities

### Tiefling Character

**Self-Reliance**:
- No homeland of their own
- Must make their own way in the world
- Must be strong to survive

**Trust**:
- Not quick to trust anyone claiming friendship
- When companions demonstrate genuine trust, tieflings reciprocate
- Once loyalty is given, it is given for life
- A tiefling friend or ally is a firm, lifelong bond

**Key Concept**: Tieflings are forged by hardship into fiercely self-reliant survivors. They are slow to trust but unshakably loyal once that trust is earned — a lifetime bond born from knowing how rare genuine acceptance truly is.
