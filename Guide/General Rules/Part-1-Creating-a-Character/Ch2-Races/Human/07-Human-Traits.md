# Human Traits

## Original Text

It's hard to make generalizations about humans, but your human character has these traits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your ability scores each increase by 1.

**Age.** Humans reach adulthood in their late teens and live less than a century.

**Alignment.** Humans tend toward no particular alignment. The best and the worst are found among them.

**Size.** Humans vary widely in height and build, from barely 5 feet to well over 6 feet tall. Regardless of your position in that range, your size is Medium.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and one extra language of your choice. Humans typically learn the languages of other peoples they deal with, including obscure dialects. They are fond of sprinkling their speech with words borrowed from other tongues: Orc curses, Elvish musical expressions, Dwarvish military phrases, and so on.


## Condensed Version

**Human Racial Traits**: Mechanical abilities all humans possess.

**Note**: "It's hard to make generalizations about humans" - reflects their diversity

### Core Traits

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]**
- **ALL ability scores increase by 1**
- Str +1, Dex +1, Con +1, Int +1, Wis +1, Cha +1
- **Unique**: Only race that increases all six abilities
- Represents human versatility and adaptability

**Age**
- **Adulthood**: Late teens
- **Lifespan**: Less than a century (under 100 years)

**Alignment**
- **No tendency**: Humans don't lean toward any particular alignment
- **Full spectrum**: "The best and the worst are found among them"
- Can be any alignment
- Reflects human diversity and free will

**Size**
- **Height**: Barely 5 feet to well over 6 feet (wide variation)
- **Build**: Varies widely
- **Size category**: Medium (regardless of position in range)

**Speed**
- **Base walking speed**: 30 feet

**Languages**
- **Common** (always)
- **One extra language** of your choice

**Language Characteristics**:
- Learn languages of peoples they deal with
- Include obscure dialects
- Borrow words from other languages:
  - Orc curses
  - Elvish musical expressions
  - Dwarvish military phrases
- Speech is a patchwork of influences

### Why Humans Are Unique

**Most Versatile Race**:
- +1 to all abilities = no weak stats
- Can excel at any class
- Extra language provides utility
- No alignment restriction

**Trade-off**: 
- Don't get +2 to any single ability (unlike other races)
- No special abilities (darkvision, etc.)
- Rely on pure versatility

**Key Concept**: Humans are the most versatile race, gaining +1 to all [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] and an extra language. They have no special abilities but can excel at any class or role due to their well-rounded stat increases.
