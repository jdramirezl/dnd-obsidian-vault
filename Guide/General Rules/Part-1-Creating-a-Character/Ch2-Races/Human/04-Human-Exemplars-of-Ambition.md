# Human - Exemplars of Ambition

## Original Text

Humans who seek adventure are the most daring and ambitious members of a daring and ambitious race. They seek to earn glory in the eyes of their fellows by amassing power, wealth, and fame. More than other people, humans champion causes rather than territories or groups.


## Condensed Version

**Human Adventurers**: The most daring and ambitious members of an already ambitious race.

### What Drives Human Adventurers

**Glory Seeking**:
- Earn glory in the eyes of their peers
- Gain recognition and renown

**Three Paths to Glory**:
1. **Power**: Amassing political or magical power
2. **Wealth**: Accumulating riches and resources
3. **Fame**: Becoming renowned and celebrated

### Unique Human Trait

**Champion Causes Over Territory**:
- More than other races, humans fight for ideals
- Champion causes and principles
- Less focused on:
  - Specific territories (unlike elves protecting forests)
  - Particular groups (unlike dwarves defending clans)

**Idealism**: Humans are driven by abstract concepts and moral principles, not just tangible holdings

**Key Concept**: Human adventurers epitomize their race's ambition, seeking glory through power, wealth, and fame. Unlike other races who defend territories or groups, humans are more likely to champion causes and fight for ideals.
