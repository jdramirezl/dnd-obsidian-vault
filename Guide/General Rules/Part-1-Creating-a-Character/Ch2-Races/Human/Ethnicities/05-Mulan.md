# Human Ethnicity - Mulan

## Original Text

Dominant in the eastern and southeastern shores of the Inner Sea, the Mulan are generally tall, slim, and amber-skinned, with eyes of hazel or brown. Their hair ranges from black to dark brown, but in the lands where the Mulan are most prominent, nobles and many other Mulan shave off all their hair.

**Mulan Names:** (Male) Aoth, Bareris, Ehput-Ki, Kethoth, Mumed, Ramas, So-Kehur, Thazar-De, Urhur; (female) Arizima, Chathi, Nephis, Nulara, Murithi, Sefris, Thola, Umara, Zolis; (surnames) Ankhalab, Anskuld, Fezim, Hahpet, Nathandem, Sepret, Uuthrakt


## Condensed Version

**Mulan Identity**: Tall, slim, amber-skinned humans from eastern shores of the Inner Sea.

### Physical Characteristics

**Build**: Generally tall and slim

**Coloration**:
- **Skin**: Amber
- **Eyes**: Hazel or brown
- **Hair**: Black to dark brown

**Cultural Practice**: In Mulan-dominant lands:
- Nobles shave off all hair
- Many other Mulan also shave heads
- Baldness as status symbol or cultural norm

### Geographic Location

**Primary Region**: Eastern and southeastern shores of the Inner Sea (Forgotten Realms)

**Dominance**: Most prominent in these regions

### Example Names

**Male Names** (9 examples):
Aoth, Bareris, Ehput-Ki, Kethoth, Mumed, Ramas, So-Kehur, Thazar-De, Urhur

**Female Names** (9 examples):

**Surnames** (7 examples):

### Naming Format

**Full Name**: [Given Name] [Surname]
- Example: Aoth Fezim
- Example: Sefris Anskuld

**Note**: Some names include hyphens (Ehput-Ki, So-Kehur, Thazar-De)

**Key Concept**: Mulan are eastern humans with distinctive amber skin and a cultural practice of head-shaving among nobles and upper classes.
