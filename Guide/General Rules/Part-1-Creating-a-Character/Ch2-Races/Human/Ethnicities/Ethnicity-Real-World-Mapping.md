# 🌍 Human Ethnicities — Real-World Equivalents

Quick reference mapping each Faerûn ethnicity to its real-world cultural inspiration, based on physical descriptions, geography, and naming conventions.

---

- **Calishite** → 🇸🇦🇮🇷 Arab / Persian / North African
  - **Appearance:** Shorter, slighter, dusky brown skin/hair/eyes
  - **Region:** Southwest Faerûn (desert lands)
  - **Names tell it:** Khemed, Sudeiman, Zasheir, Khalid, Basha
  - **Think:** Morocco, Persia, Arabian Peninsula

- **Chondathan** → 🇬🇧🇫🇷 English / French / Western European
  - **Appearance:** Slender, tall, tawny skin, brown hair, green/brown eyes
  - **Region:** Central Faerûn (dominant population — the "default")
  - **Names tell it:** Darvin, Helm, Grim, Rowan, Buckman, Greycastle
  - **Think:** Medieval England, Normandy, France

- **Damaran** → 🇷🇺🇵🇱 Slavic / Eastern European
  - **Appearance:** Moderate build, tawny to fair skin, brown/black hair
  - **Region:** Northwest Faerûn (cold, harsh lands)
  - **Names tell it:** Grigor, Pavel, Ivor, Katernin, Zora, Kulenov, Nemetsk
  - **Think:** Russia, Poland, Czech Republic

- **Illuskan** → 🇳🇴🇸🇪🇮🇸 Norse / Scandinavian / Viking
  - **Appearance:** Tall, fair skin, blue/gray eyes, raven-black hair (blond in far north)
  - **Region:** Northern Faerûn (cold, seafaring lands)
  - **Names tell it:** Bran, Stor, Urth, Westra, Stormwind, Hornraven
  - **Think:** Norway, Iceland, Viking Age Scandinavia

- **Mulan** → 🇪🇬🇪🇹 Ancient Egyptian / Nubian
  - **Appearance:** Tall, slim, amber skin, hazel/brown eyes, nobles shave their heads
  - **Region:** Eastern/southeastern shores of the Inner Sea
  - **Names tell it:** Ehput-Ki, Thazar-De, So-Kehur, Ankhalab
  - **Think:** Pharaonic Egypt, Nubia, Mesopotamia. Head-shaving = Egyptian priests

- **Rashemi** → 🇷🇺🇲🇳 Steppe peoples / Mongol-Slavic / Central Asian
  - **Appearance:** Short, stout, muscular, dusky skin, thick black hair
  - **Region:** East of the Inner Sea, intermingled with Mulan
  - **Names tell it:** Vladislak, Borivik, Ralmevik, Stayanoga
  - **Think:** Mongol steppe warriors with Slavic naming. Cossacks, Central Asian nomads

- **Shou** → 🇨🇳🇯🇵🇰🇷 Chinese / East Asian
  - **Appearance:** Yellowish-bronze skin, black hair, dark eyes
  - **Region:** Kara-Tur (explicitly the "Far East" of Faerûn)
  - **Names tell it:** Chen, Jiang, Mei, Huang, Wan — surname before given name
  - **Think:** Imperial China, dynastic East Asia

- **Tethyrian** → 🇪🇸🇵🇹🇬🇧 Iberian / mixed Western European
  - **Appearance:** Medium build, dusky skin (fairer in north), brown hair, blue eyes
  - **Region:** Entire Sword Coast (western coastline)
  - **Names:** Use Chondathan names — culturally blended
  - **Think:** Spain, Portugal, Atlantic coast peoples. A mix of Mediterranean and Northern European

- **Turami** → 🇮🇹🇪🇸🇧🇷 Afro-Latin / Southern Mediterranean
  - **Appearance:** Tall, muscular, dark mahogany skin, curly black hair
  - **Region:** Southern shore of the Inner Sea (warm, southern coast)
  - **Names tell it:** Romero, Salazar, Umbero, Luisa, Marivaldi, Calabra
  - **Think:** Italian/Spanish names with African phenotype. Afro-Brazilian, Afro-Italian, Southern Mediterranean

---

> **DM Tip:** Use these mappings for accents, food, architecture, and music at the table — not just appearance. A Calishite merchant's shop smells of spices and incense. An Illuskan tavern serves mead and salted fish. A Shou tea house has paper screens and calligraphy.
