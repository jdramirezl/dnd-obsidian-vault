# Human Ethnicity - Turami

## Original Text

Native to the southern shore of the Inner Sea, the Turami people are generally tall and muscular, with dark mahogany skin, curly black hair, and dark eyes.

**Turami Names:** (Male) Anton, Diero, Marcon, Pieron, Rimardo, Romero, Salazar, Umbero; (female) Balama, Dona, Faila, Jalana, Luisa, Marta, Quara, Selise, Vonda; (surnames) Agosto, Astorio, Calabra, Domine, Falone, Marivaldi, Pisacar, Ramondo


## Condensed Version

**Turami Identity**: Tall, muscular humans from the southern shore of the Inner Sea.

### Physical Characteristics

**Build**: Generally tall and muscular

**Coloration**:
- **Skin**: Dark mahogany
- **Hair**: Curly black
- **Eyes**: Dark

### Geographic Location

**Primary Region**: Southern shore of the Inner Sea (Forgotten Realms)

### Example Names

**Male Names** (8 examples):
Anton, Diero, Marcon, Pieron, Rimardo, Romero, Salazar, Umbero

**Female Names** (9 examples):
Balama, Dona, Faila, Jalana, Luisa, Marta, Quara, Selise, Vonda

**Surnames** (8 examples):
Agosto, Astorio, Calabra, Domine, Falone, Marivaldi, Pisacar, Ramondo

### Naming Format

**Full Name**: [Given Name] [Surname]
- Example: Salazar Agosto
- Example: Jalana Marivaldi

**Cultural Note**: Names have a Romance language feel (similar to Spanish/Italian)

**Key Concept**: Turami are southern humans with dark mahogany skin, athletic builds, and names reflecting a distinct cultural heritage.
