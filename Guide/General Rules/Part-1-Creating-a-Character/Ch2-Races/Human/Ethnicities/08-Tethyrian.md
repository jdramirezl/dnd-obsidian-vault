# Human Ethnicity - Tethyrian

## Original Text

Widespread along the entire Sword Coast at the western edge of Faerûn, Tethyrians are of medium build and height, with dusky skin that tends to grow fairer the farther north they dwell. Their hair and eye color varies widely, but brown hair and blue eyes are the most common. Tethyrians primarily use Chondathan names.


## Condensed Version

**Tethyrian Identity**: Medium-build humans widespread along the Sword Coast.

### Physical Characteristics

**Build**: Medium height and build

**Coloration**:
- **Skin**: Dusky (grows fairer in northern regions)
- **Hair**: Varies widely (brown most common)
- **Eyes**: Varies widely (blue most common)

**Regional Variation**: Skin tone correlates with latitude—fairer in the north

### Geographic Location

**Primary Region**: Entire Sword Coast at western edge of Faerûn (Forgotten Realms)

**Distribution**: Widespread throughout the region

### Example Names

**Naming Convention**: Tethyrians primarily use **Chondathan names**

**Male Names** (from Chondathan):
Darvin, Dorn, Evendur, Gorstag, Grim, Helm, Malark, Morn, Randal, Stedd

**Female Names** (from Chondathan):
Arveene, Esvele, Jhessail, Kerri, Lureene, Miri, Rowan, Shandri, Tessele

**Surnames** (from Chondathan):
Amblecrown, Buckman, Dundragon, Evenwood, Greycastle, Tallstag

### Naming Format

**Full Name**: [Given Name] [Surname]
- Example: Darvin Evenwood
- Example: Jhessail Greycastle

**Key Concept**: Tethyrians are widespread western humans with moderate builds and variable features. They share naming conventions with Chondathans, reflecting cultural connections.
