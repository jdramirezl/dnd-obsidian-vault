# Human Ethnicity - Shou

## Original Text

The Shou are the most numerous and powerful ethnic group in Kara-Tur, far to the east of Faerûn. They are yellowish-bronze in hue, with black hair and dark eyes. Shou surnames are usually presented before the given name.

**Shou Names:** (Male) An, Chen, Chi, Fai, Jiang, Jun, Lian, Long, Meng, On, Shan, Shui, Wen; (female) Bai, Chao, Jia, Lei, Mei, Qiao, Shui, Tai; (surnames) Chien, Huang, Kao, Kung, Lao, Ling, Mei, Pin, Shin, Sum, Tan, Wan


## Condensed Version

**Shou Identity**: Most numerous and powerful ethnic group in Kara-Tur (far eastern lands).

### Physical Characteristics

**Build**: Not specified (assumed average)

**Coloration**:
- **Skin**: Yellowish-bronze
- **Hair**: Black
- **Eyes**: Dark

### Geographic Location

**Primary Region**: Kara-Tur, far to the east of Faerûn (Forgotten Realms)

**Status**: Most numerous and powerful ethnic group in their region

### Example Names

**Male Names** (13 examples):
An, Chen, Chi, Fai, Jiang, Jun, Lian, Long, Meng, On, Shan, Shui, Wen

**Female Names** (8 examples):
Bai, Chao, Jia, Lei, Mei, Qiao, Shui, Tai

**Surnames** (12 examples):
Chien, Huang, Kao, Kung, Lao, Ling, Mei, Pin, Shin, Sum, Tan, Wan

### Naming Format

**IMPORTANT**: Shou surnames come **before** given names (opposite of most other cultures)

**Full Name**: [Surname] [Given Name]
- Example: Huang Chen (Huang is surname, Chen is given name)
- Example: Mei Bai (Mei is surname, Bai is given name)

**Cultural Note**: This reflects East Asian naming conventions

**Key Concept**: Shou are the dominant human ethnicity of Kara-Tur (the far east), with yellowish-bronze skin and a distinctive naming convention where surnames precede given names.
