## Races

### [[Dragonborn/README|Dragonborn]]
Proud, self-sufficient humanoids with draconic heritage. Born of dragon blood, they walk upright in a form resembling a dragon, with scales, a breath weapon, and an unwavering sense of clan honor.
- **Ability Scores**: Strength +2, Charisma +1
- **Subraces**: None (Draconic Ancestry choice determines breath weapon and resistance)
- **Source**: PHB 2014

### [[Dwarf/README|Dwarf]]
Stout, resilient folk of mountain halls and deep mines. Clannish and traditional, with long memories and longer grudges.
- **Ability Scores**: Constitution +2
- **Subraces**: Hill Dwarf, Mountain Dwarf
- **Source**: Basic Rules 2018

### [[Elf/README|Elf]]
Graceful, long-lived people of ancient forests and arcane tradition. Otherworldly beauty paired with centuries of experience.
- **Ability Scores**: Dexterity +2
- **Subraces**: High Elf, Wood Elf
- **Source**: Basic Rules 2018

### [[Gnome/README|Gnome]]
Tiny, energetic inventors and tinkerers who form close-knit communities filled with laughter, curiosity, and constant activity. Their boundless enthusiasm makes them larger than their stature suggests.
- **Ability Scores**: Intelligence +2
- **Subraces**: Forest Gnome, Rock Gnome
- **Source**: PHB 2014

### [[Half-Elf/README|Half-Elf]]
Children of two worlds who combine human ambition with elven grace, yet belong fully to neither society. Natural diplomats and wanderers.
- **Ability Scores**: Charisma +2, two others +1
- **Subraces**: None
- **Source**: Basic Rules 2018

### [[Half-Orc/README|Half-Orc]]
Born of orc and human alliances, half-orcs combine savage strength with human ambition, carrying the mark of Gruumsh within them. Scarred, strong, and relentless.
- **Ability Scores**: Strength +2, Constitution +1
- **Subraces**: None
- **Source**: Basic Rules 2018

### [[Halfling/README|Halfling]]
Small, practical folk who value comfort, community, and simple pleasures. Naturally lucky and braver than they look.
- **Ability Scores**: Dexterity +2
- **Subraces**: Lightfoot, Stout
- **Source**: Basic Rules 2018

### [[Human/README|Human]]
The most adaptable and ambitious of the common races. Diverse in culture, appearance, and ambition.
- **Ability Scores**: All +1 (or Variant: two +1 and a feat)
- **Subraces**: None (Variant Human optional)
- **Source**: Basic Rules 2018

### [[Tiefling/README|Tiefling]]
Humanoids bearing the mark of an ancient infernal pact with Asmodeus. Horns, tails, and solid-colored eyes mark them as "devil's children."
- **Ability Scores**: Intelligence +1, Charisma +2
- **Subraces**: None
- **Source**: PHB 2014
