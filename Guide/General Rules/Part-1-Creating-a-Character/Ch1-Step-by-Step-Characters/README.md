# Chapter 1: Step-by-Step Characters

Your first step in playing D&D is to create a character — a combination of game statistics, roleplaying hooks, and your imagination. This chapter walks through the process in six steps, from choosing a race to forming a party, then covers advancement beyond 1st level.
