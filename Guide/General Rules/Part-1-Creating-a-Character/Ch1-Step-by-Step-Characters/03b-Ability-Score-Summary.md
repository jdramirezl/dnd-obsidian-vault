# Ability Score Summary & Quick Build

## Original Text

### Quick Build

Each class description in chapter 3 includes a section offering suggestions to quickly build a character of that class, including how to assign your highest ability scores, a background suitable to the class, and starting spells.

### Ability Score Summary

| Strength | Natural athleticism, bodily power | Fighter | Mountain dwarf (+2), Human (+1) |

### Ability Score Summary

| Ability | Measures | Important For | Racial Increases |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | Natural athleticism, bodily power | [[01-Fighter-Overview|Fighter]] | Mountain [[Dwarf/README|dwarf]] (+2), [[Human/README|Human]] (+1) |
| **Dexterity** | Physical agility, reflexes, balance, poise | [[01-Rogue-Overview|Rogue]] | [[Elf/README|Elf]] (+2), [[Halfling/README|Halfling]] (+2), Human (+1) |
| **Constitution** | Health, stamina, vital force | Everyone | Dwarf (+2), Stout halfling (+1), Human (+1) |
| **Intelligence** | Mental acuity, information recall, analytical skill | [[01-Wizard-Overview|Wizard]] | High elf (+1), Human (+1) |
| **Wisdom** | Awareness, intuition, insight | [[01-Cleric-Overview|Cleric]] | Hill dwarf (+1), Wood elf (+1), Human (+1) |
| **Charisma** | Confidence, eloquence, leadership | Leaders and diplomatic characters | Lightfoot halfling (+1), Human (+1) |

- **Physical Abilities**: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution — govern body and health
- **Mental Abilities**: Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma — govern mind and personality
- **Constitution** is important for every class (hit points depend on it)
- **Human (+1)** applies to all six ability scores — the only race with universal increases

**Key Concept**: Each ability score has a primary class that depends on it most, and certain races provide bonuses to specific scores. Use the Quick Build section in each class description (Chapter 3) for optimized ability score assignments, background choices, and starting spells.
