# Chapter 3: Classes

Every adventurer is a member of a class. Class broadly describes a character's vocation, what special talents they possess, and the tactics they employ.

## All Classes

| Class | Hit Die | Primary Ability | Saving Throws | Armor | Key Feature |
|-------|---------|----------------|---------------|-------|-------------|
| [[Barbarian/README\|Barbarian]] | d12 | Strength | STR, CON | Medium, shields | [[Barbarian/06-Rage\|Rage]] — damage resistance and bonus damage |
| [[Bard/README\|Bard]] | d8 | Charisma | DEX, CHA | Light | [[Bard/07-Bardic-Inspiration\|Bardic Inspiration]] — scaling die to ally rolls |
| [[Cleric/README\|Cleric]] | d8 | Wisdom | WIS, CHA | Medium, shields | [[Cleric/08-Divine-Domain\|Divine Domain]] — god-granted spells and powers at 1st level |

## Classes by Role

### Frontline / Martial
- [[Barbarian/README\|Barbarian]] — Rage-fueled tank, highest HP, damage resistance
- [[Fighter/README\|Fighter]] — Versatile combatant, most attacks and ASIs
- [[Monk/README\|Monk]] — Unarmored striker, Ki-powered mobility and control
- [[Paladin/README\|Paladin]] — Armored holy warrior, Divine Smite nova damage + auras

### Full Casters
- [[Bard/README\|Bard]] — Support/face, Inspiration dice, Magical Secrets from any list
- [[Cleric/README\|Cleric]] — Divine prepared caster, healing + domain versatility
- [[Druid/README\|Druid]] — Nature caster, Wild Shape for scouting/tanking
- [[Sorcerer/README\|Sorcerer]] — Innate arcane caster, Metamagic for spell flexibility
- [[Warlock/README\|Warlock]] — Pact caster, short-rest slots + Eldritch Invocations
- [[Wizard/README\|Wizard]] — Learned arcane caster, biggest spell list, ritual versatility

### Half Casters
- [[Paladin/README\|Paladin]] — Martial + divine spells, smite synergy
- [[Ranger/README\|Ranger]] — Martial + nature spells, exploration specialist

### Skill Specialists
- [[Bard/README\|Bard]] — Jack of All Trades, Expertise, any 3 skills
- [[Rogue/README\|Rogue]] — 4 skill picks, Expertise, Reliable Talent
