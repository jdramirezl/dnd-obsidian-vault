## Original Text

### Aura of Protection (6th Level)

Starting at 6th level, whenever you or a friendly creature within 10 feet of you must make a saving throw, the creature gains a bonus to the saving throw equal to your Charisma modifier (with a minimum bonus of +1). You must be conscious to grant this bonus.

At 18th level, the range of this aura increases to 30 feet.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — +CHA Mod to All Saves for You and Nearby Allies (6th level).**

- **Range:** 10 ft (→ 30 ft at 18th level).
- **Bonus:** Your CHA modifier (minimum +1) added to every saving throw.
- **Applies to:** You and all friendly creatures in range.
- **Requirement:** You must be conscious.
- **Why it matters:** One of the strongest features in the game. A +5 CHA paladin gives the entire party +5 to all saves.
