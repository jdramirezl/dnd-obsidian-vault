## Original Text

### Aura of Courage (10th Level)

Starting at 10th level, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet of you can't be frightened while you are conscious.

At 18th level, the range of this aura increases to 30 feet.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — Frightened Immunity for You and Nearby Allies (10th level).**

- **Range:** 10 ft (→ 30 ft at 18th level).
- **Effect:** Complete immunity to the [[Frightened]] condition.
- **Requirement:** You must be conscious.
- **Note:** If a creature leaves the aura while frightened, the condition resumes; re-entering suppresses it again.
