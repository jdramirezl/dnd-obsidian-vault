## Original Text

### Extra Attack (5th Level)

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — Two Attacks per Attack Action (5th level).** Standard martial milestone. Each hit can trigger a separate Divine Smite.
