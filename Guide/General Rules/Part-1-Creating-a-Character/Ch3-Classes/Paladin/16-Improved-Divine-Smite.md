## Original Text

### Improved Divine Smite (11th Level)

By 11th level, you are so suffused with righteous might that all your melee weapon strikes carry divine power with them. Whenever you hit a creature with a melee weapon, the creature takes an extra 1d8 radiant damage. If you also use your Divine Smite with an attack, you add this damage to the extra damage of your Divine Smite.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — Passive +1d8 Radiant on Every Melee Hit (11th level).**

- **Automatic:** No action, no resource cost — every melee weapon hit deals +1d8 radiant.
- **Stacks with Divine Smite:** If you also smite, add this 1d8 on top.
- *Example:* Melee hit + Improved Divine Smite + 2nd-level Divine Smite = weapon damage + 1d8 + 3d8 = weapon + 4d8 radiant.
