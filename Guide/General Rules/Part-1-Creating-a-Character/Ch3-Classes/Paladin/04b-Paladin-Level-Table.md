## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — Half-Caster Progression, Oath at 3, ASIs at 4/8/12/16/19.**

- **Spell slots begin** at 2nd level (half-caster).
- **Subclass features** at 3rd, 7th, 15th, 20th.
- **ASIs** at 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th.
- **Aura upgrades** at 18th (10 ft → 30 ft for Protection & Courage).
- **Max spell level:** 5th (reached at 17th level).
