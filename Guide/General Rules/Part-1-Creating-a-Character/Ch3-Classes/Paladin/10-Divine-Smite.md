## Original Text

### Divine Smite (2nd Level)

Starting at 2nd level, when you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend one paladin spell slot to deal radiant damage to the target, in addition to the weapon's damage. The extra damage is 2d8 for a 1st-level spell slot, plus 1d8 for each spell level higher than 1st, to a maximum of 5d8. The damage increases by 1d8 if the target is an undead or a fiend.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — Expend a Spell Slot on a Melee Hit for Radiant Damage.**

- **Trigger:** Hit with a melee weapon attack (decided after the hit).
- **Damage:** 2d8 radiant (1st-level slot) + 1d8 per slot level above 1st.
- **Maximum:** 5d8 (using a 4th-level slot).
- **Bonus vs. undead/fiends:** +1d8 (so up to 6d8 total).
- **Not a spell** — cannot be counterspelled; uses a spell slot but no action/bonus action.

| Slot Level | Base Damage | vs. Undead/Fiend |
|:----------:|:-----------:|:----------------:|
| 1st | 2d8 | 3d8 |
| 2nd | 3d8 | 4d8 |
| 3rd | 4d8 | 5d8 |
| 4th+ | 5d8 (cap) | 6d8 (cap) |
