## Overview

Paladins are united by their oaths to stand against the forces of annihilation and corruption. A Paladin's oath is a powerful bond — a source of power that turns a devout warrior into a blessed champion.

### Becoming a Paladin…

**As a Level 1 Character:** Gain all Core Paladin Traits and level 1 features.

**As a Multiclass Character:** Gain Hit Point Die, Martial weapon proficiency, Light/Medium armor and Shield training, and level 1 features.

## Class Features

### Level 1: Lay On Hands
Pool of healing = **5 × Paladin level**. As a Bonus Action, touch a creature to restore HP. Spend 5 HP from pool to remove Poisoned.

### Level 1: Spellcasting
- **Ability:** Charisma · **Focus:** Holy Symbol
- Regain all slots on Long Rest. Replace one prepared spell per Long Rest.

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery
Use mastery properties of **two** weapon kinds (swap on Long Rest).

### Level 2: Fighting Style
Gain a Fighting Style feat, or **Blessed Warrior** (two Cleric cantrips, CHA-based).

### Level 2: Paladin's Smite
Always have *Divine Smite* prepared. Cast once free per Long Rest.

### Level 3: Channel Divinity
**2 uses** (Short Rest restores 1, Long Rest restores all; 3rd use at level 11). DC = spell save DC.
- **Divine Sense** (Bonus Action): Detect Celestials, Fiends, Undead within 60 ft for 10 min. Also detect consecrated/desecrated areas.

### Level 3: Paladin Subclass
Choose a subclass.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
ASI or feat. Repeats at 8, 12, 16.

### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack **twice** per Attack action.

### Level 5: Faithful Steed
Always have *Find Steed* prepared. Cast once free per Long Rest.

### Level 6: Aura of Protection
10-ft Emanation. You and allies gain **+CHA modifier** (min +1) to saving throws.

### Level 9: Abjure Foes
Magic action, expend 1 Channel Divinity. Target up to CHA mod creatures within 60 ft — Wisdom save or Frightened 1 min.

### Level 10: Aura of Courage
Immunity to Frightened while in your Aura of Protection.

### Level 11: Radiant Strikes
Melee/Unarmed hits deal extra **1d8 Radiant**.

### Level 14: Restoring Touch
Lay On Hands can also remove: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, Stunned (5 HP per condition).

### Level 18: Aura Expansion
Aura of Protection becomes **30-foot** Emanation.
