## Original Text

### Quick Build

You can make a paladin quickly by following these suggestions. First, Strength should be your highest ability score, followed by Charisma. Second, choose the noble background.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept — STR > CHA, Noble Background.**

1. **Highest stat:** Strength (melee attacks, heavy armor isn't enough alone).
2. **Second stat:** Charisma (spells, auras, smite DC, Channel Divinity DC).
3. **Background:** Noble.
