## Overview

Far from bustling cities, Rangers keep their unending watch in the wilderness. They track quarry as a predator does, moving stealthily through the wilds. Thanks to their connection with nature, Rangers cast spells that harness primal powers of the wilderness, honed with deadly focus to protect the world from monsters and tyrants.

### Becoming a Ranger…

**As a Level 1 Character:** Gain all Core Ranger Traits and level 1 features.

**As a Multiclass Character:** Gain Hit Point Die, Martial weapon proficiency, one skill from the Ranger list, Light/Medium armor and Shield training, and level 1 features.

## Class Features

### Level 1: Spellcasting
- **Ability:** Wisdom · **Focus:** Druidic Focus
- Regain all slots on Long Rest. Replace one prepared spell per Long Rest.

### Level 1: Favored Enemy
Always have *Hunter's Mark* prepared. Cast **twice** free per Long Rest (increases at higher levels per Favored Enemy column).

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery
Use mastery properties of **two** weapon kinds (swap on Long Rest).

### Level 2: Deft Explorer
- **Expertise** in one skill you lack it in.
- **Two** additional languages.

### Level 2: Fighting Style
Gain a Fighting Style feat, or **Druidic Warrior** (two Druid cantrips, WIS-based).

### Level 3: Ranger Subclass
Choose a subclass.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
ASI or feat. Repeats at 8, 12, 16.

### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack **twice** per Attack action.

### Level 6: Roving
+10 ft Speed (no Heavy armor). Gain Climb Speed and Swim Speed equal to your Speed.

### Level 9: Expertise
Gain Expertise in **two** more skills.

### Level 10: Tireless
- **Temp HP** (Magic action): 1d8 + WIS mod. Uses = WIS mod per Long Rest.
- **Short Rest** reduces Exhaustion by 1.

### Level 13: Relentless Hunter
Damage can't break Concentration on *Hunter's Mark*.

### Level 14: Nature's Veil
Bonus Action → Invisible until end of next turn. Uses = WIS mod per Long Rest.

### Level 17: Precise Hunter
**Advantage** on attacks against creature marked by *Hunter's Mark*.

### Level 18: Feral Senses
**Blindsight** 30 ft.

### Level 19: Epic Boon

### Level 20: Foe Slayer
*Hunter's Mark* damage die becomes **d10**.

## Subclass: Hunter

*Protect Nature and People from Destruction*

- **Lv 3 — Hunter's Lore:** While marked by *Hunter's Mark*, you know the creature's Immunities, Resistances, and Vulnerabilities.
- **Lv 3 — Hunter's Prey** (swap on Short/Long Rest):
  - *Colossus Slayer:* +1d8 damage on hit if target is missing HP (1/turn).
  - *Horde Breaker:* Extra attack against a different creature within 5 ft of original target (1/turn).
- **Lv 7 — Defensive Tactics:** Choose one option (swap on Short/Long Rest).
- **Lv 11 — Superior Hunter's Prey:** 1/turn, when you deal *Hunter's Mark* damage, also deal that extra damage to another creature within 30 ft.
- **Lv 15 — Superior Hunter's Defense:** Reaction when you take damage → Resistance to that damage type until end of turn.
