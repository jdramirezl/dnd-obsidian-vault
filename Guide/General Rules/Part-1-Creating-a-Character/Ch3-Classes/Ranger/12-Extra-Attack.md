## Original Text

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept (5th level):** Two attacks per Attack action. Standard martial progression. Does not stack with other Extra Attack features (e.g., from multiclassing into [[01-Fighter-Overview|Fighter]]). Major damage spike at level 5.
