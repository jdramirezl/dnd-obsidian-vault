## Original Text

At 20th level, you become an unparalleled hunter of your enemies. Once on each of your turns, you can add your Wisdom modifier to the attack roll or the damage roll of an attack you make against one of your favored enemies. You can choose to use this feature before or after the roll, but before any effects of the roll are applied.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept (20th-level capstone):** Once per turn, add **WIS modifier** to one attack roll OR one damage roll against a favored enemy. Choose before or after the roll, but before effects apply. Only works vs. favored enemies — campaign-dependent value. With +5 WIS, this is +5 to hit or +5 damage once per turn. Modest capstone compared to other classes.
