## Original Text

Starting at 14th level, you can use the Hide action as a bonus action on your turn. Also, you can't be tracked by nonmagical means, unless you choose to leave a trail.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept (14th level):** Hide as a **bonus action** (like [[Rogue|rogue's]] Cunning Action, but Hide only). Also: immune to nonmagical tracking unless you choose to leave a trail. Excellent for hit-and-run archery — shoot then hide each turn. Synergizes with Stealth proficiency and any Stealth bonuses.
