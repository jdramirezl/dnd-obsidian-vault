## Original Text

At 18th level, you gain preternatural senses that help you fight creatures you can't see. When you attack a creature you can't see, your inability to see it doesn't impose disadvantage on your attack rolls against it.

You are also aware of the location of any invisible creature within 30 feet of you, provided that the creature isn't hidden from you and you aren't blinded or deafened.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept (18th level):** Two benefits:
1. **No disadvantage** on attacks against unseen creatures (negates the normal penalty).
2. **Detect [[Invisible]] creatures** within 30 ft — know their location as long as they aren't hidden and you aren't [[Blinded|blinded]] or [[Deafened|deafened]].

Does NOT grant blindsight or truesight. Invisible creatures that also take the Hide action (and beat your passive Perception) remain undetected. Strong anti-invisibility tool at a very late level.
