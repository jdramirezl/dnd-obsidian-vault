# Sorcerous Origin

## Original Text

Choose a sorcerous origin, which describes the source of your innate magical power: Draconic Bloodline or Wild Magic, both detailed at the end of the class description.

Your choice grants you features when you choose it at 1st level and again at 6th, 14th, and 18th level.

---

## Condensed Version

**Sorcerous Origin** (1st Level): Your subclass — the source of your innate magical power. Chosen at level 1 with additional features at 6th, 14th, and 18th level.

### Available Origins (PHB)

| Origin | Theme | Key Strength |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| [[12-Draconic-Bloodline\|Draconic Bloodline]] | Dragon ancestor's magic in your blood | Durability (+HP, natural AC), elemental damage boost, flight |
| [[13-Wild-Magic\|Wild Magic]] | Chaotic, untamed magical forces | Unpredictable surges, luck manipulation, chaos control |

### Feature Progression

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | Origin chosen — initial features |
| 6th | Origin feature |
| 14th | Origin feature |
| 18th | Origin capstone |

### Comparison

**[[12-Draconic-Bloodline|Draconic Bloodline]]** — the reliable, durable choice:
- Extra HP and natural AC at level 1 (patches the sorcerer's fragility)
- Bonus elemental damage at 6, flight at 14, [[Frightened|fear]]/[[Charmed|charm]] aura at 18
- Best for: players who want a consistent, powerful sorcerer

**[[13-Wild-Magic|Wild Magic]]** — the chaotic, high-variance choice:
- Random magical surges (d100 table), advantage manipulation at level 1
- Bend Luck at 6, double-roll surges at 14, bonus damage at 18
- Best for: players who enjoy unpredictability and DM interaction

**Key Concept**: Sorcerous Origin is chosen at level 1 — earlier than most subclass choices (which are typically at level 2 or 3). This means your origin defines your sorcerer from the very start. Draconic Bloodline is the stronger mechanical choice; Wild Magic is the more entertaining one.
