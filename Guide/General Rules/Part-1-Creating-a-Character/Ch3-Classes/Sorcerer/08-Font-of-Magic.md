# Font of Magic

## Original Text

At 2nd level, you tap into a deep wellspring of magic within yourself. This wellspring is represented by sorcery points, which allow you to create a variety of magical effects.

### Sorcery Points

You have 2 sorcery points, and you gain more as you reach higher levels, as shown in the Sorcery Points column of the Sorcerer table. You can never have more sorcery points than shown on the table for your level. You regain all spent sorcery points when you finish a long rest.

### Flexible Casting

You can use your sorcery points to gain additional spell slots, or sacrifice spell slots to gain additional sorcery points. You learn other ways to use your sorcery points as you reach higher levels.

**Creating Spell Slots.** You can transform unexpended sorcery points into one spell slot as a bonus action on your turn. The Creating Spell Slots table shows the cost of creating a spell slot of a given level. You can create spell slots no higher in level than 5th.

| Spell Slot Level | Sorcery Point Cost |
|------------------|--------------------|
| 1st | 2 |
| 2nd | 3 |
| 3rd | 5 |
| 4th | 6 |
| 5th | 7 |

**Converting a Spell Slot to Sorcery Points.** As a bonus action on your turn, you can expend one spell slot and gain a number of sorcery points equal to the slot's level.

## Condensed Version

**Font of Magic** (2nd Level): Sorcery points — the sorcerer's unique resource that fuels [[09-Metamagic|Metamagic]] and enables spell slot conversion.

### Sorcery Points

- **Pool size** = sorcerer level (2 at level 2, 20 at level 20)
- Cannot exceed your maximum
- Regain **all** on a long rest (also 4 on short rest at level 20 via [[11-Sorcerous-Restoration|Sorcerous Restoration]])

### Flexible Casting: Sorcery Points → Spell Slots

Create a spell slot as a **bonus action** (max 5th level):

| Slot Level | Cost | Efficiency |
|------------|------|------------|
| 1st | 2 SP | 2:1 |
| 2nd | 3 SP | 1.5:1 |
| 3rd | 5 SP | 1.67:1 |
| 4th | 6 SP | 1.5:1 |
| 5th | 7 SP | 1.4:1 |

### Flexible Casting: Spell Slots → Sorcery Points

Convert a spell slot as a **bonus action** — gain sorcery points equal to the slot's level:

| Slot Level | SP Gained |
|------------|-----------|
| 1st | 1 SP |
| 2nd | 2 SP |
| 3rd | 3 SP |
| 4th | 4 SP |
| 5th | 5 SP |

### The Conversion Tax

Converting is **not efficient in both directions**:
- Creating a 1st-level slot costs 2 SP, but converting a 1st-level slot back only gives 1 SP
- You lose value every round trip — this is intentional
- **Best use**: Convert low-level slots you won't cast into SP for Metamagic

### Practical Usage

**Common patterns**:
1. **Morning ritual**: Convert unused 1st-level slots into sorcery points before adventuring
2. **Metamagic fuel**: The primary use — spend SP on [[09-Metamagic|Metamagic]] options like Twinned or Quickened Spell
3. **Emergency slots**: Create an extra spell slot when you've burned through yours in a tough fight
4. **Never convert high-level slots**: The SP-to-slot ratio is terrible for high-level slots; use them for spells

**Key Concept**: Font of Magic is what separates sorcerers from every other caster. Sorcery points fuel Metamagic (the class's defining feature) and allow flexible slot conversion. The conversion is intentionally lossy — you lose value round-tripping. The optimal play is converting low-level slots into SP for Metamagic, not creating extra spell slots. Think of sorcery points as Metamagic fuel first, emergency slot creation second.
