# Metamagic

## Original Text

At 3rd level, you gain the ability to twist your spells to suit your needs. You gain two of the following Metamagic options of your choice. You gain another one at 10th and 17th level.

You can use only one Metamagic option on a spell when you cast it, unless otherwise noted.

### Careful Spell

When you cast a spell that forces other creatures to make a saving throw, you can protect some of those creatures from the spell's full force. To do so, you spend 1 sorcery point and choose a number of those creatures up to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one creature). A chosen creature automatically succeeds on its saving throw against the spell.

### Distant Spell

When you cast a spell that has a range of 5 feet or greater, you can spend 1 sorcery point to double the range of the spell.

When you cast a spell that has a range of touch, you can spend 1 sorcery point to make the range of the spell 30 feet.

### Empowered Spell

When you roll damage for a spell, you can spend 1 sorcery point to reroll a number of the damage dice up to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one). You must use the new rolls.

You can use Empowered Spell even if you have already used a different Metamagic option during the casting of the spell.

### Extended Spell

When you cast a spell that has a duration of 1 minute or longer, you can spend 1 sorcery point to double its duration, to a maximum duration of 24 hours.

### Heightened Spell

When you cast a spell that forces a creature to make a saving throw to resist its effects, you can spend 3 sorcery points to give one target of the spell disadvantage on its first saving throw made against the spell.

### Quickened Spell

When you cast a spell that has a casting time of 1 action, you can spend 2 sorcery points to change the casting time to 1 bonus action for this casting.

### Subtle Spell

When you cast a spell, you can spend 1 sorcery point to cast it without any somatic or verbal components.

### Twinned Spell

When you cast a spell that targets only one creature and doesn't have a range of self, you can spend a number of sorcery points equal to the spell's level to target a second creature in range with the same spell (1 sorcery point if the spell is a cantrip).

## Condensed Version

**Metamagic** (3rd Level): The sorcerer's defining feature — twist your spells in ways no other class can. Choose 2 at level 3, gain a 3rd at level 10, and a 4th at level 17.

**Rule**: Only **one** Metamagic option per spell cast, **unless otherwise noted** (Empowered Spell is the exception).

---

### 1. Careful Spell — 1 SP

**Effect**: When casting a spell that forces saving throws, choose up to CHA mod creatures — they **automatically succeed** on their save.

**Best for**: AoE spells like [[Fireball|*fireball*]], [[Hypnotic Pattern|*hypnotic pattern*]], [[Web|*web*]] when allies are in the blast zone
**Limitation**: They still take half damage on spells with "half damage on success" — they auto-succeed, not auto-avoid
**Rating**: ★★☆☆☆ — Niche. Doesn't prevent half damage, which limits its value on damage spells. Better for control spells with no effect on a success.

---

### 2. Distant Spell — 1 SP

**Effect**: Double the range of a spell (5 ft+ range), or change a touch spell to 30 ft range.

**Best for**: Touch spells at range ([[Shocking Grasp|*shocking grasp*]], [[Bestow Curse|*bestow curse*]]), or extreme-range casting
**Rating**: ★★☆☆☆ — Very situational. Most combat happens within normal spell ranges. Touch-to-30ft has niche uses.

---

### 3. Empowered Spell — 1 SP

**Effect**: Reroll up to CHA mod damage dice (must use new rolls).

**Special**: Can be combined with another Metamagic option on the same spell (unique exception to the one-Metamagic rule).
**Best for**: *Fireball*, [[Chromatic Orb|*chromatic orb*]], any spell with many damage dice
**Rating**: ★★★☆☆ — Cheap and stackable. Good for blasters, especially combined with Twinned or Quickened.

---

### 4. Extended Spell — 1 SP

**Effect**: Double a spell's duration (must be 1 minute+), max 24 hours.

**Best for**: [[Mage Armor|*Mage armor*]] (8→16 hours), [[Greater Invisibility|*greater invisibility*]] (1→2 minutes), buff spells before a dungeon
**Rating**: ★★☆☆☆ — Cheap but rarely impactful in combat. Best for pre-buffing.

---

### 5. Heightened Spell — 3 SP

**Effect**: One target has **disadvantage** on its first saving throw against your spell.

**Best for**: High-value save-or-suck spells like [[Banishment|*banishment*]], [[Polymorph|*polymorph*]], [[Hold Person|*hold person*]], [[Hold Monster|*hold monster*]]
**Rating**: ★★★★☆ — Expensive but devastating. When you absolutely need a spell to land on a boss, this is how. 3 SP is steep, so save it for critical moments.

---

### 6. Quickened Spell — 2 SP

**Effect**: Change a 1-action spell to a **bonus action**.

**Best for**: Cast a leveled spell + cantrip in the same turn, or cast + Dodge/Dash/Disengage
**Important rule**: You can still only cast a **cantrip** with your action if you cast a bonus action spell (PHB spellcasting rules)
**Rating**: ★★★★★ — One of the two best Metamagic options. Action economy is king. Quickened *fireball* + *fire bolt* cantrip is a classic combo.

---

### 7. Subtle Spell — 1 SP

**Effect**: Cast without **somatic or verbal** components.

**Best for**:
- Casting while restrained, gagged, or in a silence zone
- Casting in social situations without anyone noticing ([[Suggestion|*suggestion*]], [[Charm Person|*charm person*]])
- **Cannot be counterspelled** — [[Counterspell|*counterspell*]] requires seeing a creature casting a spell (verbal/somatic components are the visible trigger)
**Rating**: ★★★★★ — The other best Metamagic option. Uncounterable spells alone makes this top-tier. Social stealth casting is a massive bonus.

---

### 8. Twinned Spell — Spell Level in SP (1 SP for cantrips)

**Effect**: Target a **second creature** with a single-target spell.

**Restrictions**: Spell must target only one creature and not have a range of self
**Best for**: [[Haste|*Haste*]] (two allies hasted for 1 spell + 3 SP), *polymorph*, *hold person*, *greater invisibility*, cantrips like [[Fire Bolt|*fire bolt*]]
**Rating**: ★★★★★ — Arguably the most powerful Metamagic. Twinned *haste* at level 5 is one of the strongest plays in the game. Scales with spell level.

---

### Metamagic Tier List

| Tier | Options | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **S-Tier** | Twinned, Quickened, Subtle | Game-changing effects, always useful |
| **A-Tier** | Heightened, Empowered | Powerful in the right builds |
| **B-Tier** | Careful, Extended, Distant | Situational, rarely worth a pick |

### Recommended First Picks (Level 3)

- **Blaster**: Twinned + Quickened (or Empowered)
- **Controller**: Twinned + Heightened
- **Social/Utility**: Subtle + Twinned
- **Safe generalist**: Twinned + Subtle

**Key Concept**: Metamagic is the reason to play a sorcerer. You only get 2 options at level 3 and 4 total by level 17 — choose carefully. Twinned Spell is almost always your first pick (doubling *haste* or *polymorph* is absurdly strong). Subtle Spell makes your spells uncounterable. Quickened Spell breaks action economy. These three define the sorcerer's power ceiling.
