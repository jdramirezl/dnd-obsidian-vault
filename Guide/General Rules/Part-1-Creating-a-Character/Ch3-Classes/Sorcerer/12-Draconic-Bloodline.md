# Draconic Bloodline

## Original Text

Your innate magic comes from draconic magic that was mingled with your blood or that of your ancestors. Most often, sorcerers with this origin trace their descent back to a mighty sorcerer of ancient times who made a bargain with a dragon or who might even have claimed a dragon parent. Some of these bloodlines are well established in the world, but most are obscure. Any given sorcerer could be the first of a new bloodline, as a result of a pact or some other exceptional circumstance.

### Dragon Ancestor

At 1st level, you choose one type of dragon as your ancestor. The damage type associated with each dragon is used by features you gain later.

#### Draconic Ancestry

| Dragon | Damage Type |
|--------|-------------|
| Black | Acid |
| Blue | Lightning |
| Brass | Fire |
| Bronze | Lightning |
| Copper | Acid |
| Gold | Fire |
| Green | Poison |
| Red | Fire |
| Silver | Cold |
| White | Cold |

You can speak, read, and write Draconic. Additionally, whenever you make a Charisma check when interacting with dragons, your proficiency bonus is doubled if it applies to the check.

### Draconic Resilience

As magic flows through your body, it causes physical traits of your dragon ancestors to emerge. At 1st level, your hit point maximum increases by 1 and increases by 1 again whenever you gain a level in this class.

Additionally, parts of your skin are covered by a thin sheen of dragon-like scales. When you aren't wearing armor, your AC equals 13 + your Dexterity modifier.

### Elemental Affinity

Starting at 6th level, when you cast a spell that deals damage of the type associated with your draconic ancestry, add your Charisma modifier to one damage roll of that spell. At the same time, you can spend 1 sorcery point to gain resistance to that damage type for 1 hour.

### Dragon Wings

At 14th level, you gain the ability to sprout a pair of dragon wings from your back, gaining a flying speed equal to your current speed. You can create these wings as a bonus action on your turn. They last until you dismiss them as a bonus action on your turn.

You can't manifest your wings while wearing armor unless the armor is made to accommodate them, and clothing not made to accommodate your wings might be destroyed when you manifest them.

### Draconic Presence

Beginning at 18th level, you can channel the dread presence of your dragon ancestor, causing those around you to become awestruck or frightened. As an action, you can spend 5 sorcery points to draw on this power and exude an aura of awe or fear (your choice) to a distance of 60 feet. For 1 minute or until you lose your concentration (as if you were casting a concentration spell), each hostile creature that starts its turn in this aura must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be charmed (if you chose awe) or frightened (if you chose fear) until the aura ends. A creature that succeeds on this saving throw is immune to your aura for 24 hours.

## Condensed Version

**Draconic Bloodline**: The reliable, durable sorcerer subclass — dragon magic in your blood grants extra HP, natural armor, elemental power, and eventually flight.

### Dragon Ancestor (1st Level)

Choose your dragon type — this locks in your damage type for all subclass features:

| Damage Type | Dragons | Best Spells |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| **Fire** | Brass, Gold, Red | [[Burning Hands|*Burning hands*]], [[Fireball|*fireball*]], [[Wall of Fire|*wall of fire*]] — most spell options |
| **Cold** | Silver, White | [[Ray of Frost|*Ray of frost*]], [[Cone of Cold|*cone of cold*]] — solid options |
| **Lightning** | Blue, Bronze | [[Witch Bolt|*Witch bolt*]], [[Lightning Bolt|*lightning bolt*]] — decent options |
| **Acid** | Black, Copper | [[Chromatic Orb|*Chromatic orb*]], [[Melfs Acid Arrow|*Melf's acid arrow*]] — fewer options |
| **Poison** | Green | Very few damage spells deal poison — **avoid** |

**Bonus**: Speak, read, and write Draconic + double proficiency bonus on CHA checks with dragons.

**Recommendation**: **Fire** (Red/Gold) has the most spell support. Avoid Green (Poison) — almost nothing deals poison damage.

### Draconic Resilience (1st Level)

**+1 HP per sorcerer level** — effectively upgrades your d6 to a d8 hit die
**Natural AC**: 13 + DEX modifier (when not wearing armor)
- With DEX 14 (+2): AC 15 — equivalent to studded leather + shield, with no proficiency needed
- Better than [[Mage Armor|*mage armor*]] (13 + DEX) by 0 points, but doesn't require a spell known or slot
- Frees up a spell known slot that would otherwise go to *mage armor*

### Elemental Affinity (6th Level)

**Add CHA modifier** to one damage roll of a spell that deals your ancestry's damage type.
- Example: *Fireball* with CHA 20 (+5) = 8d6 + 5 fire damage
- **1 SP**: Gain resistance to your damage type for 1 hour

### Dragon Wings (14th Level)

**Flying speed** equal to your walking speed — at will, no concentration, no duration limit.
- Activate/dismiss as a bonus action
- Cannot wear armor (unless specially made)
- **This is permanent, unlimited flight** — one of the best features in the game

### Draconic Presence (18th Level)

**5 SP, action**: 60-foot aura of awe ([[Charmed]]) or fear ([[Frightened]]) for 1 minute (concentration).
- Each hostile creature starting its turn in the aura: WIS save or affected
- Success = immune for 24 hours
- Uses your spell save DC

### Feature Summary

| Level | Feature | Impact |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1st | Dragon Ancestor + Draconic Resilience | +1 HP/level, AC 13+DEX, Draconic language |
| 6th | Elemental Affinity | +CHA to matching damage, resistance for 1 SP |
| 14th | Dragon Wings | Unlimited flight at will |
| 18th | Draconic Presence | 60-ft charm/fear aura for 5 SP |

**Key Concept**: Draconic Bloodline patches the sorcerer's biggest weakness — fragility. At level 1 you get extra HP and natural armor that rivals medium armor. At level 6 you add CHA to damage. At level 14 you get unlimited flight — arguably the single best subclass feature in the PHB. Choose Fire (Red/Gold) for the most spell synergy.
