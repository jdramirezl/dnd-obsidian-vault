# Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

**Ability Score Improvement** (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th Level): Standard 5 ASIs.

### ASI Levels

| Level | Typical Choice |
|-------|---------------|
| 4th | CHA to 18 (or 20 if you started 18) |
| 8th | CHA to 20 (or CON bump) |
| 12th | CON increase or feat |
| 16th | Feat or secondary stat |
| 19th | Feat or secondary stat |

### Options Per ASI

- Increase **one** ability score by 2, OR
- Increase **two** ability scores by 1 each
- Cannot exceed 20 in any score
- Can take a **feat** instead (if using optional feats rule)

### Recommended Priority

1. **Charisma to 20** — your spell save DC and attack modifier depend on it
2. **Constitution** — more HP, better concentration saves (you already have proficiency)
3. **Dexterity** — AC (especially without armor), initiative, DEX saves

### Useful Feats

| Feat | Why |
|------|-----|
| [[41-War-Caster|War Caster]] | Advantage on concentration saves, somatic components with hands full |
| [[30-Resilient|Resilient]] (DEX) | Shore up the most common save type |
| [[20-Lucky|Lucky]] | 3 rerolls per day — universally strong |
| [[10-Elemental-Adept|Elemental Adept]] | Ignore resistance for your chosen damage type (great for Draconic Bloodline) |

**Key Concept**: Max Charisma first — it's your spellcasting stat and fuels Careful Spell, Empowered Spell, and Heightened Spell effectiveness. Constitution second for concentration and survival. Sorcerers already have CON save proficiency, so War Caster is less critical than for wizards, but still strong.
