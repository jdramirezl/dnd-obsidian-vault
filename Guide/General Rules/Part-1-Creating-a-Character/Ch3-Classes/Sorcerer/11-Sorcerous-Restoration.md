# Sorcerous Restoration

## Original Text

At 20th level, you regain 4 expended sorcery points whenever you finish a short rest.

---

## Condensed Version

**Sorcerous Restoration** (20th Level): Regain **4 sorcery points** on every short rest.

### Mechanics

- Triggers on **short rest** completion
- Regain exactly **4 SP** (not up to 4 — always 4)
- Stacks with your normal long rest full recovery
- At level 20 you have 20 SP max, so this recovers 20% of your pool per short rest

### Impact

With 2–3 short rests per adventuring day:
- **8–12 extra SP** per day on top of your base 20
- Enough for 4–6 extra Quickened Spells, or 2–3 Twinned mid-level spells
- Or convert into extra spell slots (4 SP = one 2nd-level slot + 1 SP leftover)

### Comparison to Other Capstones

| Class | 20th-Level Feature | Power Level |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Sorcerer** | 4 SP per short rest | Modest |
| [[01-Wizard-Overview|Wizard]] | Signature Spells (2 free 3rd-level casts) | Strong |
| [[01-Bard-Overview|Bard]] | Superior Inspiration (free Inspiration on initiative) | Moderate |
| [[01-Warlock-Overview|Warlock]] | Eldritch Master (all slots back 1/day) | Strong |

**Key Concept**: Sorcerous Restoration is a modest capstone — 4 SP per short rest is useful but not game-changing at level 20. Most campaigns never reach this level. The sorcerer's real power peaks in the mid-levels with Metamagic and high-level spell access.
