# Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a sorcerer quickly by following these suggestions. First, Charisma should be your highest ability score, followed by Constitution. Second, choose the hermit background. Third, choose the *light*, *prestidigitation*, *ray of frost*, and *shocking grasp* cantrips, along with the 1st-level spells *shield* and *magic missile*.

---

## Condensed Version

**Quick Build**: Fast-track sorcerer creation.

### Ability Scores

1. **Charisma** — highest (spellcasting ability)
2. **Constitution** — second highest (HP, concentration saves)

### Background

**Hermit** — fits the theme of someone discovering innate power in isolation

### Spells

**Cantrips (4)**:
- [[Light|*Light*]] — utility
- [[Prestidigitation|*Prestidigitation*]] — flavor and minor tricks
- [[Ray of Frost|*Ray of frost*]] — ranged damage + slow
- [[Shocking Grasp|*Shocking grasp*]] — melee escape (no opportunity attacks)

**1st-Level Spells (2)**:
- [[Shield|*Shield*]] — +5 AC reaction, essential survival
- [[Magic Missile|*Magic missile*]] — guaranteed damage, no attack roll

**Key Concept**: Charisma first, Constitution second. The hermit background fits a sorcerer discovering their power. *Shield* is arguably the best defensive spell in the game for a d6 hit die class — take it.
