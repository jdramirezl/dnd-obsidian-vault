## Description

Sorcerers wield innate magic that is stamped into their being. Some Sorcerers can't name the origin of their power, while others trace it to strange events in their personal or family history. The raw, roiling power of magic is part of them. The essential art of a Sorcerer is learning to harness and channel that innate magic, allowing the Sorcerer to discover new and staggering ways to unleash their power.

Sorcerers are rare. Some family lines produce exactly one Sorcerer in every generation, but most of the time, the talents of sorcery appear as a fluke. A Sorcerer's magic wants to be wielded.

### Becoming a Sorcerer…

**As a Level 1 Character:** Gain all the traits in the Core Sorcerer Traits table and the Sorcerer's level 1 features.

**As a Multiclass Character:** Gain the Hit Point Die from the Core Sorcerer Traits table and the Sorcerer's level 1 features. See the multiclassing rules to determine your available spell slots.

## Class Features

### Level 1: Spellcasting

Drawing from your innate magic, you can cast spells. You know four Sorcerer cantrips of your choice. Charisma is your spellcasting ability. You can use an Arcane Focus as a Spellcasting Focus.

**Prepared Spells of Level 1+.** You prepare the list of level 1+ spells available for you to cast. To start, choose two level 1 Sorcerer spells. Whenever you gain a Sorcerer level, you can replace one spell on your list with another Sorcerer spell for which you have spell slots.

### Level 1: Innate Sorcery

As a Bonus Action, you can unleash your innate magic for 1 minute, gaining:
- The spell save DC of your Sorcerer spells increases by 1.
- You have Advantage on the attack rolls of Sorcerer spells you cast.

You can use this feature twice, regaining all uses on a Long Rest.

### Level 2: Font of Magic

You have Sorcery Points as shown in the table. You regain all expended Sorcery Points on a Long Rest.

**Converting Spell Slots to Sorcery Points.** Expend a spell slot to gain Sorcery Points equal to the slot's level (no action required).

**Creating Spell Slots.** As a Bonus Action, transform Sorcery Points into one spell slot (max level 5). Created slots vanish on a Long Rest.

| Slot Level | Cost | Min. Level |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 2 | 2 |
| 2 | 3 | 3 |
| 3 | 5 | 5 |
| 4 | 6 | 7 |
| 5 | 7 | 9 |

### Level 2: Metamagic

You gain two Metamagic options of your choice. You can use only one Metamagic option on a spell when you cast it unless otherwise noted. You gain two more options at level 10 and two more at level 17.

### Level 3: Sorcerer Subclass

You gain a Sorcerer subclass of your choice.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

You gain the ASI feat or another feat of your choice. Again at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Sorcerous Restoration

On a Short Rest, regain expended Sorcery Points up to half your Sorcerer level (round down). Once per Long Rest.

### Level 7: Sorcery Incarnate

If you have no uses of Innate Sorcery left, you can spend 2 Sorcery Points to activate it. While Innate Sorcery is active, you can use up to two Metamagic options on each spell you cast.

### Level 19: Epic Boon

You gain an Epic Boon feat or another feat of your choice.

### Level 20: Arcane Apotheosis

While Innate Sorcery is active, you can use one Metamagic option on each of your turns without spending Sorcery Points on it.

## Subclass: Draconic Sorcery

*Breathe the Magic of Dragons*

### Level 3: Draconic Resilience

HP max increases by 3 (+1 per Sorcerer level after). Without armor, AC = 10 + DEX mod + CHA mod.

### Level 3: Draconic Spells

| Sorcerer Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Alter Self, Chromatic Orb, Command, Dragon's Breath |
| 5 | Fear, Fly |
| 7 | Arcane Eye, Charm Monster |
| 9 | Legend Lore, Summon Dragon |

### Level 6: Elemental Affinity

Choose one type: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Poison. You have Resistance to it, and add CHA mod to one damage roll of spells dealing that type.

### Level 14: Dragon Wings

Bonus Action: gain Fly Speed 60 ft for 1 hour. Once per Long Rest, or spend 3 Sorcery Points to restore.

### Level 18: Dragon Companion

Cast Summon Dragon without a Material component. Once per Long Rest without a spell slot. Can modify to remove Concentration (duration becomes 1 minute).
