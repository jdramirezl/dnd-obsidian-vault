## Condensed Version

### Key Milestones

- **Level 1** — [[06-Spellcasting|Spellcasting]] + [[07-Sorcerous-Origin|Sorcerous Origin]] — core kit online, 4 cantrips + 2 spells known
- **Level 2** — [[08-Font-of-Magic|Font of Magic]] (2 sorcery points) — resource conversion begins
- **Level 3** — [[09-Metamagic|Metamagic]] (choose 2 options) — **the sorcerer's defining feature**
- **Level 4** — ASI + 5th cantrip + 5th spell known
- **Level 5** — 3rd-level spell slots — **power spike** ([[Fireball|fireball]], [[Haste|haste]], [[Counterspell|counterspell]])
- **Level 6** — Sorcerous Origin feature ([[12-Draconic-Bloodline|Elemental Affinity]] / [[13-Wild-Magic|Bend Luck]])
- **Level 10** — 3rd [[09-Metamagic|Metamagic]] option + 6th cantrip
- **Level 14** — Sorcerous Origin feature ([[12-Draconic-Bloodline|Dragon Wings]] / [[13-Wild-Magic|Controlled Chaos]])
- **Level 17** — 4th [[09-Metamagic|Metamagic]] option + 9th-level spell slots
- **Level 18** — Sorcerous Origin capstone ([[12-Draconic-Bloodline|Draconic Presence]] / [[13-Wild-Magic|Spell Bombardment]])
- **Level 20** — [[11-Sorcerous-Restoration|Sorcerous Restoration]] — regain 4 sorcery points on short rest

### Spells Known Progression

- Starts at **2** known spells (level 1), caps at **15** (level 15+)
- Can swap one spell per level up
- Cantrips: **4** at level 1, 5 at level 4, 6 at level 10
- **Sorcerers know the fewest spells of any full caster** — choose carefully

### Sorcery Points

- Equal to sorcerer level (starting at 2 at level 2)
- Fuel [[09-Metamagic|Metamagic]] and [[08-Font-of-Magic|Flexible Casting]]
- Regain all on long rest (4 on short rest at level 20)

### Spell Slots

Full caster progression (same slots as bard, cleric, druid, wizard). 9th-level slots at level 17.

**Key Concept**: The sorcerer's power curve is defined by sorcery points and Metamagic, not just spell slots. Level 3 (Metamagic) and level 5 (3rd-level spells) are the biggest early spikes. Sorcerers know the fewest spells of any full caster (15 max vs. [[Bard|bard's]] 22 or [[Wizard|wizard's]] entire spellbook), so every spell choice matters.
