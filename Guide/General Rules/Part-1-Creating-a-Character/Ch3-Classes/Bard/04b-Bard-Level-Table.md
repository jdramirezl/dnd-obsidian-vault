## Condensed Version

- **Level 1** — [[06-Spellcasting|Spellcasting]] + [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] (d6) — core kit from day one
- **Level 2** — [[08-Jack-of-All-Trades|Jack of All Trades]] + [[09-Song-of-Rest|Song of Rest]] (d6) — skill monkey and party sustain
- **Level 3** — [[10-Bard-College|Bard College]] (subclass) + [[11-Expertise|Expertise]] (2 skills) — major identity choice
- **Level 4** — [[12-Ability-Score-Improvement|ASI]] + 3rd cantrip
- **Level 5** — [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] (d8) + [[13-Font-of-Inspiration|Font of Inspiration]] (short rest recharge) — huge power spike
- **Level 6** — [[14-Countercharm|Countercharm]] + Bard College feature
- **Levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19** — **ASI ×5** (standard)
- **Level 9** — [[09-Song-of-Rest|Song of Rest]] (d8)
- **Level 10** — [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] (d10) + [[11-Expertise|Expertise]] (2 more skills) + [[15-Magical-Secrets|Magical Secrets]] (2 spells from any class) — massive level
- **Level 13** — [[09-Song-of-Rest|Song of Rest]] (d10)
- **Level 14** — [[15-Magical-Secrets|Magical Secrets]] (2 more) + Bard College feature
- **Level 15** — [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] (d12)
- **Level 17** — [[09-Song-of-Rest|Song of Rest]] (d12)
- **Level 18** — [[15-Magical-Secrets|Magical Secrets]] (2 more — 6 total stolen spells)
- **Level 20** — [[16-Superior-Inspiration|Superior Inspiration]] — capstone, never start combat without Inspiration

### Scaling Features

| Feature | Progression |
|---------|-------------|
| Bardic Inspiration die | d6 → d8 (5th) → d10 (10th) → d12 (15th) |
| Song of Rest die | d6 → d8 (9th) → d10 (13th) → d12 (17th) |
| Magical Secrets | 2 spells (10th) → 2 more (14th) → 2 more (18th) = 6 total |
| Expertise skills | 2 (3rd) → 2 more (10th) = 4 total |
| Spells Known | 4 (1st) → 22 (20th) |
| Cantrips Known | 2 (1st) → 4 (10th+) |

**Key Concept**: The bard's progression is defined by *versatility that deepens over time* — Bardic Inspiration dice grow, Song of Rest scales, Expertise doubles, and Magical Secrets lets you cherry-pick the best spells from every class. Level 10 is the bard's biggest power spike (d10 Inspiration + 2 more Expertise + first Magical Secrets).
