# Bard - Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

### Class Features

As a bard, you gain the following class features.

### Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d8 per bard level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per bard level after 1st

### Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor
Weapons: Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords
Tools: Three musical instruments of your choice
Saving Throws: Dexterity, Charisma
Skills: Choose any three

---

## Condensed Version

**Bard Class Features**: Moderate hit points, light armor, select martial weapons, and the broadest skill access of any class.

### Hit Points

**Hit Dice**: 1d8 per bard level (moderate — same as cleric, rogue)
**1st Level HP**: 8 + Constitution modifier
- Example: Con 14 (+2) = 10 HP at level 1
**Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 5) + Constitution modifier per level
- Example: Level 5, Con 14 (+2) = 8 + 4×(5+2) = 36 HP (using average)

### Armor Proficiencies

**Light armor only**: Leather, studded leather
- AC = armor base + DEX modifier
- No medium or heavy armor, no shields (unless multiclassing or [[18-College-of-Valor|College of Valor]])

### Weapon Proficiencies

**Simple weapons**: Daggers, light crossbows, quarterstaffs, etc.
**Select martial weapons**: Hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords
- Rapier is the go-to melee weapon (1d8 + DEX, finesse)

### Tool Proficiencies

**Three musical instruments** of your choice
- These double as spellcasting foci
- Choose instruments that fit your character's performance style

### Saving Throws

**Dexterity**: Dodging AoE effects (fireball, breath weapons)
**Charisma**: Resisting banishment, some charm effects

### Skills (Choose Any 3)

**Any three skills** from the entire skill list — no restrictions
- Most flexible skill selection of any class
- Combined with [[08-Jack-of-All-Trades|Jack of All Trades]] and [[11-Expertise|Expertise]], bards are the best skill class in the game

**Key Concept**: Bards have d8 hit dice, light armor, and a handful of martial weapons (rapier being the standout). Their real strength is **any 3 skills** from the full list plus three musical instrument proficiencies, making them the most skill-versatile class from level 1.
