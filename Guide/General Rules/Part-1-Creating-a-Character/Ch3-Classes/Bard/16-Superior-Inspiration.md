# Bard - Superior Inspiration

## Original Text

At 20th level, when you roll initiative and have no uses of Bardic Inspiration left, you regain one use.

---

## Condensed Version

**Superior Inspiration** (20th Level — Capstone): When you roll initiative with zero [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] uses remaining, you regain one use.

### How It Works

- **Trigger**: Rolling initiative with 0 Bardic Inspiration uses
- **Effect**: Regain 1 use of Bardic Inspiration
- **Limitation**: Only triggers if you have **no** uses left; does nothing if you have any remaining

### Assessment

This is a safety net, not a power spike. By 20th level with [[13-Font-of-Inspiration|Font of Inspiration]], you already recharge all uses on a short rest. Superior Inspiration only matters if you enter combat without having short-rested.

- Ensures you always have at least 1 Bardic Inspiration die when combat starts
- Considered one of the weaker capstone features in the game
- Most campaigns never reach level 20

**Key Concept**: Superior Inspiration guarantees you always have at least one Bardic Inspiration die when combat begins. It's a minor capstone — a safety net rather than a game-changer — since Font of Inspiration already provides short-rest recharge from 5th level onward.
