# Bard - Font of Inspiration

## Original Text

Beginning when you reach 5th level, you regain all of your expended uses of Bardic Inspiration when you finish a short or long rest.

---

## Condensed Version

**Font of Inspiration** (5th Level): [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] now recharges on a **short or long rest** instead of long rest only.

### Impact

- **Before 5th level**: Bardic Inspiration recharges on long rest only — must conserve uses
- **After 5th level**: Recharges on short rest — use freely every encounter
- With CHA 20 (+5) and 2–3 short rests per day, that's **15–20 Inspiration dice per adventuring day**

### Why This Matters

This is one of the bard's biggest power spikes. It transforms Bardic Inspiration from a rationed resource into a reliable per-encounter tool. Combined with the d8 die upgrade at the same level, 5th level is when the bard's support role fully comes online.

**Key Concept**: Font of Inspiration upgrades Bardic Inspiration to short-rest recharge, dramatically increasing the bard's support output. At 5th level, bards should be handing out Inspiration dice freely every combat.
