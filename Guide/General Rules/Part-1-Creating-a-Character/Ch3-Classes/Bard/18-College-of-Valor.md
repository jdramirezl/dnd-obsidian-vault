# Bard - College of Valor

## Original Text

Bards of the College of Valor are daring skalds whose tales keep alive the memory of the great heroes of the past, and thereby inspire a new generation of heroes. These bards gather in mead halls or around great bonfires to sing the deeds of the mighty, both past and present. They travel the land to witness great events firsthand and to ensure that the memory of those events doesn't pass from the world. With their songs, they inspire others to reach the same heights of accomplishment as the heroes of old.

### Bonus Proficiencies

When you join the College of Valor at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with medium armor, shields, and martial weapons.

### Combat Inspiration

Also at 3rd level, you learn to inspire others in battle. A creature that has a Bardic Inspiration die from you can roll that die and add the number rolled to a weapon damage roll it just made. Alternatively, when an attack roll is made against the creature, it can use its reaction to roll the Bardic Inspiration die and add the number rolled to its AC against that attack, after seeing the roll but before knowing whether it hits or misses.

### Extra Attack

Starting at 6th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

### Battle Magic

At 14th level, you have mastered the art of weaving spellcasting and weapon use into a single harmonious act. When you use your action to cast a bard spell, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action.

## Condensed Version

**College of Valor**: The warrior-skald — a battle bard who sings of heroes and fights alongside them, combining martial prowess with magic.

### 3rd Level: Bonus Proficiencies

- **Medium armor** (half plate = AC 15 + DEX mod, max +2 → AC 17)
- **Shields** (+2 AC → AC 19 with half plate)
- **Martial weapons** (longswords, warhammers, longbows, etc.)

Transforms the bard from a fragile caster into a capable frontliner.

### 3rd Level: Combat Inspiration

An ally with your [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] die can use it in two new ways:

| Use | Trigger | Effect |
|-----|---------|--------|
| **Damage boost** | After making a weapon damage roll | Add the Inspiration die to the damage |
| **AC boost** | When an attack roll is made against them | Use reaction to add the die to AC against that attack |

- **AC boost timing**: After seeing the attack roll, before knowing if it hits
- These are **in addition to** the standard uses (ability check, attack roll, saving throw)

### 6th Level: Extra Attack

- Attack **twice** when taking the Attack action
- Same as [[Fighter/README|fighter]]'s Extra Attack at 5th level (one level later)
- Makes the Valor bard a legitimate melee combatant

### 14th Level: Battle Magic

- When you **cast a bard spell** as your action, make **one weapon attack** as a bonus action
- Combines spellcasting and martial combat in a single turn
- Example: Cast *[[Hold Person|hold person]]* → bonus action rapier attack (with advantage if the target is [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]] and within 5 ft.)

### Valor vs. Lore Comparison

| Feature | College of Valor | College of Lore |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| 3rd Level | Medium armor, shields, martial weapons + Combat Inspiration | 3 bonus skills + Cutting Words |
| 6th Level | Extra Attack | Additional Magical Secrets (2 free spells from any class) |
| 14th Level | Battle Magic (cast + attack) | Peerless Skill (Inspiration on own checks) |
| Playstyle | Melee/hybrid gish | Full caster/support |
| AC potential | 19 (half plate + shield) | 12–17 (light armor + DEX, or Mage Armor) |

**Key Concept**: College of Valor turns the bard into a battle-capable gish — medium armor, shields, and martial weapons at 3rd level, Extra Attack at 6th, and Battle Magic at 14th for casting + attacking in one turn. It trades the Lore bard's skill mastery and early Magical Secrets for genuine martial competence and frontline durability.
