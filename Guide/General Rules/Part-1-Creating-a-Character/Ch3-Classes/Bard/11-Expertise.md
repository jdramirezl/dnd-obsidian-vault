# Bard - Expertise

## Original Text

At 3rd level, choose two of your skill proficiencies. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of the chosen proficiencies.

At 10th level, you can choose another two skill proficiencies to gain this benefit.

---

## Condensed Version

**Expertise** (3rd Level): Double your proficiency bonus for two chosen skill proficiencies. Choose two more at 10th level.

### How It Works

- Select **2 skill proficiencies** you already have → proficiency bonus is doubled for those skills
- At **10th level**, choose **2 more** (4 total Expertise skills)
- Only applies to **ability checks**, not saving throws or attack rolls

### Expertise Bonus by Level

| Level | Proficiency Bonus | Expertise Bonus | Total (with +5 ability mod) |
|-------|-------------------|-----------------|----------------------------|
| 3rd–4th | +2 | +4 | +9 |
| 5th–8th | +3 | +6 | +11 |
| 9th–12th | +4 | +8 | +13 |
| 13th–16th | +5 | +10 | +15 |
| 17th–20th | +6 | +12 | +17 |

### Recommended Expertise Choices

**Face Bard**: Persuasion, Deception (dominate social encounters)
**Skill Monkey**: Perception, Stealth (broadly useful)
**Lore Bard**: Arcana, History (knowledge checks)
**Utility**: Athletics (grappling), Sleight of Hand (thievery)

### Stacking with Jack of All Trades

- **Proficient skill without Expertise**: Full proficiency bonus
- **Expertise skill**: Double proficiency bonus
- **Non-proficient skill**: Half proficiency bonus ([[08-Jack-of-All-Trades|Jack of All Trades]])
- Bards cover all three tiers, making them the best skill class in the game

**Key Concept**: Expertise doubles your proficiency bonus on 4 skills total (2 at 3rd, 2 more at 10th). Combined with [[08-Jack-of-All-Trades|Jack of All Trades]] and the bard's "choose any 3 skills" proficiency, bards have the most complete skill coverage of any class — excelling at chosen specialties while remaining competent at everything else.
