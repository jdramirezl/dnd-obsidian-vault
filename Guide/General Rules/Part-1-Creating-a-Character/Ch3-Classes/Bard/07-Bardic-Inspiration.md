# Bard - Bardic Inspiration

## Original Text

You can inspire others through stirring words or music. To do so, you use a bonus action on your turn to choose one creature other than yourself within 60 feet of you who can hear you. That creature gains one Bardic Inspiration die, a d6.

Once within the next 10 minutes, the creature can roll the die and add the number rolled to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw it makes. The creature can wait until after it rolls the d20 before deciding to use the Bardic Inspiration die, but must decide before the DM says whether the roll succeeds or fails. Once the Bardic Inspiration die is rolled, it is lost. A creature can have only one Bardic Inspiration die at a time.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (a minimum of once). You regain any expended uses when you finish a long rest.

Your Bardic Inspiration die changes when you reach certain levels in this class. The die becomes a d8 at 5th level, a d10 at 10th level, and a d12 at 15th level.

---

## Condensed Version

**Bardic Inspiration** (1st Level): Use a bonus action to grant an ally a die they can add to an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw.

### How It Works

**Action**: Bonus action on your turn
**Range**: 60 feet, target must hear you
**Target**: One creature other than yourself
**Effect**: Target gains one Bardic Inspiration die
**Duration**: 10 minutes or until used
**Limit**: One Bardic Inspiration die per creature at a time

### Using the Die

The recipient can roll the die and add it to:
- An **ability check**
- An **attack roll**
- A **saving throw**

**Timing**: After the d20 roll, but before the DM announces success or failure

### Uses and Recovery

**Uses per long rest**: Equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1)
- CHA 16 (+3) = 3 uses per long rest
- CHA 20 (+5) = 5 uses per long rest
**Recovery**: Long rest (upgrades to short rest at 5th level via [[13-Font-of-Inspiration|Font of Inspiration]])

### Die Progression

| Level | Die | Average Bonus |
|-------|-----|---------------|
| 1st   | d6  | +3.5 |
| 5th   | d8  | +4.5 |
| 10th  | d10 | +5.5 |
| 15th  | d12 | +6.5 |

### Tactical Tips

- Give dice to allies who make high-impact rolls (attack rolls on big hits, crucial saves)
- Don't hoard — especially after 5th level when they recharge on short rest
- Pairs well with allies who have features triggered by hitting ([[Paladin/README|Paladin]] smites, [[Rogue/README|Rogue]] sneak attack)

**Key Concept**: Bardic Inspiration is the bard's signature feature — a bonus-action buff that lets an ally add a d6 (scaling to d12) to a critical roll. With CHA-modifier uses per rest and short-rest recharge from level 5, it's a consistent, powerful support tool that defines the bard's role as a force multiplier.
