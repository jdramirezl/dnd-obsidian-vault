# Bard - Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a bard quickly by following these suggestions. First, Charisma should be your highest ability score, followed by Dexterity. Second, choose the entertainer background. Third, choose the *dancing lights* and *vicious mockery* cantrips, along with the following 1st-level spells: *charm person*, *detect magic*, *healing word*, and *thunderwave*.

---

## Condensed Version

**Quick Build**: Fast setup for a new bard character.

### Step 1: Ability Scores

| Priority | Ability | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1st | Charisma | Spellcasting, [[07-Bardic-Inspiration\|Bardic Inspiration]], social skills |
| 2nd | Dexterity | AC (light armor), initiative, DEX saves |
| 3rd | Constitution | Hit points and concentration saves |

### Step 2: Background

**Recommended**: Entertainer
- Fits the bard's performer identity naturally
- Provides Acrobatics, Performance, disguise kit, and a musical instrument

### Step 3: Spells

**Cantrips**: *[[Dancing Lights|dancing lights]]*, *[[Vicious Mockery|vicious mockery]]*
**1st-Level Spells** (4 known):
- *[[Charm Person|charm person]]* — social manipulation
- *[[Detect Magic|detect magic]]* — utility/exploration
- *[[Healing Word|healing word]]* — bonus action healing
- *[[Thunderwave|thunderwave]]* — AoE damage/crowd control

**Key Concept**: For a quick bard build, maximize Charisma first, Dexterity second, take the entertainer background, and pick a balanced spell list covering damage (*vicious mockery*, *thunderwave*), healing (*healing word*), utility (*detect magic*), and social (*charm person*).
