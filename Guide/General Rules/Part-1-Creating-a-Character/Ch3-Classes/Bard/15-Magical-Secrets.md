# Bard - Magical Secrets

## Original Text

By 10th level, you have plundered magical knowledge from a wide spectrum of disciplines. Choose two spells from any class, including this one. A spell you choose must be of a level you can cast, as shown on the Bard table, or a cantrip.

The chosen spells count as bard spells for you and are included in the number in the Spells Known column of the Bard table.

You learn two additional spells from any class at 14th level and again at 18th level.

---

## Condensed Version

**Magical Secrets** (10th, 14th, 18th Level): Learn 2 spells from **any class's spell list** at each of these levels (6 total).

### How It Works

- Choose **2 spells from any class** (wizard, cleric, druid, paladin, ranger, warlock, sorcerer, or bard)
- Must be a level you can cast or a cantrip
- Chosen spells **count as bard spells** for you
- They **count against** your Spells Known total
- Can be swapped on level-up like any other bard spell

### Progression

| Level | Spells Gained | Total Magical Secrets |
|-------|---------------|----------------------|
| 10th  | 2 | 2 |
| 14th  | 2 | 4 |
| 18th  | 2 | 6 |

### Popular Picks

**From Wizard/Sorcerer**:
- *[[Counterspell]]* (3rd) — essential; bards add [[08-Jack-of-All-Trades|Jack of All Trades]] to the check
- *[[Fireball]]* (3rd) — AoE damage the bard list lacks
- *[[Wall of Force]]* (5th) — top-tier battlefield control
- *[[Find Greater Steed]]* ([[Paladin/README|Paladin]] 4th) — powerful mount

**From Cleric/Druid**:
- *[[Aura of Vitality]]* ([[Paladin/README|Paladin]] 3rd) — efficient healing
- *[[Swift Quiver]]* ([[Ranger/README|Ranger]] 5th) — for Valor bards
- *[[Wish]]* ([[Wizard/README|Wizard]] 9th) — the ultimate 18th-level pick

**From Paladin/Ranger**:
- *[[Find Greater Steed]]* (Paladin 4th) — flying mount
- *[[Destructive Wave]]* (Paladin 5th) — AoE damage + [[Prone|prone]]

### Note for College of Lore

[[17-College-of-Lore|College of Lore]] bards get **Additional Magical Secrets** at 6th level (2 extra spells from any class that don't count against Spells Known) — for a total of **8 stolen spells** by 18th level.

**Key Concept**: Magical Secrets is the bard's most powerful feature — it lets you cherry-pick the best spells from every class in the game. With 6 choices (8 for Lore bards), you can fill every gap in the bard spell list: AoE damage, counterspelling, powerful healing, or utility. This is what makes high-level bards the most versatile casters in D&D.
