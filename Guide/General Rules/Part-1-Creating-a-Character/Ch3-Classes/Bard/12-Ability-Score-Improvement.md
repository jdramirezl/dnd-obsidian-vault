# Bard - Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

**Ability Score Improvement** (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th Level): Increase one ability score by 2, or two scores by 1 each. Maximum 20.

### ASI Levels

| Level | Typical Priority |
|-------|-----------------|
| 4th   | CHA to 18 or 20, or a feat |
| 8th   | Max CHA to 20, or DEX/CON boost |
| 12th  | DEX, CON, or feat |
| 16th  | Round out secondary stats or feat |
| 19th  | Final optimization |

### ASI Count

**5 total** — standard for most classes (fighters get 7)

### Bard Priority

1. **Charisma 20** — affects spellcasting DC, spell attacks, [[07-Bardic-Inspiration|Bardic Inspiration]] uses, and social skills
2. **Dexterity** — AC, initiative, DEX saves
3. **Constitution** — HP and concentration saves
4. **Feats** (if using optional rule): War Caster, Resilient (CON), Lucky, Actor

**Key Concept**: Bards get 5 ASIs at the standard levels. Maximizing Charisma is the top priority since it powers spellcasting, Bardic Inspiration uses, and social skills. After CHA 20, boost DEX (AC/initiative) or CON (HP/concentration), or take feats.
