# Bard - Bard College

## Original Text

At 3rd level, you delve into the advanced techniques of a bard college of your choice: the College of Lore or the College of Valor, both detailed at the end of the class description. Your choice grants you features at 3rd level and again at 6th and 14th level.

### Bard Colleges

The way of a bard is gregarious. Bards seek each other out to swap songs and stories, boast of their accomplishments, and share their knowledge. Bards form loose associations, which they call colleges, to facilitate their gatherings and preserve their traditions.

---

## Condensed Version

**Bard College** (3rd Level): Choose your subclass — determines your bard's specialization and grants features at 3rd, 6th, and 14th level.

### Available Colleges (PHB)

| College | Identity | Features at 3rd | Features at 6th | Features at 14th |
|---------|----------|-----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| [[17-College-of-Lore\|College of Lore]] | Knowledge seeker, skill master | Bonus Proficiencies (3 skills), Cutting Words | Additional Magical Secrets | Peerless Skill |
| [[18-College-of-Valor\|College of Valor]] | Warrior-skald, battle bard | Bonus Proficiencies (medium armor, shields, martial weapons), Combat Inspiration | Extra Attack | Battle Magic |

### Choosing a College

- **Lore**: Best for pure support/caster bards — more skills, early Magical Secrets, and Cutting Words for debuffing
- **Valor**: Best for melee/hybrid bards — armor, weapons, Extra Attack, and combat-focused Inspiration

### Bard Colleges as Organizations

Colleges are loose associations, not formal institutions (though some have actual campuses). Bards gather to:
- Swap songs and stories
- Boast of accomplishments
- Share and preserve knowledge and traditions

**Key Concept**: Bard College is the bard's subclass choice at 3rd level. College of Lore doubles down on skills and spellcasting (the "full caster" path), while College of Valor adds martial capability (the "gish" path). Both grant features at 3rd, 6th, and 14th level.
