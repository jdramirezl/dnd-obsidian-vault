# Bard - Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a rapier, (b) a longsword, or (c) any simple weapon
- (a) a diplomat's pack or (b) an entertainer's pack
- (a) a lute or (b) any other musical instrument
- Leather armor and a dagger

---

## Condensed Version

**Starting Equipment**: Choose from the following options (in addition to background equipment).

### Choice 1: Weapon

| Option | Details | Damage |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **(a) Rapier** | Finesse, best bard melee option | 1d8 + DEX |
| **(b) Longsword** | Versatile (1d10 two-handed) | 1d8/1d10 + STR |
| **(c) Any simple weapon** | Flexible pick | Varies |

- **Best choice**: Rapier — finesse synergizes with DEX-based bards

### Choice 2: Adventuring Gear

| Option | Key Contents |
|--------|-------------|
| **(a) Diplomat's pack** | Chest, map case, fine clothes, ink, paper — social focus |
| **(b) Entertainer's pack** | Bedroll, costumes, candles, disguise kit — performer focus |

### Choice 3: Instrument

| Option | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **(a) Lute** | Classic bard instrument |
| **(b) Any other musical instrument** | Flute, drum, lyre, etc. |

- The instrument doubles as your spellcasting focus

### Always Included

- **Leather armor** (AC 11 + DEX modifier)
- **Dagger** (1d4, finesse, light, thrown 20/60)

### Recommended Loadout

**Standard Bard**: Rapier + entertainer's pack + lute + leather armor + dagger
- AC 11 + DEX, rapier for melee (1d8 + DEX), lute as spellcasting focus

**Key Concept**: Bard starting equipment is straightforward — take the rapier (best finesse weapon available), pick an instrument as your spellcasting focus, and choose a pack that fits your character. Leather armor and a dagger round out the kit.
