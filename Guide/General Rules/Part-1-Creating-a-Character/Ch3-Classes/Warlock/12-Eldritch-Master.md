# Warlock - Eldritch Master

## Original Text

At 20th level, you can draw on your inner reserve of mystical power while entreating your patron to regain expended spell slots. You can spend 1 minute entreating your patron for aid to regain all your expended spell slots from your Pact Magic feature. Once you regain spell slots with this feature, you must finish a long rest before you can do so again.

---

## Condensed Version

**Eldritch Master**: The 20th-level capstone — an extra full recharge of all Pact Magic slots, once per long rest.

### Mechanics

- **Action**: 1 minute of entreating your patron
- **Effect**: Regain all expended Pact Magic spell slots
- **Recharge**: Once per long rest
- **Does NOT affect**: Mystic Arcanum (those are separate)

### Effective Impact

At 20th level you have 4 slots (all 5th level). With short rests + Eldritch Master:
- **No short rests**: 4 + 4 = 8 slots per day
- **1 short rest**: 4 + 4 + 4 = 12 slots per day
- **2 short rests**: 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 = 16 slots per day (all 5th level)

**Key Concept**: Eldritch Master is essentially a free short rest for your Pact Magic slots, usable once per long rest. It's a solid but unspectacular capstone — it guarantees you always have one extra full recharge available even when the party can't take a short rest. The 1-minute casting time means it can't be used mid-combat, but it's perfect for pre-boss preparation.
