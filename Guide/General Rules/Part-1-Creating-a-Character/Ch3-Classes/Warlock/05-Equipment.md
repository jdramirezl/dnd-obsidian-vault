# Warlock - Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) any simple weapon
- (a) a component pouch or (b) an arcane focus
- (a) a scholar's pack or (b) a dungeoneer's pack
- Leather armor, any simple weapon, and two daggers

---

## Condensed Version

**Starting Equipment**: Warlocks get a ranged option, a focus, a pack, and light armor with backup weapons.

| Choice | Option A | Option B |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Weapon** | Light crossbow + 20 bolts | Any simple weapon |
| **Focus** | Component pouch | Arcane focus |
| **Pack** | Scholar's pack | Dungeoneer's pack |
| **Always** | Leather armor (AC 11 + DEX) + any simple weapon + 2 daggers | |

### Recommended Choices

- **Light crossbow** — ranged backup before you get *eldritch blast* online with [[08-Eldritch-Invocations|Agonizing Blast]] (though *eldritch blast* will outclass it quickly)
- **Arcane focus** — cleaner than tracking component pouch materials
- **Scholar's pack** — fits the lore-seeker theme; dungeoneer's pack is fine for dungeon-heavy campaigns

**Key Concept**: Equipment matters less for warlocks than most classes because [[Eldritch Blast|*eldritch blast*]] replaces weapon attacks almost immediately. The light crossbow is a decent level 1 fallback. Leather armor + DEX is your only armor option. [[09-Pact-Boon|Pact of the Blade]] warlocks will conjure their own weapon at 3rd level, making the starting weapon choice largely irrelevant.
