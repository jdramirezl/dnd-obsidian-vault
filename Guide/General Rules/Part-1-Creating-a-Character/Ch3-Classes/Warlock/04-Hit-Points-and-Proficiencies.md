# Warlock - Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per warlock level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per warlock level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armor:** Light armor
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons
- **Tools:** None
- **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
- **Skills:** Choose two skills from Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Nature, and Religion

---

## Condensed Version

**Hit Points**: d8 Hit Die — moderate durability, same as clerics, bards, and rogues.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Hit Die** | 1d8 per warlock level |
| **HP at 1st Level** | 8 + CON mod |
| **HP per Level After 1st** | 1d8 (or 5) + CON mod |

**Proficiencies**: Light armor and simple weapons — functional but limited.

| Category | Proficiencies |
|----------|--------------|
| **Armor** | Light armor |
| **Weapons** | Simple weapons |
| **Tools** | None |
| **Saving Throws** | Wisdom, Charisma |
| **Skills** | Choose 2 from: Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Nature, Religion |

**Key Concept**: Warlocks get d8 hit points (middle of the road) and light armor — tougher than [[Wizard|wizards]] and [[Sorcerer|sorcerers]] but squishier than [[Cleric|clerics]]. WIS and CHA saves are excellent defensive saves (the two most common mental save types). The skill list leans toward knowledge and social skills. [[09-Pact-Boon|Pact of the Blade]] warlocks may want to supplement their limited weapon proficiencies through invocations.
