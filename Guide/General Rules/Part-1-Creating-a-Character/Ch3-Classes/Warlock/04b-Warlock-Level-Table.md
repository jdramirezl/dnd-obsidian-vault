## Condensed Version

### Key Milestones

- **Level 1** — [[06-Otherworldly-Patron|Otherworldly Patron]] + [[07-Pact-Magic|Pact Magic]] — patron choice and 1 spell slot
- **Level 2** — [[08-Eldritch-Invocations|Eldritch Invocations]] (2) — at-will abilities online, **major power spike** (Agonizing Blast)
- **Level 3** — [[09-Pact-Boon|Pact Boon]] (Chain/Blade/Tome) — second identity-defining choice, slots become 2nd level
- **Level 5** — Slots become 3rd level, 3rd invocation — **power spike** (Thirsting Blade available for Blade pact)
- **Level 9** — Slots reach maximum: 5th level — all Pact Magic slots are now 5th level forever
- **Level 11** — [[11-Mystic-Arcanum|Mystic Arcanum]] (6th) + 3rd spell slot — access to high-level magic begins
- **Level 17** — Mystic Arcanum (9th) + 4th spell slot — full high-level access
- **Level 20** — [[12-Eldritch-Master|Eldritch Master]] — extra short rest recharge for all Pact Magic slots

### Unique Pact Magic Progression

Unlike every other caster, warlocks have:
- **Maximum 4 spell slots** (at 17th level) — never more
- **All slots are the same level** — always cast at maximum power
- **Slots recharge on short rest** — not long rest
- **Slot level caps at 5th** — higher spells come from [[11-Mystic-Arcanum|Mystic Arcanum]] (1/long rest each)

### Invocations Known Progression

Starts at 2 (level 2), gains one at levels 5, 7, 9, 12, 15, 18 → maximum 8 invocations at 18th level. Can swap one invocation per level-up.

**Key Concept**: The warlock table looks radically different from other casters. Few spell slots (max 4) but they recharge on short rests and are always cast at maximum level. Invocations provide reliable at-will power that scales independently. After level 9, spell slot level stops growing — [[11-Mystic-Arcanum|Mystic Arcanum]] fills the gap for 6th–9th level spells at 1/long rest each.
