# Warlock - Mystic Arcanum

## Original Text

At 11th level, your patron bestows upon you a magical secret called an arcanum. Choose one 6th-level spell from the warlock spell list as this arcanum.

You can cast your arcanum spell once without expending a spell slot. You must finish a long rest before you can do so again.

At higher levels, you gain more warlock spells of your choice that can be cast in this way: one 7th-level spell at 13th level, one 8th-level spell at 15th level, and one 9th-level spell at 17th level. You regain all uses of your Mystic Arcanum when you finish a long rest.

---

## Condensed Version

**Mystic Arcanum**: The warlock's solution for high-level spells (6th–9th) — one spell per level, once per long rest each.

### Arcanum Progression

| Warlock Level | Arcanum Spell Level | Uses |
|---------------|---------------------|------|
| 11th | 6th | 1/long rest |
| 13th | 7th | 1/long rest |
| 15th | 8th | 1/long rest |
| 17th | 9th | 1/long rest |

### Key Rules

- Choose one spell from the warlock spell list for each arcanum
- Cast without expending a spell slot (separate from [[07-Pact-Magic|Pact Magic]])
- Each arcanum recharges on a long rest (not short rest)
- **Cannot be swapped** — once chosen, you're locked in (unlike spells known)
- These are in addition to your normal Pact Magic slots

**Key Concept**: Mystic Arcanum is how warlocks access 6th–9th level spells, since Pact Magic slots cap at 5th level. Unlike other casters who get multiple high-level slots and can choose which spells to prepare, warlocks get exactly one spell per level (6th/7th/8th/9th) and can cast each once per long rest. Choose carefully — these cannot be changed. This makes warlock high-level casting more limited but still potent, and your Pact Magic slots (which recharge on short rests) remain your bread and butter.
