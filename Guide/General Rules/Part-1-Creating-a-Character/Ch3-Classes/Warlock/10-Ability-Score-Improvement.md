# Warlock - Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

**Ability Score Improvement**: Standard ASI progression at 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level.

| Level | ASI # |
|-------|-------|
| 4th | 1st |
| 8th | 2nd |
| 12th | 3rd |
| 16th | 4th |
| 19th | 5th |

**Options per ASI**:
- +2 to one ability score, OR
- +1 to two ability scores
- Maximum 20 in any score

### Priority for Warlocks

1. **Charisma to 20** — your spellcasting ability, affects spell save DC, spell attack, and [[08-Eldritch-Invocations|Agonizing Blast]] damage
2. **Constitution** — HP and concentration saves
3. **Dexterity** — AC in light armor, initiative

**Key Concept**: Standard 5-ASI progression (same as most classes). Maxing Charisma is the top priority for all warlock builds — it powers your spells, your save DC, and (with Agonizing Blast) your primary damage cantrip. Feats like [[41-War-Caster|War Caster]] (concentration) or [[30-Resilient|Resilient]] (Constitution) are strong alternatives to raw stat increases.
