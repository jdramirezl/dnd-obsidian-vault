# Warlock - Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a warlock quickly by following these suggestions. First, Charisma should be your highest ability score, followed by Constitution. Second, choose the charlatan background. Third, choose the *eldritch blast* and *chill touch* cantrips, along with the 1st-level spells *ray of sickness* and *witch bolt*.

---

## Condensed Version

**Quick Build**: Fast-track warlock creation in three steps.

| Step | Choice |
|------|--------|
| **Ability Scores** | Charisma highest, then Constitution |
| **Background** | Charlatan |
| **Cantrips** | [[Eldritch Blast|*eldritch blast*]], [[Chill Touch|*chill touch*]] |
| **1st-Level Spells** | [[Ray of Sickness|*ray of sickness*]], [[Witch Bolt|*witch bolt*]] |

**Key Concept**: Prioritize Charisma (your spellcasting ability) then Constitution (for HP and concentration saves). The charlatan background fits the warlock's deceptive, pact-making nature. *Eldritch blast* is the warlock's signature cantrip — it scales with level and becomes devastating with [[08-Eldritch-Invocations|Agonizing Blast]] at 2nd level. *Chill touch* provides necrotic damage and anti-healing utility.
