## Description

Warlocks quest for knowledge that lies hidden in the fabric of the multiverse. Through occult ceremony, each Warlock is drawn into a binding pact with a powerful patron. Drawing on the ancient knowledge of beings such as angels, archfey, demons, devils, hags, and alien entities of the Far Realm, Warlocks piece together arcane secrets to bolster their own power.

### Becoming a Warlock…

**As a Level 1 Character:** Gain all traits in the Core Warlock Traits table and the level 1 features.

**As a Multiclass Character:** Gain the Hit Point Die and Light armor training. Gain the level 1 features. See multiclassing rules for spell slots.

## Class Features

### Level 1: Eldritch Invocations

You gain one invocation of your choice. If an invocation has a prerequisite, you must meet it. Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one invocation with another for which you qualify. You gain more invocations at higher levels as shown in the table.

### Level 1: Pact Magic

You can cast spells through your pact. You know two Warlock cantrips. Charisma is your spellcasting ability. You can use an Arcane Focus as a Spellcasting Focus. **All Pact Magic spell slots are the same level** and you regain all expended slots on a Short or Long Rest.

**Prepared Spells of Level 1+.** To start, choose two level 1 Warlock spells. Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one spell with another of an eligible level.

### Level 2: Magical Cunning

Perform an esoteric rite for 1 minute to regain expended Pact Magic spell slots (up to half your max, round up). Once per Long Rest.

### Level 3: Warlock Subclass

You gain a Warlock subclass of your choice.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

ASI feat or another feat. Again at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 9: Contact Patron

You always have Contact Other Plane prepared. You can cast it without a spell slot to contact your patron, automatically succeeding on the saving throw. Once per Long Rest.

### Level 11: Mystic Arcanum

Choose one level 6 Warlock spell. Cast it once per Long Rest without a spell slot. Additional arcanum spells at levels 13 (7th), 15 (8th), and 17 (9th).

### Level 19: Epic Boon

You gain an Epic Boon feat or another feat.

### Level 20: Eldritch Master

When you use Magical Cunning, you regain **all** your expended Pact Magic spell slots.

## Subclass: Fiend Patron

*Make a Deal with the Lower Planes*

### Level 3: Dark One's Blessing

When you reduce an enemy to 0 HP, gain Temporary HP equal to CHA mod + Warlock level (min 1). Also triggers if someone else reduces an enemy within 10 ft of you to 0 HP.

### Level 3: Fiend Spells

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Burning Hands, Command, Scorching Ray, Suggestion |
| 5 | Fireball, Stinking Cloud |
| 7 | Fire Shield, Wall of Fire |
| 9 | Geas, Insect Plague |

### Level 6: Dark One's Own Luck

Add 1d10 to an ability check or saving throw (after seeing the roll, before effects). CHA mod uses per Long Rest, max once per roll.

### Level 10: Fiendish Resilience

Choose one damage type (not Force) on a Short or Long Rest. You have Resistance to it until you choose a different one.

### Level 14: Hurl Through Hell

Once per turn on a hit, target makes CHA save or disappears through the Lower Planes. Takes 8d10 Psychic damage (if not a Fiend) and is Incapacitated until end of your next turn. Once per Long Rest or spend a Pact Magic slot to restore.
