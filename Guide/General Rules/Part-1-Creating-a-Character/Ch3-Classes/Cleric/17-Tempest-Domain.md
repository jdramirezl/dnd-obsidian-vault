# Cleric - Tempest Domain

## Original Text

Gods whose portfolios include the Tempest domain—including Talos, Umberlee, Kord, Zeboim, the Devourer, Zeus, and Thor—govern storms, sea, and sky. They include gods of lightning and thunder, gods of earthquakes, some fire gods, and certain gods of violence, physical strength, and courage. In some pantheons, a god of this domain rules over other deities and is known for swift justice delivered by thunderbolts. In the pantheons of seafaring people, gods of this domain are ocean deities and the patrons of sailors. Tempest gods send their clerics to inspire fear in the common folk, either to keep those folk on the path of righteousness or to encourage them to offer sacrifices of propitiation to ward off divine wrath.

### Tempest Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 1st | fog cloud, thunderwave |
| 3rd | gust of wind, shatter |
| 5th | call lightning, sleet storm |
| 7th | control water, ice storm |
| 9th | destructive wave, insect plague |

### Bonus Proficiencies

At 1st level, you gain proficiency with martial weapons and heavy armor.

### Wrath of the Storm

Also at 1st level, you can thunderously rebuke attackers. When a creature within 5 feet of you that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to cause the creature to make a Dexterity saving throw. The creature takes 2d8 lightning or thunder damage (your choice) on a failed saving throw, and half as much damage on a successful one.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (a minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

### Channel Divinity: Destructive Wrath

Starting at 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to wield the power of the storm with unchecked ferocity.

When you roll lightning or thunder damage, you can use your Channel Divinity to deal maximum damage, instead of rolling.

### Thunderbolt Strike

At 6th level, when you deal lightning damage to a Large or smaller creature, you can also push it up to 10 feet away from you.

### Divine Strike

At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 thunder damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

### Stormborn

At 17th level, you have a flying speed equal to your current walking speed whenever you are not underground or indoors.

## Condensed Version

### Tempest Domain Overview

**Theme**: Storms, sea, sky, lightning, thunder, and divine wrath
**Deity Examples**: Talos, Umberlee, Kord, Zeboim, the Devourer, Zeus, Thor

### Domain Spells (Always Prepared, Free)

| Cleric Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 1st | *fog cloud*, *thunderwave* |
| 3rd | *gust of wind*, *shatter* |
| 5th | *call lightning*, *sleet storm* |
| 7th | *control water*, *ice storm* |
| 9th | *destructive wave*, *insect plague* |

### 1st Level: Bonus Proficiencies

- **Martial weapons** and **heavy armor** proficiency
- Makes Tempest clerics the most combat-ready cleric subclass

### 1st Level: Wrath of the Storm

- **Trigger**: Creature within 5 ft hits you with an attack
- **Reaction**: Force a Dex save → 2d8 lightning or thunder damage (half on save)
- **Uses**: Wisdom modifier per long rest
- Punishes melee attackers automatically

### 2nd Level: Channel Divinity — Destructive Wrath

- When you roll **lightning or thunder damage**, deal **maximum damage** instead
- Works on any source: spells, Wrath of the Storm, Divine Strike
- **Example**: *call lightning* = max 3d10 = **30 damage** instead of rolling
- **Example**: *shatter* = max 3d8 = **24 damage** instead of rolling

### 6th Level: Thunderbolt Strike

- When you deal **lightning damage** to a Large or smaller creature, push it **up to 10 ft**
- Combos with *call lightning*, Wrath of the Storm, *thunderwave*

### 8th Level: Divine Strike

- **Extra weapon damage**: +1d8 thunder per hit (once per turn)
- Increases to **2d8** at 14th level
- Can be maximized with Destructive Wrath

### 17th Level: Stormborn

- **Flying speed** equal to walking speed
- Only works **outdoors** (not underground or indoors)
- No concentration required — permanent while outside

### Tempest Domain Feature Summary

| Level | Feature | Key Benefit |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Bonus Proficiencies | Martial weapons + heavy armor |
| 1 | Wrath of the Storm | Reaction: 2d8 lightning/thunder to melee attackers |
| 2 | Destructive Wrath | Maximize lightning/thunder damage rolls |
| 6 | Thunderbolt Strike | Push creatures 10 ft when dealing lightning damage |
| 8 | Divine Strike | +1d8 (2d8 at 14) thunder on weapon hits |
| 17 | Stormborn | Fly speed = walk speed (outdoors only) |

**Key Concept**: The Tempest Domain is the most offensively powerful cleric subclass. Martial weapons, heavy armor, reactive lightning damage, and the ability to maximize lightning/thunder damage make this a devastating combatant. Destructive Wrath + *call lightning* or *shatter* delivers guaranteed massive damage. Stormborn grants permanent outdoor flight at high levels.
