# Cleric - Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a cleric quickly by following these suggestions. First, Wisdom should be your highest ability score, followed by Strength or Constitution. Second, choose the acolyte background.

---

## Condensed Version

**Quick Build Guide**: Fast-track cleric creation with optimal choices.

### Ability Score Priority

**1st Priority - Wisdom** (highest score):
- Your spellcasting ability
- Determines spell save DC
- Determines spell attack bonus
- Affects number of prepared spells

**2nd Priority - Strength or Constitution**:
- **Strength**: If you plan to fight in melee with [[03-Weapons|weapons]]
- **Constitution**: If you want more [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] and survivability
- Both are good choices depending on playstyle

### Background

**Recommended**: Acolyte background
- Thematically appropriate (temple service)
- Provides relevant skills (Insight, Religion)
- Gives religious connections

**Key Concept**: For a quick, effective cleric, maximize Wisdom (spellcasting), choose Strength or Constitution as secondary (combat/survival), and take the Acolyte background for thematic fit.
