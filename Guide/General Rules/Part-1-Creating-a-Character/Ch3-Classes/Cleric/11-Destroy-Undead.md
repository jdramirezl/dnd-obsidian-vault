# Cleric - Destroy Undead

## Original Text

Starting at 5th level, when an undead fails its saving throw against your Turn Undead feature, the creature is instantly destroyed if its challenge rating is at or below a certain threshold, as shown in the Destroy Undead table.

| Cleric Level | Destroys Undead of CR . . . |
|---|---|
| 5th | 1/2 or lower |
| 8th | 1 or lower |
| 11th | 2 or lower |
| 14th | 3 or lower |
| 17th | 4 or lower |

---

## Condensed Version

### Destroy Undead

**Gained At**: 5th level
**How It Works**: Upgrades your Turn Undead — weak undead are **instantly destroyed** instead of just turned

### CR Threshold by Level

| Cleric Level | Instantly Destroys CR . . . |
|---|---|
| 5th | 1/2 or lower (e.g., shadows, zombies) |
| 8th | 1 or lower (e.g., ghouls, specters) |
| 11th | 2 or lower (e.g., ghasts, ogre zombies) |
| 14th | 3 or lower (e.g., mummies, wights) |
| 17th | 4 or lower (e.g., banshees, ghost) |

**How It Triggers**:
1. You use Turn Undead (Channel Divinity)
2. An undead fails its Wisdom [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]
3. If its CR is at or below your threshold → **instantly destroyed**
4. If its CR is above your threshold → turned as normal

**Key Concept**: Destroy Undead is a passive upgrade to Turn Undead that instantly kills weak undead instead of just frightening them away. The CR threshold increases as you level up, letting you obliterate progressively stronger undead.
