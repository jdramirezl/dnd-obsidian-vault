# Cleric - Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

### Ability Score Improvement (ASI)

**Gained At**: 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level (5 total)

**Options Each Time**:
- Increase **one** [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] by **+2**, OR
- Increase **two** ability scores by **+1** each

**Maximum**: No ability score can exceed 20

### Common Cleric Choices

- **Wisdom +2**: Improves spell save DC, spell attack, and prepared spell count
- **Constitution +2**: More [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] and better concentration saves
- **Wisdom +1 / Constitution +1**: Balanced improvement

**Example**: A 4th-level cleric with Wisdom 16 could raise it to 18 (+2 increase), improving their spell save DC by 1 and gaining one more prepared spell per day.

**Key Concept**: Clerics get five ASIs over their career to boost [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] (max 20). Wisdom is typically the priority since it powers all cleric spellcasting.
