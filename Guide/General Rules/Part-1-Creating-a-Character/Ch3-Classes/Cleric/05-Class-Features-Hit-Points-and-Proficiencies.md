# Cleric - Class Features: Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

As a cleric, you gain the following class features.

## Hit Points

**Hit Dice:** 1d8 per cleric level
**Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
**Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per cleric level after 1st

## Proficiencies

**Armor:** Light armor, medium armor, shields
**Weapons:** Simple weapons
**Tools:** None
**Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
**Skills:** Choose two from History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, and Religion

---

## Condensed Version

**Cleric Class Features**: Basic capabilities all clerics gain.

### Hit Points

**[[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit dice]]**: 1d8 per cleric level
- Medium durability (between d6 and d10)

**Starting hit points** (1st level):
- 8 + Constitution modifier
- Example: Con +2 = 10 HP

**Hit Points at Higher Levels**:
- Roll 1d8 + Constitution modifier per level
- OR take average: 5 + Constitution modifier
- Add to hit point maximum

### Proficiencies

**[[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]]**:
- Light armor
- Medium armor
- Shields
- **Good protection** without heavy armor penalties

**[[03-Weapons|weapons]]**:
- Simple weapons only
- Includes: mace, club, quarterstaff, light crossbow, etc.
- No martial weapons (swords, axes, etc.)

**Tools**:
- None

**[[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]**:
- **Wisdom**: Primary stat, resisting mental effects
- **Charisma**: Resisting banishment, possession, etc.

**Skills** (choose 2 from 5):
- **History**: Knowledge of past events
- **Insight**: Reading people and situations
- **Medicine**: Healing and diagnosing
- **Persuasion**: Convincing others
- **Religion**: Knowledge of gods, rites, and faiths

### Typical Skill Choices

**Healer Focus**: Insight + Medicine
**Scholar Focus**: History + Religion
**Diplomat Focus**: Insight + Persuasion

**Key Concept**: Clerics have medium durability (d8 Hit Die), can wear [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] and use shields for good protection, but are limited to simple [[03-Weapons|weapons]]. They're proficient in Wisdom saves (their primary ability) and Charisma saves (divine connection).
