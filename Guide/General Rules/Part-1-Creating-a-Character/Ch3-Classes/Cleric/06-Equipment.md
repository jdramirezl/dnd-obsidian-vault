# Cleric - Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:
• (a) a mace or (b) a warhammer (if proficient)
• (a) scale mail, (b) leather armor, or (c) chain mail (if proficient)
• (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) any simple weapon
• (a) a priest's pack or (b) an explorer's pack
• A shield and a holy symbol

---

## Condensed Version

**Starting Equipment**: Choose from options below, plus equipment from your background.

### Equipment Choices

**[[03-Weapons|weapons]] Choice 1**:
- **(a)** Mace, OR
- **(b)** Warhammer (only if proficient—requires specific domain or race)

**[[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] Choice**:
- **(a)** Scale mail (medium armor, AC 14 + Dex mod max 2), OR
- **(b)** Leather armor (light armor, AC 11 + Dex mod), OR
- **(c)** Chain mail (heavy armor, AC 16, requires Str 13—only if proficient from domain)

**Weapon Choice 2**:
- **(a)** Light crossbow and 20 bolts, OR
- **(b)** Any simple weapon

**Pack Choice**:
- **(a)** Priest's pack, OR
- **(b)** Explorer's pack

**Always Included**:
- shield (+2 AC)
- Holy symbol (spellcasting focus)

### Typical Loadout Example

**Standard Cleric**:
- Mace
- Scale mail + shield (AC 16 + Dex mod)
- Light crossbow
- Priest's pack
- Holy symbol

**Heavy [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] Cleric** ([[13-Life-Domain|Life Domain]] or similar):
- Warhammer
- Chain mail + shield (AC 18)
- Any simple [[03-Weapons|weapons]]
- Priest's pack
- Holy symbol

**Key Concept**: Clerics start well-equipped with armor, shield, weapons, and a holy symbol. Armor choice depends on domain proficiencies—most clerics use scale mail + shield for AC 16.
