# Unarmored Defense

## Original Text

Beginning at 1st level, while you are wearing no armor and not wielding a shield, your AC equals 10 + your Dexterity modifier + your Wisdom modifier.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept**: AC = **10 + DEX + WIS** when wearing no armor and no shield.

### AC Comparison

| DEX mod | WIS mod | Monk AC | Equivalent Armor |
|---------|---------|---------|-----------------|
| +2 | +2 | 14 | Scale mail |
| +3 | +2 | 15 | Breastplate |
| +3 | +3 | 16 | Half plate |
| +4 | +3 | 17 | Splint |
| +5 | +4 | 19 | Plate + shield |
| +5 | +5 | 20 | Plate + shield +1 |

> [!warning] Restrictions
> - **No armor** — wearing any armor disables this feature
> - **No shield** — wielding a shield disables this feature
> - Does NOT stack with Barbarian's Unarmored Defense (choose one if multiclassing)

> [!tip] DM Note
> Monk AC starts lower than armored classes but scales well with ability score improvements. By tier 3-4, a monk can rival plate-armored fighters without any magic items. Bracers of Defense (AC +2) are the monk's best friend.
