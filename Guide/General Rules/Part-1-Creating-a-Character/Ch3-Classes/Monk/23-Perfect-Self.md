## Original Text

**Perfect Self**

At 20th level, when you roll for initiative and have no ki points remaining, you regain 4 ki points.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** At 20th level (capstone), rolling initiative with 0 ki points remaining automatically restores 4 ki points.
