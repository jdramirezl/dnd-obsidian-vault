# Monk - Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a shortsword or (b) any simple weapon
- (a) a dungeoneer's pack or (b) an explorer's pack
- 10 darts

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept**: Monks start with **minimal gear** — no armor, and their best weapon is often their own fists.

### Starting Equipment

| Choice | Option A | Option B |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Weapon | Shortsword | Any simple weapon |
| Pack | Dungeoneer's pack | Explorer's pack |
| Ranged | 10 darts | — |

> [!tip] DM Note
> **Shortsword** is the best starting choice — it's a monk weapon that deals 1d6 (better than the 1d4 Martial Arts die at level 1). Once Martial Arts die reaches d8 at level 11, unarmed strikes overtake most monk weapons. Darts provide a ranged option using DEX.
