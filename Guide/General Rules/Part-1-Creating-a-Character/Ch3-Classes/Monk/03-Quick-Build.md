# Monk - Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a monk quickly by following these suggestions. First, make Dexterity your highest ability score, followed by Wisdom. Second, choose the hermit background.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept**: DEX > WIS > CON for a standard monk build.

| Priority | Ability | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1st | **Dexterity** | Attack/damage, AC, saves |
| 2nd | **Wisdom** | AC ([[06-Unarmored-Defense\|Unarmored Defense]]), ki save DC |
| 3rd | **Constitution** | Hit points (d8 is fragile) |

**Background**: [[10-Hermit\|Hermit]] (Medicine, Religion — fits monastic theme)

> [!tip] DM Note
> Monks are MAD (Multiple Ability Dependent) — they need DEX, WIS, and CON. Point buy or standard array works better than rolling for most monk builds.
