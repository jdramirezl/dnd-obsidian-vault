## Original Text

**Way of the Four Elements**

You follow a monastic tradition that teaches you to harness the elements. When you focus your ki, you can align yourself with the forces of creation and bend the four elements to your will, using them as an extension of your body. Some members of this tradition dedicate themselves to a single element, but others weave the elements together.

### Disciple of the Elements (3rd Level)

When you choose this tradition at 3rd level, you learn magical disciplines that harness the power of the four elements. A discipline requires you to spend ki points each time you use it.

You know the Elemental Attunement discipline and one other elemental discipline of your choice. You learn one additional elemental discipline of your choice at 6th, 11th, and 17th level. Whenever you learn a new elemental discipline, you can also replace one you already know with a different discipline.

**Casting Elemental Spells.** Some elemental disciplines allow you to cast spells. To cast one of these spells, you use its casting time and other rules, but you don't need to provide material components for it. From 5th level, you can spend additional ki points to increase the spell's level by 1 per extra ki point spent.

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** Elemental spellcasting through ki — learn disciplines at 3rd/6th/11th/17th level, spend ki to cast elemental spells or create effects.

- **Disciple of the Elements (3rd):** Start with Elemental Attunement + 1 discipline. Learn new ones at 6th, 11th, 17th. Can swap one when learning a new one.
- **Ki Spell Scaling:** From 5th level, spend extra ki to upcast. Max ki per spell: 3 (5th–8th), 4 (9th–12th), 5 (13th–16th), 6 (17th–20th).
- **No-Req Disciplines:** Elemental Attunement (free cantrip), Fangs of the Fire Snake (1 ki, fire reach), Fist of Four Thunders (2 ki, *[[Thunderwave|thunderwave]]*), Fist of Unbroken Air (2 ki, blast+push), Rush of the Gale Spirits (2 ki, *[[Gust of Wind|gust of wind]]*), Shape the Flowing River (1 ki, ice/water control), Sweeping Cinder Strike (2 ki, *[[Burning Hands|burning hands]]*), Water Whip (2 ki, pull/prone).
- **6th-Level:** Clench of the North Wind (3 ki, *[[Hold Person|hold person]]*), Gong of the Summit (3 ki, *[[Shatter|shatter]]*).
- **11th-Level:** Eternal Mountain Defense (5 ki, *[[Stoneskin|stoneskin]]*), Flames of the Phoenix (4 ki, *[[Fireball|fireball]]*), Mist Stance (4 ki, *[[Gaseous Form|gaseous form]]*), Ride the Wind (4 ki, *[[Fly|fly]]*).
- **17th-Level:** Breath of Winter (6 ki, *[[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]]*), River of Hungry Flame (5 ki, *[[Wall of Fire|wall of fire]]*), Wave of Rolling Earth (6 ki, *[[Wall of Stone|wall of stone]]*).
