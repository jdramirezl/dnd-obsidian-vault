# Monk - Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice**: 1d8 per monk level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level**: 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per monk level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armor**: None
- **Weapons**: Simple weapons, shortswords
- **Tools**: Choose one type of artisan's tools or one musical instrument
- **Saving Throws**: Strength, Dexterity
- **Skills**: Choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, and Stealth

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept**: Monks have **no armor proficiency** — they rely on [[06-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]] (10 + DEX + WIS) instead.

### Hit Points

| Level | HP (average) | HP (max roll) |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| 1st | 8 + CON | 8 + CON |
| 2nd+ | +5 + CON/level | +8 + CON/level |

### Proficiency Summary

| Category | Proficiencies |
|----------|--------------|
| Armor | **None** |
| Weapons | Simple weapons, shortswords |
| Tools | 1 artisan's tool OR 1 instrument |
| Saves | **STR**, **DEX** |
| Skills | 2 from: Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, Stealth |

> [!warning] No Armor
> Monks cannot wear armor or use shields and benefit from [[07-Martial-Arts|Martial Arts]], [[06-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]], or [[09-Unarmored-Movement|Unarmored Movement]]. This is a class restriction, not just a proficiency gap.

### Recommended Skills
- **Acrobatics** — fits the agile martial artist fantasy
- **Stealth** — synergizes with Way of Shadow; useful for scouting
