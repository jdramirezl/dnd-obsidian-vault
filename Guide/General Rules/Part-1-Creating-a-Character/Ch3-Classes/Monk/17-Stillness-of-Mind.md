## Original Text

**Stillness of Mind**

Starting at 7th level, you can use your action to end one effect on yourself that is causing you to be charmed or frightened.

---

## Condensed Version

**Key Concept:** At 7th level, use your action to end one [[Charmed|charmed]] or [[Frightened|frightened]] effect on yourself.
