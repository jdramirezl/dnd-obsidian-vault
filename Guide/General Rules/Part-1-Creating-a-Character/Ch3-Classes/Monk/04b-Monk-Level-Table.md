## Condensed Version

**Key Concept**: Monks scale through three parallel tracks — **Martial Arts die** (d4→d10), **Ki Points** (2→20), and **Unarmored Movement** (+10→+30 ft).

### Key Milestones

| Level | Milestone | Why It Matters |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| 1st | Martial Arts + Unarmored Defense | Core identity online |
| 2nd | Ki (2 points) | Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, Step of the Wind |
| 3rd | Monastic Tradition | Subclass choice |
| 5th | Extra Attack + Stunning Strike + d6 | **Massive power spike** — best level for monks |
| 6th | Ki-Empowered Strikes | Unarmed = magical (bypass resistance) |
| 9th | Run on walls and water | Unarmored Movement improvement |
| 14th | Diamond Soul | Proficiency in ALL saves + ki reroll |
| 18th | Empty Body | Invisibility + resistance to all but force |
| 20th | Perfect Self | Never run out of ki |

> [!tip] DM Note
> Level 5 is the monk's biggest power spike. Stunning Strike alone can trivialize single-target encounters — plan accordingly with multiple enemies or legendary resistances.
