# Barbarian - Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

**Ability Score Improvement** (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th Level): Increase one ability score by 2, or two scores by 1 each. Maximum 20.

### ASI Levels

| Level | Typical Priority |
|-------|-----------------|
| 4th   | STR to 18 or 20, or a feat |
| 8th   | Max STR to 20, or CON boost |
| 12th  | CON, DEX, or feat |
| 16th  | Round out secondary stats or feat |
| 19th  | Final optimization |

### ASI Count

**5 total** — standard for most classes (fighters get 7)

### Barbarian Priority

1. **Strength 20** — affects attack rolls, damage rolls, Athletics checks, and grappling
2. **Constitution** — HP, [[01-Rage|Rage]] durability, and CON saves
3. **Dexterity** — AC (especially with [[01-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]]), initiative, DEX saves
4. **Feats** (if using optional rule): Great Weapon Master, Sentinel, Tough, Resilient (WIS)

**Key Concept**: Barbarians get 5 ASIs at the standard levels. Maximizing Strength is the top priority since it powers melee attacks, Athletics, and grappling. After STR 20, boost CON (HP/Rage durability) or DEX (AC with Unarmored Defense), or take feats.
