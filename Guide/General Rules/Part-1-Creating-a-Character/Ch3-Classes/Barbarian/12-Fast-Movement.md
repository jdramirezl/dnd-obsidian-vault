# Fast Movement

## Original Text

Starting at 5th level, your speed increases by 10 feet while you aren't wearing heavy armor.

---

## Condensed Version

**Fast Movement** (5th Level): +10 feet to your speed while not wearing heavy armor.

### Key Details

- **Always active**: Does not require [[06-Rage|rage]] — works all the time
- **No heavy armor**: Works with medium armor, light armor, or [[07-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]]
- **Stacks with other speed bonuses**: Dash action, mobile feat, etc.
- **Standard speed becomes 40 ft.**: Most races have 30 ft. base speed → 40 ft. with Fast Movement

### Tactical Value

- Close distance to ranged enemies faster
- Better battlefield positioning
- Harder for enemies to kite you
- Combined with Dash action: 80 ft. of movement in a single turn

**Key Concept**: Fast Movement gives barbarians 40 ft. speed (for most races), making them excellent at closing gaps to reach ranged enemies and controlling the battlefield. It's always active — no rage or action required.
