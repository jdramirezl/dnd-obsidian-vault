# Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

### Class Features

As a barbarian, you gain the following class features.

### Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d12 per barbarian level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 12 + your Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d12 (or 7) + your Constitution modifier per barbarian level after 1st

### Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor, medium armor, shields
Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons
Tools: None
Saving Throws: Strength, Constitution
Skills: Choose two from Animal Handling, Athletics, Intimidation, Nature, Perception, and Survival

---

## Condensed Version

**Barbarian Class Features**: The highest hit dice in the game (d12) with broad martial proficiencies.

### Hit Points

**Hit Dice**: 1d12 per barbarian level — **the highest in the game**
**1st Level HP**: 12 + Constitution modifier
- Example: Con 16 (+3) = 15 HP at level 1
**Higher Levels**: 1d12 (or 7) + Constitution modifier per level
- Example: Level 5, Con 16 (+3) = 12 + 4×(7+3) = 52 HP (using average)

### Armor Proficiencies

**Light armor**: Leather, studded leather
**Medium armor**: Hide, chain shirt, scale mail, breastplate, half plate
**Shields**: Yes
- Note: Heavy armor is NOT included — and [[06-Rage|Rage]] benefits require not wearing heavy armor
- [[07-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]] often competes with medium armor at higher levels

### Weapon Proficiencies

**Simple weapons**: Clubs, daggers, javelins, handaxes, etc.
**Martial weapons**: Longswords, greatswords, greataxes, etc.
- Full access to every weapon in the game

### Tool Proficiencies

**None**: Barbarians don't gain tool proficiencies from the class

### Saving Throws

**Strength**: Resisting being pushed, grappled, or physically overpowered
**Constitution**: Resisting poison, disease, and maintaining concentration (though barbarians can't concentrate while raging)

### Skills (Choose 2)

| Skill | Ability | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Animal Handling | WIS | Calm or control animals |
| Athletics | STR | Climbing, jumping, swimming, grappling |
| Intimidation | CHA | Threaten and coerce |
| Nature | INT | Recall lore about terrain, plants, animals |
| Perception | WIS | Spot hidden things |
| Survival | WIS | Track, forage, navigate |

**Recommended**: Athletics (grappling, climbing) and Perception (most-used skill in the game)

**Key Concept**: Barbarians have d12 hit dice — the highest in the game — giving them unmatched raw HP. Combined with [[06-Rage|Rage]] resistance and medium armor/shield proficiency, they are the most durable melee class. Strength and Constitution saving throws align perfectly with their front-line role.
