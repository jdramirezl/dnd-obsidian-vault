# Indomitable Might

## Original Text

Beginning at 18th level, if your total for a Strength check is less than your Strength score, you can use that score in place of the total.

---

## Condensed Version

**Indomitable Might** (18th Level): Your Strength checks can never roll below your Strength score.

### How It Works

**Trigger**: Any Strength check (Athletics, raw Strength checks, etc.)
**Effect**: If your total roll (d20 + modifiers) is less than your Strength score, use your Strength score instead
**Example**: With STR 20, your Athletics check can never be lower than 20 — even on a natural 1

### Effective Minimums

| Strength Score | Minimum Check Result |
|----------------|---------------------|
| 16 | 16 |
| 18 | 18 |
| 20 | 20 |
| 24 (with [[18-Primal-Champion|Primal Champion]]) | 24 |

### Key Details

- **Strength checks only**: Does not apply to Strength saving throws or attack rolls
- **Replaces the total**: Includes the d20 roll + all modifiers — if the total is below your score, use the score
- **Always active**: No action, no resource cost
- **Stacks with [[06-Rage|Rage]]**: Rage gives advantage on Strength checks, and Indomitable Might sets a floor — together, you almost always roll very high
- **Grappling powerhouse**: Athletics checks for grappling/shoving have a guaranteed minimum

**Key Concept**: Indomitable Might guarantees a minimum result on all Strength checks equal to your Strength score. With STR 20+, you can never fail routine Strength tasks, and with [[18-Primal-Champion|Primal Champion]] pushing STR to 24, the minimum becomes extraordinary.
