# Extra Attack

## Original Text

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

---

## Condensed Version

**Extra Attack** (5th Level): Make two attacks when you take the Attack action on your turn.

### Key Details

- **Two attacks per Attack action**: Up from one — doubles your damage output
- **Does not stack**: Unlike the [[Fighter/README|Fighter]], barbarians do NOT gain additional Extra Attacks at higher levels
- **Mix and match**: Each attack can target a different creature or use a different weapon
- **Works with [[08-Reckless-Attack|Reckless Attack]]**: Both attacks get advantage
- **[[06-Rage|Rage]] damage applies to each hit**: +2 to +4 bonus damage on every successful melee Strength attack

### Damage Comparison (Greataxe, STR 16, Raging)

| Level | Attacks | Average Damage per Turn |
|-------|---------|------------------------|
| 1st–4th | 1 | 1d12 + 3 + 2 = 11.5 |
| 5th+ | 2 | 2 × (1d12 + 3 + 2) = 23 |

**Key Concept**: Extra Attack at 5th level is the barbarian's biggest single power spike — doubling attack output. Combined with [[06-Rage|Rage]] damage on every hit and [[08-Reckless-Attack|Reckless Attack]] advantage, barbarians become devastating melee damage dealers at this level.
