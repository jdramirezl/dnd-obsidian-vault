# Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a barbarian quickly by following these suggestions. First, put your highest ability score in Strength, followed by Constitution. Second, choose the outlander background.

---

## Condensed Version

**Quick Build**: Fast barbarian creation in two steps.

### Steps

1. **Ability Scores**: Highest in **Strength**, second highest in **Constitution**
2. **Background**: **Outlander**

### Why This Works

- **Strength** powers your melee attacks, [[06-Rage|Rage]] damage bonus, and [[08-Reckless-Attack|Reckless Attack]]
- **Constitution** boosts HP (d12 hit die benefits greatly), [[07-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]] AC, and Constitution saving throws
- **Outlander** gives Athletics, Survival — both on the barbarian skill list — plus a thematic wilderness origin

**Key Concept**: Strength first, Constitution second, Outlander background. This gives you maximum melee damage, the highest possible HP, and strong Unarmored Defense AC.
