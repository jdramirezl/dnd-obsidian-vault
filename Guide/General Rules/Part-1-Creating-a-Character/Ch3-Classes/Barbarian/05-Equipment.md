# Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a greataxe or (b) any martial melee weapon
- (a) two handaxes or (b) any simple weapon
- An explorer's pack and four javelins

---

## Condensed Version

**Starting Equipment**: Choose from the following options (in addition to background equipment).

### Choice 1: Primary Weapon

| Option | Details | Damage |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **(a) Greataxe** | Heavy, two-handed | 1d12 slashing |
| **(b) Any martial melee weapon** | Your choice | Varies |

- **Greataxe**: Iconic barbarian weapon, maximizes [[14-Brutal-Critical|Brutal Critical]] (one big die = higher crit ceiling)
- **Greatsword** (2d6): Higher average damage but lower crit potential than greataxe

### Choice 2: Secondary Weapons

| Option | Details | Damage |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **(a) Two handaxes** | Light, thrown (20/60) | 1d6 slashing each |
| **(b) Any simple weapon** | Your choice | Varies |

### Fixed Equipment

- **Explorer's pack**: Backpack, bedroll, mess kit, tinderbox, 10 torches, 10 days rations, waterskin, 50 ft. rope
- **Four javelins**: Thrown (30/120), 1d6 piercing — ranged option before closing to melee

### Example Loadouts

**Classic Barbarian**: Greataxe + two handaxes + explorer's pack + 4 javelins
- Two-handed devastation with thrown backup

**Sword & Board**: Martial weapon (longsword) + shield (buy with gold) + simple weapon + explorer's pack + 4 javelins
- Higher AC, versatile damage (1d8/1d10)

**Key Concept**: Barbarian starting equipment is simple and effective. The greataxe is the iconic choice — its single d12 damage die synergizes perfectly with [[14-Brutal-Critical|Brutal Critical]] at higher levels. Javelins provide a ranged option for closing distance.
