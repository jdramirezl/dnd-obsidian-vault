# Unarmored Defense

## Original Text

While you are not wearing any armor, your Armor Class equals 10 + your Dexterity modifier + your Constitution modifier. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

---

## Condensed Version

**Unarmored Defense** (1st Level): When wearing no armor, your AC = 10 + DEX mod + CON mod. Shields still work.

### AC Calculation

**Formula**: AC = 10 + Dexterity modifier + Constitution modifier

### Example AC Values

| DEX mod | CON mod | Unarmored AC | With Shield (+2) |
|---------|---------|-------------|------------------|
| +2 | +2 | 14 | 16 |
| +2 | +3 | 15 | 17 |
| +3 | +3 | 16 | 18 |
| +2 | +4 | 16 | 18 |
| +3 | +4 | 17 | 19 |

### Key Details

- **No armor required**: You must be wearing NO armor (not even light armor)
- **Shields allowed**: A shield's +2 AC stacks with Unarmored Defense
- **Always active**: No activation needed — it's your base AC when unarmored
- **Scales with stats**: Gets better as DEX and CON increase via ASIs or magic items

### Unarmored Defense vs. Medium Armor

| Option | AC | Notes |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Half plate + shield | 17 + 2 = 19 | Max DEX +2, disadvantage on Stealth |
| Breastplate + shield | 16 + 2 = 18 | Max DEX +2, no Stealth penalty |
| Unarmored (DEX +2, CON +3) + shield | 17 | Scales higher with ASIs/items |
| Unarmored (DEX +2, CON +5) + shield | 19 | Matches half plate + shield |

- At low levels, medium armor is usually better
- At high levels (CON 20+), Unarmored Defense catches up or surpasses medium armor
- [[18-Primal-Champion|Primal Champion]] at 20th level pushes CON to 24 (+7), making Unarmored Defense exceptional

**Key Concept**: Unarmored Defense lets barbarians fight without armor and still have competitive AC, using 10 + DEX + CON. At low levels, medium armor is typically better, but Unarmored Defense scales with ability score increases and becomes very strong at high levels — especially with the [[18-Primal-Champion|Primal Champion]] capstone.
