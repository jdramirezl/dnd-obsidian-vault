# Primal Champion

## Original Text

At 20th level, you embody the power of the wilds. Your Strength and Constitution scores increase by 4. Your maximum for those scores is now 24.

---

## Condensed Version

**Primal Champion** (20th Level — Capstone): +4 to Strength and Constitution scores, with a new maximum of 24.

### What You Gain

| Stat | Before | After | Modifier Change |
|------|--------|-------|-----------------|
| Strength | 20 (max) | 24 | +5 → +7 |
| Constitution | 20 (max) | 24 | +5 → +7 |

### Impact on Other Features

- **HP**: +2 CON mod = +2 HP per level retroactively = **+40 HP** at level 20
- **[[07-Unarmored-Defense|Unarmored Defense]]**: AC increases by +2 (from CON) — e.g., AC 17 → 19 unarmored
- **[[06-Rage|Rage]] (Unlimited)**: At 20th level, rages are unlimited
- **Attack rolls**: +2 to hit from STR increase
- **Damage rolls**: +2 damage per hit from STR increase
- **[[17-Indomitable-Might|Indomitable Might]]**: Minimum Strength check becomes 24
- **Saving throws**: +2 to both STR and CON saves

### Full Level 20 Barbarian Profile (Typical)

- **HP**: ~285 (with CON 24) — effectively ~570 against physical damage while raging
- **AC**: 10 + 3 (DEX) + 7 (CON) = 20 unarmored, 22 with shield
- **Attack**: +13 to hit (proficiency +6, STR +7)
- **Damage**: 1d12 + 7 + 4 (rage) = avg 17.5 per hit, two attacks = 35 per round
- **Unlimited rages**: Never run out

**Key Concept**: Primal Champion is one of the strongest capstones in the game. +4 to both STR and CON (max 24) retroactively boosts HP by 40, increases AC, attack, damage, saves, and Strength check minimums. Combined with unlimited rages, a 20th-level barbarian is a nearly unkillable melee powerhouse.
