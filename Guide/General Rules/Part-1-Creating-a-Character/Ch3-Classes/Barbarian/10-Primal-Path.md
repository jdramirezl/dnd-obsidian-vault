# Primal Path

## Original Text

At 3rd level, you choose a path that shapes the nature of your rage. Choose the Path of the Berserker or the Path of the Totem Warrior, both detailed at the end of the class description. Your choice grants you features at 3rd level and again at 6th, 10th, and 14th levels.

---

## Condensed Version

**Primal Path** (3rd Level): Choose your subclass, which defines how your [[06-Rage|rage]] manifests.

### Available Paths

| Path | Theme | Key Benefit |
|------|-------|-------------|
| [[19-Path-of-the-Berserker|Path of the Berserker]] | Untrammeled fury, violence as purpose | Bonus action melee attack while raging (Frenzy) |
| [[20-Path-of-the-Totem-Warrior|Path of the Totem Warrior]] | Spiritual journey, animal totems | Customizable magical benefits (Bear, Eagle, Wolf) |

### Subclass Feature Levels

- **3rd level** — First path feature
- **6th level** — Second path feature
- **10th level** — Third path feature
- **14th level** — Fourth path feature (capstone)

### Path Comparison

- **Berserker**: Higher single-target damage output via Frenzy, but costs [[Exhaustion]] levels. More aggressive, less versatile.
- **Totem Warrior**: More defensive and versatile. Bear totem grants resistance to ALL damage except psychic while raging — widely considered one of the strongest subclass features in the game.

**Key Concept**: Primal Path is the barbarian's subclass choice at 3rd level. The Berserker maximizes offensive output at the cost of exhaustion, while the Totem Warrior offers powerful defensive and utility options through animal spirit choices.
