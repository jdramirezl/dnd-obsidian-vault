# Rogue - Reliable Talent

## Original Text

By 11th level, you have refined your chosen skills until they approach perfection. Whenever you make an ability check that lets you add your proficiency bonus, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.

---

## Condensed Version

### Reliable Talent (Level 11)

**What It Does**: You can never roll below 10 on proficient skill checks

### How It Works

- Applies to **any ability check** where you add your [[03-Proficiency-Bonus|proficiency bonus]]
- If you roll a **1–9** on the d20, treat it as a **10**
- Rolls of **10–20** are unaffected (use the actual roll)

### Minimum Results with Reliable Talent

**Example**: Level 11 rogue, Dex 20 (+5), Prof +4, [[06-Expertise|Expertise]] in Stealth:

| Skill | Modifier | Minimum Roll (10 + mod) |
|-------|----------|------------------------|
| Stealth (Expertise) | +13 | **23** |
| Stealth (Proficient) | +9 | **19** |
| Thieves' Tools (Expertise) | +13 | **23** |
| Perception (Proficient) | +4 to +9 | **14–19** |

### Important Details

- Works with **Expertise** — doubled proficiency still counts
- Does **not** apply to attack rolls or [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] (only ability checks)
- Does **not** apply to skills you're not proficient in
- Stacks with **Jack of All Trades** if multiclassing (since it adds prof bonus)

### Practical Impact

- You literally **cannot fail** easy or moderate skill checks in your specialties
- DC 15 lock? Automatic success with thieves' tools expertise
- DC 20 Stealth? Your minimum is 23 — guaranteed

**Key Concept**: Reliable Talent ensures you never roll below 10 on any proficient ability check, making your trained skills virtually fail-proof. Combined with Expertise, your minimum results on key skills will exceed most DCs, making you the most dependable skill user in the game.
