# Rogue - Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

As a rogue, you have the following class features.

### Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d8 per rogue level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per rogue level after 1st

### Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor
Weapons: Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords
Tools: Thieves' tools
Saving Throws: Dexterity, Intelligence
Skills: Choose four from Acrobatics, Athletics, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception, Performance, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand, and Stealth

---

## Condensed Version

### Hit Points

**[[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit dice]]**: 1d8 per rogue level
**HP at 1st Level**: 8 + Constitution modifier
- Example: Con 14 (+2) = 10 HP at level 1
**HP at Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 5) + Constitution modifier per level after 1st
- Example: Level 5, Con 14 (+2) = 10 + 4 × (5+2) = 38 HP (taking average)

### Proficiencies

**[[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]]**: Light armor only
- Leather, studded leather — keeps you mobile

**[[03-Weapons|weapons]]**: Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords
- Rapier is your best melee option (1d8 finesse)
- Shortsword for dual wielding (1d6 finesse, light)
- Hand crossbow for ranged (1d6, one-handed)

**Tools**: Thieves' tools
- Pick locks, disarm traps

**[[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]**: Dexterity, Intelligence
- Dex saves: dodge fireballs, traps
- Int saves: resist mental illusions

**Skills**: Choose **four** from:
- Acrobatics, Athletics, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception, Performance, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
- **Note**: Four skill proficiencies is the most of any class

**Key Concept**: Rogues are medium-durability characters (d8 hit die) with the broadest skill selection in the game — four proficiencies from 11 options — plus light armor, finesse weapons, and thieves' tools. Dexterity and Intelligence saving throws round out their defensive profile.
