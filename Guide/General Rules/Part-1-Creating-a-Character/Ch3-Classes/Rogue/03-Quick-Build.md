# Rogue - Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a rogue quickly by following these suggestions. First, Dexterity should be your highest ability score. Make Intelligence your next-highest if you want to excel at Investigation. Choose Charisma instead if you plan to emphasize deception and social interaction. Second, choose the criminal background.

---

## Condensed Version

### Quick Build Steps

**1. [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]** (prioritize in order):
- **Dexterity**: Highest — your primary stat for attacks, AC, stealth, and key skills
- **Intelligence**: Second-highest if you want Investigation focus
- **Charisma**: Second-highest if you want Deception and social skills

**2. Background**: Criminal

### Build Paths at a Glance

| Focus | Primary | Secondary | Background |
|-------|---------|-----------|------------|
| Investigator | Dexterity | Intelligence | Criminal |
| Social Rogue | Dexterity | Charisma | Criminal |

**Key Concept**: A quick-build rogue prioritizes Dexterity above all else, then chooses Intelligence for investigation or Charisma for social manipulation, paired with the Criminal background for thematic skills and tools.
