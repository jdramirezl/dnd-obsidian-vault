## Condensed Version

- **Sneak Attack Scaling:** Increases by 1d6 every odd level — 1d6 (1st) → 10d6 (19th–20th)
- **ASIs (6 total):** Levels 4, 8, 10, 12, 16, 19
- **Expertise:** Level 1 (choose 2 proficiencies), Level 6 (choose 2 more)
- **Archetype Features:** Level 3 (choose archetype), then 9, 13, 17
- **Core Defensive Features:** Uncanny Dodge (5th), Evasion (7th), Slippery Mind (15th), Elusive (18th)
- **Capstone:** Stroke of Luck (20th) — turn a miss into a hit, or treat a failed ability check as a 20 (1/short or long rest)

**Key Concept:** The Rogue gains power through consistent Sneak Attack scaling and skill mastery (Expertise, Reliable Talent at 11th) rather than extra attacks, making every single hit count.
