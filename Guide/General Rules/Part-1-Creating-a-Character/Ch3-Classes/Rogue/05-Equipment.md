# Rogue - Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a rapier or (b) a shortsword
- (a) a shortbow and quiver of 20 arrows or (b) a shortsword
- (a) a burglar's pack, (b) a dungeoneer's pack, or (c) an explorer's pack
- Leather armor, two daggers, and thieves' tools

---

## Condensed Version

### Starting Equipment Choices

**Melee [[03-Weapons|weapons]]** (choose one):
- **(a) Rapier** — 1d8 piercing, finesse (best single-weapon damage)
- **(b) Shortsword** — 1d6 piercing, finesse, light

**Ranged/Secondary Weapon** (choose one):
- **(a) Shortbow + 20 arrows** — 1d6 piercing, range 80/320
- **(b) Shortsword** — gives you a second melee weapon for dual wielding

**Adventure Pack** (choose one):
- **(a) Burglar's pack** — best for stealth/infiltration missions
- **(b) Dungeoneer's pack** — best for dungeon crawling
- **(c) Explorer's pack** — best for wilderness travel

**Always Included**:
- Leather [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] (AC 11 + Dex modifier)
- Two daggers (1d4 piercing, finesse, light, thrown 20/60)
- Thieves' tools

### Recommended Setup

| Style | [[03-Weapons|weapons]] 1 | Weapon 2 | Pack |
|-------|----------|----------|------|
| Classic Rogue | Rapier | Shortbow + arrows | Burglar's |
| Dual Wielder | Shortsword | Shortsword | Burglar's |
| Dungeon Delver | Rapier | Shortbow + arrows | Dungeoneer's |

**Key Concept**: Rogues start with finesse weapons (using Dexterity for attacks), a ranged option, light [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]], daggers for backup or throwing, and thieves' tools — everything needed for stealth, combat, and infiltration from level 1.
