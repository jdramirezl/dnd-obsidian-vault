# Rogue - Thieves' Cant

## Original Text

During your rogue training you learned thieves' cant, a secret mix of dialect, jargon, and code that allows you to hide messages in seemingly normal conversation. Only another creature that knows thieves' cant understands such messages. It takes four times longer to convey such a message than it does to speak the same idea plainly.

In addition, you understand a set of secret signs and symbols used to convey short, simple messages, such as whether an area is dangerous or the territory of a thieves' guild, whether loot is nearby, or whether the people in an area are easy marks or will provide a safe house for thieves on the run.

---

## Condensed Version

### Thieves' Cant (Level 1)

**What It Is**: A secret language combining dialect, jargon, and coded speech

### Spoken Cant

- **Hidden Messages**: Embed secret meaning in normal-sounding conversation
- **Who Understands**: Only creatures who know thieves' cant
- **Speed**: Takes 4× longer than speaking plainly
  - Example: A 1-minute message takes 4 minutes in thieves' cant

### Signs and Symbols

**You can read secret markings** that convey:
- Whether an area is **dangerous**
- If it's **thieves' guild territory**
- Whether **loot is nearby**
- If locals are **easy marks**
- Where to find a **safe house**

### Roleplaying Uses

- Communicate with criminal contacts without others knowing
- Read hidden signs in cities and dungeons
- Leave messages for other rogues
- Identify fellow thieves' guild members

**Key Concept**: Thieves' cant is a free "secret language" all rogues learn at 1st level — both a spoken code hidden in normal conversation (at 4× the time) and a system of written signs marking dangers, opportunities, and safe havens in the criminal underworld.
