# Rogue - Expertise

## Original Text

At 1st level, choose two of your skill proficiencies, or one of your skill proficiencies and your proficiency with thieves' tools. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of the chosen proficiencies.

At 6th level, you can choose two more of your proficiencies (in skills or with thieves' tools) to gain this benefit.

---

## Condensed Version

### Expertise (Levels 1 and 6)

**What It Does**: Doubles your [[03-Proficiency-Bonus|proficiency bonus]] for chosen skills

**At 1st Level**: Choose 2 proficiencies to double:
- Two skill proficiencies, OR
- One skill proficiency + thieves' tools

**At 6th Level**: Choose 2 more proficiencies to double

### How the Math Works

| Level | Prof Bonus | Normal | With Expertise |
|-------|-----------|--------|----------------|
| 1–4 | +2 | +2 | +4 |
| 5–8 | +3 | +3 | +6 |
| 9–12 | +4 | +4 | +8 |
| 13–16 | +5 | +5 | +10 |
| 17–20 | +6 | +6 | +12 |

**Example**: Level 5 rogue, Dex 18 (+4), Expertise in Stealth:
- Normal Stealth: +4 (Dex) + 3 (prof) = +7
- Expertise Stealth: +4 (Dex) + 6 (doubled prof) = +10

### Popular Expertise Choices

- **Stealth**: Essential for sneaking and ambushes
- **Thieves' Tools**: Picking locks and disarming traps
- **Perception**: Spotting hidden threats
- **Persuasion/Deception**: Social manipulation

**Key Concept**: Expertise is one of the rogue's defining features, doubling your [[03-Proficiency-Bonus|proficiency bonus]] on up to four skills by level 6. This makes rogues unmatched specialists — no other class can achieve such high skill bonuses so early.
