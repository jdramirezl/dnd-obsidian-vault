## Overview

Rogues rely on cunning, stealth, and their foes' vulnerabilities to get the upper hand. They prioritize subtle strikes over brute strength — one precise strike rather than a barrage of blows.

### Becoming a Rogue…

**As a Level 1 Character:** Gain all Core Rogue Traits and level 1 features.

**As a Multiclass Character:** Gain Hit Point Die, one skill, Thieves' Tools proficiency, Light armor training, and level 1 features.

## Class Features

### Level 1: Expertise
Gain Expertise in **two** skill proficiencies. Two more at level 6.

### Level 1: Sneak Attack
1/turn, deal extra damage (see table) when you have Advantage or an ally is within 5 ft of target. Requires Finesse or Ranged weapon.

### Level 1: Thieves' Cant
You know Thieves' Cant and one other language.

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery
Use mastery properties of **two** weapon kinds (swap on Long Rest).

### Level 2: Cunning Action
Bonus Action: Dash, Disengage, or Hide.

### Level 3: Rogue Subclass
Choose a subclass.

### Level 3: Steady Aim
Bonus Action → Advantage on next attack this turn. Can't have moved; Speed becomes 0.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
ASI or feat. Repeats at 8, 10, 12, 16.

### Level 5: Cunning Strike
When dealing Sneak Attack damage, forgo dice for effects (DC = 8 + DEX + Prof):
- **Poison (1d6):** CON save or Poisoned 1 min (requires Poisoner's Kit).
- **Trip (1d6):** DEX save or Prone (Large or smaller).
- **Withdraw (1d6):** Move half Speed without opportunity attacks.

### Level 5: Uncanny Dodge
Reaction when hit by visible attacker → halve the damage.

### Level 7: Evasion
DEX saves for half damage → take none on success, half on fail.

### Level 7: Reliable Talent
Skill/tool proficiency checks: treat d20 rolls of 9 or lower as 10.

### Level 11: Improved Cunning Strike
Use up to **two** Cunning Strike effects per Sneak Attack.

### Level 14: Devious Strikes
New Cunning Strike options:
- **Daze (2d6):** CON save or limited to move/action/bonus action next turn.
- **Knock Out (6d6):** CON save or Unconscious 1 min.
- **Obscure (3d6):** DEX save or Blinded until end of next turn.

### Level 15: Slippery Mind
Proficiency in Wisdom and Charisma saving throws.

### Level 18: Elusive
No attack roll can have Advantage against you (unless Incapacitated).

### Level 19: Epic Boon

### Level 20: Stroke of Luck
Turn a failed D20 Test into a 20. 1/Short or Long Rest.

## Subclass: Thief

*Hunt for Treasure as a Classic Adventurer*

- **Lv 3 — Fast Hands:** Bonus Action for Sleight of Hand, Utilize, or Magic action (magic item).
- **Lv 3 — Second-Story Work:** Climb Speed = your Speed. Use DEX for jump distance.
- **Lv 9 — Supreme Sneak:** *Stealth Attack (1d6)* — attack doesn't end Invisible if you end turn behind Three-Quarters or Total Cover.
- **Lv 13 — Use Magic Device:** Attune 4 items; on charge use, roll d6 (6 = free); use any Spell Scroll (INT-based, DC 10 + spell level for 2nd+).
- **Lv 17 — Thief's Reflexes:** Two turns in first round of combat (normal Initiative and Initiative − 10).
