# Rogue - Blindsense

## Original Text

Starting at 14th level, if you are able to hear, you are aware of the location of any hidden or invisible creature within 10 feet of you.

---

## Condensed Version

### Blindsense (Level 14)

**What It Does**: Detect hidden and [[Invisible|invisible]] creatures within 10 feet by hearing

### Requirements

- You must be **able to hear** (not [[Deafened|deafened]])
- Creature must be within **10 feet**

### What It Detects

- **Hidden creatures**: Enemies using Stealth, hiding behind cover
- **[[Invisible|invisible]] creatures**: Creatures under [[Invisibility|Invisibility]], [[Greater Invisibility|Greater Invisibility]], or natural invisibility

### What It Means in Combat

- You **know the location** of [[Invisible|invisible]] attackers nearby
- You can **target them** with attacks (though they may still have advantage/you disadvantage from being unseen)
- Invisible enemies can't sneak up on you within 10 feet
- Negates the "guessing which square" problem for invisible foes

### Limitations

- **10-foot range only** — doesn't help at distance
- Requires **hearing** — [[Silence|Silence]] spell or [[Deafened|deafened]] condition shuts it down
- Doesn't grant **sight** — you know where they are, but they're still unseen for attack roll purposes

**Key Concept**: Blindsense lets you pinpoint the exact location of any hidden or [[Invisible|invisible]] creature within 10 feet as long as you can hear, eliminating the guessing game of fighting unseen foes in close quarters. It's a niche but powerful anti-stealth and anti-invisibility tool.
