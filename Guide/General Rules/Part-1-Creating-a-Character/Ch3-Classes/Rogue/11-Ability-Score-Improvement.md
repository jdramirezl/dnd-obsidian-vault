# Rogue - Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 10th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

### Ability Score Improvement (Levels 4, 8, 10, 12, 16, 19)

**What It Does**: Increase your [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] (or take a feat, if your DM allows)

**Options Each Time**:
- Increase **one** ability score by **+2**, OR
- Increase **two** ability scores by **+1** each

**Maximum**: No ability score can exceed 20

### ASI Levels for Rogues

| Level | Total ASIs |
|-------|-----------|
| 4 | 1st |
| 8 | 2nd |
| 10 | 3rd |
| 12 | 4th |
| 16 | 5th |
| 19 | 6th |

**Note**: Rogues get **6 ASIs** — among the most of any class. This makes rogues extremely customizable.

### Priority Recommendations

1. **Dexterity to 20** — Improves attacks, AC, initiative, Stealth, and key skills
2. **Constitution** — More HP for survivability
3. **Charisma or Intelligence** — Depending on your skill focus
4. **Feats** (if allowed): Alert, Lucky, Sentinel, Skulker, Mobile

**Key Concept**: Rogues receive six [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] Improvements at levels 4, 8, 10, 12, 16, and 19 — among the most of any class. Each lets you add +2 to one score or +1 to two scores (max 20), making rogues highly adaptable over a campaign.
