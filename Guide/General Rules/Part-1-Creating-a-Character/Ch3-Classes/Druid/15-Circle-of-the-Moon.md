# Circle of the Moon

*Druid subclass — chosen at 2nd level*

## Original Text

Druids of the Circle of the Moon are fierce guardians of the wilds. Their order gathers under the full moon to share news and trade warnings. They haunt the deepest parts of the wilderness, where they might go for weeks on end before crossing paths with another humanoid creature, let alone another druid.

Changeable as the moon, a druid of this circle might prowl as a great cat one night, soar over the treetops as an eagle the next day, and crash through the undergrowth in bear form to drive off a trespassing monster. The wild is in the druid's blood.

---

## Condensed Version

**Identity**: Fierce guardians of the wilds who gather under the full moon
**Playstyle**: Wild Shape–focused combat druid — higher CR forms, bonus action shifting, elemental transformation

### Feature Progression

| Level | Feature |
|:-----:|---------|
| 2nd | Combat Wild Shape, Circle Forms |
| 6th | Primal Strike |
| 10th | Elemental Wild Shape |
| 14th | Thousand Forms |

---

## Combat Wild Shape (2nd Level)

Two benefits:

1. **Bonus Action Shift**: Use Wild Shape as a **bonus action** instead of an action
2. **Healing in Beast Form**: Expend one **spell slot** as a bonus action → regain **1d8 HP per slot level**

| Slot Level | HP Regained |
|:----------:|:-----------:|
| 1st | 1d8 |
| 2nd | 2d8 |
| 3rd | 3d8 |
| 4th | 4d8 |
| 5th | 5d8 |

> Bonus action shifting means you can cast a spell **and** Wild Shape in the same turn.

---

## Circle Forms (2nd Level)

Overrides the base [[08-Wild-Shape|Wild Shape]] CR limits:

### Moon Druid CR Limits vs. Base Druid

| Level | Base Max CR | Moon Max CR | Moon Example |
|:-----:|:-----------:|:-----------:|-------------|
| 2nd | 1/4 | **1** | Brown bear |
| 3rd | 1/4 | **1** | Dire wolf |
| 6th | 1/3 | **2** | Giant constrictor snake |
| 9th | 1/3 | **3** | Giant scorpion |
| 12th | 1/3 | **4** | Elephant |
| 15th | 1/3 | **5** | Giant crocodile |
| 18th | 1/3 | **6** | Mammoth |

**Moon CR formula (6th+)**: Druid level ÷ 3 (rounded down)

> Movement restrictions from the Beast Shapes table still apply (no fly until 8th, no swim until 4th).

---

## Primal Strike (6th Level)

Your attacks in beast form count as **magical** for overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks and damage.

> Critical for mid-to-high level play where many creatures resist nonmagical damage.

---

## Elemental Wild Shape (10th Level)

Expend **two uses** of Wild Shape at once to transform into:

| Elemental | CR |
|-----------|----|
| Air Elemental | 5 |
| Earth Elemental | 5 |
| Fire Elemental | 5 |
| Water Elemental | 5 |

> These are **not beasts** — a special exception to Wild Shape's beast-only rule. Elementals have powerful abilities (Earth Glide, Fire damage aura, etc.).

---

## Thousand Forms (14th Level)

Cast *[[Alter Self|alter self]]* **at will** (no spell slot required).

> Allows subtle physical changes — aquatic adaptation, natural weapons, change appearance. Useful for infiltration and social encounters.
