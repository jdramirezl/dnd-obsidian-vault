# Timeless Body

*18th-level druid feature*

## Original Text

Starting at 18th level, the primal magic that you wield causes you to age more slowly. For every 10 years that pass, your body ages only 1 year.

---

## Condensed Version

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Level** | 18th |
| **Effect** | Age at 1/10th the normal rate |
| **Ratio** | 10 real years = 1 year of physical aging |

> Primarily a **flavor/roleplay** feature. Mechanically minor, but explains why archdruids can be ancient yet physically vital.
