# Druid

> **Primary Ability:** Wisdom
> **Hit Point Die:** D8 per Druid level
> **Saving Throw Proficiencies:** Intelligence and Wisdom
> **Skill Proficiencies:** *Choose 2:* Animal Handling, Arcana, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, or Survival
> **Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple weapons
> **Tool Proficiencies:** Herbalism Kit
> **Armor Training:** Light armor and Shields
> **Starting Equipment:** *Choose A or B:* (A) Leather Armor, Shield, Sickle, Druidic Focus (Quarterstaff), Explorer's Pack, Herbalism Kit, and 9 GP; or (B) 50 GP

Druids belong to ancient orders that call on the forces of nature. Harnessing the magic of animals, plants, and the four elements, Druids heal, transform into animals, and wield elemental destruction.

Revering nature above all, individual Druids gain their magic from nature, a nature deity, or both, and they typically unite with other Druids to perform rites that mark the passage of the seasons and other natural cycles.

Druids are concerned with the delicate ecological balance that sustains plant and animal life and with the need for people to live in harmony with nature.

## Becoming a Druid…

### As a Level 1 Character
- Gain all the traits in the Core Druid Traits table.
- Gain the Druid's level 1 features, which are listed in the Druid Features table.

### As a Multiclass Character
- Gain the following traits: Hit Point Die and training with Light armor and Shields.
- Gain the Druid's level 1 features. See the multiclassing rules in chapter 2 to determine your available spell slots.

## Druid Class Features

### Level 1: Spellcasting

You have learned to cast spells through studying the mystical forces of nature.

**Cantrips.** You know two cantrips of your choice from the Druid spell list. Whenever you gain a Druid level, you can replace one. When you reach Druid levels 4 and 10, you learn another cantrip.

**Spell Slots.** The Druid Features table shows how many spell slots you have. You regain all expended slots when you finish a Long Rest.

**Prepared Spells of Level 1+.** You prepare the list of level 1+ spells available for you to cast. To start, choose four level 1 spells from the Druid spell list. The chosen spells must be of a level for which you have spell slots.

**Changing Your Prepared Spells.** Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can change your list of prepared spells.

**Spellcasting Ability.** Wisdom is your spellcasting ability.

**Spellcasting Focus.** You can use a Druidic Focus as a Spellcasting Focus.

### Level 1: Druidic

You know Druidic, the secret language of Druids. You always have the *Speak with Animals* spell prepared. You can use Druidic to leave hidden messages (DC 15 Intelligence/Investigation to spot, can't decipher without magic).

### Level 1: Primal Order

You have dedicated yourself to one of the following sacred roles of your choice.

### Level 2: Wild Shape

As a Bonus Action, you shape-shift into a Beast form you have learned. You stay in that form for hours equal to half your Druid level or until you use Wild Shape again, have the Incapacitated condition, or die.

**Number of Uses.** You can use Wild Shape twice. You regain one use on a Short Rest, all uses on a Long Rest.

**Known Forms.** You know four Beast forms (max CR 1/4, no Fly Speed). You can replace one known form on a Long Rest.

| Druid Level | Known Forms | Max CR | Fly Speed |
|-------------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| 2 | 4 | 1/4 | No |
| 4 | 6 | 1/2 | No |
| 8 | 8 | 1 | Yes |

**Rules While Shape-Shifted:**
- **Temporary Hit Points.** You gain Temporary HP equal to your Druid level.
- **Game Statistics.** Replaced by Beast's stat block, but you retain creature type, HP, Hit Dice, Int/Wis/Cha, class features, languages, and feats.
- **No Spellcasting.** You can't cast spells, but shape-shifting doesn't break Concentration.
- **Objects.** Equipment falls, merges, or is worn (DM decides practicality).

### Level 2: Wild Companion

As a Magic action, expend a spell slot or Wild Shape use to cast *Find Familiar* without Material components. The familiar is Fey and disappears when you finish a Long Rest.

### Level 3: Druid Subclass

You gain a Druid subclass of your choice.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

You gain the ASI feat or another feat. Again at Druid levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Wild Resurgence

Once per turn, if you have no Wild Shape uses left, you can expend a spell slot to regain one use. You can also expend one Wild Shape use to gain a level 1 spell slot (once per Long Rest).

### Level 7: Elemental Fury

The might of the elements flows through you. You gain one option of your choice.

### Level 15: Improved Elemental Fury

- **Potent Spellcasting.** Druid cantrips with range 10 ft+ gain +300 ft range.
- **Primal Strike.** Extra damage increases to 2d8.

### Level 18: Beast Spells

While in Wild Shape, you can cast spells in Beast form (except spells with costly or consumed Material components).

### Level 19: Epic Boon

You gain an Epic Boon feat or another feat of your choice.

### Level 20: Archdruid

- **Evergreen Wild Shape.** On Initiative with no Wild Shape uses left, regain one use.
- **Nature Magician.** Convert Wild Shape uses into a spell slot (each use = 2 spell levels). Once per Long Rest.
- **Longevity.** For every ten years that pass, your body ages only one year.

## Druid Subclass: Circle of the Land

*Celebrate Connection to the Natural World*

### Level 3: Circle of the Land Spells

On a Long Rest, choose a land type: arid, polar, temperate, or tropical. You have the corresponding spells prepared.

### Level 3: Land's Aid

As a Magic action, expend a Wild Shape use. Choose a point within 60 ft — 10-ft-radius Sphere. Creatures of your choice: Con save or 2d6 Necrotic (half on success). One creature of your choice regains 2d6 HP. Increases by 1d6 at levels 10 (3d6) and 14 (4d6).

### Level 6: Natural Recovery

Cast one Circle Spell without a slot (once per Long Rest). On a Short Rest, recover spell slots totaling ≤ half your Druid level (no level 6+ slots). Once per Long Rest.

### Level 10: Nature's Ward

Immune to Poisoned. Resistance based on land type: Arid=Fire, Polar=Cold, Temperate=Lightning, Tropical=Poison.

### Level 14: Nature's Sanctuary

Expend Wild Shape use: 15-ft Cube of spectral trees within 120 ft, lasts 1 minute. You and allies have Half Cover; allies gain your Nature's Ward Resistance. Bonus Action to move the Cube up to 60 ft.
