# Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- *(a)* a wooden shield or *(b)* any simple weapon
- *(a)* a scimitar or *(b)* any simple melee weapon
- Leather armor, an explorer's pack, and a druidic focus

---

## Condensed Version

| Choice | Option A | Option B |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Wooden shield | Any simple weapon |
| 2 | Scimitar | Any simple melee weapon |
| — | Leather armor, explorer's pack, druidic focus | — |

> **Sidebar — Sacred Plants and Wood**: Druids hold certain plants sacred — particularly alder, ash, birch, elder, hazel, holly, juniper, mistletoe, oak, rowan, willow, and yew. These are often used as spellcasting foci. Yew (death/rebirth) for weapon handles, ash (life) and oak (strength) for hafts and shields, alder (air) for thrown weapons. Druids from regions lacking these plants choose local substitutes (e.g., yucca and cactus in deserts).
