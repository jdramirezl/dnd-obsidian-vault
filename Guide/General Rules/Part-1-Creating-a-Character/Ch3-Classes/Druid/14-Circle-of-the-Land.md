# Circle of the Land

*Druid subclass — chosen at 2nd level*

## Original Text

The Circle of the Land is made up of mystics and sages who safeguard ancient knowledge and rites through a vast oral tradition. These druids meet within sacred circles of trees or standing stones to whisper primal secrets in Druidic. The circle's wisest members preside as the chief priests of communities that hold to the Old Faith and serve as advisors to the rulers of those folk.

As a member of this circle, your magic is influenced by the land where you were initiated into the circle's mysterious rites.

---

## Condensed Version

**Identity**: Mystics and sages who safeguard ancient knowledge through oral tradition
**Playstyle**: Enhanced spellcasting druid — more spells prepared, spell slot recovery, defensive features

## Bonus Cantrip (2nd Level)

Learn one additional druid cantrip of your choice (total: **3** at 2nd level vs. 2 for other druids).

---

## Natural Recovery (2nd Level)

During a **short rest**, recover expended spell slots:
- Combined level ≤ half your druid level (rounded **up**)
- No slot can be **6th level or higher**
- Usable **once per long rest**

| Druid Level | Max Slot Levels Recovered | Example |
|:-----------:|:-------------------------:|---------|
| 2nd | 1 level | One 1st-level slot |
| 4th | 2 levels | One 2nd or two 1st-level slots |
| 8th | 4 levels | One 4th, or combinations |

> Comparable to the [[Wizard/README|Wizard]]'s Arcane Recovery.

## Land's Stride (6th Level)

- **Nonmagical difficult terrain** costs no extra movement
- Pass through **nonmagical plants** without slowing or damage (thorns, spines)
- **Advantage** on saves vs. magically created plants that impede movement (e.g., *[[Entangle|entangle]]*)

---

## Nature's Ward (10th Level)

- **Cannot be [[Charmed|charmed]] or [[Frightened|frightened]]** by elementals or fey
- **Immune** to [[Poisoned|poison]] and disease

---

## Nature's Sanctuary (14th Level)

When a **beast or plant creature** attacks you:
1. Must make **WIS save** vs. your druid spell save DC
2. **Failed**: Must choose different target, or attack auto-misses
3. **Passed**: Immune to this effect for 24 hours
4. Creature is **aware** of this effect before attacking
