# Beast Spells

*18th-level druid feature*

## Original Text

Beginning at 18th level, you can cast many of your druid spells in any shape you assume using Wild Shape. You can perform the somatic and verbal components of a druid spell while in a beast shape, but you aren't able to provide material components.

---

## Condensed Version

| Component | Can Perform in Beast Shape? |
|-----------|:-------------------------:|
| **Verbal (V)** | ✅ Yes |
| **Somatic (S)** | ✅ Yes |
| **Material (M)** | ❌ No |

### What This Means

- You can cast **V-only**, **S-only**, and **V+S** spells freely in beast form
- Spells requiring **material components** cannot be cast in beast form (unless you're 20th level — see [[13-Archdruid]])
- This removes the biggest limitation of [[08-Wild-Shape|Wild Shape]]: the inability to cast spells while transformed

> **Massive power spike**: At 18th level, you can be a bear casting *call lightning*, *heal*, or *conjure animals*. Combined with Wild Shape's extra HP pool, this makes druids extremely durable casters.
