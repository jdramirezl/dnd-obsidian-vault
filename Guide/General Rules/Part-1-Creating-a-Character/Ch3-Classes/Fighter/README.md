## Class Summary

**Hit Die**: d10
**Primary Ability**: Strength or Dexterity
**Saves**: Strength, Constitution
**Armor**: All armor, shields
**Weapons**: Simple, martial
**Core Mechanic**: [[08-Action-Surge|Action Surge]] — take an additional action on your turn, plus the most ASIs and Extra Attacks of any class
