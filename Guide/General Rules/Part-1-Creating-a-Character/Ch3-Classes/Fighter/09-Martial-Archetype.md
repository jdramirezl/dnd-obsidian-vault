# Fighter - Martial Archetype

## Original Text

At 3rd level, you choose an archetype that you strive to emulate in your combat styles and techniques. The Champion archetype is detailed at the end of the class description; see the Player's Handbook for information on other martial archetypes. Your archetype grants you features at 3rd level and again at 7th, 10th, 15th, and 18th level.

---

## Condensed Version

**Martial Archetype** (3rd Level): Choose a subclass that defines your fighter's combat specialization.

### How It Works

**Choose at**: 3rd level
**Archetype Features at**: 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, and 18th level

### Available Archetype (Basic Rules)

**Champion**: Focuses on raw physical power and critical hits
- Straightforward and effective
- Enhances [[03-Weapons|weapons]] attacks and physical ability
- Detailed in the Basic Rules (see Champion Archetype files)

### Other Archetypes (Player's Handbook)

The following archetypes are available in the full Player's Handbook:
- **[[16-Battle-Master|Battle Master]]**: Tactical superiority with combat maneuvers
- **[[17-Eldritch-Knight|Eldritch Knight]]**: Combines martial prowess with arcane magic

### Archetype Feature Progression

| Level | What You Get |
|-------|-------------|
| 3rd | First archetype feature (e.g., Improved Critical) |
| 7th | Second archetype feature (e.g., Remarkable Athlete) |
| 10th | Third archetype feature (e.g., Additional Fighting Style) |
| 15th | Fourth archetype feature (e.g., Superior Critical) |
| 18th | Fifth archetype feature (e.g., Survivor) |

**Key Concept**: At 3rd level, you choose a Martial Archetype that shapes your fighter's identity and grants new features at 7th, 10th, 15th, and 18th level. The Basic Rules include the Champion archetype, which focuses on physical excellence and devastating critical hits.
