# Fighter - Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

### Class Features

As a fighter, you gain the following class features.

### Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d10 per fighter level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 10 + your Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per fighter level after 1st

### Proficiencies

Armor: All armor, shields
Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons
Tools: None
Saving Throws: Strength, Constitution
Skills: Choose two skills from Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, and Survival

---

## Condensed Version

**Fighter Class Features**: Strong [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] and the broadest proficiency list of any class.

### Hit Points

**[[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit dice]]**: 1d10 per fighter level (second highest in the game)
**1st Level HP**: 10 + Constitution modifier
- Example: Con 14 (+2) = 12 HP at level 1
**Higher Levels**: 1d10 (or 6) + Constitution modifier per level
- Example: Level 5, Con 14 (+2) = 10 + 4×(6+2) = 42 HP (using average)

### Armor Proficiencies

**All [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]]**: Light, medium, and heavy armor
**Shields**: Yes
- Fighters can wear any armor in the game from level 1
- No other class has this breadth of armor access

### Weapon Proficiencies

**Simple [[03-Weapons|weapons]]**: Clubs, daggers, javelins, etc.
**Martial Weapons**: Longswords, greatswords, longbows, etc.
- Full access to every weapon in the game

### Tool Proficiencies

**None**: Fighters don't gain tool proficiencies from the class

### Saving Throws

**Strength**: Resisting being pushed, [[Grappled|grappled]], or physically overpowered
**Constitution**: Resisting poison, disease, and maintaining concentration

### Skills (Choose 2)

| Skill | Ability | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Acrobatics | DEX | Balance, tumbling, flips |
| Animal Handling | WIS | Calm or control animals |
| Athletics | STR | Climbing, jumping, swimming |
| History | INT | Recall lore about events and wars |
| Insight | WIS | Read people's intentions |
| Intimidation | CHA | Threaten and coerce |
| Perception | WIS | Spot hidden things |
| Survival | WIS | Track, forage, navigate |

**Key Concept**: Fighters have d10 [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit dice]] (excellent durability), proficiency with every [[03-Weapons|weapons]] and [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] type in the game, and Strength/Constitution [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]. They are the most broadly equipped martial class from level 1.
