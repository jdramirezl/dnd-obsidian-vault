# Fighter - Second Wind

## Original Text

You have a limited well of stamina that you can draw on to protect yourself from harm. On your turn, you can use a bonus action to regain hit points equal to 1d10 + your fighter level.

Once you use this feature, you must finish a short or long rest before you can use it again.

---

## Condensed Version

**Second Wind** (1st Level): Self-healing using a bonus action.

### How It Works

**Action Cost**: Bonus action (on your turn)
**Healing**: 1d10 + your fighter level
**Recharge**: Once per short or long rest

### Healing by Level

| Fighter Level | Healing Range | Average |
|---------------|--------------|---------|
| 1st | 2–11 | 6.5 |
| 5th | 6–15 | 10.5 |
| 10th | 11–20 | 15.5 |
| 15th | 16–25 | 20.5 |
| 20th | 21–30 | 25.5 |

### Key Details

- **Bonus action**: You can still Attack and use Second Wind on the same turn
- **Self only**: Cannot heal allies
- **Short rest recharge**: Available multiple times per adventuring day
- **No spell slot**: This is not a spell — it's a class feature, so it works in antimagic zones

### Tactical Tips

- Use when you drop below half HP to stay in the fight
- Since it recharges on short rest, don't hoard it — use it each encounter if needed
- Pairs well with [[08-Action-Surge|Action Surge]]: Attack + Second Wind on the same turn

**Key Concept**: Second Wind is the fighter's signature self-healing ability, letting you regain 1d10 + fighter level HP as a bonus action once per short rest. It keeps fighters in the fight without relying on healers or potions.
