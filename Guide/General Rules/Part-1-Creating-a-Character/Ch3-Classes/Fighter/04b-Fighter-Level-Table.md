## Condensed Version

- **Level 1** — Fighting Style + Second Wind (core kit from day one)
- **Level 2** — **Action Surge** (one use) — extra action per short rest, the Fighter's signature nova
- **Level 3** — **Martial Archetype** chosen (Champion, [[16-Battle-Master|Battle Master]], [[17-Eldritch-Knight|Eldritch Knight]])
- **Levels 4, 6, 8, 12, 14, 16, 19** — **ASI ×7** — more than any other class
- **Level 5** — **Extra Attack** (2 attacks per Attack action)
- **Level 7, 10, 15, 18** — Martial Archetype features (4 subclass upgrades total)
- **Level 9** — **Indomitable** (one use) — reroll a failed [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]
- **Level 11** — **Extra Attack (2)** — 3 attacks per Attack action
- **Level 13** — Indomitable (two uses)
- **Level 17** — Action Surge (two uses) + Indomitable (three uses) — massive power spike
- **Level 20** — **Extra Attack (3)** — 4 attacks per Attack action, capstone

**Key Concept:** The Fighter is defined by *volume* — more attacks (up to 4), more ASIs (7 total, vs. 5 for most classes), and more Action Surges. No other class scales raw martial output this aggressively across all 20 levels.
