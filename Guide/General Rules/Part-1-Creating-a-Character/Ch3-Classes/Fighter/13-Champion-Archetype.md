# Fighter - Champion Archetype

## Original Text

The archetypal Champion focuses on the development of raw physical power honed to deadly perfection. Those who model themselves on this archetype combine rigorous training with physical excellence to deal devastating blows.

---

## Condensed Version

**Champion Archetype**: A martial archetype focused on raw physical power and devastating attacks.

### Archetype Identity

**Core Fantasy**: A warrior who has honed their body into a perfect [[03-Weapons|weapons]]
**Playstyle**: Simple, effective, and reliable — no complex mechanics
**Strengths**: Enhanced critical hits, physical prowess, and survivability

### Champion Feature Progression

| Level | Feature | Summary |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 3rd | Improved Critical | Crit on 19–20 |
| 7th | Remarkable Athlete | Half proficiency to STR/DEX/CON checks |
| 10th | Additional Fighting Style | Choose a second Fighting Style |
| 15th | Superior Critical | Crit on 18–20 |
| 18th | Survivor | Regenerate HP each turn when below half |

### Why Play a Champion?

**Pros**:
- Simplest fighter subclass — great for new players
- Doubled (then tripled) critical hit range is powerful and exciting
- No resources to track beyond base fighter features
- Passive bonuses that always apply

**Cons**:
- Less tactical complexity than [[16-Battle-Master|Battle Master]]
- No spellcasting like [[17-Eldritch-Knight|Eldritch Knight]]
- Relies on dice luck for crits to shine

### Synergies

- **[[11-Extra-Attack|Extra Attack]]**: More attacks = more chances to crit
- **Great [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Fighting**: Reroll low damage dice on those crits
- **[[08-Action-Surge|Action Surge]]**: Double your attacks = double your crit chances

**Key Concept**: The Champion is the straightforward fighter archetype, focusing on expanded critical hit range, physical excellence, and passive survivability. It's the ideal choice for players who want a powerful, reliable warrior without complex resource management.
