# Fighter - Action Surge

## Original Text

Starting at 2nd level, you can push yourself beyond your normal limits for a moment. On your turn, you can take one additional action.

Once you use this feature, you must finish a short or long rest before you can use it again. Starting at 17th level, you can use it twice before a rest, but only once on the same turn.

---

## Condensed Version

**Action Surge** (2nd Level): Take one additional action on your turn.

### How It Works

**Trigger**: On your turn (no action cost — it's free)
**Effect**: Gain one additional action this turn
**Recharge**: Once per short or long rest
**17th Level**: Can use twice between rests, but still only once per turn

### What You Can Do With the Extra Action

- **Attack action**: Make all your attacks again (Extra Attack applies)
- **Dash**: Double your movement for the turn
- **Dodge**: Impose disadvantage on attacks against you
- **Disengage**: Move without provoking opportunity attacks
- **Use an Object**: Interact with something in the environment
- **Cast a Spell**: If you have spellcasting (e.g., [[17-Eldritch-Knight|Eldritch Knight]])

### Damage Potential (Attack + Action Surge Attack)

| Fighter Level | Attacks per Turn (with Surge) | Example Damage (Greatsword) |
|---------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------|
| 2nd–4th | 1 + 1 = 2 attacks | 2 × (2d6 + STR) |
| 5th–10th | 2 + 2 = 4 attacks | 4 × (2d6 + STR) |
| 11th–19th | 3 + 3 = 6 attacks | 6 × (2d6 + STR) |
| 20th | 4 + 4 = 8 attacks | 8 × (2d6 + STR) |

### Key Details

- **Not an action**: Action Surge itself doesn't cost an action or bonus action
- **Stacks with [[11-Extra-Attack|Extra Attack]]**: The extra action can be an Attack action with all your attacks
- **Short rest recharge**: Available multiple times per adventuring day
- **Once per turn**: At 17th level, you get two uses between rests but can only Surge once per turn
- **Bonus action unchanged**: You still only get one bonus action per turn

### Tactical Tips

- Save for critical moments: boss fights, ambush rounds, or finishing off enemies
- At 5th level, Action Surge lets you make 4 attacks in one turn — devastating burst damage
- Recharges on short rest, so don't be afraid to use it in tough encounters

**Key Concept**: Action Surge is the fighter's most powerful feature — a free extra action on your turn that recharges on short rest. Combined with Extra Attack, it lets fighters deliver massive burst damage, making them the kings of single-turn output.
