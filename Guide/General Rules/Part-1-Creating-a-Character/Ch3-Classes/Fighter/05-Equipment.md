# Fighter - Equipment

## Original Text

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) chain mail or (b) leather armor, longbow, and 20 arrows
- (a) a martial weapon and a shield or (b) two martial weapons
- (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) two handaxes
- (a) a dungeoneer's pack or (b) an explorer's pack

---

## Condensed Version

**Starting Equipment**: Choose from the following options (in addition to background equipment).

### Choice 1: Armor

| Option | Details | AC |
|--------|---------|-----|
| **(a) Chain mail** | Heavy [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]], Str 13 required | 16 |
| **(b) Leather armor + longbow + 20 arrows** | Light armor, ranged option | 11 + DEX mod |

- **Melee fighters**: Chain mail for best starting AC
- **Ranged fighters**: Leather + longbow for archery build

### Choice 2: Weapons

| Option | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **(a) Martial [[03-Weapons|weapons]] + shield** | +2 AC from shield, one-handed weapon |
| **(b) Two martial weapons** | Dual-wield or swap between weapon types |

- **Defensive**: Longsword + shield = AC 18 with chain mail
- **Offensive**: Two greatswords, or a greatsword + backup weapon

### Choice 3: Ranged/Backup

| Option | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **(a) Light crossbow + 20 bolts** | Ranged option (1d8 damage) |
| **(b) Two handaxes** | Thrown [[03-Weapons|weapons]] (1d6, range 20/60) |

### Choice 4: Adventuring Gear

| Option | Key Contents |
|--------|-------------|
| **(a) Dungeoneer's pack** | Crowbar, pitons, torches — dungeon focused |
| **(b) Explorer's pack** | Bedroll, mess kit, tinderbox — wilderness focused |

### Example Loadouts

**Tank Build**: Chain mail + longsword + shield + light crossbow + dungeoneer's pack
- AC 18, versatile melee and ranged

**Archer Build**: Leather + longbow + 20 arrows + two martial [[03-Weapons|weapons]] + two handaxes + explorer's pack
- DEX-focused, ranged primary with melee backup

**Two-Weapon Build**: Chain mail + two martial weapons + two handaxes + dungeoneer's pack
- Aggressive melee with thrown backup

**Key Concept**: Fighter starting equipment offers flexible loadouts for any combat style. Melee fighters typically take chain mail for AC 16+, while ranged fighters take leather [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] with a longbow. The weapon choices let you build for defense (shield), offense (dual weapons), or versatility.
