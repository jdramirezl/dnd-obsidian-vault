# Fighter - Ability Score Improvement

## Original Text

When you reach 4th level, and again at 6th, 8th, 12th, 14th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## Condensed Version

**[[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] Improvement (ASI)**: Fighters get more ASIs than any other class — 7 total.

### How It Works

**Options** (choose one each time):
- Increase one [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] by +2
- Increase two ability scores by +1 each
- Take a feat instead (if using optional feat rules)

**Maximum**: No ability score can exceed 20

### ASI Levels

| Level | ASI # | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 4th | 1st | Standard (all classes get this) |
| 6th | 2nd | Fighter bonus ASI |
| 8th | 3rd | Fighter bonus ASI |
| 12th | 4th | Standard |
| 14th | 5th | Fighter bonus ASI |
| 16th | 6th | Standard |
| 19th | 7th | Standard |

**Total**: 7 ASIs (most classes get only 5)

### Why This Matters

- **2 extra ASIs** compared to most classes
- Fighters can max out their primary stat AND pick up feats
- By level 19, a fighter can have multiple maxed [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]]

### Example Progression (Strength Fighter, starting STR 16)

| Level | Choice | Result |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 4th | STR +2 | STR 18 |
| 6th | STR +2 | STR 20 (maxed) |
| 8th | CON +2 | CON 16 |
| 12th | Feat | Great [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Master |
| 14th | CON +2 | CON 18 |
| 16th | CON +2 | CON 20 (maxed) |
| 19th | Feat | Sentinel |

**Key Concept**: Fighters receive 7 [[01-Ability-Scores-and-Modifiers|ability scores]] Improvements — more than any other class. This lets them max out their primary combat stat early and still have room for feats or secondary stats, making them the most customizable class through ASIs alone.
