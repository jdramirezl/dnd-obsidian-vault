# Fighter - Extra Attack

## Original Text

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

The number of attacks increases to three when you reach 11th level in this class and to four when you reach 20th level in this class.

---

## Condensed Version

**Extra Attack** (5th Level): Make multiple attacks when you take the Attack action.

### Attack Progression

| Fighter Level | Attacks per Attack Action | Notes |
|---------------|--------------------------|-------|
| 1st–4th | 1 | Standard single attack |
| 5th–10th | 2 | Extra Attack |
| 11th–19th | 3 | Extra Attack (2) |
| 20th | 4 | Extra Attack (3) |

### Key Details

- **Only on Attack action**: Does not apply to opportunity attacks, bonus action attacks, or spells
- **Fighter exclusive scaling**: No other class gets more than 2 attacks from Extra Attack
- **Mix and match**: Each attack can target a different creature or use a different [[03-Weapons|weapons]]
- **Works with [[08-Action-Surge|Action Surge]]**: Take the Attack action again for double the attacks

### Damage Comparison (Greatsword, STR 20)

| Level | Attacks | Average Damage per Turn |
|-------|---------|------------------------|
| 5th | 2 | 2 × (2d6 + 5) = 24 |
| 11th | 3 | 3 × (2d6 + 5) = 36 |
| 20th | 4 | 4 × (2d6 + 5) = 48 |

### With Action Surge

| Level | Attacks (Surge Turn) | Average Damage |
|-------|---------------------|----------------|
| 5th | 4 | 48 |
| 11th | 6 | 72 |
| 20th | 8 | 96 |

### Tactical Notes

- At 5th level, fighters match other martial classes in attacks
- At 11th and 20th level, fighters pull ahead with 3 and 4 attacks — unique to the class
- Each attack is a separate roll, so more attacks = more chances to crit

**Key Concept**: Extra Attack is the fighter's core damage feature, scaling from 2 attacks at 5th level to 4 attacks at 20th level. Fighters are the only class that gets more than 2 attacks from this feature, making them the undisputed masters of sustained [[03-Weapons|weapons]] damage.
