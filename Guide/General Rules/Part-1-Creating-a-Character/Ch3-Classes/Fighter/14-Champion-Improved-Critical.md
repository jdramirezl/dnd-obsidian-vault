# Fighter - Champion: Improved Critical

## Original Text

Beginning when you choose this archetype at 3rd level, your weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20.

---

## Condensed Version

**Improved Critical** (3rd Level — Champion): Your [[03-Weapons|weapons]] attacks crit on 19 or 20.

### How It Works

**Normal Critical**: Roll a natural 20 on attack roll (5% chance)
**Improved Critical**: Roll a natural 19 or 20 on attack roll (10% chance)
**Effect**: Double your critical hit chance

### Critical Hit Reminder

When you score a critical hit:
- Roll all damage dice twice (double the dice, not the result)
- Add modifiers once (STR/DEX mod, magic bonuses)
- Example: Greatsword crit = 4d6 + STR mod (instead of 2d6 + STR mod)

### Crit Probability with Multiple Attacks

| Attacks per Turn | Chance of At Least One Crit (per turn) |
|-----------------|---------------------------------------|
| 1 attack | 10% |
| 2 attacks (5th level) | 19% |
| 3 attacks (11th level) | 27.1% |
| 4 attacks (20th level) | 34.4% |
| 4 attacks + Action Surge (8 attacks) | 57.0% |

### Key Details

- **[[03-Weapons|weapons]] attacks only**: Applies to melee and ranged weapon attacks
- **Always active**: No action, bonus action, or resource cost
- **Stacks with Extra Attack**: More attacks = more crit opportunities
- **Upgrades at 15th level**: Superior Critical expands to 18–20

### Weapon Synergies

| [[03-Weapons|weapons]] | Normal Crit Damage | Improved Crit Damage |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Greatsword (2d6) | 4d6 + STR | Same, but happens twice as often |
| Greataxe (1d12) | 2d12 + STR | Higher variance, bigger spikes |
| Rapier (1d8) | 2d8 + DEX | Solid for finesse builds |

**Key Concept**: Improved Critical doubles your critical hit chance from 5% to 10% per attack. Combined with the fighter's multiple attacks from Extra Attack and Action Surge, Champions score critical hits far more often than any other class, delivering devastating burst damage.
