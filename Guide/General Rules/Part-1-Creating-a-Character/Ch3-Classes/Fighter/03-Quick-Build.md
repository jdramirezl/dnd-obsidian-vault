# Fighter - Quick Build

## Original Text

You can make a fighter quickly by following these suggestions. First, make Strength or Dexterity your highest ability score, depending on whether you want to focus on melee weapons or on archery (or finesse weapons). Your next-highest score should be Constitution. Second, choose the soldier background.

---

## Condensed Version

**Quick Build**: Fast setup for a new fighter character.

### Step 1: Ability Scores

**Melee Fighter**: Make Strength your highest score
- For heavy [[03-Weapons|weapons]], longswords, and brute force

**Ranged/Finesse Fighter**: Make Dexterity your highest score
- For archery, finesse weapons, and lighter [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]]

**Second Priority**: Constitution
- More [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] = more survivability in combat

### Step 2: Background

**Recommended**: Soldier background
- Fits the fighter's martial training naturally
- Provides relevant skills and proficiencies

### Example Priority (Melee)

| Priority | Ability | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1st | Strength | Attack and damage with melee [[03-Weapons|weapons]] |
| 2nd | Constitution | [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] and durability |
| 3rd | Dexterity | AC, initiative, and saves |

### Example Priority (Ranged)

| Priority | Ability | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1st | Dexterity | Attack/damage with bows, AC, initiative |
| 2nd | Constitution | [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] and durability |
| 3rd | Strength | Carrying capacity, athletics |

**Key Concept**: For a quick fighter build, prioritize Strength (melee) or Dexterity (ranged/finesse) as your top stat, Constitution second, and take the soldier background for a thematically fitting start.
