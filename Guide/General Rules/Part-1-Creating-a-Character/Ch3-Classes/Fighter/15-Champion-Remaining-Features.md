# Fighter - Champion: Remaining Features

## Original Text

### Remarkable Athlete

Starting at 7th level, you can add half your proficiency bonus (round up) to any Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution check you make that doesn't already use your proficiency bonus.

In addition, when you make a running long jump, the distance you can cover increases by a number of feet equal to your Strength modifier.

### Additional Fighting Style

At 10th level, you can choose a second option from the Fighting Style class feature.

### Superior Critical

Starting at 15th level, your weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 18–20.

### Survivor

At 18th level, you attain the pinnacle of resilience in battle. At the start of each of your turns, you regain hit points equal to 5 + your Constitution modifier if you have no more than half of your hit points left. You don't gain this benefit if you have 0 hit points.

## Condensed Version

### Remarkable Athlete (7th Level)

**Bonus**: Add half your [[03-Proficiency-Bonus|proficiency bonus]] (rounded up) to STR, DEX, and CON checks that don't already use your proficiency bonus.

**Half Proficiency Bonus by Level**:

| Fighter Level | Proficiency Bonus | Half (Rounded Up) |
|---------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 7th–8th | +3 | +2 |
| 9th–12th | +4 | +2 |
| 13th–16th | +5 | +3 |
| 17th–20th | +6 | +3 |

**Applies To**:
- Initiative rolls (DEX check)
- Grapple/shove contests (STR check, if not proficient in Athletics)
- Ability checks for climbing, swimming, jumping, balancing, etc.

**Does NOT Apply To**:
- Checks where you already add proficiency (e.g., Athletics if proficient)
- [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] (not ability checks)
- Attack rolls

**Running Long Jump Bonus**: Distance increases by your Strength modifier (in feet)
- Example: STR 18 (+4) = 4 extra feet on running long jumps

---

### Additional Fighting Style (10th Level)

**Effect**: Choose a second option from the Fighting Style list
- Must be a different style than your first choice
- See Fighting Style file for all options

**Popular Combinations**:

| 1st Style | 2nd Style | Build |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Great [[03-Weapons|weapons]] Fighting | Defense | Two-handed + extra AC |
| Dueling | Defense | Sword-and-shield + extra AC |
| Archery | Defense | Ranged + [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]] AC boost |
| Dueling | Protection | Sword-and-shield + ally defense |

---

### Superior Critical (15th Level)

**Effect**: [[03-Weapons|weapons]] attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 18, 19, or 20
- Upgrades from Improved Critical (19–20) to 18–20
- **Triple** the normal crit chance: 15% per attack (vs. 5% normal)

**Crit Probability with Multiple Attacks**:

| Attacks per Turn | Chance of At Least One Crit |
|-----------------|----------------------------|
| 1 attack | 15% |
| 2 attacks | 27.8% |
| 3 attacks (11th level) | 38.6% |
| 4 attacks (20th level) | 47.8% |
| 8 attacks (Action Surge at 20th) | 72.8% |

---

### Survivor (18th Level)

**Effect**: At the start of each of your turns, regain 5 + Constitution modifier HP
**Condition**: Only works when you have half your HP or fewer
**Restriction**: Does NOT work at 0 HP (you must be conscious)

**Healing Per Turn by Constitution**:

| CON Modifier | HP Regained per Turn |
|-------------|---------------------|
| +2 | 7 |
| +3 | 8 |
| +4 | 9 |
| +5 | 10 |

**Why It's Powerful**:
- Automatic — no action, bonus action, or resource cost
- Triggers every turn while below half HP
- At CON 20: regain 10 HP per turn = 100 HP over a 10-round combat
- Combined with Second Wind and heavy [[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]], Champions are extremely hard to kill

**Example** (18th-level Champion, CON 20, ~184 max HP):
- Below 92 HP: Regain 10 HP at the start of each turn
- Over 5 rounds: 50 free HP while still attacking normally

**Key Concept**: The Champion's remaining features round out the archetype — Remarkable Athlete adds half proficiency to physical checks, Additional Fighting Style gives a second combat specialty, Superior Critical triples your crit range to 18–20, and Survivor provides automatic regeneration below half HP. Together, they create a fighter who excels at physical challenges, crits frequently, and is nearly impossible to bring down.
