# Fighter - Indomitable

## Original Text

Beginning at 9th level, you can reroll a saving throw that you fail. If you do so, you must use the new roll, and you can't use this feature again until you finish a long rest.

You can use this feature twice between long rests starting at 13th level and three times between long rests starting at 17th level.

---

## Condensed Version

**Indomitable** (9th Level): Reroll a failed [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]].

### How It Works

**Trigger**: You fail a [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]
**Effect**: Reroll the saving throw — must use the new result
**Recharge**: Long rest only

### Uses Per Long Rest

| Fighter Level | Uses |
|---------------|------|
| 9th–12th | 1 |
| 13th–16th | 2 |
| 17th+ | 3 |

### Key Details

- **Any [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]**: Works on Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saves
- **Must use new roll**: Even if the reroll is lower than the original
- **After you know you failed**: You use this after seeing the result
- **No limit on timing**: Works against spells, traps, breath [[03-Weapons|weapons]], or any effect requiring a save

### When to Use It

**High-priority saves** (save these uses for):
- Paralysis, stun, or incapacitation effects
- [[Banishment|Banishment]], [[Hold Person|hold person]], or similar removal effects
- Massive damage effects (dragon breath, [[Fireball|fireball]])

**Lower priority** (consider accepting the failure):
- Minor damage effects you can absorb
- Conditions that end quickly

### Comparison to Other Defenses

- **Legendary Resistance** (monsters): Auto-succeed — stronger
- **Indomitable**: Reroll — gives a second chance, not a guarantee
- Statistically, rerolling a failed save gives roughly a 40-60% chance of success depending on the DC

**Key Concept**: Indomitable lets you reroll a failed [[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]] once per long rest (scaling to three times at 17th level). It's the fighter's main defense against spells and effects that target saves, giving you a second chance against devastating abilities.
