# Wizard - School of Evocation

## Original Text

You focus your study on magic that creates powerful elemental effects such as bitter cold, searing flame, rolling thunder, crackling lightning, and burning acid. Some evokers find employment in military forces, serving as artillery to blast enemy armies from afar. Others use their spectacular power to protect the weak, while some seek their own gain as bandits, adventurers, or aspiring tyrants.

---

## Condensed Version

**School of **evocation****: Specialize in powerful elemental damage magic.

### Overview

- **Focus**: Magic that creates elemental effects — cold, fire, thunder, lightning, acid
- **Role**: The "blaster" wizard — area damage and raw destructive power

### Evoker Archetypes

- **Military Artillery**: Blast enemy armies from afar
- **Protector**: Use spectacular power to defend the weak
- **Mercenary/Adventurer**: Seek personal gain through destructive magic

### Evocation Features by Level

| Level | Feature | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| 2nd | **evocation** Savant | Half cost/time to copy evocation spells |
| 2nd | Sculpt Spells | Protect allies from your area spells |
| 6th | Potent Cantrip | Cantrips deal half damage on successful saves |
| 10th | Empowered Evocation | Add Int modifier to evocation damage |
| 14th | Overchannel | Deal maximum damage with 1st–5th level spells |

**Key Concept**: The School of Evocation is the quintessential damage-dealing wizard subclass. You specialize in elemental destruction while gaining the ability to protect allies from your own area spells and eventually deal devastating maximum damage.
