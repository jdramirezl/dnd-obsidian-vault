# Wizard - School of Conjuration

## Original Text

As a conjurer, you favor spells that produce objects and creatures out of thin air. You can conjure billowing clouds of killing fog or summon creatures from elsewhere to fight on your behalf. As your mastery grows, you learn spells of transportation and can teleport yourself across vast distances, even to other planes of existence, in an instant.

### Conjuration Savant

Beginning when you select this school at 2nd level, the gold and time you must spend to copy a conjuration spell into your spellbook is halved.

### Minor Conjuration

Starting at 2nd level when you select this school, you can use your action to conjure up an inanimate object in your hand or on the ground in an unoccupied space that you can see within 10 feet of you. This object can be no larger than 3 feet on a side and weigh no more than 10 pounds, and its form must be that of a nonmagical object that you have seen. The object is visibly magical, radiating dim light out to 5 feet.

The object disappears after 1 hour, when you use this feature again, or if it takes any damage.

### Benign Transposition

Starting at 6th level, you can use your action to teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see. Alternatively, you can choose a space within range that is occupied by a Small or Medium creature. If that creature is willing, you both teleport, swapping places.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest or you cast a conjuration spell of 1st level or higher.

### Focused Conjuration

Beginning at 10th level, while you are concentrating on a conjuration spell, your concentration can't be broken as a result of taking damage.

### Durable Summons

Starting at 14th level, any creature that you summon or create with a conjuration spell has 30 temporary hit points.

## Condensed Version

### School of Conjuration Overview

**Theme**: Summoning creatures, creating objects, and teleportation
**Identity**: The wizard who brings things into existence and moves through space

### Conjuration Savant (Level 2)

- **Half gold and time** to copy conjuration spells into your spellbook

### Minor Conjuration (Level 2)

- **Action**: Conjure a nonmagical object you've seen before
- **Limits**: 3 ft on a side, 10 lbs max, within 10 ft
- Object **glows** (dim light 5 ft) — visibly magical
- Disappears after **1 hour**, if used again, or if it takes damage
- Creative uses: keys you've seen, tools, simple weapons, rope

### Benign Transposition (Level 6)

- **Action**: Teleport 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see
- **Or**: Swap places with a willing Small/Medium creature within 30 ft
- Recharges on **long rest** or when you cast a conjuration spell (1st+)
- Effectively reusable if you cast conjuration spells regularly

### Focused Conjuration (Level 10)

- **Concentration can't be broken by damage** on conjuration spells
- Only applies to conjuration spells (e.g., *conjure elemental*, *cloudkill*)
- You can still lose concentration from other effects (incapacitated, etc.)

### Durable Summons (Level 14)

- All summoned/created creatures gain **30 temporary HP**
- Applies to *conjure animals*, *conjure elemental*, *conjure minor elementals*, etc.
- Makes your summons significantly harder to kill

### School of Conjuration Feature Summary

| Level | Feature | Key Benefit |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| 2 | Conjuration Savant | Half cost to copy conjuration spells |
| 2 | Minor Conjuration | Create small nonmagical objects at will |
| 6 | Benign Transposition | Teleport 30 ft or swap with ally; recharges on conjuration cast |
| 10 | Focused Conjuration | Damage can't break concentration on conjuration spells |
| 14 | Durable Summons | Summoned creatures get +30 temp HP |

**Key Concept**: The School of Conjuration excels at summoning and teleportation. Minor Conjuration provides infinite creative utility. Benign Transposition gives reliable short-range teleportation. Focused Conjuration ensures your summons stay active through combat damage, and Durable Summons makes them tougher. Best for wizards who want to control the battlefield with summoned creatures.
