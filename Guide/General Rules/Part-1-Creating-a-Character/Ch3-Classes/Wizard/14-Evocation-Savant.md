# Wizard - Evocation Savant

## Original Text

Beginning when you select this school at 2nd level, the gold and time you must spend to copy an evocation spell into your spellbook is halved.

---

## Condensed Version

****evocation** Savant** (2nd Level, School of Evocation): Reduced cost to copy evocation spells.

### How It Works

- **Benefit**: Gold and time to copy **evocation** spells into your spellbook is **halved**
- **Normal Cost**: 2 hours and 50 gp per spell level
- **Savant Cost**: 1 hour and 25 gp per spell level

### Cost Comparison

| Spell Level | Normal Time | Normal Gold | Savant Time | Savant Gold |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1st | 2 hours | 50 gp | 1 hour | 25 gp |
| 2nd | 4 hours | 100 gp | 2 hours | 50 gp |
| 3rd | 6 hours | 150 gp | 3 hours | 75 gp |
| 4th | 8 hours | 200 gp | 4 hours | 100 gp |
| 5th | 10 hours | 250 gp | 5 hours | 125 gp |

### Notes

- Only applies to ****evocation**** spells — other schools cost normal rates
- Applies when copying from scrolls, other spellbooks, or any found source
- Does NOT apply to the 2 free spells gained on level up (those are always free)

**Key Concept**: Evocation Savant halves the gold and time cost to copy evocation spells into your spellbook. This makes it cheaper to build a large collection of damage spells — actively seek out evocation scrolls and spellbooks to take advantage of this discount.
