# Wizard - Hit Points and Proficiencies

## Original Text

### Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d6 per wizard level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 6 + your Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d6 (or 4) + your Constitution modifier per wizard level after 1st

### Proficiencies

Armor: None
Weapons: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows
Tools: None
Saving Throws: Intelligence, Wisdom
Skills: Choose two from Arcana, History, Insight, Investigation, Medicine, and Religion

---

## Condensed Version

**Class Features**: The wizard's [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit points]] and proficiencies — the frailest class in D&D.

### Hit Points

- **[[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit dice]]**: 1d6 per wizard level (lowest in the game)
- **1st Level HP**: 6 + Constitution modifier
- **Higher Levels**: 1d6 (or 4) + Constitution modifier per level
- Example: Wizard with Con 14 (+2) has 8 HP at 1st level

### Proficiencies

**[[02-Armor-and-Shields|armor]]**: None
- You cannot wear any armor without special feats or multiclassing
- Rely on spells like [[Mage Armor|mage armor]] and [[Shield|shield]] for defense

**[[03-Weapons|weapons]]**: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows
- Limited selection — you rely on spells for combat

**Tools**: None

**[[11-Saving-Throws|saving throws]]**: Intelligence, Wisdom
- Intelligence saves resist mental effects and some spells
- Wisdom saves resist charm, fear, and many dangerous spells

**Skills** (choose 2):
- Arcana — magical knowledge
- History — lore and past events
- Insight — reading people
- Investigation — deduction and searching
- Medicine — stabilizing the dying
- Religion — divine lore

**Key Concept**: Wizards are the most fragile class with d6 [[07-Damage-and-Healing|hit dice]] and no armor proficiency. They compensate with the most powerful spell list in the game. Prioritize Constitution for survivability and use defensive spells to stay alive.
