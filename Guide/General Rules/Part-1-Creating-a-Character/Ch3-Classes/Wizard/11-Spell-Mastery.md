# Wizard - Spell Mastery

## Original Text

At 18th level, you have achieved such mastery over certain spells that you can cast them at will. Choose a 1st-level wizard spell and a 2nd-level wizard spell that are in your spellbook. You can cast those spells at their lowest level without expending a spell slot when you have them prepared. If you want to cast either spell at a higher level, you must expend a spell slot as normal.

By spending 8 hours in study, you can exchange one or both of the spells you chose for different spells of the same levels.

---

## Condensed Version

**Spell Mastery** (18th Level): Cast two spells at will, without using spell slots.

### How It Works

- **Choose**: One 1st-level spell AND one 2nd-level spell from your spellbook
- **Requirement**: Must have the spells prepared
- **Benefit**: Cast them at their lowest level without expending a spell slot
- **Upcasting**: Still requires a spell slot as normal

### Changing Your Choices

- Spend **8 hours studying** to swap one or both spells
- New spells must be the same levels (one 1st, one 2nd)

### Strong Spell Mastery Picks

**1st-Level Options**:
- [[Shield|shield]] — reaction +5 AC, unlimited uses
- [[Magic Missile|magic missile]] — guaranteed damage every turn
- [[Mage Armor|mage armor]] — always-on 13 + Dex AC

**2nd-Level Options**:
- [[Misty Step|misty step]] — bonus action teleport at will
- [[Invisibility|Invisibility]] — unlimited stealth
- [[Detect Thoughts|Detect Thoughts]] — constant mind reading

**Key Concept**: At 18th level, you can cast one 1st-level and one 2nd-level spell at will without using spell slots, as long as they're prepared. This effectively gives you unlimited uses of two spells — choose wisely for maximum impact.
