# Wizard - Arcane Recovery

## Original Text

You have learned to regain some of your magical energy by studying your spellbook. Once per day when you finish a short rest, you can choose expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less than half your wizard level (rounded up), and none of the slots can be 6th level or higher.

For example, if you're a 4th-level wizard, you can recover up to two levels worth of spell slots. You can recover either a 2nd-level spell slot or two 1st-level spell slots.

---

## Condensed Version

**Arcane Recovery** (1st Level Feature): Regain spell slots during a short rest by studying your spellbook.

### How It Works

- **Frequency**: Once per day, after a short rest
- **Recovered Slot Levels**: Combined level ≤ half your wizard level (rounded up)
- **Restriction**: No recovered slot can be 6th level or higher

### Recovery by Wizard Level

| Wizard Level | Max Combined Levels | Example Recovery Options |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 1 | One 1st-level slot |
| 2nd | 1 | One 1st-level slot |
| 3rd | 2 | One 2nd-level OR two 1st-level |
| 4th | 2 | One 2nd-level OR two 1st-level |
| 5th | 3 | One 3rd-level OR one 2nd + one 1st |
| 6th | 3 | One 3rd-level OR one 2nd + one 1st |
| 7th | 4 | One 4th-level OR various combos |
| 8th | 4 | One 4th-level OR various combos |
| 9th | 5 | One 5th-level OR various combos |
| 10th | 5 | One 5th-level OR various combos |

### Tips

- Use this during short rests to recover your most-used slots
- At low levels, recovering a single 1st-level slot is still valuable
- At higher levels, this can recover a significant amount of casting power
- Cannot recover 6th-level or higher slots, even at level 20

**Key Concept**: Arcane Recovery lets you regain spell slots once per day on a short rest, with a combined level up to half your wizard level (rounded up) and nothing above 5th level. This gives wizards extra staying power between long rests — use it every day.
