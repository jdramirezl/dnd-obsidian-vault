# 🧠 Equipment Creative Uses

> [!info] Beyond the Stat Block
> Most items in D&D have uses far beyond their description. This guide covers creative, RAW-legal (or DM-friendly) uses for common [[04-Adventuring-Gear|adventuring gear]]. Encourage your players to think beyond "I attack."

---

## 🔴 Area Denial & Battlefield Control

### Ball Bearings (1 gp, 2 lb.)
- **RAW:** Cover 10-ft square; DC 10 DEX save or fall [[Prone|prone]]; half speed = no save
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Detect invisible creatures** — scatter on the floor and listen for crunching/rolling
  - **Block a chokepoint** — doorways, hallways, bridge entrances
  - **Escape tool** — dump behind you while fleeing; pursuers must save or fall prone
  - **Alarm system** — spread on stone floor near camp; rolling balls = audible warning
  - **Combine with grease/oil** — DM may rule the DC increases or auto-prone

### Caltrops (1 gp, 2 lb.)
- **RAW:** Cover 5-ft square; DC 15 DEX save or 1 piercing + stop moving + speed −10 ft until healed
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Better than ball bearings for damage** — DC 15 vs DC 10, plus speed reduction
  - **Protect a sleeping camp** — ring your campsite with caltrops
  - **Funnel enemies** — leave one caltrop-free path and ambush it
  - **Anti-pursuit** — toss behind you; barefoot enemies (many monsters) are especially vulnerable
  - **Combine with Darkness/Fog Cloud** — enemies can't see the caltrops to avoid them

### Oil (1 sp, 1 lb.)
- **RAW:** Throw 20 ft for improvised attack; if target takes fire damage within 1 min, +5 fire damage. Pour on 5-ft square: burns 2 rounds, 5 fire damage/turn
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Area denial** — pour oil + ignite = 2 rounds of 5 fire damage per 5-ft square
  - **Coat a bridge or stairs** — DM may rule slippery surface (improvised difficult terrain)
  - **Trail marker** — pour a thin line to find your way back in a dungeon
  - **Fuel for lanterns** — 1 flask = 6 hours of light
  - **Combine with Prestidigitation** — ignite oil remotely without a tinderbox
  - **Anti-troll** — fire damage prevents troll regeneration; oil is 1 sp vs a spell slot

### Hunting Trap (5 gp, 25 lb.)
- **RAW:** DC 13 DEX save or 1d4 piercing + immobilized; DC 13 STR to free
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Guard a door** — set trap in front of a door you're resting behind
  - **Ambush setup** — hide trap under leaves/cloth, lure enemies over it
  - **Chain to a heavy object** — trapped creature is anchored in place
  - **Capture alive** — non-lethal restraint for bounty targets

## 🔧 Utility & Exploration

### 10-Foot Pole (5 cp, 7 lb.)
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Trap detection** — poke floors, walls, chests from 10 ft away
  - **Bridge tester** — tap ahead of you on rickety surfaces
  - **Lever** — wedge under heavy objects for mechanical advantage
  - **Vault pole** — DM may allow Athletics check to pole-vault small gaps
  - **Prop open** — wedge in a closing door or portcullis

### Crowbar (2 gp, 5 lb.)
- **RAW:** Advantage on Strength checks where leverage applies
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Pry open anything** — doors, coffins, crates, stuck mechanisms
  - **Improvised weapon** — treat as a club (1d4 bludgeoning)
  - **Wedge doors shut** — jam under a door to slow pursuers
  - **Remove boards/nails** — access hidden passages behind walls

### Rope, Hempen (1 gp, 10 lb.) / Silk (10 gp, 5 lb.)
- **RAW:** 2 HP, DC 17 STR to burst
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Tie up prisoners** — combine with manacles for double security
  - **Create a tripwire** — stretch across a hallway at ankle height
  - **Improvised bridge** — tie between two anchor points
  - **Rescue tool** — lower into pits, pull allies from quicksand
  - **Lasso** — DM may allow a ranged grapple attempt
  - **Measure depth** — lower into a pit or well to gauge distance
  - **Silk rope** — half the weight, same strength; worth the 10 gp for encumbrance-conscious characters

### Mirror, Steel (5 gp, ½ lb.)
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Look around corners** — avoid ambushes and traps
  - **Counter gaze attacks** — medusa's petrifying gaze requires seeing her eyes; a mirror lets you fight without direct eye contact (DM ruling)
  - **Signal allies** — reflect sunlight over long distances
  - **Check behind you** — spot followers without turning around

### Pitons (5 cp each, ¼ lb.)
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Create climbing holds** — hammer into cliff faces for safe climbing
  - **Wedge doors** — hammer into door frame to prevent opening
  - **Anchor rope** — secure rope to stone walls or ceilings
  - **Improvised stakes** — useful against vampires (DM ruling)

## ⚔️ Combat-Adjacent Uses

### Manacles (2 gp, 6 lb.)
- **RAW:** DC 20 DEX to escape, DC 20 STR to break, DC 15 thieves' tools to pick; 15 HP
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Capture NPCs alive** — manacle unconscious enemies for interrogation
  - **Restrain a charmed ally** — if a party member is mind-controlled, manacle them until the spell ends
  - **Secure a door** — loop through door handles to chain them shut
  - **Improvised flail** — swing manacles as an improvised weapon (1d4)

### Chain (5 gp, 10 lb.)
- **RAW:** 10 HP, DC 20 STR to burst
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Stronger than rope** — 10 HP vs 2 HP, DC 20 vs DC 17
  - **Anchor a hunting trap** — RAW use, but also anchor anything heavy
  - **Block a hallway** — stretch across at chest height
  - **Improvised weapon** — treat as a whip or flail (DM ruling)

### Holy Water (25 gp, 1 lb.)
- **RAW:** 2d6 radiant damage to fiends and undead (ranged improvised attack)
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Anti-undead grenade** — throw into a group of zombies/skeletons
  - **Test for fiends/undead** — splash a suspicious NPC; if they take damage, you have your answer
  - **Bless a water source** — DM may rule it purifies a small amount of water
  - **Combine with oil** — coat a weapon in holy water before fighting undead (DM ruling)

### Acid (25 gp, 1 lb.)
- **RAW:** 2d6 acid damage, throw 20 ft
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Dissolve locks** — pour acid on a lock mechanism to destroy it
  - **Destroy evidence** — dissolve documents, small objects
  - **Weaken structures** — pour on load-bearing ropes, hinges, chains
  - **Escape restraints** — carefully pour on rope/manacles binding you (risky — may take damage)

### Net (1 gp, 3 lb.)
- **RAW:** Target is [[Restrained|restrained]]; DC 10 STR or 5 slashing damage to escape
- **Creative Uses:**
  - **Capture flying creatures** — restrained condition = speed 0, they fall
  - **Non-lethal takedown** — restrain without dealing damage
  - **Block a passage** — stretch across a doorway as an improvised barrier
  - **Fishing** — it's literally a net

## 💡 Clever Combinations

| Combo | How It Works | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Oil + Fire (torch/cantrip) | Pour oil on 5-ft square, ignite | 5 fire damage/turn for 2 rounds — area denial |
| Ball Bearings + Darkness | Cast Darkness over ball bearings | Enemies can't see to avoid them — auto-prone on failed save |
| Caltrops + Fog Cloud | Cast Fog Cloud over caltrops | Same principle — DC 15 is brutal when you can't see |
| Rope + Bell | Tie bell to rope across a doorway | Improvised alarm — wakes the party |
| Manacles + Grapple | Grapple → shove prone → manacle | DC 20 to escape — effectively out of the fight |
| Mirror + Sunlight | Reflect sunlight into dark areas | Free light source, signal allies at range |
| 10-ft Pole + Rope | Tie rope to pole, lay across a gap | Improvised bridge for small gaps |
| Crowbar + Pitons | Piton a door shut, crowbar another open | Control which doors are accessible |
| Oil + Alchemist's Fire | Oil a target, then throw alchemist's fire | Oil adds +5 fire, alchemist's fire adds 1d4/turn — stacking damage |
| Hunting Trap + Caltrops | Trap in center, caltrops around it | Enemies avoiding the trap step on caltrops |

> [!tip] DM Note
> Encourage creative item use! If a player proposes something clever that isn't explicitly in the rules, consider allowing it with an appropriate ability check. Reward creativity — it makes the game more fun than just "I attack" every turn.
