## 🗡️ Melee Archetype (Fighter, Barbarian, Paladin)

**Budget: ~125 gp (Fighter/Paladin) or ~50 gp (Barbarian)**

### Priority Buy List

| Priority | Item | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Chain mail | 75 gp | AC 16 flat — best starting armor for heavy armor users |
| 2 | Greatsword or Halberd | 50 gp / 20 gp | 2d6 or 1d10+reach — your main damage source |
| 3 | Shield + Longsword | 10 gp + 15 gp | AC 18 with chain mail if you prefer defense |
| 4 | Explorer's Pack | 10 gp | Rope, torches, rations — covers survival basics |
| 5 | Javelins (5) | 2.5 gp | Ranged option for flying enemies (1d6, 30/120 ft) |

### Non-Obvious Essentials

| Item | Cost | Why You Need It |
|---|---|---|
| Hempen Rope (50 ft) | 1 gp | Climbing, tying prisoners, crossing chasms — included in Explorer's Pack |
| Manacles | 2 gp | Capture NPCs alive (DC 20 to escape, DC 15 to pick) |
| Crowbar | 2 gp | Advantage on Strength checks to pry things open |
| Oil (2 flasks) | 2 sp | Pour on ground for 5-ft fire zone (5 damage/turn for 2 rounds) |
| Healer's Kit | 5 gp | Stabilize allies at 0 HP without a Medicine check — 10 uses |

**Barbarian on 50 gp:** Greataxe (30 gp) + Explorer's Pack (10 gp) + Javelins ×4 (2 gp) + Healer's Kit (5 gp) + Manacles (2 gp) = 49 gp. Skip armor — your Unarmored Defense (10 + DEX + CON) may beat chain mail anyway.

## 🏹 Ranged Archetype (Ranger, Fighter Archer)

**Budget: ~125 gp**

### Priority Buy List

| Priority | Item | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Longbow | 50 gp | 1d8, 150/600 ft range — best ranged weapon in the game |
| 2 | Studded Leather | 45 gp | AC 12 + DEX — best light armor, no stealth penalty |
| 3 | Arrows (40) | 2 gp | You recover half after battle, but always carry extra |
| 4 | Shortsword | 10 gp | Finesse backup for melee (1d6 + DEX) |
| 5 | Explorer's Pack | 10 gp | Basics covered |
| 6 | Quiver | 1 gp | Holds 20 arrows — buy 2 for your 40 arrows |

### Non-Obvious Essentials

| Item | Cost | Why You Need It |
|---|---|---|
| Ball Bearings | 1 gp | Spread on 10-ft square — DC 10 DEX or fall prone. Buys you distance |
| Caltrops | 1 gp | 5-ft square, DC 15 DEX or 1 piercing + speed −10 ft. Better DC than ball bearings |
| Hunting Trap | 5 gp | Set and forget — DC 13 DEX or 1d4 + immobilized. Great for ambushes |
| Signal Whistle | 5 cp | Alert the party from 600+ ft away — weighs nothing |
| Pitons (10) | 5 cp each | Create climbing holds, wedge doors shut, anchor rope |

**Remaining gold (~5 gp):** Grab a tinderbox (5 sp), waterskin (2 sp), and rations (5 sp/day).

## 🔮 Caster Archetype (Wizard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Bard)

**Budget: ~75–125 gp**

### Priority Buy List

| Priority | Item | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Component Pouch OR Arcane Focus | 25 gp / 5–20 gp | Required to cast most spells |
| 2 | Spellbook (Wizard only) | 50 gp | Your entire class depends on this — guard it with your life |
| 3 | Light Crossbow + 20 bolts | 26 gp | 1d8 ranged damage when you're out of spell slots |
| 4 | Scholar's Pack or Explorer's Pack | 40 gp / 10 gp | Scholar's has ink + parchment for copying spells |
| 5 | Dagger (2) | 4 gp | Finesse, light, thrown — always have a backup |

### Non-Obvious Essentials

| Item | Cost | Why You Need It |
|---|---|---|
| 10-Foot Pole | 5 cp | Poke traps, check floors, prod suspicious objects from 10 ft away |
| Chalk (10 pieces) | 1 sp | Mark paths in dungeons, leave messages, draw ritual circles |
| Bell + String (10 ft) | 1 gp + free | Improvised alarm on a door — wake up during long rests |
| Mirror, Steel | 5 gp | Look around corners without exposing yourself to gaze attacks |
| Ink + Parchment | 10 gp + 1 sp | Copy spells, take notes, forge documents |

**Wizard on 100 gp:** Spellbook (50 gp) + Component Pouch (25 gp) + Explorer's Pack (10 gp) + Daggers ×2 (4 gp) + 10-ft Pole (5 cp) + Chalk ×10 (1 sp) + Bell (1 gp) = ~90 gp. Save the rest for copying spells later (50 gp per spell!).

## 🗝️ Sneaky Archetype (Rogue, Ranger Scout, Bard)

**Budget: ~100 gp**

### Priority Buy List

| Priority | Item | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Rapier | 25 gp | 1d8 finesse — best one-handed DEX weapon for Sneak Attack |
| 2 | Studded Leather | 45 gp | AC 12 + DEX, no stealth penalty |
| 3 | Shortbow + 20 arrows | 26 gp | Ranged Sneak Attack option (1d6 + SA dice) |
| 4 | Burglar's Pack | 16 gp | Ball bearings, crowbar, hooded lantern, rope — perfect for rogues |
| 5 | Daggers (2) | 4 gp | Thrown backup (20/60 ft), finesse for Sneak Attack |

### Non-Obvious Essentials

| Item | Cost | Why You Need It |
|---|---|---|
| Ball Bearings (1,000) | 1 gp | Included in Burglar's Pack — scatter behind you to stop pursuers |
| Caltrops | 1 gp | DC 15 DEX save — higher DC than ball bearings, punishes chasers |
| Hooded Lantern | 5 gp | 30-ft bright light, can lower hood to 5-ft dim — stealth-friendly |
| Disguise Kit | 25 gp | Change appearance without magic — great for infiltration |
| Manacles | 2 gp | Capture targets alive for interrogation |
| Thieves' Tools | 25 gp | **Essential** — you need these to pick locks and disarm traps |

> [!warning] Don't Forget Thieves' Tools!
> Rogues get proficiency with thieves' tools but **don't automatically start with them** if you're buying with gold. They cost 25 gp — budget for them first.

## 🎒 Universal Must-Haves (Every Character)

These items are cheap, light, and save lives:

| Item | Cost | Weight | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hempen Rope (50 ft) | 1 gp | 10 lb. | Climbing, tying, pulling, swinging — the #1 utility item |
| Torches (10) or Lantern | 1 cp each / 5 gp | 1 lb. each | Darkvision doesn't help your human allies |
| Rations (5 days) | 2.5 gp | 10 lb. | Starvation = exhaustion = death spiral |
| Waterskin | 2 sp | 5 lb. | Same as above |
| Tinderbox | 5 sp | 1 lb. | Light torches, campfires, oil — 1 action |
| Healer's Kit | 5 gp | 3 lb. | Stabilize at 0 HP, no check needed — 10 uses |
| Potion of Healing | 50 gp | ½ lb. | 2d4+2 HP as an action — buy one if you can afford it |

> [!tip] Pro Tip
> If your party has no healer, **everyone** should carry a Healer's Kit. It's 5 gp to save a life.
