# 💫 Incapacitated

> [!warning] Incapacitated
> **Can't take actions or reactions**
> Base condition for stunned/paralyzed/unconscious

## Effect

- An incapacitated creature can't take actions or reactions.

## Quick Reference

- **Saves**: Can't take actions or reactions
- **Combat**: Base condition for [[Stunned|stunned]]/[[Paralyzed|paralyzed]]/[[Unconscious|unconscious]]
