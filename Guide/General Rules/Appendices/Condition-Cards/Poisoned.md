# ☠️ Poisoned

> [!warning] Poisoned
> **Disadvantage on attacks**
> Disadvantage on ability checks

## Effect

- A poisoned creature has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.

## Quick Reference

- **Saves**: Disadvantage on attacks
- **Combat**: Disadvantage on ability checks

## Common Sources

- [[Ray of Sickness]], [[Stinking Cloud]]; removed by [[Lesser Restoration]], [[Protection from Poison]]
