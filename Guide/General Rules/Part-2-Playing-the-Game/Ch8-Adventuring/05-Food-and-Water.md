# Food and Water

## Original Text

Characters who don't eat or drink suffer the effects of exhaustion (see appendix A). Exhaustion caused by lack of food or water can't be removed until the character eats and drinks the full required amount.

**Food.** A character needs one pound of food per day and can make food last longer by subsisting on half rations. Eating half a pound of food in a day counts as half a day without food.

A character can go without food for a number of days equal to 3 + his or her Constitution modifier (minimum 1). At the end of each day beyond that limit, a character automatically suffers one level of exhaustion.

A normal day of eating resets the count of days without food to zero.

**Water.** A character needs one gallon of water per day, or two gallons per day if the weather is hot. A character who drinks only half that much water must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or suffer one level of exhaustion at the end of the day. A character with access to even less water automatically suffers one level of exhaustion at the end of the day.

If the character already has one or more levels of exhaustion, the character takes two levels in either case.


## Condensed Version

> [[Exhaustion|Exhaustion]] from lack of food or water **can't be removed** until the character eats/drinks the full required amount.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Water is more dangerous than food — dehydration can cause exhaustion every day with no grace period, and **doubles** if already exhausted. Food allows 3 + Con modifier days before exhaustion starts.

### Food Requirements

**Daily Need**: 1 pound of food per day

**Half Rations**: Eating ½ pound counts as half a day without food

**Starvation Timeline**:
- Can go **3 + Constitution modifier** days without food (minimum 1 day)
- After that: **1 level of exhaustion** per day automatically
- A normal day of eating resets the counter to zero

**Example** (Con 14, +2 modifier):
- Safe without food for 5 days
- Day 6+: 1 exhaustion level per day

### Water Requirements

**Daily Need**: 1 gallon (2 gallons in hot weather)

| Water Consumed | Effect |
|---------------|--------|
| Full amount | No effect |
| Half amount | DC 15 Constitution save or 1 level of exhaustion |
| Less than half | Automatic 1 level of exhaustion |

**Already Exhausted**: If character has 1+ levels of exhaustion, dehydration causes **2 levels** instead of 1

**Key Concept**: Water is more immediately dangerous than food — dehydration can cause exhaustion every day with no grace period, and doubles if already exhausted. Starvation allows a buffer of 3 + Con modifier days before exhaustion begins. Neither can be recovered from until full intake is restored.
