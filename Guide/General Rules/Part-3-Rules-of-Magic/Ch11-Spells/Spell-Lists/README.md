## Notes

- **Full casters** (Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) have spells from cantrips through 9th level.
- **Half casters** (Paladin, Ranger) have spells from 1st through 5th level only — no cantrips.
- Cleric and Wizard lists are sourced from the Basic Rules 2018; all others from the Player's Handbook.
