# Animate Objects

*5th-level transmutation*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 action |
| **Range** | 120 feet |
| **Components** | V, S |
| **Duration** | Concentration, up to 1 minute |

## Effect

Objects come to life at your command. Choose up to ten nonmagical objects within range that are not being worn or carried. Medium targets count as two objects, Large targets count as four objects, Huge targets count as eight objects. You can't animate any object larger than Huge. Each target animates and becomes a creature under your control until the spell ends or until reduced to 0 hit points.

As a bonus action, you can mentally command any creature you made with this spell if the creature is within 500 feet of you. You decide what action the creature will take and where it will move during its next turn, or you can issue a general command. If you issue no commands, the creature only defends itself.

| Size | HP | AC | Attack | Str | Dex |
|------|----|----|--------|-----|-----|
| Tiny | 20 | 18 | +8 to hit, 1d4 + 4 damage | 4 | 18 |
| Small | 25 | 16 | +6 to hit, 1d8 + 2 damage | 6 | 14 |
| Medium | 40 | 13 | +5 to hit, 2d6 + 1 damage | 10 | 12 |
| Large | 50 | 10 | +6 to hit, 2d10 + 2 damage | 14 | 10 |
| Huge | 80 | 10 | +8 to hit, 2d12 + 4 damage | 18 | 6 |

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can animate two additional objects for each slot level above 5th.

## Quick Reference

- **Level/School:** 5th-level transmutation
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 ft | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Components:** V, S
- Animate up to 10 nonmagical objects; they become creatures under your control
- Bonus action to [[Command]]; 500-ft [[Command]] range
- Tiny objects are strongest per-action (1d4+4, +8 to hit, 18 AC)
- **At Higher Levels:** +2 objects per slot level above 5th
- **Key Concept:** Swarm-style summoning — ten Tiny objects deal massive combined damage per round
