## Effect

This spell allows you to change the appearance of **any number of creatures** that you can see within range. You give each target you choose a new, illusory appearance. An unwilling target can make a **Charisma saving throw**, and if it succeeds, it is unaffected by this spell.

The spell disguises physical appearance as well as clothing, armor, weapons, and equipment. You can make each creature seem 1 foot shorter or taller and appear thin, fat, or in between. You can't change a target's body type, so you must choose a form that has the same basic arrangement of limbs. The changes wrought by this spell fail to hold up to physical inspection.

A creature can use its action to inspect a target and make an **Intelligence (Investigation) check** against your spell save DC to discern the illusion.

## Quick Reference

- **Save:** CHA (unwilling targets only)
- **Targets:** Any number of creatures within 30 ft
- **Key:** Mass disguise for 8 hours; Investigation check to see through
