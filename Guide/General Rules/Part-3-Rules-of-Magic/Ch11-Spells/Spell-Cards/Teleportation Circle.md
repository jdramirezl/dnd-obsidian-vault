## Effect

As you cast the spell, you draw a 10-foot-diameter circle on the ground inscribed with sigils that link your location to a permanent teleportation circle of your choice whose sigil sequence you know and that is on the same plane of existence as you.

A shimmering portal opens within the circle you drew and remains open until the end of your next turn. Any creature that enters the portal instantly appears within 5 feet of the destination circle or in the nearest unoccupied space.

You can create a **permanent teleportation circle** by casting this spell in the same location every day for one year.

## Quick Reference

- **Save:** None
- **Targets:** 10-ft circle on the ground
- **Key:** Instant Teleport to known circle; 50 gp consumed per cast; 365 casts to make permanent
