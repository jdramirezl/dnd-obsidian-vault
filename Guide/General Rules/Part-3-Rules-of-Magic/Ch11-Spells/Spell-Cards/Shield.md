# Shield

*1st-level abjuration*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack or targeted by the *magic missile* spell |
| **Range** | Self |
| **Components** | V, S |
| **Duration** | 1 round |

## Effect

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from *magic missile*.

---

## Quick Reference

- **Level/School:** 1st-level abjuration
- **Casting Time:** 1 reaction (when hit by attack or targeted by *[[Magic Missile|magic missile]]*)
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round
- +5 bonus to AC until start of next turn (including triggering attack)
- Blocks all [[Magic Missile|*Magic Missile*]] damage
- **Key Concept:** Reaction-based AC boost — one of the best defensive spells in the game
