# Lesser Restoration

*2nd-level abjuration*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 action |
| **Range** | Touch |
| **Components** | V, S |
| **Duration** | Instantaneous |

## Effect

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned.

---

## Quick Reference

- **Level/School:** 2nd-level abjuration
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Effect:** Ends one disease or one condition ([[Blinded|blinded]], [[Deafened|deafened]], [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]], or [[Poisoned|poisoned]]) on the target.
- **Key Concept:** Basic condition removal — one disease or one of four conditions per casting.
