# Drawmij's Instant Summons
*6th-level conjuration (ritual)*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 minute |
| **Range** | Touch |
| **Components** | V, S, M (a sapphire worth 1,000 gp) |
| **Duration** | Until dispelled |

## Effect
You touch an object weighing 10 pounds or less whose longest dimension is 6 feet or less. The spell leaves an invisible mark on its surface and invisibly inscribes the name of the item on the sapphire you use as the material component. Each time you cast this spell, you must use a different sapphire.

At any time thereafter, you can use your action to speak the item's name and crush the sapphire. The item instantly appears in your hand regardless of physical or planar distances, and the spell ends. If another creature is holding or carrying the item, crushing the sapphire doesn't transport the item to you, but instead you learn who the creature possessing the object is and roughly where that creature is at that moment.

*Dispel magic* or a similar effect successfully applied to the sapphire ends this spell's effect.

---

## Quick Reference
- Ritual; mark an object (≤10 lbs, ≤6 ft)
- Crush the sapphire to summon the object to your hand instantly
- Works across planes
- If held by another creature, learn who and where instead
