# Glibness

*8th-level transmutation*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 action |
| **Range** | Self |
| **Components** | V |
| **Duration** | 1 hour |

## Effect

Until the spell ends, when you make a Charisma check, you can replace the number you roll with a 15. Additionally, no matter what you say, magic that would determine if you are telling the truth indicates that you are being truthful.

---

## Quick Reference

- **Level/School:** 8th-level transmutation
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Components:** V
- Replace any Charisma check roll with 15 (minimum floor, not a cap)
- All truth-detecting magic reads you as truthful regardless of actual truth
- No concentration required
- **Key Concept:** Ultimate social spell — guaranteed high Charisma checks and undetectable lies
