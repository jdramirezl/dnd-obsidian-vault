# Guards and Wards

*6th-level abjuration*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Range** | Touch |
| **Components** | V, S, M (burning incense, a small measure of brimstone and oil, a knotted string, a small amount of umber hulk blood, and a small silver rod worth at least 10 gp) |
| **Duration** | 24 hours |

## Effect

You create a ward that protects up to 2,500 square feet of floor space. The warded area can be up to 20 feet tall, and shaped as you desire. You can ward several stories of a stronghold by dividing the area among them.

You place the following magical effects within the warded area:

- **Corridors.** Fog fills all the warded corridors, making them heavily obscured. Webs fill the corridors as the web spell.
- **Doors.** All doors in the warded area are magically locked, as if sealed by an arcane lock spell.
- **Stairs.** Webs fill all stairs in the warded area from top to bottom, as the web spell. These webs regrow in 10 minutes if they are burned or torn away.
- **Other Effect.** You can place one of the following effects in each warded area: dancing lights in four corridors, magic mouth in two locations, stinking cloud in two locations, gust of wind in one corridor or room, or suggestion in one 5-foot-square area.

If you cast this spell every day for a year, the spell becomes permanent.
