# Dominate Beast

*4th-level enchantment*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 action |
| **Range** | 60 feet |
| **Components** | V, S |
| **Duration** | Concentration, up to 1 minute |

## Effect

You attempt to beguile a beast that you can see within range. It must succeed on a WIS saving throw or be charmed by you for the duration. If you or creatures that are friendly to you are fighting it, it has advantage on the saving throw.

While the beast is charmed, you have a telepathic link with it as long as the two of you are on the same plane of existence. You can use this telepathic link to issue commands to the creature while you are conscious (no action required), which it does its best to obey. You can specify a simple and general course of action, such as "Attack that creature," "Run over there," or "Fetch that object." If the creature completes the order and doesn't receive further direction from you, it defends itself to the best of its ability.

You can use your action to take total and precise control of the target. Until the end of your next turn, the creature takes only the actions you choose, and doesn't do anything that you don't allow it to do.

Each time the target takes damage, it makes a new WIS saving throw against the spell. If the saving throw succeeds, the spell ends.

**At Higher Levels.** Duration increases to 10 minutes (5th), 1 hour (6th), or 8 hours (7th+).
