# Stone Shape

*4th-level transmutation*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 action |
| **Range** | Touch |
| **Components** | V, S, M (soft clay, which must be worked into roughly the desired shape of the stone object) |
| **Duration** | Instantaneous |

## Effect

You touch a stone object of Medium size or smaller or a section of stone no more than 5 feet in any dimension and form it into any shape that suits your purpose. So, for example, you could shape a large rock into a weapon, idol, or coffer, or make a small passage through a wall, as long as the wall is less than 5 feet thick. You could also shape a stone door or its frame to seal the door shut. The object you create can have up to two hinges and a latch, but finer mechanical detail isn't possible.

## Quick Reference

- **Level/School:** 4th-level transmutation
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Components:** V, S, M (soft clay worked into rough desired shape)

- Can create passages through walls (< 5 ft thick), seal doors, shape weapons/objects
- Up to 2 hinges and a latch; no finer mechanical detail
- **Key Concept:** Utility shaping — bypass stone barriers, seal passages, or craft stone objects on the spot
