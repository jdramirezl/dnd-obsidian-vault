## Effect

You can see and hear a particular creature you choose that is on the same plane of existence as you. The target must make a **Wisdom saving throw**, which is modified by how well you know the target and the sort of physical connection you have to it.

| Knowledge                  | Save Modifier |
| -------------------------- | ------------- |
| Secondhand (heard of)      | +5            |
| Firsthand (you've met)     | +0            |
| Familiar (you know well)   | −5            |
| Likeness or picture        | −2            |
| Possession or garment      | −4            |
| Body part, hair, nail, etc.| −10           |

On a successful save, the target isn't affected, and you can't use this spell against it again for 24 hours. On a failed save, the spell creates an invisible sensor within 10 feet of the target. You can see and hear through the sensor as if you were there.

## Quick Reference

- **Save:** WIS (modified by familiarity)
- **Targets:** One creature on the same plane
- **Key:** Remote surveillance; save modifier based on connection to target; focus not consumed
