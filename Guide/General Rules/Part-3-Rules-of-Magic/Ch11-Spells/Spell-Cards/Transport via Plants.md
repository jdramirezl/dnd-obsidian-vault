# Transport via Plants

*6th-level conjuration*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 1 action |
| **Range** | 10 feet |
| **Components** | V, S |
| **Duration** | 1 round |

## Effect

This spell creates a magical link between a Large or larger inanimate plant within range and another plant, at any distance, on the same plane of existence. You must have seen or touched the destination plant at least once before. For the duration, any creature can step into the target plant and exit from the destination plant by using 5 feet of movement.

## Quick Reference

- **Level/School:** 6th-level **conjuration**
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 1 round
- **Effect:** Link a Large or larger plant to another known plant on the same plane. Any creature can step through using 5 feet of movement for 1 round.
- **Key Concept:** Instant long-range teleportation network between plants; any number of creatures can pass through.
