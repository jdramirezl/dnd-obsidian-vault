# Hallow

*5th-level evocation*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Casting Time** | 24 hours |
| **Range** | Touch |
| **Components** | V, S, M (herbs, oils, and incense worth at least 1,000 gp, which the spell consumes) |
| **Duration** | Until dispelled |

## Effect

You touch a point and infuse an area around it with holy (or unholy) power. The area can have a radius up to 60 feet, and the spell fails if the radius includes an area already under the effect of a *hallow* spell. The affected area is subject to the following effects.

First, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead can't enter the area, nor can such creatures charm, frighten, or possess creatures within it. Any creature charmed, frightened, or possessed by such a creature is no longer charmed, frightened, or possessed upon entering the area. You can exclude one or more of those creature types from this effect.

Second, you can bind an extra effect to the area. Choose the effect from the following list, or choose an effect offered by the DM. Some of these effects apply to creatures in the area; you can designate whether the effect applies to all creatures, creatures that follow a specific deity or leader, or creatures of a specific sort, such as orcs or trolls. When a creature that would be affected enters the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it can make a Charisma saving throw. On a success, the creature ignores the extra effect until it leaves the area.

| Effect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Courage** | Affected creatures can't be frightened while in the area |
| **Darkness** | Darkness fills the area; normal light and darkvision can't illuminate it |
| **Daylight** | Bright light fills the area; magical darkness can't extinguish it |
| **Energy Protection** | Resistance to one chosen damage type (except bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing) |
| **Energy Vulnerability** | Vulnerability to one chosen damage type (except bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing) |
| **Everlasting Rest** | Dead bodies can't become undead in the area |
| **Extradimensional Interference** | No extradimensional movement or travel in the area |
| **Fear** | Affected creatures are frightened while in the area |
| **Silence** | No sound can emanate from or enter the area |
| **Tongues** | Affected creatures can communicate with any other creature in the area |
