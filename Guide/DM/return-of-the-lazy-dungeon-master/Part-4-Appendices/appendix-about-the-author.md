# Appendices - About the Book and Author

## Original Text

### Back Cover

**PREPARE WHAT BENEFITS YOUR GAME**

Return of the Lazy Dungeon Master is a book designed to help Gamemasters of all kinds get more out of our roleplaying games by preparing less. This book makes use of the experiences of thousands of other GMs to help you focus on how you prepare your games, how you run your games, and how you think about your games.

Built around an eight-step process for lightweight game preparation, it covers a wide range of practical steps and preparation activities that will bring the biggest impact to your game.

Building off the concepts of the well-received GM's guidebook The Lazy Dungeon Master, Return of the Lazy Dungeon Master updates the lessons of that book with five years of new insights, new approaches, and new observations of the way people prepare and run RPGs.

Funded by over 6,700 backers on Kickstarter, Return of the Lazy Dungeon Master offers a new, focused approach for preparing and running our roleplaying games.

### Other Books by Sly Flourish

**Sly Flourish's Fantastic Locations** — Twenty locations to drop into your favorite fantasy roleplaying game. Each location builds on a fantastic theme, such as a mysterious ancient structure under the ice, a cursed castle of a mad king, a fallen celestial fortress, and a dwarven mine that broke into the tomb of a dead god. Completely system agnostic. Funded by 779 Kickstarter backers.

**Sly Flourish's Fantastic Adventures** — Ten short adventures for the fifth edition of the world's most popular roleplaying game. Each adventure is designed to give you everything you need to run a game and to prepare in as little time as possible. Written for characters of 2nd to 5th level, designed to be dropped into any fantasy campaign world. Funded by over 1,100 Kickstarter backers. Includes companion book with full-color artwork and maps.

## Condensed Version

**Return of the Lazy Dungeon Master** by Mike Shea (Sly Flourish), published August 2017. Funded by 6,700+ Kickstarter backers.

**Core premise:** An eight-step lightweight prep process that helps GMs focus on what actually benefits their game, built on five years of refinement from the original *The Lazy Dungeon Master* (2012) and informed by surveys of thousands of DMs.

**Author:** Mike Shea runs [Sly Flourish](http://slyflourish.com), one of the most popular D&D GM advice sites. His other books include *Fantastic Locations* (system-agnostic drop-in locations) and *Fantastic Adventures* (short 5e adventures designed for minimal prep).
