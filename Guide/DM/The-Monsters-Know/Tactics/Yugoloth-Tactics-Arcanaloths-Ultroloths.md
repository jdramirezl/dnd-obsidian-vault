# Yugoloth Tactics: Arcanaloths and Ultroloths

> [!info] Source
> The arcanaloth and ultroloth are covered in the original "Yugoloth Tactics" post alongside mezzoloths and nycaloths. See also: [[Yugoloth-Tactics-Dhergoloths]], [[Yugoloth-Tactics-Canoloths]].

## Shared Yugoloth Traits

All yugoloths: Perception proficiency; immune to acid/poison; resistant to cold, fire, lightning, nonmagical physical; Magic Resistance; darkvision/truesight; innate *darkness*; Teleport. Permanently killed only in Gehenna — no reason to retreat or flee.

---

## Arcanaloth (CR 12)

Spellcasting specialist. Very high Cha, extraordinarily high Int. Proficient in Deception + Insight. **Smooth customers — parley first.** Want information above all else.

### Combat Opener
1. *Darkness* first (120 ft. truesight) — sustain until a better concentration spell is needed
2. **8th-level *chain lightning*** (up to 5 targets) — first combat spell
3. **6th-level *chain lightning*** (up to 3 targets) — second

### Spell Priority
- *Chain lightning* (8th, then 6th): opening damage — always
- *Finger of death* (7th): single target, Con save ≤ +2, especially ≤ 60 hp (zombification)
- *Banishment* (4th, or 5th for 2 targets): troublesome foe with Cha save ≤ +2
- *Hold monster* (5th): only if one foe is significantly more threatening AND Wis save ≤ +2; or vs. summoned nonhumanoid
- *Fireball* (3rd-5th): clusters of 4+ after *chain lightning* expended; never use last 3rd/4th slot
- *Fear* (3rd): situational — disarm magic weapon wielder + provoke OA on Dash
- *Counterspell* (reaction): auto vs. spell attacks ≤ 5th level
- *Shield* (reaction, 1st): vs. weapon attacks; competes with *counterspell* for reaction
- *Mirror image*: vs. skilled weapon attacker when other spells aren't deterring
- *Fire bolt* (3d10): default ranged attack when conserving slots

### Positioning
**Flies 15 ft. up**, descends only when forced into melee. Claw attack: 8 slashing + 8 poison.

---

## Ultroloth (CR 13)

Not a team player — only **one** encountered at a time. Flies, wades into battle.

### Combat Routine
1. *Darkness* first (120 ft. truesight) if beneficial
2. **Hypnotic Gaze** (1/turn) → stun target → 3x longsword (two-handed) with advantage
3. Between turns: hover 30 ft. up, swoop down to attack, swoop back — ignores OAs
4. *Fire storm* (1/day): if it can catch **all** enemies in the area
5. *Wall of fire*: ring in 4+ enemies, then fly/Teleport out to attack anyone outside
6. *Fear*: disarm magic weapons + provoke OA on Dash
7. *Dimension door*: avoid disincorporation if convenient
8. *Mass suggestion* (if capturing): only if PCs aren't mostly elves and don't have Wis saves ≥ +3
