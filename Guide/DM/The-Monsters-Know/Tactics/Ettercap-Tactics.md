# Ettercap Tactics

> [!summary]
> Arachnid-humanoid ambush predators that herd giant spiders. High Dex with Stealth/Survival, Spider Climb, Web Sense, and Web Walker. Blanket forests with webs, ambush from hiding, restrain targets with Web, then claw/bite. Always accompanied by giant spiders.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Monstrosity, Medium
- Robust physical abilities, Dex leading slightly
- Proficient in Stealth, Survival; darkvision
- Spider Climb, Web Sense, Web Walker

---

## Pre-Combat Setup
- Blanket dimly lit forest areas with webs — wait for travelers to get stuck
- Lay webs across roads/trails — even noticed webs stop movement, giving ettercaps time to attack
- May encircle a sleeping campsite with webs overnight
- Always accompanied by **giant spiders** (at minimum a few)

---

## Combat Behavior

### Initiative
- Attack from hiding (trees via Spider Climb); try to gain surprise

### Target Priority
1. **Restrained PC** (stuck in web) → Multiattack: claw then bite
2. **PC who injured a spider** (reduced to ≤18 hp) → nearest ettercap focuses that PC
3. Otherwise: indiscriminate

### Action Selection
| Situation | Action |
|---|---|
| Target restrained | Multiattack (claw + bite) |
| Target unrestrained, Web available | Web (try to restrain) |
| Web on cooldown, melee engaged | Multiattack any engaged opponent or poisoned target |
| No obvious target, ranged/multiple attackers | Dodge (wait for Web recharge) |

- Ettercaps can tell which targets are poisoned (by ettercap or spider bite) — prioritize those
- Giant spiders also restrain and poison targets → no shortage of debilitated targets

---

## Retreat
- **Seriously injured (≤17 hp):** Disengage + full movement retreat
- Takes spiders with it

> [!tip] DM Tips
> Seed the encounter with webs everywhere. Ettercaps start hidden in trees. Giant spiders provide a steady supply of restrained/poisoned targets. The Dodge action while waiting for Web recharge is the smart play when no good target exists.
