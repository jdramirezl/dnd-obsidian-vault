# Leucrotta Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Leucrotta Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/leucrotta-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary

Leucrottas are large, fast (50 ft), strong brutes created by Yeenoghu. They lack Stealth proficiency but compensate with Mimicry, Keen Smell, darkvision, and a specific hunting pattern: they operate on **foggy nights** to exploit heavy obscurity, using Mimicry to lure victims while remaining unseen.

## Combat Behavior

- **Hunting Conditions:** Foggy nights only. Fog provides heavy obscurity for all creatures (even those with darkvision), but the leucrotta's Keen Smell (advantage, passive olfactory Perception 18) lets it locate hidden prey by scent.
- **Lure:** Uses Mimicry to imitate creatures in distress, drawing victims toward it through fog.
- **Surprise Attack:** Nearly guaranteed surprise in round 1. Charges when it scents prey within 50 ft. In fog, blinded attacker disadvantage and blinded target advantage cancel out — leucrotta attacks at normal rolls.
- **Core Loop:** Multiattack (Bite × 2, with extra crit die on Bite). Bite deals 3 dice on a critical hit instead of 2.
- **Rampage:** When it drops an enemy to 0 HP, gets bonus movement + another attack — chain kills.
- **Kicking Retreat:** Bonus action Disengage. Used when fog is dispersed (e.g., *gust of wind*) or in non-foggy terrain with cover. Multiattack → Kicking Retreat → trot 40–50 ft away → return next turn.
- **No need to Disengage in fog:** Can't make opportunity attacks against targets you can't see.
- **Association with gnolls:** Smarter than gnoll pack lords. Treats gnolls as pawns, associates out of convenience.

## Key Decision Points

1. **Fog is essential.** Without fog, the leucrotta's lack of Stealth proficiency makes ambush difficult. Use Kicking Retreat + terrain cover as fallback.
2. **Target selection:** Charge the softest target first, hoping for a Rampage chain.
3. **Kicking Retreat timing:** Only needed when fog is dispersed or in non-foggy environments.

## Retreat Conditions

- More bloodthirsty than typical predators — won't run until **seriously wounded (≤26 HP)**.
- Uses **Kicking Retreat** to Disengage when fleeing.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> A foggy night is a leucrotta night. The combination of Mimicry + Keen Smell + fog makes it nearly impossible for PCs to avoid surprise. If your party has *gust of wind* or similar, the leucrotta falls back to Kicking Retreat hit-and-run using terrain cover. Pair with gnolls for a Yeenoghu-themed encounter.
