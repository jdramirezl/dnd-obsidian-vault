# Oni Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Oni Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/oni-tactics/)
> **Bestiary**: [[Oni]]

Cousins to ogres but far more intelligent. Innate spellcasting, unstoppable regeneration (no fire/acid weakness). Brute contour (high Str/Con, avg Dex) plus high Int/Cha, above-avg Wis. Proficient in Dex/Con/Wis saves. Can fly. Masters of attrition — the longer the battle, the better.

## Spells

| Spell | Notes |
|---|---|
| *Cone of cold* (1/day) | Big gun. Use only after opponents prove dangerous (≤77 hp). |
| *Gaseous form* (1/day) | Time-killer for regen. Also pursues through locked doors. Concentration, 10 ft speed. |
| *Sleep* (1/day) | Useless once squishiest enemy >16 hp. |
| *Charm person* (1/day) | Pre-combat only on low-Wis targets. Waste after combat starts. |
| *Darkness* (at will) | Impedes oni too (no blindsight). Use to blind troublesome spellcasters. Backseat to *invisibility*. |
| *Invisibility* (at will) | Key tool — avoid damage, set up attacks, flee. |

## Change Shape

Costs an action. Polymorph into Small/Medium humanoid or other Large giant (ogre, ettin, troll). Stats don't change — only size/appearance/glaive damage. Used mainly to pass unnoticed. Reverts to true form to fight.

## Combat

**Target selection**: All doing comparable damage → focus weakest first. One or two doing far more → focus strongest. Never attack charmed targets.

**Fly-by attacks**: Feasible with 10-ft glaive reach. But may fight toe-to-toe as a brute.

**Won't initiate combat during daytime** — attacks at night for darkvision advantage.

## Attrition Pattern

After opponents deal >30 hp in a round:
1. *Invisibility*, lie low 1 round + 1 per 10 hp above 30
2. Position behind target, attack (becomes visible)

**Moderately wounded (≤77 hp after regen)**: *Cone of cold* if can catch all opponents (or 6). *Sleep* on non-elf spellcaster ≤16 hp.

## Retreat

**Seriously wounded (≤44 hp after regen)**:
- Last opponent barely holding on → *invisibility* to regen above 44, then coup de grâce
- Otherwise → *invisibility* + fly away
- Opponent penetrates *invisibility* → Dodge while fleeing
- Regens above 77 hp → may turn around and fight again

Oni generally don't want to fight experienced adventurers — they want to kidnap and eat people in peace.
