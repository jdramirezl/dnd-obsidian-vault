# Guardinal Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/guardinal-tactics/) | **Date**: October 1, 2024
> **Book**: Planescape: Adventures in the Multiverse

---

## Musteval Guardinal (CR 2)

- **Tiny shock attacker**. Dex-primary, 35 ft speed. Finesse melee, no ranged attacks.
- Proficient in Dex saves, Perception, Stealth. High Int/Wis → excellent tactical assessment.
- Spells: *dancing lights* (at will), *disguise self* 1/day, *invisibility* 1/day.
- **Skirmish Movement** (reaction): Back off when charged without provoking OA. Doesn't escape existing melee — need Disengage for that.

### Encounter Shape

- Encountered in **patrols of 4–6**, not singly. Often spies/scouts.
- Prefer *invisibility* at **start of combat** (ambush opener), not as escape hatch.
- Drop invisibility all at once to surround opposition or get surprise Multiattack.
- 1 MG per non-robust foe; 2–3 MGs team up vs robust melee combatants.
- **Nonlethal** attacks unless targets are fiends, undead, or total rotters.

### Retreat

- Individual: Disengages at ≤15 hp; allies step in to delay.
- Group: Retreats when ≥half seriously wounded or 1 is killed.

## Equinal Guardinal (CR 4)

- **Big and horsey**. Extraordinary Str, very high Dex/Con. Speed 50 ft. Not subtle.

### Attack Sequence

1. Throw rocks until **50 ft from target** → stop.
2. Next turn: **Headfirst Charge** (30 ft min) for extra 4d6 + knockdown.
3. Follow with **two Fist attacks** (advantage if prone).
4. **Shout** (recharge 6): When within 30 ft of ≥6 opponents. Use after failed knockdown or when target stands.

### Retreat

- Disengages at ≤37 hp, or when one ally is slain.

---

## Avoral Guardinal (CR 9)

- **Flying shock attacker**. 50 ft fly + Flyby trait.

### Dive-Bomb Loop

1. Dive from 30 ft up → **Dive Attack** (knock prone) → two **Talon attacks** (advantage vs prone) → soar 20 ft back up.
2. Repeat.

### Spell Substitution

- Multiattack lets it swap one Talon for a spell.
- ***Hold person***: Only if Dive Attack failed AND target has low Wis save (requires prior recon).
- ***Command***: Same function but can't sustain — needs allies on field.
- **Caveat**: Never worth forgoing one attack just for advantage on a second.

> [!note] MG + AG Combo
> MG's *invisibility* isn't tagged "self only" — can cast on AG buddy for invisible dive-bomb openers.

### Retreat

- Flees at ≤68 hp, when half the group is seriously wounded, or when one ally is slain.
