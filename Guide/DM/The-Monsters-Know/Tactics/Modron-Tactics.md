# Modron Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/modron-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- Constructs from Mechanus (plane of pure law). CRs top out at 2—dangerous only in legions
- All share natural armor, above-average Dex, 120 ft truesight, Axiomatic Mind, and Disintegration
- Lawful neutral: default disposition is indifference. Fight only when PCs break rules or cause a ruckus
- Can only communicate one rank up or down—killing middlemen disrupts the chain of command

## By Type

### Monodrone (CR 1/8)
- Animal-level Int. One instruction, followed to the letter, no adaptation
- Approach to 30 ft → throw javelin → close to melee with dagger. Retrieve javelin if opponent defeated
- No self-preservation. Fights until destroyed

### Duodrone (CR 1/4)
- Can't fly. Attacks twice per action (same weapon). Carries 5 javelins (odd number)
- Approach to 30 ft → throw 2 → throw 2 more if not engaged → keep 1 for melee
- Retrieves javelins only in pairs. No retreat

### Tridrone (CR 1/2)
- Near-average Int. Adapts to terrain, cover, chokepoints. Carries 7 javelins (3n+1)
- Orders lesser modrons to Disengage and reposition. Covers their retreat
- Recognizes anti-construct weapons and focuses fire on that enemy

### Quadrone (CR 1)
- Archer: 4 attacks/turn with shortbow, ~20 arrows. Leads from the rear at 35–80 ft
- After 5 rounds (out of arrows): orders tactical retreat toward reinforcements

### Pentadrone (CR 2)
- Large brute. Paralysis Gas (30-ft cone) when 3+ enemies catchable
- Attacks non-paralyzed targets; leaves paralyzed ones to lesser modrons (which need advantage more)
- Orders retreat only after 5 rounds

## Combined Tactics
- When pentadrones lead: lesser modrons rush paralyzed opponents for auto-crit melee hits
- Prefer to subdue (nonlethal melee) for trial—but ranged-optimized modrons won't compromise effectiveness
- Solution to any problem: throw more modrons at it. Mechanus never runs out

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Kill the duodrones before tridrones can relay withdrawal orders to monodrones—the monodrones will mindlessly fight to the death in place. Modrons are terrifying in formation, pathetic when their hierarchy is disrupted.
