# Morkoth Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/morkoth-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- CR 11 aberration: paranoid, tentacled hoarder drifting through planes on its own private island
- Lair immersed in water by default (swim 50 ft, land 25 ft). Can toggle water breathability at will
- Intelligence 20; prefers spellcasting over melee. Multiattack reserved for enemies in its face
- Spell Reflection reaction redirects ~2/3 of incoming spell effects at casters' allies. *Loves* enemy spellcasters
- Resistant to nonmagical physical damage. Achilles' heel: Constitution saves (no proficiency)
- Perception +10, Stealth proficiency, 30 ft blindsight, 120 ft darkvision

## Combat Behavior
- **Environment**: Darkens lair water preemptively. Total darkness suits it—blindsight + Perception +10
- **Hypnosis** (30-ft cone): 3+ enemies. Draws charmed targets in. Used to shut down Con-save casters, ranged magic-weapon users, or *counterspell* users—avoids drawing in melee magic-weapon fighters
- **Spell priority**:
  - *Shatter*: Good opener for 2+ clustered front-liners
  - *Evard's black tentacles*: 4+ enemies in a 20-ft square, sets up *lightning bolt*
  - *Lightning bolt*: 3+ in a line (3 if restrained). Boost to 5th level for 4+
  - *Chain lightning* (6th level): Saved for ≤91 hp—the "these PCs are real" moment
  - *Dispel magic*: Only vs. 3rd–5th level buffs. Favorite target: *water breathing*
  - *Darkness*: Backup when enemies stop casting (denying Spell Reflection) or morkoth flubs 2+ saves
  - *Shield*: Vs. magic weapon attacks; weigh against Spell Reflection
- **Target priority**: Preys on the weak first (archers, rogues, monks, bards, druids). Ignores non-magic-weapon front-liners. Redirects spells intelligently—supporters get Dex saves, rogues get Wis saves

## Lair Actions (Initiative 20)
- Can't repeat same lair action two turns in a row
- *Misty step*: Most common. Avoids opportunity attacks, enhances movement
- *Dispel magic*: Only for 3rd-level effects (can't boost as lair action)
- *Darkness* / Hypnosis: Only when it would use them anyway

## Retreat Conditions
- Retreats between 52–91 hp when clearly losing—uses *dimension door* to hide elsewhere on its island
- Doesn't flee permanently. Recuperates and returns for payback. It's never over

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The morkoth *wants* your casters to throw spells at it—Spell Reflection turns enemy magic into friendly fire. Dispelling *water breathing* mid-fight is peak villainy.
