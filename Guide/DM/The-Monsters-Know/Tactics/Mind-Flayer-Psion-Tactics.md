# Mind Flayer Psion Tactics

> **Source**: [Mind Flayer Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/mind-flayer-tactics/) / [Revisited](https://www.themonstersknow.com/mind-flayers-revisited/)
> *No dedicated "Mind Flayer Psion" article exists. This synthesizes the base mind flayer and elder brain articles.*

---

## Tactical Summary
- Enhanced mind flayer with additional psionic spellcasting atop standard innate abilities
- Base mind flayer: CR 7, Int 19, proficiency in all mental saves, Magic Resistance, Stealth/Perception
- Primary weapon: Mind Blast (60-ft cone, recharge 5–6), stuns on failed Int save
- Tentacle attack grapples and stuns—reserved for already-incapacitated targets. Extract Brain kills a stunned+grappled foe outright
- Uses minions (troglodytes, grimlocks, quaggoths, ogres, grells) as a front line

## Combat Behavior
- **Positioning**: 60 ft behind the front line, *levitating* 10 ft up so melee can't reach it
- **Primary action**: Mind Blast whenever available and 6+ enemies (or all) are in the cone
- **Secondary**: *Dominate monster* on the most dangerous ranged attacker (archers bypass Magic Resistance and outrange Mind Blast)
- **Melee**: Only after a target is stunned. Move in → Tentacles to grapple → Extract Brain next turn
- **Psion additions**: Layer wizard spells—*wall of force* to isolate, *confusion* to disrupt, *blur* for self-protection

## Retreat Conditions
- Seriously injured (≤28 hp): *Plane shift* to escape
- Non-critical lair: cuts losses at ≤49 hp, lets minions handle cleanup
- Colony defense: fights to the death for the elder brain

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The mind flayer should never be alone—it needs expendable minions. Levitate above the fray, Mind Blast the party, then swoop in to extract brains from the stunned. If an archer is shredding it, *dominate* them immediately.
