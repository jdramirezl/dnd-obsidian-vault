# Eyedrake & Elder Brain Dragon Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/eyedrake-and-elder-brain-dragon-tactics/) | **Date**: November 10, 2022
> **Book**: Fizban's Treasury of Dragons

---

## Eyedrake

- **Quasi-beholder** born from a beholder's jealousy of a dragon rival. More brutish than beholders (very high Str).
- **Ambush attacker**: Expertise in Perception and Stealth. Prefers total darkness (darkvision advantage).
- **Slow flight (30 ft)**: Once in melee, not in a hurry to leave. Smart enough to move out of reach of Extra Attack foes.
- **Antimagic Breath (recharge 6 only)**: Wait for TWO conditions: (1) aim at ≥3 opponents, (2) at least one is buffed by a 1st–3rd level spell. Don't waste it round 1 with no buffs active.
  - Round 1: Use only if both conditions met.
  - Round 2: Use if conditions met OR already moderately wounded (≤83 hp).
  - Round 3+: Use if not already used; use again if recharged + conditions/wounded.
  - Also use to dispel darkness (cast on object) or light (in total darkness).
- **Eye Rays**: Fire at random, but choose best targets:
  - Freezing Ray → finesse weapon / speed >30 ft
  - Debilitating Ray → non-melee with robust action economy
  - Repulsion/Paralyzing Ray → rogues, monks, Dex shock attackers
  - Fire/Death Ray → tough melee opponents
- **Fights to the death** — compulsion too strong to imagine not fighting.

## Elder Brain Dragon

- **Nightmarish CR enemy**: Dragon puppeted by an elder brain. Fast, hovering, blindsight + long-range telepathy.
- **Brute** (colossal Str, extraordinary Int/Cha, high Con). Siege Monster — smashes buildings first.
- **Multiattack backbone**: Claw (10-ft reach — can attack from outside most melee weapon reach while airborne), Bite, and Tentacle (15-ft reach, grapples on hit).
- **Tadpole Brine Breath (recharge 5–6)**: 120-ft line, 15 ft wide. Use whenever available. Hard floor: ≥4 targets, but try to hit more. Prioritize squishy/badly wounded targets.
  - Infests ALL targets with illithid tadpoles regardless of save result.
  - Requires 3 successful saves, remove curse, or ≥40 hp healing at once to purge.
  - 0 hp while infested → begin transforming into mind flayer (6–72 hours).
- **Tentacle + Shatter Concentration (2 legendary actions)**: Grapple a concentrating caster → auto-terminate their concentration. Reads focused attention on faces. Only use Shatter Concentration when needed — otherwise spend legendary actions on more Tentacle attacks.
- **Retreat at ≤140 hp**: Withdraws (Disengage if needed, Dash otherwise). Allies cover retreat.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Monsters
> The eyedrake is a nuisance for intermediate+ parties. The elder brain dragon is a campaign-defining threat. Its Tadpole Brine Breath infests regardless of save — make sure players understand the stakes. The Tentacle + Shatter Concentration combo specifically punishes spellcasters maintaining buffs.
