# Dimir NPC Tactics

## Tactical Summary

House Dimir is a guild of secret agents, assassins, and shadowy horrors. If its members find themselves in a head-to-head fight, they've done something wrong. Dimir doesn't use soldiers — their assets are **spies**.

## Spy Categories

### Snoops (Gray People)
- Unremarkable people whose job is to observe, report, and **not be noticed**
- Low CHA — self-control (WIS) matters more than personality
- Personas: taciturn bartender, market beggar, ragpicker
- Carry only a hidden dagger (weapon of last resort)
- **If busted:** Run — Cunning Action to Disengage/Dash/Hide; vanish from that city forever

### Plants (Undercover Agents)
- Go undercover to observe and conduct clandestine activities; strive not to be **doubted**
- Expertise in Deception (+7); proficiency in extra skills for false identity
- Weapons stashed nearby, not on person
- **If busted:** Deny everything, act wounded, propose alternative explanations, forgive accusers
- **If forced to run:** Move cautiously, stay hidden, never take potshots (exposes position)
- **If cornered:** Fight to the death — captured spies are killed

### Plant Modifications
- Deception attempts against them have **Disadvantage**
- Expertise in Deception (+7), 2 extra skill proficiencies, 1 ASI per 4 party levels, PB matches PCs'

## Thought Spy

- Less CHA, more INT, darkvision, psionic spellcasting replaces Sneak Attack
- **Recommended fix:** Raise Investigation to +7, Perception to +5 (no CR impact)
- **Gaseous form:** Primary escape — slip through cracks; Cunning Action to Dash or Hide as mist
- **Disguise self:** Maintain false identity; snoops use it to become utterly unremarkable
- **Detect thoughts:** Surface thoughts only in the field — probing deeper **reveals the spy to the target**
- **Charm person + disguise self:** Killer combo — neither needs concentration, both last 1 hour; target knows who charmed them but that person doesn't exist
- **Encode thoughts:** Tangible thought strands for evidence/blackmail — dangerous if caught carrying one
- Combat: be gone before found → Disengage and flee → speed + *disguise self* + big city = vanish

## Mind Mage

- Rarely in the field; often flaunts identity (people are terrified)
- Specializes in interrogation, crowd control, mind-wiping, *dominate person*
- Uses *spies' murmur* so a confederate always has the information
- Uses *encode thoughts* **after** interrogation, not during (subject would clam up)
- Interrogation: slick, professional — gets subjects *wanting* to talk; always seeking **specific** info
- **If losing control:** Leave — a different mind mage takes over
- **If cornered:** *Mass suggestion* or *dominate person*; if that fails, **surrender unconditionally** — House Dimir pays ransom

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Dimir encounters should start with social intrigue, not combat
> - Thought spies should never deep-probe *detect thoughts* in the field
> - For a Dimir spy, capture almost always means death — this produces zealotry overriding self-preservation
> - Mind mages are "rough stuff" specialists — save for interrogation scenes
