# Planescape Faction Agent Tactics, Part 3

## Hands of Havoc Fire Starter (CR 5)

### Tactical Summary

Strong, well-armored radicals. High STR + WIS. Equally comfortable melee or ranged (≤30 ft). Havoc Hammers deal more damage than Havoc Flasks.

### Combat Behavior

- High WIS = pick fights carefully, choose the moment to strike
- **Weapon choice:** Ranged attacks vs melee-favoring opponents; melee attacks vs ranged-favoring opponents
- Havoc Hammers deal more damage — may never need to switch to flasks
- **Disguise:** Often pose as members of other factions. May gain surprise by initiating combat in disguise — even better, a group each disguised as a different faction

### Retreat Conditions

- Moderately wounded (≤45 hp): begin withdrawing
- Lob Havoc Flasks to cover retreat, switch to Dodge when out of flasks

### Homebrew Note

> Havoc Flasks should deal extra 2d12 thunder damage to objects/structures (rebranded Molotov cocktails).

## Heralds of Dust Remnant (CR 4)

### Tactical Summary

Arcane washouts trapped between life and death. Spellcasters with *mage armor* (always up). Proficiency in Perception, expertise in Stealth — ambush attackers.

### Combat Behavior

- **Necrotic Surge:** 2x/turn via Multiattack, 0-120 ft range, avg 28 damage if both hit
- **Phase (bonus action, concentration):** Resistance to ALL physical damage. Continuous until concentration broken, can re-up once
- **Opportunity cost:** *Bane* and *web* both require concentration = lose Phase resistance
  - Solo remnant: never cast *bane*/*web* while Phase is available
  - Group of 4+ remnants: one may cast *web* (advantage = ~+20% hit chance for allies). Need 3+ allies benefiting to justify the caster's lost damage
  - *Web* caster stays 40-60 ft back, behind cover
- **Dimension Door (1/day):** Exit strategy. Remnants know exactly where their staging chambers are

### Retreat Conditions

- Zealous streak — stick it out longer than most
- Seriously wounded (≤18 hp): *dimension door* out
- Once both Phase uses are spent: take that as cue to leave

## Mind's Eye Matter Smith (CR 5)

### Tactical Summary

Flat ability contour, excels in CHA. Spellcasters indifferent to range. *Mage armor* assumed pre-cast. No combat spells (1/day spells are *create food and water* and *fabricate*).

### Combat Behavior

- **Manifested Force:** Multiattack, avg 10 damage/hit, melee or ranged
- **Planar Smithing (bonus action):**
  - **Magic Shield:** Always works, increases AC. Use every time
  - **Chains:** Low DC, likely fails ≥45% of the time. Only use if damage to target is unacceptable
- Focus fire: after 1 round or with advance intel, identify priority target and concentrate attacks

### Retreat Conditions

- Highly developed self-preservation
- Moderately wounded (≤54 hp): withdraw
- Smart enough to Disengage when necessary, otherwise Dash

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Fire starters in disguise make great ambush encounters. Remnants are terrifying in groups — one casting *web* while others blast with advantage. Matter smiths are straightforward but disciplined focus-firers.
