# Nothic Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/nothic-tactics/) | **Date**: September 1, 2016
> **Book**: Monster Manual

---

## Overview

- Former wizards cursed by Vecna — aberration/monstrosity hybrid driven by compulsion.
- Proficient in Stealth, Perception, Arcana, Insight. **Truesight 120 ft**.
- Seeks social interaction (one-way) and secrets. Intensely solitary — only ever one at a time.
- Communicates in Undercommon; some possess **telepathy** (DM's choice — recommended for PC interaction).

## Pre-Combat Behavior

1. **Detects PCs at 120 ft** via truesight. Initially curious, not hostile (treat as "indifferent").
2. **Stalks** PCs if it's the only sentient creature in the area; otherwise waits for them to come to it.
3. At **30 ft**: begins using **Weird Insight** — learns a secret about each PC (extrapolate from backgrounds/characteristics).
4. If telepathic: shares disturbing, nihilistic observations about each PC's secrets. Stays hidden — telepathy doesn't reveal position.
5. Emerges from hiding only if a PC **initiates conversation**. Keeps 20–30 ft distance. Eccentric and sinister but not threatening.
6. Interested in **magical secrets** — may trade knowledge for a magic item.

> [!tip] Running the Nothic
> The nothic's whispers should be personalized and unsettling. Put a sinister spin on each PC's backstory. Aim for 80% creeped out, 20% "yeah, that's about right."

## Combat

- **Does not want to fight**. Attacks only in self-defense.
- If cornered: uses **Rotting Gaze** reflexively (like a skunk's spray) against the first PC within 20 ft.
- Otherwise: **Dodge + move** to maximum cover. Only uses double claw attack if it has nowhere to run.
- If a melee attacker drops to 0 hp: switches to Rotting Gaze against ranged attackers within 30 ft, then seeks escape.
- **Always** seeks to hide or escape rather than fight. Uses Rotting Gaze against any pursuer who gets too close.

## Retreat

- Doesn't wait until seriously wounded — tries to flee **immediately** when combat starts.

## PC Fears (Optional Enhancement)

If Weird Insight check succeeds by 5+, the nothic intuits what the PC fears. Sample fears by alignment:

- **LG**: Defect in your character will lead you astray / No one will look out for you / No one cares what you need
- **NG**: You're useless and helpless / Nothing good can last / You're not that important
- **CG**: Freedom can't last forever / You're nobody without the spotlight / Softness means losing freedom
- **LN**: They know you haven't lived up to your standards / Everyone will abandon you / The one you love loves someone else
- **N**: You're not the expert you claim / Changes are coming and you're not ready / Nobody appreciates you
- **CN**: Life is boring and there's nothing better / Everyone sees through you / You've lost control
- **LE**: You're out of control, your actions are unjustifiable / They hate you, destroy them first / Your selfishness drives people away
- **NE**: You can't defend yourself from what's closing in / You brought this on yourself / Everything is hopeless
- **CE**: You've ruined yourself / You're an empty phony about to be exposed / You can't hold out much longer
