# Boros Angel & Skyjek Roc Tactics

> [!summary]
> Ravnica's Boros air force. Skyjek rocs carry knights for recon, bombardment (Sunforger hammer bombs!), and dogfighting. Battleforce angels are flying field officers who buff allies with Battlefield Inspiration. Firemane angels are solo champions who lock down powerful foes with *compelled duel* and 120-ft flying speed.

---

## Skyjek Roc (CR 2)
- Horse-sized avian; strong brute, 90 ft fly speed
- Proficient: DEX/WIS saves, Perception (advantage on sight)
- Usually a **controlled mount**

### Missions
- **Recon:** Roc Dodges while rider observes; Dashes away on threat (180 ft/turn)
- **Bombardment:** Rider hurls **Sunforger warhammer** (120 ft, explodes like ¾ fireball); roc Dashes away next round; rider retrieves hammer remotely. One bomb per knight.
- **Dogfighting:** Controlled (Dodge) if knight handles it alone; independent (attacks) if extra damage needed

### Retreat
- Withdraw when rider ≤20 hp or roc ≤14 hp
- Independent roc needs rider to tell it to withdraw (1-turn delay)

---

## Battleforce Angel (CR 5)
- Exceptional CHA, very high WIS + STR; Flyby; Magic Resistance
- 120 ft darkvision + truesight; proficient Perception + Investigation

### Battlefield Inspiration (In Multiattack)
- 3 allies within 30 ft → +1d4 attacks and saves (like *bless*, no concentration)

### Combat Flow
1. Fly in → Multiattack (2× Longsword two-handed) + Battlefield Inspiration
2. Fly out (Flyby — no opportunity attack); bait tough foes away from soldiers
3. Treats **concealed/disguised enemies** as primary targets (truesight)
4. Helps neutralize spellcasters threatening its troops

### Retreat
- Fights to the bitter end; may order troops to withdraw while covering them

---

## Firemane Angel (CR 12)
- Extraordinary CHA + STR, very high CON; 120 ft fly speed
- Solitary champion vs. unique powerful adversaries

### Combat Flow
1. Fly within 30 ft → **compelled duel** (bonus action)
2. Close to 5 ft → Multiattack (2× Longsword, radiant damage)
3. Withdraw **30 ft up** (max without breaking spell) — grounded foe can't reach it
4. Repeat: fly in, strike, fly up
5. **Fireball** (6th level) only against reinforcing minions (breaks *compelled duel*)
6. **Guiding bolt** (5th level) only when melee is impossible

### Retreat
- **Fights to the death, always** — absolute zealot, no exceptions

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Sunforger Bombing Runs
> Each Skyknight gets one explosion per encounter. The hammer is retrievable at any range. Holy warhammers raining down and exploding is peak Ravnica.

- CR 3 knight + CR 2 roc ≈ CR 4 for encounter math
- Battleforce angel stays airborne at all times
- Firemane's *compelled duel* + flight = brutal yo-yo for grounded foes
