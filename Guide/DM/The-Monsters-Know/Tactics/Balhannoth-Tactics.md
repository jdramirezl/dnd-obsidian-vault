# Balhannoth Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Half ambush predator, half reality-warping lure. A Shadowfell denizen that reshapes its lair to mimic a victim's deepest desires, then snatches them. In combat it's a mechanistic brute (INT 6) — grab with tentacles, bite with advantage, repeat. The real danger is the pre-combat setup.

## Pre-Combat Lure Sequence

Execute in this order:

1. **Sense desires** (regional, 1 mile) — detect longings; do NOT yet implant "fulfillment is near"
2. **Reshape lair** (lair action, 500 ft. square) — takes **10 minutes**; must start before victim arrives
3. **Implant longing** (regional) — only after the set is built, lure victim in
4. **Become invisible** (lair action, 500 ft.) — turn invisible to the specific victim
5. **Teleport victim** (lair action) — yoink them in; skip if invisibility failed
6. **Vanish** (legendary action) — full invisibility before attacking

Allow ~30 seconds of the victim enjoying their "dream" before combat begins.

## Combat Behavior

### Multiattack
- **2× Tentacle + Bite** — default; grapple then bite with advantage (restrained target)
- **4× Tentacle** — when rushed by multiple enemies; grab many, then Bite Attack as legendary action

### Tentacle Mechanics
- Grapple + restrain on hit; up to 4 targets simultaneously
- Hold difficult targets at 10 ft. (out of melee reach); bite easier ones up close

### Legendary Actions (3/round)
1. **Bite Attack** — chomp a grappled victim (most common)
2. **Teleport** (60 ft.) — reposition, escape heavy hitters (12+ dmg), or carry victim away
3. **Vanish** — turn invisible; use after last grappled victim escapes, then reposition

### Target Selection
- First attack targets AC 15+ to maximize unseen-attacker advantage
- AC ≤14 = easy prey → grab and teleport away to eat in peace
- AC 15–19 = worth eating only while restrained; release if they escape
- AC 20+ = not worth the trouble

## Retreat Conditions

- ≤79 hp: breaks off if victim fights back effectively
- ≤45 hp: flees; prefers to teleport away with one victim rather than fight a group

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - The lure sequence happens outside initiative — bend the rules or half the kit is useless
> - The "dream campsite" should be detailed but subtly wrong — uncanny valley for maximum creep
> - It grabs one victim and teleports away to eat in peace; it's a predator, not a brawler
