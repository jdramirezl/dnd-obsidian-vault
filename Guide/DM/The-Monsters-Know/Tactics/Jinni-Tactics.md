# Jinni Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/jinni-tactics/) | **Date**: October 27, 2016

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Humanoid soul bound to elemental air**. MM uses "djinni" — jinni is the standard spelling. Chaotic good but haughty and vengeful.
- **Ability contour**: Extraordinarily high Str/Con/Cha, high Dex/Int/Wis. Can fight however it wants. Strong self-preservation, shrewd target selection, willing to negotiate.
- **Not afraid of most spellcasters** thanks to saving throws and damage immunities.
- **Default behavior is parley**, not combat. Will seek a bargain if it recognizes the PCs as a major threat.

## Parley Threshold

A jinni takes PCs seriously if they can inflict moderate injury (reduce to ≤112 hp) or demonstrate the ability to do so. Benchmarks:

| Party Size | Damage Per PC Per Turn |
|---|---|
| 4 | ≥12 hp |
| 5 | ≥10 hp |
| 6 | ≥8 hp |

These are probabilistic averages accounting for chance to hit vs. AC 17.

**If respected**: Seeks a bargain serving both its interests and the PCs'.
**If insulted**: Seeks a bargain that *seems* to serve the PCs but actually screws them. Uses *creation* (products fall to dust in 10 min–1 hr) or summons an elemental "servant" that turns on them once the jinni leaves.

## Combat Behavior

**Round 1 — Create Whirlwind**: Immediately. Targets depend on opposition:
- Cluster of opponents
- Most powerful melee opponent (restrain)
- Bothersome ranged attacker (take out of play)

Moves whirlwind to scoop up multiple opponents. Capacity: four 5-ft squares, 30 ft high — holds 3–4 Medium creatures per "story." Jinni flies out of melee reach while doing this.

**Subsequent rounds — Scimitar Multiattack**: Triple attack (slashing + lightning/thunder bonus). Expected damage vs. AC 15: ~35 hp normally, ~44 hp against restrained targets. Targets the most powerful opponent within reach, especially restrained ones.

**If flanked by melee opponents**: Casts *thunderwave* (at will, DC 17) to repel, then flies to face all enemies. Thunderwave = Disengage + free damage.

**Offensive *plane shift***: Against a spellcaster powerful enough to deal ≥49 hp past defenses who botches a DC 17 Cha save — banished to the Elemental Plane of Air.

## Spell Usage

| Spell | Use |
|---|---|
| *Conjure elemental* | Summon an ally |
| *Thunderwave* (at will) | Repel flanking melee attackers (DC 17) |
| *Major image* | Create a distraction |
| *Plane shift* | Offensive banishment or escape |
| *Gaseous form* | Escape |
| *Invisibility* | Escape |

## Retreat Conditions

- **Seriously injured (≤64 hp)**: Bugs out via *gaseous form* or *plane shift*.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The jinni prefers to talk, not fight. Lead with personality — haughty, proud, willing to deal but intolerant of disrespect. Combat opens with Create Whirlwind to lock down threats, then scimitar Multiattack from the air. *Thunderwave* at will is the secret weapon against melee pressure. If the PCs are rude, the jinni's "help" should be a beautifully crafted trap.
