# Otyugh Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Brute. High STR, extraordinary CON. Animal-level INT (6) but Limited Telepathy. Subterranean ambush feeder — not aggressive unless hungry. Darkvision 120 ft.

## Combat Behavior

- **Telepathy:** Only message is *"Hungry."* Wise PCs throw food and steer clear
- **Trigger:** Hungry otyugh attacks when prey comes within 10 ft. Starving otyugh attacks at 40 ft
- **Multiattack:** Two Tentacle attacks first (grapple + restrain), then Bite
  - 1st tentacle hits → 2nd tentacle grabs a second target if in reach, otherwise smacks the first again
  - Bite always directed at a grappled target (advantage)
- **Tentacle Slam:** Used when starting turn with grappled victim(s) — bludgeon into submission
- **Swallow (homebrew addition):** Grappled victim reduced to 0 hp by Bite is eaten. Model on remorhaz Swallow but reduce digestion to 4d6. Only works on unconscious targets. If victim drops to 0 hp from tentacle/slam, otyugh needs an additional Bite to Swallow

## Retreat Conditions

- Doesn't move unless it has to. Approaches at normal speed if meals back away
- Retreats only if seriously wounded (≤45 hp)
- If starving: won't retreat even then

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Better as a hazard than a boss. Keep encounters at Medium difficulty or below. Possible to bargain with — its only interest is food.
