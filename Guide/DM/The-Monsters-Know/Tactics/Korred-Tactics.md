# Korred Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Korred Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/korred-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary

Korreds are Small fey with ridiculous Strength and Constitution — brute fighters designed to be underestimated. They have natural armor, resistance to nonmagical physical damage, darkvision, tremorsense, and Stone Camouflage for advantage on Stealth in rocky terrain. Chaotic neutral and territorial, they attack when insulted or annoyed by mining sounds.

## Combat Behavior

- **Ambush:** Lie in wait using Stone Camouflage + Stealth. Begin with **Command Hair** (bonus action, DC 13 Dex save to restrain) — not an attack, so doesn't reveal hidden positions.
- **Core Loop:** Command Hair (bonus action) → Multiattack (action). Restrained targets grant advantage on attacks.
- **Ranged:** Hurl rocks if available (mountainous/subterranean) — more damage than greatclubs.
- **Melee Fallback:** Greatclubs when no rocks available.
- **Command Hair:** Restraining rope requires destruction to escape (no additional saves). A korred can carry multiple ropes and restrain multiple targets, but commands only one per bonus action.
- **Conjure Elemental:** Pre-summoned before planned encounters (1-min casting time). Include elemental CR in encounter budget.
- **Target Priority:** Magic weapon wielders, Extra Attack fighters, and spellcasters first.
- **Numbers:** Won't pick fights they can't win; attack only in sufficient numbers.

## Key Decision Points

1. **Planned vs. chance encounter:** If planned, elementals pre-summoned and korreds hidden. If chance, skip *conjure elemental* and go straight to Command Hair + Multiattack.
2. **Rocks vs. clubs:** Rocks deal more damage; clubs only when rocks unavailable.
3. **Multiple ropes:** Deploy against multiple high-threat targets rather than focusing all on one.

## Retreat Conditions

- Retreats when **seriously wounded (≤40 HP)**.
- **Rocky terrain:** *Meld into stone* (at will) — no pursuit possible.
- **No rock:** Burrows through earth at 60 ft/round using Dash.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Korreds know many languages and can be talked out of a fight. Their tactics are simple (Command Hair → Multiattack → repeat) but target selection is shrewd. If they've decided to fight, they're fighting to kill.
