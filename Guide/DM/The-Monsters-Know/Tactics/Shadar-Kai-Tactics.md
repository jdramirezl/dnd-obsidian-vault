# Shadar-Kai Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/shadar-kai-tactics/) | **Category**: Humanoids

---

## Overview
Elf-kin shaped by the Shadowfell. Three types: shadow dancer, gloom weaver, soul monger. Designed to synergize as a team.

## Shadow Dancer
- Shock troops for darkness; bright light hobbles them; outstanding Dex, Stealth proficiency → ambush
- **Shadow Jump**: Teleport up to 30 ft between dark/dim points
- **Spiked Chain** (3x Multiattack, 10-ft reach) — three rider options on failed DC 14 Dex save:
  1. *Grapple/Restrain* — always choose first on a hit (one target at a time)
  2. *Prone* — use only if target likely to escape grapple or against multiple foes in succession
  3. *Necrotic damage* — the big play (~20 extra damage)
- **Optimal**: Grapple (1st) → Necrotic (2nd) → Necrotic (3rd); if 1st misses/save succeeds → necrotic for rest
- Always attacks from 10 ft (grappled targets can't reach without long weapons)
- Starting hidden = ~50% more damage; eliminate darkvision opponents in initial ambush
- **Retreat**: ≤28 hp; Shadow Jump if possible, then Dash; Disengage from multiple opponents

## Gloom Weaver
- Sorcerer-warlock hybrid; exceptional Dex/Cha; shock attacker, not backline caster
- **Burden of Time**: Creatures within 10 ft have disadvantage on saves
- **Misty Escape** (1/encounter): Invisible + teleport as reaction after damage; save for getaway
- **Multiattack**: Two Shadow Spear (avg 34 dmg each!) + one spell
- **Spell priorities**: *mage armor* (always), *invisibility* 5th-level (self+4), *armor of Agathys* (25 temp hp), *blight* (within 10 ft for Burden of Time), *fear* (steal magic weapons), *hypnotic pattern*/*confusion* (3+ clustered), *eldritch blast* (3d10+12, ranged only — disadvantage at point-blank)
- Melee attacks vs. squishy backliners; ranged spells vs. front-liners in wrong position
- **Retreat**: ≤41 hp; Misty Escape head start, then Dash

## Soul Monger
- Wizard-type with devastating melee; completes the caster trifecta
- **Wave of Weariness** (recharge 4–6): Huge AoE, DC 16 Con — psychic damage + exhaustion; hits ALL creatures including allies → use before allies engage
- **Weight of Ages**: Adjacent creatures lose 20 ft movement — traps opponents
- **Soul Thirst**: Huge temp HP + advantage on attacks when creature dies nearby → finish wounded foes
- **Phantasmal Dagger**: Hefty damage + disadvantage on saves until start of next turn → softens for allies
- **Key spells**: *chain lightning* (4+ foes with save disadvantage), *finger of death* (start Soul Thirst on target ≤55 hp), *phantasmal killer* (only if team ensures failed saves)
- **Self-preservation**: Won't retreat; insists on plundering life force; last to leave if allies retreat

## Team Synergies
- Shadow dancers restrain → gloom weavers blast at disadvantage → soul monger finishes with chain lightning
- Burden of Time + Weight of Ages + Fear = target can't flee
- Phantasmal Dagger disadvantage feeds shadow dancer Spiked Chain saves
