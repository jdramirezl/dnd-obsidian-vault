# Undead Tactics: The Mummy Lord

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Undead Tactics: Mummy Lord](https://www.themonstersknow.com/undead-tactics-mummy-lord/)

**Bestiary:** [[Mummies]]

---

## Overview

Mummy lords are boss-level undead on par with adult dragons. Their compulsion is **vainglory** — they don't believe PCs can hurt them and devote energy to shutting them down and punishing insolence unless proven otherwise.

## Key Defenses

- Immune to physical damage from nonmagical weapons
- Vulnerable to **fire** (destroy its heart with fire to prevent Rejuvenation)
- Magic Resistance (advantage on all saves vs magic)
- Hefty save bonuses on Con, Int, Wis, Cha; gap is **Dexterity**
- Rejuvenation — can't truly die unless heart is destroyed

## What Gets a Mummy Lord's Attention

Any PC who does **two or more** of: magical weapon hit, direct fire damage, 60+ hp in one round → *harm* is cast on them immediately. One of those things → *contagion* (Slimy Doom) or *hold person*.

## Spell Slot Economy

- **6th level (1 slot):** Reserved for *harm*
- **5th level (2 slots):** Reserved for *contagion* and *insect plague*
- **4th level (3 slots):** One for *guardian of faith*; two flex slots (boost *hold person* or extra *dispel magic*)
- **3rd level:** Retained for *dispel magic*
- **2nd level:** *Spiritual weapon* (auto-cast), *hold person*, *silence* in reserve
- **1st level:** Least useful; save the slots

## Combat Sequence

### Round 1
- Move toward No. 1 aggressor/transgressor
- **Dreadful Glare** + **Rotting Fist** if in reach (action)
- Cast *spiritual weapon* (bonus action)

### Round 2+
- If first PC fled → turn to second; if not → Dreadful Glare on secondary target, Rotting Fist + *spiritual weapon* on primary
- Repeat unless triggered conditions occur

### Conditional Responses
- 4+ non-fighter PCs within 40 ft of each other → *insect plague*
- Significant threat proven → *harm* (single), *hold person* at 4th level (2–3 threats), *contagion* Slimy Doom (single moderate threat)
- Insignificant damage or immune condition attempted → *contagion* Blinding Sickness (contempt)
- 4+ casters within 40 ft → *silence* (if not already concentrating)
- Continuous beneficial magic → *dispel magic*
- Target out of reach but reachable next turn → *guiding bolt*
- Reduced to ≤38 hp after Whirlwind retreat → *guardian of faith* between self and PCs

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

- **Multiple melee opponents** → Blinding Dust
- **4+ opponents within 10 ft** → Blasphemous Word (stun)
- **2/3 of party injured** → Channel Negative Energy (prevent healing)
- **Reduced to ≤38 hp** → Whirlwind of Sand (retreat 40–60 ft); use ASAP if 2–3 actions left
- **Otherwise** → Attack at end of remaining PC turns

## Lair Actions (Initiative 20)

- **Priority:** Wracking pain on spellcasters (first round they can cast, then alternating rounds)
- Off-rounds: whichever other lair action applies; if neither, forgo
