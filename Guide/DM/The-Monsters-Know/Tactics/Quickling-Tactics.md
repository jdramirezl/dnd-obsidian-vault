# Quickling Tactics

Quickling tactics are covered in the combined article [Fey Tactics: Darklings, Quicklings and Redcaps](https://www.themonstersknow.com/fey-tactics-darklings-quicklings-redcaps/). See also: [[Fey-Tactics-Darklings-Quicklings-Redcaps]]

## Tactical Summary

Malicious pranksters, not killers. Won't fight unless cornered. Blurred Movement = disadvantage on all attacks against them (unless incapacitated/restrained). 120 ft speed.

## Combat Behavior

- **Default behavior:** Relentless nuisances. Disrupt PCs' lives and plans, then hightail it
- **Won't ambush** despite Stealth proficiency — pranks, not murder
- **If cornered:** Multiattack with dagger → move through any gap at 4x normal speed
- Opportunity attacks are meaningless against them (Blurred Movement = disadvantage)
- **Once free:** Dash. Nothing catches them. 120 ft base + Dash = 240 ft/round

## Retreat Conditions

- Doesn't need to retreat — simply leaves whenever it wants
- Only fights if literally cornered with no escape route

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Quicklings are **chase encounters**, not fights. Use the Chase rules (DMG pp. 252-55) and impose lots of obstacles. They exist to frustrate and harass, not to kill.
