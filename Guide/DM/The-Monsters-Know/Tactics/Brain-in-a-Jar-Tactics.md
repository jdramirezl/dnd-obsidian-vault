# Brain in a Jar Tactics

> [!summary]
> A literal brain in a jar — extraordinary INT, decent CHA, dismal DEX. It prefers conversation over combat, using *zone of truth* and *detect thoughts* to gather intel. When talk fails, it goes full aggro with Mind Blast (60-ft cone, recharge 5–6) and Chill Touch. Better as a social encounter or support creature with minions than a solo combatant.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Undead, Small
- **Extraordinary INT (19), decent CHA (15), dismal DEX (−4)**
- AC 11 (jar); HP 55; 10 ft fly (hover)
- **120 ft blindsight**; Detect Sentience 300 ft
- Speaks psionically (projects disembodied voice)
- Innate Spellcasting (Psionic), save DC 14

---

## Key Features

### Detect Sentience (Passive)
- Senses thinking beings within 300 ft — always knows someone's coming

### Mind Blast (Recharge 5–6)
- 60-ft cone; INT save or 3d8+4 psychic + stunned for 1 minute
- Optimal when hitting **6 targets** (60-ft cone area)

### Chill Touch (Cantrip)
- Ranged attack; fallback when Mind Blast is on cooldown

## Combat Sequence

### Pre-Combat (Social Phase)
1. Detects approach at 300 ft; on alert
2. At 60 ft: casts **zone of truth** where visitors must stand to converse
3. At 30 ft: casts **detect thoughts** on the twitchiest visitor
4. **Charm person** on anyone with detected hostile intent (watch out for elves — advantage on save)
5. Prefers to **ask for what it wants** — decent CHA, no combat proficiency

### When Talk Fails
1. Positions to hit **6 targets** (or all foes) in Mind Blast cone (~60° arc)
2. **Mind Blast** whenever it recharges
3. **Chill Touch** between blasts
4. Math check: if target's AC > WIS save mod + 14, try *charm/hold person* instead of Chill Touch

### Last Resort
- Seriously wounded or all foes wounded: **sleep** as defense of last resort
- Finish conscious enemies, then deal with unconscious ones
- If that fails: panic, try to parley its way out

---

## Retreat
- **Cannot flee** — 10 ft fly speed (20 ft Dashing); hopeless
- Smart enough to pick a room with an escape route (chimney, air vent, pneumatic tube)

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Better with Minions
> A lone brain is a social encounter. Give it minions (undead, cultists, constructs) and its hold/stun/sleep abilities become devastating support — minions capitalize on paralyzed/stunned targets with advantage and auto-crits.

- Elves are the brain's worst enemy: advantage on charm saves, immune to *sleep*
- *Zone of truth* doesn't require concentration — layer it with *detect thoughts*
- Using any psionic ability costs the action it could spend on Chill Touch — opportunity cost is real
- *Compulsion* + *mage hand* = march someone into a cage, then slam it shut
