# Bullywug Tactics

> [!summary]
> [[Bullywug|Bullywugs]] are petty, bad-tempered frog-folk who ambush in swamps, prefer to capture rather than kill, and flee like cowards when things go south. They attack in 3-to-1 numbers, spring from hiding with surprise, then demand surrender in a language no one understands. Near water is their escape route.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Humanoid, Medium
- **All physical abilities modestly above average** — no combat preference from stats
- Proficient: Stealth; **Swamp Camouflage** (advantage on Stealth in swamps)
- **Standing Leap:** 20 ft long jump (full speed) — ignores difficult swamp terrain
- **40 ft swim speed** — primary escape method
- Speaks Bullywug only
- Can communicate with frogs/toads

---

## Key Features

### Swamp Camouflage
- Advantage on DEX (Stealth) in swampy terrain
- Won't venture outside swamps — too much advantage to give up

### Standing Leap
- 20 ft long jump without running start (normal rules: only 6 ft)
- Covers difficult marshy terrain without halving movement
- Flavor: bouncing around like ornery superballs during combat
- Still provokes opportunity attacks when leaping out of reach

---

## Combat Sequence

### Setup
- Ambush near **solid ground adjacent to water ≥3 ft deep** (swim escape route)
- Attack in **3-to-1 numbers** to surround and overwhelm
- Hide 15–20 ft from expected path of approach

### Round 1 — Surprise
1. Spring from hiding → land next to PCs
2. **Surprise round:** spear + bite attacks
3. Damage serves as incentive to surrender

### Round 2–3 — Demand Surrender
1. Keep attacking while demanding surrender — **in Bullywug** (PCs don't understand)
2. Round 3: same demands, louder and slower
3. If a PC drops their weapon: nearest bullywug **grapples** them, drags them away, rifles pockets

### Giant Frogs
- Tame giant frogs to **swallow victims whole** — only works on Small/Tiny targets
- Not reliable against typical adventuring parties

---

## Retreat
- **Moderately wounded (≤7 hp):** leaps away, hides, repositions; looks for chance to rejoin fight alongside less-wounded allies
- **Seriously wounded (≤4 hp):** flees — Dash action, leaps into nearest deep water, swims away
- No deep water nearby: leaps twice (20 ft each with Dash)
- **Numbers reduced below 1.5× PCs:** all flee, regroup with other bullywugs, stalk, and re-ambush later

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Swamp Superballs
> Have bullywugs bouncing around in 3D during combat — leaping onto logs, off trees, across pools. It's more fun than 2D trudging and reinforces their home-turf advantage.

- Encounters always in swamps — they never leave their terrain advantage
- Ideal ambush: solid ground for fighting, deep water within one leap for escape
- Frogs can mask ambush sounds or serve as long-range communication
- Bullywugs hiding underwater is even harder to spot — players rarely check
- They want captives for their rulers, not corpses — capture-first mentality
