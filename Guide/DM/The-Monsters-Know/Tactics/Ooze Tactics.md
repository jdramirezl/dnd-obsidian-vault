# Ooze Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Ooze Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/ooze-tactics/)
> **Bestiary**: [[Oozes]]

Barely intelligent subterranean scavengers. Common traits: negligible Int/Cha, low Dex/Wis, high Con, acidic pseudopod, 60 ft blindsight. All but gelatinous cube are Amorphous and can climb walls.

## Gray Ooze

False Appearance blends with wet rock (+2 Stealth). Lies in wait, follows prey on wet rock only. Pseudopod = squeezing/constricting (not punching). Corrodes metal on contact.

- Keeps attacking until prey is completely dead (0 hp → engulfed → continues digesting)
- **Retreat ≤8 hp**: Lets go, Dashes away (20 ft), slithers into cracks

## Ochre Jelly

Like gray ooze but: Spider Climb (prefers ceilings, drops on prey) and Split.

| Size | HP | Retreat Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Large | 45 | ≤18 hp (non-slash/lightning) |
| Medium | 22 | ≤8 hp |
| Small | 10 | ≤4 hp |

Small jellies can't Split. Retreating jellies go up the nearest wall to the ceiling.

## Black Pudding

Corrodes weapons/armor AND hangs from ceilings AND Splits. Spreads across ceiling, reaches down with pseudopod. Cutting: one half falls, one stays on ceiling.

| Size | Retreat Threshold |
|---|---|
| Large | ≤34 hp (non-slash/lightning) |
| Medium | ≤16 hp |
| Small | ≤8 hp |

## Gelatinous Cube

Dungeon Roomba. Always moving, no stealth. Should be an enormous blob, not a cube.

1. Normally 5 ft/round (thorough scavenging)
2. Senses creatures → full speed to 25 ft
3. Waits motionless 1 round (Transparency)
4. Prey in reach → surprise attack
5. Prey at 25 ft → full movement + Engulf (another 15 ft)
6. Prey retreats → follows, Dashing if needed

Doesn't leave after absorbing a victim — keeps Engulfing everyone in path.
**Retreat ≤33 hp**: Dashes away. (Woe to PCs who popped out the back.)
