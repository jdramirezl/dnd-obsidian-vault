# Alhoon Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/alhoon-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- Undead mind flayer spellcaster (CR 10) immune to nonmagical physical damage, with Magic Resistance and a deep spell list
- Ambush-oriented: 120 ft truesight + Stealth proficiency; it knows you're coming and hides first
- Mind Blast is the signature opener—stuns on failed Int save, doesn't reveal hiding position
- Layered defenses: *mirror image*, *wall of force*, *shield*, *counterspell*, *globe of invulnerability*
- Vulnerable to magic weapons (especially fire/thunder/acid/radiant) and Dex-save spells like *fireball*

## Combat Behavior
- **Opening**: From hiding, Mind Blast if 6+ opponents (or all if fewer) are in the 60-ft cone. Doesn't break stealth. Follow up with advantage attacks next round
- **Round 2**: *Mirror image* for magic-weapon defense, then *wall of force* to bubble the most dangerous magic-weapon wielder
- **Mid-combat**: *Evard's black tentacles* on 4+ grouped enemies (replaces *wall of force* concentration), then boosted *scorching ray*. Always reserve one 3rd and one 4th-level slot for *counterspell*
- **Reactions**: *Shield* vs. magic weapon attacks rolling 15–19 (skip if spell threat is higher—save for *counterspell*). *Counterspell* after failing one save against magic
- **Cantrips**: *Shocking grasp* in melee vs. mundane-weapon foes. *Chill touch* at 120 ft as a finishing move vs. fleeing enemies

## Key Decision Points
- **6th-level slot**: Wait until round 3. Winning → *disintegrate* a key foe. Losing → *globe of invulnerability*
- **Mind Blast recharge**: Use every time it recharges AND positioning criterion is met
- **Spell slot conservation**: Never spend the last 3rd/4th-level slot on anything except *counterspell*

## Retreat Conditions
- Withdraws at 49–72 hp: Dodge (1 melee foe), Disengage (2+), Dash (alone)
- *Plane shifts* out at ≤48 hp
- Will flee before combat if telepathic assessment reveals it's outmatched

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Open from stealth with Mind Blast to stun half the party, then lock down the rest with *wall of force* and *Evard's black tentacles* before they can respond—the alhoon's power is in layered action-economy denial.
