# Vampiric Mind Flayer Tactics

## Overview

Hybrid of vampire spawn and mind flayer — created from vampirism-infected mind flayer larvae. Approximately as dangerous as a vampire spawn, serving an elder brain. INT craters to near-orangutan level; can only *feel* telepathically, not communicate.

## Key Stats

- AC 15, climbing speed = walking speed + Spider Climb
- Proficient saves: DEX, INT, WIS, CHA
- Resistant to necrotic and psychic damage (**no** physical damage resistance)
- Immune to charmed, exhaustion, frightened
- 120 ft darkvision, Perception + Stealth proficiency
- Sunlight Sensitivity → strictly nocturnal or subterranean

## Multiattack

- **Default**: 1 Claw + 1 Tentacles (Tentacles imposes grapple → required for Drink Sapience)
- **2 Claws**: Only when already grappling AND harassed by another melee opponent
- **Never** voluntarily releases a grappled victim until it reaches 0 hp

## Drink Sapience

Separate action (not Multiattack). Only targets grappled creatures. Functions like vampire Bite's hp transfer, themed as brain-drain.

## Disrupt Psyche (Bonus Action, Recharge 5–6)

15-ft cone, INT save DC 15. Incapacitates on failure. Neither attack nor spell → **does not reveal position** when hidden.

### Best Opening Play (Ambush)

1. Hide on ceiling/rafters via Spider Climb
2. Wait for 6+ creatures (or entire group) within 30 ft
3. **Disrupt Psyche** (bonus action) — doesn't break hiding
4. **Tentacles** with advantage (unseen attacker) → grapple
5. **Claw** follow-up — all on the same turn

### Mid-Combat

Fire Disrupt Psyche whenever available to keep enemies off while drinking sapience.

## Retreat Behavior

**None.** Compulsion to *serve*, not feed. Elder brain considers them expendable and doesn't name them. Fight to destruction. Only **sunlight** forces retreat.

## Encounter Design

Always Deadly — if it wouldn't be, the VMF wouldn't engage. High WIS gives good instinct for when not to pick a fight.
