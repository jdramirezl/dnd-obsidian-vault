# Succubus/Incubus Tactics

> **Source:** *The Monsters Know What They're Doing* — Keith Ammann
> **Bestiary:** Monster Manual
> **Creatures:** Succubus, Incubus (CR 4)

---

## Tactical Summary

The succubus/incubus is not a combat monster — it's a long-game social predator. Its entire toolkit is built around a multi-session subplot of corruption: spy from the Ethereal Plane, charm a sleeping target, seduce them into moral compromise over days or weeks, then kill with Draining Kiss once the soul is sufficiently tainted. Direct combat is a last resort and a sign that something went wrong.

## Combat Behavior

### The Corruption Campaign (Feature Sequence)

1. **Etherealness** — Spy on the target from the Ethereal Plane. Reconnaissance and undetectable travel
2. **Deception / Insight / Persuasion** — Identify the target's fears and desires (infer from PC's Ideal, Bond, Flaw). Build a "marketing plan"
3. **Telepathy (60 ft)** — Communicate with any creature nearby
4. **Charm (DC 15 WIS, 24 hours)** — Charm a sleeping target at night. RAW: targets don't need to be conscious to be charmed. If the save fails, proceed. If it succeeds, Etherealness away and try again the next night
5. **Telepathic Bond** — Unlimited-range communication with charmed targets, even across planes
6. **Shapechanger** — Assume an alluring humanoid form. Approach the target in the flesh during the day
7. **Stealth** — Approach without being detected by others
8. **Draining Kiss** — The kill shot. Drains HP from a charmed or willing target. Kills if HP reaches 0 from this alone

### Target Selection

- Favors **low-Wisdom** targets (WIS save modifier ≤ 0)
- May intentionally target high-Wisdom PCs for the challenge — "the more virtuous the prey, the more rewarding the downfall"
- Must re-charm every 24 hours, requiring repeated nightly visits

### The Kill Sequence

- Ideally, the target trusts the fiend enough to accept the kiss **willingly** (no Charm needed)
- Use Draining Kiss first → then re-Charm → then Draining Kiss again
- Willing kiss avoids triggering the extra WIS save that harmful actions grant
- If the re-Charm fails or the second kiss doesn't finish the job, the mission has failed

### Daytime Operations

- Telepathic suggestions to compel sinful behavior (pride, envy, gluttony, sloth, lust, avarice, wrath)
- Shapechanged visits in person — discourages the PC from telling others ("keep it our little secret")
- May lead a public life as a prominent figure so the relationship seems normal

### If Discovered / Forced to Fight

- **Escape priority:** Etherealness is the primary escape — mostly foolproof
- If Etherealness is blocked: revert to fiend form (1 action) → fly away (60 ft fly speed in fiend form)
- Can move 30 ft in humanoid form → Shapechange to fiend → fly 30 ft more in the same turn
- Uses Claw attack **only** if inescapably cornered
- Dex 17, Str 8 — built for subterfuge, not direct aggression
- Resistant to nonmagical physical damage but vulnerable to acid, force, necrotic, radiant, thunder

## Key Decision Points

| Situation | Decision |
|---|---|
| Choosing a target | Low-WIS PCs preferred; read Ideal/Bond/Flaw for leverage |
| Charm fails at night | Etherealness away, try again tomorrow |
| Charmed PC's allies get suspicious | Discourage PC from revealing the relationship; operate in secret |
| Ready for the kill | Draining Kiss (willing) → re-Charm → Draining Kiss (charmed) |
| Kill combo fails | Mission is over — one chance only |
| Discovered mid-scheme | Etherealness escape immediately |
| Etherealness blocked | Shapechange to fiend form → fly away |
| Cornered with no escape | Claw attacks as last resort |

## Retreat Conditions

- **Always flees if possible.** The succubus/incubus values its own life above all else — no territory, no family, nothing worth dying for
- Etherealness is the preferred exit (action)
- Flying in fiend form is the backup
- Claw attacks only when inescapably cornered — this creature does not want to fight

> [!tip] DM Tip
> This is a subplot monster, not an encounter monster. Run it across multiple sessions: nightly Charm attempts, daytime seduction in disguise, escalating moral compromises. Use the PC's own Ideal/Bond/Flaw against them. The best succubus encounter is one where the player doesn't realize what's happening until the Draining Kiss. If other PCs catch on, the fiend vanishes to the Ethereal Plane and starts over with a new approach — or a new target.
