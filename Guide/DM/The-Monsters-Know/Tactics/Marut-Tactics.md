# Marut Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/marut-tactics/) | **Date**: November 15, 2018

---

## Overview

- Interplanar marshals enforcing mystically signed contracts. Lawful neutral — indifferent by default. Only care about the **letter** of a contract, not the spirit.
- 400+ hp, AC 22, extraordinary STR/CON, exceptional INT/CHA, high WIS. Expertise in Insight, Intimidation, Perception. Brilliant brutes.
- Immune to poison; resistant to thunder and nonmagical physical damage. Can't be charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, or knocked unconscious. Use Legendary Resistance vs prone, blind, restrain, stun.

## Unerring Slam

- **Automatic hit** — no attack roll. 60 force damage, pushes Huge or smaller target one square. Gets two per Multiattack.
- Against interlopers: positions to push them out of its path. Against punishment targets: doesn't care about push direction.

## Blazing Edict

- 60-ft cube AoE. Only cares if **designated targets** are in the area — not aiming at bystanders, not avoiding them either.
- Opens combat with **Intimidation** (social action): warns quarry to halt, everyone else to get behind it. Won't warn twice.
- Next turn: positions targets in front, bystanders behind → Blazing Edict. Failed save → **stunned** (incapacitation → auto-fail vs Justify).

## Justify

- Teleports up to two creatures + itself to interplanar courthouse. Marut **must go with them** if either fails.
- **Beatdown**: Multiattack stunned targets with advantage, Blazing Edict again when they recover.
- **Hearing required**: Blazing Edict → Justify while stunned.
- **Preemptive surrender**: skip Blazing Edict, straight to Justify.

## Special Considerations

- Multiple maruts for >2 summonees (one per two + one for remainder). Don't Blazing Edict each other.
- **Clerics, paladins, warlocks** (proficient WIS + CHA saves): repeated Blazing Edicts until they fail, no Justify until stunned. Each gets a **personal marut**.
- Same for any target with +5 on one save and >+5 on the other.

## Retreat

- **Never flees**. Departs via *plane shift* after beating targets unconscious, or leaves with targets via Justify.
- If a marut fails, the Hall of Concordance sends **two more**.
