# Colossus of Akros Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/colossus-of-akros-tactics/) | **Date**: December 8, 2025

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Massive metal golem** defending the polis of Akros (Theros's Sparta). Gargantuan construct with golden retriever intelligence (Int 3).
- **Mega-brute**: Extraordinary Str and Con, 60-ft speed, 15-ft reach. Mundane weapons can't hurt it.
- **Fire heals it**: Fire damage causes it to magically re-form where battered.
- **Siege Monster**: Attacks cover/structures with its sword when enemies hide.
- **Fights until it crumbles**: Retreat is preposterous.

## Combat Behavior

**Flames of Akros (recharge)**: Targets three creatures within 90 ft, wherever they stand (no defined shape). Failed saves cause targets to catch fire and keep burning. Uses whenever available with 3+ visible targets.

**Multiattack**: Two attacks/turn. Sword preferred (more damage). Spear thrown only when no target in melee reach — returns magically but only one ranged attack/turn.

**Target selection**: Focuses on whichever foe is biggest. Divides Sword swings between multiple melee attackers. Attacks the slowest runner when opponents flee. Takes at least 35 damage at once to even notice a hit from a smaller enemy.

**Crumbling Destruction**: When destroyed, debris falls — heroes must dodge. Doesn't affect tactics.

## Key Decision Points

- **Flames available + 3 visible targets?** → Flames of Akros.
- **No target in reach?** → Throw spear.
- **Enemies behind cover?** → Sword the cover (Siege Monster).
- **Fire damage?** → Heals the Colossus.

## Retreat Conditions

- **None**: Fights until destroyed.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> A cinematic set-piece, not a tactical puzzle. Hits the biggest thing near it and uses Flames whenever possible. For drama, let Flames incinerate up to a dozen CR ≤1 NPCs as a single target. The real challenge: mundane weapons bounce off and fire heals it.
