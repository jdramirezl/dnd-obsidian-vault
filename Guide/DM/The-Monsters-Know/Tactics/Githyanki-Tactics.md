# Githyanki Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/githyanki-tactics/) | **Date**: October 13, 2016
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Gith]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- Planar raiders with high Strength, Dexterity, and psionic mobility. Move fast, strike hard, be somewhere else.
- Misty step (bonus action) is the key ingredient — enables hit-and-run no other melee creature can match.
- Warriors are shock troops; knights are elite commanders with telekinesis and plane shift.
- Intelligent and disciplined — Disengage when retreating and cover each other's withdrawal.

## Combat Behavior

### Githyanki Warrior
**Misty step combinations** (the core tactic):
- Move toward opponent → Multiattack (action) → *misty step* (bonus) past the opponent.
- *Misty step* (bonus) toward opponent → Multiattack (action) to finish off → move.
- Multiattack (action) → *misty step* (bonus) → move out of sight.

**Jump**: Costs a full action. Only useful when not attacking — chasing, repositioning, or fleeing. Still constrained by movement speed. Limited tactical value in practice.

**Retreat**: Disengage when withdrawing. Least wounded warrior covers others and fights to the death. Others retreat at ≤19 hp.

### Githyanki Knight
- Higher Con than Dex (brute profile). Engages the most formidable melee opponents.
- Uses *misty step* only to intervene if an ally struggles or to close with a new target.
- **Telekinesis** (1/day): Push/pull a PC 30 ft, hold suspended, shove off ledge, drag fleeing enemy back. One use — choose conservatively.
- **Plane shift** (1/day): Permanently banish a PC (Cha save) or escape with allies.
- **Tongues**: The knight parleys. Lawful evil — "parley" means dictating terms.
- Stays as rearguard for warriors, then plane shifts away. Won't retreat while warriors are present.
- Plane shifts away at ≤36 hp unless duty-bound to guard a location.

## Key Decision Points

- Warrior: misty step available? → Use every turn for hit-and-run.
- Knight: problematic enemy elsewhere? → Misty step to intervene.
- Knight: pesky skirmisher? → Telekinesis to hoist or shove. Target low-Str PCs.
- Knight: high-value target with low Charisma? → Plane shift them permanently.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Warriors**: Disengage at ≤19 hp. Least wounded covers retreat to the death.
- **Knights**: Don't retreat while warriors present. Plane shift away at ≤36 hp.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Warriors should never just stand and swing. Every turn involves misty step — appear, strike, vanish. Knights are the real threat: telekinesis to yank the wizard into melee, plane shift to permanently remove a PC. Use these powers conservatively but decisively.
