# Shield Guardian Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/construct-tactics-scarecrows-helmed-horrors-shield-guardians/) | **Related**: [[Shield-Guardian]]

---

## Tactical Summary
- Construct bodyguard; pure brute ability contour; attacks only with fists
- Not really a creature — a device; does only what commanded; tactics are extension of controller's tactics
- Bound to an amulet; whoever holds the amulet controls the guardian

## Combat Behavior
- **Shield feature**: Increases controller's AC by 2 (like Protection fighting style but AC bonus instead of disadvantage)
- **Primary role**: Toe-to-toe melee-bot; optimized for sustained close combat
- **Spell Storing**: Discharges stored spell when commanded OR when predefined conditions met — functions as dead man's switch (activates when controller loses consciousness or afterward)
- **Not resistant** to weapon damage, but regenerates regardless of damage type
- **Controller determines behavior**: Malevolent → demolish enemies; Defensive → attack only direct threats

## Key Interactions
- Killing controller does NOT destroy amulet — character who takes amulet takes control
- Killing guardian presumably destroys amulet
- Uncommanded guardian after controller death: DM choice — stop in tracks or go haywire interpreting last command (evergreen encounter hook)

## Retreat Conditions
- None — fights until destroyed; no self-preservation instinct
