# Mind Flayer Lich / Alhoon Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/alhoon-tactics/)
> See also: [[Alhoon-Tactics]]

---

## Tactical Summary
- Undead mind flayer spellcaster (CR 10) that lengthens its lifespan through humanoid sacrifice
- Immune to nonmagical physical damage, resistant to cold/lightning/necrotic, immune to poison. Magic Resistance grants advantage on saves vs. spells
- Ambush predator: 120 ft truesight + Stealth proficiency. Hides first, always
- Mind Blast stuns on failed Int save and doesn't reveal hiding position (neither attack nor spell)
- Chief vulnerability: magic weapons (especially fire, thunder, acid, radiant) and Dex-save spells like *fireball*

## Combat Behavior
- **Opening**: From hiding, Mind Blast if 6+ opponents (or all) in the 60-ft cone. Follow up with advantage attacks
- **Defenses**: *Mirror image* first, then *wall of force* to bubble the most dangerous magic-weapon wielder
- **Mid-combat**: *Evard's black tentacles* on 4+ grouped enemies, then boosted *scorching ray*. Always reserve one 3rd- and one 4th-level slot for *counterspell*
- **Reactions**: *Shield* vs. magic weapon attacks rolling 15–19. *Counterspell* after failing one save against magic
- **Cantrips**: *Shocking grasp* in melee vs. mundane-weapon foes. *Chill touch* at 120 ft vs. fleeing enemies

## Key Decision Points
- **6th-level slot**: Wait until round 3. Winning → *disintegrate*. Losing → *globe of invulnerability*
- **Mind Blast recharge**: Use every time it recharges AND positioning criterion is met
- **Spell slot conservation**: Never spend the last 3rd/4th-level slot on anything except *counterspell*

## Retreat Conditions
- Withdraws at 49–72 hp: Dodge (1 melee foe), Disengage (2+), Dash (alone)
- *Plane shifts* out at ≤48 hp

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Open from stealth with Mind Blast to stun half the party, then lock down the rest with *wall of force* and *Evard's black tentacles*—the alhoon's power is in layered action-economy denial.
