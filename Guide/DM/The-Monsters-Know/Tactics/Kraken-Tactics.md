# Kraken Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Kraken Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/kraken-tactics/)
> **Bestiary:** [[Kraken]]

---

## Tactical Summary

The kraken is a natural disaster with godlike Strength, Constitution, *and* mental abilities (Int 22). It knows everyone's weaknesses and fights with calculated malice. Immune to lightning, frightened, paralyzed, and nonmagical physical damage. AC 18. Proficient in every save except Charisma (*banishment* is its Achilles' heel).

## Combat Behavior

### Attack Actions (one per turn)
- **Tentacle ×3 (Multiattack):** 30 ft reach, auto-grapples (restrained). Can mix with Fling.
- **Fling:** Aimed (Int 22). Throws at other enemies (bludgeoning + prone) or solid surfaces. Holds targets with Athletics/Acrobatics ≤+3; Flings those ≥+4.
- **Bite:** Swallows grappled target — blinded, no movement, acid damage/round. NOT against targets dealing 50+ damage/turn from inside.
- **Lightning Storm:** Up to 3 targets at 120 ft. Huge Dex DC, damage on success. Nuclear option for fragile high-damage enemies.

### Threat Assessment
1. **Ships first:** Lightning Storm (auto-hits objects, Siege Monster doubles damage). Tentacles for Small/Large boats.
2. **Undamaged (≥331 HP) + Deadly encounter:** Targets **weakest/most vulnerable** first — the PC whose loss demoralizes most.
3. **Moderately wounded (≤330 HP):** Shifts to **most dangerous** opponents. Grapple, squeeze, swallow, or Fling based on escape probability.
4. **All threats grappled:** Grabs remaining opponents or closes distance. Lightning Storm for unreachable targets.

### Legendary Actions
- **Tentacle/Fling (1):** Retaliatory vs melee attackers; Fling slippery targets before their turn.
- **Lightning Storm (2):** When no tentacle/Fling needed and 2+ actions remain.
- **Ink Cloud (3, underwater):** At ≤330 HP when all enemies within 60 ft; again at ≤188 HP before retreating.

### Lair Actions (initiative 20, underwater)
- **Default (#3):** Water does lightning damage within 120 ft.
- **#2:** Doubles lightning damage within 60 ft — use with Lightning Storm at 30–60 ft targets.
- **#1:** Pushes creatures away — only when seriously wounded or protecting grappled target from rescuers.
- No restriction on repeating same lair action consecutively.

## Key Decision Points

1. **Ship vs. swimmers:** Always destroy the ship first.
2. **Grapple vs. Fling vs. Swallow:** Hold (≤+3 escape), Fling (≥+4 escape), Swallow (troublesome, <50 dmg/turn).
3. **Lightning Storm vs. Tentacles:** Tentacles within 30 ft (grapple advantage); Lightning Storm for distant/fragile targets.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Outside lair:** Flees to lair at **≤188 HP**, forcing enemies to fight on its turf.
- **Inside lair:** Desperately doesn't want to leave. Ink Cloud + Lightning Storm + lair actions as last-ditch defense. Disengages or Dashes away if outmatched.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> The kraken's only weak save is Charisma — *banishment* and *divine word* are its biggest fears. Its Int 22 means it knows everything you know about the PCs. Outside its lair it plays offense; inside, defense from turn one.
