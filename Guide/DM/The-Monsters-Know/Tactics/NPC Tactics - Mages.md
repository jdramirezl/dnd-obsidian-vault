# NPC Tactics: Mages

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — NPC Tactics: Mages](https://www.themonstersknow.com/npc-tactics-mages/)

The **mage** NPC is basically a level 9 wizard. Low Str, above-average Dex — ranged attacker. Very high Int means it knows which enemies to target. Above-average Wis means it prioritizes self-preservation — escape protocols at ≤28 hp.

No reason to use weapons — *fire bolt* (avg 11) crushes the dagger (avg 4.5).

## Action Economy

- Only bonus-action spell: *misty step* (pair with *fire bolt*)
- *Shield* or *counterspell* as reaction — only one reaction per round
- **Must finish fights fast**: 2 rounds, maybe 3, then bail

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Always have escape** — position near exits, *misty step* to maneuver, windows for *fly*
2. **Avoid damage** — stay 30+ ft away, *shield*/*counterspell*, pre-cast *mage armor*
3. **Max damage per spell** — AoE before single-target, debuffs before pure damage

## Spell Priority

| Spell | Slot | Total Expected Damage |
|---|---|---|
| *Cone of cold* | 5th | 162 hp (27 × 6 targets) |
| *Fireball* | 4th | 95 hp (23 × 4) |
| *Fireball* | 3rd | 84 hp (21 × 4) |
| *Ice storm* | 4th | 69 hp (17 × 4) + difficult terrain |
| *Magic missile* | Any | 10–21 hp (never worth 3rd+ slot) |

## Slot Management

- **5th**: Exclusively *cone of cold*
- **4th**: One reserved for *greater invisibility*; other for boosted *fireball* or *ice storm*
- **3rd**: One for *counterspell*, possibly one for *fly*; rest for *fireball*

## Round-by-Round

**R1**: Dangerous PCs → *greater invisibility*. Nuisance PCs → *cone of cold*.
**R2**: If invisible → *cone of cold*. Enemy in melee → *misty step* + *fire bolt*. Otherwise → *fireball* (4th).
**R3**: Visible + wounded → *greater invisibility* and flee. Otherwise → *ice storm* or *fireball* (3rd).
**R4**: Easy pickings → *magic missile*. Clustered → *fireball*. Otherwise → escape.
**R5**: Get out of there.
