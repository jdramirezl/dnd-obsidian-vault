# Lycanthrope Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/lycanthrope-tactics/) | **Date**: October 19, 2016
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Lycanthropes]]

---

## General Traits

- All werebeasts have Perception proficiency, immunity to physical damage from nonmagical/nonsilvered weapons, and human/beast/hybrid forms.
- Hybrid form preferred for combat (allows Multiattack), except werebears who have Multiattack in all forms.
- Shapechanger action generally not worth using mid-combat — high opportunity cost, no benefit, contradicts involuntary transformation lore.

## Werewolves

- Strictly melee in hybrid form. Stealth proficient — strongly prefer attacking from hiding with surprise.
- Keen Hearing and Smell gives advantage on Perception, but **no darkvision** — disadvantage attacking in darkness.
- Immunity to normal weapons makes them unafraid of opportunity attacks. Attack weakest first (commoners over adventurers).
- If struck by ranged/spell attack → Dash out of sight, then Hide and resume stalking.
- If a PC's melee attack misses or does ≤5 hp → counterattack with claw/bite.
- Struck by magical or silvered weapon → **flee** (Dash action).

## Wererats

- Urban creatures. Lower STR than DEX/CON → skirmish fighters in gangs. Attack victims they outnumber ≥2:1.
- Snipe with hand crossbows from hiding, behind cover, multiple directions. Wear down targets, close in when all victims at ≤40% max HP.
- If engaged in melee → fight back unless struck by magical/silvered weapon or spell → Dash into shadows.
- On pursuit: scatter, find hiding spots, Ready Attack actions vs passing PCs (prioritize magic/silver wielders). Converge on smallest group if PCs split up.

## Wereboars

- Country gangs. Brute attackers, no stealth. Initiate with **Charge** → tusk/maul Multiattack (tusk first for prone → advantage on maul).
- Opponent retreats → press, Charge if ≥15 ft away. Knock unconscious → Charge next.
- **Relentless** keeps them going at 0 hp. Retreat at ≤54 hp from magical/silvered weapons or ≤31 hp from spells.
- Retreat style: parting Multiattack, back off at full speed, ignoring opportunity attacks.

## Weretigers

- Solitary (unless female lair group). Remote areas, generally don't hunt humanoids — rogues are the exception.
- Darkvision + Stealth → nocturnal ambush. **Pounce**: movement + two claws + bonus bite.
- Won't attack groups of 3+ unless weakened. Won't touch 6+. Withdraw at ≤84 hp → Dash, then Hide.
- Non-rogue: stay human, hide. Shapechange to tiger (40 ft speed) to flee. Fight to death only defending lair.

## Werebears

- Good, solitary, want to be left alone. Two greataxe (humanoid) or two claw (hybrid/beast) attacks per turn. 135 hp + damage immunity.
- Generally **won't bite** (avoid transmitting lycanthropy; bite not in Multiattack).
- Flee at ≤94 hp in the wild. **Fight to the death** defending lair.
