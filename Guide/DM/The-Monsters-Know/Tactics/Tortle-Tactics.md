## General Notes (Inferred from Stat Block)

Tortles are peaceful, nomadic humanoids with natural armor (AC 17 from shell).

- **Shell Defense** — withdraw into shell as action; +4 AC, advantage on Str/Con saves, speed 0, disadvantage on Dex saves, can't take reactions; end as bonus action
- **Hold Breath** — up to 1 hour
- **Claws** — natural melee weapon
- Generally **lawful good** and non-aggressive
- Prefer to **withdraw into shells** rather than fight
- If forced to fight: straightforward melee combatants with high natural AC
- Low Dexterity, decent Strength and Wisdom — brute profile but not aggressive by nature
