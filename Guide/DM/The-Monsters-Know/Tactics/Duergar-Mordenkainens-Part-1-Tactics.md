# Duergar Tactics: Mordenkainen's Duergar, Part 1

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/duergar-tactics-mordenkainens-duergar-part-1/) | **Date**: August 8, 2019
> **Book**: Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes

---

## Core Duergar Enlarge/Invisibility Decision

- **Enlarge break-even**: Round 3. Over 1–2 rounds, not worth the lost action. Over 4+, clear advantage. Use when fight will be prolonged.
- **Invisibility**: Only for ambush or flight — once-per-combat, broken by attacking/casting/Enlarging.
- Not all Mordenkainen's duergar have both features. Four of seven variants do; one has Invisibility on 4–6 recharge.

## Duergar Soulblade

- **Shock trooper**. High Dex offense/defense. Soulblade deals bonus 1d6 force on attacks with advantage.
- **Gaining advantage is central**: Two sources — Invisibility and *true strike*. Each costs a full action.
- **Invisibility vs *hunter's mark***: Both require concentration — either-or. Invisibility wins: grants advantage AND the extra 1d6 from Soulblade.
- ***Jump*-powered assault** (alongside allies initiating combat):
  - Round 1: Cast *jump* from 40 ft away. Round 2: Leap + *true strike* mid-air + Create Soulblade. Round 3: Land, attack with advantage + bonus 1d6. Round 4: Invisibility → attack with unseen-attacker advantage.
- **In total darkness** vs foes without darkvision: advantage is automatic — skip setup, join fight immediately.
- **Only 18 hp**. Retreat at ≤7 hp. Indiscriminate targets.

## Duergar Stone Guard

- **Tougher standard duergar** with Phalanx Formation (advantage on attacks adjacent to shield-wielding duergar ally; advantage on Dex saves).
- **Deployment**: Block for javeliniers/xarrorn, or hold strategic locations.
- Enlarge/Invisibility same as standard duergar.
- **Retreat**: When ≥half the line at ≤15 hp, Disengage and fall back in formation.

## Duergar Xarrorn

- **Brute** with Fire Lance (long reach) and Fire Spray (5–6 recharge AoE).
- **Fire Spray**: Always vs ≥2 foes. Repositions if needed. Preferred when available and not Enlarged.
- **Enlarged + advantage vs AC ≤14**: Fire Lance deals more. Favors lance against lightly armored targets.
- **Retreat at ≤10 hp**: Invisible and flee.

## Duergar Kavalrachni

- **Spider cavalry** on female steeders. Cavalry Training: successful attack → steeder follow-up.
- **No Enlarge** — double War Pick Multiattack compensates.
- **Shared Invisibility**: Steeder can attack without breaking rider's invisibility. Allows invisible approach → steeder attacks with advantage → rider attacks with advantage. Only works with independent mount.
- **Cavalry role**: Smash through front line, run down backline targets.
- Mount Disengages through enemy zones of control (protects both). Steeder's Extraordinary Leap is superior to *jump* (no two-turn delay).
- **Retreat**: Rider ≤10 hp OR steeder ≤12 hp. Independent steeders may bolt at ≤21 hp.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Variants
> Soulblades leap onto the backline while stone guard hold the front. Kavalrachni on independent steeders are the most mobile option. In total darkness vs foes without darkvision, all duergar gain automatic advantage.
