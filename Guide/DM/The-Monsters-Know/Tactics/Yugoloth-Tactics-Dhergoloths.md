# Yugoloth Tactics: Dhergoloths

## Tactical Summary

Riot cops of Gehenna — unique knack for mowing down mobs. Creatures of instinct, no independent judgment or flexibility. Exceptional Str and Con, everything else unexceptional — pure brutes. Resistant to nonmagical physical, cold, fire, lightning; immune to acid and poison. 60 ft. darkvision AND 60 ft. blindsight (blindsight is the key).

## Darkness Strategy

- *Darkness* (at will, concentration): cast **before combat begins** to exploit targets' confusion — unnatural darkness descends, targets have no idea what's coming
- Blindsight lets dhergoloth "see" through its own *darkness* → advantage on attacks, disadvantage on enemies' attacks
- If *darkness* is knocked down (e.g., *daylight* spell, broken concentration) → **next action is to recast it**
- *Darkness* can't be moved once cast (dhergoloth not smart enough to cast on an object)
- More helpful in **confined spaces** than large open ones

## Fear vs. Darkness

Both require concentration — can't use simultaneously. *Darkness* is **nearly always better**. Dhergoloth casts *fear* only if ordered to (and not always then). DC 10 save makes *fear* nearly useless anyway.

## Sleep

3/day, unboosted (avg. 22 hp affected). Useless against PCs of appropriate level. Only good against commoner bystanders.

## Offensive Actions

### Flailing Claws (Recharge)
- Moves up to walking speed in a straight line + deals damage to all within 5 ft. of path
- Essentially a **Dash that also damages** (can't change direction)
- DC 14 Dex save; **no damage on success** — roughly 2/3 of mid-level PCs will save
- Use only when it can plow through **3+ opponents** along its path (reasonable chance of hitting at least one)
- Repositioning beforehand is fine; doesn't worry about OAs unless hit by magic weapon
- If *haste* is applied: speed doubles → Flailing Claws range doubles

### Claw/Claw Multiattack (Default)
- Used when Flailing Claws is on cooldown
- *Darkness* grants advantage on these attacks

## Pain Response (≥ 12 damage after reductions)

- If Flailing Claws available → use it immediately (retaliates against the attacker who hurt it; collateral damage is bonus, not requirement)
- If Flailing Claws unavailable → **Teleport** to another location (ideally within *darkness* sphere; blindsight counts as "seeing" for Teleport)
- If engaged in melee by 2+ foes dealing non-immune/non-resistant damage → Teleport away, preferably next to a weaker foe

## Pursuit

Aggressively chases fleeing foes. If movement alone won't keep up → **Teleport into foe's path** within 5 ft. reach (provokes OA if they turn and run back).

## Retreat

Self-preservation kicks in when seriously wounded (≤ 47 hp), regardless of plane. Teleports 60 ft. away (maximum distance) + uses remaining movement to keep going. Not smart enough to consider planar implications.
