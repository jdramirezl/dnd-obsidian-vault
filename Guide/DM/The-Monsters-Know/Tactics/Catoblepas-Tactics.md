# Catoblepas Tactics

> [!summary]
> The catoblepas is a foul-tempered, beast-intelligence scavenger that befouls its environment and obliterates trespassers with a devastating Death Ray. It charges the first intruder it detects, blasts with necrotic energy, then clubs with its tail. Simple, brutal, and surprisingly willing to retreat early.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Monstrosity, Large, unaligned
- **STR and CON extraordinary**, DEX above-average
- **INT 3** — beast-level intelligence
- **WIS 14** — good self-preservation instinct
- Darkvision → crepuscular/nocturnal
- **Keen Smell** + above-average passive Perception = effective passive Perception **17** (if upwind)
- **Stench:** creatures within 10 ft: CON save or **poisoned** until start of next turn

---

## Combat Sequence
1. Detects intruder by sight or smell → howls, bellows, charges
2. At **30 ft** → **Death Ray** on first interloper it sees
   - Massive necrotic damage — can kill level 2–3 PCs even on a *successful* save
3. If Death Ray recharges → uses it again on **same target**
4. Otherwise → **Tail** attack against same target
5. Target down → charges **next-closest enemy**, repeats

---

## Retreat
- **Moderately wounded (≤58 hp)** → turns tail and **Dashes** away
- Retreats *early* — WIS 14 tells it any foe that can hurt it is dangerous
- **Protecting young:** Dodges and retreats at normal speed to cover offspring's escape, then Dashes once young is safe

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Death Ray is Devastating
> DC 16 CON save for 8d8 necrotic. Use against parties that can handle it, or as a terrifying environmental threat to be avoided.

- Pronunciation: ca-*TOH*-bleh-pahs (plural: catoblepes, ca-*TOH*-bleh-peez)
- Nocturnal — unlikely in broad daylight
- Fixates on one target at a time until it's down
