# Darkweaver Tactics

## Tactical Summary

A blindingly fast (50 ft. walk/climb) shock attacker with Spider Climb, 120 ft. darkvision, and Shadowy Form (harder to hit in dim light/darkness, even vs. darkvision). It ambushes from darkness, snatches a victim with Shadow Web (120 ft.), Reels them 60 ft., closes the gap, and delivers a devastating Bite (10 ft. reach) dealing heavy necrotic damage that reduces HP max. A true horror-movie predator.

## Combat Behavior

### Opening — Snatch and Drag
1. Lurks in darkness, targeting the **squishiest PC** (wizards, sorcerers)
2. **Shadow Web** (120 ft.) — grapples on hit, ~16 avg damage
3. **Reel** — pulls grappled target up to 60 ft. toward itself
4. Uses 50 ft. movement to close remaining distance
5. **Bite** (10 ft. reach) — ~31 avg damage (mostly necrotic), reduces HP max
6. Combined round 1: **~47 damage** — can drop a level 6–7 squishy outright

### Round 2 — Finish or Flee with Prey
- Moves 50 ft. deeper into darkness (Reels victim instead of dragging — no speed penalty)
- Bites again — **auto-crit** if target is unconscious and within 5 ft., doubled dice, likely kills via HP max reduction to 0
- Lobs remaining Shadow Webs at pursuing allies to slow rescue

### Prolonged Feeding (Narrative Option)
- Forgo Bite until victim is far from rescuers
- Shadow Web + Reel + movement only to drag victim away
- Shadow Web pursuers to delay them

### In Its Lair
- Fights to destroy invaders (no leisurely meal)
- **Lair Actions (Init 20):**
  1. **Extinguish** — snuff light sources first (maximizes Shadowy Form)
  2. **Shadow Fears** — default after Extinguish (can't repeat consecutive turns)
  3. **Shadow Step** — only when cornered at lair's heart
- Stays out of melee; immobilizes foes with Shadow Web
- Reels frailest enemies to its side for Bite; kills weakest first

## Key Decision Points

| Situation | Response |
|---|---|
| Hunting in the wild | Shadow Web → Reel → close → Bite squishiest target |
| PCs carry light | Not concerned — dim light still triggers Shadowy Form |
| PCs have sunlight magic | Immediately repulsed — flees |
| Moderately wounded (≤104 hp) | Dashes into darkness |
| Cornered, no escape | Fights to the death |

## Retreat Conditions

- **Moderately wounded (≤104 hp):** Dashes off into darkness
- **Cornered:** Fights to the death
- Sunlight Hypersensitivity: any sunlight-creating magic (*dawn*, *sun blade*) causes immediate retreat

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Shadow Web → Reel → Bite can one-shot a level 6–7 wizard
> - Use vertical space: ceilings, walls, trees — Spider Climb makes it 3D
> - Pair with an existing combat for max horror: it snatches a wounded PC mid-fight
> - Suitable solo foe for levels 6–8 (outside lair) or 7–9 (in lair)
