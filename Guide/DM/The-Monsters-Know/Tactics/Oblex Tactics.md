# Oblex Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Oblex Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/oblex-tactics/)
> **Bestiary**: [[Oozes]]

Slime-creatures from mind flayer experimentation that feed on humanoids' memories. Don't need to kill victims. Unusual ability contour: peaks in Dex, Con, Int. 60 ft blindsight. Amorphous (1-inch cracks). Aversion to Fire. Immune to charmed.

## Oblex Spawn

Weakest form — can't consume memories yet. Goal: avoid engagement, get somewhere safe to grow. Fights back with pseudopod only if engaged. Even 1 point of fire damage or ≤12 hp → Dash away.

## Adult Oblex

Extraordinary Dex/Int, very high Con/Cha. Expert Deception (impersonation).

**Target**: Highest Int in area. Ideal: high Int AND low Wis (save mod +0 or lower ideal; +5 or higher = no go; Int must be ≥14).

**Modus Operandi**:
1. Sulfurous Impersonation → present as person(s), maintain with Deception
2. If Deception fails → *charm person* (boosted to 5 targets)
3. *Detect thoughts* to confirm target
4. Maneuver close to target, others far away
5. Eat Memories — one use per target is enough

**Slime-tether detection**: DC 15 (kludge). Good passive Perception may notice immediately.

**Spells**: *Charm person* (5 targets), *hold person* (2 targets — disable dangerous foes), *color spray* (cover escape), *detect thoughts* (confirm target).

**Actions**: Eat Memories alone (just eating), Pseudopod alone (defending retreat), Multiattack (eat + defend simultaneously).

**Retreat**: Relies on source crevice + *hold person*. Prefers Dash; Disengage if 3+ enemies. If body found → all-out Multiattack, eat memories for damage.

## Elder Oblex

Up to 13 simulacra. Eat Memories DC 18. Con 21, big HP pool. Ditches *color spray* for *confusion*, *dominate person*, *fear*, *hallucinatory terrain*, *hypnotic pattern*, *dimension door*, *telekinesis*.

**Escape spells by enemy count**: 1 → *hold person/monster*. 2 → *dominate person*. 3 clustered → *fear*. 4+ → *hypnotic pattern*. Natural environment → *hallucinatory terrain*.

**Retreat**: Moderately wounded (≤80 hp) → crack or *dimension door*. AC good enough for Dodge while retreating. Truly cornered → fights to death with Multiattack.
