# Froghemoth Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/froghemoth-tactics/) | **Date**: July 6, 2017

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Huge ambush predator** (monstrosity despite alien origins). Pure brute with Stealth proficiency.
- **Goal**: Swallow two targets. Hides underwater, strikes with surprise.
- **Conditional Multiattack**: Two tentacles + either tongue or bite. Tongue-and-maybe-bite is almost always better.
- **Tentacle grapples but doesn't restrain**: Grappled targets can still act — just can't move.
- **Tongue pulls but doesn't grapple**: Slurps grappled target to mouth → bonus bite → swallow on hit.
- **Stupid (Int 2) but Wis 12**: Enough animal cunning to pick good prey.

## Target Selection

**Pre-calculate for each PC**: AC (without Dex mod) + Strength modifier. Sort low-to-high before session.

- **Best prey**: Lowest AC + Str mod.
- **Tentacle targets**: Closest first, then lowest AC + Str mod in reach.
- **Tongue threshold**: Only tongues grappled target if AC + Str mod ≤ 15.
- **Free target threshold**: Stricter — AC 14/Str −2; AC 13/Str 0; AC 12/Str +2; AC 11/Str +3.
- **Round 2+**: Ignores highest AC + Str mod. Pursues lowest.

## Lightning Vulnerability

- Lightning staggers: loses Multiattack, speed halved for 1 turn.
- **Freaks out**: Drops all grappled targets, moves toward lightning-dealer at half speed, single bite or tentacle.
- Rearranges priorities permanently — wants lightning-dealer swallowed.
- Exception: if lightning reduces to ≤73 hp, flees instead.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Any damage reducing to ≤73 hp**: Dashes into water.
- **Does NOT release grappled targets** — too dumb to let go. Drags them underwater.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Pre-calculate AC (no Dex) + Str mod for every PC before the session. The lightning interaction is a trap: fire hurts most, but lightning recharges aggression. The "forgets to let go" retreat is a drowning scenario — have escape DCs ready. Huge creature is NOT slowed by dragging Medium targets.
