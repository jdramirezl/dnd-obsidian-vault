# Redcap Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Vicious Small fey brutes — exceptional STR and CON, live for murder. Iron Boots announce their presence (no stealth). Magically created, not evolved. Must soak cap in fresh blood to sustain existence. Relentless pursuers with a survival instinct that barely overrides bloodlust.

## Opening: Ironbound Pursuit

- Combines movement + attack in one action: move up to speed, then kick
- Effectively doubles closing distance (25 ft base + 25 ft Ironbound = 50 ft in one round)
- Target: DC 14 DEX save or 3d10+4 bludgeoning + knocked **prone**
- Always the opening move

## Sustained Combat: Multiattack

- **3× Wicked Sickle** per action — avg 26 total damage
- Relentless: pursues any fleeing enemy with Ironbound Pursuit if normal movement isn't enough
- Familiarity with Chase rules essential — redcaps don't give up

## Outsize Strength

- Treated as **Medium** for grappling — can grapple Large creatures (horses, Wild Shaped druids)
- Grapple replaces entire Multiattack (not a single attack substitution)
- Use case: preemptively grapple to prevent escape
- Sequence: Turn 1 Ironbound Pursuit → Turn 2 grapple (if target fled) → Turn 3 Multiattack grappled target
- Heavy weapon proficiency is largely irrelevant — 3 sickle hits vastly outdamage any single heavy weapon strike

## Retreat Conditions

- **Seriously wounded** (≤18 hp) while opponent ≥40% HP: flees (Dash)
- **Moderately wounded** (≤31 hp) while opponent ≥70% HP: flees (Dash)
- Otherwise fights until target is dead

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Iron Boots are loud — no surprise, but the dread of hearing them approach is its own horror
> - In groups, one redcap grapples while others Multiattack the held target
> - Heavy weapon proficiency from Outsize Strength is a near-useless vestige
