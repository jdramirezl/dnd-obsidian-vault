# Wood Woad Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Lawful neutral living plant — territorial guardian, not aggressor. 10 HD, exceptional Strength/Constitution, two-swing Multiattack with Magic Club. Proficient in Perception, Stealth, and Athletics. Advantage on Stealth in plant-rich terrain. First attack is always an ambush from concealment (unseen-attacker advantage). Uses Tree Stride to close distance instantly.

## Engagement Philosophy

- **Don't start none, won't be none** — indifferent unless territory is trespassed or it's attacked outright
- If not outnumbered, may **grapple and bounce** trespassers to the edge of its territory rather than attack to harm
- May shove enemies into pits/traps if serving a master, or into nearby ravines if antagonized
- Ambush first, always — waits in concealment until trespassers come within reach, or Tree Strides to close

## Fire Response (Vulnerable)

Fire inhibits Regeneration but **angers rather than deters** a wood woad on duty:

- **Melee fire weapon** → uses attacks to **disarm** (DMG p. 271), picks up weapon as free action, hurls it 60+ ft. away, then resumes attacking with particular ill will
- **Fire spell** → closes distance (Tree Stride if needed), focuses all attacks on caster until unconscious — top priority
- **Mixed fire sources** → judgment call: easier opponent to "extinguish" goes first (disarm melee or pound caster)

## Retreat Conditions

| Situation | Threshold | Behavior |
|---|---|---|
| Guarding assigned post | Never | Fights to the death |
| Roaming (no fire) | ≤ 30 hp (seriously wounded) | Dodge + Tree Stride away |
| Roaming (fire attacks) | ≤ 52 hp (moderately wounded) | Dodge + Tree Stride away |
