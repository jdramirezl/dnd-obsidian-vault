# Orcs Revisited

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Orcs Revisited](https://www.themonstersknow.com/orcs-revisited/)
> **Bestiary**: [[Orcs]]

*Volo's Guide* variants building on the orcish pantheon. Five new varieties, two spellcasters.

## Orc Blade of Ilneval

Front-line battlefield commander. Foe Smiter (+1 die longsword damage), Multiattack (2 weapon attacks + Ilneval's Command if available). Ilneval's Command (recharge): grants attack reactions to 3 allies within 120 ft.

**Simple**: Charge, fight with longsword, use Ilneval's Command whenever available. Slightly higher mental abilities — can adjust failing tactics, leads parley-taunting, prioritizes most threatening targets.

## Orc Claw of Luthic

Support caster with wicked melee (4 claw attacks when below half HP).

- **Bestow curse** (touch): Charge with Aggressive → cast on target → next round, 4-claw Multiattack with bonus necrotic. Target: low Wis (save mod +0 or lower ideal, +5+ = no go). Alternative: turn-wasting option vs. fighters with Extra Attack, or disadvantage-on-attacks option.
- **Warding bond**: Cast pre-combat on powerful ally (takes half their damage, +1 AC/saves). Sleaze: cast during parley-taunting phase.
- **Bane**: Alternative to *bestow curse* if target has high Wis but low Cha. Cast at 3rd level → 5 targets.
- **Cure wounds**: On seriously wounded allies (≤40% hp). 2nd-level slot for powerful allies, 1st for grunts.
- **Guiding bolt**: Prelude to Aggressive charge (7 radiant + advantage on next attack). Use when out of higher slots.

## Orc Hand of Yurtrus

Spellcaster, role varies between support and assault. Not suited for toe-to-toe melee (only Con is high physically).

- **Silence** or **blindness/deafness**: Shut down enemy spellcasters
- **Inflict wounds** (2nd level): 4d10 necrotic — the big damage dealer. Cast repeatedly (2nd-level slots first, then 1st). Only real way to hurt opponents.
- **Bane**: If no enemy spellcaster to stifle

Charges because Aggressive, but relies on *inflict wounds* to take opponents down rapidly.

## Orc Nurtured One of Yurtrus

Walking plague bomb. Corrupted Carrier + Corrupted Vengeance: voluntarily explode as action, splashing toxic fluids over 10-ft radius (DC 13 Con save or poisoned + 4d6 damage). Claw attack is measly (1d4+2 + 1d4 necrotic).

**Sole purpose**: Charge into midst of enemies, detonate where it contaminates ≥2 targets. Sacrifice for the team.

## Orc Red Fang of Shargaas

Assassin. *Darkness* at will (no material components), sees through it. Slayer: advantage on attacks vs. targets that haven't acted; hits on surprised targets = crits.

**Don't drop *darkness* before attacking** — advantage from Slayer is equal to advantage from blinding, and *darkness* removes surprise (no crits). Use *darkness* defensively after presence is revealed.

Uses Cunning Action Dash (same as Aggressive) → Multiattack with Slayer on first round.

## Tanarukk

Straightforward brute. Stupid, savage, no self-preservation. Increased damage resistance and ferocity. Fights until enemies or it are destroyed. Run on autopilot.
