# Guardian Giant Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/guardian-giant-tactics/) | **Date**: February 19, 2026
> **Book**: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica

---

## Overview

- **Gatekeeping sentinels** of the Boros Legion with beef against the Gruul Clans.
- Absolute tanks: extraordinary Str and Con, exceptional Wis. Proficient in Dex and Wis saves.
- Can't be surprised. Extremely hard to sneak past. Lawful neutral.

## Combat Tactics

- **Front-line brute**. Holds ground when defending, moves directly to melee when attacking.
- Uses spear one-handed (carries shield). Speed 40 ft.
- **Slows to stay within 10 ft of Boros allies** when advancing together.
- **Protection reaction**: Hinders attacks against important allies. Prioritizes by rank; equal rank → whoever's nearby.

## Strategic Awareness (Wis 18)

- Immediately recognizes **key enemy combatants**. Directs allies to engage threats it can't handle itself.
- Unerring instinct for when the tide of battle shifts.
- Intuitively assesses encounter difficulty (Easy/Medium/Hard/Deadly).

### Decision Matrix

| Situation | Response |
|---|---|
| Deadly/High Difficulty for own side | Advise against engagement |
| Defending + bad odds | Everyone shelter behind walls; giant is last inside |
| Planning attack + bad odds | Refuses to budge unless odds improve |
| Attack going poorly | Delays enemy for allied withdrawal |
| Under siege + unexpected losses | Delays enemy for allied withdrawal |

## Negotiation

- Will request a halt to talk terms. Not a natural diplomat but **reasonable and shrewd**.
- Insight proficiency → detects bad-faith bargaining, calls it out immediately.
- Knows its BATNA; won't accept worse terms.
- Play as: **loyal, forthright, shrewd**.

## Retreat

- Recognizes losing battles early — orders retreat **well before** seriously wounded (≤54 hp).
- First priority: get smaller allies away safely. Saving itself is secondary.
