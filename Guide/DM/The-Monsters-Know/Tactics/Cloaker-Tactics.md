# Cloaker Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/cloaker-tactics/) | **Date**: May 7, 2018
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Cloaker]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Ambush predator**: Exceptionally high Str and high Dex with Stealth proficiency and False Appearance — seeks to take down prey in a single strike.
- **Phantasms before attacking**: Creates illusory duplicates (like *mirror image*) while still hidden — works only in dim light or darkness.
- **Bite requires advantage**: The head-enveloping rider only triggers if the attack was made with advantage. Attacking from stealth is practically mandatory.
- **Damage Transfer**: Half of all incoming damage passes through the cloaker into the enveloped creature — discourages allies from attacking it.
- **Light Sensitivity**: Bright light is an immediate deterrent.

## Combat Behavior

1. **Select prey**: Someone old, young, weak, wounded, isolated, or oblivious. Judgment can be wrong (Int/Wis only above-average).
2. **Hide and stalk**: Uses Stealth to approach undetected.
3. **Phantasms**: Activates illusory duplicates while hidden (not an attack, doesn't reveal position). Closes to 40 ft.
4. **Strike**: Bites first — successful bite with advantage envelops the target's head (blinded, unable to breathe/speak/cast verbal spells). Follow-up tail strike with advantage if envelop succeeded.
5. **Second round**: Multiattacks again. Uses tail against an ally of prey if needed.
6. **Disengage or finish**: After two rounds, if prey isn't dead, detaches and withdraws. Uses Moan on departure (60-ft radius frighten) to prevent pursuit.

## Key Decision Points

- **Prey enveloped, allies not intervening?** → Stay and finish the meal, even 3+ rounds.
- **Bright light?** → Immediate withdrawal.
- **Moderately wounded (≤54 hp)?** → Detach and flee.
- **3+ melee opponents engaged?** → Detach and flee.
- **Suffocation ruling**: Target is "unable to breathe" — DM may rule immediate suffocation (Con mod rounds to 0 hp) rather than held breath.

## Retreat Conditions

- Reduced to 54 hp or fewer
- Bright light exposure
- 3+ melee attackers engaged
- Uses Moan on exit to frighten pursuers

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The cloaker is a horror-movie ambush predator. Its gameplan: hide → phantasms → strike from stealth → envelop head → suffocate. Damage Transfer makes allies hesitate to attack it. Design encounters in dim light or darkness. Moan is best saved for retreat, not offense.
