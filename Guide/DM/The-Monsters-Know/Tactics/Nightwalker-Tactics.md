# Nightwalker Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/nightwalker-tactics/) | **Date**: November 19, 2018
> **Book**: Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes

---

## Overview

- Released into the material plane when someone visits the Negative Plane. **Destroying it traps the visitor forever**; *banishment* is the clean solution.
- Extraordinary Str/Con/Dex → nimble brute. Weak mental abilities (Wis 8 is highest).
- **Indiscriminate** target selection, operates on instinct, zero tactical flexibility.
- Immune or resistant to nearly everything except **force, radiant, and magic weapon damage**.
- Can't be exhausted, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, or restrained. *Can* be blinded or stunned.
- 120 ft darkvision. **Strictly nocturnal** — no reason to go out during the day.
- 40 ft walk + 40 ft fly. Glides eerily — imagine walking motions while flying, stillness while "walking."

## Passive Features

- **Annihilating Aura** (30-ft radius): Positions itself within 30 ft of all potential victims (or at least 6) at end of turn.
- **Life Eater**: Passive — no tactical decisions needed.

## Attack Pattern

### Multiattack: 2× Enervating Focus OR 1× Enervating Focus + 1× Finger of Doom

**Finger of Doom** (ranged, 300 ft, recharge):
- Use when available AND has advantage against any opponent within **40 ft + remaining movement**.
- Prefer targets affected by Annihilating Aura or unable to see in the dark.
- If it can reach the target same turn: Finger of Doom first → if paralysis succeeds → close distance → Enervating Focus on that target.
- If it can't reach: move toward target, use Enervating Focus on a convenient target along the way.

**Enervating Focus** (melee, 15-ft reach):
- If only one opponent with advantage: both attacks on that target.
- If multiple: may split attacks, but always pathing toward optimal Annihilating Aura positioning.

## Reactions to Magic Weapons

- Ignores opportunity attacks **except** from magic weapon wielders.
- Won't knowingly move within reach of magic weapons (15-ft reach helps avoid this).
- If engaged by a magic weapon wielder: **rises into the air** out of reach, focuses ALL attacks on that opponent.
- Multiple magic weapon attackers: stays out of melee reach, divides attacks among them, uses Finger of Doom on ranged magic attackers first.
- **Ignores** opponents without magic weapons or force/radiant damage.

## Retreat

- **Never flees**. No survival instinct. Gets a kick out of knowing its destruction traps someone on the Negative Plane forever.
