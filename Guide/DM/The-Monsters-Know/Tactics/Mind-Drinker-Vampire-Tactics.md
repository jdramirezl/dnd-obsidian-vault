# Mind Drinker Vampire Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/mind-drinker-vampire-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- CR 4 psionic vampire (Ravnica/House Dimir) with extraordinary Intelligence, exceptional Dexterity, and Sunlight Sensitivity
- Shadow Stealth: Hide as a bonus action in dim light/darkness—strictly nocturnal operative
- Mind Siphon is the key ability: psychic damage + surface thought reading. Not an attack or spell, so it doesn't reveal a hidden MDV's position
- All psionic "spells" lack verbal components—a hidden MDV can cast any of them undetected
- Fragile for CR 4; compensates with three saving throw proficiencies and necrotic resistance

## Combat Behavior
- **Preferred mode**: Accomplish the mission without ever leaving hiding. Mind Siphon key targets from concealment
- **Attack sequence**: From concealment, Multiattack—Unarmed Strike first (advantage from unseen, grapples on hit), then Bite (requires grappled/incapacitated/restrained target). Bite imposes disadvantage on subsequent Mind Siphon save
- **Mind Siphon**: Only on key targets (recharges over several rounds). Target must have ≥20 hp for 95% certainty Bite won't kill before thought extraction
- **Psionic spells**:
  - *Charm person*: Social scalpel from hiding; step out on failed save, plan B on success
  - *Major image*: Lure targets into reach or distract them away from the MDV's path
  - *Hold person*: Useful when time isn't pressing—can attempt repeatedly from hiding without detection
  - *Sleep* (2024 rules): Incapacitates on failed Wis save; pairs with intellect devourers for Body Thief
  - *Mirror image*: Prophylaxis when emerging—four identical MDVs swirl out of shadows
  - *Gaseous form*: Escape hatch, only within 10 ft of an aperture enemies can't follow through

## Retreat Conditions
- Withdraws at ≤25 hp (~45% of max), earlier than typical due to fragility
- Flies away using Shadow Stealth to re-hide as a bonus action
- *Gaseous form* as last resort near a small aperture

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The MDV is a commando, not a brawler. Mind Siphon from the shadows, Unarmed Strike → Bite only when necessary, and vanish before the party knows what hit them.
