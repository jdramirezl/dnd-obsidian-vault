# Unspeakable Horror Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Intentionally vague, customizable horror from *Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft*. Exists to give flesh to victims' fears. Huge size. STR/CON peaks → melee brute. Animal-level INT — no complex plans, just zeroes in on foes.

## Body Composition Options

- **Loathsome Limbs** → grants **Relentless Stride**: move through creature spaces and knock prone. As Huge, already moves through Medium or smaller; this adds Large creatures (mounts, summoned elementals)
- Aberrant Armor, Malleable Mass, Oozing Organs: flavor only

## Limb Modifications

Bone Blade, Corrosive Pseudopod, Grasping Tentacle, Poisonous Limb — all flavor variations on the same brute approach.

## Hex Blast (Recharge 5–6)

The key ability. Use when available and aimed at 3+ foes. Likely only fires once per encounter.

### Randomization

Don't choose on the fly (too dumb). Either:
- **d6**: 1–4 = Hex Blast table; 5–6 = default
- **Coin flip**: Heads = default; Tails = 1d4 on table

Choose 1–2 hexes *in advance* to tailor the encounter. Once fired, the horror exploits results by instinct.

### Hex Blast Variants

| Variant | Effect | Exploitation |
|---|---|---|
| **Default** | Straight necrotic damage | None |
| **Beguiling Hex** | Incapacitates on failed save | Free hits on incapacitated targets |
| **Bile Hex** | Recurring damage on failed save | Ignore those targets, focus elsewhere |
| **Petrifying Hex** | Restrained → petrified | Ignore petrified; restrained gives advantage but narratively odd |
| **Reality-Stealing Hex** | Deafened + blinded | Blinded = prime melee targets |

## Retreat Behavior

**None.** Barely real — no survival instinct. Fights until destroyed.
