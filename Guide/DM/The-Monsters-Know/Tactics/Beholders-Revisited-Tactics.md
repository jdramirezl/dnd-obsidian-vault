# Beholders Revisited (Volo's Addendum)

## Tactical Summary

Volo's Guide supplements the original beholder analysis. Key updates: the beholder should still aim randomly selected rays at optimal targets (siding with MM over Volo's), and the Antimagic Cone is best used as a "fourth eye ray" at close range by repositioning to aim it at spellcasters while leaving other targets exposed to eye rays.

## Eye Ray Targeting: MM vs. Volo's

- **Volo's says:** Focus all rays on the most dangerous foe
- **MM says:** Rays fire at random; choose optimal targets for each
- **Verdict: Side with MM.** A beholder aims randomly selected rays at whoever is least able to resist them. Focusing an Enervation Ray on a high-CON fighter wastes its potential. Exception: if one PC is substantially higher level than the rest, the beholder may focus rays on them even suboptimally.

## Antimagic Cone: Use Freely (Up Close)

- At range: the 150 ft. cone is too wide — suppresses the beholder's own rays
- **Up close:** the cone narrows enough to target 1–2 spellcasters while leaving others exposed to eye rays
- Use vertical movement to reposition: hover close, aim cone at casters, fire rays at everyone else
- Treat it as a "fourth eye ray" — a positioning tool, not a passive defense

## Variant Eye Rays (Volo's, p. 12)

Swap standard rays for spell effects to surprise players who've memorized the MM stat block. Key rule: **saving throw uses the new spell's ability score**, not the replaced ray's.

| Standard Ray | Save | Variant | Save |
|---|---|---|---|
| Charm | WIS | *banishment* | CHA |
| Death | DEX | *circle of death* | CON |
| Death | DEX | *feeblemind* | INT |
| Disintegration | DEX | *eyebite* | WIS |
| Enervation | CON | *polymorph* | WIS |
| Fear | WIS | *moonbeam* | CON |
| Paralyzing | CON | *modify memory* | WIS |
| Petrification | DEX | *Otto's irresistible dance* | WIS |
| Sleep | WIS | *blindness/deafness* | CON |
| Slowing | DEX | *bestow curse* | WIS |
| Telekinesis | STR | *geas* | WIS |

- Environmental variants (*silence*, *sleet storm*, *wall of ice*, *wall of force*) require no saves
- *Create undead* replaces Enervation Ray if corpses are available
- **Deadly combo:** Eyebite (Panicked) + Otto's Irresistible Dance on the same creature = out of the fight

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Variant rays let you tailor the beholder to target your party's specific weak saves
> - Swapping Enervation (CON) for *polymorph* (WIS) lets the beholder turn that tough fighter into a goat
> - *Power word stun* replacing Antimagic Cone significantly increases beholder power — the cone's absence frees up all eye rays every round
> - Use 3D positioning: the cone is a 3D shape; angling up/down changes its ground footprint
