# Couatl Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/couatl-tactics/) | **Date**: August 17, 2017
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Couatl]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Lawful good celestial**: Exceptional stats across the board, Dex and Wis especially extraordinary. Immune to normal weapon damage and psychic damage, resistant to radiant.
- **90-ft fly speed, AC 19**: Hovers in the air, dive-bombs targets, flies back out of reach without concern for opportunity attacks.
- **Truesight 120 ft**: Sees through all illusions and invisibility.
- **Spellcasting (Cha-based)**: Most spells are social/utility, not combat. Key combat spells: *bless*, *cure wounds*, *sanctuary*, *shield*.
- **Unlikely to pick fights** except with intrinsically evil beings (fiends, undead) or when guarding a charge.

## Combat Behavior

**Opening**: Casts *bless* on allies first (lawful good — supports before attacking). Casts *sanctuary* (bonus action) on the most vulnerable creature under its protection.

**Standard attack — Constrict**: Swoops down, Constricts to grapple AND restrain on hit. If it hits, flies back up with the grappled target (movement halved but still 45 ft). If it misses, flies back up out of reach.

**Follow-up — Bite**: Next turn, bites the restrained target with advantage, hoping to put it to sleep. If successful, flies the sleeping enemy outside and dumps them like a celestial bouncer, then returns.

**Against larger groups (3+ enemies)**: Forgoes the constrict-and-carry tactic. Relies on Bite instead, prioritizing enemies by chaoticness/evilness, swooping down one by one to put them to sleep.

***Shield* (reaction, 3/day)**: Casts at the first moment it needs to — lasts until start of its next turn, protecting against all subsequent attacks that round.

***Cure wounds***: Weak for its action cost (avg 8 hp). Only used to save a seriously wounded ally from imminent death, followed by *sanctuary* on the next turn.

## Key Decision Points

- **Allies present?** → *Bless* first, then *sanctuary* on the most vulnerable.
- **Small group (≤3 enemies)?** → Constrict → fly up → Bite to sleep → dump outside.
- **Larger group (4+ enemies)?** → Bite-focused, prioritize by alignment.
- **Incoming hit?** → *Shield* at first opportunity (lasts until next turn).

## Retreat Conditions

- **No duty to protect**: Retreats when seriously wounded (≤38 hp), flying at full speed with Dodge action.
- **Guarding a person/place/relic**: Fights to the death. Couatls know when they'll die, though not how.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Couatls are celestial bouncers — constrict, fly up, bite to sleep, dump outside. They're lawful good and support allies before attacking. The constrict-and-carry tactic is time-consuming, so only use it against small groups. Against larger groups, rapid-fire Bite to put enemies to sleep one by one. Remember: *shield* lasts a full round, so cast it at the first hit.
