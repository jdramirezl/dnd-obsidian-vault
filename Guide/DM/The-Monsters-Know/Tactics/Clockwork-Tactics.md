# Clockwork Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/clockwork-tactics/) | **Date**: September 9, 2019

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Rock gnome defense constructs** from *Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes*. Share condition/damage immunities, darkvision, and obey controller commands.
- **No independent judgment** (Int 3): Rigid, mechanistic behavior unless actively controlled in real-time.
- **Immune to physical damage** from nonmagical, non-adamantine weapons (bronze scout, iron cobra, oaken bolter).
- **Best used at night or underground** due to darkvision.

## Bronze Scout

- **Mobile land mine / ambush initiator**: Burrows with double Stealth proficiency and telescoping eyestalks.
- **Sequence**: Stealthy approach → burrow → poke eyestalks → check 3+ enemies within 15 ft → Lightning Flare → allies attack.
- **Earth Armor**: Burrowing provokes no opportunity attacks. Seriously damaged (≤7 hp) → dives underground and flees.
- **Scouting mode**: Readies Lightning Flare for "when any creature winds up to attack it" (interrupt trigger — fires before the attack lands).

## Iron Cobra

- **Mobile assassin**: High Stealth, approaches hidden in poor visibility. Cannot hide from alert targets with clear sightlines.
- **Follows instructions to the letter**. Bite poison effects should be part of controller's orders:
  - **Paralysis** → follow-up attacks or Dex-save effects.
  - **Confusion** → supporters near allies.
  - **Poison damage** → all-purpose.
- **Int 3 caps instruction complexity** to ~3 conditions. No retreat unless instructed.

## Stone Defender

- **Front-line bodyguard**: Always adjacent to an ally. Intercept Attack reaction blocks the first hit each round.
- **Slam** targets enemies that attacked it or its ally. Knocks prone — allies follow up. Fights until destroyed.

## Oaken Bolter

- **Artillery**: Ideal 40–100 ft from enemies. Lancing Bolt is both ranged and melee — no point-blank penalty.
- **Combo**: Harpoon first → bonus action pulls target 20 ft closer → Lancing Bolt follow-up.
- **Harpoon uses**: Pull casters from backline, keep shock attackers from escaping, yank melee off wounded allies.
- **Explosive Bolt (recharge 5–6)**: Use when 4+ opponents in 40-ft diameter. Fights until destroyed.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Monsters
> Clockworks are only as smart as their instructions. Incomplete commands cause single-minded, potentially absurd behavior — lean into this. Use enhancement/malfunction tables for flavor, and trigger a random malfunction at 40% hp or below.
