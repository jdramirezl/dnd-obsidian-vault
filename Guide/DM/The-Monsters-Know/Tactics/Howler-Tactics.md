# Howler Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/howler-tactics/) | **Date**: August 13, 2020

---

## Tactical Summary

- CR 8 pack-hunting predators from Pandemonium. **Never appear alone** — either with other howlers or serving a more powerful fiend/evil master.
- Very high physical scores, extraordinary peak in **WIS** (powers Mind-Breaking Howl, keen senses). Classified as **shock attackers** — move fast, hit hard.
- Darkvision → attack between dusk and dawn.
- **Resistances**: Cold, fire, lightning, nonmagical physical. Acid/thunder gets their attention. Necrotic, radiant, psychic, or force damage **really** draws their ire.

## Encounter Sizing

- Trained howlers: minimum 2 per fiend master. Independent pack: minimum 4 (can split and still use Pack Tactics vs 2 targets).
- **Ceiling**: 1 howler per PC + 1 per magic weapon. One howler can challenge a 20th-level PC.

## Target Selection

- Standard predator formula: the young, the old, the weak, the isolated, the oblivious. **Plus**: the frightened (failed saves vs Mind-Breaking Howl).
- Deviate only when taking necrotic/radiant/psychic/force damage → turn on that attacker.

## Mind-Breaking Howl

- Recharge 6 only → most likely once per encounter. Pack hunters stagger usage by dominance order.
- Effect lasts only until end of howler's next turn. Successful save = immunity.
- Before using: positions to encompass ≥6 opponents (or all if fewer) in the cone.
- If recharges, uses it again (practically a reflex) unless a higher-status howler's also recharged.

## Combat

- When Howl is on cooldown: **Multiattack**, always in concert with packmates for Pack Tactics.
- Uncommonly tenacious — won't stop tearing apart a victim unless attacker wields a magic weapon or deals an aggravating damage type.
- If provoked: the struck howler + one other turn on the attacker. Remaining howlers continue on original victim unless only one is left, then it joins the others.

## Retreat

- Truculence overcomes self-preservation as long as even one ally is present.
- **Only retreats when last one standing** and at least moderately wounded (≤63 hp).
- Uses Dash to flee — unless Mind-Breaking Howl recharges, in which case wheels around and uses it against pursuers before resuming flight.
