# Vegepygmy Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Fungus in humanoid form. Low STR, high DEX and CON → skirmisher. Animal-level INT, acts on instinct. Darkvision + Plant Camouflage + Stealth proficiency → ambush attacker. Attacks aboveground at night/fog or in the Underdark. Neutral alignment — indifferent by default, not hostile.

## Basic Vegepygmy

- Attacks only with large numerical advantage (3:1 ratio)
- Melee (claws) does 1 more avg damage than ranged (sling), but ranged is safer
- ~1 ranged fighter per 3 melee fighters in a group
- **Regeneration** keeps them in the fight; if one takes damage, it Disengages and retreats while companions continue, returns when healed. Can switch to ranged while regenerating
- Instinctively surround a *group* (not smart enough to actively flank a single target)

### Ambush Pattern

Remain hidden until foes pass close → all spring out at once.

### Retreat Behavior

- **Moderately wounded** (≤6 hp): Disengage and back off (instinct, not discipline); re-engage at full 9 hp
- **Seriously wounded** (≤3 hp): Makes a weird whistling noise; stops when above 3 hp
- **Half the group whistling**: All Disengage and retreat; vanish into foliage when pursuers look away

### Cold / Fire / Necrotic Damage

A vegepygmy taking any of these damage types **shrieks**. Every other vegepygmy swarms the damage-dealer until that foe is dealt with, then returns to normal behavior. Vegepygmies who took cold/fire/necrotic don't return — they Disengage and skedaddle.

### Non-Lethal Intent

If repelling trespassers or taking prisoners, they don't attack creatures not fighting back — just stand menacingly, gesturing and making fungusy noises.

## Vegepygmy Chief

Stronger, more HP, melee Multiattack. Always in melee scuffle. Wields spear two-handed (no shield). Retreats at ≤23 hp, returns at ≥30 hp.

- **Spores** (1/day): 15-ft radius. Use when 3+ enemies in range; will position for maximum effect with other vegepygmies blocking for it

## Thorny

Grown from four-legged creatures. Less INT, brute profile → always melee. Doesn't withdraw unless all vegepygmies are withdrawing or it takes cold/fire/necrotic.
