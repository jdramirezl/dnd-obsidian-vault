# Dragon Tactics, Part 3 — Gem Dragons

> [!summary]
> Gem dragons (crystal, topaz, emerald, sapphire, amethyst + moonstone) are neutral, psionic, reclusive brute fighters from Fizban's. Lower AC and fewer hit dice than chromatic/metallic, but compensate with saving throw resistance, breath weapon riders, and psionic abilities. They fight only when their peace is disturbed.

---

## Gem Dragon Overview

| Type | Personality (Young) |
|---|---|
| **Crystal** | Stargazers, lovers of metaphysics, daffy |
| **Topaz** | Emo edgelords |
| **Emerald** | Shy bookworms, suspicious of others |
| **Sapphire** | Obsessive military history buffs |
| **Amethyst** | Awkward, repressed science nerds |
| **Moonstone** | Feywild natives, Charisma-focused (see below) |

### Key Differences from Chromatic/Metallic
- **Neutral** — indifferent by default, not hostile
- **Psionic abilities** instead of innate spellcasting
- **Breath weapons always conical** + always have a rider effect
- **Resistance to 2 damage types** (one matches breath weapon)
- **Lower AC and fewer hit dice** than equivalents
- **No Frightful Presence, no Detect/Tail/Wing legendary actions**
- Universally despise the **Far Realm**

---

## Tactics by Age

### Wyrmling
- 10 ft blindsight, mostly nocturnal
- **Breath weapon first** (if 2+ enemies in cone), then Bite until recharge
- Use alternative movement to hide and escape; don't fight on the ground
- Focus attacks on foes dealing most weapon/spell damage
- Don't engage 2+ melee opponents — reposition when ganged up on
- **Flee at ≤70% hp**

### Young
- 30 ft blindsight, 120 ft telepathy; speak Common
- Gains **Multiattack:** Claw/Claw/Bite; breath range 30 ft — **use against 3+**
- Willing to fight 2 melee opponents
- Retreat: ≤70% hp if unwanted fight; ≤40% hp if they chose to fight

### Adult (Legendary)
- 60 ft blindsight; Legendary Resistance; more psionic tricks
- Breath weapon AoE expands — **best against all opponents at once**
- Gains **Change Shape** and **Psychic Step** (bonus actions)

#### Legendary Actions (Different from Chromatic/Metallic!)

| Action | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Claw** | 1 | Single extra Claw attack |
| **Psychic Step** | 2 | 60 ft *misty step* — repositioning |
| **Unique Damaging** | 3 | Varies by type (see below) |

> [!warning] No Detect, Tail, or Wing Attack
> Enemies can mob gem dragons more easily. Sneaky rogues have better odds. Keep lair actions in reserve for Psychic Step escapes.

#### 3-Cost Legendary Actions

| Dragon | Action | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Crystal | Starlight Strike | 1 target, half on save |
| Topaz | Essential Reduction | 1 target; **disintegrates at 0 hp** |
| Emerald | Emerald Embers | 1 target, half on save |
| Sapphire | Telekinetic Fling | 1 target, half on save |
| Amethyst | Explosive Crystal | 20 ft radius AoE — only worth it vs 4+ |

#### Lair Actions
All share **Beguiling Whisper** (charm lowest-WIS PC; amethyst identifies instantly).

| Category | 4 of 5 Types | Amethyst |
|---|---|---|
| **Shared** | Beguiling Whisper | Beguiling Whisper |
| **Damage** | Direct damage + debuff | **Imprisoning Force** (*forcecage*) |
| **Defense** | Enhances dragon's movement | **Spatial Projection** (duplicate) |

**Rotation:** Alternate Beguiling Whisper and damage. Save defense for retreat.
**Amethyst:** Imprisoning Force once on biggest problem, then alternate Whisper and Projection.

### Ancient — New Psionic Abilities

| Dragon | Gains |
|---|---|
| Crystal | *Greater restoration*, *invisibility* |
| Topaz | *Antilife shell* |
| Emerald | *Etherealness*, *mislead* |
| Sapphire | *Teleport* |
| Amethyst | *Freedom of movement*, *globe of invulnerability*, *plane shift* |

## Retreat Behavior

| Situation | Threshold | Behavior |
|---|---|---|
| Outside lair, unwanted fight | ≤70% hp | Crystal: flee. Others: **parley** |
| Parley style | — | Amethyst/sapphire: good faith. Emerald: bamboozle. Topaz: creepy smack |
| Parley fails | ≤40% hp | Withdraw — defensive lair action → Change Shape |

## Moonstone Dragons — A Breed Apart

Feywild natives that favor magic over brute force. **Charisma** is primary offensive ability.

### Key Differences
- **Spells, not psionics**
- No damage resistance, but **immune to charmed**
- Proficient in INT/WIS/CHA saves (not DEX/CON)
- Ancient: **120 ft truesight**
- **No Beguiling Whisper** lair action

### Moonstone Tactics by Age

| Age | Opening | Follow-up | Retreat |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wyrmling** | Dream Breath (huge AoE, 10 min sleep) | Bite awake foes → Moonlight Breath on 2+ | ≤27 hp: fly away |
| **Young** | Dream Breath → *faerie fire* (4+ targets) | Multiattack limned targets (advantage). *Calm emotions* to avoid fight | Context-dependent |
| **Adult** | Dream Breath (DC 18) → *faerie fire* | **Never attacks without advantage** (*faerie fire* or *invisibility*). Save *invisibility* for escape | Attacked: parley ≤136 hp. Chose fight: ≤78 hp |
| **Ancient** | Same as adult | *Calm emotions* ×2, *invisibility* ×2, *dispel magic* ×2 (legendary action to counter spells) | ≤231 hp / ≤132 hp |

### Moonstone Lair Actions

| Action | Effect | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Banish Into Dream** | Penalty box 1 round (CHA save) | Most dangerous enemy |
| **Compulsive Dance** | Incapacitate + immobilize (INT save) | Alternative save target |
| **Disorienting Visions** | Disadvantage on ability checks | Cover escape (foil Perception). Rogues with Reliable Talent resist |

### Moonstone Legendary Actions

| Action | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Tail Attack** | 1 | 15 ft reach — smacks coy opponents |
| **Cast a Spell** | 2 | Re-up *faerie fire*, *invisibility* for escape, or clutch *revivify* |

> [!tip] DM Note
> Moonstone dragons make excellent Feywild encounters. Dream Breath can knock out half a party. The dragon **never attacks without advantage**. Their lair spans Feywild, Material, and Ethereal planes — retreating across planes can trigger the DMG's Feywild memory loss rule (DC 13 WIS or forget everything).
