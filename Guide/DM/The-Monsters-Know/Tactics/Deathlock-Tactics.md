# Deathlock Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Undead warlocks bound to serve their patron after death. Spellslingers (peak CHA/DEX) with only 2 spell slots (Pact Magic) — lean heavily on cantrips, especially *eldritch blast* (2 beams). No self-preservation instinct; compulsion is to serve. Prefer darkness; use *disguise self* or *invisibility* pre-combat.

## Deathlock (CR 4)

### Standing Decision
- **Always *chill touch*** over Deathly Claw at range — same avg damage, better to-hit, healing suppression
- **Deathly Claw** only at melee range or vs. paralyzed target (auto-crit within 5 ft.)

### Spell Slots (2 slots, all at 3rd level)

| Spell | When |
|---|---|
| *Hold person* | Best option: targets WIS, affects 2 targets at 3rd level. Avoid vs. clerics/druids/monks/paladins |
| *Arms of Hadar* | Escape 2+ melee opponents, but only if ≤1 is STR-based (STR save DC 13) |
| *Hunger of Hadar* | Control vs. 4+ foes in 20 ft. radius, not including itself |
| *Dispel magic* | Only vs. *protection from evil and good*, *slow*, or *faerie fire* |
| *Invisibility* | Pre-combat only — avoid detection, not escape |
| *Spider climb* | Offense only — attack from ceiling with EB while out of melee reach |

### Patron Variants
- **Archfey:** Avoid/confuse, accomplish mission, depart
- **Fiend:** Actively hurt with fire (*fireball*, *burning hands*, *hellish rebuke*)
- **Great Old One:** Ignores opponents unless attacked

## Deathlock Mastermind (CR 8)

- **Grave Bolts** replace EB: 4d8 damage + chance to restrain (DC 16 STR). Follow up with advantage next turn
- **Default spell list is bad** — use patron-specific lists:
  - **Fiend:** *Fireball* (10d6), *scorching ray* (6 rays), *wall of fire* — nearly CR 9
  - **Archfey:** *Greater invisibility*, *dominate person*, *sleep* (13d8)
  - **Great Old One:** *Dissonant whispers* (7d6), *dominate person*, *Evard's black tentacles*
- If stuck with default: only *arms of Hadar*, *blight*, *fly*, *dimension door* are worth casting
- Escapes via *dimension door* — not self-preservation, but patron's investment

## Deathlock Wight (CR 3)

1. Stay at range, **Grave Bolt ×2** targeting clerics/paladins/squishies
2. 3+ enemies within 30 ft. → *fear* (once/day)
3. Watch for wounded foe who failed WIS saves → *hold person* → *misty step* adjacent → **Life Drain**
4. In groups: one holds, one fears, rest Life Drain
5. **Sunlight Sensitivity** — must operate in darkness; aboveground encounters always at night

## Retreat Conditions

- **None.** Fight until destroyed.
- **Exception:** Mastermind escapes via *dimension door* (patron values it)

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Deathlocks are mission-oriented — combat isn't necessarily the goal
> - *Hold person* at 3rd level paralyzing 2 targets is the deathlock's best trick
> - At-will *mage armor* on undead minions (no self-only restriction) is a sneaky AC boost
> - Fiend-patron masterminds are borderline CR 9 — adjust encounter difficulty
