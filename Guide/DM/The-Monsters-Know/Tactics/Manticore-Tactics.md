# Manticore Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/manticore-tactics/) | **Date**: October 6, 2016
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Manticore]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- Tactics laid out in MM flavor text: open with Tail Spike volley, then land and use claws/bite. When outdoors and outnumbered, stay aloft and attack from range until spikes depleted.
- Very high STR, DEX, and CON — equally suited for ranged and melee. Tail Spike is strong with generous range; three per Multiattack.

## Target Selection

- **Indiscriminate**. Low INT (7), sees all PCs as equally weak. Doesn't distinguish ranged vs melee or casters vs non-casters.
- A PC dealing ≥20 hp in a single turn gets its attention — as likely to enrage it as make it hesitate.

## Negotiation

- WIS 12 — can't be tricked into a bad deal. Only things more appealing than food: **more food** or a steady supply. Possibly eliminating a territorial rival (chimeras, griffons, perytons, wyverns).

## Movement & Environment

- Land 30 ft, fly 50 ft → moves by leaping, not hindered by difficult terrain. Likely lives/hunts in difficult terrain where prey is slowed.
- **Darkvision** — advantage over many PCs at night/twilight.

## Retreat

- Flees when seriously injured (≤27 hp) by **Dashing** through the air. Not smart enough to Disengage, but tough enough it usually doesn't need to.

## Encounter Notes

- Often hunt in **packs** (MM flavor). Good opponents for intermediate-level PCs despite CR 3. Unlikely more than 4–5 in an encounter.
