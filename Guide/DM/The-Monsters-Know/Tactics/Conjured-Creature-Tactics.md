# Conjured Creature Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/conjured-creature-tactics/) | **Date**: October 15, 2019

---

## Tactical Summary

- **For players AND DMs**: Covers *conjure animals*, *conjure woodland beings*, *conjure minor elementals*.
- **The DM chooses what appears**, not the player. Player only chooses how many (1/2/4/8). Sage Advice confirms.
- **Use mob attack rules** (DMG ch. 8) for groups of 4+ to keep combat moving.
- **Swarms cannot be conjured**: Each summoned creature is an individual.
- **Ground rules**: DM picks environment-appropriate creatures at max CR; if below max CR, use max hp.

## Key Spell Mechanics

- Creatures appear in separate unoccupied spaces.
- Caster rolls initiative; all act together on same count.
- Friendly but must be told what to do. Commands limited by creature Intelligence.
- Uncommanded creatures only defend themselves.

## Best Creatures by CR

### CR 2 (1 creature)
- **Allosaurus/Saber-toothed tiger**: Pounce from distance for bonus Bite on knockdown.
- **Giant constrictor snake**: Constrict → restrain → Bite with advantage; allies get advantage too.
- **Quetzalcoatlus**: Dive Attack + Flyby from 30–40 ft up.

### CR 1 (2 creatures)
- **Dire wolf**: Both on same target — Pack Tactics advantage without special positioning.
- **Giant spider**: Web (recharge) restrains for follow-up advantage attacks. Stealth + climbing.
- **Giant toad**: Swallow Medium or smaller. Standing Leap ignores difficult terrain.

### CR 1/2 (4 creatures — mob attacks)
- **Warhorse**: Best as independent mounts. Trampling Charge is tremendous.
- **Crocodile**: Stealth + Bite grapples/restrains for ally follow-up.

### CR 1/4 (8 creatures — mob attacks essential)
- **Wolf/Velociraptor**: Pack Tactics — subtract 4 from d20 needed to hit.
- **Giant owl**: Large, fast Flyby. Int 8 — follows complex commands.
- **Panther/Boar**: Pounce/Charge for knockdown follow-ups.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Spell
> The biggest threat isn't power — it's time. Enforce mob attacks for 4+ creatures. If d20 needed is 6–12, send in pairs (one auto-hit each). Have conjured creatures deal average damage. The spell is only "broken" if you let it bog down combat.
