# Bone Knight Tactics

> [!summary]
> The bone knight is an Eberron champion of the Order of the Emerald Claw — a humanoid in bone armor (AC 20) who fights like a brute paladin, commands up to 12 skeletons/zombies, and negotiates through intimidation, deception, and abusive manipulation. Opens with social pressure, then unleashes undead hordes backed by smite spells.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Humanoid (any race), Medium
- **Outstanding STR + CHA**; moderate CON/DEX; decent INT/WIS
- **AC 20** (bonecraft armor)
- Proficient: Athletics, Intimidation, Deception
- Level 8 half-caster (paladin-style spell slots)
- Weapons: Greatsword (primary), Longbow (backup)
- Multiattack: two weapon attacks

---

## Key Features

### Commander of Bones
- Commands up to **12 skeletons and/or zombies**
- Undead act with greater purpose and coordination

### Master of the Pallid Banner
- Commanded undead get **advantage vs. being turned** (and Destroy Undead)

### Undead Composition
- **Skeletons:** more damage, ranged volleys, harder to hit; vulnerable to bludgeoning
- **Zombies:** tougher, Undead Fortitude, no bludgeoning vulnerability; better for attrition
- Choice depends on range, terrain, and expected fight duration

---

## Social Tactics
- High CHA + Intimidation + Deception = **abusive negotiator**
- Prefers to get what it wants without fighting
- Arsenal: direct threats, gaslighting, DARVO, explosive anger, dividing enemies

---

## Spell Priority
| Spell | Use |
|---|---|
| **Wrathful smite** (1st) | Primary — sudden violence for dominance; 2+ slots |
| **Branding smite** (2nd) | Opens ranged combat; works on Longbow |
| **Compelled duel** (1st) | Draws off a PC effective against minions |
| **Hellish rebuke** (1st, reaction) | Cast once for deterrence |
| **Command** (1st) | Underlines abusive manipulations |
| **Darkness** (2nd) | Only when minions vastly outnumber foes |
| **Find steed** | Arrives with warhorse or warhorse skeleton |

---

## Combat Flow
1. **Greatsword Multiattack** ~80% of the time
2. **Wrathful smite** on first melee hit
3. **Hellish rebuke** as reaction when hit (once)
4. Athletics for **grappling hostages** or **shoving prone** before undead (advantage)
5. Encounter: 1 bone knight + 12 undead + 1 steed; sweet spot: 4 PCs level 7–8

---

## Retreat
- **Retreats when seriously wounded (≤33 hp)**
- Leaves expendable undead behind to cover retreat
- May cast **Darkness** to cover escape

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Mixed Undead Formation
> Zombies on the front line, skeletons with bows in the back. Bone knight rides between lines. Fireball wrecks this — best against parties without it.

- Warhorse skeleton: darkvision, immune to exhaustion/poison, but vulnerable to bludgeoning, no Trampling Charge
- *Find steed* bond = independent mount that attacks on its own
- Can steal PC necromancer's undead with Commander of Bones (bonus action)
