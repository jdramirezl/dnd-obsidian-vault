# Star Spawn Tactics, Part 1

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/star-spawn-tactics-part-1/) | **Date**: June 20, 2018

---

## Overview

Star spawn are aberrations associated with Elder Evils across multiple planes (Far Realm, Shadowfell, Gray Waste, Abyss). Speak Deep Speech. Variety for every level of play.

---

## Star Spawn Grue

- Weak skirmisher. Extremely low STR → **never alone**, always in swarms. 60 ft darkvision — nocturnal or subterranean.
- **Aura of Madness** (20 ft): Disadvantage on saves and attacks against non-grues. Makes grues excellent auxiliaries to stronger star spawn.
- **Confounding Bite**: Two dice piercing + WIS save or attacks against the target have advantage for 1 round. Synergizes with Aura of Madness (disadvantage on the save).
- **Retreat**: At ≤6 hp. One melee opponent → move away, Ready a Confounding Bite vs pursuers. Multiple melee opponents → Disengage first.

## Star Spawn Mangler

- Low-STR skirmisher, never alone. Proficient in DEX/CON saves (weak to WIS → aggressively targets mental-manipulation casters).
- Climbs → makes fights 3D. **Shadow Stealth** → keeps ambushing from dark corners.

### Flurry of Claws (Recharge 4–6)

- **Six** claw attacks + bonus movement without opportunity attacks. First round with Ambush: advantage on *all* attacks (not just first) → bonus psychic damage on each. Potential **93 avg / 144 max damage** in one turn.
- Recharges on 4–6 (50%) → usable every other turn on average, still better than Multiattack over two rounds.

### Combat Script

1. **Round 1**: Strike from hiding. Flurry of Claws with Ambush advantage. Immediately move to shadow, bonus action Hide.
2. **Subsequent rounds**: If Flurry recharges → spring out, Flurry, Hide again. If not → **stay hidden** and wait. After 3 consecutive failed recharges → spring out with Multiattack (can't afford to fall behind on damage). Stays in the open until Flurry recharges.
3. Grues attack *after* manglers, never before — manglers must strike first for Ambush.

### Retreat

- At ≤28 hp, but not immediately. Retreats the first time it gets to Hide as part of Flurry of Claws — then simply doesn't come back out.

## Star Spawn Hulk

- Brute (name says it all). DEX/WIS save proficiency; CON already +5 naturally. Resistant to nonmagical physical damage. A tank.
- **Reaping Arms** (recharge): Attacks every enemy within 10 ft, potentially knocking prone. The hulk **wants** to wade into the midst of enemies. Moves to hit ≥3 targets even if it provokes opportunity attacks.
- **Multiattack** (when Reaping Arms is on cooldown): Two slams. Both against one target = extra psychic damage + potential stun. Strong incentive to focus fire.
- **Target selection**: Enemies that can hurt it back — magic weapons, spell attacks. Prefers reachable targets over distant ones. A target knocked prone by one hulk is highly desirable for another.
- **Psychic Mirror**: Doesn't affect the hulk's own tactics, but allies (mind flayers, seers) can exploit it — psychic damage to the hulk is broadcast to every creature within 10 ft.

### Retreat

- At ≤54 hp, grudgingly. Continues attacking (Reaping Arms or Multiattack) while backing away, using debilitating effects to avoid opportunity strikes.
