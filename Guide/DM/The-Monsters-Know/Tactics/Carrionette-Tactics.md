# Carrionette Tactics

> [!summary]
> The carrionette is an animated toy (Ravenloft construct) whose entire strategy hinges on one hit plus two failed CHA saves to steal a victim's body via Soul Swap. It attacks from surprise using False Object and flees immediately if its opening fails. CR 1 — solo enemy for level 1 PCs only.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Construct, Small (Keith recommends Tiny)
- **High DEX** — shock attacker profile
- **INT 8** — barely recognizes when things go wrong
- **WIS 14** — good at choosing the right moment
- Movement 25 ft (slower than humanoid average)
- **False Object:** appears as ordinary toy until it acts

---

## Behavior
- Goal is **Soul Swap** — requires: 1 hit + 2 failed CHA saves (low DC)
- Average adventurer (+1 CHA save): ~25% chance both saves fail
- Compensates by waiting for victim to be **alone**, observing for lowest AC + CHA mod target
- Assessment takes at least 1 round per enemy (INT 8 = slow)

---

## Combat Sequence

### If Silver Needle Succeeds (Hit + Failed Save)
1. Surprise attack via **False Object** → **Silver Needle** on least-armored target
2. Target fails CHA save → slowed, baned, set up for Soul Swap
3. Next turn: **Soul Swap** → another CHA save
4. Success: carrionette enters victim's body; victim trapped in toy for 1 hour
5. Must **protect former body** — destroying it kills both

### If Opening Fails
1. **Dash** for nearest mousehole or pile of toys
2. Eats opportunity attacks — too slow for Disengage
3. Blends in with other toys, waits for another chance

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Scaling Up
> For higher-level PCs: significantly boost stats (DMG Ch. 9) or use as opportunistic attacker during another fight. Change size to **Tiny** (8-inch toy) for better narrative fit.

- Cleverest use: ignore PCs, **Soul Swap a beloved NPC** offscreen
- After swap, carrionette retains high WIS + CHA but low INT — shrewd but derpy
