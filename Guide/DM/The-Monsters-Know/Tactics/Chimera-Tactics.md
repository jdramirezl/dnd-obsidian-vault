# Chimera Tactics

> [!summary]
> The chimera is a chaotic evil brute that closes to melee as fast as possible, uses Fire Breath every time it recharges (repositioning regardless of opportunity attacks), and fights to the death — or toys with weakened prey before finishing them off. Stupid, brutal, unpredictable.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Monstrosity, Large, chaotic evil
- **STR and CON exceptionally high**, DEX average — pure brute
- **INT 3** — no target selection, attacks whatever's in front of it
- **WIS 14** — should have self-preservation, but flavor text says fights to the death
- **Fly 60 ft**, land 30 ft
- Darkvision, +8 Perception
- **Fire Breath:** 15-ft cone, recharge 5–6

---

## Multiattack (Spectacular)
- **With Fire Breath available:** Fire Breath + 2 melee attacks (Bite or Horns + Claws)
- **Without Fire Breath:** 3 melee attacks (Bite + Horns + Claws)
- Even dragons don't get to combine breath with melee attacks

### Head Attack Arcs (Flavor)
- **Goat head (Horns):** left, left-forward, forward
- **Dragon head (Bite/Fire Breath):** right, right-forward, forward
- **Lion head (Bite/Claws):** forward, left-forward, right-forward
- Fire Breath substitutes for Bite (not Horns — goats can't breathe fire)

---

## Combat Sequence
1. Enters combat flying → positions to catch **at least 2 targets** in 15-ft Fire Breath cone
2. **Fire Breath** every time it recharges, immediately
3. Repositions to maximize targets in cone — **ignores opportunity attacks** to do so
4. Between breaths: 3 melee attacks against whatever's in front of it
5. No target prioritization (INT 3)

---

## Retreat / Death
- Flavor text: fights to the death
- Keith's suggestion: at **≤45 hp**, INT check (DC 10 or half excess damage) to flee via flight + Dash
- Optional: chimera **toys with prey** — breaks off when all PCs ≤40% HP, returns in 30 min before short rest completes

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Terrain Advantage
> Flying speed > land speed, so the chimera moves by leaping and ignores difficult terrain. It may prefer to hunt in difficult terrain where prey is slowed.

- Fire Breath + 2 melee attacks in one Multiattack is extremely powerful for CR 6
- +8 Perception — don't try to sneak up on it
- Darkvision → often attacks at dusk or night, or dwells underground
- If offered food and treasure, might spare travelers (chaotic evil, not mindless evil)
