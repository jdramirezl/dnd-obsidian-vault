# Wights and Revenants Tactics

## Wight

Intelligent undead warriors with above-average physical stats and decent mental stats. Retain combat instincts from life — fight with weapons and tactics, not mindless aggression.

### Key Features

- **Life Drain**: Melee attack reducing target's max HP. Targets killed rise as zombies under wight's control at midnight
- **Sunlight Sensitivity**: Disadvantage on attacks and Perception in sunlight → strictly nocturnal or subterranean
- **Multiattack**: Two longsword or two longbow attacks; can substitute Life Drain for one
- Resistant to necrotic and nonmagical/non-silvered physical damage
- Immune to poison and exhaustion

### Combat Behavior

- Fights like a disciplined soldier — uses terrain, flanking, and zombie minions
- Opens with longbow if possible, closes to melee when advantageous
- Prioritizes Life Drain against low-CON targets (casters, rogues) to create zombie reinforcements
- Commands zombie minions to screen and absorb attacks
- Retreats when seriously wounded — self-preservation instinct intact

## Revenant

See dedicated note: [[Revenant-Tactics]]

Driven by vengeance against its killer. Regenerates 10 hp/round. Effectively unkillable — returns in a new body 24 hours after destruction. Ignores all other creatures unless they stand between it and its target.
