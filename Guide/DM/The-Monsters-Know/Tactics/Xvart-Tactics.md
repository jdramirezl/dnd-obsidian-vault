# Xvart Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Low-Strength, average-Con anti-brutes — averse to melee slugfests. Seek strength in numbers and animal allies (giant rats, giant bats). Low Int/Wis: can't adapt, but cowardly — may flee prematurely. Low Cunning grants Disengage as bonus action (instinct, not discipline). Darkvision + Stealth proficiency.

## Encounter Composition

- Minimum **2 xvarts per PC** + 1 animal ally per 2 xvarts
- Giant rats: Pack Tactics synergy. Giant bats: tougher.

## Kidnapping Tactics (Default)

1. **Attack only at twilight/night** — minimize detection, gain unseen-attacker advantage
2. Send animal allies in first to distract (rats attack in pairs for Pack Tactics advantage)
3. Xvarts move in on **round 2**, still hidden — if spotted before attacking, **do not attack**, run and Hide again
4. **Overbearing Pack** → shove prone (advantage if ally within 5 ft. of target; effective +3/+4 despite -1 Str)
5. Second xvart immediately **grapples** prone target (advantage from prone + adjacent)
6. Drag victim away — auxiliaries use **Help** action; throw nets/sacks over grappled victims
7. DM discretion: combine net's restrained effect with xvarts' advantage on Athletics to resist escape

## Xvart Warlock Changes

When a warlock leads, xvarts seek **magical treasure**, not victims:
- 1 rat per target, then 2 xvarts attack normally with shortswords (better expected damage than shove combo)
- Both xvarts **Disengage** (bonus) + retreat 15 ft. — pursuing target eats rat OA
- Warlock casts *mage armor* first, then watches for finishing blows (Raxivort's Blessing: 4 temp hp on kill)
- Offensive spells: *burning hands* (2+ targets, ~21 expected damage), *scorching ray* (120 ft. range, half damage)
- Saves one spell slot for *expeditious retreat* (cover available) or *invisibility* (open terrain)

## Retreat

- Xvarts: moderately wounded (≤ 4 hp) → Disengage (bonus) + Dash (action)
- Xvart warlock: moderately wounded (≤ 15 hp) → *expeditious retreat* or *invisibility* + flee
- Grappled victim breaks free → all involved xvarts flee immediately
