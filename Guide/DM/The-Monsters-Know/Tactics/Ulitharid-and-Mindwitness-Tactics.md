# Ulitharid and Mindwitness Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Ulitharid and Mindwitness Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/ulitharid-and-mindwitness-tactics/)

**Bestiary:** [[Mind-Flayer]]

---

## Ulitharid

Elite, extra-large mind flayers with better ability scores, additional psionic spells, 2-mile telepathy, and more tentacles. Extend colony influence with elder brains via Psionic Hub.

### Profile
- Higher Str/Con than normal mind flayers, but extraordinary mental abilities still dominate
- Predisposed toward **spellcasting and Mind Blast** over melee
- Higher Con than Dex → willing to **charge forward**; leads from the front (behind thralls)
- Lacks Deception/Persuasion — the muscle, not the face

### Psionic Spells (1/day each)
- *Confusion* → pair of tough front-liners cutting through thralls
- *Eyebite* → Sleep on tough front-liner; Sicken on skirmisher; Frighten on spellcaster
- *Feeblemind* → non-wizard casters (wizards resist Int saves too well)
- *Mass suggestion* → pre-combat: "Nothing to see here" or "The real enemy is in that other room"
- *Telekinesis* → deliver foes to brutes, block movement, yoink magic items (only vs stunned/paralyzed or Str mod ≤0)
- *Project image* → wild goose chases
- *Scrying* → spy on creatures sensed miles out (not combat)

### Combat Sequence
1. **Mind Blast** whenever available vs 6+ enemies; aim cone over allies via *levitate* or height
2. Otherwise stand behind thralls, use **Tentacles** (10 ft reach) past them
3. Successful stun → push forward, **Extract Brain**
4. Kill → drop corpse, fall back; takes **13+ hp** while extracting → steps back, drags target behind thrall line

### Retreat
- **Moderately wounded (≤88 hp)** → Insight check for parley; if none, defensive stance
- **Severely wounded (≤50 hp)** → *plane shift* to exit

## Mindwitness

A beholder abducted and modified by mind flayers. Loses Int, Con/Cha, half HP, Antimagic Cone. Gains Multiattack and Telepathic Hub.

### Eye Rays (random selection, mindful targeting)
- Aversion/Psychic Ray → front-line warriors
- Fear Ray → enemies on periphery
- Slowing Ray → front-liners and supporters
- Stunning/Telekinetic Ray → squishy backline

### Melee
- Unlike beholders, **willing to float down** and engage a single melee opponent
- **Tentacles first** (stun on failed save) → **Bite** with advantage on stunned target

### Retreat
- While part of colony: **never flees**
- Colony wiped out + time with other beings → self-preservation returns → exits when **moderately wounded (≤52 hp)**
