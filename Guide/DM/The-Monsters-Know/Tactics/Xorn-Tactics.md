# Xorn Tactics

> [!note] Book-Exclusive Content
> The xorn tactics analysis appears only in Keith Ammann's book *The Monsters Know What They're Doing* (Saga Press, 2019) and was never published on the blog. The URL `themonstersknow.com/xorn-tactics/` does not exist.

## Key Traits (from stat block)

- **Earth Glide** — moves through unworked earth/stone without disturbing it
- **Treasure Sense** — pinpoints precious metals and gems within 60 ft. by scent
- **Stone Camouflage** — advantage on Stealth in rocky terrain
- Burrow 20 ft., walk 20 ft. — slow but can ambush from below
- Multiattack: 3 Claws + 1 Bite
- CR 5, Medium elemental, neutral

## Likely Tactical Profile

- **Ambush predator** — uses Earth Glide + Stone Camouflage to lurk in stone, emerges to attack
- Motivated by **hunger for gems and precious metals**, not malice
- Treasure Sense draws it to adventurers carrying valuables
- Slow speed means it relies on surprise rather than pursuit
- If it can't get what it wants through intimidation, it attacks; if seriously hurt, it Earth Glides away through solid stone where pursuers can't follow
