# Star Spawn Emissary Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/star-spawn-emissary-tactics/) | **Date**: April 12, 2023

---

## Overview

Two-phase boss (CR 23, 55,000 XP total). Arrives disguised as a Small/Medium creature. Reveals true form when the alien scheme is discovered or unstoppable.

---

## Lesser Star Spawn Emissary

- Extraordinary abilities across the board except merely exceptional DEX. Peak in **INT** → prefers mental attacks (Psychic Orb, Psychic Lash) over physical (Lashing Maw).
- CON > DEX → willing to absorb hits rather than dodge, but not at the cost of disadvantage on ranged Psychic Orb.
- **Preferred position**: 15 ft from nearest foe (Lashing Maw reach). Flies — hovers ~10 ft up, 10 ft away (14 ft hypotenuse).

### Change Shape

- Full action → no Multiattack in surprise round. Alternatively, attack in disguised form (stats are identical regardless of form) for shock value — but then stuck in that form.

### Target Priority

1. **Wizards, sorcerers, warlocks** — evocation spells requiring DEX/CON saves (emissary's weakest saves). These chip Legendary Resistance fast.
2. Clerics dealing radiant damage at range.
3. Ranged weapon attackers (secondary — only if outdamaging casters).
- Pelt priority targets with **Psychic Orbs** between turns via legendary actions.

### Lashing Maw vs Psychic Orb

- Lashing Maw deals more expected damage vs AC 18 (20 vs 14), but reserved for melee threats that close distance.

### Legendary Actions

- **Psychic Orb** (1 action): Default — keep pelting priority targets.
- **Teleportation Maw** (2 actions): Evade melee attackers who fly close, or blitz a caster within 45 ft.
- **Psychic Lash** (3 actions): Nuclear option. 2× Psychic Orb damage + stun. Only vs targets with WIS save ≤+6 (ideally ≤+2), within 30 ft, dealing ≥54 damage/turn. Study PC sheets in advance.

### Retreat

- **Fights until destroyed** — dissolves into goo and re-forms as the greater emissary.

---

## Greater Star Spawn Emissary

- Same contour but DEX much lower, everything else higher. Gains CON save proficiency but **AC significantly reduced** → must split attention between casters and melee.

### Unearthly Bile (Recharge 5–6)

- Huge acid damage over huge area + **spawns gibbering mouthers** from failed saves. Use whenever available.
- Target ≥6 opponents (or all, whichever is less). Include both casters and front-liners if possible.
- **Do not include itself** in AoE — DEX saves are its #1 weakness. If forced to choose: drench whichever group has worse DEX saves.

### Multiattack

- Focus Psychic Orbs on lowest-AC targets (usually casters). Switch to front-liners once casters are down.

### Legendary Actions

- **Attack** (1 action): Psychic Orb or Lashing Maw + short-range teleport. Teleport away from melee opponents *about* to attack (watches initiative order).
- **Warp Space** (2 actions): Good vs monks, rangers, rogues — damage + difficult terrain slowing.
- **Mind Cloud** (3 actions): 30 ft radius, targets WIS. Useless vs clerics/druids. Count foes within 30 ft (excluding WIS save ≥+8) + buff spells on them + debuffs on emissary. If sum ≥6, use it.

### Retreat

- **Fights to the death**. Give your players the satisfaction of the final kill.

## Encounter Note

- Lesser + Greater = one CR 23 monster fought in succession. Hard for four level 16–18 PCs, Deadly below that. Gibbering mouthers are factored into the CR.
