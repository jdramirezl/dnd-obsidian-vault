# Planescape Faction Agent Tactics, Part 1

## Bleak Cabal Void Soother (CR 3)

### Tactical Summary

Existential nihilists who are agents of mercy. Shock troop profile: STR primary offense, DEX primary defense, high WIS for spellcasting. Force damage on Mace and Void Bolt.

### Combat Behavior

- **Preferred attack:** Void Bolt (all force damage, 90 ft range) over Mace (mostly bludgeoning). Many supernatural creatures resist nonmagical bludgeoning
- **Typical script:**
  - Round 1: Advance 30 ft, Void Bolt (action), Soothing Word (bonus action, heals ally)
  - Round 2: Same
  - Round 3: Close to melee, Soothing Word (bonus), Mace (action) to finish
- **Calm Emotions (1/day):** Two situational uses:
  1. Clear charm/fright from an ally who can otherwise handle themselves
  2. Stop a humanoid attacker with very low CHA (7 or less) to administer aid without interference

### Retreat Conditions

- Uncommon courage — stays under fire longer than prudent
- Retreats when seriously wounded (≤22 hp)
- Disengage or Dodge to withdraw (not Dash)

## Society of Sensation Muse (CR 3)

### Tactical Summary

CHA primary offense, DEX primary defense. Spellcaster avoiding melee. Provides experiences to win converts. Unlikely to start fights — may mess with Fated shakers pushing people around.

### Combat Behavior

- **Beguiling Resonance:** Psychic damage at 90 ft range + chance of disadvantage on target's next attack
- **Enchanting Presence (bonus):** Chance to charm all living creatures within 30 ft
- **Hypnotic Pattern (1/day):** Last resort AoE

### Offensive Scenario (messing with a Fated thug)

1. Open with Beguiling Resonance from 50-70 ft (surprise, disrupts next attack)
2. When thug turns attention to muse: Enchanting Presence to charm bystanders into bodyguards
3. If assailant breaks through: Hypnotic Pattern, then Dash away

### Defensive Scenario (attacked)

1. Enchanting Presence first (may charm attacker directly)
2. If fails: Hypnotic Pattern
3. If both fail: run, lobbing Beguiling Resonance while retreating
4. Moderately wounded (≤30 hp): stop attacking, just Dash

### Notes

- High INT + WIS: good at assessing enemies and choosing battles
- Excellent Stealth + Perception: effective spy if outmatched

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Void soothers are combat medics — they rush *into* fights to heal, not to kill. Muses are social manipulators who turn crowds into shields. Both are CR 3 but play very differently.
