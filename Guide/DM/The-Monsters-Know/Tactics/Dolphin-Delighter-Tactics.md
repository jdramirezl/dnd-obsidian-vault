## Tactical Summary

The dolphin delighter is a fey dolphin — a teleporting, telepathic aquatic creature from the Feywild. It is unaligned and generally friendly, serving as a faithful ally against forces of gloom and brutality under the waves.

## Stat Block Overview

- **AC 14** (natural armor), **HP 27**, **Speed** 0 ft land / 60 ft swim
- **Abilities:** Decent STR and DEX, moderate CON, low INT, moderate WIS, high CHA
- **Hold Breath:** 20 minutes
- **Telepathy:** Can communicate telepathically
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves vs. charmed, magic can't put it to sleep
- **Limited Teleportation:** Can teleport short distances — key tactical feature

## Combat Behavior

### General Approach
- Dolphin delighters are **allies**, not enemies — PCs are more likely to fight alongside them
- If forced into combat, uses hit-and-run aquatic tactics
- **Teleportation** allows repositioning without provoking opportunity attacks
- 60 ft swim speed makes it extremely mobile in water

### As an Ally
- Telepathically coordinates with PCs
- Uses teleportation to flank enemies or rescue drowning allies
- Faithful against forces of darkness — won't abandon allies

### If Threatened
- Teleports away from danger
- 60 ft swim speed allows rapid escape
- Low HP (27) means it avoids prolonged combat
- Retreats when moderately wounded — self-preservation is strong

## Key Decision Points

| Situation | Response |
|---|---|
| Allied PCs in aquatic combat | Coordinate via telepathy, flank with teleportation |
| Ally drowning or restrained | Teleport adjacent, assist |
| Directly threatened | Teleport away, reposition |
| Seriously wounded | Flee at 60 ft swim speed |

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Best used as an NPC ally in aquatic encounters — gives the party a telepathic coordinator
> - Teleportation makes it nearly impossible to pin down in water
> - Pair with aquatic threats (sahuagin, sea hags) for a memorable underwater encounter
> - The "delighter" in the name is earned — play it as joyful and encouraging
