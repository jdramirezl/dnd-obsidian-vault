# Unicorn Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Celestial guardian — surprisingly formidable. Legendary creature with exceptional STR, high DEX and CON, very high WIS. Base speed 50 ft. Magic Resistance. Not resistant to physical damage, moderate AC — less durable than stats suggest.

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

| Action | Cost | Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Hooves** | 1 | Single hoof attack on another creature's turn |
| **Shimmering Shield** | 2 | +2 AC to self or nearby creature until end of next turn — use proactively (high WIS foresees hits) |
| **Heal Self** | 3 | Restores avg 11 hp — use when moderately wounded (≤46 hp) |

## Combat Pattern

- **Charge** → horn attack with bonus piercing + STR save or prone → hoof attack (advantage if prone)
- **Alternating rounds**: Charge → Disengage + run → Charge → Disengage. Cannot risk opportunity attacks with low AC
- Extensive use of Shimmering Shield despite the 2-LA cost

## Innate Spellcasting

- **Entangle** (1/day): 20-ft radius difficult terrain + restrained (STR save). Use when 4+ enemies in AoE. Still has 30 ft movement after difficult terrain to Charge
- **Dispel evil and good** (1/day): Only vs extraplanar allies of PCs. Break Enchantment if ally charmed/frightened/possessed; Dismissal vs single summoned creature; sustain for disadvantage vs multiple
- At-will spells (*detect evil and good*, *druidcraft*, *pass without trace*): Pre-combat only

## Target Selection (Very High WIS)

1. Evil foes first, then chaotic
2. Ranged/melee attackers before spellcasters (Magic Resistance makes casters less threatening)

## Defensive Actions

- **Healing Touch**: Primarily for guarded creatures
- **Teleport** (self + up to 3 willing within 5 ft, up to 1 mile): Escape when seriously wounded (≤26 hp) or evacuate wards
- **Pass without trace**: Concealment when overmatched + 50 ft speed to stay out of reach

## Encounter Design

- Never alone if combat expected — always enough allies to make it Deadly
- Never flees without ensuring guarded creatures can also escape
- **If PCs are about to kill a unicorn**: Escalate dramatically. Only 1,800 XP for CR 5. *You attacked a unicorn, you reprobates.*
