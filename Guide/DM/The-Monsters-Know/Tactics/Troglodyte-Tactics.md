## General Notes (Inferred from Stat Block)

Troglodytes are savage, degenerate reptilian humanoids dwelling in the shallow Underdark. Chaotic evil, constantly at war.

### Key Features
- **Stench** — creatures within 5 ft must DC 12 Con save or be poisoned until start of next turn; on success, immune 1 hour
- **Sunlight Sensitivity** — disadvantage on attacks and Perception in sunlight
- **Chameleon Skin** — advantage on Stealth to hide
- **Multiattack** — one Bite + one Claw, or three Javelins

### Likely Tactics
- Ambush predators using Chameleon Skin in cavern environments, then swarm
- Fight in packs; Stench debuffs nearby enemies
- Avoid sunlight — strictly subterranean or nocturnal raiders
- Low Intelligence (6) — simple, direct: charge and overwhelm
- Likely fight to the death defending territory; may flee if raiding party is badly mauled
- Often serve as thralls to more powerful Underdark creatures (mind flayers, etc.)
