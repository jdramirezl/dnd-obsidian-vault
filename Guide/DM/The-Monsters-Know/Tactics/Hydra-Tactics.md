# Hydra Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/hydra-tactics/) | **Date**: July 14, 2017
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Hydra]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- Straightforward brute with extraordinary STR and CON. Extremely stupid (INT 2), indiscriminate in target selection. Only attack: one bite per head (starts with 5).
- Running a hydra encounter is primarily **accounting**: track damage, fire damage, and current head count. Destroying a head without cauterizing → two sprout back. Extra heads = extra opportunity attacks.

## Positioning

- Each round, moves to where it can attack **as many targets as possible**, up to the number of heads it has.
- Heads have 10-ft reach — targets don't need to be immediately adjacent.
- Not smart enough to avoid OAs → moves to optimal location within 30 ft, then attacks with every head.

## Attack Priority

1. Attack every opponent within reach with one head each.
2. Leftover heads go to the **biggest opponent** first, then second-biggest, etc.
3. **Exception**: If an enemy severs a head and two grow back, both new heads attack *that* enemy.

## Limitations

- Advantage on saves vs various conditions. Can't tell a spellcaster from any other target, can't distinguish magic weapons except by how much they hurt.

## Retreat

- Seriously wounded at **≤68 hp**.
- Doesn't flee exactly, but **retreats instinctively**: Disengage action (snapping and feinting with jaws), backs away to nearest body of water, dives, swims away.
