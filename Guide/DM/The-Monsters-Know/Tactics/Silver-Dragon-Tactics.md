# Silver Dragon Tactics

> [!summary]
> Silver dragons are the sweethearts of the metallic family — friendly, knowledge-hungry, and genuinely kind. They may not even realize they're in danger until combat breaks out, at which point they use Paralyzing Breath to freeze enemies and flee. Cold Breath is reserved for clearly evil foes. Notably, the MM forgot to give silver dragons an alternative movement mode.

---

## Silver-Specific Traits
- **No alternative movement mode** — the MM editors appear to have forgotten (unlike every other metallic dragon)
- **Cold Breath** (damaging, cone) + **Paralyzing Breath** (nonlethal, cone — paralyzes targets)
- **Social skills:** interested in exchanging knowledge
- **Personality:** Sweethearts. Young silver dragons are genuinely kind and eager to share knowledge
- Adult and ancient silver dragons can **Change Shape**

---

## By Age

### Wyrmling
- Friendly — may walk right up to adventurers and try talking in Draconic
- If attacked: **Paralyzing Breath once** → fly away at max speed
- Only uses Cold Breath if already damaged; only Bites if cornered

### Young
- Sweetheart — initiates social interaction, interested in exchanging knowledge
- **May not even realize they're in danger** until combat actually breaks out
- When combat starts: **Paralyzing Breath** → fly away
- Uses Cold Breath only if already hurt; melee only if cornered

### Adult (Legendary)
- Would rather talk, but **will start a fight** if PCs seem to have evil intent
- Opens with nonlethal means: Fog Cloud (lair action) + Paralyzing Breath
- Uses **Multiattack (Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite) before Cold Breath**
- Cold Breath is a last-ditch trump card

#### Lair Actions (Pick 1 per round, never same twice)

| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Fog Cloud** | Cloud action (obscures vision) | Everyone — nonlethal opener |
| **Cold Wind** | Push action (deals damage, does NOT inhibit movement — exception) | Anyone in range |

##### Fizban's New Lair Actions

| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Icy Fortress** | Movement restrictor + defensive (ice walls) | Approaching melee fighters |
| **Stunning Brilliance** | Debilitator (stun, AoE) | 4+ targets — stunned creatures can't act |
| **Twilight's End** | Debilitator (dispels magical effects) | Buffed enemies — strips protective spells |

### Ancient
- Same tactics as adult; **Change Shape** available
- Prefers disguise outside lair — human form among humans, elven form among elves

## Combat Personality

> [!important] Naive Kindness
> Silver dragons are the most likely metallic dragon to be caught off guard. Young silvers may not recognize danger until it's too late.

> [!important] Retreat at ≤70% hp
> Unless defending their lair — Disengage → fly away at max speed.

---

## Quick Reference: Combat Flow
1. **Pre-combat:** Eager to exchange knowledge, genuinely friendly
2. **Hostile intent (or surprise combat):** Fog Cloud (lair) → Paralyzing Breath
3. **If PCs persist:** Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite
4. **Last resort:** Cold Breath (cone)
5. **≤70% hp:** Disengage → fly away
6. **In disguise:** Talks way out → Change Shape to escape

> [!tip] DM Note
> Silver dragons make excellent recurring NPCs in disguise. They genuinely enjoy humanoid company and may befriend the party without revealing their true nature. Paralyzing Breath is one of the most powerful nonlethal breath weapons — paralyzed creatures auto-fail STR/DEX saves and attacks against them have advantage.
