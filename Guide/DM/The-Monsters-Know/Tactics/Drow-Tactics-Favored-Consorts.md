# Drow Tactics: Favored Consorts

> [Source](https://www.themonstersknow.com/drow-tactics-favored-consorts/)
> Bestiary: [[Elves-Drow]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- Arcane adviser hybrid: long-range caster + sniper + shock attacker; extraordinary DEX, exceptional INT/CHA
- Not a zealot — a survivor; amenable to parley, will sell out its side for protection
- Very few concentration spells; usually opens with *haste* and keeps it running
- War Magic: when casting a spell as action, make one weapon attack as bonus action

## Combat Behavior

- **Round 1**: *haste* (on self or shadowblade/house captain/elite warrior); two extra attacks on casting turn via haste action + War Magic bonus
- **Under haste**: Multiattack (3x Scimitar, each base + 4d8 poison) as main action; extra Attack or Disengage as haste action; *misty step* for repositioning
- **Scimitar rider**: imposes disadvantage on target's next save vs consort's spells — hit 3 different targets, then AoE next turn
- **Spell priority**: *chain lightning* (6th, 4 targets) > *cone of cold* (5th, 6+) > *fireball* (4th+, 4+); *shatter* (2nd) for 2 targets or high-CON foes
- If cold-resistant targets (INT 18, will know after first *cone of cold*): boost *fireball* with 5th-level slots
- *Counterspell*/*shield* in reserve; always save one 4th-level slot for *dimension door*

## Key Decision Points

- Observes all enemies round 1: AoE vs tanks/support; Multiattack vs glass-cannon back row; crossbow vs mobile enemies; *cone of cold* vs everybody
- *Haste* on self vs ally depends on who benefits more from extra action
- Killer combo: Multiattack 3 different targets (disadvantage on saves) → next turn blast all three with *chain lightning*/*cone of cold*

## Retreat Conditions

- Plays it safe; *dimension door* when moderately wounded (≤157 hp), as soon as no important drow is watching
- Will sell out its side for a deal offering protection + escape

> [!tip] The scimitar-into-AoE combo is devastating: hit 3 targets on one turn (giving each disadvantage on saves), then blast all three next turn. Requires targets close enough to reach with Disengage + *misty step*.
