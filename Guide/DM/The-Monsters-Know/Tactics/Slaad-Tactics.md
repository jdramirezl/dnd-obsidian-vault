# Slaad Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/slaad-tactics/) | **Related**: [[Slaadi]]

---

## Overview
Beings of pure chaos from Limbo; vaguely humanoid salamanders. High-challenge monsters — even one slaad is a threat that needs squelching pronto due to exponential reproduction. Resistant to acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder; advantage on saves vs. magic.

## Slaad Tadpole
- Tiny, weak, frisky; high Dex, Stealth proficiency
- **Goal**: Grow up — flees immediately upon emerging from host using Dash
- Bites only if grabbed; behaves like a cornered rat
- Use Chase rules (DMG 252–54); chaos nature causes extra complications (roll twice on 11–20)
- Apply Stealth vs. Perception contest when it leaves field of vision

## Red & Blue Slaadi
- Dumb brutes; very high Str/Con; vicious toe-to-toe melee
- **Goal**: Deposit eggs/bacteriophages in humanoid hosts via claw attacks
- Multiattack: claw/claw/bite; target nearest humanoid not yet clawed
- Once clawed, assume success, move to next target; attack same target again only if both claws miss
- After clawing every nearby humanoid: Dash away seeking new group
- Indiscriminate target selection; no retreat behavior

## Green Slaad
- Sentient spellcaster; primary goal is discovering key to metamorphosis into gray slaad
- Uses Shapechanger to assume inconspicuous humanoid forms — but behavior is glaringly weird (only speaks Slaad)
- Fights only if slaad-nature is discovered; primary combat goal is escape, not killing
- **Escape sequence**:
  1. Turn invisible
  2. Revert to slaad form (while invisible — shape change isn't attack or spell)
  3. If pursued despite invisibility: *fireball* + *fear* on pursuers (fireball ends invisibility)
  4. *Mage hand* for micro-poltergeist mayhem (slam doors, prod animals, throw cabbages)
  5. Dash until escaped or cornered
- **If cornered**: Fight with staff + bite; use *invisibility* again if escape path appears
- Always prefers fleeing over fighting; sets lots of stuff on fire when panicking
- **Damage comparison** (slaad form): claw/claw/bite = 26 avg > staff/staff/bite = 33 avg > 2x Hurl Flame = 16 avg (adjusted)

## Gray Slaad
- Reached pinnacle of natural metamorphosis; relishes fighting; usually on specific mission of mayhem
- *Invisibility* at will — uses to approach and get the jump, not to escape
- *Tongues* to disguise inability to speak Common
- *Major image* at will — fake personae, distractions during chases; may disguise as VIP with illusory bodyguard (or vice versa)
- *Fly* for skirmishing: swoop in, greatsword/greatsword/bite, soar back out of melee reach (AC 18, indifferent to OAs)
- *Fireball* twice per day
- Favors skirmishing and large single hits over toe-to-toe; prefers greatsword over claws
- Low Wisdom → random target selection unless on mission against specific people
- *Fear* only against bystanders, not intended targets
- **Retreat**: ≤50 hp → *plane shift* to escape

## Death Slaad
- Leads invasion forces of red/blue slaadi — potentially deadly even for top-tier adventurers
- Brute, not skirmisher; *fly* to close quickly with targets
- Bizarrely high Charisma — *intriguingly* weird in humanoid form
- *Cloudkill* (1/day): Specifically for killing people the death slaad wants dead (vs. *fireball* for general mayhem)
- Prefers greatsword over claws; fights in slaad form for bite in Multiattack
- **Target priority**: Zeroes in on lawful characters; after defeating one, moves to next lawful PC; then random
- **Retreat**: ≤68 hp → *plane shift* to escape
