# Grick Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/grick-tactics/) | **Date**: September 9, 2016
> **Book**: Monster Manual

---

## Overview

- **Grayish subterranean snake-worm** with beaked mouth and four clawed tentacles.
- High Str and Dex, average Con → **ambush predator**.
- Primarily nocturnal (darkvision). Stone Camouflage gives advantage on Stealth in rocky terrain.
- Inexplicable resistance to nonmagical physical damage.

## Ambush Tactics

1. Hides in rocky terrain (caves, crevices, rocky outcrops).
2. Waits for target to draw near.
3. **Attacks from hiding with advantage**.
4. Multiattack: Tentacle first → if hit, follows with Beak. Must grab before it can bite.

### Grick Alpha

- Same grab-then-bite requirement, plus a **Tail** attack.
- Much tougher, but same tactical pattern.

## Committed to Melee

- 30 ft movement + 30 ft climb speed.
- **Cannot re-hide effectively** once revealed — opponent can simply back away.
- Once cover is blown, committed to melee until it wins or retreats.

## Damage Resistance

- Resistant to nonmagical bludgeoning/piercing/slashing.
- **Persistent predator** — ordinary weapons won't deter it.
- Only **magical or elemental damage** makes it back off.

## Retreat

- **Grick**: Retreats after ≥9 hp of magical/elemental damage.
- **Grick Alpha**: Retreats after ≥23 hp of magical/elemental damage.
- High Wisdom = strong self-preservation instinct.

## After the Kill

- Wraps tentacles around unconscious victim and **drags it to its lair** to devour.
- Aboveground: heads for a cave, burrow, or concealed spot.
- Won't keep fighting for fighting's sake — kills to eat, then wants to eat in peace.
