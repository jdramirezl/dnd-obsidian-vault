# Yuan-ti Tactics

## Shared Traits

All yuan-ti: innate *suggestion*, magic resistance (no extra fear of spellcasters), darkvision. Caste society — more snakelike = higher rank.

## Yuan-ti Purebloods (CR 1)

- Average physical stats, elevated Dex; high Int and Cha — mental-first combatants
- Proficient in Deception + Stealth; *poison spray* 3/day, *animal friendship* (snakes)
- **Opening**: cloaked/hooded, cast *suggestion* at 30 ft. on lowest-Wis PC: "Come with us, we'll help you"
- If *suggestion* succeeds → lead PCs to settlement, overwhelm with numbers + grappling, prep sacrifice
- If fails → melee (2x scimitar) or ranged (poisoned arrows); stay where combat starts
- Retreat: seriously wounded (≤ 16 hp) → Dash away (no Disengage training)

## Yuan-ti Malisons (CR 3)

- High Str, Dex, Cha, Int — commanders and shock troops
- Can Shapechange to snake form — **never do this in combat** (no advantage, lose equipment)
- **Type 3 (snake lower body) is best**: Constrict grapples + restrains, hands free → next turn 2x scimitar with advantage
- Ranged: 2x longbow (+4, 1d8+2 + 2d6 poison) = 14 expected damage/turn — superior to melee at distance
- Retreat: seriously wounded (≤ 26 hp) → **Disengage** then retreat (disciplined)

## Combined Encounter (3 purebloods + 2 type 3 malisons)

1. Malisons hide 120 ft. from purebloods
2. Purebloods use *suggestion* on PCs
3. If *suggestion* fails/breaks → purebloods draw scimitars
4. Malisons begin **sniping** — target elves and barbarians first (best charm resistance)
5. When all purebloods moderately wounded or any one seriously wounded → malisons Dash in
6. Malisons: Constrict first, then scimitar attacks; if PC breaks grapple, don't re-attempt Constrict on that PC

## Yuan-ti Abominations (CR 7)

- Giant serpent with humanoid arms. Spectacular stats (Str 19 standout)
- Constrict + 3x scimitar with advantage (+7, 87.75% hit vs. AC 15) — devastating boss
- *Fear* 1/day: used only when (a) protecting sacred place (cast first round) or (b) losing the fight (cast at ≤ 88 hp)
- Very intelligent — issues commands to allies, directs focus on strong/tough PCs, effective ranged fighters, radiant/necrotic damage dealers, clerics/paladins (sectarian loathing)
- Exploits choke points, high ground, strategic terrain
- **Monologues while fighting** (Cha 18 fanatical boss energy)
- Protecting sacred place → fights to death; allies fetch reinforcements when abomination moderately wounded
- Otherwise: seriously wounded (≤ 50 hp) → Disengage + retreat
