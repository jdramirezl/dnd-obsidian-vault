# Kruthik Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Kruthik Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/kruthik-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary

Kruthiks are insectoid tunnel-dwellers that just want to eat and breed. High AC, burrowing/climbing, darkvision, tremorsense, Keen Smell, Pack Tactics, and Tunneler. They fight in coordinated groups using tunnel networks for ambush and reinforcement.

## Combat Behavior

### Young Kruthiks
- Require **3-to-1** vs Medium prey (6-to-1 vs Large); melee only (Stab)
- Must swarm in open areas; not mature enough to reliably dig ambush boltholes
- When hunting: keep attacking past 0 HP until prey is dead (devouring)

### Adult Kruthiks
- Squads of **3–5 minimum**; never fewer than 3
- **Tunnel ambush:** One baits enemies in. Others dig boltholes on sides/ceiling and Hide. When pursuer reaches them, retreating kruthik turns and all attack. Rear kruthik adds ranged Spikes.
- **Open cavern:** 50/50 split — half melee, half Spikes from 15–20 ft. Reinforcement waves from tunnels max at 2 ranged per 1 melee.
- When hunting: one alone attacks Medium prey if hungry; two for Large. May knock to 0 HP nonlethally and drag to hive lord.

### Kruthik Hive Lord
- **Never outside the nest.** Brute melee fighter alongside adult soldiers.
- **Acid Spray** when hitting 2+ enemies; otherwise Stab/Stab Multiattack.
- Switches to **Spike ranged** vs ranged/magic threats while soldiers run interference.
- **Smart enough to parley** when hopelessly outmatched — vocalizations, gestures, floor-tapping.

## Key Decision Points

1. **Young vs. adult response:** Young only fight in open areas with 3:1 numbers. Adults use tunnel ambush.
2. **Hive lord Acid Spray vs. Multiattack:** Acid Spray only when 2+ enemies in cone.
3. **Parley:** Hive lord may negotiate — food supply + territorial respect for peace.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Young:** Dodge + retreat at **≤6 HP** — climb walls, burrow through ceiling.
- **Adults:** Dodge + retreat at **≤15 HP** — existing tunnels or burrow upward.
- **Adults near hive lord:** Fight to the death as rearguard.
- **Hive lord:** Withdraws at **≤40 HP** — Dodges and burrows to safety.
- Retreating kruthiks Dodge (not Disengage) — high AC makes this sufficient.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Design lairs with tunnel mouths in every wall, ceiling, and floor. Reinforcement waves create escalating tension. The hive lord's willingness to parley is a great RP opportunity — but communication is extremely difficult (Kruthik language only).
