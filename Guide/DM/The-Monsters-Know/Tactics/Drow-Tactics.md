# Drow Tactics: Arachnomancers

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/drow-tactics/) | **Date**: December 12, 2018
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Elves-Drow]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- **CR 15 warlock** that shapeshifts between humanoid and Large giant spider (bonus action). All leveled spells cast at 5th level, limited to 3 slots per encounter.
- **Long-range spellslinger** — extraordinary Int, very high Dex/Cha. Stays out of melee.
- **Ambush specialist**: Perception + Stealth proficiency. Knows exactly which glass cannon to smash first.
- **120 ft darkvision + blindsight**: Darkness is advantageous but problematic for most allies (no blindsight).
- **Spider Climb + Web Walker in both forms**: Incentive to spam webs across the battle zone.

## Best Concentration Spells

- **Web**: Restrained → advantage on all attacks. Best synergy source despite being 2nd level.
- **Hold monster (5th)**: Paralyzes one target. Devastating with Poisonous Touch/Bite follow-up.
- **Conjure animals**: 16 giant wolf spiders (with ally webs) or 4 giant spiders.
- **Insect plague**: Area damage. Best as second part of combo after allies restrain targets.
- **Fear**: Hobbles non-spellcaster counterattacks in confined spaces.

## Non-Concentration Attacks

- **Chill touch (3d8)**: vs single spellcaster — suppresses healing.
- **Eldritch blast (3d10)**: vs targets of opportunity or groups lacking healing.
- **Poisonous Touch / Bite**: Self-defense or shock attacks only.

## Game Plans

| Situation | Plan |
|---|---|
| Single fragile spellcaster | Hold monster → rush in with Poisonous Touch/Bite |
| Multiple spellcasters | Web them → advantage on all attacks |
| Allies can web the area | Allies web → insect plague on restrained → cantrips |
| Allies web (variant) | Allies web → conjure 16 giant wolf spiders → cantrips |
| Non-spellcasters, confined | Fear → allies melee → cantrip potshots |

## Retreat Conditions

- **Zealots but intelligent** — objective comes first. Retreat if objective unachievable.
- **Abandons allies** without hesitation (chaotic evil, hierarchical contempt).
- Escape via etherealness or dimension door (brings one VIP with dimension door).

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Plan spell choices before the session. Web is deceptively powerful as concentration despite being 2nd level. Giant spider form's non-concentration web attack is key for combos. Always target enemy spellcasters first.
