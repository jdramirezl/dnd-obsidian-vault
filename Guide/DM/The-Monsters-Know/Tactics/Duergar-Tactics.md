# Duergar Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/duergar-tactics/) | **Date**: October 30, 2017
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Duergar]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Brute profile** — unambiguous. Duergar Resilience and Sunlight Sensitivity are passive. Armed with ordinary melee/ranged weapons.
- **The core question**: When to Enlarge vs when to turn Invisible.
- **Enlarge costs one round's action** but increases damage from 1d8+2 (6.5 avg) to 2d8+2 (11 avg) per hit.
- **Break-even point is ~3 rounds** — Enlarge only pays off in fights lasting longer than 3 rounds.

## Enlarge vs Invisibility by Encounter Difficulty

| Difficulty | Duration | Duergar Reaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Easy** | Over quickly | Invisibility → flee |
| **Medium** | 2–4 rounds | ~50/50. May Invisibility + flee, or Enlarge then realize mistake → Invisibility + flee |
| **Hard / Deadly** | 3–5 rounds | **Enlarge round 1**. Invisibility + flee only at ≤10 hp |
| **Super-Deadly** | Over quickly (duergar wins) | Fight at normal size — no extra advantage needed |

## Key Points

- **Don't cheat**: Don't throw already-Enlarged duergar if they'd have no reason to expect a fight.
- **Intelligence 11**: Average humanoid. Cooperation level ≈ street gang or pickup sports team.
- **CR assumes Enlarged damage**: Only fights at normal size with overwhelming advantage.
- **Pre-plan encounter difficulty** using DMG pp. 82–84 before the session.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> You don't need to think about duergar tactics on the fly. Figure out encounter difficulty beforehand: vanish and run, Enlarge and charge, or simply charge.
