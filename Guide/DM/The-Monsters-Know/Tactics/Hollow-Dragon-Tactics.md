# Hollow Dragon Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/hollow-dragon-tactics/) | **Date**: November 2, 2022

---

## Tactical Summary

- Former metallic dragon whose life force was replaced with imperishable radiant energy to guard something eternally. Tireless, unwavering.
- **Does not retreat, does not surrender, does not get drawn off task.** Damage is irrelevant — never abandons its post.
- Size, AC, movement, senses, and Legendary Resistance of a typical adult dragon. Primary abilities: STR, CON, CHA. Melee-favoring brute.
- **Resistances**: Necrotic. **Immunities**: Poison, radiant, plus various conditions. **Lost**: DEX save proficiency, some WIS.

## Key Actions

- **Sapping Presence**: Replaces Frightful Presence. Drains energy from nearby creatures. Part of Multiattack — must repeat every turn. Not active on turns when Radiant Breath is used.
- **Radiant Breath (Recharge 5–6)**: Radiant damage in 60-ft cone. Use whenever available, positioning to hit all foes at once.
- With tough natural armor and swift flying speed, happy to chance OAs from flyby targets to remain airborne between turns. No need to land except to block a defended passageway.

## Target Selection

- Willing to negotiate unfavorable fights (relies on natural CHA, no social skill proficiency).
- Once combat starts, assesses weaknesses after observing opponents. Notes who avoids melee (likely low STR/CON).
- Resistances/immunities don't help much vs fighters or arcane casters — protect best against rogues with poisoned blades, clerics, paladins.
- **Focuses on foes casting DEX-save spells** — its one true weakness.

## Legendary Actions

| Cost | Action | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Claw | Flying opponents in its face; when grounded by mission |
| 2 | Ghostly Binding | Vexing spellslinger/skirmisher it plans to follow up on |
| 3 | Booming Scales | Dive within 10 ft of ≥2 frailer foes; ensure problematic enemies in range |

## Reconstruction

- At 0 hp, breaks into inert pieces. If not thoroughly destroyed, pieces rejoin within days → reanimates at full hp.
- If original purpose still viable → straight back to duty. If thwarted → may find something new, pass on, or **go full revenant** if opponents were jerks.
