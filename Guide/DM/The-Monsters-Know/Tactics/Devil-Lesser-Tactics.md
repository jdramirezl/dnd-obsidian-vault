# Devil Tactics: Lesser Devils

## General Devil Principles

All devils: 120 ft darkvision, Devil's Sight (prefer darkness/night/underground), resistance to unsilvered weapons/cold/magical effects, immunity to fire and poison. Motivation is to **dominate**. A devil on assigned duty **never retreats**.

## Imp (CR 1)

- Errand runners and information gatherers
- **Skirmisher:** Low STR, high CON, very high DEX; flight, invisibility, Stealth
- Best Shapechange form: **raven** (faster flying speed)
- Can close for poisonous sting/bite thanks to damage resistances
- **Priority is the mission** — if combat threatens duty, turns invisible and flies away
- As guards: raven form, perch 30 ft up with Stealth; dive-bomb trespassers and fly back up
- Ordered to guard → will not flee

## Spined Devil (CR 2)

- Messengers and spies — high DEX, prefers ranged
- Maintains 40 ft range (aerial), flies to 20 ft, flings 2 tail spines, flies back
- If enemy closes: retreats 20 ft → spines → retreats 20 ft
- **Flyby** — no opportunity attacks on swoop
- Switches to bite/fork only when out of spines (12 total)
- Won't fight if not ordered to; if ordered, fights to the death

## Bearded Devil (CR 3)

- Diabolical warrior — high physical stats, no flight
- Stays at **10 ft** with glaive (beyond most opportunity attack ranges)
- Glaive inflicts **infernal wounds** (bleed hp each round)
- If enemy closes to 5 ft → Multiattack with glaive + beard
- Beard poison prevents hp recovery (magical healing still stops bleeding)
- **Fights to the death**
- Devil Summoning: 30% → only when seriously injured (≤20 hp)

## Barbed Devil (CR 5)

- Soldiers and bodyguards — very high physical stats, no flight
- Melee Multiattack (claw/claw/tail) slightly better than ranged (Hurl Flame ×2)
- **Prefers melee** — Hurl Flame only vs. ranged attackers with magic weapons or casters using DEX-save/non-resisted elemental damage
- Focuses down most dangerous enemies; ignores those who can't hurt it
- Devil Summoning: 30% → only when seriously injured (≤44 hp)

## Chain Devil (CR 8)

- Jailers and torturers — exceptional STR/CON, no flight, 10 ft chain reach
- Closes to **10 ft**, not 5 ft
- **Animate Chains (first action of combat):** brings nearby chains to life as allies
  - Animated chains grapple one target each
  - Devil grapples strongest enemy with one chain, beats them with the other
  - Animated chains seize next-most-powerful enemies
- **Unnerving Mask** (reaction, 1/round): targets nearby enemy most likely to fail WIS save
- Never retreats

## Bone Devil (CR 9)

- Infernal middle management — brute profile, can fly
- Fights **hovering at 10–15 ft** — opponents without reach can't fight back
- Identifies most threatening enemies and focuses them
- **Devil Summoning (40% chance):**
  - Summon 2d6 spined devils = **do this round 1, always**
  - Average 7 spined devils × ~8 hp expected damage/turn = 56 hp/turn
  - Net expected gain of ~1.7 extra actions — a no-brainer
- Never retreats

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Devils on the Material Plane are either summoned by a mortal or advancing a superior's interests
> - Lemures are unlikely outside the Nine Hells — skip to imps for material plane encounters
> - Bone devil summoning spined devils is the standout: always do it round 1
> - Chain devil Animate Chains is essentially a free action economy boost — use it immediately
