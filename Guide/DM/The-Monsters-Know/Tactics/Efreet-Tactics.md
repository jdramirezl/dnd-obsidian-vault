# Efreet Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/efreet-tactics/) | **Date**: October 28, 2016
> **Book**: Monster Manual

---

## Overview

- **Brute fighter, but not dumb**. Extraordinary Str/Con, high Int/Wis/Cha. Proficiency in Wis/Int/Cha saves but NOT Dex/Con. Slightly wary of spellcasters (barely above-average Dex).
- Genies of fire. Wicked, malicious, view mortals as fit only for enslavement.
- Large creature, 200 hp, fire immune, AC high.

## Melee vs Ranged

- **Double scimitar** (+10 to hit, slashing + fire): Primary attack. Superior to Hurl Flame in both modifier and damage.
- **Hurl Flame** (5d6 fire, 120 ft): Only when a ranged opponent is giving more trouble than melee opponents AND is >60 ft away. Within 60 ft: rush them and scimitar instead.
- **Spellcasters are primary Hurl Flame targets** (lower AC, can circumvent efreet's high AC).

## Key Spells

- ***Wall of fire* (1/day)**: 5d8 fire/turn to all within or within 10 ft of one side. Opaque — cuts off spellcasters/ranged. Even better: 20-ft diameter ring around itself, trapping melee opponents inside with it. "I'm not trapped in here with you — you're trapped in here with me!" Efreet is immune to the fire.
- ***Enlarge/reduce* (3/day)**: *Enlarge* for +1d4 per weapon strike. But costs a full action — opportunity cost exceeds 2d4. Only use when Huge size avoids an attack/effect that Large doesn't (e.g., preventing Medium creatures from grappling it).
- ***Conjure elemental* (1/day)**: Summons a fire elemental ally. **Note**: 1-minute casting time — must be pre-combat, not mid-fight.
- ***Gaseous form / Invisibility / Plane shift* (1/day each)**: Escape when seriously wounded (≤80 hp). *Plane shift* can also be used offensively against a single foe.
- ***Major image***: Distraction.

## Tactical Behavior

- **Clever, patient fighters** who outlast fragile opponents. If melee opponents give trouble: Disengage (action) and fly out of reach, reposition to face all opponents without being flanked.
- From the air: Hurl Flame to suppress spellcasters/ranged, then descend to clobber melee opponents.
- **Escape at ≤80 hp**: *Plane shift* > *gaseous form* > *invisibility*.

## Social Interaction

- Don't parley readily. Always seek an edge or loophole. Helping them doesn't guarantee reciprocity.
- Accept surrender — but surrender means becoming a thrall, possibly with an indefinite sojourn on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running the Efreet
> The *wall of fire* ring is the signature move — trap melee fighters inside a burning ring while the efreet hacks away, immune to its own fire. Pre-summon a fire elemental before combat if possible. Efreets are patient enough to let enemies exhaust themselves before committing.
