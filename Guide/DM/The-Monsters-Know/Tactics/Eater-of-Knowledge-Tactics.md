# Eater of Knowledge Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/eater-of-knowledge-tactics/) | **Date**: March 14, 2024
> **Book**: Planescape: Adventures in the Multiverse

---

## Overview

- **Mighty brute + psionic caster**. Exceptional Str/Int, very high Wis/Con, high Cha, average Dex.
- Cracks skulls, scoops out brains and knowledge. Psionic powers grow with brains consumed (like neh-thalggu).
- **Goal**: Consume as many brains as safely possible, then *plane shift* out.
- Gets in your face and stays there — Con is primary defense.

## Core Loop: Stun → Extract Brain

1. **Multiattack** (two Slams) against same target. Both hit + failed Int save → **stunned** (incapacitated).
2. **Extract Brain** on incapacitated target.
- Always both Slams at same target when trying to stun. Splitting loses the stun chance.
- Vs wizards/artificers/druids/rogues (high Int or Int save proficiency): may split since stun unlikely. But still worth trying.

## Target Priority

- **Low Int, no Int save proficiency first**: Fighters, barbarians, paladins, monks, bards, sorcerers, warlocks, rangers.
- Save wizards/artificers/druids/rogues for last.
- After round 1: ranks by (1) Int low→high, (2) Str/Dex bonus low→high, (3) AC low→high.

## Psionic Powers (Ranked)

- ***Hypnotic pattern***: **Best**. Mass incapacitation → skip to Extract Brain. Great vs armored fighters.
- ***Greater invisibility***: Potent. Increases hit chance significantly. Concentration.
- ***Mislead/Invisibility***: Shock value or advantage on one hit.
- ***Telekinesis***: Dispose of wizard/rogue near cliff/lava. Or hoist half-ton rock → drop for 10d10 bludgeoning (DC 15 Dex). Don't try on rogues.
- ***Major/Silent image***: Might lure creatures close. Not ideal.
- ***Mass suggestion***: Targets Wis (fewer dump Wis). Hard to phrase reasonably.
- ***Arcane eye/Detect magic***: Reconnaissance only.

## Combat Flow

1. Open with best incapacitation (*hypnotic pattern* > *greater invisibility* > *invisibility*).
2. Work through easy targets (low Int, no Int save proficiency).
3. After easy targets done: Barely scratched (91–72 hp) → stay. Moderately wounded (≤71 hp) → *plane shift* out. **Seriously wounded (≤40 hp)** → *plane shift* immediately.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running the Eater of Knowledge
> Open with *hypnotic pattern*, then Extract Brain the lowest-Int PC. It's a hit-and-run predator on a cosmic scale — *plane shift* is its guaranteed escape. An invisible eater consuming a hireling's brain in front of the party is a terrifying opener.
