# Succubus/Incubus Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/succubusincubus-tactics/) | **Date**: October 24, 2016

---

## Overview

[[Succubus-Incubus]] are not combat monsters — they are **long-game corruptors**. Their encounter is an entire subplot, not a single scene. Goal: corrupt a mortal's soul through free will, then kill them to claim it.

## Feature Sequence (order of use)

1. **Etherealness**: Reconnaissance, spying from the Ethereal Plane.
2. **Deception/Insight/Persuasion proficiency**: Identify target's fears and desires, build a manipulation plan.
3. **Telepathy** (60 ft): Communicate with any creature.
4. **Charm** (1 humanoid, 24 hrs, DC not stupendous): Charm a sleeping target — RAW, target need not be conscious. Favors low-WIS targets (save mod ≤0), though ambitious fiends may target high-WIS PCs for the challenge.
5. **Telepathic Bond**: Unlimited-distance communication with charmed targets, even cross-planar.
6. **Shapechanger**: Assume alluring Small/Medium humanoid form.
7. **Stealth proficiency**: Approach victims undetected.
8. **Draining Kiss**: Suck HP from charmed/willing targets. Kills if HP reduced to 0 by this method alone.

## The Corruption Campaign

1. **Choose target** — spy from Ethereal Plane.
2. **Develop a plan** based on target's Ideal, Bond, and Flaw.
3. **Nightly Charm**: Etherealness → Stealth → Charm sleeping target. If it fails, slip away and try again tomorrow.
4. **Daytime corruption**: Telepathic suggestions toward sinful behavior (pride, envy, gluttony, sloth, lust, avarice, wrath). May also appear in alluring form to seduce in person. Discourages target from telling others ("our little secret"). May maintain a public persona so involvement seems normal.
5. **The Kill**:
   - Ideally, establish enough trust that the target accepts the kiss **willingly** (no Charm needed for first kiss).
   - **Draining Kiss** → re-Charm → **Draining Kiss** again.
   - If re-Charm fails: may try to *persuade* the target they're already too far gone.
   - If the combo fails, the mission is blown — unlikely to get a second chance.
6. **Escape**: Revert to fiend form (one action, artistic effect only) → Etherealness on next action.

## If Busted

- **Etherealness** is a mostly foolproof escape. May reveal fiend form for dramatic flair before vanishing.
- If ethereal escape is blocked and forced to fight: revert to fiend form for claw attack.
- **Shapechange + movement**: Move 30 ft in humanoid form → transform → fly 30 ft in fiend form (same turn, per "Using Different Speeds" rules).

## Combat (last resort)

- Creature of subterfuge, not aggression (DEX 17, STR 8). Resistant to nonmagical physical damage but not immune. No protection from acid, force, necrotic, radiant, or thunder.
- **Will not stick around to fight** if it has any choice. Escape via Ethereal Plane > fly away > claw attack only if inescapably cornered.
- Values nothing more than its own life.
