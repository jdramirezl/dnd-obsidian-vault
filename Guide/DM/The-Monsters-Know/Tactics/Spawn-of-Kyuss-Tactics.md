# Spawn of Kyuss Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/spawn-of-kyuss-tactics/) | **Date**: October 22, 2019

---

## Tactical Summary

- Largely mindless brute. Very high STR, exceptional CON, dismal INT/WIS/CHA. No flexibility, no independent judgment, minimal self-preservation.
- Driven by a single compulsion: **spread its parasitic infection**.
- All traits besides attacks are passive.

## Combat

- **Multiattack**: Two Claw attacks (piercing + necrotic) + one **Burrowing Worm** (parasitic maggot; failed save = ongoing damage until removed).
- **Wind-up-and-let-go monster**. No capacity to distinguish opponents. No reason to stop. No DM judgment required — aim at PCs and let them do their thing.
- Essentially a high-CR, mobile swarm of rot grubs.

## DM Notes

- The interest comes from how PCs react to infection, not from the monster's tactics.
- Consider introducing lesser diseases first so the party has a chance to prepare.
- Don't throw at a party without *some* path to curing disease.
