# Demon Tactics: Manes and Type 1

## Universal Demon Traits
- **Can't be permanently killed** outside the Abyss — re-form there if destroyed elsewhere
- Therefore: **no self-preservation instinct**, never retreat, never back down
- Resistant/immune to cold, fire, lightning; immune to poison
- Darkvision or truesight — prefer operating in darkness

## Manes (CR 1/8)

Demonic cannon fodder. Stupid, slow, no special abilities. Point in a direction and let it lurch. Hardly worth bothering with.

## Shadow Demon (CR 4)

### Tactical Summary
Skirmisher with Light Sensitivity and Shadow Stealth (Hide as bonus action in dim light/darkness). Vulnerable to radiant damage. Extra 2d6 damage on attacks with advantage (like Sneak Attack).

### Combat Behavior
1. Hides in darkness → attacks with advantage → bonus damage
2. Targets the **weakest opponent** (fewest HP — INT 14/WIS 13 can judge accurately)
3. Strikes fast and hard, hoping to finish in 1–2 rounds
4. If first blow doesn't deal ≥60% of target's max HP, or second doesn't finish them: **Dash away** (action) + **Hide** (bonus action) once beyond sight range
5. Repositions stealthily, then creeps back for another ambush
6. Repeats until it or all opponents are defeated — this is repositioning, not fleeing
7. If struck with radiant damage: simultaneously enraged and terrified — avoids that enemy but doesn't flee

### Limitations
- Not fast enough to get out of sight in one move without Dashing
- Incorporeal Movement lets it pass through opponents but they can still turn and make opportunity attacks

## Barlgura (CR 5)

### Tactical Summary
Dumb brute (INT 7) with Stealth proficiency, Running Leap, Reckless (always on — it's just their nature), and innate spellcasting (*invisibility*, *phantasmal force*, *entangle*, *disguise self*).

### Ambush Combo
1. Cast *phantasmal force* to create bait (treasure pile, food, cute animal) — draws one victim
2. Lie in wait, hidden (use *invisibility* to improve hiding)
3. When victims approach: one barlgura casts *entangle* beneath them
4. All barlguras barrel in with **Reckless Multiattack**
5. If solo: *entangle* first (gives away surprise), then attack

### Advantage/Disadvantage Tracking
Barlguras may have advantage from: hiding, Reckless, restrained opponent, blinded opponent. PCs may have advantage from Reckless barlgura. Any advantage cancels any disadvantage.

## Vrock (CR 6)

### Tactical Summary
Flying brute (60 ft. fly speed), resistant to physical damage from normal weapons. Hovers, flies into melee, attacks, flies back out — not overly concerned about opportunity attacks.

### Action Priority
1. **Spores** (recharge): Use whenever available and can affect **3+ enemies** — poisons targets, imposing disadvantage on attacks/checks, plus ongoing damage
2. **Stunning Screech** (1/day): Stuns in 20 ft. radius for 1 round. Only useful if allies can follow up immediately — a lone vrock wastes this. Best with multiple vrocks or a commanding demon
3. **Multiattack** (beak + talons): Default action

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Shadow demons are ambush predators — use hit-and-run in dark environments
> - Barlguras often roam in packs; the *entangle* + Reckless combo is their signature
> - Vrocks should flock together (3+) to get value from Stunning Screech
> - A lone vrock is an outcast or spy — severely vulnerable
> - Track advantage/disadvantage carefully in barlgura fights — they stack up fast
