# Skull Lord Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/skull-lord-tactics/) | **Category**: Undead (CR 15)

---

## Tactical Summary
- Agglomeration of three rival warlords sharing one body with three skinless heads
- Compulsions: to conquer and to bicker
- Combination spellslinger/skirmisher; Charisma highest (spellcasting), very high Dex/Con/Int
- Wants to drag combat out; stays close to front line as support caster

## Key Features
- **Master of the Grave**: Undead allies within 30 ft get advantage — stay behind front line
- **Summon Undead** (legendary, costs all 3): Spam zombies (Undead Fortitude = attrition) or skeletons (ranged, spread battlefield)
- **Evasion**: Effective resistance to Dex-save damage
- **Legendary Resistance** (3/day): Conserve — never allow paralysis/restraint; always resist radiant; otherwise only for 5th+ level non-Dex save failures
- **Resistances**: Physical (normal weapons), cold, necrotic; immune to poison

## Legendary Action Priority
1. **Move** (Disengage + Dash): When melee opponent deals 32+ damage after resistances
2. **Summon Undead**: When Move wasn't needed — first batch forms front line; later batches harass backliners
3. **Bone staff / cantrip**: Burn remaining actions after Move

## Spell Priority
- **Cone of cold** (opening, ≥6 clustered targets): Expected 162 damage; use before summoning allies
- **Cloudkill** (after minions on field): Foes choose toxic fumes or OAs fleeing; skull lord immune; can cast twice
- **Finger of death** (finishing move): Most-hated opponent at ≤40% HP; corpse becomes zombie
- **Haste** (self): When opposition won't cooperate with attrition; high risk/reward
- **Scorching ray** (ranged): Flexible but inferior to bone staff Multiattack
- **Ice storm**: Reserve for flying enemies
- **Cantrips**: *fire bolt* (16), *chill touch* (14, inhibits healing), *ray of frost* (14, slows)
- **Shocking grasp**: Damage + retreat without OA (spares legendary Move)
- **Dimension door**: Emergency escape only

## Target Priority
Most-hated = wielding spells/magic weapon + dealing radiant damage + being good/pious → direct minions and attacks at this opponent

## The Bickering (Roleplay)
Three heads — "General," "Warlock," "Sneak":
- Move → General accuses Sneak of cowardice
- Bone staff → Warlock demands spells; Sneak berates General for exposure
- Spellcasting → General says it's bored
- Evasion → Sneak: *"You're welcome"*

## Retreat
- Generally none — fights until destroyed
- If created by another being: *dimension door* escape to try again later
