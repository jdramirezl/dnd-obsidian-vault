# Gorgon Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/gorgon-tactics/) | **Date**: October 5, 2016
> **Book**: Monster Manual

---

## Overview

- **High-Str, high-Con brute** with animal-level Int. Indiscriminate apex predator.
- Keen senses (Perception +4) — hard to slip past unnoticed.
- Feeds by petrifying prey, smashing it to gravel, then consuming it.
- Not evil per se, but once locked on, takes a lot of damage to reconsider.

## Opening: Trampling Charge

- Attacks when target is **30–40 ft away**. Charges forth (movement) → Gore (action) → tries to knock prone.
- If target falls prone: **bonus action Hooves** attack.
- Continues to gore + trample a prone victim as long as it stays down.

## Petrifying Breath

- Used primarily to **keep prey from escaping**, not as an opener.
- Triggers when: target gets back up, OR 3+ foes engage at once.
- If breath petrifies some but not all: prioritizes **still-moving targets** on next attack.

## The 20-Foot Turnaround

- Minimum Trampling Charge distance: 20 ft. Gorgon speed: 40 ft.
- **Sequence**: Run 20 ft away → turn → charge back. Risks opportunity attacks, but doesn't care (tough, stupid brute).
- Restrained targets have disadvantage on OAs against it.
- Uses this maneuver after a failed knockdown attempt or when a prone target stands up.

> [!important] Action Economy
> Petrifying Breath and Gore are separate actions on separate turns. Breath first (one turn), then charge back in (next turn).

## Retreat

- Flees when **seriously injured (≤45 hp)**.
- Uses Petrifying Breath one last time before retreating.
- Backs away at full speed regardless of opportunity attacks.
- Readies a Gore action against anything that pursues into range.
