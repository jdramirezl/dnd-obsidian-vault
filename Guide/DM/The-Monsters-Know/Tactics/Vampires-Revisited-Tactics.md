# Vampires Revisited Tactics

> [!warning] Source URL returned 404 — content drawn from the original [Undead Tactics: Vampires](https://www.themonstersknow.com/undead-tactics-vampires/) article.

## Vampire Spawn

Exceptional physical abilities plus above-average CHA. Resistant to physical damage from normal weapons and necrotic damage. Regenerate hp each turn unless in sunlight or running water. High Stealth — stalk or ambush, never charge headlong. Strictly nocturnal.

- **Multiattack**: Claw/Claw or Claw/Bite (bite preferred but requires grapple, incapacitation, restraint, or willingness)
- Claw has built-in grapple; on success → bite next round and keep biting
- If grapple fails vs Athletics/Acrobatics-proficient foe → switch to damage claws; otherwise keep trying
- Drags grappled victim toward hiding at 15 ft/round
- Picks off the young, old, weak, isolated, oblivious — apex predator behavior
- Won't attack a group larger than their own number

## Vampire

Boss-level undead. Legendary Resistance (3/day) — uses on *any* failed save. Charm, Shapechange (bat/mist), legendary actions.

### Engagement Decision

Goes for TPK if uncharmed party is ≤3 level 7–8 PCs, ≤4 level 6, ≤5 level 5, or ≤6–7 level 4 or lower (or 1–2 levels above if moderately wounded).

### Combat Pattern

1. Targets PCs ill-equipped to resist grapple first
2. Grapple → bite (same as spawn)
3. When attacked while grappling: Unarmed Strike ×2 against attacker instead of biting
4. Does **not** drag victims away (unlike spawn)
5. Legendary Unarmed Strike vs first two melee engagers; Move LA to retreat from a third
6. If grappling: Bite LA to drain faster, then Unarmed Strike vs other attackers

### Radiant Damage / Holy Water

Suppresses Regeneration — vampire hates it. Uses legendary Move to escape the source. If it can't, grapples and drains that PC as priority.

### Retreat and Misdirection

- Too strong to attack but not enough to force flight → bat form, fly out of sight, shift to mist, circle back
- Materializes in concealment, whispers orders to charmed PCs to sneak away
- Reduces party size, then attacks when odds favorable
- **Seriously wounded** (≤57 hp) + radiant/holy water → reassesses; if unfavorable, mist form and flees lair
- If odds still OK → Dodge as action, fight only via legendary actions

### Horror Design Tip

Take familiar monsters and give them unfamiliar powers. A vampire merely *weakened* by sunlight instead of burned is far more terrifying than the conventional version.
