# Duergar Tactics: Mordenkainen's Duergar, Part 2

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/duergar-tactics-mordenkainens-duergar-part-2/) | **Date**: August 9, 2019
> **Book**: Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes

---

## Duergar Mind Master

- **Shock trooper** (despite Int 15). Dex-based offense/defense.
- **Mind Mastery (DC 12 Int save)**: Terrible in open combat. Only useful from ambush — doesn't break Invisibility, isn't an attack or spell. Forces target to sucker-punch an ally or walk into a hazard.
- **Multiattack**: Two Mind-Poison Dagger attacks (3d6 psychic bonus each). Very vicious.
- **Invisibility (4–6 recharge)**: Available roughly every other turn. But Invisibility → Multiattack (22 dmg/2 turns) loses to Multiattack × 2 (35 dmg). Only go invisible when needing defense or relocating.
- **Reduce** (replaces Enlarge): AC 14 → 19, Hide as bonus action (+5 Stealth), no speed penalty, dagger damage unchanged. Reduce → bonus Hide is the strongest advantage source.
- **Retreat at ≤15 hp**: Invisible + flee. If on cooldown: Dash (Reduced) or Disengage (not Reduced).

## Duergar Warlord

- **Tough brute**. ~3× standard duergar hp.
- **Multiattack includes Enlarge**: Enlarge AND attack same turn — always Enlarge round 1.
- **Subsequent rounds**: Melee attacks + **Call to Attack** (grants reaction attacks to duergar allies). Best vs tough front-liners unlikely to flee.
- **Psychic-Attuned Hammer >> Javelin**. Javelins only at range.
- **Invisibility (4–6 recharge)**: Reposition to surprising locations, not just for one attack's advantage.
- **Scouring Instruction (reaction)**: Damages ally to grant advantage. Use when warlord won't need its own OA. Won't use if ally within 4 hp of seriously wounded.
- **Retreat at ≤30 hp**: Dodge (vs 1) or Dash (vs 2+).

## Duergar Despot

- **Juggernaut**. Extraordinary Str/Con, rock-bottom Dex.
- **Multiattack**: Two Iron Fists + two Stomping Feet.
  - Vs 1: Iron Fist (prone, 0 knockback) → Stomping Foot ×2 (advantage) → Iron Fist. Limit knockback to ≤25 ft, follow target.
  - Vs 2: One Iron Fist + one Stomping Foot each.
- **Flame Jet** (replaces any/all melee attacks in Multiattack): Vs 1 non-prone: better than Stomping Foot. Vs 2+: pure damage hose.
- ***Misty step* (1/day)**: Teleport to incinerate 3+ foes lined up for Flame Jet.
- ***Stinking cloud***: Only in confined spaces where targets can't walk out AND no duergar allies need to enter. Duergar constructs ARE immune.
- ***Counterspell***: Targets 2nd–3rd level Dex-save spells only (despot's Dex save is dismal).
- **Psychic Engine**: Passive AoE on crit/0 hp — incentive to stay in melee with many enemies.
- **Retreat at ≤47 hp**: Iron Fist knockback 30 ft + Dodge. Flame Jet opportunistically.

## Duergar Hammerer & Screamer (Constructs)

- **Hammerer**: Destroys objects/structures. No subtlety, no target selection, no self-preservation.
- **Screamer**: Rolling *thunderwave* + melee. Rolls toward clustered enemies and blasts through. Random target if not clustered. No independent judgment.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Variants
> Mind master shines as invisible battlefield controller — Mind Mastery from hiding sows chaos. Warlord is a force multiplier (Enlarge turn 1, Call to Attack every round). Despot is a boss — Flame Jet replacing Multiattack attacks is staggeringly powerful. Pair constructs with despot (immune to *stinking cloud*).
