# Kraken Priest Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — NPC Tactics: Kraken Priests](https://www.themonstersknow.com/npc-tactics-kraken-priests/)
> **Bestiary:** [[Kraken]]

---

## Tactical Summary

The kraken priest is a support spellcaster — high Constitution and Wisdom, resistance to nonmagical physical damage, amphibious. Fights alongside minions (cultists, kuo-toa, sahuagin) rather than alone, leading with a concentration spell and supporting from behind the front line.

## Combat Behavior

- **Opening Spell (concentration — pick ONE based on terrain):**
  - **Open water / ships:** *Control water* — Flood to smash ships, Part Water to drop them, Whirlpool to torment swimmers, Redirect Flow to impede cave entry.
  - **Indoors / tight quarters:** *Evard's black tentacles* — 4+ enemies minimum.
  - **Open sky (non-ship):** *Call lightning* — especially effective vs front-line melee with low Dex.
- **If concentration broken:** Switch to next-most-logical concentration spell.
- **Voice of the Kraken:** Once per combat. DC 14 Cha save, frightened 1 min, 300 ft radius (~60 creatures). Best for panicking bystanders and imposing disadvantage on attackers for 1–2 rounds.
- **Thunderous Touch:** Avg 27.5 thunder damage melee spell attack. Defensive "get away from me" attack when enemies close to melee.
- **Command:** Force prone, provoke OAs, pull into melee. "Disembark!" effective vs ship-based opponents.
- **Pre-combat rituals:** *Water breathing* or *water walk* on up to 10 minions.
- **Darkness:** Blind 3+ spellcasters (requires concentration).

## Key Decision Points

1. **Which concentration spell?** Terrain dictates: *control water* on water, *Evard's* indoors, *call lightning* outdoors.
2. **Voice of the Kraken timing:** Early, to impose disadvantage and panic bystanders.
3. **Thunderous Touch vs. spellcasting:** Only if an enemy reaches melee; otherwise maintain concentration.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Fights to the death.** The kraken priest is a fanatic.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> The three best spells are mutually exclusive (concentration). Pair two kraken priests so one runs *Evard's* while the other runs *call lightning*. Best minion pairings: kuo-toa, sahuagin, sea spawn, or deep scions.
