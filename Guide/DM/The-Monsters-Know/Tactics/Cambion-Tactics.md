# Cambion Tactics

> [!summary]
> The cambion is a fiend-humanoid hybrid optimized as a shock attacker — fast, hard-hitting, and socially manipulative. It flies, charms, strafes with Fire Ray or spear, and negotiates mid-combat when things drag on. It fights decisively and retreats via *plane shift* when seriously wounded.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Fiend, Medium, any evil alignment
- **STR 18 / DEX 18 / CON 16 / INT 14 / CHA 16**
- Shock attacker: highest STR + DEX, high CHA for social manipulation
- **Fly 60 ft**, land 30 ft
- **AC 19**, **HP 82**
- Resistant to cold, fire, lightning, poison; resistant to nonmagical B/P/S
- Proficient in Deception, Intimidation, Perception, Stealth

---

## Behavior
- Cunning and manipulative — prefers the easy path
- Will charm and fast-talk before resorting to violence
- Bullies weaker opponents; outfoxes equal or stronger ones
- Proficient in Stealth — apt for ambush
- Fiendish parentage determines deal-keeping:
  - **Devil offspring:** letter of the law, exploits loopholes
  - **Demon offspring:** breaks word instantly when advantageous
  - **Yugoloth offspring:** keeps word while getting something out of it
  - **Succubus/incubus offspring:** keeps word as long as it's amusing

---

## Combat Sequence

### Pre-Combat: Fiendish Charm
- Uses **Fiendish Charm** from hiding on targets within 30 ft
- Not an attack — remains hidden even on failure
- Targets non-elven, non-hill-dwarf bards, rogues, barbarians, sorcerers, warlocks, wizards
- Avoids clerics, druids, rangers, monks (high WIS saves)
- Commands charmed PCs: "Do not interfere!" as free communication

### Opening Round
- Attacks from hiding with **surprise**
- Spear (two-handed) if in reach; **Fire Ray** otherwise
- Prioritizes: fragile spellcasters, magic weapon wielders, isolated targets

### Aerial Strafe
- Fights from **10–30 ft in the air**
- Flies down → spear → flies back up
- Unconcerned about OAs (resistant to nonmagical damage)
- Switches to **Fire Ray** from air if target lands multiple OA hits or wields magic weapons

### Spells
- **Command:** drop weapon (then swoop to grab), or flee through allies' threatened spaces
- **Alter self:** sprouts natural weapons if disarmed in melee (magical, +1, extra 1d6 fire)
- **Plane shift (self only):** escape hatch

### Mid-Combat Negotiation
- After one round per uncharmed enemy without decisive victory → starts talking
- Fights while talking — stops only if deal is reached
- Talks sooner if **moderately wounded (≤57 hp)**

---

## Retreat
- **Plane shifts** when **seriously injured (≤32 hp)**
- May flee sooner if fight is going badly and opponents won't negotiate

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Social Monster
> The cambion's real danger is Fiendish Charm + social skills. A cambion that charms 1–2 PCs before combat starts can turn the party against itself.

- Can solo a party of level 1–2 PCs; needs allies against intermediate-level PCs
- Does NOT want prolonged combat — if it can't drop ~1 opponent per round, it negotiates or leaves
