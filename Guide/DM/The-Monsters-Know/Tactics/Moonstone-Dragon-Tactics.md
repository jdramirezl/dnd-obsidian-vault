## General Notes (from Fizban's Treasury of Dragons)
- Fey-aligned dragons found in the Feywild and near fey crossings on the Material Plane
- Whimsical, curious, and mercurial—more likely to toy with intruders than attack outright
- Breath weapon deals radiant damage in a cone and can charm targets (Wis save)
- Innate spellcasting includes fey-themed spells: *faerie fire*, *moonbeam*, *dispel magic*, *charm monster*, *dream*
- Prefer deception, misdirection, and enchantment over brute force
- Lair actions and regional effects emphasize illusion, moonlight, and dream manipulation

## Inferred Tactical Approach
- **Opening**: Breath weapon to charm as many targets as possible, then pick off uncharmed enemies
- **Spellcasting**: *Faerie fire* to negate invisibility, *moonbeam* for sustained radiant damage, *charm monster* on the biggest threat
- **Disposition**: More likely to parley, test, or redirect intruders than fight to the death
- **Retreat**: Plane shifts to the Feywild if seriously threatened
