# Aboleth Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/aboleth-tactics/) | **Related**: [[Aboleth]]

---

## Tactical Summary
- Ancient aberration schemer (CR 10) with exceptional mental stats; fights in underwater lairs where PCs are at severe disadvantage
- Tentacles (10-ft reach, 3x Multiattack) transmit disease making skin allergic to air; Mucous Cloud prevents air breathing
- Enslave targets the party's weakest link—especially whoever sustains water breathing for the group
- Legendary actions (Detect, Tail Swipe, Psychic Drain) keep it active every turn
- Cannot be permanently killed but still prefers to avoid the inconvenience of reforming

## Combat Behavior
- **Pre-combat**: Telepathically probes creatures to learn desires and assess minion potential. Make the social encounter alien and unsettling
- **Opening**: Enslave the weak link—ideally whoever maintains the group's water breathing, then command them to drop it
- **Melee targets** (priority): elves, Dex-save AoE casters, magic item wielders, 40+ dmg/turn dealers, high-Wis characters (switch to low-Wis if moderately injured)
- **Legendary actions**: Tail Swipe vs. flankers, Detect vs. sneakers, Psychic Drain on expendable enslaved creatures when injured
- **Lair actions**: Psychic AoE (90 ft, Wis save) only when ALL opponents are in range; *phantasmal force* as reset; grasping tide only to pull land foes into water

## Key Decision Points
- Enslave the concentration caster, not random PCs—don't waste it making party members fight each other
- Best lair action can't repeat without using another first; *phantasmal force* on a low-Int foe is the reset
- Psychic Drain only on unimportant enslaved creatures, not your strategically enslaved caster

## Retreat Conditions
- Retreats at ≤54 hp, Disengaging if necessary, otherwise Dodging while withdrawing
- More willing to abandon lair than other legendary creatures (weak lair actions)
- Pair with chuuls for encounter variety

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The aboleth encounter should be 80% creepy social interaction and 20% combat—lean into the alien telepathy and the horror of the skin-disease before initiative is ever rolled.
