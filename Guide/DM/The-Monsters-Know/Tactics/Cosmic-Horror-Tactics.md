# Cosmic Horror Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/cosmic-horror-tactics/) | **Date**: September 27, 2023

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Massive astral aberration** — larger than an astral dreadnought. Hyperintelligent (Int 21), malevolent. Gargantuan, 100-ft fly speed.
- **Melee brute with 30-ft reach**: Str/Con highest, but Int/Cha also extraordinary. Positions 10–15 ft above opponents.
- **Legendary Resistance**: Auto-succeeds on failed saves.
- **Achilles' heel**: Humanoid-average Dex, no Dex save proficiency. Target with Dex-save spells.
- **Immune**: Acid, poison damage; charmed/frightened/poisoned conditions.
- **NOT immune to normal weapon damage**.

## Combat Behavior

**Psychic Whispers (recharge)**: Preferred action when 3+ targets visible. May or may not penetrate total cover (DM ruling).

**Tentacle attacks**: Force damage. Retaliates against any single opponent dealing 28+ damage in one round.

**Target priority**: Sorcerers, warlocks, wizards — recognizes their strengths and knows they cast Dex-save spells. Int so high it can predict PC moves as well as the DM.

**Positioning**: Hovers 10–15 ft above. Can stretch to 20×100 ft rectangle with 25-ft reach in every direction. Only safe place on a ship is belowdecks — but it flies faster than any ship and will patiently batter the hull.

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

- **Crushing Tentacle (1)**: Default. ~27 avg damage from two uses.
- **Poison Jet (2)**: Worth it only with 3+ targets lined up.
- **Teleport (2)**: Rarely worth mid-combat. Excellent opener — teleporting directly above the party is terrifying.

## Roleplay: Alien Communication

May open with incoherent telepathic "conversation" before combat. Example: *"Extra sick fish need special training for extra fun. We will have a happy party now."* No intonation, no parseable intent. Conveys intelligence without comprehensibility — there's no "getting to yes" with an alien mind.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Seriously wounded (≤112 hp)**: Flees. Teleports + Dash to escape.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Open with Teleport legendary action before initiative — appearing directly above the ship is the scariest introduction. Prioritize casters targeting Dex saves. For roleplay, lean into the alien-mind angle: coherent grammar, zero comprehensible meaning.
