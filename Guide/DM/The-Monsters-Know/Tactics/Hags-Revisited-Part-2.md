# Hags Revisited, Part 2

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/hags-revisited-part-2/) | **Date**: March 17, 2017
> **Book**: Volo's Guide to Monsters

---

## Prophecy Coven

- Almost **no tactical advantages**. *Arcane eye* hunts fleeing foes; *dispel magic* is straightforward.
- *Bane*/*bless* are low-power sustained spells. Most spells are rituals or divination.
- Better suited to **social interaction** encounters than combat. Hags may not even cast these in front of PCs.

---

## Nature Coven

The tactical nightmare. Three hags each sustain a separate concentration spell (shared slots).

### Key Combos

**Combo 1: Entangle + Call Lightning + Flaming Sphere**
- *Entangle* restrains targets (failed Str save) → disadvantage on Dex saves.
- *Call lightning* and *flaming sphere* exploit that Dex save disadvantage.
- Hag with *flaming sphere* is free to cast another non-concentration spell next turn.

**Combo 2: Spike Growth + Plant Growth + Entangle**
- *Spike growth*: Difficult, damaging terrain (automatic damage, no save).
- *Plant growth*: Movement slowed ×4 (instantaneous, no concentration).
- *Entangle*: Restrained targets can barely escape the spiky, overgrown terrain.
- Third hag is free for *call lightning* or *flaming sphere* while sisters sustain the other two.

**Combo 3: Entangle + Grasping Vine**
- *Grasping vine* (bonus action) yanks a victim out of *entangle* for a claw attack.

### Other Nature Spells

| Spell | Use |
|---|---|
| *Dominate beast* | Meanest thing to do to a Beastmaster ranger. Turn mount against rider. |
| *Wall of thorns* | Better as exit-strategy spell than combat combo (only one 6th-level slot). |
| *Tree stride* | Confound opponents by popping tree-to-tree. Only seriously wounded hag uses it to escape (breaks coven). |
| *Moonbeam* | Doesn't combo well. Drags on action economy (moving it costs a full action). Use singly/situationally. |
| *Insect plague* | Doesn't combo well. Use singly/situationally. |

---

## Death Coven

Less tactical flexibility. Built around the **contagion → follow-up damage** loop.

### Core Combo: Slimy Doom

1. **Contagion (slimy doom)**: Touch attack. Imposes: "whenever creature takes damage, it is stunned until end of its next turn."
2. Stunned = can't act, auto-fail Str/Dex saves, attacks against it have advantage.
3. If target NOT yet stunned: other hags cast ***blight*** (Con save, single target damage).
4. If target IS stunned: other hags use ***inflict wounds*** with advantage (boosted to 3rd level if slot available).

### Other Death Spells

| Spell | Use |
|---|---|
| *Circle of death* | Last-resort AoE before fleeing. Cast **without regard for sister hags in the area**. |
| *Death ward* | Prophylactic on self when moderately wounded. |
| *Ray of enfeeblement* | Budget option if out of 4th/5th-level slots. Or vs Str-based Extra Attack fighters. |
| *False life* | Prophylactic when moderately wounded. Modest effect. |
| *Animate dead* | Pre-cast. Start encounter with skeleton/zombie already present. |

> [!warning] Hags Are Bad Team Players
> *Circle of death* is cast without caring if sister hags are in the blast. Covens are alliances of convenience held together by mutual hatred.
