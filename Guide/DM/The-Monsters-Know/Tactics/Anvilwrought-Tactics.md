# Anvilwrought Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/anvilwrought-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- Four Theran constructs from *Mythic Odysseys of Theros*, each with "clever animal" Intelligence—behavioral patterns, not strategies
- Raptor: recon shock attacker; hits hard for 2 rounds then Disengages and flies off
- Bronze Sable: ambush shock attacker with False Appearance and Surprise Attack; devastating first-round damage with Pack Tactics
- Burnished Hart: swift brute that charges from 20 ft for Charge + Multiattack combo
- Gold-Forged Sentinel: inscrutable construct with Fire Breath, Charge, Spell Turning, and unpredictable hostility triggers

## Combat Behavior
- **Raptor**: Avoids combat (recon unit). If provoked, 2 rounds of attacks (~11 dmg/round), then Disengages and flies. Withdraws early at ≤7 hp
- **Bronze Sable**: Poses as inanimate object. Surprise Attack adds massive bonus on each first-round hit vs. surprised targets. Pack Tactics grants advantage with multiple sables. Sticks with initial target. Targets smaller creatures when choosing
- **Burnished Hart**: When shot at, trots to 20 ft from archer and stops. Next turn charges for piercing + fire + prone chance + hoof Multiattack. Fights until foe is finished, then next aggressor in order
- **Gold-Forged Sentinel**: Only hostile when provoked. Fire Breath (recharge 5–6) over Multiattack when 2+ enemies in cone. Leaps over nearby foes to reach smaller distant targets via Charge. Spell Turning gives advantage vs. targeted spells but being targeted makes the caster an enemy. Support spells don't provoke it

## Key Decision Points
- Raptor: always prioritizes returning with intel over fighting
- Bronze Sable: recon sables flee at ≤12 hp; guard sables fight to destruction
- Burnished Hart: 20-ft positioning ensures archer can't escape Charge range. Flummoxed by 40+ ft speed foes
- Gold-Forged Sentinel: retreat is DM's choice—roll d6 or Wisdom check (DC by encounter difficulty)

## Retreat Conditions
- Raptor: ≤7 hp, Disengages and flies
- Bronze Sable (recon): ≤12 hp; (guard): never
- Burnished Hart: ≤18 hp, Disengages then Dashes
- Gold-Forged Sentinel: variable (DM choice), Disengages then Dashes when clear

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> These constructs shine as environmental flavor that escalates—a bronze sable spy in a temple, a burnished hart grazing near a sacred grove, a sentinel wandering with forgotten purpose.
