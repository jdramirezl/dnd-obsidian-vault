# Jackalwere Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/jackalwere-tactics/) | **Date**: May 9, 2017

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Jackals cursed with humanity**, not the other way around. Adopt hybrid form in combat.
- **Ability contour**: High Dex, average Str/Con, above-average Int. Looks like a sniper profile but attacks are melee. Unsuited to drawn-out fights.
- **Proficient in Deception and Stealth**. Immune to nonmagical, non-silvered physical damage.
- **Pack Tactics**: Won't start fights without overwhelming numerical advantage.
- **Sleep Gaze is weak individually** (DC 10 Wis save) but stacks across multiple [[Jackalwere|jackalweres]] — immunity is per-jackalwere, not universal.

## Combat Behavior

**Goal: end the fight in round 1.** Required numbers to initiate:
- 3 per elf (immune to Sleep Gaze — must be attacked directly)
- 4 per non-elf displaying holy symbol or wielding staff
- 2 per other non-elf

**Round 1**: Assign Sleep Gaze users to targets per the ratios above. Remaining jackalweres draw scimitars and attack immune/resistant targets directly with Pack Tactics advantage.

**Round 2**: Sleep Gaze won't work on the same targets again. Surviving victims are greatly outnumbered — all attackers gain Pack Tactics advantage.

**Round 3**: Continue melee assault.

**Round 4**: If foes aren't subdued, all Disengage and flee.

## Post-Combat

- **Working for a lamia**: Kidnap unconscious victims, bind and drag away.
- **Independent**: Chaotic evil — they eat you. Sheathe blades, switch to bite attacks against unconscious targets (auto-crit).

## Retreat Conditions

- **Individual seriously injured (≤7 hp)**: Disengage, then Dash away.
- **Half the pack fled or slain**: Remaining Dash away without Disengaging.
- **Ambushed without sufficient numbers**: Immediately Dash and flee.

## Encounter Design

- **Below level 5**: Any pack willing to fight the PCs will overwhelm them. Avoid unless working as lamia minions.
- **Level 5**: Sweet spot for a standalone jackalwere encounter.
- **Level 6–7**: Serious threat only alongside a lamia.
- **Level 8+**: Play for comedy.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> [[Jackalwere|Jackalweres]] are intelligent and crafty. They can tell who carries a holy symbol or staff and know elves are immune to their gaze. They won't attack unless the numbers are overwhelmingly in their favor. The Sleep Gaze immunity is per-individual jackalwere — gang up multiple gazers on single targets to beat the odds.
