# Devil Tactics: Greater Devils

## Shared Traits

All greater devils: 120 ft darkvision, Devil's Sight, immunity to fire/poison, resistance to cold/magical effects/unsilvered weapons. Ice devils also immune to cold. All brute profiles. A devil on duty **never flees**.

## Horned Devil (CR 11)

- Extraordinary STR/CON, triple melee Multiattack, flight
- **Hovers**, swoops to attack at **10 ft reach** (fork + tail), flies back out of range
- Opponents without reach weapons can't make opportunity attacks
- **Hurl Flame** only used against targets beyond 30 ft that are conspicuously more threatening (acid/radiant damage, magic weapons) — simply casting spells isn't enough (Magic Resistance)
- Tail inflicts **infernal wounds** (bleed) — devil won't give this up
- Excellent judge of which enemies are most dangerous
- Devil Summoning: 30% → only when seriously injured (≤71 hp)

## Erinyes (CR 12)

- Warriors, enforcers, agents of retribution — brute with decent DEX
- **Opens with longbow** to inflict poisoned condition (disadvantage on attacks/checks)
- Then casts bow aside and closes to melee
- Hovers, swoops, attacks, swoops out — uses **Parry** reaction vs. meaningful opportunity attacks
- Proficient in DEX/CON/WIS/CHA saves + Magic Resistance → magic goes to die here
- **Devil Summoning (50%):** Summon 3d6 spined devils as **first action** — devastating ranged damage

## Ice Devil (CR 14)

- Straightforward brute; distinctive feature: **Wall of Ice**
- High INT/WIS → shrewd wall placement to trap or split enemies; can drop walls for cold damage
- Otherwise: melee with 10 ft reach
- **Ice Spear variant:** less damage (47 vs 66 hp/turn) but slows targets — use against non-trivial parties
- **Devil Summoning (60%):** First action of combat, always — comes out ahead even in 2-round follow-up

## Pit Fiend (CR 20)

- 300 hp, fourfold Multiattack, Fear Aura (20 ft), *fireball* at will
- **Round 1:** Devil Summoning (100% success) → **1d4 barbed devils**
- If most powerful enemy is 60–90 ft away → *hold monster*
- Wants to be within 20 ft of enemies on *their* turns (Fear Aura) → no swoop tactics until all opponents have saved
- **Wall of Fire + Fireball combo:** Ring-shaped *wall of fire* (20 ft diameter) on self if 2+ enemies inside → *fireball* on self (immune to fire) → enemies take 8d6; fleeing costs 5d8 through the wall
- Melee Multiattack (~95 hp/turn vs AC 15) beats *fireball* (~84 hp on 4 targets) → melee at ≤4 enemies
- Ranged nuisance dealing non-resisted damage → fly to 90 ft → *hold monster* → close → drub unconscious
- Lacks CHA save proficiency — *banishment* attempts will **infuriate** it

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - A pit fiend encounter should strike fear into even the highest-level PCs — don't hold back
> - Erinyes with Parry can shrug off opportunity attacks — let them be aggressive
> - Ice devil Wall of Ice placement is the key tactical decision — think about splitting the party
> - Greater devils unerringly identify the most dangerous PC
