## General Notes (from Volo's Guide to Monsters)
- CR 6 Huge giant—a hill giant cursed with insatiable, maddening hunger by Grolantor
- Extremely high Strength (21) and Constitution (21) but abysmal Intelligence (5) and Wisdom (7)
- Chaotic evil with no tactical sophistication—a pure brute driven by hunger
- **Bite** can swallow a Medium or smaller creature on a hit (Str save to avoid). Swallowed creatures take acid damage each turn
- **Multiattack**: One Bite + one Fist per turn
- **Mouth of Madness**: Disadvantage on Wisdom saves; advantage on saves vs. being frightened

## Inferred Tactical Approach
- **Behavior**: Charges the nearest creature and tries to eat it. No target prioritization beyond proximity and size
- **Opening**: Rush in, Bite to swallow, Fist the next closest target
- **Swallow priority**: Targets that fail the Str save are removed from combat—focuses on swallowing as many PCs as possible
- **No retreat**: Too mad and hungry to flee. Fights to the death
- **Allies**: Other hill giants may be nearby but treat the Mouth with fear and disgust. May attack its own kin if hungry enough

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The Mouth of Grolantor is a horror-movie monster wearing a giant's skin. It doesn't think, it doesn't plan—it just eats. Have it burst through a wall mid-meal, already chewing on something.
