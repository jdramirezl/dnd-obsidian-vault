# Intellect Devourer Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/intellect-devourer-tactics/) | **Date**: November 21, 2016
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Intellect-Devourer]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- Aberration created to serve mind flayers. Not independent — always on a mission. Very low STR, high DEX and CON (skirmisher profile relying on numbers). Slightly above-average INT, average WIS/CHA — can plan and adapt.
- Proficiency in Stealth, 60-ft blindsight, 300-ft Detect Sentience. Resistant to nonmagical weapon damage.

## Core Combo: Devour Intellect → Body Thief

- **Multiattack** includes one Devour Intellect. If it stuns or knocks the target unconscious (both include incapacitation), it follows with **Body Thief** against the incapacitated humanoid.
- Body Thief doesn't grant immunity on failure — can retry.

## Probability of Stunning

| Target | Fail Save | Stun Chance | Combined |
|---|---|---|---|
| Commoner (INT 10) | 55% | ~62% | ~34% |
| Average adventurer (INT 12) | 50% | ~37% | ~19% |
| Low-level wizard (INT 15, +2 prof) | 35% | ~9% | ~3% |

- Takes 2+ rounds for even a 50/50 chance vs a commoner. Adventurers are very unlikely to get their brains eaten in open combat.
- **Conclusion**: The only PC an intellect devourer will target with Devour Intellect is one who chose INT as a dump stat.

## Typical Encounter: Already Puppeting a Host

- When PCs first encounter an intellect devourer, it's probably **puppeting a commoner's body** whose intellect it already devoured.
- The host body is only useful as long as the guise is maintained.
- Note: Other incapacitation sources work too — *hold person*, *hypnotic pattern*, *Tasha's hideous laughter*, *sleep*. An intellect devourer in a spellcaster's body could use these to prepare hosts for other intellect devourers.

## Ambush Tactics (Targeting a Dim PC)

1. Uses Stealth to hide in ambush until target comes within 10 ft.
2. Uses **only Devour Intellect** (not the claw/Devour Multiattack) — this way it doesn't give away its position on failure (target just has an unexplained splitting headache).
3. If it can't bring the target down in **3 rounds**, abandons the effort and scampers away.

## If Ejected from Host

- Runs like a rat, fighting only if cornered.
- If cornered: uses Multiattack (claws + Devour Intellect). May or may not use Body Thief on an incapacitated opponent — depends on whether it helps the getaway.
- Resistant to nonmagical weapons, not especially high AC, high movement speed → **Dashes** rather than Dodges when fleeing.
- **Exception**: First escape turn, if 2+ unsurprised melee attackers are adjacent, first action is **Disengage**.
