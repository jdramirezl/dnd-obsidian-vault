# Boggle Tactics

> [!summary]
> Boggles are fey pranksters summoned by loneliness — the embodiment of negative attention-seeking. They can't seriously hurt most adventurers. They harass, steal, and flee. Their toolkit: slippery/sticky oil, Dimensional Rifts through "frames," and Sleight of Hand through portals. Better as MacGuffins and comic subplots than villains.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Fey, Small
- **Low STR, exceptional DEX, above-average CON**
- Feeble melee (Pummel), no ranged attack
- Proficient: Perception, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
- **60 ft darkvision**, advantage on smell-based Perception
- Resistant to fire; operates in dim light/darkness/underground

---

## Key Features

### Oil Secretion (Bonus Action to Switch)
- **Slippery:** escape grapples, squeeze through narrow spaces
- **Sticky:** cling to walls/ceilings, harder for enemies to escape grapple

### Dimensional Rift (Bonus Action)
- 30 ft tunnel only the boggle can use (move, see, reach through)
- Requires a **frame** — window, door, hole, fireplace, ladder, basket, hollow tree, animal body
- Lasts until end of boggle's next turn; max 10 ft on a side

### Oil Puddle (Action)
- Fills boggle's square; lasts 1 hour
- **Slippery:** DEX save or fall prone
- **Sticky:** STR save or restrained

---

## Tactics Playbook

### Ambush + Oil Trap
1. Hide near pre-placed oil puddle → attack while unseen (advantage)
2. Attack again if target slips/gets stuck
3. Attack (action) → Dimensional Rift (bonus) → pass through and run

### Pickpocket Through Rift
1. Dimensional Rift (bonus) near victim → Sleight of Hand to filch item (action)
2. Run; if pursued, squeeze through narrow space + Oil Puddle to block

### Pummel Through Rift
1. Dimensional Rift (bonus) → Pummel through rift (action)
2. Target **cannot attack back** through the rift → run away

### Group Tactics
- Vanguard boggle commits mischief, gets chased toward the group
- Group has pre-oiled the ground; pursuers slip/get stuck
- Group pelts them with rocks, then everyone runs

---

## Retreat
- **Dashes away immediately** upon taking *any* damage, however minor
- Beelines to nearest frame → Dimensional Rift for extra 30 ft + Dash

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Better as a Plot Device
> Boggles are no threat to most parties. Use them as MacGuffins leading PCs into other situations, comic subplots, or running gags. A boggle tormenting a Beastmaster ranger's animal companion is gold.

- Solitary by nature (Volo's flavor text)
- Dimensional Rift requires a frame — plan the environment accordingly
- Oil puddles last an hour — a boggle with time can oil up large areas
