# Hag Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/hag-tactics/) | **Date**: November 3, 2016
> **Book**: Monster Manual

---

## Overview

- All hags have very high Str and Con → won't shy from melee.
- Evil fey (or fiend, for night hags) relying on magic and deceit.
- By the time PCs realize they're fighting hags, it's often too late.

## Coven Basics

- Must all be within **30 ft** of each other to cast coven spells.
- Fight facing outward, backs toward each other. Vulnerable to surrounding but protected from flanking.
- **Hag eye**: Surveillance device, usually given to a minion. Concealed carefully — destruction causes damage + temporary blindness.

## Coven Spell Priorities

| Spell | Notes |
|---|---|
| *Bestow curse* | **Cast at 5th level** (no concentration, 8 hrs). Wasted-action option vs Extra Attack. Only vs Wis save ≤+3. |
| *Eyebite* | Sustained. Sleep vs melee, sicken vs ranged, panic vs casters. |
| *Counterspell* | Auto-reflex vs incoming damage spells. Costs reaction + 3rd-level slot. |
| *Lightning bolt* | Only if 3+ in line AND all enemy casters shut down (else save slot for *counterspell*). |
| *Hold person* | At 5th level: paralyze up to 4 opponents. |
| *Ray of sickness* | Attack roll (not Wis save) → vs low-AC, high-Wis targets. At 5th level: 6d8 damage. |
| *Polymorph* | Turn a PC into a newt. Sustained, blocks other concentration spells. |

> [!warning] Wisdom Saves
> Hag covens wreck low-Wis parties. Against high-Wis parties, fall back on claws + *ray of sickness*.

## Sea Hag

- Weakest type. Can appear as ugly humanoids but not disguise hideousness.
- **Opening**: Maintain illusion until all PCs within 30 ft → revert to true form (bonus action) → DC 11 Wis save or frightened.
- **Death Glare**: Targets frightened opponents, starting with lowest-Wis-looking PC.
- If no one frightened: claw attacks; in coven, cast *phantasmal killer* to set up Death Glare.
- **Retreat**: Flees at ≤20 hp, Dashing (by water if possible). Coven breaks → hag with ≥37 hp turns on wounded sister.

## Green Hag

- Can disguise as attractive humanoids. Almost always found in covens.
- *Eyebite* priorities: sleep (melee), sicken (ranged), panic (casters). Skip *phantasmal killer* → *bestow curse*.
- **Invisible Passage**: All three go invisible together (stay within 30 ft) to converge on isolated troublemakers. One alone won't use it — unless seriously wounded.
- **Retreat**: Flees at ≤32 hp via Invisible Passage. Tries to secure hag eye. Hag with ≥58 hp turns on wounded sister.

## Night Hag

- **Fiend**, not fey. Changes Shape physically (action to undo — can't use for surprise).
- Core loop: **Etherealness + Nightmare Haunting**. Long-running subplot, not a single encounter.
- More likely to work **alone** than in covens. Innate spells are weak for CR 5.
- **If cover blown**: Cuts and runs after just **12 hp of damage** via Ethereal Plane or *plane shift* (self only). Only fights if cornered.
