# Wyvern Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Beast-grade dragon, basic brute with a critical subtlety: 20 ft. walk / 80 ft. fly gap means it never lands willingly. Strafing melee fighter — swoops in, attacks, swoops out. Venomous stinger does massive poison damage.

## The Reach Trick (Pseudo-Flyby)

- Bite: **10 ft. reach**. Stinger: **10 ft. reach**. Claws: 5 ft. reach.
- Default Multiattack: **Bite + Stinger** at 10 ft. — never enters 5 ft. reach of target, never leaves it, **no opportunity attack provoked**
- Accomplishes Flyby effect without the Flyby feature
- Exception: polearm wielders (wyvern too dumb to adapt)

## When to Use Claws

- Only when **grounded** (knocked prone, grappled, restrained) and regaining movement
- Claw/Stinger Multiattack **while launching back into the air** — eats one OA per opponent but avoids staying grounded against Extra Attack enemies
- If it **can't** get airborne: Bite/Stinger only (claws require flight per MM)

## Prey Selection and Grapple

- Predator — targets **weakest-looking, isolated, or oblivious** individual
- Singles out one target for full aggression
- When prey falls unconscious: forgoes Multiattack → **grapple attack** (with advantage vs. unconscious)
- On successful grapple: flies off to nest. Small creature = full speed; Medium = half speed (80 ft. with Dash)

## Retreat

Seriously wounded (≤ 44 hp) → **Dashes away** to find easier prey.
