# Neogi Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/neogi-tactics/) | **Date**: October 2, 2017
> **Book**: Volo's Guide to Monsters

---

## Overview

- Spider-bodied, worm-headed psychic slavers with a hyper-hierarchical worldview.
- **Physically weak** — power comes from psychic abilities.
- High Dex + Con, low Str → **skirmishers** that prefer to outnumber opponents.
- Equal-status neogi cooperate only under a higher-status neogi's command. Lone neogi avoid fights without a clear advantage.
- Darkvision (60 ft) + Perception proficiency → prefer night/underground engagements.
- Proficient in Intimidation; above-average Wis → will bluff and bluster when outmatched.

## Combat — Basic Neogi

1. **Goal**: Capture a creature to Enslave. Fighters, rogues, bards (low Wis) are prime targets.
2. **Multiattack**: Claw (slashing) + Bite (poison damage + poisoned condition → disadvantage on attacks/checks).
3. **Enslave** (1/encounter): Attempts to dominate a target's mind. Won't try against creatures with advantage on charm saves (elves, gnomes).
4. **Movement**: Uses climbing speed to stay out of reach of tougher opponents. Uses Dodge action if can't reach a target.

## Retreat

- **Solo neogi**: Retreats when moderately wounded (≤23 hp). Dodges until clear, then Dashes.
- **Group**: Sticks around until seriously wounded (≤13 hp). When more than half have fled, the rest follow.

## Neogi Master

- 2× the hit points of a regular neogi + spellcasting.
- **Hold person** is the star spell — cast at 4th level to immobilize up to 3 targets. Shuts down the enslavement target AND their defenders.
- If *hold person* fails and master gets rushed: *arms of Hadar* (5d6 necrotic to everyone within 10 ft) or *dimension door* to escape.
- **Eldritch blast** (2 salvos, 1d10+4 each) for ranged sniping while minions do the dirty work.
- Multiattack is still preferred if it can reach its desired target.

## Neogi Hatchlings

- Even weaker than adults. **Fight only in self-defense**.
- If feeding on a great old master: stay put, bite anything that comes near. Won't flee or pursue.
- If nothing anchors them: Dash away, preferably up walls and across ceilings.
