# Beholder-Kin Tactics

Three beholder variants from Volo's: death kiss (CR 10), gauth (CR 6), and gazer (CR ½).

## Death Kiss

Melee brute with blood-draining tentacles. No ranged attack.

- **Hovers 10 ft. up** (15 ft. if any foe has a polearm) — 20 ft. tentacle reach keeps it out of melee
- **Tentacle hit → grapple/restrain → Blood Drain** (damages enemy, heals death kiss)
- 3 tentacle attacks per Multiattack; substitute Blood Drain for any tentacle on a grappled target
- **Picks off the weakest** target first; drains one victim dry before moving on
- With 3 tentacles on one victim: all 3 Multiattack uses become Blood Drain
- **Opportunity attacks at 20 ft. reach** — creatures can wander into and out of range without realizing; lash out with a free tentacle
- Multiple grappled victims: divide Blood Drain proportionally (more tentacles = more drain)
- Can fly with grappled prey (full speed if Small, half if Medium+)
- **Retreat:** ≤64 hp → release prey and Dash away; ≤112 hp → move away from ranged attackers
- **Lightning Blood:** Piercing/slashing melee hits deal lightning damage back to the attacker

## Gauth

Mini-beholder that behaves like a standard beholder with lower INT/WIS.

- **Stunning Gaze** is superior to Antimagic Cone — doesn't suppress its own Eye Rays; always active, forces enemies to avert eyes (disadvantage on attacks) or risk being stunned
- **Devour Magic Ray** suppresses magic item properties until start of gauth's next turn — doesn't permanently destroy enchantments (don't tell the players that)
- **Death Throes:** AoE damage on death; doesn't influence tactics
- Targets and ray selection follow standard beholder logic but with less precision
- **Retreat:** ≤26 hp → Disengage (action) then Dash away; smart enough to Disengage

## Gazer

Tiny, truculent micro-beholder. Usually found in clusters around a full beholder.

- Proficient in Stealth; hides in dark recesses of the lair
- Stays at max Eye Ray range (60 ft.); takes potshots from shadows
- If approached: retreat to safe distance, Dash if necessary, then Eye Rays again
- **Not smart enough** to Disengage or Dodge
- Aggressive trait used only if needed to close to 60 ft.
- **Retreat:** ≤5 hp → Dash away

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Death kiss opportunity attacks at 20 ft. are nasty — track reach carefully on the grid
> - Gauth's Devour Magic Ray is a great dramatic moment: describe the magic item going dead, let the player panic, then it reignites next round
> - Gazers are minions/sentries — scatter them around a beholder lair as early warning
