# Flameskull Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/flameskull-tactics/) | **Date**: September 22, 2016

---

## Tactical Summary

- **Undead guardian** compelled to protect a place, item, or person. No self-preservation — fights to destruction.
- **Rejuvenation**: Re-forms fully healed 1 hour after destruction unless fragments are hit with *holy water*, *dispel magic*, or *remove curse*. Party may fight it again on the way out.
- **Int 16**: Smart targeting — prioritizes clerics/paladins (who can turn it), then wizards/warlocks/sorcerers/druids.
- **All ranged/magical attacks**. Hovers out of melee reach when possible.
- **Illumination is flavor**: Dim glow base state, bright when alerted. Does NOT use darkness to hide.

## Combat Behavior

**Opening move depends on space**:
- PCs have >40 ft to spread out → *fireball* immediately before they scatter.
- Tight space → holds *fireball* in reserve; opens with *flaming sphere* for bonus-action economy.

**Spell priority**:
- *Fireball* (8d6, 20-ft radius, ~87 hp expected across 4 targets) — the nuke. Saved for when PCs cluster or when moderately damaged (≤28 hp).
- *Flaming sphere* (2d6/turn, sustained) — rammed into cleric > paladin > spellcaster each turn as bonus action.
- *Blur* — cast instead of *flaming sphere* if taking bludgeoning, slashing, acid, radiant, or thunder damage.
- *Magic missile* (10.5 hp; 2nd-level = 14 hp) — used when all enemies are in melee (disadvantage on ranged).
- *Shield* (reaction) — pops whenever attack roll lands 13–17.

**Fire Ray Multiattack**: Default when out of relevant slots. Two rays at +5, 3d6 fire each. ~12 hp expected at range, ~6 hp in melee (disadvantage).

## Retreat Conditions

- **Never retreats or flees**. Bound by compulsion.
- If turned, beelines back to post as soon as turning ends.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The *fireball* opener is devastating to low-level parties — seed warnings (scorch marks, charred remains). Rejuvenation is the real surprise: unless PCs use *holy water* or *dispel magic*, they face it again in an hour. Keep it hovering 40+ ft up when ceilings allow.
