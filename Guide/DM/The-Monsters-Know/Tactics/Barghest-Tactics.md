# Barghest Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Fiendish shapechanger — fast (60 ft.), intelligent shock attacker. Infiltrates in goblin form by day, hunts in true form at night. Fearless against most damage but terrified of sustained fire (Fire Banishment) and wary of radiant/thunder. No Multiattack — one devastating bite per round, hit-and-run.

## Shapechanger

### Goblin Form (Day)
- Blends into goblinoid communities; proficient in Deception + Stealth
- **Conducts recon** — identifies party composition, notes spellcasters
- Claw only (no bite); never fights in this form willingly
- If cornered: *dimension door* escape → wait a full day → return in true form with surprise

### True Form (Night)
- 60 ft. speed, darkvision + blindsight; attacks in pitch darkness
- Always initiates from hiding (Stealth proficiency)

## Spellcasting

| Spell | Use |
|---|---|
| *Pass without trace* (at will) | Clean getaway when pursued |
| *Charm person* (1/day) | Fallback if Deception fails |
| *Suggestion* (1/day) | Manipulate NPCs |
| *Dimension door* (1/day) | Emergency escape |
| *Levitate* (at will) | Access rooftops; never if observed |

## Combat

- **Bite only** (2 dice); never uses claw if bite available; no Multiattack
- Indifferent to OAs — high AC + resistances; charges straight to priority target
- Hit-and-run: bite priority target, keep moving; if no target in 60 ft., bite someone along the way
- **Avoids:** magic weapon wielders entirely

### Priority Targets
1. Casters using damaging/evocation spells (fire = Fire Banishment risk)
2. Radiant/thunder dealers (paladins)
3. Weakest available target

## Fire Banishment (Critical Weakness)

- Starts turn in fire ≥10 ft. wide → risks banishment to Gehenna
- Requires persistent flames (*wall of fire*, bonfires) — NOT *fireball*
- Barghest can't predict fire spells — legitimate exploitable weakness

## Retreat Conditions

- ≤36 hp: flees (*dimension door* or Dodge + speed)
- ≤63 hp: flees early if opponents deal radiant, thunder, necrotic, or force damage
- Paladin on field: may avoid the fight entirely

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Goblin form is a social encounter — it's gathering intel before it ever attacks
> - If it spots a druid/wizard, it may never attack or neutralize them first
> - Can be killed on the material plane — real survival stakes
> - Great "something is hunting goblins" mystery that becomes "something is hunting *us*"
