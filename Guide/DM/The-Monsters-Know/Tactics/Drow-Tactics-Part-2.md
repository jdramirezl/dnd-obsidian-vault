# Drow Tactics, Part 2: Drow Mage

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/drow-tactics-part-2/) | **Date**: September 28, 2016
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Elves-Drow]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- **CR 7 spellcaster** — fragile in melee (Str 9). Has shield but NOT counterspell.
- **Priority**: Eliminate enemy spellcasters first.
- **Shadow demon summoning** is always the first action — 50% chance of CR 4 ally dealing ~12 hp/round independently.
- **Core decision**: Cloudkill OR greater invisibility (concentration conflict).

## Summon Demon

- **Always attempt shadow demon first** (50% chance, ~12 hp expected damage/round).
- Shadow demon hides as bonus action (+7 Stealth), attacks with advantage. Immune to poison — drifts through cloudkill hitting backline.

## Cloudkill vs Greater Invisibility

**Cloudkill when ALL THREE conditions met**:
1. ≥2 identifiable non-dwarf spellcasters in party
2. Room small enough to trap PCs, or ≥2 non-dwarf spellcasters close enough to engulf
3. Drow mage has escape route not through the cloud

**Otherwise**: Greater invisibility.

## Combat Sequence

1. Summon shadow demon
2. Cloudkill or greater invisibility
3. **If invisible**: Line up lightning bolt through ≥2 PCs (prioritize spellcasters). Highest slot available. Out of slots → magic missile.
4. **If visible, cloudkill dropped**: Evard's black tentacles on melee fighters → witch bolt on toughest restrained target.
5. **Melee opponent closes**: Misty step (bonus) → ray of frost (action). Ray of frost second to avoid point-blank disadvantage.
6. **Fighters escape tentacles**: Web to restrain again.

## Key Spell Notes

- **Shield (reaction)**: Pop when attacker rolls 15–19 to hit.
- **Witch bolt**: Damage hose from single slot — staying power.
- **Darkness**: Drop on enemy spellcaster if no melee attacker requires misty step escape.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Moderately wounded (≤31 hp)**: Starts thinking about retreating.
- **Seriously wounded (≤18 hp)**: Retreats immediately.
- Leaves shadow demon as rearguard if present.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The shadow demon fights independently while the mage slings spells. The cloudkill-vs-invisibility decision should be made before the encounter based on room size and party composition. The mage is fond of its skin and will flee early.
