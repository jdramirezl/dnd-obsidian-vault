# Astral Dreadnought Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/astral-dreadnought-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- Gargantuan astral predator (CR 21) with epic Strength/Constitution but animal-level Intelligence—pure instinct, no strategy
- Antimagic Cone (150-ft, 53° arc) suppresses spells AND magic weapon properties, forcing foes to close in
- Passive traits do heavy lifting: Astral Entity, Demiplanar Donjon, Sever Silver Cord, Magic Weapons
- Multiattack is Claw/Claw/Bite; Bite can swallow weakened prey into its internal demiplane
- Legendary actions: Claw (retaliatory), Donjon Visit (2 actions, removes a foe for 1 round), Psychic Projection (3 actions, AoE psychic)

## Combat Behavior
- **Antimagic Cone**: Aimed at start of turn at the greatest number of foes (counts farthest-to-nearest to break ties). Can move backward 40 ft to reposition the cone over enemies. Can reorient vertically
- **Target selection**: Attacks whoever's hurting it most. If nobody is, targets the weakest (easiest to eat). Bite is reserved for eating; Claws for removing hindrances to eating
- **Multiattack order**: Claw/Claw/Bite normally. If first Claw reduces target to ≤34 hp, Bite immediately after. If incapacitated foe is in Bite reach, Bite first
- **Positioning**: Wants prey within 10 ft for Bite. Stays 20 ft from "hindrance" targets for Claw-only. OAs: Bites prey moving beyond 10 ft; Claws serious combatants moving beyond 20 ft
- **Legendary Resistance**: Used reflexively every time it fails any save (not strategic enough to conserve)

## Key Decision Points
- **Legendary actions**: Claw = retaliatory (any foe dealing >0 but <30 dmg). Donjon Visit = removes foe dealing 30+ dmg in one turn (costs 2). Psychic Projection = all 3 actions, used only when ALL foes are within 60 ft, or as reaction to being moderately/seriously wounded with ≥2/3 of foes in range
- **Antimagic Cone vs. melee**: The cone forces enemies to close in, but once they fan out at close range, it can only cover a narrow arc. This is the dreadnought's key weakness
- **Eating vs. fighting**: It distinguishes between prey (close to 10 ft, Bite) and hindrances (keep at 20 ft, Claw only)

## Retreat Conditions
- Deadly encounter: pushes on regardless
- Hard encounter: backs off at ≤118 hp (seriously wounded), Dodging while retreating (not smart enough to Disengage)
- Medium/Easy encounter: backs off immediately after taking moderate damage (≤207 hp)

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The Antimagic Cone creates a tactical puzzle—players must fan out at close range to avoid it, but the dreadnought wants them close. Let the cone's positioning drive the encounter's geometry.
