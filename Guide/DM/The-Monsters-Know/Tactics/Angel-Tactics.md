# Angel Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/angel-tactics/) | **Related**: [[Angels]]

---

## Tactical Summary
- All angels share: incredible stats, magic weapon resistance, charm/fear/exhaustion immunity, flight, Healing Touch, lawful good alignment
- Angels **attack to subdue, not kill** (except vs. fiends and undead)—they use "Knocking a Creature Out" on melee hits
- Target priority by alignment: CE → CN/NE → LE/N → LN/CG → NG → LG
- Can't be fooled—high Insight, Divine Awareness (planetar/solar), they know your weaknesses instantly
- Fight while hovering, exploiting superior flight speed

## Combat Behavior
- **Deva**: 2x mace Multiattack. Skirmishes vs. brutes (swoop, attack, fly up accepting OA risk). Closes and stays vs. ranged/casters. Change Shape is disguise-only—reverts before combat
- **Planetar**: Deva tactics plus spells. *Blade barrier* ring to isolate the most evil foe. *Flame strike* on 2+ top targets. *Insect plague* on 4+. *Invisibility* to disengage from foes it doesn't want to fight. *Dispel evil and good* for Dismissal or concentration disadvantage vs. fiends/undead
- **Solar**: Legendary Teleport (up to 3x/round on opponents' turns) stays one step ahead. Searing Burst on 2+ targets within 10 ft. Greatsword-only unless fighting fiends/undead (then adds Slaying Longbow via Flying Sword). *Blade barrier* wall + Teleport forces enemies through it repeatedly

## Key Decision Points
- Always subdue living creatures; only kill fiends and undead
- Deva retreat at ≤54 hp (situational—depends on duty). Dodge vs. single attack, Dash vs. Extra Attack, Disengage when outnumbered
- Planetar retreat at ≤80 hp. Can use *dispel evil and good* Dismissal on itself to return home
- Solar: never retreats

## Retreat Conditions
- Devas: Situational at ≤54 hp
- Planetars: ≤80 hp
- Solars: Never

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Angels should feel like an overwhelming force that *doesn't want to hurt you*—make players feel the weight of fighting something genuinely merciful yet terrifyingly competent.
