# Relentless Killer Tactics

## Relentless Slasher (CR 8)

### Tactical Summary

Shock attacker — exceptional DEX, high INT. Strikes hard and fast, avoids damage. Shrouded Presence hides from mind-reading, Divine Sense, *detect evil and good* — but no Stealth proficiency. Uses obstructed sight lines (forests, rooftops, urban areas) and 120 ft darkvision. Strikes at night.

### Attack Pattern

- **Slasher's Knife**: melee or ranged (30 ft), avg 27.5 damage (6.5 slashing + 21 necrotic) + lingering HP loss
- Preferred: within 30 ft but **not** melee reach — fling 2 knives, move behind cover
- 8 knives total — switches to melee after 7 throws

### Legendary Actions: Vanishing Strike Only

- **Never use Slice** — 3 Slices = 22.5 damage, no riders
- **1 Vanishing Strike** (3 LA) = Slasher's Knife + teleport — strictly superior
- Edge case: Slice ×3 to pile death saves on unconscious target

### Initiative Awareness

- High INT → tracks initiative after round 1
- Times Vanishing Strike to teleport away just before strongest melee fighter's turn

### Retreat

- **Never** — fights until target is dead-dead or slasher is destroyed

## Relentless Juggernaut (CR 12)

### Tactical Summary

Fearless brute — bonkers STR, high CON, caveman INT (8). Regenerates unless radiant damage taken. 120 ft darkvision, stalks at night.

### Multiattack

- **Executioner's Pick ×2**: avg 34 damage, slows target — default
- **Fist**: only worth it if **disarmed** (math proves Pick always better otherwise)
- **Deadly Shaping** (if available): always worth using
  - **Flying Stone** vs melee who stand and fight (prevents fighting back 1 round)
  - **Scything Shrapnel** vs weaklings or fleeing opponents (more damage over time)

### Legendary Actions

- **Recharge Deadly Shaping** (3 LA at will) — amazing
- **Implacable Advance**: smash through barricades — situational

### Retreat

- **Never** — nothing named "relentless juggernaut" runs from a fight

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Slasher = supernatural serial killer (Michael Myers / Jason Voorhees archetype)
> - Juggernaut uses Legendary Resistances reflexively (caveman brain)
> - Both originated as other creature types transformed into fiends
