# NPC Tactics: Scouts and Spies

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — NPC Tactics: Scouts and Spies](https://www.themonstersknow.com/npc-tactics-scouts-and-spies/)

## Scouts

Spotters and lookouts. Proficient in Perception and Stealth. Job: gather info and return — combat is undesirable.

- Position ≥75 ft from targets (150 ft if good view)
- Attack only in self-defense
- Pursuers ≤30 ft speed and >75 ft away → potshots (Multiattack, Longbow ×2)
- Pursuers closer or faster → Dash away
- 2+ enemies in melee or can't afford a hit → Disengage
- Surrounded with no escape → drop bow, draw sword
- May surrender if capture isn't a death sentence

## Spies

Operate in enemies' midst. Not assassins but may fight to the death if caught. Need advantage for Sneak Attack but stat block provides no way to blind/paralyze/prone/restrain/stun.

**Multiattack loophole**: "Two melee attacks" (not named) → can grapple/shove. But Str 10 makes this unreliable. Only useful for interrogation under threat, not killing.

**Best melee Sneak Attack use**: Taking out guards in dim light from hiding — avg 18 damage (50% more than ranged).

**If busted by a party**: Disengage (2+ opponents in melee) or Dash (can afford one hit). Cunning Action: Hide as soon as line of sight breaks. Can Dash as action AND bonus → 90 ft. Or Disengage + Dash → 60 ft, zero OAs.

**If cornered**: Fight to the death. Captured spies are nearly always killed; organizations depend on secrecy.

**Spies operate solo** — tactically disadvantageous but operationally necessary.
