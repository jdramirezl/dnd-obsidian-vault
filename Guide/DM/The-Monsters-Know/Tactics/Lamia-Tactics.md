# Lamia Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Lamia Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/lamia-tactics/)
> **Bestiary:** [[Lamia]]

---

## Tactical Summary

Lamias are smooth seducers and corrupters with high Strength, Charisma, and double proficiency in Deception. They prefer guile over brute force — manipulation is plan A, melee combat is plan B. They use *disguise self* to hide their monstrous nature, but at ~8 ft tall (minimum ~7 ft disguised), their height is a tell.

## Combat Behavior

### Pre-Combat (Manipulation Phase)
- Controls access to its presence — grants audience to **one PC at a time**
- Uses *disguise self* to appear humanoid (still suspiciously tall at ~7 ft minimum)
- Approaches PC, uses **Intoxicating Touch** (disadvantage on Wis saves and all ability checks for 1 hour) disguised as a caress
- Uses Deception to seem plausible, Insight to read intentions
- Follows with *charm person* or *geas* to bend the PC to its will
- Once one PC is controlled, invites the next for the same treatment
- DC 13 Intelligence (Investigation) to see through *disguise self* — but with disadvantage after Intoxicating Touch

### Combat Phase (cover blown)
- **First action:** *Mirror image* (reduces incoming damage by ~75%). Recasts when third duplicate is destroyed — up to 3 total castings.
- **Every other turn:** Claw/dagger Multiattack (avg 20 HP damage if both hit). No more Intoxicating Touch — the goal is now to slash and stab.
- **Target Priority:** Extra Attack opponents (lamia's AC is unexceptional), glass-cannon spellcasters within reach, high-Wisdom opponents (out of spite, but won't override battlefield judgment).
- **Minion Direction:** Directs minions (jackalweres, cultists) to wherever opponents are attacking most effectively.

## Key Decision Points

1. **One at a time:** Never let the whole party see the lamia at once during the manipulation phase.
2. **Intoxicating Touch timing:** Before combat, disguised as a caress. In combat, only useful for imposing disadvantage on grappling or Stealth checks — not worth the action.
3. **Mirror image management:** Recast each time the third duplicate falls. Three total castings available.

## Retreat Conditions

- Attempts to **surrender and bargain** when **seriously wounded (≤38 HP)**.
- Values power above all — will give up treasure but not influential position.
- Won't accept public abasement or readily release slaves/thralls.
- Will offer scrying services, manpower, or other favors.
- **Will betray any bargain** if given an opening to turn the tables.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Masculine lamias exist and make great fakeouts. The lamia's height (~7 ft minimum with *disguise self*) is the key tell — let PCs make that DC 13 Investigation check before Intoxicating Touch lands, because afterward they have disadvantage. The lamia is covetous, unprincipled, and scheming — it will absolutely renege on any deal.
