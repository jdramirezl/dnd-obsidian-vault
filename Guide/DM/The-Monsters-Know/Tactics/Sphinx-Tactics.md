# Sphinx Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/sphinx-tactics/) | **Date**: December 9, 2016

---

## Overview

[[Sphinxes]] are bosses — divinely summoned spirits. Physical brutes with exceptional mental stats across the board. 120 ft truesight, immune to charm/frighten/psychic damage. Fly faster than they walk. Two magical claw attacks per action. Three legendary actions: single claw, teleport, or cast a spell (3 actions — emergency only). Inscrutable shields them from mind-reading.

## Default Combat

- **Brutes** → default is toe-to-toe melee. No compelling reason to vary.
- Teleport (legendary action) at end of a distant foe's turn to close distance, then legendary claw attack before that foe's next turn.

## Shared Spells

- **Banishment** (4th level): Pack targets off to another plane. Gynosphinx can boost to 5th (2 targets); androsphinx to 6th (3 targets). Safe bet if target's CHA save mod is ≤+1 (gynosphinx) or ≤+3 (androsphinx). Avoid bards, paladins, sorcerers, warlocks. Purpose: punish the impertinent, remove magic-weapon wielders and heavy spellcasters.
- **Dispel Magic**: Only if a nerf/buff is causing actual trouble. Worth a 4th-level slot if needed, not 5th+.

## Gynosphinx-Specific Spells

- **Greater Invisibility**: Only if no good banishment target *and* taking moderate damage (40+ hp) from ranged attackers. Requires concentration (can't stack with banishment).
- **Darkness**: Advantageous (truesight) but concentration-locked — inferior to banishment/invisibility.
- **Suggestion**: Only to mess with opponents already failing to do meaningful damage.
- **Shield**: Automatic reaction when a magic-weapon attacker rolls 17–21 to hit.

## Androsphinx-Specific Spells

- **Flame Strike** (5th level): Only if ≥2 enemies in the AoE. Good vs airborne. Save the 6th-level slot for banishment. Best used *after* Second Roar paralyzes targets.
- **Freedom of Movement**: Only to escape magical restraint (not paralysis — somatic component).
- **Command**: Marginal — only to provoke opportunity attacks. Low value given other options.
- **Sacred Flame**: 3d8 radiant (12th-level caster). Decent fallback.

## Androsphinx Roar (3/day, sequential)

1. **First Roar** (always round 1): Frighten within 500 ft (WIS save). Disadvantage on attacks/checks, can't approach. Needs +4 WIS save to have 1-in-3 chance of resisting.
2. **Second Roar**: Frighten + deafen + **paralyze**. Use when ≥ half opponents are no longer frightened from First Roar. Follow immediately with flame strike (auto-fail DEX saves). Melee attacks get advantage + auto-crits.
3. **Third Roar**: Massive thunder damage + prone (CON save). Use after flame strike follow-up (or decision not to).

## Retreat

- **Never**. Duty-driven, not self-preserving. Fights to the death in service to its mission.
