# Kobold Inventor & Kobold Scale Sorcerer Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Kobolds Revisited](https://www.themonstersknow.com/kobolds-revisited/)
> **Bestiary:** [[Kobolds]]

---

## Tactical Summary

Kobolds are highly cooperative ambush fighters that instinctively work together without discussion. They rely on superior numbers, traps, hit-and-run tactics, and prepared battlefields. The Volo's variants — dragonshield, inventor, and scale sorcerer — add specialized roles to the kobold warband.

## Combat Behavior

### General Kobold Tactics
- Attack light sources to exploit darkvision; leave bait to lure into traps
- Hit-and-run: flee between attacks to better vantage points, drawing enemies into danger
- Use poison from vermin on weapons or leave pots of vermin as false treasure
- Lairs feature deadfall traps, choke points, murder holes, escape tunnels, narrow passages

### Kobold Dragonshield
- Effective **skirmisher** with above-average Dex/Con
- Charges in to jab with spear, then **Dodges and retreats**, drawing pursuers into traps
- Attacks until taking ≥5 HP damage, then switches to Dodge + retreat
- Heart of the Dragon shakes off frightened/paralyzed for itself and nearby allies

### Kobold Scale Sorcerer
- *Scorching ray* and *chromatic orb* — both benefit from Pack Tactics as spell attacks
- *Expeditious retreat* to slip away from melee; *fire bolt*/*poison spray* when out of slots
- **Heightened Spell** with *charm person* to get enemies to walk into traps
- **Subtle Spell** with *mage hand* to trigger traps remotely; also bypasses *counterspell*

### Kobold Inventor
- Round 1 Weapon Invention (ranked): 1) Green Slime Pot (6 HP + wastes action), 2) Rot Grub Pot (9 HP recurring), 3) Basket of Centipedes (8 HP + paralysis on KO), 4) Wasp Nest (8 HP), 5) Skunk in a Cage (hilarious but risky), 6) Scorpion on a Stick (2 HP/round reusable), 7) Acid Flask (3 HP), 8) Alchemist's Fire (weak)

## Key Decision Points

1. **Inventor's invention:** Green Slime Pot is best general choice; Rot Grub Pot for area denial.
2. **Scale Sorcerer positioning:** Behind dragonshields; *charm person* + Heightened to weaponize traps.
3. **Dragonshield commitment:** Only after traps have softened the party.

## Retreat Conditions

- All kobold variants retreat and regroup when injured or isolated.
- Dragonshields Dodge and retreat after taking ≥5 HP damage.
- Kobolds flee entirely if they lose numerical superiority.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Use multiple initiative rolls for large kobold groups (20+) to spread turns throughout the round. The scale sorcerer's *scorching ray* benefits from Pack Tactics — don't forget advantage on those attack rolls.
