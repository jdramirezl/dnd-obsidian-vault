# Kirin Tactics

> **Source:** [The Monsters Know — Kirin Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/kirin-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary

The kirin is a lawful good celestial that avoids violence whenever possible. Combat occurs only when a PC attacks it or when it defends against intrinsically evil creatures (fiends, undead). Equally suited to melee and spellcasting with extraordinary Strength matched by high Int, Wis, and Cha. Fights from the air, swooping to strike and retreating out of reach, leveraging AC 20 and legendary actions.

## Combat Behavior

- **Movement:** Fights airborne exclusively — flying speed is double ground speed, can hover. Swoops for melee, flies back up out of reach.
- **Opening Moves:** *Guardian of faith* (60 free radiant damage) if protecting someone. *Sanctuary* (bonus action) on protected character turn 1, *protection from evil and good* turn 2 vs fiends/undead.
- **Melee Multiattack:** Two hooves + one horn. Reserved for direct confrontation with fiends, undead, or evil PCs after defenses are laid.
- **Key Combat Spells:** *Banishment* (single outsize evil threat), *mass cure wounds* (when allies within 30 ft have taken ≥14 damage), *silence* (clustered enemy casters), *dispel magic* (harmful enemy spells), *sacred flame* (4d8 radiant cantrip, combinable with *sanctuary*).
- **Legendary Actions:** Detect (Perception/Insight), Smite (free hoof or *sacred flame*), Move (30 ft ground / 60 ft fly, no OAs). Uses Smite against enemies that fly up to engage.
- **Legendary Resistance:** Always to avoid incapacitation, restraint, paralysis, petrification, stun, unconsciousness, or banishment. Avoids damage only when moderately wounded (≤106 HP).
- **Targeting:** Chaotic evil first (most powerful), then neutral evil, then lawful evil.
- **Parley:** Once all evil characters are neutralized, pauses to reason with remaining opponents.

## Key Decision Points

1. **Guardian of faith vs. banishment:** Guardian if protecting someone or facing many threats; banishment if one enemy is disproportionately dangerous.
2. **Melee vs. spellcasting:** Spells from altitude first, melee only after defenses are established.
3. **Dodge stalling:** Can simply Dodge turn after turn with AC 20, giving disadvantage on all attacks.

## Retreat Conditions

- Flees via *plane shift* to Mount Celestia when **seriously injured (≤60 HP)**.
- *Gaseous form* as secondary escape if *plane shift* is unavailable.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> The kirin's spell list is enormous but most are non-combat favors. In practice, combat spells boil down to: *guardian of faith*, *banishment*, *mass cure wounds*, *silence*, *dispel magic*, *sacred flame*, and *spare the dying*. Pre-cast *true seeing* and *freedom of movement* if trouble is anticipated.
