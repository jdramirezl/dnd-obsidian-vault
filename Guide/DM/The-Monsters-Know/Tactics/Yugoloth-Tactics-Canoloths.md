# Yugoloth Tactics: Canoloths

## Tactical Summary

Quadrupedal, doglike yugoloth guard. Expertise in Perception + Investigation, 120 ft. truesight, immune to surprise (unless incapacitated). **Dimensional Lock**: suppresses teleportation in 60 ft. radius. Exceptional Str, Con, Wis. Low Int — can't do much with its perceptiveness tactically. **Never leaves its post.**

## Combat Routine

1. **Tongue attack** (30 ft. ranged) — lashes out at first trespasser entering its zone of control
2. **Multiattack: Tongue + Claws** — Tongue grapples/restrains on hit, pulls target adjacent; Claws with advantage (target restrained)
3. While grappling (one at a time): **Bite + Claws** Multiattack with advantage, every turn
4. **Terrier focus**: ignores all other enemies while holding a grappled foe — nothing distracts it

## If Target Escapes (DC 15)

- Tongue is a **ranged** weapon attack → **cannot** make opportunity attacks with it (RAW)
- Uses **Bite** as opportunity attack when foe leaves 5 ft. reach
- (House rule option: treat Tongue as melee for OA purposes)

## Damage Threshold Switching

- **Melee attacker** deals ≥ 12 damage (past immunities/resistances): switches attention → Bite/Claws Multiattack. Multiple melee attackers dealing ≥ 12: attacks whoever dealt the most.
- **Ranged attacker** deals ≥ 12 damage within 30 ft.: Tongue/Claws to snatch them.
- Ranged attacker **out of reach** or no attacker deals ≥ 12: picks a melee opponent at random.
- Taking ranged damage from > 30 ft. with no melee opponent: **howls in frustration**, telepathically summons help — but **stays at post**.

## Retreat

**Never.** Not even in Gehenna. A canoloth fights to the death at its post.

## Special Orders

If ordered to capture rather than kill: follows same combat heuristic but makes attacks **nonlethal**.

## Note on Dimensional Lock

Low Int (5) means the canoloth doesn't notice its own aura's effect. But if fighting alongside other yugoloths (nycaloths, yagnoloths), it **locks down their Teleport abilities** — potentially changing allied tactics.
