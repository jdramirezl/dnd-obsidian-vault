# Thri-kreen Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Thri-kreen Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/thri-kreen-tactics/)

**Bestiary:** [[Thri-kreen]]

---

## Overview

Thri-kreen are four-armed mantis-warrior humanoids. Chaotic neutral — they largely want to be left alone. They consider all living creatures as potential nourishment (especially elves), but won't attack humanoids just to eat them unless food is scarce.

## Ability Profile

- **High Dexterity**, above-average Strength/Constitution — flexible between brute melee and skirmishing, slight bias toward skirmishing
- Speed 40 ft, proficiency in **Perception** and **Stealth**, **Chameleon Carapace** (advantage on Hide) — ambush attackers
- **Standing Leap** — home field advantage over slower foes in difficult terrain (savanna grass, desert rocks/sand)
- **Poisonous Bite** — DC easy to beat for high-Con targets; poisoned opponents attack with disadvantage

## Combat Tactics

- Ambush from hiding when foes come within **30 feet**, attack with surprise
- Distribute attacks across opponents who don't appear to have above-average Constitution
- Fight hand-to-hand until opponent is down or thri-kreen is **moderately wounded (≤23 hp)**
- At that point, chitter for help — allies who've poisoned their opponents leap over to assist; wounded thri-kreen leaps away to fight someone easier
- Constant chittering: "I'm hurt," "This one is tough," "This one is poisoned" — they **exchange info but don't truly coordinate**
- 1–2 others may flank behind a tough opponent for flanking bonus

### Retreat

- **Half the group seriously wounded (≤13 hp)** or **all moderately wounded** → call retreat
- Outnumbered → **Dash**; not outnumbered → **Dodge**
- Regular thri-kreen are not smart enough to Disengage

## Variant: Soldier Thri-kreen

- Disciplined enough to **Disengage**; stay to cover allies' escape
- Engage the toughest opponents
- Round 1: close to 30 ft, throw two chatkchas (Multiattack)
- Round 2+: close and fight with gythka (double-bladed polearm), two attacks per Multiattack

## Variant: Psionic Thri-kreen

- Usually also soldiers (need weapons for *magic weapon* on polearms, not chatkchas)
- Use **invisibility** (1/day) to vanish and relocate after taking 10+ damage
- After using invisibility, cast **blur** upon re-manifesting
