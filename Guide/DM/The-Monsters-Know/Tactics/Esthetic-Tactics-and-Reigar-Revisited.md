# Esthetic Tactics (and Reigar Revisited)

> [!summary]
> The esthetic is a CR 12 living spelljammer ship — a melee brute with extraordinary Str/Con, blindsight 300 ft, and a recharging Jammerscream that disables enemy ships. Paired with its reigar owner, it disables ships, plucks crew from decks, and creates openings for the reigar's spells. Tier 3 encounter minimum.

---

## Esthetic
- 60 ft flying speed — faster than most spelljamming ships (only damselfly/shrike are faster)
- Melee brute: extraordinary Str/Con, blindsight 300 ft
- Tentacles grapple creatures (not ships); can yank crew off other ships' decks

### Jammerscream (Bonus Action, Recharge 6)
- Disables another ship's helm — days if pilot doesn't save, hours on failed save, minutes on success
- Recharges only on 6 — count on using it once per encounter
- Can sucker-punch fast ships (damselfly/shrike) if they enter 300-ft range

### Combat Behavior
- Disables ship with Jammerscream → plucks crew from decks with tentacles
- Grappled targets take automatic damage each round (but aren't restrained)
- Reacts to 22+ damage in a single turn from a grappled target: dual-Tentacle Multiattack to smash it against the ship
- Otherwise uses Multiattack to strike the ship itself
- Not intelligent enough for other plans on its own

### Retreat
- Seriously wounded (≤86 hp): departs regardless of reigar's wishes
- Drops grappled targets to deck or leaves them floating in space
- Reigar must *dimension door* back on its own

---

## Reigar (Revisited)
- Emerges from esthetic after Jammerscream disables enemy ship
- First free round: Summon Duplicate (formerly "Summon Golem")
- *Mass suggestion* on grappled foes: artsy, psychopathic demands ("Sing 'Hooray!' for the hardness of walls!")
- After that: slugfest — reigar + duplicate chase crew, shooting Chromatic Bolts

---

## Encounter Design
- Absolutely a **tier 3 encounter** for a fair fight
- Potentially deadly for tier 2; fatal at tier 1
- Crew may not realize the reigar is a separate threat at first — gives it a free round

> [!tip] DM Tips
> Jammerscream first, always. Esthetic plucks crew while reigar summons duplicate and casts *mass suggestion*. The esthetic is the real threat (CR 12) — the reigar is the personality. Esthetic leaves when hurt regardless of orders.
