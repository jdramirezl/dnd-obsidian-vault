# Dragon Follower & Dragonborn Champion Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/dragon-follower-and-dragonborn-champion-tactics/) | **Date**: September 1, 2022
> **Book**: Fizban's Treasury of Dragons

---

## Dragon Speaker

- **Backline support/commander**. Primary offense: Charisma. Primary defense: Dexterity.
- Hangs back behind cultist minions, hurls Thunder Bolts (works in melee too).
- **Command (1/day)**: Emergency escape — "Halt!" or "Flee!" to melee attacker, then withdraw without OA.
- **Disarming Words (3/day reaction)**: Reserves for self-defense if attacked; otherwise softens first hit on any ally.
- **Retreat at ≤14 hp**: Dashes away.

## Dragon Chosen

- **Shock attacker**: Exceptional Str/Dex. Triple Multiattack. Biting Rebuke (reaction).
- **Fights to the death** (fanatic).

## Dragon Blessed

- **Support caster** (Wisdom-driven). Opening: enhance ability (Bear's Endurance for temp hp) or flame strike variant.
- **Radiant Bolt**: Go-to when at least lightly wounded (≤67 hp) — deals damage AND heals self.
- **Mace**: Only if stuck in melee. Mass cure wounds when ≥6 allies each ≥19 hp below max.
- **Fights to the death**.

## Dragonborn of Sardior

- **Psionic spellcaster**. Exceptional Int, very high Dex/Con. Flies and hovers.
- **Preferred range**: 10–20 ft (Mind Blade ×3). At 20–60 ft: spells/breath only (disadvantage on Mind Blade).
- **Heat Breath (recharge 6)**: When ≥3 opponents in 30-ft cone. Wait for the moment.
- **Bigby's hand**: Favored concentration spell — versatile. Reskin as dragon claw.
- **Hypnotic pattern**: Use instead vs clearly strong/tough foes (targets Wis).
- Neutralizes ranged threats first from the air. **Fights to the death**.

## Dragonborn of Tiamat

- **Straightforward brute**. Extraordinary Str, exceptional Con. Cannot fly.
- Charges into melee, hacks away. Minimal target selection.
- **Necrotic Breath (recharge 6)**: When ≥3 in cone. Frightens on failed Wis save (can re-frighten).
- **Never retreats**.

## Dragonborn of Bahamut

- **Flying brute**. Charges boldly, goes airborne after seeing opponent with Extra Attack/Multiattack.
- **Radiant Breath (recharge 6)**: Dual-purpose — damages foes AND heals allies in cone. Use when ≥3 targets are foes or wounded allies (≤70% max hp).
- **Healing Touch**: Reserved for allies seriously wounded (≤40% max hp). Prioritizes worst-off by percentage.
- **Never retreats** (lawful good zealot is still a zealot).

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Monsters
> Dragon followers work best as entourage for actual dragons or Tyranny of Dragons cultist upgrades. Consider reskinning damage types to match patron dragon. All dragonborn champions are zealots — they never flee. Sardior is most tactically complex; Tiamat is simplest.
