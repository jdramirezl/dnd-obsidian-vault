# Neothelid Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/neothelid-tactics/) | **Date**: March 27, 2017
> **Book**: Volo's Guide to Monsters

---

## Overview

- **Mind flayer tadpole gone wrong** — grew unchecked, devouring everything. Gargantuan brute.
- Extraordinary Str/Con, below-average Dex, **subsentient Intelligence** (Int 3), high Wis.
- 120 ft blindsight; detects intelligent creatures up to 1 mile away (unless magically masked).
- **Animal cunning** but zero tactical flexibility. Operates purely on instinct.
- Can choose its moment to attack and avoid creatures of comparable power.
- Imperfectly detects which victims are weakest → goes after them first.
- Magic Resistance, but doesn't know what magic *is* → doesn't treat spellcasters differently.

## Attack Priority

### 1. Acid Breath (Recharge)
- 60-foot cone. Use when available AND can hit all opponents (or at least 6 if 7+).
- Part of the digestive process — dissolves tissue, leaves the brain intact.

### 2. Tentacle Attack
- 15-ft reach, bludgeoning + psychic damage. Str save or be **swallowed**.
- Two-thirds chance of swallowing targets with Str save modifier ≤ +3.
- Neothelid judges by **size and mass**, not training — a Str 16 fighter looks like easy prey even if their save modifier is +5.

### 3. Innate Spellcasting (Fallback Only)
Used only when Acid Breath is unavailable AND no target is within tentacle range (45 ft including movement).

**Three zones around the neothelid** (beyond 45 ft):
| Zone | Range | Spell |
|---|---|---|
| Red (45–105 ft) | *Telekinesis* | Pull a target with Str modifier ≤ 0 toward itself. Maintain until tentacle range. |
| Red/Blue (45–135 ft) | *Confusion* | Requires 2+ targets within 20 ft of each other. Maintain until tentacle attacks land. |
| Black (135–195 ft) | *Feeblemind* | Last resort. No good target selection basis — may target whoever's closest or whoever damaged it from range. |

- *Levitate* (at will) — useful for preventing prey from fleeing (target can't move without fly speed).

## Retreat

- Flees when **seriously wounded (≤130 hp)**. Dashes away.
- Drops *telekinesis* or *confusion* concentration immediately when fleeing.
