# Deep Scion Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Brainwashed humanoid transformed by an undersea pact. In humanoid form: a spy (expertise in Deception, proficiency in Insight/Sleight of Hand/Stealth). In hybrid form: a brute (exceptional STR, very high CON). Fights only when cover is blown — deception first, violence second. Preferred settings: **nighttime** and **underwater** (120 ft. darkvision, 40 ft. swim in hybrid form).

## Combat Behavior

### Form Management
- **Humanoid:** 30 ft. walk / 15 ft. swim
- **Hybrid:** 20 ft. walk / 40 ft. swim
- Transform at the **shoreline** mid-turn to maximize movement: walk to water → transform → 10 ft. swim remaining

### Underwater (Preferred)
1. **Psychic Screech** first (once/encounter, 30 ft.) — move within range of farthest foe, then screech
2. Close and maul with **Bite + 2 Claws**
3. Ideally attack a **stunned** opponent for advantage
4. Enemies fight at **disadvantage** underwater (most weapons)

### On Land
- **Daytime:** Runs toward nearest water — does not stop to transform and screech (too slow)
- **Night:** Darkvision gives edge over opponents without it
- Uses 2 battle axe strikes (nearly as much damage as Bite/Claw, but no underwater disadvantage on enemies)

### Social Fallback
- **Vs. level 4+ PCs:** Realizes it's outmatched at **≤46 hp** — tries to strike a deal (Insight to read PCs, Deception expertise to sell it). Has no intention of honoring it
- **Vs. level 2–3 PCs:** Fights until **≤26 hp**, then Disengages + Dashes into the deep

## Retreat Conditions

- Vs. higher-level PCs: negotiates dishonestly when moderately wounded
- Vs. level 2–3 PCs: Disengage + Dash into deep water when seriously wounded
- On land in daylight: flees toward water immediately

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - The real encounter is social, not combat — deep scions are spies first
> - Psychic Screech is once/encounter; always open with it underwater
> - Suitable boss only for levels 2–3; higher parties outclass it fast
> - PCs without water breathing can't pursue far — the deep scion knows this
> - Killed in hybrid form, it reverts to its original appearance — great dramatic reveal
