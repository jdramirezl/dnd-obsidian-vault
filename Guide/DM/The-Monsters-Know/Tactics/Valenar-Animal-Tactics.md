# Valenar Animal Tactics

## Overview

Fey animals inhabited by spirits of long-dead elven druids, bonded to humanoids (usually elves) as companions. Telepathic communication within 100 ft via **Bonding** trait. Classified as fey, not beasts.

## DIY Valenar Template

Apply to any beast stat block:
1. Add 21 total points to ability scores (INT ≥ 9, WIS ≥ 15, CHA ≥ 11)
2. Add Perception proficiency + adjust passive Perception
3. Understands Common, Elvish, Sylvan
4. Add Bonding trait
5. Recalculate attack bonus, damage, save DCs

## Combat Behavior

All three are **fast, flexible shock attackers** that Disengage and escape easily. Enhanced INT/WIS + Bonding enable coordination:

| Animal | Role |
|---|---|
| **Hawk** | Keen Sight spotter — telepathically directs companion's shots |
| **Hound** | Keen Smell finds hidden enemies → Bite knocks prone → tells companion to close for advantage |
| **Steed** | Excellent independent mount — acts freely while discussing options with rider |

## Who Runs It?

DM (it's an NPC). Ethically obligated not to screw the player, but not required to sacrifice the animal to save the PC.

## Self-Preservation

- **Seriously wounded** (≤40% max HP): Retreats, apologizes telepathically
- **Healthy + companion wounded**: Interposes itself to buy withdrawal time

## Sidekick Potential (Tasha's)

All qualify (CR ≤ 1/2). If spellcasting sidekick, limit to **Healer** role (ex-druids):
- Hawk: *sacred flame* on hidden targets
- Hound: *cure wounds* via licking
- Horse: *guidance* while being ridden

## Summoning Notes

- *Conjure animals*: Ineligible (fey, not beasts)
- *Conjure woodland beings*: Legal but wasteful — higher CR = fewer creatures, can't use Bonding in spell duration
- *Conjure fey*: Even more wasteful, but CR 6 ceiling allows Valenar elephant, orca, polar bear
