# Cadaver Collector Tactics

> [!summary]
> The cadaver collector is a monstrous Roomba from Acheron — a mechanistic juggernaut that scoops up corpses and recycles souls into specters. INT 5 means zero tactical variation: march toward the biggest cluster of bodies (alive or dead), Paralyzing Breath when 3+ targets are in the cone, Slam anything in reach. It never retreats.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Construct, Large
- **Extraordinary STR + CON** — pure brute juggernaut
- **INT 5** — mechanistic, no independent judgment
- **WIS 11** — can't discriminate among targets
- Immune: poison, psychic; nonmagical non-adamantine physical damage
- Immune: charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned
- Magic Resistance

---

## Key Features

### Paralyzing Breath (Recharge 5–6)
- 30-ft cone; CON save or paralyzed for 1 minute (repeat save each turn)
- Use when **3+ targets** are in the cone

### Multiattack
- 2× Slam attacks
- Advantage + auto-crit on paralyzed targets

### Summon Specters (Bonus Action, 1/combat)
- Summons specters from collected souls
- Specters persist until the collector is destroyed
- **When to use:** DM's call — use when it feels dramatically right
  - Options: first round for max difficulty, when moderately damaged (≤132 hp), or when the tide of battle needs a nudge

---

## Combat Sequence

### Behavior Loop (Mechanistic)
1. March straight toward the **largest concentration of bodies** (alive or dead)
2. 3+ targets in 30-ft cone + Paralyzing Breath available? → **Paralyzing Breath**
3. Paralyzed target in reach? → **Slam ×2** (advantage, auto-crit)
4. Cheeky attackers with magic/adamantine weapons run up? → **Slam ×2**
5. Attacked by nonmagical, non-adamantine weapons? → **Ignore completely**, keep marching

### Repositioning
- If 3 opponents cluster around it: steps back (ignores opportunity attacks — they can't hurt it) to catch all 3 in Paralyzing Breath cone

### Summon Specters
- Use once per combat at the dramatically appropriate moment
- Specters don't vanish between combats — a collector can accumulate an army over time

---

## Retreat
- **Never retreats** — no self-preservation instinct
- Does its thing until destroyed
- It's a construct; it has a function and completes it

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Specters Accumulate
> RAW, specters persist until the collector is destroyed. A collector that's been active for a long time could have specters from previous combats still following it. Scale to taste.

- Nonmagical, non-adamantine weapons are **completely useless** — make sure PCs know this
- Magic Resistance on top of immunities makes it extremely hard to debuff
- Paralyzing Breath + Slam auto-crits = devastating burst damage
- Errant collectors (no master) just keep Roomba-ing — great as environmental hazards on old battlefields
- Specters released by Summon Specters are uncontrolled berserkers — they may wander off to attack anything living nearby
