# Firenewt Tactics

## Firenewt Warrior

### Tactical Summary

Aggressive, wrathful, cruel raiders and slavers. Above-average DEX/CON, average STR. Fight as brutes (DEX-primary with scimitars, medium armor + shield, Multiattack). INT 7 — no imagination or adaptability.

### Combat Behavior

- **Spit Fire (1/encounter):** More damage than Multiattack, partial on save. Use immediately upon closing to 10 ft range
- **Then:** Scimitar Multiattack for remainder of engagement
- If captured: regains Spit Fire after 1 hour (short rest equivalent)

### Retreat Conditions

- On a mission with other firenewts: fight to the death (fanatic conviction)
- Minding own business: Dash away at ≤8 hp

## Giant Strider

### Tactical Summary

Beaked dinosaur mount. 50 ft speed. Animal INT — no zealotry.

### Combat Behavior

- **Fire Burst (recharge):** Use whenever available against 2+ enemies within range and ≤20 ft apart
- Otherwise: Bite
- Same initiative as rider (matching DEX modifiers)

### Retreat Conditions

Dash away at ≤8 hp, taking rider along. Rider must DC 15 DEX check to dismount without losing movement (fail = prone).

## Firenewt Warlock of Imix

### Tactical Summary

Brute profile (STR-primary), melee weapon attacker. Always has *mage armor* pre-cast (at will, no slot). Two spell slots — one always saved for *hellish rebuke* (reaction).

### Combat Behavior

- **One big enemy (6'8" barbarian type):** *Flaming sphere* → ram as bonus action each turn
- **2+ clustered front-liners within 15 ft cone:** *Burning hands*
- **Neither condition:** *Scorching ray* at backline (3 rays, +5 to hit, 7 avg each)
- **Otherwise:** Morningstar melee or *fire bolt* if can't reach melee

### Retreat Conditions

Same as warriors — fight to death on missions, flee at ≤8 hp otherwise.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Firenewts fight like primitives — charge, spit, slash. The warlock adds tactical depth with spell selection. Giant striders can heal each other with Fire Burst (fire absorption) — let them discover this mid-fight for a nasty surprise.
