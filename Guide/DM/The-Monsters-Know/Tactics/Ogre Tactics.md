# Ogre Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Ogre Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/ogre-tactics/)
> **Bestiary**: [[Ogres]]

Basic ogres have no tactics — dumb brutes. The *Mordenkainen's* variants are semi-domesticated warbeasts used by other species. Trainer's Intelligence determines effectiveness.

## Basic Ogre

- 60 ft darkvision → hunts at night only. Night encounter = ogre found PCs; day = PCs found ogre.
- Melee greatclub > ranged javelin (high Str, low Dex). Javelin only at 10–30 ft.
- Predatory beast. Picks off smallest/weakest/isolated first.
- Wis 7 → no self-preservation. Fights until dead.

## Ogre Battering Ram

**Bash** (default, melee + pushback) and **Block the Path** (chokepoint lockdown — disadvantage on attacks vs. ogre, Sentinel-like escape prevention, massive OA damage). Ironically more defensive than offensive.

## Ogre Bolt Launcher

Impressive ranged attack. Still a brute — if single enemy comes within sight, Wis save or it drops weapon, Dashes, and starts pummeling with fists.

## Ogre Chain Brute

Area control. Chain hits every creature within 10 ft (allies too!).

- **Chain Smash**: vs. prone targets (recharges on 6)
- **Chain Sweep**: vs. 2+ non-prone enemies (sets up Chain Smash)
- **Fist**: all other circumstances
- Keeps pummeling unconscious enemies if not commanded to move

## Ogre Howdah

Mace only. Small creatures ride on back with ¾ cover (likely goblins, kobolds, or derro). Riders use ranged attacks or polearms/lances/whips/spears for melee. Ogre wants to smash → Wis save to resist charging. If riders want melee, they direct the ogre.
