# Bronze Dragon Tactics

> [!summary]
> Bronze dragons are shy, guarded observers who watch before engaging. They use nonlethal Repulsion Breath to push enemies away, fight only when necessary, and flee immediately if things turn hostile. Adult and ancient bronze dragons can Change Shape and prefer to go about in disguise.

---

## Metallic Dragon Shared Traits
- Same brute profile as chromatic dragons: high STR + CON, proficient in all "big three" saves + CHA
- Blindsight, darkvision, flying + one alternative movement mode
- **Two breath weapons:** one damaging, one nonlethal
- **Social skill proficiencies** in addition to Perception + Stealth
- Adult/ancient: legendary actions, Legendary Resistance, Frightful Presence
- Adult/ancient: only **2 lair actions** (vs chromatic's 3)
- **Good-aligned** — encounters rarely begin with combat

## Bronze-Specific Traits
- **Swim speed** — alternative movement
- **Lightning Breath** (damaging, line) + **Repulsion Breath** (nonlethal, cone — pushes targets away)
- **Social skills:** guarded, tries to read what PCs want before engaging deeper (Insight-focused)
- **Personality:** Shy observers. Young bronze dragons are guarded and watchful
- Adult and ancient bronze dragons can **Change Shape**

---

## By Age

### Wyrmling
- Hides and observes watchfully — won't flee if approached unless PCs seem belligerent
- If attacked: **Repulsion Breath once** to push attackers away, then fly/swim away at max speed
- Only uses Lightning Breath if already damaged
- Only Bites if cornered

### Young
- Shy observer — won't initiate social interaction unless approached with clearly nonhostile intentions
- If encounter turns hostile: **flies away immediately** — no hesitation
- Uses Repulsion Breath as sucker punch before retreating
- Uses Lightning Breath only if already hurt; melee only if cornered

### Adult (Legendary)
- Would rather talk than fight, but **will start a fight** if PCs seem to have evil intent
- Opens with nonlethal means: "cloud" lair action + Repulsion Breath
- Uses **Multiattack (Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite) before Lightning Breath**
- Lightning Breath is a last-ditch trump card

#### Lair Actions (Pick 1 per round, never same twice)
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Fog Cloud** | Cloud action (obscures vision) | Everyone — nonlethal opener |
| **Thunderclap** | Push action (deals damage + inhibits movement) | Approaching melee fighters |

##### Fizban's New Lair Actions
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Ocean's Call** | Direct damage (huge AoE, trifling damage) | All enemies in water — for aquatic lairs |
| **Salt Burst** | Direct damage (conventional) | 4+ enemies in range |
| **Whelming Water** | Debilitator (large AoE) | All enemies in water — for partially/fully submerged lairs |

#### Legendary Actions (3/round)
| Action | Cost | Use When |
|---|---|---|
| **Detect** | 1 | Any stealthy movement within line of sight |
| **Tail Attack** | 1 | PC behind the dragon |
| **Wing Attack** | 2 | 3+ PCs within 10 ft — knocks prone + bonus fly to reposition |

### Ancient
- Same tactics as adult; **Change Shape** available
- Prefers to go about in disguise outside its lair — takes human form among humans, elven form among elves, beast form in wilderness
- If threatened in disguise: talks its way out, even surrenders to avoid revealing true nature
- Once eyes are off it: reverts or assumes different form to escape

## Metallic Dragon Combat Personality

> [!important] Flight Over Fight
> Bronze dragons are among the quickest metallic dragons to disengage. Young bronze dragons **fly away immediately** when things turn hostile — no parley, no hesitation.

> [!important] Retreat Threshold
> Unless defending their lair, adult/ancient metallic dragons **retreat at ≤70% hp** — Disengage (action) → fly/swim away at max speed.

---

## Quick Reference: Combat Flow
1. **Pre-combat:** Observes from hiding — tries to read PCs' intentions before engaging
2. **Hostile intent detected:** Fog Cloud (lair action) → Repulsion Breath (push enemies away)
3. **If PCs persist:** Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite Multiattack
4. **Last resort (clearly evil PCs):** Lightning Breath
5. **≤70% hp:** Disengage → fly/swim away at max speed
6. **Outside lair in disguise:** Talks way out of any situation → Change Shape to escape when unwatched

> [!tip] DM Note
> Bronze dragons are the most aquatic of the metallic dragons. Their Fizban's lair actions (Ocean's Call, Whelming Water) are designed for underwater or coastal lairs. If your bronze dragon's lair is submerged, these lair actions affect *all* enemies in the water.
