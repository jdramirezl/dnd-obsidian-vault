# Blue Dragon Tactics

> [!summary]
> Blue dragons are patient, pragmatic, and relentless. They burrow to ambush, use linear Lightning Breath, and disengage to snipe from range when wounded. They never give up — retreating only to return again and again until either they or their enemies are dead.

---

## Universal Dragon Traits (All Colors)
- Fly at 2× land speed; blindsight + darkvision
- High STR + CON (brute profile); proficient in Perception + Stealth
- Saves: DEX, CON, WIS, CHA
- Breath weapon recharges on 5–6 (avg every 3 rounds)
- **Legendary Resistance (adult+):** use every failed save without conserving

## Blue-Specific Traits
- **Burrow speed** — ambush by erupting from sand/earth
- **Lightning Breath** is a **line**, not a cone — harder to catch multiple targets; position to line up 2–3+ PCs
- **Personality:** Patient, pragmatic, proud. Young blues are "preening narcissists"

---

## By Age

### Wyrmling
- Stealth-first: burrow beneath sand, erupt to ambush
- **Lightning Breath** first (line), then Bite on subsequent turns
- Won't engage 2+ melee opponents — Dodge + reposition if ganged up on
- **Flee at ≤40% hp**

### Young
- Gains **Multiattack:** Claw/Claw/Bite
- Willing to engage **2 melee opponents** (not 3)
- Same ambush and breath-first tactics
- Adolescent personality: preening narcissist

### Adult (Legendary)
- **Lair actions, legendary actions, Frightful Presence**
- Breath weapon always preferred — repositions to line up maximum targets in the line AoE
- If breath unavailable: **Frightful Presence** + Claw/Claw/Bite

#### Lair Actions (Pick 1 per round, never same twice)
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Ceiling Collapse** | Movement restrictor (single target, DEX save + STR check) | Low-DEX *and* low-STR targets |
| **Cloud of Sand** | Debilitator (blindness, 20 ft radius) | Everyone — especially spellcasters and rogues (nullifies advantage/disadvantage) |
| **Arc Lightning** | Direct damage (line) | Anyone in range — general softener |

##### Fizban's New Lair Actions
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Misleading Mirage** | Movement redirector | Healthy: steer enemies into Lightning Breath path. Wounded: send pursuers wrong direction. Always: steer away from hoard |
| **Sudden Sinkhole** | Debilitator + restrictor + damage (single target) | Low-DEX supporters (healers/buffers) — dump them in a pit |

#### Legendary Actions (3/round)
| Action | Cost | Use When |
|---|---|---|
| **Detect** | 1 | Any stealthy movement within line of sight |
| **Tail Attack** | 1 | PC behind the dragon; max once per round if 3+ melee attackers threaten |
| **Wing Attack** | 2 | **3+ PCs within 10 ft** — knocks prone + bonus fly to reposition. Also escape trigger at ≤40% hp |

### Ancient
- Same tactics as adult with bigger numbers, extended ranges
- Save DCs increase, breath weapon range extends

## Blue Dragon Combat Personality

> [!important] Key Difference — Pragmatic Retreat + Relentless Pursuit
> When moderately wounded (≤70% hp), blue dragons **Disengage and fly to a safe distance**, then take potshots with Lightning Breath (catching 2+ PCs per blast). If ranged attackers keep hurting it, it flies out of sight — **but it will be back**, again and again, until either it or the PCs are dead. Its pride demands it.

| Dragon Color | At Moderate Wounds |
|---|---|
| White | Keeps fighting (dense, truculent) |
| Red | Keeps fighting (wrathful, arrogant) |
| **Blue** | **Disengages, snipes from range, retreats and returns repeatedly** |
| Black | Skedaddles |
| Green | Stops fighting, parleys |

## Quick Reference: Combat Flow
1. **Pre-combat:** Stealth → burrow beneath sand/earth, erupt to ambush
2. **Round 1:** Lightning Breath (line — reposition to line up max targets)
3. **Breath unavailable:** Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite
4. **Lair action (init 20):** Cloud of Sand to blind clusters (especially casters/rogues), Ceiling Collapse on weak single targets, Arc Lightning for damage
5. **Legendary actions:** Wing Attack if 3+ in melee → reposition; Tail Attack for rear threats; Detect for sneakers
6. **≤70% hp:** Disengage → fly to safe distance → snipe with Lightning Breath (2+ targets per blast)
7. **Ranged attackers still hurting it:** Fly out of sight. **Will return.** Again and again.
8. **≤40% hp:** Wing Attack → fly away for real (temporarily)

> [!tip] DM Note
> Lightning Breath is a **line** — much harder to catch multiple PCs than a cone. Use minions (dragonborn, kobolds) to force PCs into single-file formations. Blue dragons in desert lairs can combine burrow speed with regional effects (dust devils, sinkholes) for devastating ambush combos.
