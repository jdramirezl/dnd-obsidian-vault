# Faerie Dragon Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Tiny good-natured tricksters. Extraordinary DEX, above-average CON, clever and charismatic. No ranged attacks — hit-and-run via 60 ft fly speed + Superior Invisibility (at-will, concentration). Telepathic communication calls reinforcements: 2 → 5 → 12 → 36.

See also: [[Faerie-Dragon]]

## Combat Behavior

- **Euphoria Breath (recharge ~3 rounds):** DC 11 WIS save. Trivial from one — devastating from a dozen (4+ breaths/round across the party)
- **Invisibility Cycling:** Visible → Euphoria Breath → invisible. If PC Readies an attack, target someone else or stay invisible
- **Bite:** 1 damage. Cooldown filler only, same visible/invisible cycling
- **Illusion Spells (by age):** *Color spray* (blind), *mirror image* (multiply), *major image* (distraction, requires concentration = no invisibility), *hallucinatory terrain* (no concentration), *polymorph* (beasts only, not other faerie dragons)
- **Swarm illusion:** Few dragons zoom target-to-target to seem like many. Goal is harassment, not killing

## Retreat Conditions

Individual: flees at ≤9 hp. The flight keeps fighting until territory is safe.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Don't be fair to players who pick fights with faerie dragons. Scale the swarm to teach that not everything needs killing. If the party plays along with pranks, reward them with info or a guide.
