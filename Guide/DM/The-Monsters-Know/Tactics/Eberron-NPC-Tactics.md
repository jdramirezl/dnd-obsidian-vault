# Eberron NPC Tactics (Index)

> **Source**: [Eberron Tag](https://www.themonstersknow.com/tag/eberron/) | **Book**: Eberron: Rising From the Last War
> ⚠️ **Note**: The URL `themonstersknow.com/eberron-npc-tactics/` does not exist as a single article. The blog covers Eberron creatures across multiple individual posts. This note indexes them.

## Eberron Creature Articles

### Constructs
- **[Expeditious Messenger & Iron Defender](https://www.themonstersknow.com/expeditious-messenger-and-iron-defender-tactics/)**: Homunculi that aid their master. Low-challenge, narrow applications. Messengers deliver; iron defenders guard.
- **[Living Spells](https://www.themonstersknow.com/living-spell-tactics/)**: Constructs with no creator except calamity. Home in on nearest living thing, detonate Spell Mimicry, then Magical Strike until recharge. No discrimination, no self-preservation.
- **[Warforged Titan & Colossus](https://www.themonstersknow.com/warforged-titan-and-colossus-tactics/)**: Barely sentient brutes/mega-brutes. Purpose-built soldiers with no introspection.

### Beasts
- **[Fastieth & Clawfoot](https://www.themonstersknow.com/fastieth-and-clawfoot-tactics/)**: Dinosaur mounts used by Talenta Plains halflings. Steampunk fantasy with dinosaurs.

### Fey
- **[Valenar Animals](https://www.themonstersknow.com/valenar-animal-tactics/)**: Fey animals inhabited by spirits of dead elven druids. Bond telepathically to humanoids (usually elves). Template can be applied to any beast stat block.
- **[Dusk Hag](https://www.themonstersknow.com/dusk-hag-tactics/)**: Exploits the unconscious condition. Only attacks targets who can't fight back. Skillful planner — won't pick a fight unless it's Deadly difficulty.
