# Cerberus Tactics

> [!summary]
> From *Mythic Odysseys of Theros*: two-headed and Underworld cerberi are fast-moving ambush predators that charge from hiding, unleash a lava breath weapon, then Multiattack — preferring restrained targets. They have Pack Tactics, are immune to fire/necrotic and many conditions.

---

## Stat Block Profile (Both Types)
- **Type:** Monstrosity
- Proficient in **Stealth**, double proficiency in **Perception**
- Immune to **fire and necrotic**; can't be surprised, charmed, frightened, blinded, deafened, exhausted, stunned
- **Aggressive:** bonus action to move up to speed toward hostile creature
- **Pack Tactics:** advantage when ally within 5 ft of target
- **Avoidance:** advantage on saves vs. being knocked unconscious
- **Multiattack:** one Bite per head
- **Breath Weapon (Recharge 5–6):** cone of lava — fire damage + rock encasement (restrained)

### Two-Headed (CR 6)
- 60 ft speed, 15-ft cone — target at least **2 opponents**

### Underworld (CR 19)
- 60 ft speed, 30-ft cone — target at least **3–5 opponents**

---

## Combat Sequence

### Opening: Charge + Breath
1. Waits hidden for sufficient targets within threshold:
   - Two-headed: 2+ within **95 ft**; Underworld: 3–5+ within **150 ft**
2. **Charges** (full movement + Aggressive bonus action)
3. **Breath Weapon** on clustered targets — far superior to surprise Multiattack

### Subsequent Rounds
4. Breath Weapon again if recharged + enough targets without provoking OAs
5. Otherwise → **Multiattack** (one Bite per head)
6. **Prefers restrained targets** (lava-encased) — advantage, faster elimination
7. If ally within 5 ft of unrestrained foe → attacks that foe (Pack Tactics)

### Target Priority
- Restrained targets first; unrestrained with Pack Tactics second
- Conventional weapon wielders over spellcasters (immunities make casters less threatening)

---

## Retreat
- **Two-headed:** withdraw at **≤15 hp**
- **Underworld (trained sentry):** fights to the death
- **Underworld (wild):** withdraw at **≤41 hp**

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Breath Weapon Opener
> Charge + breath is far superior to a surprise Multiattack. Always open with breath if enough targets are clustered.

- 60 ft speed + Aggressive = **120 ft charge range**
- Pack Tactics means cerberi are much more dangerous with allies
- "Cerberus" literally means "the spotted one" — Hades named his dog Spot
