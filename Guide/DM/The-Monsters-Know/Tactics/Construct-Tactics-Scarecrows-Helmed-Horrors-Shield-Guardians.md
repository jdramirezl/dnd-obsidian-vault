# Construct Tactics: Scarecrows, Helmed Horrors & Shield Guardians

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/construct-tactics-scarecrows-helmed-horrors-shield-guardians/) | **Date**: May 12, 2017
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Scarecrow]], [[Helmed-Horror]], [[Shield-Guardian]]

---

## Scarecrow

- **Shock attacker** powered by a slain fiend's spirit. Above-average Dex/Cha, vulnerable to fire, resistant to normal weapon damage.
- **Terrifying Glare is the key**: Frightened → paralyzed. Paralysis = auto-crits from melee + advantage on attacks.
- **Fire creates irrational behavior**: Survival instinct conflicts with creator's commands — behave erratically near fire.

### Combat Sequence
1. **False Appearance**: Lurks motionless until target within 30 ft.
2. **Terrifying Glare** (not an attack — doesn't reveal it): Repeats until target is frightened + paralyzed. Gives up after 2 failed saves on same target.
3. **Spring into action**: Next turn after paralysis, closes to melee, Multiattacks with claws. Advantage + auto-crits.
4. **Two rounds melee max**: Retreats 30 ft (ignores OAs due to damage resistance), tries Glare again.
5. **Darkvision**: Attacks non-darkvision creatures freely in darkness.
6. **Seriously wounded (≤14 hp)**: Flees via Dash unless ordered to fight to death.

---

## Helmed Horror

- **High-Str/Con brute**: Longsword Multiattack, AC 20, flight, normal weapon resistance.
- **Targets glass cannons first**: Can identify class and assess weaknesses.
- **Fly-by melee**: 10 ft up → fly down to attack → fly back up. Ignores OAs.
- **Spell immunities**: Default list, or tailored if creator has intel on the party.
- **Fights until destroyed**.

---

## Shield Guardian

- **Bodyguard device**: Tactics are the controller's tactics, not its own.
- **Shield reaction**: +2 AC to controller when adjacent.
- **Regenerates** regardless of damage type. NOT resistant to weapon damage.
- **Spell Storing**: Can function as a dead man's switch — activates on predefined conditions, even after controller is unconscious.
- **Amulet transfers control**: Killing controller doesn't destroy amulet.
- **Fights until destroyed**.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Monsters
> Scarecrows are horror encounters — Terrifying Glare into auto-crit claws is devastating at low levels. Helmed horrors are aerial brutes that punish parties without magic weapons. Shield guardians are only as clever as their controllers.
