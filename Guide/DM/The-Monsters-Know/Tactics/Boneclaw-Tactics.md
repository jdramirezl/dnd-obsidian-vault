# Boneclaw Tactics

> [!summary]
> The boneclaw is a failed lich — a powerful undead shock attacker bound to a random master. It ambushes from shadow with 15-ft-reach claws, grapples and drags victims, then Shadow Jumps away with its prey. Can't be permanently killed unless its master dies or repents. Nocturnal, relentless, terrifying.

---

## Stat Block Profile
- **Type:** Undead, Large
- **Extraordinary STR; very high DEX, CON, WIS; INT 13** (trauma-reduced)
- Proficient saves: DEX, CON, WIS — resists most save-based attacks
- Proficient: Perception, Stealth — ambush predator
- Resistant: cold, necrotic; nonmagical physical damage
- Immune: charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned
- **60 ft darkvision**, 40 ft speed
- **Piercing Claw reach: 15 ft**

---

## Key Features

### Shadow Stealth (Bonus Action)
- Hide while in dim light or darkness → repeated unseen attacks

### Shadow Jump (Action)
- Teleport up to 60 ft between dim light/darkness areas
- Deals necrotic damage to all adjacent creatures on departure
- Unambiguously an **exit maneuver**

### Deadly Reach (Reaction)
- Free attack when a creature **enters** its 15 ft reach

### Rejuvenation
- Reforms in 1d10 hours unless master is dead or redeemed
- Effectively unkillable without dealing with the master

---

## Combat Sequence

### Round 1 — Ambush
1. **Multiattack** (2× Piercing Claw) from hiding while unseen
2. Grapple target on hit → drag toward boneclaw
3. Stay in dim light/darkness → **Shadow Stealth** to Hide again

### Round 2 — Repeat
1. Attack from hiding again (advantage)
2. 15 ft reach = strike without being adjacent
3. **Deadly Reach** punishes any ally who charges in
4. Opportunity attack if target tears free and runs; overtake with 40 ft speed

### Round 3+ — Exit
1. After 2 rounds, follows planned exit path with grappled victim
2. If 3+ enemies adjacent: **Shadow Jump** away (necrotic AoE on departure)
3. May return a few rounds later to repeat the cycle

### Dealing with Light
- Bright light denies Shadow Jump and Shadow Stealth
- Moves to nearest shadow using **Dash** (ignores opportunity attacks)
- Shadow Stealth to vanish, then keeps moving

---

## Retreat
- **Never flees** — Rejuvenation means death is temporary
- High WIS is for target selection, timing, and exit planning — not self-preservation

---

## DM Encounter Tips

> [!tip] Recurring Nightmare
> The boneclaw reforms in 1d10 hours. Use it as a recurring antagonist across sessions. The only permanent solution is finding and dealing with the master.

- MotM version: Shadow Jump on recharge (5–6); loses nonmagical resistance; gains necrotic on Piercing Claw
- Charisma is its dump stat — it does not negotiate
- Pairs narratively with an unwitting or unwilling master
