# Oinoloth Tactics

## Tactical Summary

CR 12 yugoloth. Avatar of pestilence. No weakness — STR and CHA extraordinary, lowest score is very high WIS. Brute by default but plays any position. 40 ft speed. Magic Resistance, proficiency in CON + WIS saves. Immune/resistant to most evocation damage types.

**Bringer of Plagues** (bonus action, recharge 5–6): 30 ft radius blight zone. Poisons creatures + deals necrotic damage on failed saves. Centerpiece trait.

> [!warning] DM Decision
> RAW, Bringer of Plagues may damage the oinoloth and allied yugoloths (immune to poisoned but NOT necrotic). Consider house-ruling oinoloths immune to own effect, or that only poisoned creatures take necrotic.

## Actions

### Multiattack (default)

**Transfixing Gaze** + **Claw × 2**. Gaze charms + restrains (lasts until end of oinoloth's *next* turn). Enables chaining:
1. Turn 1: Transfix A → Claw A with advantage × 2
2. Turn 2: Claw A with advantage × 2 (still transfixed) + Transfix B
3. Turn 3: Claw B with advantage × 2 + Transfix C...

### Corrupted Healing (recharge 6)

Heals ALL damage + removes a condition on willing creature within 5 ft. Side effects: 1 exhaustion level + reduced HP max. Use when:
- Transfixing Gaze just failed (chain broken, turn partially wasted)
- Target ≤40% HP, or ≤70% HP and blinded, or ≤90% HP and paralyzed
- Can use on itself (always within 5 ft, always willing)

### Teleport (full action)

Costs entire action (unlike nycaloth/yagnoloth). Combine with Bringer of Plagues (bonus action): swoop into cluster → drop plague → Teleport out, or vice versa.

## Spells

### At Will

- **Darkness, detect magic, dispel magic, invisibility** — same as yagnoloth

### 1/Day

- **Feeblemind** — target bards/clerics/sorcerers/warlocks with average INT. Not wizards (INT too high) or melee fighters (can't cast). Must be causing real pain
- **Globe of invulnerability** — consider as first action. Nullifies spells ≤5th level unless caster enters within 10 ft. No squish wants that close
- **Wall of fire / Wall of ice** — trap enemies inside blight fields. Ice blocks movement; fire forces choice between fire and necrotic damage

## Bringer of Plagues Strategy

**Offensive (kill):** Position to affect ≥6 enemies. Teleport + Plagues combo to drop and escape.

**Defensive (hold position):** Gauntlet of overlapping blight zones on the approach. *Wall of fire/ice* to make progress tortuous.

**Invisible plague bombing:** Bringer of Plagues isn't an attack or spell — doesn't break *invisibility*. Drop field → zip out → wait for recharge → drop another. Nobody knows what's happening.

## Target Selection

No simple rule — no real weakness means no enemy screams "take me out first." Adapt:
- Magic weapon wielders = persons of interest (bypass resistance)
- Less tough opponents = lower CON saves = easy Plagues targets
- Transfixing Gaze best vs physically-focused foes (likely lower WIS/CHA)

## Retreat Conditions

- **Off home plane:** Fights to the death
- **Home plane, seriously wounded (≤50 hp):** Bargains dishonestly (proficient in Deception, not Persuasion)

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Open with invisible plague bombing — devastating. Then reveal with *globe of invulnerability* up and chain Transfixing Gaze. Use *wall of fire/ice* to herd PCs through plague fields. Corrupted Healing is a nasty "gift" if hired as ally — it won't mention the exhaustion and HP reduction.
