# NPC Tactics: Guards and Soldiers

> **Book**: Monster Manual (NPC stat blocks)

---

## Guard (CR 1/8)

- Chain shirt, shield, spear. AC 16 — surprisingly tough for CR 1/8.
- **Disciplined but not elite**. Fights in formation, holds position, calls for backup.
- Won't pursue fleeing enemies far from their post. **Duty-bound** to their station.
- Uses spear at reach (thrown or melee). Stays behind shield wall when possible.
- Retreats to a defensible position if overwhelmed, rather than fleeing outright.

## Scout (CR 1/2)

- Light armor, longbow, shortsword. Proficient in Nature, Perception, Stealth, Survival.
- **Ranged skirmisher**. Stays at longbow range (150/600 ft), uses Stealth to reposition.
- Multiattack (2 melee attacks) if forced into close combat, but prefers to avoid it.
- Primary role is **reconnaissance and early warning** — will disengage and report rather than fight to the death.
- Keen Hearing and Sight → advantage on Perception checks. Hard to surprise.

## Veteran (CR 3)

- The professional soldier. Splint armor, longsword, shortsword, heavy crossbow.
- **Multiattack**: 2 longsword strikes OR 2 crossbow shots. Solid, reliable damage.
- Fights with **discipline and experience**. Holds the line, protects weaker allies, focuses fire on the biggest threat.
- Won't break ranks easily. Retreats only on orders or when the situation is clearly hopeless.

## Knight (CR 3)

- Heavy armor (plate + shield, AC 18), greatsword. Leadership reaction (reroll a failed save for an ally within 30 ft).
- **Frontline commander**. Leads from the front, uses Leadership to bolster allies.
- Brave trait → advantage on saves vs frightened.
- Fights to protect their charge or cause. Will **parley** if given the opportunity — knights have a code.
- Retreats only to protect someone they're sworn to defend.
