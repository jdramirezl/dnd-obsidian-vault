## General Notes (from stat block)

- CR 1 construct. Petrified humanoids reanimated through alchemical ritual. Obedient guards, cleverer than many constructs but slow (15 ft speed).
- **False Appearance**: While motionless, indistinguishable from a normal statue. Ideal ambush predators — attack intruders from stealth.
- **Petrifying Claws**: On a critical hit, the target begins turning to stone (restrained → petrified over failed saves). This is the stone cursed's signature threat.
- **Stone Anxiety**: Vulnerability to thunder damage.
- **Tactics**: Lie in wait as statues. Strike when intruders pass within reach. Focus attacks on a single target to maximize crit chances for petrification. Too slow to chase — if enemies retreat beyond reach, they return to statue positions and wait.
