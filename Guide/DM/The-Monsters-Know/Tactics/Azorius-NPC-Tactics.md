# Azorius NPC Tactics

## Tactical Summary

The Azorius Senate fields soldiers, precognitive mages, and lawmages as Ravnica's government enforcers. They fight to apprehend, not kill — unless one of their own is slain. Encounters scale by tier: soldiers at T1, soldiers + precog at T2, soldiers + lawmages at T3.

## Soldiers

- Balanced combatants; longsword one-handed with shield
- Move in tight formation for **Formation Tactics** (advantage when adjacent to ally)
- Default **nonlethal** — switch to lethal if a soldier is killed
- Hussars (mounted): mounts act independently; horse hussars Ready attacks to follow Trampling Charge; own mount counts as ally for Formation Tactics
- **Retreat:** 1 soldier death = squad withdraws; company withdraws at 10% losses; all soldiers ≤6 hp = withdraw

## Precognitive Mages

- **Out of combat:** *locate object* → *clairvoyance* surveillance loop (1 week per location)
- **Combat:** Hang back 30 ft.; **Precognitive Insight** (reaction) imposes disadvantage, then **Glimpse the Temporal Flood** (recharge) for psychic + stun; can use Insight again on the stun save
- Quarterstaff is worthless — if rushed, Disengage and call for help
- **Retreat:** ≤44 hp or 1 soldier falls → signal withdrawal

## Lawmages

- High INT wizard with high CON — stands just behind front line
- **Key combat spells:** *hold person* (upcast 4th for 3 targets), *slow*, *fire bolt* (2d10), *shield*
- **Out of combat:** *locate creature*, *arcane lock*, *detect thoughts*, *alarm*
- *Stoneskin* is a trap — concentration better on *slow*/*hold person*
- **Scaling:** Senate sends enough lawmages + soldiers to make encounter Deadly
- **Retreat:** ≤33 hp or half forces lost; may surrender if opponents respect laws of war

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Azorius encounters are law enforcement — give PCs a chance to comply before combat
> - Withdrawal means falling back to a fortified position with reinforcements
> - Precog's double-Insight in one round is devastating; telegraph it clearly
