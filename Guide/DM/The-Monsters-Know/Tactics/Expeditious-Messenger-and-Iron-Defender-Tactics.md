# Expeditious Messenger & Iron Defender Tactics

> [!summary]
> Two Eberron homunculi from *Rising From the Last War*. The expeditious messenger is a flying courier that evades interception via speed and Flyby. The iron defender is a loyal guard beast that bites, grapples, and delays attackers while its master escapes. Both share telepathic bonds with their creators.

---

## Expeditious Messenger
- Flying construct built to convey messages; Tiny creature
- Flying speed 60 ft (double its ground speed); can't hover
- Proficient in Acrobatics (+5, helps escape grapples) and Stealth
- **Flyby:** never needs to Disengage — immune to opportunity attacks
- Immune to exhaustion — can Dash indefinitely (huge advantage in chases)
- Shock attacker profile (high Dex) but **not aggressive** — avoids combat

### Behavior
- Stays out of reach of other creatures for speed/efficiency
- If grabbed: one Bite attack as deterrent, then flies off (Flyby)
- Default action: **Dash or Dodge** depending on environment

| Situation | Action |
|---|---|
| Invisible foe, heavy obscurement, or multiple attackers | Dash |
| Clear/light obscurement, one visible foe with one attack/round | Dodge |

- Zigs and zags through cover; as Tiny, fits through 2½-ft openings (1¼ ft squeezing)
- **Delivering a message:** singleminded determination to reach recipient
- **Returning from delivery:** dives for cover on taking even 1 damage, telepathically informs master of location, waits until safe
- Can deliver messages **verbally** (intelligent, has speech) — destroying it doesn't reveal message contents

---

## Iron Defender
- Loyal guard beast with brute ability contour
- Keen Senses: advantage on all Perception checks (sight, sound, smell)
- Telepathic Bond with master; not smart enough for target selection on its own — master directs priorities

### Combat Behavior
- **Bite and don't let go** — straightforward grapple-and-hold
- Can grapple one target and Bite a different one simultaneously (possibly unintended but not errata'd)
- Strategic role: **delay attackers while master escapes**
- Backs into choke points so enemies can't run around it
- Engages whichever enemy has the best chance of catching its master

### Master Escapes
- Once master is safely away (communicated telepathically): withdraws, Dodging as it goes
- Lacks Intelligence to Disengage — Dodge is its retreat action
- **Loyalty is absolute:** while defending master, fights until destroyed

> [!tip] DM Tips
> Expeditious messenger: great for chase encounters and intrigue campaigns (intercept the mail!). Low HP means it can be one-shot — but verbal messages can't be recovered from wreckage. Iron defender: park it in a doorway and let it buy time. It's a guard dog, not a hunter.
