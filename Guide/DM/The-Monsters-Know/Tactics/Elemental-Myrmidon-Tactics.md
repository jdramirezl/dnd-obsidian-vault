# Elemental Myrmidon Tactics

> [!summary]
> Elemental spirits bound into plate armor — part elemental, part construct. More intelligent than ordinary elementals but command-bound. Melee only, each with a slow-recharge special attack. Never retreat unless ordered to.

---

## Shared Traits
- Plate armor, resistance to nonmagical physical damage
- Immune: poison, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone
- Magical weapon attacks, darkvision, no ranged attacks
- Each has one recharging special melee attack + Multiattack

---

## Air Elemental Myrmidon
- **Lightning Strike (Recharge 6):** bonus lightning + potential stun (DC 13 Con)
- Damage barely exceeds normal Multi (26.5 vs 25.5) — **value is the stun**
- Target Dex-reliant enemies (rogues, monks, archers, glass cannons) — weak Con saves
- Can hover 10 ft up between rounds, fly in to strike, fly back out
- Abandons hover tactic if it takes two full-damage opportunity hits in same/consecutive turns
- After successful stun → Multiattack the stunned target next turn (advantage)

## Earth Elemental Myrmidon
- **Thunderous Strike (Recharge 6):** bonus thunder + potential prone (DC 14 Str)
- Better damage ratio (27.5 vs 22 normal Multi)
- Stays in melee thick; no good movement abilities
- Triggered by: 39+ nonmagical weapon damage, 13+ magical damage, or any debilitating condition

## Fire Elemental Myrmidon
- **Fiery Strikes (Recharge 6):** extra fire damage on Multiattack, no save, no reduced attacks
- **Use whenever available** — no targeting criteria, run on autopilot
- Water Susceptibility exists but no vulnerability — no special reaction to water/cold

## Water Elemental Myrmidon
- **Freezing Strikes (Recharge 6):** extra cold + **slows target temporarily**
- Target skirmishers/shock attackers darting in and out of reach
- After successful slow → chase and Multiattack the slowed target next turn
- 40 ft swim speed enables swim-in/swim-out strafing underwater
- Always wields trident two-handed (no shield)

---

## Retreat

> [!important] No Discretion
> Myrmidons cannot choose to retreat. If ordered, they retreat. Otherwise, fight until destroyed.

> [!tip] DM Tips
> AEM: hover-strafe, Lightning Strike Dex-monkeys. EEM: park and smash, Thunderous Strike the rogue. FEM: autopilot. WEM: Freezing Strikes on mobile enemies, chase the slowed.
