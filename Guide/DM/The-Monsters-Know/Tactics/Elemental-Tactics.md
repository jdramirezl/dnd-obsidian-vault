# Elemental Tactics

> [!summary]
> Summoned spirits of air, fire, water, and earth — peevish about being bound, eager to return home. Fight to the death with zero self-preservation. Each has a distinct combat style dictated by element.

---

## Shared Traits
- Physically powerful, not bright, darkvision, two melee attacks/turn
- Resistant to nonmagical physical damage
- Immune: exhaustion, paralysis, petrification, poison, unconsciousness
- No survival instinct — destruction sends them home
- Indiscriminate targeting unless controller directs otherwise

---

## Air Elemental
- 90 ft flying speed (only movement), extremely high Dex
- Immune to grapple, restraint, prone
- **Whirlwind (Recharge):** use when 2+ enemies in a 10-ft square; flings targets 20 ft random direction + prone
- Moves in continuous circles, looping through enemies, slamming any two it passes over
- Uses full movement every round; ignores opportunity attacks entirely
- Multiattack can split between two targets mid-movement

## Earth Elemental
- Burrowing, tremorsense, extremely high Str/Con, low Dex; vulnerable to thunder
- Picks a target randomly then **stubbornly sticks with it** until subdued
- Earth Glide: unworked earth/stone only — not castle walls
- Siege Monster: double damage to objects — destroys cover for ranged allies

## Fire Elemental
- High Dex/Con, average Str — scrapper; immune to fire, grapple, restraint, prone
- **Fire Form is NOT an action** — triggers when entering a creature's space
- **Rush through every enemy in a line**, setting each on fire (1d10 each), then still make 2 touch attacks — every turn
- Target priority: anyone not on fire → rush past to ignite → touch attack if reachable
- Dislikes water/cold damage but too dumb to decide flee vs attack source — flip a coin

## Water Elemental
- High Dex, extremely high Str/Con — brute-leaning; resistant to acid
- **Whelm (Recharge):** use when 2+ enemies clustered; grapples and drowns 1–2 targets
- Releases unconscious targets (death sentence in open water — they sink)
- Does NOT rush around — moves only to nearest opponent, then slams
- If 30+ ft to travel: favors clustered enemies over solitary ones

---

## Uncontrolled Elementals
- Immediately turns on summoner, then beelines for nearest volume of its element
- Air/Earth: vanish into air or burrow into ground
- Water: massive damage en route to nearest body of water
- Fire: **runs amok** — sets everything ablaze

> [!tip] DM Tips
> Air: constant motion, Whirlwind clusters, slam drive-bys. Earth: pick target, pound flat, repeat. Fire: rush through the whole party every round igniting everyone. Water: Whelm clusters, park and slam.
