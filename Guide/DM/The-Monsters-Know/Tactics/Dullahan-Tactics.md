# Dullahan Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/dullahan-tactics/) | **Date**: January 23, 2023
> **Book**: Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft

---

## Overview

- **Mythic creature** (killed twice). Headless undead warrior driven by vengeance/recapitation.
- **Brute**: Exceptional Str, very high Con/Cha, high Wis. Immune to charmed/frightened/poisoned. Resistant to cold, lightning, poison — but NOT nonmagical physical damage.
- Irish origin: *dulachán*. Consider a mount (riding horse, warhorse, or nightmare — treat as controlled).

## Target Selection

- **Compulsion-driven**, not tactical. If seeking a head: targets whoever's head most resembles its own. If seeking revenge: whoever most reminds it of a rival. Extraordinary provocation needed to redirect.

## On Foot

- **Opens with Head Hunt** (legendary action, all 3): Move 30 ft + Battleaxe with advantage. Crit + failed Con save = decapitation. Use round 1 — legendary actions don't matter yet.
- **In melee**: Multiattack with Battleaxe. Never Fiery Skull in melee (disadvantage). Fiery Skull only vs fleeing targets.
- **After turn**: Head Hunt again unless ≥6 foes in melee → Frightful Presence first, then Attack legendary action vs most troublesome melee opponent.

## Mounted

- Mount can't move during Head Hunt. Mounted dullahan waits for own turn to charge.
- Before turn: Fiery Skull via Attack legendary actions. If engaged in melee before turn: switch to Battleaxe, but still rush preferred target on own turn (even provoking OAs).
- **Pro tip**: Mount charges on its turn (shared initiative, DM picks who goes first), then dullahan takes Head Hunt at end of mount's turn.

## Killing Intent

- **Wants the head**, not just 0 hp. Attacks until target is dead. Melee vs unconscious = auto-crit → better decapitation chance.
- If target dies without decapitation: one final Battleaxe on the corpse.
- **Once head is claimed**: Done. Walks away. Only Frightful Presence if bothered.

## Mythic Phase (0 hp)

- Reinforced by **three death's heads**. Death's heads handle frail targets; dullahan hacks tough ones.
- Head Hunt continues until head claimed. After: Headless Wail (≥2 foes in 10 ft) → Frightful Presence → Coordinated Assault (or Fiery Skull if no melee target).
- Now wants to vanquish ALL enemies. Chases fleeing foes (content to knock unconscious — only needs the one head).
- **Never retreats. Fights until destroyed.**

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running the Dullahan
> Head Hunt opener with advantage + potential decapitation is epic. A nightmare mount with Ethereal Stride virtually guarantees surprise. The mythic phase with death's heads escalates the horror perfectly.
