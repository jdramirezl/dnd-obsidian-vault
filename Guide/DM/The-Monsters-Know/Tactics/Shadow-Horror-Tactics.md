# Shadow Horror Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/horror-tactics/) | **Category**: Aberrations (Ravnica)

---

## Overview
Horrors are barely sentient living nightmares pressed into service by Ravnica's guilds — especially House Dimir. Three varieties: flying, shadow, and skittering. All have 120-ft darkvision and Sunlight Sensitivity (active exclusively at night).

## Flying Horror
- Extraordinary Dex — shock attacker; terrify, maul, skedaddle
- **Round 1**: Stealth to within 90 ft, fly 60 ft, Frightening Screech (stays airborne, out of melee)
- **Rounds 2–3**: Swoop, Claws (avg 22 total per hit — downs level 3–4 PCs in two rounds)
- **Round 4**: Departs via Dash (flying), accepting OAs
- High AC for CR but low HP, no damage resistance — relies on frightened disadvantage
- **Driven off**: ≤19 hp, any radiant damage, or 15+ damage after downing target in round 2

## Shadow Horror
- Large (bear-sized+); standout Charisma, high Wis/Dex; prefers ranged but stat block is inconsistent
- **Lashing Shadows** (recharge 5–6): 60-ft necrotic AoE, hits everyone except other horrors — primary weapon
- **Shadow Stealth**: Hide as bonus action in dim light/darkness (action economy enhancer, not "hide in any shadow")
- **Shadow Stride**: Teleport between shadows within 60 ft (bonus action, mutually exclusive with Shadow Stealth)
- **Incorporeal Movement**: Move through solid objects
- **Opening**: Beeline through walls → within 60 ft → Lashing Shadows → move to nearest usable shadow
- **Subsequent turns**: Lashing Shadows if recharged; otherwise Shadow Stride to target's shadow → Multiattack
- **If wrong enemy engages**: Shadow Stride to intended target, Multiattack
- **Retreat**: ≤48 hp or 12+ radiant damage; through nearest wall, find dim spot, Hide, keep moving
- Often accompanied by flying horrors — threatening through tier 2 into tier 3

## Skittering Horror
- Tier 3; elephantine; extraordinary Strength; Spider Climb + 40-ft climb speed
- Enters through windows, across ceilings; positions above target
- **Sequence**: Maddening Presence → Bite + 2 Claws (10-ft reach, attacks from ceiling)
- Avg 76 damage/round — downs most PCs level 8 or lower in one round, level 16 or lower in two
- Confines melee to assigned target; Maddening Presence on intervening allies
- Drops from ceiling only to auto-crit paralyzed targets
- **Driven off**: ≤91 hp or 23+ radiant damage
