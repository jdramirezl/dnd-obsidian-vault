# Shambling Mound Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/shambling-mound-tactics/) | **Related**: [[Shambling-Mound]]

---

## Tactical Summary
- Plant brute; proficient in Stealth (natural camouflage)
- Immune to blindness, deafness, exhaustion; resistant to cold and fire; immune to lightning (absorbs it to regenerate)
- Blindsight 60 ft only — no other senses

## Combat Behavior
- **Approach**: Stealthy whether waiting or trudging; gains surprise
- **Multiattack combo**: Two Slams (+7) → if both hit, target grappled → Engulf (blinds, restrains, suffocates)
- **After Engulf**: Shambles away with meal — can't Engulf and Attack in same turn except via the Multiattack combo
- **If target escapes**: Repeats Multiattack against same target or switches to another within reach

## Sensory Limitations
- Only detects via blindsight (60 ft); anything beyond is invisible to it
- Ranged attacks from beyond 60 ft: doesn't know source, moves opposite direction
- Conflicting ranged attacks: averages them out or stops moving
- Attacks within blindsight: moves toward attackers
- **Lightning damage**: Moves full speed toward source, hoping for more, even beyond sensory radius

## Retreat Conditions
- Feigns death at ≤54 hp (40% max) — root-stem collapses the mound

## DM Notes
- Pair with will-o'-wisps (lure prey; wisp lightning heals mound)
- Pair with lightning creatures (blue/bronze dragons, storm giants, druids)
- Has swim speed — can drag engulfed targets underwater
- Not intelligent enough to team up; smarter allies exploit its natural behavior
