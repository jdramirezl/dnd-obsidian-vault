# Fleecemane Lion Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/fleecemane-lion-tactics/) | **Date**: January 16, 2025

---

## Tactical Summary

- **CR 3 legendary guardian** of places where Nyx seeps into Theros. Two legendary actions per round.
- **Shock attacker / ambusher**: Very high Dex (higher than Con), proficiency in Perception and Stealth.
- **Spell Turning**: Passive — nasty surprise for spellcasters, makes it bolder around casters.
- **Running Leap**: 25-ft running long jump. Main value is bypassing difficult terrain.

## Combat Behavior

**Pounce is the script**: Requires 20-ft straight-line start. Strikes when target is 25–55 ft away (25–40 ft in difficult terrain). Knocks prone → bonus action Bite.

**Opening sequence**:
1. Burst from hiding, Multiattack (Claw + Bite). Pounce knocks prone → bonus Bite.
2. Both legendary actions: Claw at target.
3. Round 2: Multiattack again. Still no Roar — damage is priority.

**Roar timing**: Do NOT Roar early. Frightened ends at end of fleecemane's next turn — wasted on surprised foes. Save for round 3+ after disengaging, used at end of next creature's turn.

**After round 2 (if prey isn't down)**: One more Multiattack, full movement to back off (may provoke OAs — not smart enough to Disengage). Roar from distance. Circle warily, look for another Pounce.

## Guardian Behavior

- **Fights to the death**. Kills downed foes dead before moving on — more brutal than a common beast.
- If encounter would be Easy/Medium difficulty, emerges slowly growling in warning rather than pouncing.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Place hiding spot 25–40 ft from where PCs enter. Include difficult terrain for Running Leap. Roar is a trap if used early; save for round 3+. For 5E24: consider removing the save from Pounce (auto-prone) — DC 12 is barely worth rolling.
