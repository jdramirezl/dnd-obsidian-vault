# Undercity Medusa and Devkarin Lich Tactics

## Undercity Medusa

Nimbler, stealthier, stronger, and smarter than ordinary medusas. Golgari alliance is convenience, not ideology.

### Spells

- **Fog cloud** (1/day): Often counterproductive — heavily obscured nullifies Surprise Attack advantage. Save for emergency disengagement
- **Expeditious retreat**: Fleeing or circling opponents in large spaces only
- **Misty step**: Limited in tight tunnels; excellent for disengaging from melee

### Round 1 Pattern

1. Ambush front-liner with two **Claw** attacks (Surprise Attack → advantage + 3d6 extra/hit, avg 46 total)
2. **Petrifying Gaze** on bard or wizard (low CON save)
3. Bonus action **misty step** away → into melee with back-liner

### Subsequent Rounds

- Claws against easier-AC targets; Petrifying Gaze continues on low-CON enemies
- Sorcerers have CON save proficiency → use Multiattack instead

### Retreat

Seriously wounded (≤48 hp), 3 rounds elapsed, or disappointed by damage → misty step to disengage, then Dash.

### Allies

1–2 Devkarin warriors (drow elite warrior) hold front line while medusa wrecks squishies.

## Devkarin Lich

Close relative of the standard lich. No *counterspell* or *dimension door*; has *create undead*. Noxious Touch (poisoned) instead of Paralyzing Touch. Regeneration (troll-like) + Undead Fortitude (zombie-like) instead of Rejuvenation. No Frightening Gaze LA. No lair actions.

### Spell Priority

| Situation | Opening Spell |
|---|---|
| Many enemies, open ground | *Circle of death* (huge AoE) |
| Fewer enemies, tighter space | *Cloudkill* (immune to poison → cast at ground zero) |
| Enemies won't close in | *Insect plague* (stationary, lich isn't immune) |

Then: *finger of death* → *blight* → *black tentacles* (only if concentration dropped) → *chromatic orb* → cantrips

### Legendary Actions

- **3+ foes within 30 ft**: Disrupt Life (all 3 LAs)
- **Otherwise**: Cantrips — *poison spray* (default), *acid splash* (2 adjacent), *chill touch* (suppress healing)
- All cantrips deal 3 dice (14th-level caster)
- Noxious Touch (2 LAs) almost never worth it — CON save vs front-liners

### Situational Spells

- **Bestow curse**: Devastating vs rogues (disadvantage → no Sneak Attack) or exploit poor WIS for disadvantage on CON saves
- **Web / Vampiric touch**: Specific problem-solving only; hard to justify over *cloudkill* damage

### Minions

Pre-combat *create undead*: ~12 ghouls or handful of ogre zombies / Erstwhile wights. All poison-immune → unaffected by *cloudkill*. Block while lich casts from range.

### Retreat

Seriously wounded (≤38 hp) → retreats, minions delay pursuers. Doesn't care if they're destroyed.
