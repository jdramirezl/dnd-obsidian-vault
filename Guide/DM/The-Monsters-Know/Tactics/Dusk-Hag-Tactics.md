# Dusk Hag Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/dusk-hag-tactics/) | **Date**: March 4, 2022
> **Book**: Eberron: Rising from the Last War

---

## Overview

- **Distance caster, allergic to melee**. Exceptional Cha, very good Int/Wis, high Dex. Only attacks targets who can't fight back.
- **Core mechanic**: Exploits unconscious condition. Best when targets are unconscious but NOT at 0 hp.
- **CR 6 as printed, effectively CR 7–8** when played optimally. 60 ft blindsight + Perception proficiency.

## The *Sleep* + Nightmare Touch Combo

- **Innate *sleep***: 9d8 hp (avg ≥38 two-thirds of the time). Bypasses already-unconscious targets.
- **Nightmare Touch**: Huge psychic damage. Vs unconscious: auto-crit + curse (no save) reducing hp max daily.
- **Two-hit sequence**: 14d6+4 (crit) + 4d6+4 (normal) = avg 71 damage. Against prone targets (still prone after waking), second attack also has advantage.

> [!warning] Deadly Math
> Against unconscious targets, avg 71 damage per round kills level 2–4 PCs outright. Three fixes: (1) No auto-crit on psychic vs unconscious. (2) Dusk hags don't roam — *sleep* is defensive. (3) Compromise: hag wants to curse, not kill — one Nightmare Touch per target, Multiattack always splits.

## Attack Sequence (Compromise)

1. **Reconnoiter** via *disguise self*, *scrying*, *dream*. Wait for the right moment.
2. **Cast *sleep*** from max range when it will knock out everyone awake (e.g., sentries during rest).
3. **Move in**: Nightmare Touch sleeping targets (one hit each, split Multiattack). Prioritize naturally sleeping targets, melee-averse targets. Skip elf paladins.
4. **Dream Eater (reaction)**: At ≤70 hp, regain hp from waking target. Skip clerics/druids/paladins.
5. **Exit**: *Hypnotic pattern* to boggle melee fighters, then bug out. If fails: Dodge (vs 1) or Disengage (vs 2+).

## If Attacked on Home Turf

- Wants no part of it. *Hypnotic pattern* if charged; *sleep* behind it while withdrawing otherwise. Nightmare Touch only if caught and grappled.

## Coven

- Can form covens. In coven: lean on coven spells (*eyebite*, *polymorph*, *lightning bolt*, *ray of sickness*). Keep enemies at bay with ranged spells.

## Retreat

- No fixed hp threshold. Exits as soon as work is done or trouble is sensed.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running the Dusk Hag
> Horror encounter, not a fair fight. Slow-burn villain — curse PCs in their sleep, watch them scramble for a cure. Use the compromise approach to avoid TPKs. The daily hp max reduction is the real threat.
