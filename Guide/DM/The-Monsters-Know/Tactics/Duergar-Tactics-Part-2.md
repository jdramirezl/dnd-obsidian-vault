# Duergar Tactics: Mordenkainen's Duergar, Part 2

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/duergar-tactics-mordenkainens-duergar-part-2/) | **Date**: August 9, 2019
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Duergar]]

---

## Duergar Mind Master (CR 2)

- **Mind Mastery is terrible in open combat** (DC 12 Int save). Only useful in ambush — not an attack or spell, so doesn't reveal position.
- **Treat as shock trooper**: Two Mind-Poison Dagger attacks (3d6 psychic + weapon each). Very vicious.
- **Invisibility (recharge 4–6)**: Only turn invisible for defense (double/triple-teamed) or relocating after a kill. Not worth it just for advantage.
- **Reduce is the real power**: AC 14→19, Hide as bonus action (+5 Stealth), no speed penalty. Reduce first, then bonus Hide same turn.
- **Retreat at ≤15 hp**: Invisibility and flee. If on cooldown: Dash (Reduced) or Disengage (not Reduced).

## Duergar Warlord

- **Multiattack includes Enlarge**: Always Enlarge on turn 1 (before attacking). No reason not to.
- **Subsequent rounds**: Melee attacks + Call to Attack (grants ally a reaction attack).
- **Psychic-Attuned Hammer >> Javelin**: Javelins only when can't reach. Invisibility to reposition, not for single-attack advantage.
- **Scouring Instruction (reaction)**: Damages ally but warlord doesn't care (lawful evil). Don't use when ally is ≤4 hp from seriously wounded.
- **Retreat at ≤30 hp**: Dodge (vs 1 foe) or Dash (vs multiple). Disengaging is pointless — too slow.

## Duergar Despot

- **Juggernaut**: Extraordinary Str/Con. Siege Monster.
- **Optimal Multiattack vs 1**: Iron Fist (prone, 0 ft knockback) → Stomping Foot ×2 (advantage) → Iron Fist. Limit knockback to ≤25 ft, stay in their face.
- **Flame Jet replaces ANY melee attack**: vs 1 non-prone foe: better than Stomping Foot. vs 2+: better than Stomping Foot vs ANY target. vs 3+: never shut it off.
- **Misty step (1/day)**: Teleport when 3 foes line up for Flame Jet.
- **Counterspell**: Only vs 2nd–3rd level Dex-save spells (fireball, lightning bolt, web). Ignores 1st-level and 4th+.
- **Retreat at ≤47 hp**: Iron Fist knockback 30 ft + Dodge. Flame Jet opportunistically.

## Duergar Constructs

- **Hammerer**: Mindless. Destroys objects/structures. No subtlety or self-preservation.
- **Screamer**: Rolling thunderwave + melee. Rolls toward clustered enemies. Random target if not clustered.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running These Monsters
> Mind master's Reduce + Hide is its real strength. Warlord Enlarges turn 1 every time. Despot's Flame Jet replacing any melee attack is staggeringly powerful. Constructs are mindless tools — commanders determine deployment.
