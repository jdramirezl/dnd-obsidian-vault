# Marid Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/marid-tactics/) | **Date**: October 31, 2016

---

## Overview

- Brute fighter with extraordinary STR/CON and extraordinary mental attributes. Proficient in DEX/WIS/CHA saves; CON high enough without proficiency. Little to fear from spellcasters.
- Chaotic neutral, haughty and whimsical. Quick to take offense.

## Primary Attack: Water Jet

- 60-ft line, guaranteed damage, pushes enemies and knocks prone. Consumes full action.
- Against two targets, Water Jet does more damage on average than two trident strikes in almost all cases. Exception: targets with DEX save ≥+8 and AC ≤12.
- INT 18 — intuitively knows which attack is better against which targets.
- Also useful as **damaging Disengage** (reduces OA risk), knocking PCs into hazards/off boats, and setting up prone targets for minion advantage attacks.

## Spellcasting

- Standard genie package: *gaseous form*, *invisibility*, *plane shift*, *conjure elemental*.
- **Fog cloud** (at will, concentration): heavily obscures area. Marid has 30-ft blindsight — advantage on melee vs blinded PCs, they have disadvantage vs it. Barbarian Danger Sense doesn't work while blinded. With fog cloud, trident viable vs AC ≤15.
- **Control water** (1/day): whirlpool for bludgeoning, flood area, or 20-ft tidal wave.
- Can cast *water breathing*, *water walk*, *create/destroy water*, *purify food and drink* as favors.

## Combat Flow

1. Default: **Water Jet** for damage, *conjure elemental* to exploit prone.
2. PCs dodge Water Jet easily → *fog cloud*, switch to **trident** vs poorly/moderately armored.
3. In water with PCs → *control water* for whirlpool or wave.
4. Special grudge → *plane shift* a PC to the Elemental Plane of Water.

## Retreat

- At ≤160 hp or PCs reliably deal ≥17 hp/turn → **strike a bargain** (may be eccentric/offensive).
- Seriously injured (≤91 hp) → flees via *gaseous form*, *invisibility*, or *plane shift*.
