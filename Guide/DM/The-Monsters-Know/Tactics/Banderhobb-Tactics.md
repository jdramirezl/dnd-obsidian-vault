# Banderhobb Tactics

## Tactical Summary

A magically created frog-like hunter-kidnapper with extraordinary Stealth, Shadow Step teleportation, and a swallow that renders victims unconscious but stable. Exists solely to fulfill its creator's orders. Zero survival instinct. Strictly nocturnal.

## Key Abilities

- **Shadow Step:** Action — teleport ≤30 ft. into dim/dark + one melee attack
- **Shadow Stealth:** Bonus action — Hide in dim light or darkness
- **120 ft. darkvision**, Stealth proficiency

## Attack Sequence: Tongue → Bite → Swallow

1. **Tongue** (15 ft.) — damage + pull adjacent + bonus Bite
2. **Bite** (5 ft.) — damage + grapple/restrain (Large or smaller)
3. **Swallow** (grappled target) — Bite with advantage; on hit, target swallowed

### Kill Mission: Repeat Bite with advantage on restrained target
### Kidnap Mission: Swallow ASAP → victim drops to 0 hp → unconscious but stable → flee home

## Movement Combos

- **Offensive:** Shadow Step into darkness → Shadow Stealth (Hide) → next round attack with advantage
- **Retreat:** Spite-bite → Shadow Step away → Shadow Stealth → Dash home (CON 20 = sprint forever)
- No opportunity attacks possible on Shadow Step (teleportation, not movement)

## Behavioral Rules

- **Single-target focused:** Ignores everyone except its designated target unless physically blocked
- **No retreat until mission complete:** Takes any damage without fleeing
- **Leaves immediately after success:** Shadow Steps away, then Dashes to master
- **Theft missions:** Materialize → Tongue (15 ft.) grab object → Shadow Step away — may end before PCs react

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Horror-movie monster: appears from darkness, grabs someone, vanishes
> - Swallow doesn't kill — victim wakes up wherever the master wants them
> - Great plot device: NPC goes missing, party tracks banderhobb to its creator
> - Shadow Step + Shadow Stealth mid-action is RAW legal
