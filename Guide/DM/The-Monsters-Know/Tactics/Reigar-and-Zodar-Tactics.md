# Reigar and Zodar Tactics

## Reigar (CR 8)

### Tactical Summary

Alien aesthetes — destroyed their home planet as performance art. Extraordinary CHA (primary offense is psionic), exceptional STR, high DEX, above-average CON. Prefer ranged Chromatic Bolt over melee Trident. Every act of violence is intended to create something "beautiful."

### Combat Sequence

1. **Round 1**: Summon Golem (talarith duplicate — uses reigar stats minus talarith, so reduced damage). Must summon early for action economy.
2. **Round 2**: *Mass suggestion* — aesthetically inspired rather than strategically optimal.
3. **Round 3+**: **Chromatic Bolt** every turn — single bolt deals as much or more than two Trident hits.

### Spells

- *Mass suggestion* (1/day): go-to spell, affects most targets
- *Dimension door* (2/day): entry and exit only
- *Phantasmal force* (2/day): weak — mostly embellishment
- *Sending* (1/day): no combat application

### Retreat

- Either peaces out early (≤57 hp) or fights to the death in a blaze of glory — no middle ground

---

## Zodar (CR 12)

### Tactical Summary

Genuinely weird aberrations. Extraordinary CON, bonkers STR. Melee brutes. Default state is **indifference** — hover in silence. Only fight if meddled with. Nothing will make them retreat.

### Multiattack

- **Forced Teleport** + 2× Crushing Fist (any order)
- Forced Teleport: CON save, 60 ft relocation + 4d10 force damage
- **Pull in**: teleport target to melee → pummel twice (avg 64 damage)
- **Throw out**: pummel twice → teleport away ("Take Out the Trash")

### Key Behavior

- After pummeling an intruder, considers the matter **settled** — returns to idle
- If opponents persist, fights indefinitely — **never retreats**
- Wish ability reserved for out-of-combat use

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Reigar CR 8 assumes the golem is summoned — lone reigar is closer to CR 5-6
> - Zodars make excellent mysterious recurring NPCs — introduce early, have it do something terrifying much later
> - Zodar's Forced Teleport can't drop targets in midair (RAW)
