# Eater of Hope Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/eater-of-hope-tactics/) | **Date**: September 19, 2025
> **Book**: Mythic Odysseys of Theros

---

## Overview

- **Shock attacker** (CR 6). Extraordinary Str, very high Dex. 60-ft fly, 120-ft darkvision.
- Theran "demon" — corrupted mortal soul obsessed with wealth. Uses treasure as bait but must steal it.
- **Top targets**: Heroes returning from adventure, laden with loot and weakened. Strikes after sunset.
- No Perception/Stealth proficiency — not an ambush predator. Dive-bombs from high perches.
- **Dead is dead** — no Underworld reformation.

## Combat Sequence

1. **Breath of Hopelessness** (5–6 recharge, 30-ft cone): Always open with this. Position for ≥3 targets. Wait if threshold not met.
2. **Multiattack cursed targets**: Claws deal extra necrotic vs failed Cha saves. Hit (avg 22) + breath (avg 26) = 48 total — knocks out level 5 PCs.
3. **Fly back up** after knocking foe unconscious (no OA from unconscious).
4. **After cursed targets down**: Momentum fades. Claws only 15 damage. Risks OAs (5-ft reach). High AC but low hp.
5. **Recharge Breath**: Use again if available. Otherwise Multiattack whoever hurt it most (reactive, not strategic).

## Target Selection

- Smart enough to plan but not disciplined enough to stick to it. Reacts to every change without big-picture thinking.
- Should target frail/healers but instead attacks whoever hurt it most recently.
- Always prioritizes cursed targets for extra necrotic.

## Social Proficiencies

- Intimidation, Deception, Persuasion — apply to non-combat: enticing victims toward treasure-laden ruins, then robbing them when they emerge weakened. Two distinct use cases (combat predator vs social manipulator).

## Retreat

- **At ≤36 hp**: Snarl, Disengage if necessary (Dash otherwise), fly away.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running the Eater of Hope
> Best as a post-dungeon ambush — party is wounded, loaded with treasure, camping for the night. Breath of Hopelessness opener into dive-bombing cursed targets is devastating against tier 2 parties.
