# Astral Elf Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/astral-elf-tactics/)

---

## Tactical Summary
- Spelljammer humanoids across five stat blocks: warrior (CR 3), star priest (CR 5), honor guard (CR 5), commander (CR 7), aristocrat (CR 8)
- Very high mental stats but warriors/commanders lack spellcasting—they're systematic sharpshooters and shock attackers
- Only engage in Deadly encounters; parley (bluff via Intimidation/Deception) in Hard encounters; flee in Medium/Easy
- Commanders can *teleport* strike teams of up to 8 directly to an enemy ship's helm
- Aristocrats are glass cannons with *mislead*, Radiant Beam, Summon Solar Dragon, and Starlight Step

## Combat Behavior
- **Warrior**: Shoot from 60–150 ft until enemies are softened, then charge in with blades. Systematic target selection based on perceived weakness. Retreat at ≤40 hp (moderate) if enemies aren't worse off; always at ≤23 hp (serious)
- **Commander**: Negotiates when parley is wiser. Uses *teleport* (2/day) to lead 8-ally strike teams directly to enemy spelljammer helms. Retreats at ≤57 hp or when all underlings are retreating/dead
- **Star Priest**: Stays way in back (60+ ft from nearest enemy, behind cover). Rain of Radiance to focus down effective fighters; *hold person* on low-Wis melee warriors during boarding. If any enemy gets within 30 ft, Starlight Step away + Dash. Heals itself once at ≤36 hp; retreats at ≤63 hp
- **Honor Guard**: Stands next to VIP, hurls Radiant Rays at approaching threats, switches to Longsword when they close. Fights to the death protecting its charge; bulk stays behind to delay if aristocrat retreats
- **Aristocrat**: With full honor guard, stays behind defenders using Radiant Beam opportunistically. Casts *mislead* when enemies charge—illusory double stays while real aristocrat repositions invisibly. Alone or with few guards, casts *fly* for mobile Radiant Beam + Scimitar + Starlight Step hit-and-run. Summon Solar Dragon only when needed to balance a Hard encounter (and it just works—no roll)

## Key Decision Points
- Encounter difficulty determines behavior: Deadly → fight, Hard → bluff/parley, Medium/Easy → flee
- Commander *teleport* strike: near-guaranteed success with a spy's object or thorough scrying; 99% success rate in two attempts
- Aristocrat *mislead*: the illusory double appears to take damage and doesn't retreat, keeping enemies fighting a phantom while the real aristocrat repositions

## Retreat Conditions
- Warriors: ≤40 hp if enemies aren't hurt; always at ≤23 hp (a few least-wounded stay to delay)
- Commander: ≤57 hp or when all underlings are gone
- Star Priest: ≤63 hp after self-healing
- Honor Guard: fights to the death
- Aristocrat: ≤72 hp, disciplined withdrawal with 2 guards; remaining guards delay

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Astral elves are at their best in ship-to-ship encounters—use the commander's *teleport* strike to bypass the arrow-storm phase entirely and drop a kill team on the party's helm.
