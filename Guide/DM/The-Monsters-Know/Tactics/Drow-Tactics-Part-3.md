# Drow Tactics, Part 3: Drow Priestess of Lolth

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/drow-tactics-part-3/) | **Date**: September 29, 2016
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Elves-Drow]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- **High-challenge spellcasting variant** with high Wisdom and Charisma — as strong in social interaction as in combat.
- **Summon Demon**: 30% chance of a CR 10 yochlol. Risky but payoff is enormous (~16 hp/round independently + dominate person + web at will).
- **No bonus action spells** (no spiritual weapon) — relies on allies (demons, giant spiders) for action economy.
- **Master manipulator**: Insight proficiency + high Cha zeroes in on PCs' psychological weaknesses.

## Social Interaction

- **Ambush reflex** like all drow — hides, observes, attacks with surprise or parleys if threat is substantial or nonexistent.
- **Insight proficiency**: Understands what PCs want. Zeroes in on psychological Achilles' heels — pity, ego, insecurity, greed, scruples.
- **+4 to Persuasion/Intimidation/Deception** from Charisma alone. Interactions should feel slippery and treacherous.

## Combat Sequence

1. **Parley fails** → Summon Demon (yochlol, 30% chance)
2. **Next round**: Insect plague (if ≥4 non-fighter PCs within 40 ft) OR conjure animals (2 giant spiders)
3. **Restrain enemies**: Giant spider allies spin webs (priestess's web spell also requires concentration — can't stack)
4. **Ray of sickness**: Aim at webbed targets, boost to 4th level (4th-level slots otherwise useless)
5. **Out of 4th-level slots**: Melee scourge vs webbed opponents. 1st-level ray of sickness vs unwebbed approaching foes.

## Yochlol Tactics (if summoned)

- Picks PC with highest Strength-to-Wisdom ratio → dominate person
- Casts web on unrestrained PCs (melee fighters first)
- Melee attacks against restrained targets
- Mist Form only if PCs flee into enclosed area

## Retreat Conditions

- **Flight at ≤28 hp**: Disengages if allies run interference; Dashes otherwise.
- Likely accompanied by 1–6 drow elite warrior bodyguards. With honor guard, emphasizes control spells over personal melee.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> Lead with social interaction — the priestess should make PCs feel outmaneuvered before combat starts. Pre-roll the yochlol summoning (use Divination to justify knowing the outcome). Her biggest weakness is lack of bonus action economy — she needs allies.
