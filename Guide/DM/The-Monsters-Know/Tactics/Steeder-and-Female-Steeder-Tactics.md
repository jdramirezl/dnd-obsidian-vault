## General Notes (from stat block)

- Giant spiders used as mounts by Underdark races (primarily duergar).
- **Male steeder** (CR 1/4): Small beast. Spider Climb, Sticky Leg (grapple on hit). Low threat individually — used in swarms or as mounts.
- **Female steeder** (CR 1): Larger, more dangerous. Extraordinary Leap (up to 90 ft). Bite + Sticky Leg. More aggressive and territorial.
- As beasts with very low INT, steeders are simple predators or obedient mounts. No complex tactics — they attack the nearest threat, grapple with Sticky Leg, and bite. Females leap onto prey from above when possible.
