# Tlincalli Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Tlincalli Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/tlincalli-tactics/)

**Bestiary:** (scorpion-centaur monstrosity from *Volo's Guide*)

---

## Overview

The tlincalli (pronounced tlhin-KAHL-lee, Nahuatl) is a centaur-like monstrosity with a humanoid torso atop a scorpioid body. Neutral evil desert-dwelling nomadic hunters that hunt at dawn and dusk.

## Ability Profile

- **Very high Strength and Constitution**, above-average Dexterity — brutes unafraid of direct melee
- Below-average Intelligence, above-average Wisdom — good at picking promising prey (old, young, weak, isolated)
- Proficiency in **Perception**, **Stealth**, **Survival** — ambush attackers and trackers
- **Darkvision**; no social skills, below-average Charisma — parley is off the table

## Combat Tactics

### Initiation
- Ambush from hiding (sneak up via Stealth); gain advantage + surprise
- Pursue prey aggressively until bagged or fought off

### Multiattack Heuristic (Revised per Math Analysis)

Each Multiattack = one Longsword or Spiked Chain + one Sting.

- **Target NOT already paralyzed/restrained:** Always lead with **Spiked Chain** → follow with **Sting**
  - Chain hit auto-grapples and restrains → grants advantage on the more powerful Sting
  - Slightly more damage AND higher chance to paralyze
- **Target IS already paralyzed/restrained:** Use **Longsword + Sting** in any order

### Key Attacks
- **Sting** (5 ft reach) — avg 20 damage, potential paralysis on save super-fail
- **Spiked Chain** (10 ft reach) — hit auto-grapples and restrains; opportunity attacks in 30-ft sphere
- **Longsword** — always one-handed (other hand holds chain); greater damage than chain

### Hunting Goal
1. Paralyze or restrain prey → wrap with Spiked Chain → drag away
2. If first round doesn't bring prey down → beat unconscious before hauling off

### Retreat
- Neutral evil — more truculent than a predatory beast, fights longer
- Flees when **seriously injured (≤34 hp)** using **Dash**
