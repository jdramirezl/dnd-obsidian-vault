# Drow Tactics: Matron Mothers

> [Source](https://www.themonstersknow.com/drow-tactics-matron-mothers/)
> Bestiary: [[Elves-Drow]]

---

## Tactical Summary

- CR 20 legendary spellcaster (up to 9th level); second only to Lolth in drow hierarchy
- Spellcaster first, skirmisher second; always surrounded by minions; legendary actions include Compel Demon
- AC 17 is low for a boss — focus on neutralizing ranged/spell attackers
- Summon Servant (yochlol/retriever) always succeeds; Lolth's Fickle Favor hurts ally but grants advantage

## Combat Behavior

- **Round 1**: *spiritual weapon* (bonus) + Summon Servant (action); hang back behind minions
- **Legendary actions**: spend low-level slots in Tower of Hanoi pattern (1st, 2nd, 1st, 3rd...); always *dispel magic* for important ≤3rd-level enemy spells
- **Round 2**: concentration spell by situation:
  - *Holy aura* (8th): clustered, defensive — advantage on saves, disadvantage on incoming attacks
  - *Blade barrier* (6th): clustered, offensive/ambush
  - *Spirit guardians* (3rd-5th): spread out, defensive, melee-heavy foes
  - *Hold person* at 5th: exactly 4 opponents — paralyze all
  - *Contagion*: if rushed (poisons immediately, no concentration)
- **Melee**: Demon Staff (two-handed, finesse) when advantaged; Tentacle Rod only vs frail unarmored casters (AC ≤12)
- **Key spells**: *gate* (9th) to summon named creature; *divine word* (7th) finishing move vs ≤50 hp; *bestow curse* at 5th (8 hr, no conc.); *plane shift* emergency exit

## Key Decision Points

- Doesn't underestimate opposition — works top-down through spell list fast
- *Death ward* pre-cast if danger anticipated (mark off slot)
- *Gate* only when seriously wounded (≤104 hp) or ≥50% minions lost
- Lolth's Fickle Favor best on elite warriors vs medium/heavy armor; never on regular drow or shadowblades

## Retreat Conditions

- *Plane shift* only when clearly, irremediably defeated
- *Gate* for reinforcements when seriously wounded or losing minions
- Has *death ward* — does not retreat lightly

> [!tip] Between action, bonus (*spiritual weapon*), legendary actions (cast spells on others' turns), and Compel Demon, the matron mother takes 5+ meaningful actions per round. Plan her turns in advance or she'll slow the game to a crawl.
