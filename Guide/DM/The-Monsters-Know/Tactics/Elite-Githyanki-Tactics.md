# Elite Githyanki Tactics

> [!summary]
> Mordenkainen's elite githyanki: the gish (fighter/mage shock attacker), kith'rak (company captain), and supreme commander (legendary leader). Highly disciplined, coordinate with allies, retreat strategically when seriously wounded.

---

## Githyanki Gish
- Eldritch knight/war mage — fierce shock attacker + potent spellcaster
- High scores across the board; proficient in Perception, Stealth, Con saves
- Excels at ambush

### Key Spells
- **Haste:** first priority — cast on self; massive psychic sword damage boost
- **Counterspell:** reserve 2 slots; shuts down enemy casters
- **Fireball:** only if 4+ enemies clustered; lowest priority of 3rd-level trio
- **Misty step:** teleport next to casters; Stealth check on arrival
- **Dimension door:** tactical entry/exit (×2); *plane shift* still in reserve
- **Plane shift:** emergency evac OR surprise assault (brings 8 allies)
- **Telekinesis:** push shock attackers into hazards/away from allies
- **Thunderwave:** knock 2+ prone → War Magic bonus longsword (advantage)

### Combat Sequence
1. Identify biggest threat — usually Dex-save AoE caster
2. *Misty step* adjacent → Stealth check → longsword Multiattack (advantage if unseen)
3. *Counterspell* the caster's response
4. If threat is shock attacker: *telekinesis* to push/immobilize, or interpose + *haste* + War Magic barrage
5. Allies engage enemy melee fighters to lock them down

### Surprise Assault
- *Haste* on self → *plane shift* with up to 8 allies into enemy position
- Must arrive from a different plane; *haste* can be cast before *plane shift*

### Retreat
- Seriously wounded (≤49 hp) or half+ allies down → calls retreat
- Stays behind while allies withdraw; may attempt nonverbal parley

---

## Githyanki Kith'rak
- Company captain — essentially a stronger githyanki warrior
- Rally the Troops: use as applicable
- Parry: reaction vs melee attack rolls of 18–21
- Seriously wounded at ≤72 hp; nearly always last to retreat

---

## Githyanki Supreme Commander
- Shares knight tactics plus: *Bigby's hand*, *mass suggestion*, *levitate* (self), Parry, legendary actions

### Bigby's Hand (Concentration)
- Clenched Fist: smash low-AC casters
- Grasping Hand: immobilize rogues/monks
- Forceful Hand: shove into hazards
- Interposing Hand: block pursuers

### Mass Suggestion
- "Drop your weapons, surrender and kneel!" — synergizes with Intimidation
- Needs a githyanki knight to translate (only speaks Gith)

### Legendary Actions
- **Command Ally (1):** default — ally attacks with advantage; use on 3rd-to-last turn before SC's turn. Don't use on kith'rak (needs reaction for Parry)
- **Teleport (1):** disengage when 2+ enemies deal 57+ total damage in a round
- **Attack (2):** only if 2 legendary actions remain on last turn before SC's turn

### Retreat
- Seriously wounded (≤74 hp): if troops equally hurt → halts and parleys via intimidation (threats and posturing, not diplomacy)

> [!tip] DM Tips
> Gish: *haste* → *misty step* to caster → longsword barrage → *counterspell*. Kith'rak: stronger warrior with Parry, always last to leave. Supreme Commander: Command Ally is default legendary action; Attack is fallback.
