# Umber Hulk Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Umber Hulk Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/umber-hulk-tactics/)

**Bestiary:** [[Umber-Hulk]]

---

## Overview

A straightforward brute that runs on autopilot — but its Confusing Gaze creates a damned-if-you-do, damned-if-you-don't dilemma.

## Ability Profile

- **Extraordinary Strength, very high Constitution**, above-average Dexterity
- 120 ft darkvision, 60 ft tremorsense; burrow 20 ft; **Tunneler** (rock = difficult terrain)

## The Confusing Gaze Dilemma

Within **30 ft** at start of turn, can see umber hulk → avert eyes or **DC 15 Cha save**:
- 50% stand and stare; 25% walk random direction; 25% attack random target

**The Catch-22:**
- No darkvision + no light → blinded (umber hulk advantage, you disadvantage)
- Darkvision or light → susceptible to Confusing Gaze

**Only two unhandicapped options:** high Cha + Cha save proficiency, or stay at range.

## Combat Tactics

- **Ambush from underground** — burrow beneath, burst up as unseen attacker (advantage on first strike)
- Close to melee with **nearest enemy**, no discrimination
- **Multiattack:** two Claws + one Bite every round — pure autopilot
- If opponent retreats → umber hulk **advances**

## Retreat

- Breaks off when **seriously wounded (≤37 hp)**
- Prefers **burrowing through loose earth** (collapses behind it)
- If no loose earth: **Dodge while retreating** for 2 rounds (backward, facing enemies — Confusing Gaze disorients pursuers)
- After 2 rounds Dodging → **Dash**
