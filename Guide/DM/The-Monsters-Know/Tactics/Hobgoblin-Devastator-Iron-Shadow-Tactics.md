# Hobgoblin Tactics: Devastator and Iron Shadow

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/hobgoblin-tactics-devastator-iron-shadow/) | **Date**: January 10, 2017
> **Related Bestiary**: [[Hobgoblins]]

---

## Hobgoblin Devastator (Battle Wizard)

- Strong physical stats (CON highest), very high INT, above-average WIS. Accurately assesses enemy weaknesses.
- **Army Arcana**: Like Sculpt Spells — lob AoE without regard for allies in the area.
- **Arcane Advantage**: Extra 2d6 on ranged spell *attacks* (not saves) vs foes within 5 ft of an ally. Once per turn.

### Spell Priority

| Situation | Spell |
|---|---|
| Clustered enemies | *Fireball* (8d6, ~84 total expected vs 4 targets) |
| Slow clustered enemies | *Ice storm* (2d8+4d6, ~69 total expected vs 4 targets) |
| One priority target | *Scorching ray* (doubled by Arcane Advantage) |
| Cover retreat / signal withdrawal | *Fog cloud* |
| Dispel enemy fog, hold chokepoints | *Gust of wind* |
| Charged by melee attacker | *Gust of wind* > *fly* > *thunderwave* > *shocking grasp* |
| Out of higher slots | *Magic missile* (should have retreated already) |
| Cantrips w/ Arcane Advantage | *Fire bolt* (18 hp), *ray of frost* (16 hp + slow), *shocking grasp* (16 hp + no OA) |

- Only boosts *scorching ray* to 3rd, and only if focusing one target outweighs *fireball* vs a cluster.
- Quarterstaff only under *silence*.
- **Retreat**: Seriously injured at ≤18 hp → casts *fog cloud* as signal to retreat.

## Hobgoblin Iron Shadow (Rogue-Monk-Mage)

- DEX > CON > STR → skirmisher. AC 15. Stealth proficiency. **Operates only at night** (Shadow Jaunt needs dim light/darkness + darkvision).
- **Multiattack**: 4 attacks (ranged or melee, any combo) + Shadow Jaunt before or after any. Can simulate two attackers (dart–dart–Jaunt–dart–dart). Think in 3D — teleport onto ledges/rafters.

### Key Combo: *Expeditious Retreat*

- Cast as bonus action round 1, sustain throughout. Grants Dash as bonus action.
- Full turn: move → dart → dart → Shadow Jaunt → dart → dart → move again (base 40 ft).

### Target Priority

1. **Clerics** (especially with darkvision) — high Perception can spot it; cast *bane* targeting CHA.
2. Bards and other spellcasters, enemies with darkvision.
3. Whoever stands between it and its mission objective.

### Combat Style

- Ends turn out of view whenever possible. Avoids unarmed strikes (melee → OAs). Uses darts for hit-and-run.
- Unarmed only when: target in full cover, target grappled, or forced into melee.
- **Kidnap combo**: From hiding, grapple (action) with advantage → full movement + Dash (bonus) = drag target 40 ft.
- **Retreat**: Seriously wounded at ≤12 hp → Disengages and flees, still using *expeditious retreat*.
