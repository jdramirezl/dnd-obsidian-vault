# Bariaur Wanderer Tactics

## Tactical Summary

Celestial ranger-brutes native to Ysgard. Exceptional Strength, high Dex/Con, above-average AC and HP. Straightforward melee combatants — close, jab, jab, repeat. Disappointingly simple design: Ram doesn't combo with Multiattack, spells are all utility.

## Combat Behavior

### Multiattack (Default)
- 2× Barbed Javelin or 2× Shortbow every round
- Barbed Javelin reduces target speed by 10 ft. per hit (RAW stacking debatable)
- Shortbow deals reduced damage to unwounded targets, full to wounded (Colossus Slayer nod)

### Ram (Situational)
- Requires 20+ ft. straight-line charge; can knock prone
- Excluded from Multiattack — can't follow up on the prone target alone
- Best in groups: one Rams to knock prone, another Multiattacks with advantage

### Mighty Leap (Bonus Action)
- 40 ft. jump any direction; bypasses difficult terrain
- Combined with 40 ft. base speed = 80 ft. effective movement/turn

### Spells: All utilitarian, irrelevant to combat

## Retreat Conditions

- Seriously wounded (≤28 hp): withdraws
- No reason to fight to the death

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Elementary combatants — use as allies, guides, or mid-tier foes without overthinking
> - In groups, coordinate Ram + Multiattack across bariaurs for the prone combo
> - Portal Sense (30 ft.) is a great plot hook — they instinctively know where portals lead
> - Corrupted bariaurs picking up lower-plane habits make interesting morally gray encounters
