# NPC Tactics: Cultists, Priests and Druids

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — NPC Tactics: Cultists and Priests](https://www.themonstersknow.com/npc-tactics-cultists-priests-druids/)

## Cultists

Shifty commoners with exotic swords. No spells, no stealth. Deception proficiency for hiding identity or recruiting. Priority #1: don't be discovered. Fight only if cover blown and they outnumber PCs; flee if outnumbered. Fight to the death (fanaticism + fear of execution).

## Cult Fanatics

Serious threat. Key spells: *spiritual weapon* (bonus action, 9 rounds — indispensable) and *inflict wounds* (3d10 at 1st level, 4d10 at 2nd — "the Bad Touch").

- With cultist allies: leave melee to them, use *sacred flame* + *spiritual weapon* from range
- Cast *shield of faith* (bonus, turn 1) → *spiritual weapon* (bonus, turn 2)
- **Combos**: *Command: "Approach!"* → *inflict wounds* next turn. *Hold person* → crit with *spiritual weapon*. *Command: "Scram!"* → provoke OAs from all engaged allies.
- Fight to the death.

## Acolytes

Not combat-equipped. Commoners with Wis and spells. *Bless* on allies, *cure wounds* on wounded, *sanctuary* on key/injured allies, *sacred flame* otherwise. Likely to freeze or flee unless protecting someone.

## Priests

Self-defense only. **Sequence**: *Spirit guardians* (R1 action) → *spiritual weapon* (R2 bonus) + *sacred flame* (R2 action). If enemy closes: Divine Eminence (bonus) + weapon Attack. With allies: *guiding bolt* (4d6 + grants advantage) or *cure wounds*/*sanctuary*. Seriously wounded (≤10 hp) → surrender/flee unless fighting ideological enemies.

## Druids

Scrappier. Above-average Dex/Con. *Shillelagh* (bonus, +4/1d8+2). Big gun: *entangle* (area restraint).

**Combos**: *Entangle* → *produce flame* with advantage (1d8 fire). Or *shillelagh* + melee; *thunderwave* if surrounded by 3+.
**Retreat**: *Longstrider* (speed 40) → Dash into wilderness. Encounters on druid's home turf.
