# Nightveil Specter and Gloamwing Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/nightveil-specter-and-gloamwing-tactics/) | **Date**: March 30, 2026
> **Book**: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica

---

## Overview — Nightveil Specter

- **House Dimir's memory-wiping enforcer** — a former captive stripped of identity and repurposed as undead.
- Extraordinary Dex, exceptional Str. Proficient in Perception and Stealth.
- Int is low (ape-level), but very high Wis powers intuition and senses. Understands Common (can eavesdrop).
- Immune to charm and fright. Resistant to ordinary weapons. 120 ft darkvision → **nocturnal only**.
- Almost never encountered without its **gloamwing** mount.

## Overview — Gloamwing

- Large undead mount (possibly animated from a lesser dragon/wyvern).
- Exceptional Str, very high Dex/Con, lizard-level Int. **Sunlight Sensitivity**.
- Proficient in Perception and Stealth → owl-like silent gliding.
- Can carry up to 600 lbs — enough for 2 Medium humanoids in its claws.
- **Flyby** trait exempts both itself and its rider from opportunity attacks.

## Mission Logic: Lethal vs Nonlethal

| Scenario | Response |
|---|---|
| Intrusion discovered within 24 hours | Knock out → relocate → **Reap Memory** (erase the relevant hour) |
| Trespasser about to discover something | Erase how they got there, drop them elsewhere |
| Leak is 24+ hours old | **Marked for death** |

## Combat Sequence

### Opening: Mind Twist (Recharge 5–6)
- 60-ft cone, psychic damage + stunned condition. Generously large AoE.
- Gloamwing positions the specter for optimal coverage.
- **Only used once** — subsequent damage must be nonlethal (Mind Twist can't be made nonlethal).

### After Mind Twist
1. **Specter dismounts**, attacks stunned targets with Scythe (Multiattack, 2 hits) — **nonlethal damage** to knock them to 0 hp.
2. **Gloamwing** (independent mount, own initiative) attacks non-stunned targets. Also nonlethal.
3. Goal: **knock everyone unconscious** (0 hp from nonlethal = 1d4 hours out).

### Cleanup
1. Gloamwing grabs 2 unconscious bodies at a time, transports them to a neutral location up to 1 mile away.
2. Specter uses **Reap Memory** (3/day) on unconscious targets at leisure — erases all memory of the encounter.
3. If more than 3 targets: gloamwing fetches another specter to help (rare case of gloamwing without rider).

> [!warning] Excess Targets
> If there are more targets than Reap Memory uses, House Dimir may kill the extras — but discourages this against other guild members to avoid retaliation.

## Retreat

- **Neither specter nor gloamwing retreats** from damage — compulsion to obey is too strong.
- Killing a gloamwing destroys its bond and drives the specter into **vengeful frenzy**.
- Killing a specter destroys its gloamwing.
- House Dimir may order retreat *after* memory erasure to protect the work (memories restore if specter dies within 24 hours).
