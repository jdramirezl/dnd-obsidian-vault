# Green Dragon Tactics

> [!summary]
> Green dragons are the craftiest and most manipulative of the chromatic dragons. They swim to ambush, use Poison Breath in cones, and are proficient in Deception, Insight, and Persuasion — skills no other chromatic dragon has. When wounded, they parley rather than flee, always scheming for advantage.

---

## Universal Dragon Traits (All Colors)
- Fly at 2× land speed; blindsight + darkvision
- High STR + CON (brute profile); proficient in Perception + Stealth
- Saves: DEX, CON, WIS, CHA
- Breath weapon recharges on 5–6 (avg every 3 rounds)
- **Legendary Resistance (adult+):** use every failed save without conserving

## Green-Specific Traits
- **Swim speed** — ambush from water like a crocodile
- **Poison Breath** (cone)
- **Social skill proficiencies:** Deception, Insight, Persuasion — unique among chromatic dragons
- **Personality:** Crafty, manipulative, scheming. Young greens are "cocky hustlers"
- Green wyrmlings have lower CON — may prefer Stealth avoidance over direct combat

---

## By Age

### Wyrmling
- Stealth-first: swim beneath water, surface to ambush
- Lower CON than other chromatic wyrmlings — may avoid combat unless attacked
- **Poison Breath** first (cone), then Bite on subsequent turns
- Won't engage 2+ melee opponents — Dodge + reposition if ganged up on
- **Flee at ≤40% hp**

### Young
- Gains **Multiattack:** Claw/Claw/Bite
- Willing to engage **2 melee opponents** (not 3)
- Adolescent personality: cocky hustler — uses social skills to bluff and manipulate

### Adult (Legendary)
- **Lair actions, legendary actions, Frightful Presence**
- Breath weapon always preferred — repositions to maximize cone coverage
- If breath unavailable: **Frightful Presence** + Claw/Claw/Bite

#### Lair Actions (Pick 1 per round, never same twice)
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Grasping Roots** | Movement restrictor (restrain) | Melee opponents — restrained = can't approach or escape |
| **Enchanting Fog** | Debilitator (charm one creature) | Non-elf with lowest Wisdom |
| **Thorny Brush** | Direct damage | Anyone in range — general softener |

##### Fizban's New Lair Actions
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Creeper Vines** | Indirect damage (conjures zombies) | Distraction only — zombies at CR 1/4 don't significantly contribute vs CR 15+ dragon. Treat as "indirect direct damage" |
| **Lashing Root** | Direct damage + knockdown (single target) | Troublesome single enemy — prone effect lasts only until their turn |

#### Legendary Actions (3/round)
| Action | Cost | Use When |
|---|---|---|
| **Detect** | 1 | Any stealthy movement within line of sight |
| **Tail Attack** | 1 | PC behind the dragon; max once per round if 3+ melee attackers threaten |
| **Wing Attack** | 2 | **3+ PCs within 10 ft** — knocks prone + bonus fly to reposition. Also escape trigger at ≤40% hp |

### Ancient
- Same tactics as adult with bigger numbers, extended ranges
- Poison Breath cone is massive — spread-out parties still get caught

## Green Dragon Combat Personality

> [!important] Key Difference — Parley and Manipulation
> When moderately wounded (≤70% hp), green dragons **stop fighting and parley**, making full use of Deception, Insight, and Persuasion. They will even surrender — on terms favorable to themselves — always watching for any opportunity to turn against their new "masters."

| Dragon Color | At Moderate Wounds |
|---|---|
| White | Keeps fighting (dense, truculent) |
| Red | Keeps fighting (wrathful, arrogant) |
| Blue | Disengages, snipes from range |
| Black | Skedaddles |
| **Green** | **Stops fighting, parleys — manipulates and schemes** |

---

## Quick Reference: Combat Flow
1. **Pre-combat:** Stealth → swim ambush from water, or hide in dense forest
2. **Round 1:** Poison Breath (cone — reposition to catch max targets, ignore opportunity attacks)
3. **Breath unavailable:** Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite
4. **Lair action (init 20):** Enchanting Fog on lowest-WIS non-elf, Grasping Roots to restrain melee fighters, Thorny Brush for damage
5. **Legendary actions:** Wing Attack if 3+ in melee → reposition; Tail Attack for rear threats; Detect for sneakers
6. **≤70% hp:** Stop fighting. **Parley.** Use Deception/Insight/Persuasion to negotiate favorable terms
7. **≤40% hp:** If parley fails, Wing Attack → fly away

> [!tip] DM Note
> Green dragons are the only chromatic dragons with social skills. A green dragon encounter should feel like a negotiation as much as a fight. Even in surrender, the green dragon is scheming — it will honor the letter of any agreement while looking for loopholes and opportunities to betray.
