# White Dragon Tactics

> [!summary]
> White dragons are the least intelligent chromatic dragons — dense, truculent, barely articulate predators. They fight with brute stubbornness and never parley. They burrow to ambush and use Cold Breath to devastating effect.

---

## Universal Dragon Traits (All Colors)
- Fly at 2× land speed; blindsight + darkvision
- High STR + CON (brute profile); proficient in Perception + Stealth
- Saves: DEX, CON, WIS, CHA
- Breath weapon recharges on 5–6 (avg every 3 rounds, but dice are fickle)
- **Legendary Resistance (adult+):** use every failed save without conserving

## White-Specific Traits
- **Burrow speed** — ambush from beneath ice/snow
- **Ice Walk** — no difficult terrain penalty on ice
- **Personality:** Dense, truculent, feral. Young whites are "barely articulate predators"

---

## By Age

### Wyrmling
- Stealth-first: burrow up from beneath snow/ice to ambush
- **Breath weapon** first, then Bite on subsequent turns
- Won't engage 2+ melee opponents — Dodge + reposition if ganged up on
- **Flee at ≤40% hp** — Dodge and fly away

### Young
- Gains **Multiattack:** Claw/Claw/Bite
- Willing to engage **2 melee opponents** (not 3)
- Same ambush and breath-first tactics as wyrmling

### Adult (Legendary)
- **Lair actions, legendary actions, Frightful Presence**
- Breath weapon remains top priority — repositions to catch max targets (ignores opportunity attacks)
- If breath unavailable: **Frightful Presence** (first use only matters) + Claw/Claw/Bite

#### Lair Actions (Pick 1 per round, never same twice)
| Action | Type | Best Against |
|---|---|---|
| **Wall of Ice** | Movement restrictor | Cut off escape routes or split the party |
| **Ice Shards** | Direct damage | Anyone in range — good general softener |
| **Freezing Fog** | Debilitator (heavily obscures + cold dmg) | Spellcasters, ranged attackers |

#### Legendary Actions (3/round)
| Action | Cost | Use When |
|---|---|---|
| **Detect** | 1 | Any stealthy movement — dragons are very hard to sneak up on |
| **Tail Attack** | 1 | PC behind the dragon; don't use twice if 3+ melee attackers threaten |
| **Wing Attack** | 2 | **3+ PCs within 10 ft** — knocks prone + bonus fly movement to reposition. Also used to initiate escape at ≤40% hp |

### Ancient
- Same tactics as adult, just bigger numbers, longer ranges, higher DCs
- No new features

## White Dragon Combat Personality

> [!important] Key Difference from Other Chromatics
> White dragons are **too stubborn to retreat tactically**. When moderately wounded (≤70% hp), they just keep fighting. They only flee when seriously wounded (≤40% hp).

| Dragon Color | At Moderate Wounds |
|---|---|
| **White** | Keeps fighting (dense, truculent) |
| Red | Keeps fighting (wrathful, arrogant) |
| Blue | Disengages, takes potshots from range |
| Black | Skedaddles |
| Green | Stops fighting, parleys |

---

## Quick Reference: Combat Flow
1. **Pre-combat:** Stealth → burrow ambush from beneath ice
2. **Round 1:** Cold Breath (reposition to catch max targets)
3. **Breath unavailable:** Frightful Presence + Claw/Claw/Bite
4. **Lair action (init 20):** Freezing Fog to blind casters, Wall of Ice to trap, or Ice Shards to soften
5. **Legendary actions:** Wing Attack if 3+ in melee (reposition after), Tail Attack for rear threats, Detect for sneakers
6. **≤40% hp:** Wing Attack to knock prone → fly away
