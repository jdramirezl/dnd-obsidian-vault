# Meenlock Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/meenlock-tactics/) | **Date**: May 30, 2017

---

## Overview

- Unseelie fey: deformed, sadistic, dark-dwelling predators. Small, not strong — rely on DEX-based shock attacks, psychic terror, and paralyzing touch. May hunt in groups.
- Goal: **abduct and torture** victims to turn them into new meenlocks, not kill.

## Light Preferences

- **Light Sensitivity**: disadvantage on attacks/Perception in bright light → shun daylight.
- **Fear Aura** requires prey to see them → total darkness not ideal (unless prey has darkvision).
- Most active at **twilight**; drawn to dim light of torches/campfires.

## Shadow Teleport

- Recharging (~1 turn in 3). Both origin and destination must be dim light or darkness.
- Better as **ambush tactic** than escape — not reliable enough for latter. Bonus action → teleport first, then attack.

## Attack Pattern (Solo)

1. Dim light → Shadow Teleport to isolated victim → claw attacks until:
   - **Paralyzed** → stop attacking, drag away at full 30 ft (paralyzed can't resist). Subsequent turns: Dash.
   - Killed → mission failed (wanted to capture).
   - Shadow Teleport recharges → give up and teleport away (may retry if not wounded).
2. If paralysis wears off during drag → resume claws. If wounded and teleport recharged → flee.

## Group Tactics

- More aggressive, less skittish. Stay until seriously wounded (≤12 hp).
- Focus on capturing at least **one** victim. Coordinate: two run interference, two paralyze and drag.

## Target Selection

- Mostly indiscriminate — pick most **physically isolated** person. Favor smaller targets. **Avoid dwarves** (resist paralysis).

## Retreat

- Single meenlock flees at ≤12 hp regardless.
