# Yagnoloth Tactics

## Tactical Summary

CR 11 yugoloth. Daemonic contract lawyers and commanders of lower-level yugoloths. Large creature with asymmetrical arms (one Medium, one Huge). No real weakness — nadir is DEX, still well above humanoid average. STR + CON highest (brute), but exceptional CHA and very high INT. Casts spells from the front line.

Equally potent in social interaction as combat: Deception, Persuasion, Insight modifiers equal to attack bonuses. Uses *suggestion* (at will) shamelessly to compel unfavorable contract terms. Standard yugoloth immunities/resistances. Magic Resistance. Blindsight + darkvision. Telepathy (Abyssal/Infernal).

## Attack Options (Strongest to Weakest)

### 1. Lightning Bolt vs 3 Targets
~63 total expected damage. Save 3/day uses for this alignment. Only use vs 2 targets (~42 dmg) when moderately wounded (≤102 hp) and no better option.

### 2. Life Leech
36 damage + 18 temp HP = 54 hp swing. Target must be incapacitated. Stun from Massive Arm creates this condition. Nearly always preceded by a Massive Arm hit.

### 3. Multiattack A: Massive Arm + Electrified Touch
~50 damage if both hit + chance to stun. At least one target must be adjacent. Same target: Massive Arm first (stun grants advantage on follow-up Electrified Touch).

### 4. Battlefield Cunning
Forgoes attack; two allied yugoloths attack instead. Only useful when: no targets in Massive Arm + Electrified Touch reach, Life Leech unavailable, not recharging. Best allies: canoloths (highest single-attack damage among lower loths, plus they shut down teleportation within 60 ft).

### 5. Multiattack B: Massive Arm + Teleport
~24 damage + stun chance. Allows 60 ft relocation before or after attack. Rare combo (Teleport included in Multiattack). Preferred over *lightning bolt* vs 2 targets because Multiattack isn't limited-use.

## Canoloth Synergy

Best sidekicks despite not quite matching Battlefield Cunning's damage threshold. Two uses:
- Move within melee reach of enemies >15 ft from yagnoloth → use reaction Bite for extra damage
- Stay by yagnoloth's side → Tongue attack to yoink enemies from 20-30 ft into yagnoloth's reach, grapple + restrain for advantage

## Spell Analysis

- **Darkness** — overwhelmingly advantageous if enemies can't walk out of the murksphere (confined areas)
- **Lightning bolt** (3/day) — save for 3-target alignments; 2-target only when desperate
- **Dispel magic** — innate (can't boost past base level). Against 4th+ level spells, needs ally Help action for advantage on the check. Worth dispelling: *circle of power, greater invisibility, confusion, hold monster, holy weapon, globe of invulnerability*
- **Detect magic** — assume always on during negotiations. Drops it to try *suggestion* once confirmed no arcane tricks
- **Suggestion** (at will) — primary social weapon

## Target Selection

Good problem-solver. Strikes at the point where the *group* is most vulnerable, not necessarily the weakest individual. Looks for the linchpin: strongest fighter, most powerful caster, most selfless healer, buff bard, protective paladin. Distinguishes deed from doer — if the bard's buff is the problem but the bard is otherwise weak, *dispel magic* the buff and move on.

## Social Behavior

- Prefers talking over fighting. Much easier for a clever, charismatic fiend
- WIS 15: won't get taunted into a fight
- If taunters are weaker (Deadly encounter): taste of Massive Arm to teach humility
- If taunters are stronger (Easy/Medium/Hard): won't take the bait
- Fights only in defense of itself or yugoloths under its command

## Retreat Conditions

- **Off Gehenna/Hades:** No reason to retreat (essence re-forms)
- **On home plane, moderately wounded (≤102 hp):** Mood to parley — but has read *Getting to Yes* cover to cover. Exceptional dirty trickster
- **On home plane, seriously wounded (≤58 hp):** Surrenders on the most favorable terms it can get

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Yagnoloths are contract lawyers first, fighters second. The encounter should start as a negotiation with *detect magic* running and *suggestion* ready. Combat only happens when talks break down. Pair with 2 canoloths for the best tactical synergy. The Massive Arm → Life Leech combo is devastating — stun then drain.
