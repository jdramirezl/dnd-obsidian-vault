# Grung Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/grung-tactics/) | **Date**: March 29, 2017
> **Book**: Volo's Guide to Monsters

---

## Overview

- **Poison arrow frog people**. Arboreal, tribal, territorial. Low-Str, high-Dex, high-Con skirmishers.
- Always encountered in **large numbers** (≥6). Need 3:1 advantage to initiate.
- Amphibious + Standing Leap. Climb + Stealth proficiency → hide in trees, drop from above.
- Average Int/Wis — adapt tactics, know when beaten.

## Combat Sequence (Ordinary Grung)

1. **Green warriors hide in trees**, scouting. Attack at 3:1+ odds.
2. **Turn 1**: Leap down, attack from hiding (advantage) with daggers.
3. **Turn 2**: If hit lightly (≤6 hp damage) → leap away, Hide, prepare to ambush again. If hit hard (≥7 hp) → Dash home. If unhit → attack again.
4. **Turn 3**: If still 2:1+ odds, half **grapple** targets while half **Help** (giving advantage).
5. **Turn 4+**: Successful grapplers haul victims away. Failed grapplers keep trying. Give up after 2 consecutive failures → back to daggers.

> [!note] Poisonous Skin
> Applies when grungs grapple others too (not just when grappled). Successful grapple → DC 12 Con save vs poison. Aids kidnapping efforts.

## Goal: Capture, Not Kill

- Grungs want **slaves**. Two+ grungs cooperate to carry unconscious victims at 12 ft/turn (terrain penalty only).
- Green grung poison limits movement to climbing and standing jumps — once poisoned, grungs stop attacking and take captive.

## Retreat

- Flee when outnumbered less than 2:1, or when majority are seriously injured (≤4 hp) or killed.

## Grung Wildling (Red)

- **Killer combo**: *Plant growth* (Turn 1, readied) → *spike growth* (Turn 2, if 4+ enemies in AoE).
- *Plant growth* slows movement ×4 but doesn't affect grungs (they jump over it).
- *Spike growth* damages anything moving through it. Combined = devastating.
- If enemies too spread for *spike growth*: *barkskin* instead (can't sustain both).
- Turn 3+: Shortbow support. Uses *jump* (75 ft leap distance, 1 min) to escape. *Cure wounds* only for elite warriors.

## Grung Elite Warrior (Orange)

- **Mesmerizing Chirr**: Stuns non-grungs in earshot on failed save. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves, attacks have advantage vs stunned.
  - If spotted before ambush: Chirr immediately to regain advantage.
  - Otherwise: Chirr on Turn 2, then again on recharge.
- Doesn't leap away unless seriously wounded (≤19 hp).
- Calls retreat → **Disengages** as rearguard. Leaps away only after all allies are safe.
- Orange poison: target **frightened of own allies**. Disadvantage on ALL attack rolls while any ally is visible.
- **Key location guards**: Don't kidnap. Stand at distance and pincushion intruders with poisoned arrows.
