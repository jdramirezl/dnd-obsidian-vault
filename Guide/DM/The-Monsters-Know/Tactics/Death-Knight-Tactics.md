# Death Knight Tactics

## Tactical Summary

A corrupted paladin raised as undead, driven by compulsion to *punish*. Brute stats (extraordinary STR/CON) with exceptional CHA and 19th-level paladin spellcasting. Marshal Undead protects up to 12 undead followers from turning — it is a *leader* of undead and should never be encountered alone. Fights on the front line, targeting specific transgressors with relentless melee and devastating spells.

## Combat Behavior

### Composition
- Always accompanied by up to **12 undead followers** (ghouls, ghasts, mummies, wights, wraiths)
- Not skeletons/zombies (too weak) or vampires/liches (too independent)
- Minions fan out to engage non-front-line PCs per their usual heuristics, modified by the death knight's commands

### Core Combo
- *Hold person* → paralyze melee opponents → longsword Multiattack
- Against paralyzed targets within 5 ft.: all hits are **auto-crits** — potential ~168 damage/round (3 attacks)

### Spell Priority (19th-level Paladin)

| Slot | Spell | When |
|---|---|---|
| 5th | *Destructive wave* (×2) | 3+ enemies in 30 ft. semicircle |
| 5th½ | **Hellfire Orb** (not a spell) | 4+ enemies clustered AND 2+ have infuriated it |
| 4th | *Banishment* | Single non-target interferer (good vs. wizards; bad vs. CHA casters) |
| 3rd–4th | *Hold person* (upcast) | Paralyze 2–3 melee opponents |
| 3rd | *Dispel magic* | Only vs. CON-save spells, anti-undead effects, or fire/necrotic protection |
| 1st | *Compelled duel* | Force the "destined" PC into single combat |
| 1st | *Command* | Substitute for *compelled duel* when concentrating on something else |
| — | *Staggering smite* | Only when no future need for concentration spells |
| — | *Elemental weapon* | Almost never (costs action + concentration) |

### Hellfire Orb Conditions (Both Required)
1. 4+ enemies within 20 ft. radius
2. 2+ of those have infuriated it: dealt 54+ damage, inflicted radiant damage, called it by its former name, or showed cheeky defiance

### Parry Reaction
- Uses vs. melee attack rolls of 20–25
- Saves Parry for weapons that deal extra damage to undead or non-necrotic/poison elemental effects

## Retreat Conditions

- **Never retreats.** May stand down once all transgressors are dead — survivors must flee.

> [!tip] DM Tips
> - Decide the death knight's grudge before the session
> - Pre-plan minion composition and target selection
> - Hellfire Orb bypasses *counterspell* (not a spell) and can combo with bonus-action smites
> - Highest-level caster in the Monster Manual (19th)
> - *Hold person* → auto-crit Multiattack is the kill combo — telegraph the danger
