# Troll Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Troll Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/troll-tactics/)

**Bestiary:** [[Troll]]

---

## Overview

The vanilla troll is a straightforward brute. But the **Loathsome Limbs variant** makes trolls vastly more interesting.

## Ability Profile

- **Exceptionally high Strength, extraordinarily high Constitution**, low-to-middling mental abilities
- Darkvision — prefers night or underground; operates more by smell than sight
- **Regeneration** — recovers HP unless struck by fire or acid

## Loathsome Limbs Variant (Recommended)

Severed limbs and head keep fighting independently. Troll can reattach them.

### Severing
- Requires **15+ hp of slashing damage** in one hit
- 50% chance something happens; then: 40% leg, 40% arm, 20% head

### Severed Part Behavior
- **Leg** — 5 ft/round movement, no attack; moves back toward body
- **Arm** — 5 ft/round movement, can claw; attacks if troll sees both arm and target, otherwise moves back
- **Head** — cannot move, can bite; troll moves toward it

### Reattachment
- Pick up and reattach one part as **bonus action** (same square/hex)

## Fire/Acid Response

| Wound Level | HP | Reaction |
|---|---|---|
| Lightly wounded | ≥59 | **Provoked** — focuses on fire/acid source |
| Moderately wounded | 34–58 | **Recoils** — repositions away from source |
| Seriously wounded | ≤33 | **Flees** — Dash at top speed |

- Body does NOT react to fire/acid hitting its severed parts
- If body is hit, severed limbs returning still keep coming
- If head is being burned, body stops moving toward it

## PCs Carrying Off Limbs

Limbs **fight back** (treat as grappled):
- Leg: resists via Athletics
- Arm: claws at holder or struggles free
- Head: bites whoever's holding it
