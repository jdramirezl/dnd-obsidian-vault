# Eyedrake and Elder Brain Dragon Tactics

## Eyedrake

### Tactical Summary

Quasi-beholder born from jealousy of dragons. Very high STR/CON/CHA. Ambush attacker (expertise Perception + Stealth), sluggish 30 ft flight. Prefers total darkness.

### Combat Behavior

- **Antimagic Breath (recharge 6):** Wait for 3+ targets AND at least one buffed by a 1st–3rd level spell. Round 1 only if both conditions met; Round 2 if conditions met OR ≤83 hp; Round 3 use regardless. Also dispels *darkness* on objects or *light* in dark environments
- **Eye Rays (random, choose targets):** Freezing → finesse/fast; Debilitating → strong action economy; Repulsion/Paralyzing → DEX rogues/monks; Fire/Death → tough melee
- Smart enough to stay out of Extra Attack reach

### Retreat Conditions

Fights to the death — compulsive jealousy overrides survival instinct.

## Elder Brain Dragon

### Tactical Summary

Dragon puppeted by elder brain. CR 22 brute, colossal STR, extraordinary INT/CHA. Full dragon speed + hover. Immune to psychic/charm/fright. Blindsight, telepathy, Siege Monster.

### Combat Behavior

- **Tadpole Brine Breath (recharge 5–6):** Use whenever available. 120 ft × 15 ft line. Floor: 4 targets. Prioritize squishy/wounded. Infests ALL targets (even on save) with tadpoles — 3 successful saves, *remove curse*, or 40+ hp healing to purge. 0 hp while infested = mind flayer transformation
- **Multiattack:** Claw/Claw/Bite. Claw has 10 ft reach — stay airborne, immune to most OAs
- **Tentacle (15 ft):** Grapples on hit. Set up Shatter Concentration
- **Shatter Concentration (2 legendary actions):** Auto-breaks concentration on grappled targets. If nothing to break, spend on extra Tentacle attacks instead

### Retreat Conditions

Withdraws at ≤140 hp. Disengages or Dashes. Allies cover retreat — too valuable to lose.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Eyedrake: mid-tier nuisance, fight in total darkness. Elder brain dragon: campaign-defining threat. Telegraph with mind flayer activity. The tadpole infestation creates urgency even after the fight ends.
