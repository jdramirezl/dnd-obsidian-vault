# Orc Tactics

> [!info] Source
> [The Monsters Know — Orc Tactics](https://www.themonstersknow.com/orc-tactics/)
> **Bestiary**: [[Orcs]]

Strong, tough brutes. Not smart but average Wisdom, decent Dex. Aggressive feature: move full speed toward hostile creature as bonus action (effectively Dash + Attack). Intimidation +2. Greataxe can be deadly to level 1 characters.

## Basic Orcs

**Parley**: First contact at 30–60 ft. Initially hostile. Brief parley of demands/threats/taunts. Smooth talker can attempt Persuasion (disadvantage, DC 15–20) to shift hostile → indifferent. Deception (disadvantage, opposed by Int/Wis) to bluff past. Intimidation (DC 20, opposed by Wis) to threaten back.

**Combat**: Aggressive (bonus) to charge + Attack (action) with greataxe. Slugfest from there. If front-line PC falls and another is 30–60 ft back → Aggressive again to reach archers/casters.

**Retreat (≤6 hp)**: Disengage + retreat, unless fellows have been slain (then fight to death). Orcs seeing fellows retreat successfully are more willing to retreat themselves.

**Flanked by 2+ enemies**: Reposition (move, risking OAs) + Attack. Won't Dodge or Disengage to reposition — too un-orc-like. If can't evade extra attackers → Disengage + retreat or fight to death.

**Retreated orcs** will seek payback when PCs seem weakened.

## Orogs

Stronger, tougher, smarter. Multiattack. Will Disengage before repositioning. Group with orogs waits until nightfall (darkvision advantage). Same "death before dishonor" attitude when low on hp.

## Orc War Chief

Extremely formidable. Gruumsh's Fury (+1d8 weapon damage, passive). Battle Cry (1/day, action): allies get advantage on attacks for next turn + war chief gets one bonus-action attack.

**Battle Cry math**: Worth it with ≥10 orcs (use before charge). With 5–9 orcs, use after charge (still gets bonus-action attack). Fewer than 5 → just Attack.

## Orc Eye of Gruumsh

Battlefield cleric. Smarter/wiser but not stronger. Gruumsh's Fury + spellcasting.

**Combat sequence**:
1. Charge with other orcs (Aggressive)
2. R2: *Spiritual weapon* (bonus) + Attack (action)
3. R3: *Bless* (action, on self + up to 2 leaders) + *spiritual weapon* (bonus)
4. Subsequent: Attack (action) + *spiritual weapon* (bonus)

**Command**: Cast on enemy engaged by 4+ orcs (or 2+ with leader). Order "Scram!" to provoke OAs from every orc in reach. Only worth forgoing own Attack when enough allies can capitalize.
