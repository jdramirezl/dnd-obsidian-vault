## Overview

- A **nilbog** is a goblin possessed by a trickster spirit — "goblin" spelled backwards.
- The spirit of nilbogism inverts normal expectations: healing damages it, damage heals it.
- **Nilbog's Reversal** (reaction): When damaged, can reduce damage to 0 and regain hp instead.
- **Nilbogism** aura: Creatures that try to attack must succeed on a Cha save or be charmed into praising the nilbog instead.
- Innate spellcasting: *Tasha's hideous laughter*, *vicious mockery*.

## Tactical Notes

- The nilbog is a **chaos agent** among goblins — it disrupts both allies and enemies.
- It doesn't fight conventionally. Its goal is to **sow confusion and disorder**.
- Uses *vicious mockery* to impose disadvantage on the most dangerous attacker.
- Uses *Tasha's hideous laughter* to incapacitate a key target (Wis save).
- Relies on Nilbogism to discourage attacks — PCs who fail the Cha save waste their action praising it.
- Stays near other goblins for protection. The goblins revere and fear the nilbog.
- If the trickster spirit is driven out (nilbog reduced to 0 hp), it possesses another goblin within 30 ft.
