# Planescape Faction Agent Tactics, Part 2

## Harmonium Peacekeeper (CR 3)

### Tactical Summary

Heavily armored, very high STR, high CON + WIS. Pack Tactics — always work in squads of 6-12, divided into teams of 2-4. Lawful alignment determines lethality.

### Combat Behavior

- **Electrified Mancatcher:** Grapples + restrains on hit, deals ongoing damage, but takes the peacekeeper out of play while holding a target
- If outnumbered: call for backup immediately, wait to engage
- Maintain tight formation for Pack Tactics advantage
- A lone peacekeeper never breaks from the group — if one breaks off, a second goes too

### Stun vs Kill (by alignment)

- **Lawful Good:** Nonlethal unless vs chaotic evil
- **Lawful Neutral:** Lethal vs CE, NE, or CN
- **Lawful Evil:** Always lethal

### Retreat Conditions

- Seriously wounded (≤18 hp) — unless opposition is a clear danger to law and order
- If half the team is seriously wounded: less wounded delay, more wounded withdraw
- Last one standing: withdraws. Disengage vs 2+ melee foes, Dodge otherwise. Dash only vs CR 12+

## Harmonium Captain (CR 8)

### Tactical Summary

Even better armored. Extraordinary STR, very high WIS + CHA, high CON. Fearless front-line leader. No Pack Tactics — free to go where needed. Multiattack: 3x Harmonium Blade + 1x Dictate (recharge).

### Combat Behavior

- **Alignment determines opening:**
  - LG: Dictate only first, targeting most chaotic foes. Wait to see if it works before drawing steel
  - LN: Multiattack round 1 — Dictate the most chaotic, attack those who save
  - LE: Same as LN, but may attack charmed foes or uninvolved opponents anyway
- Stun/kill rules same as peacekeepers, but captain's alignment overrides subordinates'
- Positions to provide Pack Tactics advantage to peacekeepers when not needed elsewhere
- Takes on troublesome archers/spellcasters personally

## Transcendent Order Instinct (CR 3)

### Tactical Summary

Very high DEX + WIS. Shock attacker, no ranged attack. Deflect Blow reaction (3x/round). Tougher than they look.

### Combat Behavior

- Run up and Multiattack. Limited options
- Choose battles wisely — only engage with some advantage (foes AC ≤13, or conditions granting advantage)
- Use Acrobatics in 3-D environments to go where opponents can't follow
- After 2 rounds: move out of reach, provoke OA (soften with Deflect Blow), force opponents to waste turns

### Retreat Conditions

- Neutral good: attack to knock out, not kill (unless fighting evil creatures causing harm)
- Fight not won in 3 rounds + moderately wounded (≤34 hp) + no mayhem: withdraw
- If mayhem: fight until seriously wounded (≤19 hp)

## Transcendent Order Conduit (CR 8)

### Tactical Summary

Enhanced instinct. Unarmed Strikes with riders: Push (10 ft, no OA) and Incapacitate.

### Combat Behavior

- **Push:** Default choice — always works. In 3-D environments = "off a rooftop." Also repositions enemies
- **Incapacitate:** Only vs frail-looking targets (low CON). Sure thing first, then try Incapacitate
- **Don't Be There (reaction):** Situational, reflexive — not strategic
- Moderately wounded: ≤67 hp. Seriously wounded: ≤38 hp

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Harmonium encounters scale with alignment — a LG captain plays very differently from a LE one. Transcendent Order agents are best in vertical, complex environments where their Acrobatics shine.
