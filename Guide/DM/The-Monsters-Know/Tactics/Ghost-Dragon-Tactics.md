# Ghost Dragon Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/ghost-dragon-tactics/) | **Date**: October 27, 2022

---

## Tactical Summary

- **CR 17 incorporeal undead dragon** with unfinished business — refuses to let go of its hoard. No legendary or lair actions despite being as challenging as an adult dragon.
- **Brute**: Extraordinary Str/Con/Cha, high Int/Wis. Expertise in Stealth — ambush attacker.
- **Resistant to physical damage — even from magic weapons**. Immune to acid, cold, necrotic, poison + many conditions.
- **Oddly low AC** (no natural armor due to incorporeality) but ridiculous HP pool + damage resistances compensate.
- **Bite**: 23% more damage than adult red dragon's Bite. Also halves target's speed on hit.
- **Terrifying Breath** (recharge 6): 90-ft cone (ancient dragon size), cold damage + frightened + **paralyzed** on failed save.

## Combat Behavior

**Opening**: Hide (Stealth expertise), attack from hiding for unseen attacker advantage on first Bite. THEN reposition and use Terrifying Breath to catch all targets in cone.

**After Terrifying Breath**: Focus attacks on paralyzed targets most likely to shake it off first (least frail-looking). Window is narrow.

**Threat priority**: Clerics/paladins (radiant damage, turning risk) > druids (elemental damage) > sorcerers (higher Con than other casters). Vulnerable damage types: fire, lightning, thunder, radiant, force.

**Between turns**: If target has Extra Attack/Multiattack, withdraws out of reach — happy to chance one OA to avoid 2–4 hits.

**Terrifying Breath recharge**: If regained mid-combat, holds it until most dangerous foes shake off fear/paralysis, then re-applies.

**Preferred targets**: Picks on those least equipped to fight back in melee. Loves melee against foes who hate melee.

## Retreat Conditions

- **Never flees**. Undead compulsion to remain with hoard. Already died once — not afraid of dying again.

## Negotiation

- Can potentially be mollified by sincerely promising to place its most treasured item in a specially protected location while acknowledging the ghost dragon's continued ownership. You get the item's use + a spectral guardian.

## DM Tips

> [!tip] Running This Monster
> The paralyzed condition from Terrifying Breath is the real killer — worst status in 5E short of unconscious/dead. The "resistant to magic weapons" is easy to miss and brutal. The half-speed Bite creates a nightmare chase feel. Don't waste the ambush opener — Stealth expertise exists for a reason.
