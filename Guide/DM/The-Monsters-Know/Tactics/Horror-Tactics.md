# Horror Tactics

> **Source**: [Original Article](https://www.themonstersknow.com/horror-tactics/) | **Date**: March 6, 2026

---

## Overview

- Barely sentient living nightmares pressed into service by Ravnican guilds (especially House Dimir). Three varieties: flying, shadow, skittering.
- All have 120-ft darkvision and Sunlight Sensitivity → **active exclusively at night**. All abhor radiant damage.

## Flying Horror (Tier 1)

- Extraordinary DEX → shock attacker. High AC for CR but low hp, no damage resistance.
- **Round 1**: Stealth to within 90 ft, then 60-ft fly to close. **Frightening Screech** (stays airborne, out of melee). Low DC — relies on imposing disadvantage via frightened.
- **Rounds 2–3**: Swoops, strikes with Claws (avg 22 dmg — downs level 3–4 PCs in two rounds).
- **Round 4**: Departs, Dashing while flying.
- **Driven off by**: ≤19 hp, any radiant damage, or downing target in round 2 then taking ≥15 damage before round 3.

## Shadow Horror (Tier 2)

- CHA primary, then WIS and DEX. Large. Prefers ranged necrotic damage. Consummate ambush attacker (Perception proficiency, Stealth expertise).
- **Shadow Stealth**: Hide as bonus action in dim light/darkness. **Shadow Stride**: Teleport between shadows within 60 ft (bonus action, no line of sight needed). **Incorporeal Movement**: Pass through solid objects.
- Often accompanied by flying horrors (Lashing Shadows excludes "other horrors").
- **Sequence**: Beeline toward target (walls can't stop it) → within 60 ft → **Lashing Shadows** (necrotic, recharge 5–6) → head for nearest shadow, stay put. If Lashing Shadows recharges, use again. Otherwise Shadow Stride to target → Multiattack.
- **Retreat**: ≤48 hp or ≥12 radiant damage → through nearest wall, find dim light, Hide, keep moving in shadows.

## Skittering Horror (Tier 3)

- Extraordinary STR, elephant-sized. Spider Climb + 40-ft climb speed. Enters through windows/ceilings, positions above target.
- **Multiattack**: Maddening Presence first, then Bite + Claws (10-ft reach — attacks from ceiling). Avg 76 dmg/round → downs most PCs ≤level 8 in one round, ≤level 16 in two.
- Confines melee to assigned target. Uses Maddening Presence on helpers. Stays on ceiling; drops for auto-crits vs paralyzed targets.
- **Driven off by**: ≤91 hp or ≥23 radiant damage. Otherwise leaves when work is done.
