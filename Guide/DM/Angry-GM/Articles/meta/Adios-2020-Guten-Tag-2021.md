# Adios 2020, Guten Tag 2021

## Summary

Published December 31, 2020. Angry's year-end reflection on 2020 — both personally and professionally. He defends the value of New Year's resolutions as conscious choices to take responsibility for your own life, contrasting his own struggles (letting circumstances drag him down for months) with The Tiny GM's resilience through job changes and disruptions. Professionally, he notes significant growth in the back half of 2020: a new drafting/revision/editing process that improved his writing quality and release consistency, and finally finishing the long-delayed adventure module (sent to editor). He shares his gaming resolutions for 2021, including plans for AngryCraft and continued site content. The core message: resolutions aren't magic, but they're the start of a promise to yourself, and whether you keep that promise depends entirely on you.

> [!tip] Context
> A personal/professional update article. Useful for understanding the timeline of Angry's products and his evolving writing process, but not a GM advice piece.
