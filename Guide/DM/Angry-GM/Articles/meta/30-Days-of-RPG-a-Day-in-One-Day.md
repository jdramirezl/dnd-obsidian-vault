# 30 Days of RPG a Day in One Day

## Summary

Published August 31, 2017. Angry tackles the entire #RPGaDay challenge — 31 daily questions about RPGs meant to celebrate the hobby — in a single article on the last day of August. Rather than a structured advice piece, this is a fun, unfiltered personal article where Angry shares his gaming preferences, favorite memories, cherished RPGs, and opinions on the hobby. He discusses everything from his favorite games (D&D 5E, Alternity) to RPG cover art, what he'd change about the industry, and his dream of a relic-hunters RPG. It's a rare, casual look at Angry as a gamer rather than as a GM advice-giver.

> [!tip] Context
> This is a personal/community participation article, not a GM advice column. Useful for understanding Angry's gaming tastes and philosophy in a more relaxed format.
