# Monster Building 202 Supplement: Monster Building with Angry

**Source:** [The Angry GM](https://theangrygm.com/monster-building-202-supplement-monster-building-with-angry/)
**Date:** October 22, 2015
**Series:** Custom Monster Building 101

---

A transcript of a live Skype session where Angry helps @Maialideth build a hobgoblin warcaster. A practical example of the monster building process.

---

## The Goal

Build a **hobgoblin warcaster** (wizard) to appear with a team of hobgoblins. Originally targeting CR 3, revised to **CR 2**.

---

## Step 1: Racial Trait

Every monster race has a signature trait. Hobgoblins have **Martial Advantage** — extra damage when an ally is adjacent. That doesn't work for a spellcaster.

**Solution — two new traits preserving the "militant teamwork" flavor:**

> ***Precision.*** Enemies do not benefit from cover against the hobgoblin's ranged spell attacks if that cover is due to an ally of the hobgoblin that can see and hear it.

> ***Warcaster Advantage.*** Once per turn, deal an extra 7 (2d6) damage to a creature hit with a ranged spell attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of the hobgoblin that isn't incapacitated.

---

## Step 2: Offense (Offensive CR 4)

Target: 27–32 damage per round over three rounds.

**Spell choice:** Scorching Ray (2nd level) — 3 rays × 7 (2d6) = 21 damage. Plus Warcaster Advantage on one ray = +7 (2d6). **Total: 28 damage/round** for first two rounds.

### Precision Trait Impact
- Removing cover penalty = effectively +2 attack sometimes
- Situational → treat as +1 effective attack bonus
- +1 isn't enough to bump Offensive CR (needs +2 deviation)

### Attack Modifier
- Prof +2, Int +3 → Spell Attack +5, Save DC 13

---

## Step 3: Defense (Defensive CR 1/2)

Originally targeting Def CR 2, revised to glass cannon.

**AC:** Mage Armor = 13 + Dex. With Dex +0 → **AC 13** (fine for CR 1/2).

**HP:** Con +1, average 5.5 per d8. Target ~55 → **HP 55 (10d8+10)**

---

## Step 4: Final CR

- Offensive CR 4, Defensive CR 1/2
- (4 + 0.5) / 2 = 2.25 → **CR 2**

---

## Final Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| AC | 13 (mage armor) |
| HP | 55 (10d8+10) |
| Dex | +0, Con +1, Int +3 |
| Spell Attack | +5 |
| Save DC | 13 |
| Spellcaster Level | 3rd |

**Key Spells:** Scorching Ray, Mage Armor, Shield (reaction)

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Start with the racial trait** — adapt it for different roles while preserving racial identity
2. **Set Challenge first** — don't build then see what CR you get
3. **Offense and Defense are separate knobs** — glass cannon wizard = high Off CR + low Def CR
4. **Work backwards from damage** — figure spells that produce the right output, then set ability scores
5. **Situational bonuses** are worth about half their face value for CR calculation
6. **A buddy is the best armor a wizard can wear** — lower CR lets you add another ally
