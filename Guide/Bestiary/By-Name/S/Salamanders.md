# Salamanders

![Salamanders](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/salamander.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium/Large elemental, neutral evil | **CR**: 1 / 5 | **Source**: Monster Manual p.266–267

## Salamander

> **Type**: Large elemental, neutral evil | **CR**: 5 (1,800 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.267

### Stat Block

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 90 (12d10 + 24)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 15 (+2) | 11 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 12 (+1) |

**Damage Vulnerabilities** cold
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
**Damage Immunities** fire
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages** Ignan
**Challenge** 5 (1,800 XP)

#### Traits

**Heated Body.** A creature that touches the salamander or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 7 (2d6) fire damage.

**Heated Weapons.** Any metal melee weapon the salamander wields deals an extra 3 (1d6) fire damage on a hit (included in the attack).

#### Actions

**Multiattack.** The salamander makes two attacks: one with its spear and one with its tail.

**Spear.** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage, or 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack, plus 3 (1d6) fire damage.

**Tail.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage, and the target is [[Grappled|grappled]] (escape DC 14). Until this grapple ends, the target is [[Restrained|restrained]], the salamander can automatically hit the target with its tail, and the salamander can't make tail attacks against other targets.

## Lore

**Fire Snakes.** Salamanders hatch from eggs that are two-foot-diameter spheres of smoldering obsidian. A fire snake matures into a salamander adult within a year.

**Slaves of the Efreet.** The efreet enslaved the salamanders to unleash war and destruction across the planes. Salamanders despise the azers, believing that if the efreet had succeeded in dominating that race of elemental crafters, the salamanders would still be free.

**Domineering Nobles.** Free salamanders rule their own societies according to the efreet model. As salamanders age, they increase in size and status, rising to positions of power as cruel nobles among their kind.

**Living Forges.** Salamanders generate intense heat, allowing them to soften and shape iron and steel with their bare hands. They number among the greatest metalsmiths in all the planes.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!quote] Tactical Deep Dive
> See [[Salamander-Tactics]] for detailed combat tactics from *The Monsters Know What They're Doing*.


> [!tip] Tail Grapple = Auto-Hit Death Spiral
> The salamander's tail grapple restrains AND auto-hits on subsequent turns (2d6+4 + 2d6 fire per round). Meanwhile, Heated Body deals 2d6 fire to anyone who attacks it in melee. A restrained PC takes ~25 damage/round with no attack roll needed. Prioritize breaking the grapple (DC 14).

- **What they are:** Fire elementals — fire snakes (CR 1) are juveniles, salamanders (CR 5) are adults. Both have Heated Body, cold vulnerability, fire immunity, and nonmagical weapon resistance.
- **When to use:** Elemental Plane of Fire, volcanic lairs, efreet strongholds, fire cult dungeons, or forges.
- **Key combat tactics:**
  - *Fire Snake (CR 1):* Heated Body (1d6) punishes melee. Two attacks/turn, each adding 1d6 fire. Cold vulnerability is a major weakness.
  - *Salamander (CR 5):* Heated Body deals 2d6 fire to melee attackers. Tail grapple (DC 14) [[Restrained|restrains]] and auto-hits on subsequent turns. Spear does 2d6+4 plus 1d6 fire at range.
- **Notable abilities:** The salamander's tail grapple is the real threat — a [[Restrained|restrained]] target takes automatic tail damage (2d6+4 + 2d6 fire) every turn plus Heated Body (2d6 fire) if they attack back.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Grappled|grappled]], [[Restrained|restrained]] (Salamander tail)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Salamanders with fire snakes in a volcanic forge. A salamander enslaved by an efreet. Fire snakes as fire cult temple guardians. Pair with azers for faction conflict.
