# Scarecrow

![Scarecrow](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/scarecrow.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium construct, chaotic evil | **CR**: 1 (200 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.269

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 11
**Hit Points** 36 (8d8)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 11 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 11 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) |

**Damage Vulnerabilities** fire
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** [[Charmed|charmed]], exhaustion, [[Frightened|frightened]], [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]], [[Poisoned|poisoned]], [[Unconscious|unconscious]]
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages** understands the languages of its creator but can't speak
**Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

### Traits

**False Appearance.** While the scarecrow remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from an ordinary, inanimate scarecrow.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The scarecrow makes two claw attacks.

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (2d4 + 1) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Wisdom saving throw or be [[Frightened|frightened]] until the end of the scarecrow's next turn.

**Terrifying Glare.** The scarecrow targets one creature it can see within 30 feet of it. If the target can see the scarecrow, the target must succeed on a DC 11 Wisdom saving throw or be magically [[Frightened|frightened]] until the end of the scarecrow's next turn. The [[Frightened|frightened]] target is [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]].

## Lore

**Spirit-Powered Constructs.** A scarecrow is animated by the bound spirit of a slain evil creature, granting it purpose and mobility. Hags and witches often bind scarecrows with the spirits of demons, but any evil spirit will do. If its creator dies, the spirit inhabiting a scarecrow either continues to follow its last commands, seeks revenge for its creator's death, or destroys itself.

**Construct Nature.** A scarecrow doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] Terrifying Glare Paralyzes at CR 1
> Frightened AND paralyzed on a single failed DC 11 Wis save is devastating for CR 1. Multiple scarecrows in a cornfield can lock down an entire party. Fire vulnerability is the hard counter — a single *fire bolt* or torch can turn the tide.

- **What it is:** A construct animated by a bound evil spirit. False Appearance makes it a perfect ambush predator. Terrifying Glare paralyzes through fear — punching well above CR 1.
- **When to use:** Hag lairs, cursed farmlands, haunted fields. Multiple scarecrows in a cornfield is a classic horror setup.
- **Key combat tactics:** Open with Terrifying Glare to [[Paralyzed|paralyze]] a target (DC 11 Wis), then Multiattack with two claws. Each claw hit also forces DC 11 Wis vs. [[Frightened|frightened]]. Fire vulnerability is a major weakness. False Appearance enables surprise rounds.
- **Notable abilities:** Terrifying Glare — [[Frightened|frightened]] AND [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]] on a single failed save is devastating for CR 1. Resistant to nonmagical B/P/S but vulnerable to fire.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Frightened|frightened]] (Claw, Terrifying Glare), [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]] (Terrifying Glare)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** 2–3 scarecrows in a hag's territory as sentinels. A scarecrow with a will-o'-wisp in a cursed field. Pair with a night hag as the creator.
