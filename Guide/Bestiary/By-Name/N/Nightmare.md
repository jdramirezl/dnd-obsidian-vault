# Nightmare

![Nightmare](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/nightmare.jpg)


> **Type**: Large fiend, neutral evil | **CR**: 3 (700 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.235–236

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 68 (8d10 + 24)
**Speed** 60 ft., fly 90 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 15 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 15 (+2) |

**Damage Immunities** fire
**Senses** passive Perception 11
**Languages** understands Abyssal, Common, and Infernal but can't speak
**Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

### Traits

**Confer Fire Resistance.** The nightmare can grant resistance to fire damage to anyone riding it.

**Illumination.** The nightmare sheds bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet.

### Actions

**Hooves.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage.

**Ethereal Stride.** The nightmare and up to three willing creatures within 5 feet of it magically enter the Ethereal Plane from the Material Plane, or vice versa.

## Lore

A nightmare appears in a cloud of roiling smoke, its mane, tail, and hooves wreathed in flame. The creature's unearthly black form moves with supernatural speed, vanishing in a cloud of brimstone as quickly as it appeared.

**Dread Steed.** Also called a "demon horse" or "hell horse," the nightmare serves as a steed for creatures of exceptional evil, carrying demons, devils, death knights, liches, night hags, and other vile monsters. It resembles a fiendish horse, and a nightmare's fiery red eyes betray its malevolent intelligence. A nightmare can be summoned from the Lower Planes, but unless a worthy sacrifice is offered to it as food upon its arrival, the nightmare displays no special loyalty to the creature it serves.

**Creating a Nightmare.** Nightmares don't appear naturally in the multiverse. They must be created from pegasi. The ritual that creates a nightmare requires the torturous removal of a pegasus's wings, driving that noble creature to evil as it is transformed by dark magic.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] Ethereal Stride = Unbeatable Escape
> Ethereal Stride has no save, no attack roll, and moves the nightmare + up to 3 allies to the Ethereal Plane instantly. Any villain mounted on a nightmare can escape any encounter at will. Plan for this — the party needs *banishment*, *plane shift*, or a way to block planar travel.

- **What it is:** A fiendish flaming horse that serves as a mount for powerful evil creatures. CR 3 with excellent mobility — 60 ft. speed and 90 ft. fly.
- **When to use:** As a mount for death knights, liches, night hags, or other villains. Dramatic entrance/escape vehicle. Ethereal Plane travel encounters.
- **Key combat tactics:** Hooves deal 13 bludgeoning + 7 fire per hit — solid single-attack damage. Ethereal Stride is the key ability: the nightmare can plane-shift itself and up to 3 riders/allies, enabling dramatic escapes or ambushes from the Ethereal Plane. Confer Fire Resistance protects its rider.
- **Notable abilities:** Ethereal Stride is the defining feature — no save, no attack roll, just instant planar travel for a small group. This makes nightmares nearly impossible to corner. Fire immunity and Illumination mean it's always visibly burning. Understands 3 languages but can't speak.
- **Conditions inflicted:** None
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Mounted by a death knight or wight commander. Night hag riding a nightmare during a dream sequence. A villain escaping via Ethereal Stride mid-combat. Pair with hell hounds for a fiendish cavalry theme.
