# Pseudodragon

![Pseudodragon](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/pseudodragon.jpg)


> **Type**: Tiny dragon, neutral good | **CR**: 1/4 (50 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.254–255

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 7 (2d4 + 2)
**Speed** 15 ft., fly 60 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 6 (-2) | 15 (+2) | 13 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) |

**Skills** Perception +3, Stealth +4
**Senses** blindsight 10 ft., darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** understands Common and Draconic but can't speak
**Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

### Traits

**Keen Senses.** The pseudodragon has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight, hearing, or smell.

**Magic Resistance.** The pseudodragon has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Limited Telepathy.** The pseudodragon can magically communicate simple ideas, emotions, and images telepathically with any creature within 100 feet of it that can understand a language.

### Actions

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

**Sting.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 hour. If the saving throw fails by 5 or more, the target falls [[Unconscious|unconscious]] for the same duration, or until it takes damage or another creature uses an action to shake it awake.

## Variant: Pseudodragon Familiar

Some pseudodragons are willing to serve spellcasters as a familiar. Such pseudodragons have the following trait.

**Familiar.** The pseudodragon can serve another creature as a familiar, forming a magic, telepathic bond with that willing companion. While the two are bonded, the companion can sense what the pseudodragon senses as long as they are within 1 mile of each other. While the pseudodragon is within 10 feet of its companion, the companion shares the pseudodragon's Magic Resistance trait. At any time and for any reason, the pseudodragon can end its service as a familiar, ending the telepathic bond.

## Lore

The elusive pseudodragon dwells in the quiet places of the world, making its home in the hollows of trees and small caves. With its red-brown scales, horns, and a maw filled with sharp teeth, a pseudodragon resembles a tiny red dragon but its disposition is playful.

**Quiet and Defensive.** Pseudodragons have little interest in other creatures, and they avoid them whenever possible. If attacked, a pseudodragon fights back using the poisonous stinger at the tip of its tail.

**Draconic Familiars.** Mages often seek out pseudodragons, whose agreeable disposition, telepathic ability, and resistance to magic make them superior familiars. Pseudodragons are selective when it comes to choosing companions, but they can sometimes be won over with gifts of food or treasure.

**Language of Emotion.** Pseudodragons can't speak, but they communicate using a limited form of telepathy. A rasping purr indicates pleasure, while a hiss means unpleasant surprise. A bird-like chirping represents desire, and a growl always means anger or discontent.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] Magic Resistance Sharing Is Broken Good
> As a familiar, the pseudodragon shares Magic Resistance (advantage on all spell saves) with its companion within 10 ft. This is one of the strongest familiar features in the game. A wizard with a pseudodragon familiar is significantly harder to shut down with spells.

> [!example] Pseudodragon Familiar Variant
> The Familiar trait creates a telepathic bond (1 mile range) and shares Magic Resistance within 10 ft. The pseudodragon can end the bond at any time — it's a willing partnership, not servitude. Great as a reward for a spellcaster who earns its trust through roleplay.

- **What it is:** A tiny, playful dragon with a poisonous sting and telepathy. CR 1/4 but valuable as a familiar — shares Magic Resistance within 10 ft. and provides 1-mile telepathic sensory link. The sting can knock creatures [[Unconscious|unconscious]] on a bad save.
- **When to use:** Wizard's tower, enchanted forest, Feywild border. A pseudodragon seeking a companion is a great roleplaying encounter. As a familiar, it's one of the best in the game — Magic Resistance sharing is extremely powerful.
- **Key combat tactics:** Avoids direct combat. If cornered, uses Sting to poison/knock out threats (DC 11 Con; unconscious if failed by 5+). Fly 60 ft. allows easy escape. As a familiar, it scouts via telepathic sensory link from up to 1 mile away.
- **Notable abilities:** Magic Resistance shared with companion within 10 ft. is the standout familiar feature. Limited Telepathy (100 ft.) for communication. Keen Senses makes it an excellent scout. Sting's unconscious rider can take enemies out of a fight entirely.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Poisoned|poisoned]] (Sting, DC 11 Con, 1 hour), [[Unconscious|unconscious]] (Sting, if save fails by 5+)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** A pseudodragon nesting in a wizard's abandoned tower, potential familiar for a party spellcaster. A pseudodragon being hunted by a mage who wants to force it into service. Pair with other small fey creatures in a whimsical forest encounter.
