# Oozes

![Oozes](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/gelatinous-cube.jpg)


> **Type**: Ooze, unaligned | **CR**: 1/2 / 2 / 4 | **Source**: Monster Manual p.240–243

Oozes thrive in the dark, shunning areas of bright light and extreme temperatures. They flow through the damp underground, feeding on any creature or object that can be dissolved.

---

## Black Pudding

> **Type**: Large ooze, unaligned | **CR**: 4 (1,100 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.241

### Stat Block

**Armor Class** 7
**Hit Points** 85 (10d10 + 30)
**Speed** 20 ft., climb 20 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 16 (+3) | 5 (-3) | 16 (+3) | 1 (-5) | 6 (-2) | 1 (-5) |

**Damage Immunities** acid, cold, lightning, slashing
**Condition Immunities** [[Blinded|blinded]], [[Charmed|charmed]], deafened, exhaustion, [[Frightened|frightened]], [[Prone|prone]]
**Senses** blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 8
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP)

#### Traits

**Amorphous.** The pudding can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

**Corrosive Form.** A creature that touches the pudding or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 4 (1d8) acid damage. Any nonmagical weapon made of metal or wood that hits the pudding corrodes. After dealing damage, the weapon takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to damage rolls. If its penalty drops to −5, the weapon is destroyed. Nonmagical ammunition made of metal or wood that hits the pudding is destroyed after dealing damage. The pudding can eat through 2-inch-thick, nonmagical wood or metal in 1 round.

**Spider Climb.** The pudding can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

#### Actions

**Pseudopod.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage plus 18 (4d8) acid damage. In addition, nonmagical armor worn by the target is partly dissolved and takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to the AC it offers. The armor is destroyed if the penalty reduces its AC to 10.

#### Reactions

**Split.** When a pudding that is Medium or larger is subjected to lightning or slashing damage, it splits into two new puddings if it has at least 10 hit points. Each new pudding has hit points equal to half the original pudding's, rounded down. New puddings are one size smaller than the original pudding.

## Gelatinous Cube

> **Type**: Large ooze, unaligned | **CR**: 2 (450 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.242

### Stat Block

**Armor Class** 6
**Hit Points** 84 (8d10 + 40)
**Speed** 15 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 3 (-4) | 20 (+5) | 1 (-5) | 6 (-2) | 1 (-5) |

**Condition Immunities** [[Blinded|blinded]], [[Charmed|charmed]], deafened, exhaustion, [[Frightened|frightened]], [[Prone|prone]]
**Senses** blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 8
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 2 (450 XP)

#### Traits

**Ooze Cube.** The cube takes up its entire space. Other creatures can enter the space, but a creature that does so is subjected to the cube's Engulf and has disadvantage on the saving throw. Creatures inside the cube can be seen but have total cover. A creature within 5 feet of the cube can take an action to pull a creature or object out of the cube. Doing so requires a successful DC 12 Strength check, and the creature making the attempt takes 10 (3d6) acid damage. The cube can hold only one Large creature or up to four Medium or smaller creatures inside it at a time.

**Transparent.** Even when the cube is in plain sight, it takes a successful DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) check to spot a cube that has neither moved nor attacked. A creature that tries to enter the cube's space while unaware of the cube is surprised by the cube.

#### Actions

**Pseudopod.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 10 (3d6) acid damage.

**Engulf.** The cube moves up to its speed. While doing so, it can enter Large or smaller creatures' spaces. Whenever the cube enters a creature's space, the creature must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw.

On a successful save, the creature can choose to be pushed 5 feet back or to the side of the cube. A creature that chooses not to be pushed suffers the consequences of a failed saving throw.

On a failed save, the cube enters the creature's space, and the creature takes 10 (3d6) acid damage and is engulfed. The engulfed creature can't breathe, is [[Restrained|restrained]], and takes 21 (6d6) acid damage at the start of each of the cube's turns. When the cube moves, the engulfed creature moves with it.

An engulfed creature can try to escape by taking an action to make a DC 12 Strength check. On a success, the creature escapes and enters a space of its choice within 5 feet of the cube.

>
> A psionic gray ooze has an Intelligence score of 6 (−2), as well as the following additional action:
>
> **Psychic Crush (Recharge 5–6).** The ooze targets one creature that it can sense within 60 feet of it. The target must make a DC 10 Intelligence saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

## Lore

**Slow Death.** An ooze kills its prey slowly. Some varieties, such as black puddings and gelatinous cubes, engulf creatures to prevent escape. Since not every ooze digests every type of substance, some have coins, metal gear, bones, and other debris suspended within their quivering bodies.

**Unwitting Servants.** Although an ooze lacks the intelligence to ally itself with other creatures, others that understand an ooze's need to feed might lure it into a location where it can be of use to them. Clever monsters keep oozes around to defend passageways or consume refuse. Crafty creatures place torches and flaming braziers in strategic areas to dissuade an ooze from leaving a particular tunnel or room.

**Spawn of Juiblex.** According to the *Demonomicon of Iggwilv* and other sources, oozes are scattered fragments or offspring of the demon lord Juiblex. Under the control of Juiblex, they exhibit glimmers of sentience and malevolent intent.

**Ooze Nature.** An ooze doesn't require sleep.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] Split Is the Trap — Warn Your Players Subtly
> Black Puddings and Ochre Jellies split into two when hit with slashing or lightning damage. A fighter with a longsword or a sorcerer casting *lightning bolt* will double the enemy count. Describe the ooze "quivering" when slashed to hint at this before it's too late.

> [!example] Psychic Gray Ooze Variant
> A gray ooze near mind flayers can evolve Int 6 and gain Psychic Crush (Recharge 5–6, 60 ft., DC 10 Int, 3d6 psychic). This turns a CR 1/2 nuisance into a ranged threat that pairs perfectly with Underdark psionic encounters.

- **What they are:** Mindless dungeon hazards that dissolve organic matter and corrode equipment. Four varieties from CR 1/2 to CR 4, each with distinct mechanics. The quintessential "the dungeon itself is trying to kill you" monsters.
- **When to use:** Dungeons, sewers, caves, underground passages. An immaculately clean corridor is a classic sign of ooze presence. Use them as environmental hazards as much as combat encounters.
- **Key combat tactics:**
  - *Black Pudding (CR 4):* The most dangerous. Corrosive Form punishes melee attackers (1d8 acid on touch). Pseudopod deals 6 + 18 acid AND corrodes armor. Immune to acid, cold, lightning, slashing. Split reaction means slashing/lightning damage creates MORE puddings. Spider Climb lets it drop from ceilings.
  - *Gelatinous Cube (CR 2):* Transparent (DC 15 Perception to spot). Engulf is the signature move — DC 12 Dex or be [[Restrained|restrained]] inside, taking 21 (6d6) acid per turn. 84 HP and 20 Con make it surprisingly tanky. Fills its entire space — perfect for 10-ft. corridors.
  - *Gray Ooze (CR 1/2):* False Appearance disguises it as a pool or wet rock. Corrode Metal destroys nonmagical metal weapons that hit it. Low threat alone but nasty for equipment. Psychic variant adds ranged psionic attack.
  - *Ochre Jelly (CR 2):* Immune to lightning and slashing. Split reaction (same as Black Pudding). Spider Climb. Less damaging than Black Pudding but still splits when hit wrong.
- **Notable abilities:** Split (Black Pudding & Ochre Jelly) is the trap — players who use slashing weapons or lightning spells create more enemies. Corrosive Form / Corrode Metal destroy equipment permanently. Transparent (Gelatinous Cube) enables surprise. All oozes are blind beyond blindsight 60 ft.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Restrained|restrained]] (Gelatinous Cube Engulf)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Gelatinous cube in a 10-ft. corridor — classic. Black pudding on a ceiling above a treasure pile. Gray ooze disguised as a puddle near a mind flayer lair (psychic variant). Ochre jelly in a sewer system. Multiple ooze types in a Juiblex-themed dungeon.
