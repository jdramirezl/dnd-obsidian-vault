# Oni

![Oni](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/oni.jpg)


> **Type**: Large giant, lawful evil | **CR**: 7 (2,900 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.239–240

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 16 (chain mail)
**Hit Points** 110 (13d10 + 39)
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 19 (+4) | 11 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 15 (+2) |

**Saving Throws** Dex +3, Con +6, Wis +4, Cha +5
**Skills** Arcana +5, Deception +8, Perception +4
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14
**Languages** Common, Giant
**Challenge** 7 (2,900 XP)

### Traits

**Innate Spellcasting.** The oni's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 13). The oni can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:
- At will: *darkness*, *invisibility*
- 1/day each: *charm person*, *cone of cold*, *gaseous form*, *sleep*

**Magic Weapons.** The oni's weapon attacks are magical.

**Regeneration.** The oni regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The oni makes two attacks, either with its claws or its glaive.

**Claw (Oni Form Only).** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4) slashing damage.

**Glaive.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d10 + 4) slashing damage, or 9 (1d10 + 4) slashing damage in Small or Medium form.

**Change Shape.** The oni magically polymorphs into a Small or Medium humanoid, into a Large giant, or back into its true form. Other than its size, its statistics are the same in each form. The only equipment that is transformed is its glaive, which shrinks so that it can be wielded in humanoid form. If the oni dies, it reverts to its true form, and its glaive reverts to its normal size.

## Lore

In nursery rhymes, oni are fearsome bogeymen that haunt the nightmares of children and adults alike, yet they are very real and always hungry. They find human babies especially delicious. Oni look like demonic ogres with blue or green skin, dark hair, and a pair of short ivory horns protruding from their foreheads. Their eyes are dark with strikingly white pupils, and their teeth and claws are jet black.

**Night Haunters.** By the light of day, an oni hides its true form with magic, gaining the trust of those it intends to betray when darkness descends. These creatures can change their size as well as their shape, appearing as humanoids as they pass through towns, pretending to be travelers, woodcutters, or frontier folk. In such a form, an oni takes stock of the selection of humanoids in a settlement and devises ways to abduct and devour some of them.

**Magical Ogres.** Oni are sometimes called ogre mages because of their innate magical ability. Though they are only distantly related to true ogres, they share the ogres' habit of joining forces with other evil creatures. An oni serves a master if doing so proves lucrative or provides it with a luxurious, well-defended home. Oni covet magic, and they work for evil wizards and hags in exchange for useful magic items.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] Regeneration With No Off-Switch
> Unlike trolls, the oni's regeneration (10 HP/turn) has NO vulnerability that shuts it down. The party must burst it from above 10 HP to 0 in a single round, or it just keeps healing. Combined with at-will *invisibility* and *gaseous form* for escape, an oni is nearly impossible to corner.

- **What it is:** A shapeshifting giant that infiltrates communities in humanoid form before preying on them. CR 7 with regeneration, flight, innate spellcasting, and Change Shape — a versatile and dangerous foe.
- **When to use:** Mystery/investigation scenarios where a trusted NPC is secretly an oni. Frontier settlements with disappearing villagers. As a lieutenant for a hag coven or evil wizard. Japanese folklore-themed campaigns.
- **Key combat tactics:** Uses Change Shape and Deception +8 to infiltrate. In combat, opens with *[[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]]* (8d8 cold, DC 13 Dex) for massive AoE damage. Goes *[[Invisible|invisible]]* at will to reposition or escape. Two glaive attacks per round for 30 average damage. Regeneration (10 HP/turn) makes it very hard to wear down — the party must burst it down or it outlasts them. Flies 30 ft. to stay out of melee reach if needed.
- **Notable abilities:** Regeneration has no off-switch (unlike trolls) — it heals every turn as long as it has 1+ HP. Change Shape has no duration limit and no concentration. *[[Invisibility|Invisibility]]* at will means it can vanish and flee whenever threatened. **Gaseous form** 1/day is an emergency escape. Magic Weapons bypasses resistance.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Charmed|charmed]] (charm person), sleep ([[Unconscious|unconscious]])
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Disguised as a village elder or merchant with hired thugs. Working alongside a hag coven. Leading a band of ogres or hobgoblins. Lurking in a mountain pass with goblinoid minions.
