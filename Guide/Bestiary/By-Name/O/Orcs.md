# Orcs

![Orcs](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/orc.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium humanoid (orc), chaotic evil | **CR**: 1/2 / 2 / 4 | **Source**: Monster Manual p.244–247

Orcs are savage raiders and pillagers with stooped postures, low foreheads, and piggish faces with prominent lower canines that resemble tusks.

## Lore

**Gruumsh One-Eye.** Orcs worship Gruumsh, the mightiest of the orc deities and their creator. The orcs believe that in ancient days, the gods gathered to divide the world among their followers. Each place that Gruumsh wanted had already been claimed. Grasping his mighty spear, he laid waste to the mountains, set the forests aflame, and carved great furrows in the fields. Such was the role of the orcs — to take and destroy all that the other races would deny them.

**Hatred of Elves.** Orcs hold a particular hatred for elves. The elven god Corellon Larethian half-blinded Gruumsh with a well-placed arrow to the orc god's eye. Since then, the orcs have taken particular joy in slaughtering elves.

**Tribes like Plagues.** Orcs gather in tribes that exert their dominance and satisfy their bloodlust by plundering villages, devouring or driving off roaming herds, and slaying any humanoids that stand against them.

**Leadership and Might.** Orc tribes are mostly patriarchal. Occasionally, a powerful war chief unites scattered orc tribes into a single rampaging horde.

**Orc War Chief.** The war chief of an orc tribe is its strongest and most cunning member. Gruumsh bestows special blessings upon war chiefs who prove themselves in battle, imbuing them with slivers of his savagery.

**Orc Eye of Gruumsh.** When an orc slays an elf in Gruumsh's name and plucks out one of its own eyes, Gruumsh might grant the orc spellcasting ability and the right to call itself an Eye of Gruumsh.

**Orog.** Orogs are orcs blessed with a surprisingly keen intellect that ordinary orcs believe is a gift from the orc goddess Luthic. Stronger and smarter than typical orcs, they form their own detachments within larger orc hordes and are always at the forefront of any attack.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!quote] Tactical Deep Dive
> See [[Orc-Tactics]] for detailed combat tactics from *The Monsters Know What They're Doing*.


> [!tip] Aggressive + Battle Cry = Alpha Strike
> Every orc can move its full speed toward an enemy as a bonus action (Aggressive). When the War Chief uses Battle Cry, all nearby orcs get advantage on attacks for a round. Six orcs with advantage charging 30 ft. and attacking in one turn is devastating — plan the ambush spacing carefully.

- **What they are:** The iconic evil humanoid warband. Four stat blocks from CR 1/2 to CR 4, all sharing the Aggressive trait. Orcs are meant to be fielded in groups with a leadership hierarchy.
- **When to use:** Wilderness raids, frontier settlements under attack, orc strongholds, war campaigns. Orcs are the go-to humanoid enemy for levels 1–5.
- **Key combat tactics:**
  - *Orc (CR 1/2):* Aggressive lets it close 30 ft. as a bonus action then attack — effectively a charge every turn. Greataxe for 9 damage. Dangerous in groups of 4–8.
  - *Orc War Chief (CR 4):* Two attacks per round with Gruumsh's Fury (+1d8 per hit). Battle Cry (1/Day) gives all nearby allies advantage on attacks for a round — devastating when 6+ orcs all have advantage. 93 HP and AC 16 make it durable.
  - *Orc Eye of Gruumsh (CR 2):* 3rd-level cleric. *[[Bless|Bless]]* buffs 3 allies' attacks and saves. *[[Spiritual Weapon|Spiritual weapon]]* adds bonus action damage each turn. *[[Command|Command]]* for single-target control. Gruumsh's Fury adds 1d8 to melee. The force multiplier of an orc warband.
  - *Orog (CR 2):* AC 18 (plate) is very high for CR 2. Two greataxe attacks for 20 average damage per round. The elite shock trooper — tougher and smarter than regular orcs.
- **Notable abilities:** Aggressive (all) is the defining orc trait — bonus action move toward enemies means orcs always close to melee. Battle Cry (War Chief) is a massive force multiplier. *Bless* (Eye of Gruumsh) stacks with Battle Cry for terrifying accuracy.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Charmed|charmed]] (command)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** War Chief + Eye of Gruumsh + 6 orcs is a classic warband. Orcs with ogre muscle. Orog-led raiding party. Orcs serving a hill giant or fire giant. Orc camp with dire wolves as mounts/guards.
