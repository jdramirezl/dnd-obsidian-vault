# Tarrasque

![Tarrasque](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/tarrasque.jpg)


> **Type**: Gargantuan monstrosity (titan), unaligned | **CR**: 30 (155,000 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.286

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 25 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 676 (33d20 + 330)
**Speed** 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 30 (+10) | 11 (+0) | 30 (+10) | 3 (-4) | 11 (+0) | 11 (+0) |

**Saving Throws** Int +5, Wis +9, Cha +9
**Damage Immunities** fire, poison; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
**Condition Immunities** [[Charmed|charmed]], [[Frightened|frightened]], [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]], [[Poisoned|poisoned]]
**Senses** blindsight 120 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 30 (155,000 XP)

### Traits

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day).** If the tarrasque fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance.** The tarrasque has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Reflective Carapace.** Any time the tarrasque is targeted by a *magic missile* spell, a line spell, or a spell that requires a ranged attack roll, roll a d6. On a 1 to 5, the tarrasque is unaffected. On a 6, the tarrasque is unaffected, and the effect is reflected back at the caster as though it originated from the tarrasque, turning the caster into the target.

**Siege Monster.** The tarrasque deals double damage to objects and structures.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The tarrasque can use its Frightful Presence. It then makes five attacks: one with its bite, two with its claws, one with its horns, and one with its tail. It can use its Swallow instead of its bite.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +19 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 36 (4d12 + 10) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it is [[Grappled|grappled]] (escape DC 20). Until this grapple ends, the target is [[Restrained|restrained]], and the tarrasque can't bite another target.

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +19 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. *Hit:* 28 (4d8 + 10) slashing damage.

**Horns.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +19 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 32 (4d10 + 10) piercing damage.

**Tail.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +19 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. *Hit:* 24 (4d6 + 10) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 20 Strength saving throw or be knocked [[Prone|prone]].

**Frightful Presence.** Each creature of the tarrasque's choice within 120 feet of it and aware of it must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or become [[Frightened|frightened]] for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, with disadvantage if the tarrasque is within line of sight, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to the tarrasque's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.

**Swallow.** The tarrasque makes one bite attack against a Large or smaller creature it is grappling. If the attack hits, the target takes the bite's damage, the target is swallowed, and the grapple ends. While swallowed, the creature is [[Blinded|blinded]] and [[Restrained|restrained]], it has total cover against attacks and other effects outside the tarrasque, and it takes 56 (16d6) acid damage at the start of each of the tarrasque's turns. If the tarrasque takes 60 damage or more on a single turn from a creature inside it, the tarrasque must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution saving throw at the end of that turn or regurgitate all swallowed creatures, which fall [[Prone|prone]] in a space within 10 feet of the tarrasque. If the tarrasque dies, a swallowed creature is no longer [[Restrained|restrained]] by it and can escape from the corpse by using 30 feet of movement, exiting [[Prone|prone]].

### Legendary Actions

The tarrasque can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The tarrasque regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

**Attack.** The tarrasque makes one claw attack or tail attack.

**Move.** The tarrasque moves up to half its speed.

**Chomp (Costs 2 Actions).** The tarrasque makes one bite attack or uses its Swallow.

## Lore

The legendary tarrasque is possibly the most dreaded monster of the Material Plane. It is widely believed that only one of these creatures exists, though no one can predict where and when it will strike. A scaly biped, the tarrasque is fifty feet tall and seventy feet long, weighing hundreds of tons. It carries itself like a bird of prey, leaning forward and using its powerful lashing tail for balance. Its cavernous maw yawns wide enough to swallow all but the largest creatures, and so great is its hunger that it can devour the populations of whole towns.

**Legendary Destruction.** The destructive potential of the tarrasque is so vast that some cultures incorporate the monster into religious doctrine, weaving its sporadic appearance into stories of divine judgment and wrath. Legends tell how the tarrasque slumbers in its secret lair beneath the earth, remaining in a dormant state for decades or centuries. When it awakens in answer to some inscrutable cosmic call, it rises from the depths to obliterate everything in its path.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!warning] CR 30 — The End of the World
> 676 HP, AC 25, +19 to hit, five attacks/turn for ~148 damage, Reflective Carapace negates most spells, Legendary Resistance (3/day), and Swallow (56 acid/round). There is no RAW method to permanently kill it. This is not an encounter — it's a campaign event. Plan sessions around it.

> [!tip] Reflective Carapace Shuts Down Casters
> 5/6 chance to negate any ranged spell attack or line spell, 1/6 chance to reflect it back. *Magic missile*, *ray of frost*, *lightning bolt* — all useless or dangerous. Only AoE save spells like *meteor swarm* bypass it. Warn casters before they waste their best slots.

- **What it is:** The most powerful monster in the game. CR 30, 676 HP, AC 25, +19 to hit on all attacks, five attacks per turn dealing ~148 average damage. Immune to fire, poison, nonmagical B/P/S, and four conditions. Legendary Resistance (3/day), Magic Resistance (advantage on all spell saves), and Reflective Carapace (5/6 chance to negate ranged/line spells, 1/6 chance to reflect them). This is the "end of the world" monster.
- **When to use:** Campaign finales, apocalyptic events, divine punishment scenarios. The tarrasque is not a random encounter — it's a world-ending threat that entire armies and adventuring parties unite against. It slumbers for decades or centuries, then awakens to obliterate everything. Use it when you want the players to feel truly outmatched, or as the ultimate test of a high-level party.
- **Key combat tactics:** The tarrasque is straightforward — it charges the nearest threat and destroys it. Five attacks per turn: Bite (36 dmg + grapple for Swallow setup), 2× Claw (28 each), Horns (32), Tail (24 + prone DC 20). Frightful Presence every turn for free (DC 17 Wis, disadvantage on re-saves if it's in sight). Legendary actions add a claw/tail between turns, movement to close gaps, or Chomp (2 actions) to bite/swallow off-turn. Swallow deals 56 acid damage per turn — lethal in 2-3 rounds for most PCs.
- **Dealing with the tarrasque (for DMs planning the encounter):**
  - **Spellcasters are nearly useless.** Reflective Carapace negates *[[Magic Missile|magic missile]]*, line spells, and ranged spell attacks 5/6 of the time (and reflects them 1/6). Magic Resistance gives advantage on saves. Legendary Resistance burns 3 failed saves. AoE save spells like *meteor swarm* are the best bet — they bypass Reflective Carapace.
  - **Martial characters need magic weapons.** Immune to nonmagical B/P/S. AC 25 means even +11 attackers hit only 35% of the time.
  - **Flight is the classic counter.** 40 ft. speed, no ranged attacks, no flight. A flying party can kite it — but it has 676 HP, so this takes a very long time. The tarrasque can still use Move legendary action (20 ft.) to reposition and Frightful Presence has 120 ft. range.
  - **Swallow is the kill move.** Bite grapples (DC 20 escape) → next turn Swallow for 56 acid/round inside. A swallowed PC needs to deal 60+ damage in one turn to force regurgitation (DC 20 Con save for the tarrasque). Swallow works on Large or smaller creatures.
  - **It can't be permanently killed by RAW.** No listed method of permanent death. Some DMs rule **wish** can do it; others say it simply regenerates and slumbers again.
- **Notable abilities:** Reflective Carapace is unique to the tarrasque — no other creature reflects spells. Siege Monster (double damage to objects/structures) means it levels cities in minutes. Blindsight 120 ft. means *[[Invisibility|invisibility]]* and *[[Darkness|darkness]]* don't help. +19 to hit is the highest attack bonus in the Monster Manual.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Frightened|frightened]] (Frightful Presence, DC 17 Wis), [[Grappled|grappled]] (Bite, DC 20 escape), [[Restrained|restrained]] (Bite/Swallow), [[Prone|prone]] (Tail, DC 20 Str), [[Blinded|blinded]] (Swallow)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** The tarrasque fights alone — it IS the encounter. Run it as a multi-session event: the party must evacuate a city, rally allies, find legendary weapons, and then face it. Consider environmental hazards (collapsing buildings, fleeing crowds) to add complexity. An ancient dragon or archmage NPC ally can help the party feel the scale. If you want to be truly cruel, have cultists *trying to wake it* as the real adventure — fighting the tarrasque means you've already failed.
