# Troglodyte

![Troglodyte](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/troglodyte.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium humanoid (troglodyte), chaotic evil | **CR**: 1/4 (50 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.290

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 11 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 13 (2d8 + 4)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 6 (-2) | 10 (+0) | 6 (-2) |

**Skills** Stealth +2
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages** Troglodyte
**Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

### Traits

**Chameleon Skin.** The troglodyte has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide.

**Stench.** Any creature other than a troglodyte that starts its turn within 5 feet of the troglodyte must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be [[Poisoned|poisoned]] until the start of the creature's next turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to the stench of all troglodytes for 1 hour.

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** While in sunlight, the troglodyte has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The troglodyte makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.

## Lore

The savage, degenerate troglodytes squat in the shallow depths of the Underdark in a constant state of war against their neighbors and one another. They mark the borders of their territories with cracked bones and skulls, or with pictographs painted in blood or dung. Perhaps the most loathsome of all humanoids, troglodytes eat anything they can stomach and dwell in filth.

**Simpleminded Brutes.** Troglodytes have a simple, communal culture devoted almost entirely to procuring food. Too simple to plan more than a few days into the future, they rely on constant raids and hunting to survive. They take sadistic pleasure in hunting intelligent creatures weaker than themselves. The largest and toughest troglodytes lead the hunt and become the leaders of their tribes — but if a leader shows any weakness, other troglodytes attack and eat it in a frenzy.

**Devotees of Laogzed.** Some troglodytes venerate Laogzed, a demonic, monstrously fat toad-lizard that slumbers in the Abyss. Laogzed offers the troglodytes nothing in return except aspiration, for it is the dream of his troglodyte worshipers to become as fat, well-fed, and wearily content as he seems to be.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!quote] Tactical Deep Dive
> See [[Troglodyte-Tactics]] for detailed combat tactics from *The Monsters Know What They're Doing*.


> [!tip] Stench Is the Real Weapon
> The passive Stench aura forces DC 12 Con saves every turn for anyone in melee. Poisoned = disadvantage on attacks and ability checks. Even if troglodyte damage is low, poisoning the fighter's attacks degrades the whole party's effectiveness. Swarm them.

- **What it is:** A low-CR Underdark humanoid with three attacks per turn (unusual for CR 1/4) and a passive Stench aura that poisons nearby creatures. 13 HP, AC 11 — fragile but offensively punchy with 12 average damage per round across three attacks.
- **When to use:** Early Underdark encounters, cave systems, sewer dungeons. Troglodytes are cannon fodder that fight in large groups. Stench makes melee combat unpleasant — poisoned means disadvantage on attack rolls and [[04-Ability-Checks|ability checks]]. Sunlight Sensitivity keeps them underground.
- **Key combat tactics:** Swarm in groups of 4-8. Stench forces DC 12 Con saves on every melee PC each turn — even if the damage is low, the poisoned condition (disadvantage on attacks) degrades party effectiveness. Chameleon Skin gives advantage on Stealth for ambushes in caves. Three attacks at +4 means they occasionally land hits even against decent AC.
- **Notable abilities:** Stench is the real threat — it's passive, automatic, and forces a save every turn for anyone in melee. Three attacks at CR 1/4 is exceptional action economy. Chameleon Skin for ambush setups.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Poisoned|poisoned]] (Stench, DC 12 Con, 1 round)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** A raiding party of 6-8 troglodytes in Underdark tunnels. Troglodytes with a troglodyte champion (use a beefed-up stat block or half-dragon template). Pair with giant lizards as mounts or pets. A troglodyte lair with crude traps and a Laogzed shrine.
