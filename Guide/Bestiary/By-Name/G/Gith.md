# Gith

![Gith](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/githyanki.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium humanoid (gith) | **CR**: 2 / 3 / 6 / 8 | **Source**: Monster Manual p.158–162

The warlike githyanki and the contemplative githzerai are a sundered people — two cultures that utterly despise one another. Before there were githyanki or githzerai, these creatures were a single race enslaved by the mind flayers. A great leader named Gith arose and led the rebellion. After victory, Zerthimon challenged Gith's motives, and a rift erupted between followers of each leader.

## Lore

**Githyanki** — The githyanki plunder countless worlds from the decks of their astral vessels and the backs of red dragons. Since winning their freedom from the mind flayers, the githyanki have become ruthless conquerors under the rulership of their dread lich-queen, Vlaakith. Silver swords are priceless relics — githyanki knights will hunt down any non-githyanki that dares carry one.

**Githzerai** — Focused philosophers and austere ascetics, the githzerai pursue lives of rigid order. They willingly dwell in the heart of utter chaos in Limbo, taming the plane's chaotic elements with the power of their minds. Skilled githzerai monks that best exemplify the teachings of Zerthimon are called zerths.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Githyanki Knights (CR 8) ride red dragons and wield silver greatswords that deal 5d8+Str psychic damage. Githzerai Zerths (CR 6) have *phantasmal killer* and psychic defense. Both factions hate mind flayers — use that as a hook for alliances or conflicts.

- **What they are:** Two factions of a psionic race formerly enslaved by mind flayers. Githyanki (LE, militaristic astral raiders) and Githzerai (LN, monastic Limbo-dwellers). All have psionic innate spellcasting and psychic damage on attacks.
- **When to use:** Astral Plane adventures, mind flayer storylines, planar campaigns. Githyanki raiding parties make excellent mid-level antagonists. Githzerai monasteries are sources of wisdom and training.
- **Key combat tactics:**
  - *Githyanki Warrior (CR 3):* Two greatsword attacks dealing 2d6+2 slashing + 2d6 psychic each. *Misty step* 3/day for mobility. AC 17.
  - *Githyanki Knight (CR 8):* Silver greatsword deals 2d6+6 + 3d6 psychic (avg 23/hit). *Plane shift* and *telekinesis* 1/day. Can sever astral cords on crits. AC 18.
  - *Githzerai Monk (CR 2):* Two unarmed strikes dealing 1d8+2 + 2d8 psychic. *Shield* 3/day for AC 19. Psychic Defense adds Wis to AC.
  - *Githzerai Zerth (CR 6):* Two unarmed strikes dealing 2d6+4 + 3d8 psychic (avg 28/round). *Plane shift* and *phantasmal killer* 1/day. AC 17.
- **Notable abilities:** All attacks deal psychic damage — bypasses most resistances. Githyanki knights' silver greatswords are +3 magic weapons in their hands. Githzerai *shield* reaction is very strong defensively.
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Githyanki war party (1 knight + 4 warriors) on red dragon mounts. Githzerai monastery with monks and a zerth leader. Either faction hunting mind flayers — potential temporary allies for the party.
