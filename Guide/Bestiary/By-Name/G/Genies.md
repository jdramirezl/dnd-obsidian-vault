# Genies

![Genies](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/djinni.jpg)


> **Type**: Large elemental | **CR**: 11 (7,200 XP) each | **Source**: Monster Manual p.141–147

Genies are rare elemental creatures out of story and legend. Only a few can be found on the Material Plane. The rest reside on the Elemental Planes, where they rule from lavish palaces and are attended by worshipful slaves. Genies are as brilliant as they are mighty, as proud as they are majestic.

---

## Dao

> **Type**: Large elemental, neutral evil | **CR**: 11 (7,200 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.143

### Stat Block

**Armor Class** 18 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 187 (15d10 + 105)
**Speed** 30 ft., burrow 30 ft., fly 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 23 (+6) | 12 (+1) | 24 (+7) | 12 (+1) | 13 (+1) | 14 (+2) |

**Saving Throws** Int +5, Wis +5, Cha +6
**Condition Immunities** [[Petrified|petrified]]
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** Terran
**Challenge** 11 (7,200 XP)

#### Traits

**Earth Glide.** The dao can burrow through nonmagical, unworked earth and stone. While doing so, the dao doesn't disturb the material it moves through.

**Elemental Demise.** If the dao dies, its body disintegrates into crystalline powder, leaving behind only equipment the dao was wearing or carrying.

**Innate Spellcasting.** The dao's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:
- At will: *detect evil and good*, *detect magic*, *stone shape*
- 3/day each: *passwall*, *move earth*, *tongues*
- 1/day each: *conjure elemental* (earth elemental only), *gaseous form*, *invisibility*, *phantasmal killer*, *plane shift*, *wall of stone*

**Sure-Footed.** The dao has advantage on Strength and Dexterity saving throws made against effects that would knock it [[Prone|prone]].

#### Actions

**Multiattack.** The dao makes two fist attacks or two maul attacks.

**Fist.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.

**Maul.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 20 (4d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 18 Strength check or be knocked [[Prone|prone]].

**Saving Throws** Dex +6, Wis +7, Cha +9
**Damage Immunities** lightning, thunder
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** Auran
**Challenge** 11 (7,200 XP)

#### Traits

**Elemental Demise.** If the djinni dies, its body disintegrates into a warm breeze, leaving behind only equipment the djinni was wearing or carrying.

**Innate Spellcasting.** The djinni's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:
- At will: *detect evil and good*, *detect magic*, *thunderwave*
- 3/day each: *create food and water* (can create wine instead of water), *tongues*, *wind walk*
- 1/day each: *conjure elemental* (air elemental only), *creation*, *gaseous form*, *invisibility*, *major image*, *plane shift*

#### Actions

**Multiattack.** The djinni makes three scimitar attacks.

**Scimitar.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 12 (2d6 + 5) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) lightning or thunder damage (djinni's choice).

**Create Whirlwind.** A 5-foot-radius, 30-foot-tall cylinder of swirling air magically forms on a point the djinni can see within 120 feet of it. The whirlwind lasts as long as the djinni maintains concentration (as if concentrating on a spell). Any creature but the djinni that enters the whirlwind must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be [[Restrained|restrained]] by it. The djinni can move the whirlwind up to 60 feet as an action. A creature can use its action to free a creature [[Restrained|restrained]] by the whirlwind, including itself, by succeeding on a DC 18 Strength check.

On a failure, a target takes 21 (6d6) bludgeoning damage and, if it is Huge or smaller, is pushed up to 20 feet away from the marid and knocked [[Prone|prone]]. On a success, a target takes half the bludgeoning damage, but is neither pushed nor knocked [[Prone|prone]].

## Variant: Genie Powers

**Disguises.** Some genies can veil themselves in illusion to pass as other similarly shaped creatures. Such genies can innately cast *disguise self* at will, often with a longer duration. Mightier genies can cast *true polymorph* one to three times per day.

**Wishes.** The genie power to grant wishes is legendary among mortals. Only the most potent genies, such as those among the nobility, can do so. A particular genie that has this power can grant one to three wishes to a creature that isn't a genie. Once a genie has granted its limit of wishes, it can't grant wishes again for some amount of time (usually 1 year), and cosmic law dictates that the same genie can expend its limit of wishes on a specific creature only once in that creature's existence.

## Lore

**Creatures of the Elements.** A genie is born when the soul of a sentient living creature melds with the primordial matter of an elemental plane. When a genie perishes, it leaves nothing behind except what it was wearing or carrying, along with a small trace of its native element.

**Rule or Be Ruled.** Mortal slaves serve to validate a genie's power. A genie without property amounts to nothing among its own kind. In contrast to their love of slaves, most genies loathe being bound to service themselves.

**Dao** — Greedy, malicious genies from the Elemental Plane of Earth. They adorn themselves with jewelry crafted from precious gems and rare metals. Dao are on speaking terms with efreet but have nothing but scorn for djinn and marids.

**Djinni** — Proud, sensuous genies from the Elemental Plane of Air. Attractive, tall, well-muscled humanoids with blue skin. Known for their sense of mischief and favorable attitude toward mortals. Of all genies, djinn are the most amenable to servitude.

**Efreeti** — Hulking genies of the Elemental Plane of Fire. Deceptive, cunning, and cruel to the point of ruthlessness. They rule as oppressive tyrants from great domed fortresses of black glass and basalt, or in the fabled City of Brass.

**Marid** — The most wondrous of genie-kind, hailing from the Elemental Plane of Water. Large and piscine, marids are champion tale-tellers and consider it a crime for a lesser being to interrupt one of their tales.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!warning] Deadly Encounter
> All four genies are CR 11 — recommended for parties of level 8+. Each has *plane shift*, *conjure elemental*, and *invisibility*. Noble variants can grant three wishes — campaign-altering power.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Efreet are the classic villains (LE, fire immune, *wall of fire*). Djinn are potential allies (CG, *create food and water*). All genies can *plane shift* to escape — they never fight to the death. Noble genie wish-granting is a campaign-defining moment.

- **What they are:** Four elemental noble beings, all CR 11. Dao (earth, NE), Djinni (air, CG), Efreeti (fire, LE), Marid (water, CN). All have innate spellcasting, *plane shift*, *conjure elemental*, and *[[Invisibility|invisibility]]*. Noble variants can grant wishes.
- **When to use:** Wish-granting quests, planar adventures, lamp/bottle encounters. Djinn are potential allies; efreet are classic villains. Dao make great Underdark power brokers. Marids are unpredictable wildcards.
- **Key combat tactics:**
  - *Dao:* Earth Glide lets it phase through stone for hit-and-run. Maul knocks [[Prone|prone]] on DC 18 Str. *Wall of stone* and *passwall* reshape the battlefield.
  - *Djinni:* Three scimitar attacks + lightning/thunder damage. Create Whirlwind [[Restrained|restrains]] on DC 18 Str. Fly 90 ft for superior mobility.
  - *Efreeti:* Hurl Flame at 120 ft for 5d6 fire. Scimitar adds 2d6 fire. *Wall of fire* for area denial. Highest HP (200).
  - *Marid:* Water Jet is a 60-ft line for 6d6 + push + [[Prone|prone]]. Highest HP (229) and most resistances. *Control water* in aquatic environments is devastating.
- **Notable abilities:** All can *plane shift* to escape. *Conjure elemental* adds a CR 5 ally. *Invisibility* and *gaseous form* for escape. Noble Genie variant adds wish-granting.
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** A dao with earth elementals and enslaved miners. A djinni with air elementals in a cloud palace. An efreeti with fire elementals and azers in the City of Brass. A marid with water elementals in a coral fortress.
