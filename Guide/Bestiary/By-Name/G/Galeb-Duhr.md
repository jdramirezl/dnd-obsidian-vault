# Galeb Duhr

![Galeb Duhr](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/galeb-duhr.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium elemental, neutral | **CR**: 6 (2,300 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.139–140

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 85 (9d8 + 45)
**Speed** 15 ft. (30 ft. when rolling, 60 ft. rolling downhill)

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 11 (+0) | 12 (+1) | 11 (+0) |

**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]], [[Poisoned|poisoned]], [[Petrified|petrified]]
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., tremorsense 60 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** Terran
**Challenge** 6 (2,300 XP)

### Traits

**False Appearance.** While the galeb duhr remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal boulder.

**Rolling Charge.** If the galeb duhr rolls at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a slam attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked [[Prone|prone]].

### Actions

**Slam.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage.

**Animate Boulders (1/Day).** The galeb duhr magically animates up to two boulders it can see within 60 feet of it. A boulder has statistics like those of a galeb duhr, except it has Intelligence 1 and Charisma 1, it can't be [[Charmed|charmed]] or [[Frightened|frightened]], and it lacks this action option. A boulder remains animated as long as the galeb duhr maintains concentration, up to 1 minute (as if concentrating on a spell).

## Lore

The galeb duhr is a boulder-like creature with stumpy appendages that act as arms and legs. It has the ability to animate the rocks and boulders around it, and is thus usually encountered in rocky terrain.

**Stone Guardian.** A galeb duhr doesn't age or require sustenance, making it an excellent sentinel. A powerful druid might charge a galeb duhr with protecting a stone circle or sacred hilltop. When it chooses to, the galeb duhr can make itself look like an ordinary boulder, remaining perfectly still for years at a time.

**Stone Connection.** A galeb duhr can become one with the earth around it, allowing it to imbue nearby rocks and boulders with a semblance of life. It uses its animated boulders to frighten away interlopers. When it needs to move close to intruders, it presses its limbs tight to its body and rolls forward at a furious pace.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Animate Boulders (concentration) creates two copies of itself — tripling its action economy. Three creatures at +8 to hit with Rolling Charge (2d6 bonus + DC 16 Str knockdown). False Appearance means the party walks right past it.

- **What it is:** A boulder-like elemental sentinel that animates nearby rocks. CR 6 with strong defenses (AC 16, damage resistances, 85 HP) and the ability to effectively triple its action economy.
- **When to use:** Guarding druid circles, mountain passes, underground tombs, or wizard towers. Excellent ambush creature thanks to False Appearance. A galeb duhr that's been guarding something for centuries makes a great encounter with built-in lore.
- **Key combat tactics:** Opens with Animate Boulders to create two copies of itself (concentration). That's three creatures with +8 to hit and 2d6+5 damage. Rolling Charge adds 2d6 and a DC 16 Str knockdown. Tremorsense 60 ft means it detects approaching creatures through the ground.
- **Notable abilities:** Animate Boulders is the key — effectively triples its combat presence. False Appearance for ambush. Variable speed (15/30/60 ft depending on rolling) is unique. Permanently bound to the Material Plane.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Prone|prone]] (Rolling Charge)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Alone as a guardian of a sacred site. With earth elementals in a Plane of Earth-touched area. As a friendly NPC that shares information about its environment if not threatened.
