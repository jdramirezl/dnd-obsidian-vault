# Gnome, Deep (Svirfneblin)

![Gnome, Deep (Svirfneblin)](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/deep-gnome.jpg)


> **Type**: Small humanoid (gnome), neutral good | **CR**: 1/2 (100 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.164–165

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 15 (chain shirt)
**Hit Points** 16 (3d6 + 6)
**Speed** 20 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 15 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 9 (-1) |

**Skills** Investigation +3, Perception +2, Stealth +4
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** Gnomish, Terran, Undercommon
**Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

### Traits

**Stone Camouflage.** The gnome has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in rocky terrain.

**Gnome Cunning.** The gnome has advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic.

**Innate Spellcasting.** The gnome's innate spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 11). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:
- At will: *nondetection* (self only)
- 1/day each: *blindness/deafness*, *blur*, *disguise self*

### Actions

**War Pick.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.

**Poisoned Dart.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

## Lore

Deep gnomes, or svirfneblin, live far below the world's surface in twisting warrens and sculpted caverns. They survive by virtue of their stealth, cleverness, and tenacity. Their gray skin allows them to blend in with surrounding stonework.

**Gemstone Harvesters.** Svirfneblin cherish fine gemstones, especially rubies, which they harvest from mines deep in the Underdark. The hunt for gems often brings them into conflict with beholders, drow, kuo-toa, duergar, and mind flayers.

**Earth Friends.** Deep gnomes are often encountered in the company of creatures from the Elemental Plane of Earth. Some svirfneblin can summon such creatures. Earth creatures guard svirfneblin settlements, especially xorn enticed to service with the promise of gems to feed on.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Gnome Cunning (advantage on Int/Wis/Cha saves vs. magic) + at-will *nondetection* makes svirfneblin nearly impossible to find with divination. Best used as Underdark allies and informants, not enemies. Stone Camouflage gives advantage on Stealth in rocky terrain.

- **What it is:** A stealthy Underdark gnome with innate magic and poison darts. CR 1/2 but well-equipped with defensive abilities. Neutral good — one of the few friendly Underdark races.
- **When to use:** Underdark exploration as allies, traders, or quest-givers. Svirfneblin enclaves are safe havens in the Underdark. They can provide information about nearby threats (drow, mind flayers, duergar).
- **Key combat tactics:** Stone Camouflage + Stealth +4 for ambushes in rocky terrain. [[Poisoned|poisoned]] Dart inflicts [[Poisoned|poisoned]] on DC 12 Con. *Blur* and *nondetection* (at will) make them hard to find and hit. *Blindness/deafness* 1/day is a solid debuff. Gnome Cunning gives advantage on mental saves vs. magic.
- **Notable abilities:** *Nondetection* at will means divination magic can't find them — explains how they survive near mind flayers. Gnome Cunning is extremely strong defensively. Darkvision 120 ft is double the standard range.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Poisoned|poisoned]] (Poisoned Dart), [[Blinded|blinded]]/[[Deafened|deafened]] (*blindness/deafness*)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** A svirfneblin patrol with xorn allies. An enclave under siege by drow or duergar. As guides through the Underdark for the party. Pair with earth elementals or galeb duhr as guardians.
