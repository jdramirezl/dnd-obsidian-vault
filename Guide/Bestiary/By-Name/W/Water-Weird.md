### Traits

**Invisible in Water.** The water weird is invisible while fully immersed in water.

**Water Bound.** The water weird dies if it leaves the water to which it is bound or if that water is destroyed.

### Actions

**Constrict.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 13 (3d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the target is Medium or smaller, it is [[Grappled|grappled]] (escape DC 13) and pulled 5 feet toward the water weird. Until this grapple ends, the target is [[Restrained|restrained]], the water weird tries to drown it, and the water weird can't constrict another target.

## Lore

A water weird is an elemental guardian bound to a specific water-filled location, such as a pool or fountain. Invisible while immersed in water, its serpentine shape becomes clear only when it emerges to attack, using its coils to crush any creature other than its summoner and those its summoner declares as off limits. When slain, a water weird becomes an inanimate pool of water.

**Good and Evil Weirds.** Like most elementals, a water weird has no concept of good or evil. However, a water weird bound to a sacred or befouled source of water begins to take on the nature of that site, becoming neutral good or neutral evil. A neutral good water weird tries to frighten away interlopers rather than kill them, while a neutral evil water weird kills its victims for pleasure and might turn against its summoner. A water weird loses its evil alignment if its waters are cleansed with a *purify food and drink* spell.

**Elemental Nature.** A water weird doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep.

## DM Quick Reference

> See [[Water-Weird-Tactics]] for detailed combat tactics from *The Monsters Know What They're Doing*.


> [!tip] Drain the Water, Kill the Weird
> Water Bound means the water weird dies instantly if its water source is destroyed or drained. Smart parties can bypass the fight entirely by tipping over the fountain or using *shape water*. Build the encounter around whether the party figures this out before someone drowns.

- **What it is:** An [[Invisible|invisible]] elemental serpent bound to a specific body of water. CR 3 guardian that grapples and drowns intruders. Only one target at a time, but restrained + drowning is lethal if the party can't break the grapple.
- **When to use:** Guarding sacred pools, fountains, wells, or flooded temple chambers. Works best in locations where the party must interact with the water — retrieving a submerged item, crossing a flooded room, or drinking from a fountain.
- **Key combat tactics:** Invisible until it attacks — surprise round is almost guaranteed. Grapples and restrains one target, then tries to drown them underwater. Party must break the grapple (DC 13) or kill the weird to save their ally. Can't leave its water, so ranged attackers are safe if they stay back.
- **Notable abilities:** Invisible in Water makes it undetectable until it strikes. Water Bound means destroying or draining the water kills it instantly. Fire resistance and poison immunity. Blindsight 30 ft. means invisibility and darkness don't help against it.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Grappled|grappled]] (DC 13), [[Restrained|restrained]] (Constrict)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Guarding a pool in a temple alongside traps. Multiple water weirds in a flooded dungeon corridor. Paired with a caster who summoned it. A neutral evil water weird in a befouled swamp spring alongside will-o'-wisps.
