## Remorhaz

> **Type**: Huge monstrosity, unaligned | **CR**: 11 (7,200 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.259

### Stat Block

**Armor Class** 17 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 195 (17d12 + 85)
**Speed** 30 ft., burrow 20 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 24 (+7) | 13 (+1) | 21 (+5) | 4 (-3) | 10 (+0) | 5 (-3) |

**Damage Immunities** cold, fire
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., tremorsense 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 11 (7,200 XP)

#### Traits

**Heated Body.** A creature that touches the remorhaz or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 10 (3d6) fire damage.

#### Actions

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 40 (6d10 + 7) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) fire damage. If the target is a creature, it is grappled (escape DC 17). Until this grapple ends, the target is [[Restrained|restrained]], and the remorhaz can't bite another target.

**Swallow.** The remorhaz makes one bite attack against a Medium or smaller creature it is grappling. If the attack hits, that creature takes the bite's damage and is swallowed, and the grapple ends. While swallowed, the creature is [[Blinded|blinded]] and [[Restrained|restrained]], it has total cover against attacks and other effects outside the remorhaz, and it takes 21 (6d6) acid damage at the start of each of the remorhaz's turns.

If the remorhaz takes 30 damage or more on a single turn from a creature inside it, the remorhaz must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw at the end of that turn or regurgitate all swallowed creatures, which fall [[Prone|prone]] in a space within 10 feet of the remorhaz. If the remorhaz dies, a swallowed creature is no longer [[Restrained|restrained]] by it and can escape from the corpse using 15 feet of movement, exiting [[Prone|prone]].

## Lore

**Arctic Predators.** Remorhazes live in arctic climes, preying on elk, polar bears, and other creatures sharing their territory. They can't tolerate warm weather, having adapted to the cold by generating a furnace-like heat within their bodies. When hunting, a remorhaz burrows deep below the snow and ice and lies in wait for the faint vibrations created by a creature moving above it.

**Young Ones.** Frost giant hunters scour the icy wastes for remorhaz nests and eggs. The giants prize young remorhazes, which can be trained from hatching to obey commands and guard the giants' icy citadels. Unlike fully grown specimens, young remorhazes gnaw on their victims instead of swallowing them whole.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!warning] Adult Remorhaz — CR 11 Melee Punisher
> Heated Body deals 3d6 fire to anyone who hits it in melee — every single time. The bite grapples (DC 17), then Swallow on the next turn for 6d6 acid/round inside. Immune to both cold AND fire. Melee fighters are punished for engaging; ranged attackers are the answer.

> [!tip] Heated Body Tax
> Every melee hit against a remorhaz costs the attacker 3d6 (avg 10) fire damage automatically. A fighter making two attacks per round takes 20 fire damage just for hitting. Factor this "melee tax" into encounter balance — the remorhaz is effectively dealing damage on the party's turn too.

- **What they are:** Arctic burrowing predators that generate intense internal heat. The Young Remorhaz (CR 5) is a Large bruiser with Heated Body (2d6 fire to melee attackers) and a powerful bite. The adult Remorhaz (CR 11) is Huge with 195 HP, grapple-into-swallow mechanics, and Heated Body dealing 3d6 fire.
- **When to use:** Arctic tundra, frozen mountain passes, frost giant lairs. A remorhaz erupting from beneath the snow is a dramatic set-piece. Young remorhazes as frost giant pets add variety to giant encounters.
- **Key combat tactics:**
  - *Young:* Burrows under snow, erupts to bite for 20 + 7 fire damage. Heated Body punishes melee attackers — ranged characters have the advantage. Immune to cold and fire.
  - *Adult:* Bite grapples (DC 17 escape), then Swallow on the next turn for 6d6 acid/round inside. Heated Body (3d6 fire) makes melee combat costly. 40 piercing + 10 fire on a bite hit is devastating. Tremorsense 60 ft. for ambush detection.
- **Notable abilities:** Heated Body is the signature — melee fighters take automatic fire damage every time they hit. Cold and fire immunity means two common damage types are useless. Adult's Swallow mechanic (30 damage threshold, DC 15 Con to regurgitate) creates urgency.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Restrained|restrained]], [[Grappled|grappled]] (adult Bite); [[Blinded|blinded]], [[Restrained|restrained]] (Swallow); [[Prone|prone]] (regurgitation)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** An adult remorhaz guarding a frost giant citadel entrance. Young remorhazes as pets in a frost giant lair. A remorhaz erupting mid-blizzard while the party crosses a frozen lake. Pair with ice mephits or winter wolves for an arctic-themed encounter.
