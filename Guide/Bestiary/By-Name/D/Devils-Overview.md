# Devils

> **Type**: Fiend (devil), lawful evil | **Source**: Monster Manual pp.66–78

## Overview

Devils personify tyranny, with a totalitarian society dedicated to the domination of mortal life. The shadow of the Nine Hells of Baator extends far across the multiverse, and Asmodeus, the dark lord of Nessus, strives to subjugate the cosmos to satisfy his thirst for power. To do so, he must continually expand his infernal armies, sending his servants to the mortal realm to corrupt the souls from which new devils are spawned.

**Lords of Tyranny.** Devils live to conquer, enslave, and oppress. They take perverse delight in exercising authority over the weak. Every interaction is an opportunity for a devil to display its power. Devils understand the failings that plague intelligent mortals, and they use that knowledge to lead mortals into temptation and darkness, turning creatures into slaves to their own corruption. On the Material Plane, devils manipulate humanoid rulers — whispering evil thoughts, fomenting paranoia, and eventually driving them to tyrannical actions.

**Obedience and Ambition.** In accordance with their lawful alignment, devils obey even when they envy or dislike their superiors, knowing that their obedience will be rewarded. The hierarchy of the Nine Hells depends on this unswerving loyalty, without which that fiendish plane would become as anarchic as the Abyss. Yet it is in the nature of devils to scheme, creating in some a desire to rule that eclipses their contentment to be ruled.

**Dark Dealers and Soul Mongers.** Devils love to strike bargains with mortals seeking to gain some benefit or prize. A contract with even the lowliest devil is enforced by Asmodeus's will. Any mortal creature that breaks such a contract instantly forfeits its soul, which is spirited away to the Nine Hells. To own a creature's soul is to have absolute control over that creature, and most devils accept no other currency. Only divine intervention can release a soul after a devil has claimed it.

## The Infernal Hierarchy

Asmodeus is the supreme ruler of all devils, and the only creature in the Nine Hells with the powers of a lesser god. He commands scores of pit fiend generals, which in turn command legions of subordinates. He delegates most matters of rulership to the pit fiends and lesser archdevils that make up the infernal bureaucracy.

If a devil dies outside the Nine Hells, it disappears in a cloud of sulfurous smoke or dissolves into a pool of ichor, instantly returning to its home layer, where it reforms at full strength. Devils that die *in* the Nine Hells are destroyed forever — a fate that even Asmodeus fears.

**Archdevils** include all current and deposed rulers of the Nine Hells, as well as the dukes and duchesses that make up their courts. Every archdevil is a unique being with an appearance that reflects its particular evil nature.

**Greater Devils** include [[Devil-Pit-Fiend|pit fiends]], [[Devil-Erinyes|erinyes]], [[Devil-Horned|horned devils]], and [[Devil-Ice|ice devils]] that command lesser devils and attend the archdevils.

**Lesser Devils** include [[Devil-Imp|imps]], [[Devil-Chain|chain devils]], [[Devil-Spined|spined devils]], [[Devil-Bearded|bearded devils]], [[Devil-Barbed|barbed devils]], and [[Devil-Bone|bone devils]].

**Lemures** are the lowest form of devil — the twisted and tormented souls of evil and corrupted mortals. A [[Devil-Lemure|lemure]] killed in the Nine Hells is only permanently destroyed if killed with a blessed weapon or if its corpse is splashed with holy water before it can return to life.

### Promotion and Demotion

When the soul of an evil mortal sinks into the Nine Hells, it takes on the physical form of a wretched lemure. Archdevils and greater devils have the power to promote lemures to lesser devils. Archdevils can promote lesser devils to greater devils, and Asmodeus alone can promote a greater devil to archdevil status. Demotion is the customary punishment for failure or disobedience. No devil can promote or demote another devil that has not sworn fealty to it.

## The Nine Hells

The Nine Hells are a single plane comprising nine separate layers. The first eight are each ruled by archdevils that answer to Asmodeus, the Archduke of Nessus. To reach the deepest layer, one must descend through all eight layers above it. The most expeditious means is the River Styx.

## Devil True Names and Talismans

Every devil above a lemure in station has a true name that it keeps secret. A devil can be forced to disclose its true name if [[Charmed|charmed]], and ancient scrolls and tomes are said to exist that list the true names of certain devils. A mortal who learns a devil's true name can use powerful summoning magic to call the devil from the Nine Hells and bind it into service. Binding can also be accomplished with a devil talisman — an ancient relic inscribed with the true name of a devil it controls, bathed in the blood of a worthy sacrifice.

## Variant: Devil Summoning

Some devils can have an action option that allows them to summon other devils.

**Summon Devil (1/Day).** The devil chooses what to summon and attempts a magical summoning.

| Devil | Chance | Summons |
|-------|--------|---------|

| [[Devil-Bearded|Bearded Devil]] | 30% | 1 bearded devil |
| [[Devil-Bone|Bone Devil]] | 40% | 2d6 spined devils or 1 bone devil |
| [[Devil-Erinyes|Erinyes]] | 50% | 3d6 spined devils, 1d6 bearded devils, or 1 erinyes |
| [[Devil-Horned|Horned Devil]] | 30% | 1 horned devil |
| [[Devil-Ice|Ice Devil]] | 60% | 1 ice devil |
| [[Devil-Pit-Fiend|Pit Fiend]] | Auto | 2d4 bearded devils, 1d4 barbed devils, or 1 erinyes |

A summoned devil appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can't summon other devils. It remains for 1 minute, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> All devils share: Devil's Sight (magical darkness vision), Magic Resistance, fire/poison immunity, cold resistance, and nonmagical/non-silvered weapon resistance. They honor contracts — use this for RP. Death outside the Nine Hells is temporary.

- **All devils** share: Devil's Sight (see through magical darkness), Magic Resistance (advantage on saves vs. spells), fire immunity, poison immunity, cold resistance, nonmagical/non-silvered weapon resistance
- **Lawful evil** — devils honor contracts and hierarchies; use this for RP
- **Soul economy** — devils trade in souls; broken contracts = instant soul forfeiture
- **Summoning variant** — adds action economy; use sparingly to avoid overwhelming parties
- **Death outside Nine Hells** — devil reforms on home layer; only permanent death is in the Nine Hells
- **Promotion/demotion** — great plot hooks; a devil might betray its master to earn promotion
- **True names** — powerful summoning/binding tool; great quest reward or MacGuffin
- **Contrast with [[Demons-Overview|Demons]]** — devils are lawful (contracts, hierarchy, discipline) vs. demons' chaotic (destruction, madness, entropy)
