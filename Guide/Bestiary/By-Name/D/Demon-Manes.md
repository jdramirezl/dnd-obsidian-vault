## Lore

Souls of evil creatures that descend to the Lower Planes are transformed into manes—the lowest form of demonkind. These wretched fiends attack any non-demon they see, and they are called to the Material Plane by those seeking to sow death and chaos.

Orcus, the Prince of Undeath, has the power to transform manes into undead monsters, most often ghouls and shadows. Other demon lords feed on manes, destroying them utterly. Otherwise, killing a manes causes it to dissipate into a cloud of reeking vapor that reforms into another manes after one day.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Use in massive swarms (10+) for action economy pressure. They reform after 1 day unless killed by a demon lord or in the Abyss — endless waves of fodder in planar encounters.

**Minor demon.** The absolute bottom of the demonic hierarchy—use in massive swarms.

- **9 HP, AC 9** — dies to almost anything
- **Immune to [[Charmed|charmed]], [[Frightened|frightened]], [[Poisoned|poisoned]]** — surprisingly resilient against conditions for its CR
- **Reforms after death** — dissipates into vapor and reforms as a new manes after 1 day (unless destroyed by a demon lord or killed in the Abyss)
- **No speech** — understands Abyssal but can't communicate

**Tactics:** Throw 20+ at the party as a living tide. They exist to absorb actions and resources. Pair with a hezrou or vrock that can use Demon Summoning to call 2d6 dretches or similar reinforcements while the manes screen.
