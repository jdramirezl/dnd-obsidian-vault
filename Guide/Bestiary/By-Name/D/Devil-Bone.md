# Bone Devil (Osyluth)

![Bone Devil (Osyluth)](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/bone-devil.jpg)


> **Type**: Large fiend (devil), lawful evil | **CR**: 9 (5,000 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.71

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 19 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 142 (15d10 + 60)
**Speed** 40 ft., fly 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 13 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) |

**Saving Throws** Int +5, Wis +6, Cha +7
**Skills** Deception +7, Insight +6
**Damage Resistances** cold; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't silvered
**Damage Immunities** fire, poison
**Condition Immunities** [[Poisoned|poisoned]]
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** Infernal, telepathy 120 ft.

### Traits

**Devil's Sight.** Magical darkness doesn't impede the devil's darkvision.

**Magic Resistance.** The devil has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The devil makes three attacks: two with its claws and one with its sting.

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4) slashing damage.

**Sting.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 17 (5d6) poison damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

### Variant: Hooked Polearm

Some bone devils have the following action options instead:

**Multiattack.** The devil makes two attacks: one with its hooked polearm and one with its sting.

**Hooked Polearm.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d12 + 4) piercing damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, it is [[Grappled|grappled]] (escape DC 14). Until this grapple ends, the devil can't use its polearm on another target.

## Lore

Driven by hate, lust, and envy, bone devils act as the cruel taskmasters of the Nine Hells. They set weaker devils to work, taking special delight in seeing fiends that defy them demoted. At the same time, they long for promotion and are bitterly envious of their superiors, attempting to curry favor though it irks them to do so.

A bone devil appears as a humanoid husk, with dried skin stretched tight across its skeletal frame. It bears a fearsome skull-like head and the tail of a scorpion, and a foul odor of decay hangs in the air around it. Though they are devastating in combat with their claws, bone devils also wield hooked polearms made of bone, which they use to subdue enemies before striking with their venomous tails.

See [[Devils-Overview]] for shared devil lore, the infernal hierarchy, and the Nine Hells.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Sting deals ~30 avg damage (2d8+4 + 5d6 poison) and poisons on DC 14 Con. AC 19 + Magic Resistance + fly 40 ft. makes this an extremely durable CR 9 threat. Focus sting on the party's healer.

- **Opener:** Fly in, sting priority target — 2d8+4 piercing + 5d6 poison (~30 avg) is brutal
- **Melee combo (default):** 2 claws (1d8+4 each) + sting (2d8+4 + 5d6 poison) = ~46 avg/round
- **Polearm variant:** Grapple + sting combo — hook a target (2d12+4, grapple) then sting while held
- **Poison condition:** DC 14 Con — [[Poisoned|poisoned]] gives disadvantage on attacks and [[04-Ability-Checks|ability checks]]
- **Defenses:** AC 19 is exceptional for CR 9; 142 HP, fly 40 ft., Magic Resistance
- **Variant:** [[Devils-Overview#Variant: Devil Summoning|Devil Summoning]] — 40% chance to summon 2d6 [[Devil-Spined|spined devils]] or 1 bone devil
- **Weakness:** No ranged attacks; force it to close distance
- **Encounter use:** Taskmaster commanding [[Devil-Bearded|bearded devils]] or [[Devil-Spined|spined devils]]; mid-tier Nine Hells officer
