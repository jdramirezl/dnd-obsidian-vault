# Dretch

![Dretch](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/demon-dretch.jpg)


> **Type**: Small fiend (demon), chaotic evil | **CR**: 1/4 (50 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.57

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 11 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 18 (4d6 + 4)
**Speed** 20 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 11 (+0) | 11 (+0) | 12 (+1) | 5 (−3) | 8 (−1) | 3 (−4) |

**Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** [[Poisoned|poisoned]]
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 9
**Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 60 ft. (works only with creatures that understand Abyssal)

### Traits

None.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The dretch makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 3 (1d6) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (2d4) slashing damage.

**Fetid Cloud (1/Day).** A 10-foot radius of disgusting green gas extends out from the dretch. The gas spreads around corners, and its area is lightly obscured. It lasts for 1 minute or until a strong wind disperses it. Any creature that starts its turn in that area must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or be [[Poisoned|poisoned]] until the start of its next turn. While [[Poisoned|poisoned]] in this way, the target can take either an action or a bonus action on its turn, not both, and can't take reactions.

## Lore

Dretches are among the weakest of demons—repulsive, self-loathing creatures doomed to spend eternity in a state of perpetual discontent. Their low intelligence makes dretches unsuitable for anything but the simplest tasks. However, what they lack in potential, they make up for in sheer malice. Dretches mill about in mobs, voicing their displeasure as an unsettling din of hoots, snarls, and grunts.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Cannon fodder — use in mobs of 6+. Fetid Cloud (1/day) poisons in a 10 ft. radius, costing targets their action or bonus action. Resistant to cold/fire/lightning, so AoE spells are less effective than expected.

**Minor demon.** Cannon fodder—use in large mobs to overwhelm low-level parties or as expendable minions for greater demons.

- **Fetid Cloud (1/Day)** — DC 11 CON or [[Poisoned|poisoned]] (lose action OR bonus action, no reactions); 10 ft. radius; lightly obscured
- **Low stats across the board** — +2 to hit, 18 HP, AC 11
- **Resistances** — cold, fire, lightning; immune to poison

**Tactics:** Swarm in groups of 6–12. Have each dretch use Fetid Cloud on round 1 to blanket the area in overlapping gas clouds, then mob weakened targets. Expendable—greater demons send them in first.
