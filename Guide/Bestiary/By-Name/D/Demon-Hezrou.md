# Hezrou

![Hezrou](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/demon-hezrou.jpg)


> **Type**: Large fiend (demon), chaotic evil | **CR**: 8 (3,900 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.60

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 136 (13d10 + 65)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 19 (+4) | 17 (+3) | 20 (+5) | 5 (−3) | 12 (+1) | 13 (+1) |

**Saving Throws** Str +7, Con +8, Wis +4
**Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** [[Poisoned|poisoned]]
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 120 ft.

### Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The hezrou has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Stench.** Any creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the hezrou must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be [[Poisoned|poisoned]] until the start of its next turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to the hezrou's stench for 24 hours.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The hezrou makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage.

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

## Lore

Hezrous serve as foot soldiers in the demonic hordes of the Abyss. Although physically powerful, they are weak-minded and hezrous can easily be duped into sacrificing themselves by more powerful demons. As they press their attacks into the heart of an enemy's forces, their foul stench can sicken even the toughest foes.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Stench aura (10 ft., DC 14 Con) poisons melee fighters — disadvantage on attacks and ability checks. Park the hezrou in the frontline and let the aura do the work while it Multiattacks for 37 avg/round.

**Type 2 demon.** A durable front-line brute with a debilitating stench aura.

- **Stench** — DC 14 CON or [[Poisoned|poisoned]] (disadvantage on attacks and [[04-Ability-Checks|ability checks]]); 10 ft. radius; punishes melee fighters
- **Multiattack** — 3 attacks for ~37 avg damage
- **136 HP, AC 16** — tanky; resistant to nonmagical B/P/S, cold, fire, lightning
- **Weak-minded** — low INT (−3) and WIS (+1); vulnerable to mental saves
- **Variant: Demon Summoning** — 30% chance to summon 2d6 dretches or 1 hezrou

**Tactics:** Wade into melee to force Stench saves on as many PCs as possible. Pair with ranged demons who benefit from the party being [[Poisoned|poisoned]]. Expendable—send them in first to soften up the enemy.
