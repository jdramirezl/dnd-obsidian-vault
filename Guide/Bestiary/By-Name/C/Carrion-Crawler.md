# Carrion Crawler

![Carrion Crawler](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/carrion-crawler.jpg)


> **Type**: Large monstrosity, unaligned | **CR**: 2 (450 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.37

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 51 (6d10 + 18)
**Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

| 14 (+2) | 13 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 1 (-5) | 12 (+1) | 5 (-3) |

**Skills** Perception +3
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 2 (450 XP)

### Traits

**Keen Smell.** The carrion crawler has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

**Spider Climb.** The carrion crawler can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The carrion crawler makes two attacks: one with its tentacles and one with its bite.

**Tentacles.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) poison damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 minute. Until this poison ends, the target is [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]]. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the poison on itself on a success.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (2d4 + 2) piercing damage.

## Lore

Carrion crawlers scour putrid flesh from carcasses and gobble the slimy bones that remain. They aggressively attack any creature that trespasses on their territory or disturbs their feasting.

**Carrion Eaters.** A carrion crawler follows the scent of death to its food, but it prefers not to compete with other scavengers. These foul creatures hunker down in territories where death is plentiful and other carrion eaters have limited mobility. Caves, sewers, dungeons, and forested marshes are their favored lairs, but carrion crawlers are also drawn to battlefields and cemeteries.

**Patient Predators.** Whether in subterranean darkness or while hunting at night, light signals a potential meal. A carrion crawler might follow a light source from a distance for hours, hoping to pick up the scent of blood. Once a victim goes rigid with paralysis, the carrion crawler wraps it with its tentacles and drags it away to a high ledge or isolated passageway, where it can be killed safely.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Spider Climb lets it attack from the ceiling — players rarely look up. Tentacles paralyze on a DC 13 Con fail, then it drags the victim away. Multiple crawlers can paralyze-lock an entire party.

- **Ambush predator:** Spider Climb lets it attack from ceilings — players rarely look up; give it surprise
- **Tentacles are the threat:** +8 to hit, 10 ft. reach, DC 13 Con or [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]] — devastating for CR 2
- **Combo:** Paralyze with tentacles → bite the [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]] target (auto-crit in melee)
- **Multiple crawlers:** 2–3 can paralyze-lock an entire party; use sparingly at low levels
- **Retreat behavior:** Drags [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]] victims away — party has a ticking clock to rescue allies
- **Weakness:** Low AC (13), no resistances, Int 1 — no tactics, just relentless hunger
- **Environment:** Best in tight tunnels, sewers, or dungeon corridors where ceiling ambush is plausible
