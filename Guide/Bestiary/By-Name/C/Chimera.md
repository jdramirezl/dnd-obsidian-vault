### Actions

**Multiattack.** The chimera makes three attacks: one with its bite, one with its horns, and one with its claws. When its fire breath is available, it can use the breath in place of its bite or horns.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.

**Horns.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (1d12 + 4) bludgeoning damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

**Fire Breath (Recharge 5–6).** The dragon head exhales fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 31 (7d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

## Lore

Chimeras were created after mortals summoned Demogorgon to the world. The Prince of Demons, unimpressed with the creatures that surrounded it, transformed them into horrific, multi-headed monstrosities. This act gave rise to the first chimeras.

A typical specimen has the hindquarters of a large goat, the forequarters of a lion, and the leathery wings of a dragon, along with the heads of all three of those creatures. The monster likes to surprise its victims, swooping down from the sky and engulfing prey with its fiery breath before landing.

**Conflicted Creature.** A chimera combines the worst aspects of its three parts. Its dragon head drives it to raid, plunder, and accumulate a great hoard. Its leonine nature compels it to hunt and kill powerful creatures that threaten its territory. Its goat head grants it a vicious, stubborn streak that compels it to fight to the death.

**Servant of Evil.** Though chimeras are far from cunning, their draconic ego makes them susceptible to flattery and gifts. A villain can lure a chimera into service by keeping it well fed and its treasure hoard well stocked.

> [!tip] DM Tip
> Fire Breath substitutes for bite or horns on Multiattack — don't waste a full action on it. Three attacks/round (avg 32 damage) plus breath weapon makes this a relentless damage dealer. No resistances or immunities — CC spells shut it down hard.

- **Opener:** Swoop in (60 ft. fly), Fire Breath the clustered party (15 ft. cone, 7d8 avg 31 fire, DC 15 Dex), then land
- **Three attacks/round:** Bite (2d6+4) + Horns (1d12+4) + Claws (2d6+4) = avg 32 damage — brutal sustained DPR
- **Fire Breath:** Recharge 5–6; substitute for bite or horns on Multiattack — don't waste a full action
- **Flight:** 60 ft. fly; if party lacks ranged damage, it can kite indefinitely
- **Fights to the death:** Int 3, goat-head stubbornness — no retreat, no tactics beyond "attack nearest"
- **HP tank:** 114 HP at AC 14 absorbs a lot of punishment; solid mid-tier boss
- **Weakness:** No resistances, no save bonuses, no condition immunities — CC spells shut it down hard
