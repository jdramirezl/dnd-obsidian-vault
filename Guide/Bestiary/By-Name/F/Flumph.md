# Flumph

![Flumph](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/flumph.jpg)


> **Type**: Small aberration, lawful good | **CR**: 1/8 (25 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.135–136

## Stat Block

**Armor Class** 12
**Hit Points** 7 (2d6)
**Speed** 5 ft., fly 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 6 (-2) | 15 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 11 (+0) |

**Skills** Arcana +4, History +4, Religion +4
**Damage Vulnerabilities** psychic
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** understands Undercommon but can't speak, telepathy 60 ft.
**Challenge** 1/8 (25 XP)

### Traits

**Advanced Telepathy.** The flumph can perceive the content of any telepathic communication used within 60 feet of it, and it can't be surprised by creatures with any form of telepathy.

**Prone Deficiency.** If the flumph is knocked [[Prone|prone]], roll a die. On an odd result, the flumph lands upside-down and is [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]]. At the end of each of its turns, the flumph can make a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw, righting itself and ending the [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] condition if it succeeds.

**Telepathic Shroud.** The flumph is immune to any effect that would sense its emotions or read its thoughts, as well as all divination spells.

### Actions

**Tendrils.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage plus 2 (1d4) acid damage. At the end of each of its turns, the target must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw, taking 2 (1d4) acid damage on a failure or ending the recurring acid damage on a success. A *lesser restoration* spell cast on the target also ends the recurring acid damage.

**Stench Spray (1/Day).** Each creature in a 15-foot cone originating from the flumph must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or be coated in a foul-smelling liquid. A coated creature exudes a horrible stench for 1d4 hours. The coated creature is [[Poisoned|poisoned]] as long as the stench lasts, and other creatures are [[Poisoned|poisoned]] while within 5 feet of the coated creature. A creature can remove the stench on itself by using a short rest to bathe in water, alcohol, or vinegar.

## Lore

The mysterious flumphs drift through the Underdark, propelled through the air by the jets whose sound gives them their name. A flumph glows faintly, reflecting its moods in its color. Soft pink means it is amused, deep blue is sadness, green expresses curiosity, and crimson is anger.

**Intelligent and Wise.** Flumphs communicate telepathically. Though they resemble jellyfish, flumphs are sentient beings of great intelligence and wisdom, possessing advanced knowledge of religion, philosophy, mathematics, and countless other subjects.

**Psionic Siphons.** Flumphs feed by siphoning mental energy from psionic creatures, and they can be found lurking near communities of mind flayers, aboleths, githyanki, and githzerai. When flumphs encounter good-hearted adventurers, they eagerly share the dark secrets they have learned.

**Flumph Society.** Flumphs live in complex and organized groups called cloisters, within which each flumph has a place and purpose.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] DM Tip
> One of the only good-aligned monsters in the MM — use as a friendly Underdark informant, not a combat encounter. Flumphs lurk near mind flayer colonies and can warn the party about psionic threats. If knocked prone on an odd roll, it's helpless.

- **What it is:** A lawful good aberration — one of the only good-aligned monsters in the MM. A flying jellyfish-like creature that feeds on psionic energy and shares secrets with adventurers. CR 1/8, essentially a helpful NPC.
- **When to use:** Underdark exploration as a friendly information source. Flumphs lurk near mind flayer colonies and can warn the party about illithid plans, aboleth lairs, or other psionic threats. Great for comic relief that's also genuinely useful.
- **Key combat tactics:** Flumphs don't fight unless cornered. Stench Spray is defensive — [[Poisoned|poisons]] attackers. [[Prone|prone]] Deficiency is a critical weakness: if knocked [[Prone|prone]] on an odd roll, it's [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] and helpless.
- **Notable abilities:** Advanced Telepathy lets it eavesdrop on all telepathic communication within 60 ft — invaluable near mind flayers. Telepathic Shroud makes it undetectable by divination. Psychic vulnerability means psionic creatures can one-shot it.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Poisoned|poisoned]] (Stench Spray)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Place near a mind flayer colony as a secret ally. A flumph cloister can serve as a safe haven in the Underdark.
