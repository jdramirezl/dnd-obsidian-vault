# Mephits

![Mephits](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/ice-mephit.jpg)


> **Type**: Small elemental, neutral evil | **CR**: 1/4 – 1/2 | **Source**: Monster Manual p.215–218

Mephits are capricious, imp-like creatures native to the elemental planes. They come in six varieties, each one representing the mixture of two elements. Ageless tricksters, mephits gather in large numbers on the Elemental Planes and in the Elemental Chaos. They also find their way to the Material Plane, where they prefer to dwell in places where their base elements are abundant.

**Elemental Nature.** A mephit doesn't require food, drink, or sleep.

---

## Dust Mephit

> **Type**: Small elemental, neutral evil | **CR**: 1/2 (100 XP) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.215

Composed of earth and air, dust mephits are drawn to catacombs and find death morbidly fascinating.

### Stat Block

**Armor Class** 12
**Hit Points** 17 (5d6)
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 5 (-3) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 9 (-1) | 11 (+0) | 10 (+0) |

**Skills** Perception +2, Stealth +4
**Damage Vulnerabilities** fire
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** [[Poisoned|poisoned]]
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** Auran, Terran
**Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

#### Traits

**Death Burst.** When the mephit dies, it explodes in a burst of dust. Each creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or be [[Blinded|blinded]] for 1 minute. A [[Blinded|blinded]] creature can repeat the saving throw on each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

**Innate Spellcasting (1/Day).** The mephit can innately cast *sleep*, requiring no material components. Its innate spellcasting ability is Charisma.

#### Actions

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.

**Blinding Breath (Recharge 6).** The mephit exhales a 15-foot cone of blinding dust. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or be [[Blinded|blinded]] for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

## Variant: Mephit Summoning

Some mephits can have an action option that allows them to summon other mephits.

**Summon Mephits (1/Day).** The mephit has a 25 percent chance of summoning 1d4 mephits of its kind. A summoned mephit appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can't summon other mephits. It remains for 1 minute, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!tip] Death Burst Planning
> Every mephit explodes on death. When running groups, track which mephits are adjacent — killing one can trigger saves that soften up others, creating chain reactions. Mud mephit Death Burst (restrain) is especially nasty before other mephits attack.

> [!example] Mephit Summoning Variant
> Any mephit can have a 25% chance to summon 1d4 more of its kind (1/day). This can snowball a simple encounter into a swarm. Decide before the encounter which mephits have this ability — don't spring it without planning for the action economy spike.

- **What they are:** Six elemental imp-like tricksters, each a mix of two elements. CR 1/4 (Mud, Smoke, Steam) or CR 1/2 (Dust, Ice, Magma). Low HP, annoying abilities, and Death Burst on death.
- **When to use:** Elemental-themed encounters, conjurer's minions, environmental hazards near planar rifts. Great as comic relief or swarm encounters.
- **Key combat tactics:** Each has a Recharge 6 breath weapon (15 ft. cone or single target) and a 1/day innate spell. Death Burst triggers on death — plan for chain reactions. Mephit Summoning variant (25% chance) can snowball an encounter.
- **Quick comparison:**
  - *Dust:* Blinds (breath + Death Burst), casts *[[Sleep|sleep]]*
  - *Ice:* Cold damage, False Appearance ambush, casts **fog cloud**
  - *Magma:* Fire damage, False Appearance, casts **heat metal**
  - *Mud:* [[Restrained|Restrains]] (breath + Death Burst), toughest HP (27)
  - *Smoke:* Blinds (short duration), Death Burst creates obscurement, casts *dancing lights*
  - *Steam:* Fire damage, casts *[[Blur|blur]]* (defensive)
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Blinded|blinded]] (Dust, Smoke), [[Restrained|restrained]] (Mud)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** Mixed groups of 4–6 mephits near elemental rifts. Summoned by a conjurer or elemental cultist. Pair with their parent elemental type.
