# Mind Flayer

![Mind Flayer](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/mind-flayer.jpg)


> **Type**: Medium aberration, lawful evil | **CR**: 7 / 8 (Arcanist) | **Source**: Monster Manual p.221–222

Mind flayers, also called illithids, are the scourge of sentient creatures across countless worlds. Psionic tyrants, slavers, and interdimensional voyagers, they are insidious masterminds that harvest entire races for their own twisted ends. Four tentacles snake from their octopus-like heads, flexing in hungry anticipation when sentient creatures come near.

**Saving Throws** Int +7, Wis +6, Cha +6
**Skills** Arcana +7, Deception +6, Insight +6, Perception +6, Persuasion +6, Stealth +4
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 16
**Languages** Deep Speech, Undercommon, telepathy 120 ft.
**Challenge** 7 (2,900 XP)

#### Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The mind flayer has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Innate Spellcasting (Psionics).** The mind flayer's innate spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 15). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no components:
- At will: *detect thoughts*, *levitate*
- 1/day each: *dominate monster*, *plane shift* (self only)

#### Actions

**Tentacles.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 15 (2d10 + 4) psychic damage. If the target is Medium or smaller, it is [[Grappled|grappled]] (escape DC 15) and must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw or be [[Stunned|stunned]] until this grapple ends.

**Extract Brain.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] humanoid [[Grappled|grappled]] by the mind flayer. *Hit:* The target takes 55 (10d10) piercing damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the mind flayer kills the target by extracting and devouring its brain.

**Mind Blast (Recharge 5–6).** The mind flayer magically emits psychic energy in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw or take 22 (4d8 + 4) psychic damage and be [[Stunned|stunned]] for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

A few mind flayers supplement their psionic power with arcane spells. However, they are regarded as deviants by their illithid peers and usually shunned. A mind flayer arcanist has a challenge rating of 8 (3,900 XP) and the following additional trait:

**Spellcasting.** The mind flayer is a 10th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). The mind flayer has the following wizard spells prepared:

## Lore

**Psionic Commanders.** Mind flayers possess psionic powers that enable them to control the minds of creatures such as troglodytes, grimlocks, quaggoths, and ogres. Illithids prefer to communicate via telepathy and use their telepathy when issuing commands to their thralls. When an illithid meets strong resistance, it avoids initial combat as it orders its thralls to attack, sacrificing their lives so that their master can escape.

**Hive Mind Colonies.** Solitary mind flayers are likely rogues and outcasts. Most illithids belong to a colony of sibling mind flayers devoted to an elder brain — a massive brain-like being that resides in a briny pool near the center of a mind flayer community. From its pool, an elder brain telepathically dictates its desires to each individual mind flayer within 5 miles of it.

**Hunger of the Mind.** Illithids subsist on the brains of humanoids. The brains provide enzymes, hormones, and psychic energy necessary for their survival. An illithid experiences euphoria as it devours the brain of a humanoid, along with its memories, personality, and innermost fears. Mind flayers will sometimes harvest a brain rather than devour it, using it as part of some alien experiment or transforming it into an [[Intellect-Devourer|intellect devourer]].

**Qualith.** On the rare occasion that mind flayers need to write something down, they do so in Qualith — a system of tactile writing (similar to braille) read by an illithid's tentacles. Qualith is written in four-line stanzas and is so alien in construction that non-illithids must resort to magic to discern its meaning.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!quote] Tactical Deep Dive
> See [[Mind-Flayer-Tactics]] for detailed combat tactics from *The Monsters Know What They're Doing*.


> [!tip] The Stun-Extract-Kill Pipeline
> Mind Blast stuns → Tentacles grapple + stun again → Extract Brain kills at 0 HP. This three-step combo can kill a PC in 2–3 rounds. The mind flayer's escape plan (*plane shift* self only) means it never fights to the death. Always have thralls engage first.

> [!example] Mind Flayer Arcanist (CR 8)
> Adds 10th-level wizard casting to an already terrifying creature. *Wall of force* splits the party, *shield* adds +5 AC as a reaction, and *lightning bolt* provides AoE the base mind flayer lacks. Significantly more dangerous — use sparingly.

- **What it is:** The iconic psionic horror — tentacle-faced brain eaters. CR 7 with devastating crowd control (Mind Blast) and an instant-kill mechanic (Extract Brain). One of the most feared monsters in D&D.
- **When to use:** Underdark campaigns, horror scenarios, BBEG masterminds. A single mind flayer with thralls is a signature encounter for levels 6–8.
- **Key combat tactics:** Opens with Mind Blast (60 ft. cone, DC 15 Int, 4d8+4 psychic + [[Stunned|stunned]] for 1 min). Sends thralls to engage while it stays back. Grapples [[Stunned|stunned]] targets with Tentacles (2d10+4 psychic + auto-stun on failed DC 15 Int save). Extract Brain on [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] + [[Grappled|grappled]] targets = 10d10 damage, instant kill at 0 HP. *[[Dominate Monster|Dominate monster]]* (1/day) turns a PC against the party. **Plane shift** (self only) is the escape button.
- **Arcanist variant (CR 8):** Adds 10th-level wizard casting. Key spells: *[[Shield|shield]]* (+5 AC reaction), *[[Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]]* (8d6), **wall of force** (battlefield control), **confusion** (more crowd control). Significantly more dangerous.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Stunned|stunned]] (Mind Blast, Tentacles), [[Grappled|grappled]] (Tentacles)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** With 2–4 grimlocks or quaggoth thralls. A mind flayer colony with an elder brain. Pair with [[Intellect-Devourer|intellect devourers]] as scouts. An arcanist as a rogue outcast villain.
