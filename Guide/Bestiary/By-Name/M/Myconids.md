# Myconids

![Myconids](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/images/myconid.jpg)


> **Type**: Small/Medium/Large plant, lawful neutral | **CR**: 0 / 1/2 / 2 + Spore Servant template | **Source**: Monster Manual p.230–232

Myconids are intelligent, ambulatory fungi that live in the Underdark, seek enlightenment, and deplore violence. If approached peacefully, myconids gladly provide shelter or allow safe passage through their colonies.

## Spore Servant Template

A spore servant is any Large or smaller creature brought back to life by the animating spores of a myconid sovereign. A creature that was never flesh and blood to begin with (such as a construct, elemental, ooze, plant, or undead) can't be turned into a spore servant.

- **Retained:** AC, HP, Hit Dice, Str, Dex, Con, vulnerabilities, resistances, immunities
- **Lost:** Saving throw and skill bonuses, special senses, special traits. Loses any action that isn't Multiattack or a melee weapon attack dealing bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage.
- **Type:** Plant (loses any tags)
- **Alignment:** Unaligned
- **Speed:** Reduce all speeds by 10 ft. (minimum 5 ft.)
- **Ability Scores:** Int 2 (-4), Wis 6 (-2), Cha 1 (-5)
- **Senses:** Blindsight 30 ft. (blind beyond this radius)
- **Condition Immunities:** [[Blinded|blinded]], [[Charmed|charmed]], [[Frightened|frightened]], [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]]
- **Languages:** None (responds to rapport spore orders from myconids)

## Lore

**Circles and Melds.** The largest myconid in a colony is its sovereign, which presides over one or more social groups called circles. A circle consists of twenty or more myconids that work, live, and meld together. A meld is a form of communal meditation that allows myconids to transcend their dull subterranean existence. The myconids' rapport spores bind the participants into a group consciousness. Hallucination spores then induce a shared dream that provides entertainment and social interaction. Myconids consider melding to be the purpose of their existence.

**Myconid Reproduction.** Like other fungi, myconids reproduce by mundane sporing. They carefully control their spores' release to avoid overpopulation.

## DM Quick Reference

> [!quote] Tactical Deep Dive
> See [[Myconid-Tactics]] for detailed combat tactics from *The Monsters Know What They're Doing*.


> [!tip] Peaceful First — Spores Second
> Myconids avoid combat and use Rapport Spores for telepathic communication. Always offer the party a chance to communicate before hostilities. A myconid colony is one of the few safe havens in the Underdark — don't waste that narrative potential.

> [!example] Spore Servant Template
> Apply to any Large or smaller corpse: retains physical stats but becomes a mindless plant with blindsight 30 ft. The quaggoth is just the sample — a spore servant hook horror, drow, or even a young dragon is far more terrifying. DM's choice.

- **What they are:** Peaceful fungal Underdark dwellers. Sprout (CR 0), Adult (CR 1/2), Sovereign (CR 2). Non-violent by nature — they use Rapport Spores for telepathic communication and prefer to stun or incapacitate threats rather than kill. The Sovereign can create Spore Servants from corpses.
- **When to use:** Underdark exploration, peaceful encounters, fungal cavern settings. Myconids are one of the few friendly Underdark factions — great as allies, quest-givers, or safe havens. Spore Servants add a creepy undead-plant vibe.
- **Key combat tactics:** Myconids avoid combat. If forced to fight: Pacifying Spores [[Stunned|stun]] (DC 11/12 Con), Hallucination Spores [[Poisoned|poison]] + [[Incapacitated|incapacitate]] (DC 12 Con, Sovereign only). Distress Spores alert all myconids within 240 ft. when one is hurt — the whole colony responds. Sovereign's Multiattack = spore + fist (3d4+1 + 3d4 poison, avg 15). Sun Sickness means they never leave the Underdark.
- **Spore Servant template:** Apply to any Large or smaller corpse. Retains physical stats but becomes a mindless plant with blindsight 30 ft. DMs can create spore servants from any creature — the quaggoth is just the sample.
- **Conditions inflicted:** [[Stunned|stunned]] (Pacifying Spores), [[Poisoned|poisoned]]/[[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] (Hallucination Spores)
- **Suggested encounter pairings:** A myconid colony offering shelter in the Underdark. Sovereign with 2–3 quaggoth spore servants as guards. Myconids caught between drow and duergar factions. A meld ceremony the party can join for visions.
