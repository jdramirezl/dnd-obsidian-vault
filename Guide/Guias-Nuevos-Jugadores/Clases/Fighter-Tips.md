# ⚔️ Fighter — New Player Tips

> Quick-reference guide for getting the most out of your Fighter at levels 1–5.

---

## 1. Creative Uses for Class Features

### Second Wind — More Than Emergency Healing
- **Second Wind is a bonus action.** You can Attack AND heal on the same turn. Never waste a full turn just healing.
- **It recharges on SHORT rest.** Use it every fight if you need it — you'll get it back in an hour.
- **It's not a spell.** Works in antimagic fields, while silenced, and can't be counterspelled.

### Action Surge — The Ultimate Flex
- **Action Surge gives a full extra ACTION, not just an attack.** You can Dash (double movement), Dodge, Help, Use an Object, or any other action.
- **Dash + Attack in one turn.** Move full speed, Attack, Action Surge to Dash, move again. Cover 60+ feet and still attack.
- **Action Surge + Grapple.** Attack action (grapple), then Action Surge Attack action (shove prone). Enemy is grappled AND prone in one turn.
- **Eldritch Knights: Action Surge lets you cast TWO leveled spells in one turn** (the bonus-action spell rule doesn't apply since neither is a bonus action spell).
- **Save it for the moment it matters most.** Opening round of a boss fight or finishing a fleeing enemy.

### Fighting Style — Hidden Depth
- **Dueling works with a shield.** "One hand, no other weapons" — a shield isn't a weapon. Sword + shield + Dueling = +2 damage AND high AC.
- **Archery's +2 to hit is the best flat accuracy bonus in the game.** Offsets cover penalties and the -5 from Sharpshooter.
- **Protection is a reaction every round.** Free disadvantage on attacks against allies within 5 ft.

### Battle Master Maneuvers — Tactical Genius
- **Trip Attack before your second attack.** If the target falls prone, your remaining attacks have advantage (melee).
- **Precision Attack can be declared AFTER you roll but BEFORE effects apply.** See a 12 that might miss? Add the d8.
- **Riposte gives you attacks on the enemy's turn.** Extra damage per round without using your action.
- **Commander's Strike lets your Rogue get a second Sneak Attack** in a round (once on your turn, once on theirs).
- **Maneuvering Attack repositions allies without provoking opportunity attacks.** Get your wizard out of melee.

## 2. Common Mistakes & Misunderstandings

| ❌ Mistake | ✅ Correct Rule |
|-----------|----------------|
| Action Surge gives a bonus action | Action Surge grants an extra **action**, not a bonus action. Still only one bonus action per turn. |
| Extra Attack stacks when multiclassing | Fighter 5/Ranger 5 does NOT give 3 attacks. Extra Attack features don't stack. |
| GWF rerolls Smite/Sneak Attack dice | GWF only rerolls the **weapon's damage dice**, not extra dice from other features. |
| Forgetting Second Wind recharges on short rest | Use it freely — you'll get it back in an hour. |
| Two maneuvers on one attack | Only **one maneuver per attack**. With Extra Attack, use a different maneuver on each attack. |
| Protection style without a shield | Protection **requires** wielding a shield. |
| Action Surge = two turns | You get one extra action, not a full extra turn. Movement and bonus action are unchanged. |

## 3. Combat Tactics Cheat Sheet (Levels 1–5)

### Level 1 — Solid Foundation
| Phase | What to Do |
|-------|-----------|
| **Action** | Attack with your weapon (match your Fighting Style) |
| **Bonus Action** | Second Wind if you're hurt (1d10 + 1 HP) |
| **Reaction** | Protection style to protect an ally, or Opportunity Attack |

### Level 2 — Action Surge Online
| Phase | What to Do |
|-------|-----------|
| **Action** | Attack |
| **Action Surge** | Attack AGAIN (or Dash to close distance, or Dodge if surrounded) |
| **Bonus Action** | Second Wind if needed |

### Level 3 — Subclass Defines You
| Subclass | Priority |
|----------|----------|
| **Champion** | Attack every turn. Crits on 19–20. Simple and effective. |
| **Battle Master** | Open with **Trip Attack** on first hit → advantage on second. Use **Precision Attack** to turn misses into hits. Save **Riposte** for enemy turns. |
| **Eldritch Knight** | Learn *Shield* (+5 AC reaction) and *Absorb Elements*. Weapons are your main damage; spells are defensive. |

### Level 5 — Extra Attack Power Spike
| Phase | What to Do |
|-------|-----------|
| **Action** | Attack TWICE (Extra Attack) |
| **Action Surge** | Attack TWICE MORE = 4 attacks in one turn |
| **Battle Master** | Trip on hit 1 → advantage on hits 2, 3, 4. Devastating. |
| **Champion** | 4 attacks = 4 chances to crit on 19–20. ~34% chance of at least one crit. |

### General Tips
- **Short rests are your best friend.** Action Surge and Second Wind recharge on short rest. Push for them.
- **You are the most consistent damage dealer.** No spell slots to run out of, no rage to expire.
- **Fighters get MORE ASIs than any other class (7 total).** Use them for powerful feats.

## 4. Roleplay Prompts

### What Motivates a Fighter?
- **Professional soldier** — fighting is your trade, your craft, your livelihood
- **Protector** — you fight so others don't have to. Every scar is someone else's life saved.
- **Glory seeker** — you want your name in songs and your deeds in history books
- **Student of war** — combat is an art form, and you study it like a scholar studies books
- **Reluctant warrior** — you're good at violence but wish you didn't have to be

### How Does a Fighter Train?
- Daily weapon drills at dawn — 100 swings with each weapon, every morning
- Studying battlefield tactics from history books (Battle Master especially)
- Sparring with anyone willing — and some who aren't
- Maintaining armor and weapons obsessively — a well-oiled blade is a reliable friend
- Running in full armor to build endurance

### Daily Routine
- Wake early, exercise and drill before breakfast
- Check and maintain all equipment — sharpen blades, oil armor joints, restring bows
- Study the terrain ahead — chokepoints, high ground, escape routes
- Evening: clean weapons, discuss tactics with the party, plan march order

### Personality Hooks
- Keeps a journal of every battle — tactics used, mistakes made, lessons learned
- Gives nicknames to weapons and talks to them
- Instinctively positions between danger and allies, even in social situations
- Respects competence above all else — doesn't care about titles, only skill
- Has a pre-battle ritual (prayer, stretching routine, lucky charm)

## 5. Multiclass Dip Tips

| Dip | Levels | What You Get | Why It's Good |
|-----|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Barbarian 1–2** | 1–2 levels | Rage, Reckless Attack | Rage resistance + Reckless advantage. Drop heavy armor for medium. |
| **Rogue 1** | 1 level | Sneak Attack (1d6), Expertise | Sneak Attack triggers with advantage sources. Expertise in Athletics for grappling. |
| **Cleric 1** | 1 level | *Bless*, *Healing Word*, heavy armor (some domains) | *Bless* adds 1d4 to attacks AND saves. *Healing Word* picks up downed allies as bonus action. |
| **Warlock 1–2** | 1–2 levels | *Hex* (1d6/hit), short rest spell slots | *Hex* on 2–4 attacks/turn is massive. Warlock slots recharge on short rest like your features. |
| **Wizard 1** | 1 level (EK) | More spell slots, *Find Familiar*, rituals | Familiar gives advantage via Help. More slots = more *Shield* casts. |

### Best Combos
- **Fighter 5 / Barbarian 2**: Extra Attack + Reckless Attack + Rage. Four reckless attacks with Action Surge.
- **Fighter X / Cleric 1 (War)**: Heavy armor + *Bless* + bonus action attacks from War Priest.
- **Fighter 2 / Warlock X**: Action Surge on a Warlock = two *Eldritch Blast* volleys in one turn.

> [!warning] Multiclass Requirements
> Fighter requires **STR 13 or DEX 13** to multiclass into or out of.
