# 🗡️ Martial Class Tips — New Player Guides

Quick-reference guides for new players covering creative feature uses, common mistakes, combat tactics (levels 1–5), roleplay prompts, and multiclass dip tips.

## Martial Classes

| Class | Guide | Core Fantasy |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 🪓 Barbarian | [[Barbarian-Tips]] | Primal warrior fueled by Rage — damage sponge and grappler |
| ⚔️ Fighter | [[Fighter-Tips]] | Master of weapons and tactics — most consistent damage dealer |
| 👊 Monk | [[Monk-Tips]] | Ki-powered martial artist — mobile skirmisher and stunner |
| 🛡️ Paladin | [[Paladin-Tips]] | Holy warrior — burst damage (Smite), healing, and party auras |
| 🏹 Ranger | [[Ranger-Tips]] | Wilderness hunter — sustained damage, tracking, and nature magic |

## How to Use These Guides

Each guide has 5 sections:
1. **Creative Uses** — non-obvious tricks new players miss
2. **Common Mistakes** — rules people get wrong
3. **Combat Cheat Sheet** — what to do on your turn at levels 1–5
4. **Roleplay Prompts** — motivation, training, daily routine, personality hooks
5. **Multiclass Dip Tips** — 1–2 level dips that synergize well
