## Quick Reference: Spell Slot Recovery

| Class | Long Rest | Short Rest | Special |
|-------|-----------|------------|---------|
| Bard | All slots | — | Font of Inspiration (Bardic Inspiration recharges on short rest at 5th) |
| Cleric | All slots | Channel Divinity recharges | — |
| Druid | All slots | Wild Shape recharges | Natural Recovery (Circle of the Land) |
| Sorcerer | All slots + all Sorcery Points | — | Convert slots ↔ Sorcery Points |
| Warlock | All slots | **All Pact Magic slots** | Warlock is THE short-rest class |
| Wizard | All slots | Arcane Recovery (1/day) | Recover slots ≤ half wizard level |
