# 🌿 Druid — New Player Tips (Levels 1–5)

> **Role**: Controller, healer, shapeshifter, utility caster
> **Casting Stat**: Wisdom | **System**: Prepared caster (full druid spell list daily)
> **Spell Slots**: Full caster progression, recover on long rest
> **Focus**: Druidic focus (staff, totem, mistletoe, yew wand)

---

## 1. Creative Uses for Class Features

### Wild Shape (2nd Level)
- **Scout as a spider or cat**: Tiny beasts can squeeze through cracks, hide easily, and nobody suspects a house cat. Use Wild Shape for infiltration, not just combat
- **Second HP pool**: Wild Shape gives you the beast's HP. When it drops to 0, you revert with your original HP. It's basically free hit points
- **Maintain concentration while shifted**: Cast *Moonbeam* or *Call Lightning*, THEN Wild Shape. You keep concentration and the spell keeps dealing damage while you're a bear
- **Escape grapples**: Wild Shape into something tiny (spider) to slip free, or something huge to break the grapple
- **Utility forms**: Need to cross a river? Giant frog. Need to climb a wall? Spider. Need to carry something heavy? Draft horse. Think beyond combat

### Circle of the Moon (2nd Level)
- **Bonus action shift**: Moon druids shift as a bonus action, so you can cast a spell AND shift in the same turn
- **Brown Bear at level 2**: CR 1 with 34 HP and multiattack. You're the strongest melee character in the party at level 2
- **Heal in beast form**: Spend a spell slot as a bonus action to regain 1d8 HP per slot level while in beast form. This extends your tanking dramatically

### Circle of the Land (2nd Level)
- **Natural Recovery**: Recover spell slots on a short rest (combined level ≤ half druid level, rounded up). At level 4, recover a 2nd-level slot — that's a free *Moonbeam*
- **Bonus cantrip**: You get 3 cantrips at level 2 instead of 2. Take *Shillelagh* if you're in melee, or *Thorn Whip* for pulling enemies into hazards
- **Circle Spells are always prepared**: They don't count against your limit. Grassland gives you *Haste* at level 5 — a spell not even on the druid list

### Druidic (1st Level)
- **Secret messages**: Leave hidden messages only other druids can read (DC 15 to spot, can't decipher without magic). Great for marking safe paths or warning of danger

## 2. Spell Selection Tips (Levels 1–5)

### Always Prepare These
| Spell | Level | Why |
|-------|-------|-----|
| *Healing Word* | 1st | Bonus action ranged heal. Same reason as every other caster — pick up downed allies |
| *Entangle* | 1st | Restrained condition in a 20-foot square. Devastating at level 1. Concentration |
| *Goodberry* | 1st | 10 berries, each heals 1 HP. Cast at end of day with leftover slots. Berries last 24 hours — free breakfast healing |
| *Moonbeam* | 2nd | Moveable damage zone. 2d10 radiant per turn, forces shapechangers to revert. Concentration |
| *Conjure Animals* | 3rd | Summon 8 wolves for pack tactics and massive damage output. One of the strongest 3rd-level spells in the game |

### Avoid These
| Spell | Why |
|-------|-----|
| *Barkskin* | Sets AC to 16 minimum, but requires concentration. Your concentration is better spent on *Moonbeam* or *Entangle* |
| *Flame Blade* | Requires concentration, uses your action each turn, and doesn't scale well. *Moonbeam* is better in every way |
| *Animal Friendship* | Too situational. Prepare it only when you know you'll encounter beasts |

## 3. Common Druid Mistakes

1. **Forgetting you can't cast spells in Wild Shape**: You keep concentration on pre-cast spells, but you CANNOT cast new spells while shifted (until Beast Spells at level 18). Plan your spell before shifting
2. **Not using Wild Shape for utility**: Combat isn't the only use. A spider can scout, a fish can swim underwater, a bird (level 8+) can fly. Think creatively
3. **Ignoring Goodberry**: Cast it with your last spell slot before a long rest. The berries last 24 hours — that's 10 HP of healing for tomorrow at no real cost
4. **Forgetting you're a prepared caster**: You can swap your ENTIRE spell list every long rest. Going underground? Prepare *Darkvision*. Expecting undead? Prepare *Protection from Evil and Good*
5. **Moon Druids forgetting to heal in beast form**: You can spend spell slots as a bonus action to regain HP while shifted. Don't just revert when your bear HP runs out — heal and keep fighting

## 4. Combat Tactics Cheat Sheet (Levels 1–5)

### Level 1: Nature's Wrath
**Round 1**: *Entangle* (20-foot square, restrained) → position behind cover
**Round 2+**: Cantrip (*Produce Flame* for ranged, *Shillelagh* + melee if brave)
**Emergency**: *Healing Word* (bonus action) + cantrip

### Level 2–4 (Circle of the Moon): Bear Mode
**Round 1**: Cast *Moonbeam* or *Flaming Sphere* (action) → Wild Shape into Brown Bear (bonus action)
**Round 2+**: Move *Moonbeam* onto enemies (bonus action) → Multiattack as bear (action)
**When bear HP drops**: Spend a spell slot to heal (bonus action) → keep attacking
**When bear drops to 0**: Revert to druid form, still concentrating on your spell

### Level 2–4 (Circle of the Land): Spell Artillery
**Round 1**: *Entangle* or *Moonbeam* (concentration) → position safely
**Round 2+**: Move *Moonbeam* or cantrip → *Healing Word* if needed
**Short rest**: Use Natural Recovery to get a slot back

### Level 5: Summon the Pack
**Round 1**: *Conjure Animals* (8 wolves with pack tactics) → let them swarm
**Alternative**: *Call Lightning* for sustained 3d10 damage each round (bonus action to call bolts)
**Moon Druid**: Cast *Call Lightning* → shift into Dire Wolf → call lightning bolts while biting

### Golden Rule
> **Cast your concentration spell FIRST, then Wild Shape.** The spell persists through the transformation. A bear concentrating on *Moonbeam* is terrifying.

## 5. Spell Slot Management

- **Levels 1–2**: 2–3 slots. *Entangle* in the first fight, save a slot for *Healing Word*. Cast *Goodberry* with your last slot before sleeping
- **Level 3–4**: 4–6 slots. *Moonbeam* is your workhorse — 1 slot for potentially 10 rounds of 2d10 damage. Wild Shape is free (2 uses per short rest)
- **Level 5**: 7 slots including 3rd-level. *Conjure Animals* or *Call Lightning* for big fights. 1st-level slots for *Healing Word* and end-of-day *Goodberry*
- **Wild Shape recharges on short rest**: 2 uses per short rest. In a day with 2 short rests, that's 6 Wild Shapes. Use them freely for scouting and combat
- **Circle of the Land Natural Recovery**: Recover slots on a short rest (1/day). Always use this — it's free spells
- **Goodberry trick**: Always cast *Goodberry* with leftover slots before a long rest. You regain the slots anyway, and the berries last 24 hours
